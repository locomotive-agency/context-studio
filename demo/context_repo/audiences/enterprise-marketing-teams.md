# Enterprise Marketing Teams

Enterprise marketing teams want AI workflows that reuse approved messaging, audience guidance, proof points, and campaign constraints without requiring every marketer to rebuild context manually.

## Needs

- Consistent claims across campaigns and channels.
- A clear distinction between approved guidance and raw research.
- A way to inspect what context an AI workflow received.
- Folder-level defaults so teams do not repeat governance fields on every file.

## Common Objections

- "Search returns too much and does not tell us what is approved."
- "We already have source files, but they are not organized for AI use."
- "We do not want to approve hundreds of raw notes one by one."
