# 11x — Alice

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI SDR / AI-drafted-outreach surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous AI SDR that runs the entire outbound motion — live-signal prospecting, per-prospect research, multi-channel personalized outreach, follow-up, and continuous optimization on replies and meetings.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI SDR agents replacing an entire role (not augmenting reps). Alice's "before/after" stack-replacement diagram is the strongest spotlight angle — particularly when comparing all-in-one autonomous workers vs. ZoomInfo's data + GTM Workspace AI-agent layer.

## Cite
- Key features: Live-signal prospecting, per-prospect AI research, multi-channel sequences (email/social/phone), continuous optimization on replies/meetings, proprietary deliverability + mailbox management
- Strengths: Digital-worker replacing-the-role framing; consolidates data + engagement + intent + warmup; live-signal vs. static database
- Gaps: No MCP / agent-builder ecosystem; no NL audience builder; data layer not sold as a contact database
