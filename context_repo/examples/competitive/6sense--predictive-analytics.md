# 6sense — Predictive Analytics

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (direct overlap at the predictive-reasoning layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Proprietary predictive marketing analytics that score accounts and prospects based on buying-stage prediction; available standalone or bundled with Sales Intelligence.

## Spotlight when
The article is about AI/ML-driven predictive scoring, intent reasoning, or how a "data layer" is different from a "reasoning layer." Best place to make the GTM Context Graph case (Pillar 2) — the contrast is structural: 6sense predicts a single dimension (funnel stage) where GTM Context Graph fuses multiple signals.
