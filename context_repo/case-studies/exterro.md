## Summary

Exterro increased sales productivity with ZoomInfo’s data enrichment. After trying to solve the routing problem in-house, Cody realized they needed an external solution like ZoomInfo that could handle the complexities of their routing strategies and have the flexibility to scale with Exterro’s continued growth. Challenge Exterro’s growth and expansion into new markets and regions meant they needed efficient operations and data management processes to lay the foundation for future growth; however, their former in-house lead-to-account routing process posed several challenges.

## Key Results

- Exterro increased sales productivity with ZoomInfo’s data enrichment
- Exterro has saved even more time by automating data management processes and replacing the 90 evaluations in Process Builder with only 2 triggers that enrich, segment, and route leads
