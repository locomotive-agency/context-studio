**Industry:** SaaS / Software | **Size:** enterprise | **Products:** [[crm-enrichment|CRM Enrichment]], [[operations|Operations]], [[data|ZoomInfo Data]]

## Summary

Datto improved CRM quality with ZoomInfo’s enriched IT contact data. Challenge With a steadily growing database of 950,000 names and counting, Datto was finding that not every incoming record was clean or complete, resulting in three major challenges for Datto; an excess of duplicates, unstandardized data, and a need for improved lead-to-account matching. The number of duplicates in Datto’s database also made it difficult to quantify the Marketing team’s contribution, making accurate reporting and revenue attribution nearly impossible.
