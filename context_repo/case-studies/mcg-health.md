## Summary

MCG Health cleaned 10,000 duplicate records and improved ROI with ZoomInfo. MCG was faced with challenges as a result of having a Salesforce instance that was over a decade old. Challenge Having quality and relevant data is essential to business productivity.

## Key Results

- MCG Health cleaned 10,000 duplicate records and improved ROI with ZoomInfo
- The Results With 10,000 duplicates merged on the first cleanse, Vaughn has seen reduced error rates and has gained control over parent and child hierarchical account information and relationships
- Merged scores are now added together to accurately identify high-potential leads, which aids in their increased ROI of MCG’s marketing activities
- MCG Health has noticed an improved account lifespan since they have unified accounts with a single view of a customer for more personalized and effective account-based marketing
