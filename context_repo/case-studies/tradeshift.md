## Summary

Tradeshift accelerated global sales prospecting with ZoomInfo data. As a result, it became increasingly difficult to manage their global compliance obligations. Challenge Tradeshift’s database contained many duplicates created intentionally to support their specific database structure and product offerings.

- 23% increase
- The Results Tradeshift orchestrated its data using OperationsOS, which reduced the risk of compliance breaches and ensured that opt-ins, opt-outs, and unsubscribers are registered
