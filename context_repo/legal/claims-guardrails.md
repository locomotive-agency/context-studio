# Claims Guardrails

## Allowed Claims

- Context Studio helps teams separate governed Documents from supporting source Collections.
- Context Studio supports metadata-first retrieval through MCP tools.
- Context Studio stores governed Documents in a Git-backed repository.

## Restricted Claims

- Do not claim automated legal approval.
- Do not claim the system prevents all hallucinations.
- Do not claim transcript language is approved customer evidence unless a governed proof record says so.
