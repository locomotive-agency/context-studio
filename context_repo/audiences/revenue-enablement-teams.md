# Revenue Enablement Teams

Revenue enablement teams use Context Studio to produce talk tracks, objection handling, and training material from approved Documents supported by cited buyer language.

## Needs

- Fast access to approved value propositions and proof points.
- Search over transcripts for current objections and language.
- Confidence that transcript snippets are cited as supporting sources, not treated as official claims.
- Viewer access for field teams that should test context without editing records.
