# Advanced Audience Targeting

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/features/ad-targeting](https://www.zoominfo.com/features/ad-targeting)

## Summary

B2B audience targeting solution for display and social advertising campaigns. Builds audiences from companies showing buying intent, filtered by job title, management level, and department — achieving match rates 25% higher than industry average.


## Key Capabilities

- Intent-based audience curation for display and social ads
- Targeting by job title, seniority, department, and firmographics
- Native syndication network for campaign distribution
- Campaign management for creative, copy, and spend
