# Synthetic demo claim guardrails

All companies, people, quotes, transcripts, and outcomes in the `context-system-marketing` folder and `sample_sources/sales-conversations` folder are fictional.

## Required handling

- Label this material as synthetic when using it outside local testing.
- Do not describe any transcript as a real customer call.
- Do not convert a transcript passage into an approved claim without a separate approved proof point.
- Do not claim the system guarantees compliance or automatically approves marketing language.
- Do not imply real revenue, pipeline, or productivity outcomes from these examples.

## Safe wording

- "In this synthetic transcript..."
- "For demo purposes, the buyer says..."
- "This Collection passage is supporting context, not approved proof."
- "The approved OKF record and the cited Collection passage are returned separately."
