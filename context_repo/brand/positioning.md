# Context Studio Brand Positioning

Context Studio is a governed context workspace for teams that want AI systems to use the right marketing knowledge without treating every source as equal.

The product helps teams separate approved Documents from searchable supporting sources. Documents hold durable guidance such as positioning, claims, audience profiles, and value propositions. Collections hold raw material such as transcripts, interview notes, and research files.

The core promise is simple: give AI agents enough context to know what exists, enough governance to know what can be trusted, and enough traceability to retrieve the source when detail is needed.

## Message Pillars

1. Govern the facts that matter.
2. Keep raw sources useful without making them authoritative.
3. Let agents browse metadata first and read full content only when needed.

## Audience Fit

Context Studio is for marketing, enablement, product marketing, and operations teams managing reusable AI context across campaigns, content, and sales workflows.
