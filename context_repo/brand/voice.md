# Context Studio Voice

Context Studio speaks with calm authority. The voice is practical, specific, and grounded in the work of marketing teams that need AI outputs they can inspect and trust.

## Attributes

- Clear: use concrete language and avoid vague AI claims.
- Useful: explain the decision the reader can make next.
- Governed: distinguish approved guidance from supporting evidence.
- Modern: sound current without relying on slang or hype.

## Avoid

- Claims that imply magic, automation without oversight, or fully autonomous decision-making.
- Treating search results as approved truth.
- Overstating the maturity of a customer process.
