# FindThatLead

## Sub-products

- [[findthatlead--prospector|FindThatLead Prospector + Manager]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales
- [[findthatlead--email-finder|FindThatLead Email Finder + Verifier + Chrome Extension]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales

## Summary

FindThatLead is an SMB-leaning B2B email-finder and cold-email suite — the buyer is a sales rep, growth marketer, or recruiter who needs a low-cost prospecting + outbound stack rather than an enterprise GTM platform. Its core surfaces (Search, Prospector, Email Sender, Email Verifier, internal Manager CRM, Chrome extension) directly overlap ZoomInfo's Data foundation and the legacy ZoomInfo Sales prospecting UI on contact-finding workflows, but FindThatLead has no analog to GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, intent data, conversation intelligence, or APIs & MCP. The company self-describes as a "B2B sales prospecting tool" and never claims platform/intelligence-graph language, signaling a tactical point-tool position downstream of where ZoomInfo competes. Pricing tiers are JS-rendered and were not exposed on extraction.

## Product Offerings

### Search (Email Finder)

- **What it does:** Find a prospect's business email by name, company, or website domain; supports lookup by name, by company, by domain, by technology used, and from URLs/profiles.
- **Key features:**
  - Lookup email by name, by company, and by domain
  - Filter leads by the technology they use
  - Bulk email lookup from uploaded list of names + URLs/companies
  - Double-verification process; cited >90% accuracy
  - Free plan: 10 monthly searches
  - GDPR-compliant; returns name, email, and job title
- **Source URL:** https://findthatlead.com/en/lead-search/

### Prospector

- **What it does:** Automated B2B prospecting tool — define industry, role, location, or keywords and FindThatLead returns a tailored list of prospects with phone numbers and email addresses.
- **Key features:**
  - Filter by industry, job title, location, or keywords
  - Real-time email generation against an updated database
  - Outputs both phone numbers and email addresses
  - Integrates with Salesforce, Pipedrive, HubSpot via Zapier (per FAQ)
  - Most popular plan priced at $55/month (per Prospector FAQ)
- **Source URL:** https://findthatlead.com/en/prospector/

### Email Sender (cold email software)

- **What it does:** Cold-email campaign + drip-marketing tool that sends ultra-personalized automated email sequences from FindThatLead-found contact lists.
- **Key features:**
  - Drip campaign sequences with scheduled follow-ups
  - Email personalization (name, job title, company tokens)
  - Free to use — does not consume credits
  - Recommended cap: 250 emails/day per account
  - Gmail account compatible for sending
- **Source URL:** https://findthatlead.com/en/email-sender/

### Email Verifier

- **What it does:** Bulk and single email verification tool that checks syntax, domain, and SMTP deliverability before outreach.
- **Key features:**
  - Catch-all email checking
  - MX record testing and SMTP authentication testing
  - Bulk verification supported
  - Three-score verification with up to 99% confidence on highest tier
  - Free plan: 10 monthly verifications; 1 credit per verification
- **Source URL:** https://findthatlead.com/en/email-verifier/

### Chrome Extension (Email Extractor)

- **What it does:** Browser extension that extracts emails, job titles, and company info from any webpage or profile (including Crunchbase) using credits from a FindThatLead account.
- **Key features:**
  - One-click email extraction from profiles or search results
  - Scrapes emails directly from Crunchbase company profiles
  - Uses 1 credit per contact found; 10 monthly credits on free plan
  - Returns email, job title, and company information
  - Up to 99% confidence based on three verification scores
- **Source URL:** https://findthatlead.com/en/chrome-extension/

### Manager (internal Sales CRM)

- **What it does:** Built-in CRM to store and organize a team's leads inside FindThatLead so users do not require an external CRM for basic lead management.
- **Key features:**
  - Stores and organizes lead lists generated by other FindThatLead tools
  - Team-shared lead organization
- **Source URL:** https://findthatlead.com/en/

### Adjacent suite surfaces (Social Search, Local, Agency, Implementation Service)

- **What it does:** Additional tools and managed-service add-ons surfaced on the homepage nav — Social Search (find email from a social-media URL), Local (local-business contact generation), Agency (multichannel B2B lead-gen managed service), and Implementation Service (paid 2-hour onboarding).
- **Key features:**
  - Social Search: paste a social URL to generate the prospect's email
  - Local: local-business contact generation
  - Agency: managed multichannel B2B lead-generation service
  - Implementation Service: 2-hour paid onboarding for setup, warmup, first list generation
- **Source URL:** https://findthatlead.com/en/

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Not explicitly called out — broad B2B (testimonials cite digital project management, influencer-partnership marketing, customer intelligence)
- **Company size called out:** SMB-leaning — homepage cites "300,000 companies trust FindThatLead"; free plan + low monthly entry signal small-team buyer
- **Persona / role focus:** Sales reps / sales teams, growth marketers, recruiters, B2B-companies operators
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Find email and phone for prospects by name, company, or domain
  - Build targeted prospect lists by industry, role, location, or technographic
  - Cold-email outbound and drip-sequence campaigns
  - Email verification to reduce bounce rate and protect deliverability
  - Chrome-extension lookup directly inside Crunchbase / web pages
  - Recruiter sourcing — find candidate email and social info

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/prospector/)
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Built-in Email Sender (in-house cold-email tool); no Outreach / Salesloft integration surfaced
- **Data / enrichment:** Crunchbase (scraped via Chrome extension and lookup engine) (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/chrome-extension/)
- **Other notable integrations:** Zapier (cited as bridge for CRMs and "thousands of tools"), Make.com (Gmail integration documented), Gmail (sending account)

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Free plan across the suite (10 searches + 10 verifications/month, no credit card) lowers the entry point well below enterprise data vendors (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/lead-search/).
- All-in-one suite for SMB outbound — find + verify + send + manage — inside one credit system, including a built-in CRM ("Manager") and an in-house cold-email tool ("Email Sender") (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/).
- Three-score email-verification algorithm with stated 99% confidence on highest tier and a 30-day re-verification cycle on stored data (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/ourdata/).
- Crunchbase-native lookup — Chrome extension scrapes emails directly from Crunchbase company profiles (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/chrome-extension/).
- GDPR compliance cited prominently across every product page (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/ourdata/).
- Optional managed-service add-ons: Agency (done-for-you lead gen) and a 2-hour Implementation Service for paid onboarding (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation ↔ FindThatLead Search + Prospector + Our Data.** FindThatLead claims 200M professional profiles, 9M company profiles, real-time email generation with up to 99% confidence, and outputs both phone numbers and emails — the same contact-data lane ZoomInfo's Data pillar sells (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/ourdata/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (legacy prospecting UI) ↔ FindThatLead Prospector.** Prospector lets users define industry, role, location, or keywords and returns a tailored prospect list with emails and phone numbers — the same workflow ZoomInfo Sales surfaces (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/prospector/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** **ZoomInfo Marketing (engagement automations) and ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality) ↔ FindThatLead Email Sender + Email Verifier + Manager.** FindThatLead bundles cold-email/drip sequences, email verification, and an internal CRM — adjacent to ZoomInfo Marketing's engagement layer and ZoomInfo Operations' data-quality layer, but FindThatLead's tools are tactical SMB versions, not enterprise platforms (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/email-sender/).
- **No overlap:** No reviewed FindThatLead page references GTM Context Graph-style reasoning, Intent Data, conversation intelligence (no Chorus analog), APIs & MCP server / agent-builder ecosystem, an Enterprise API, ABM orchestration, or account-level scoring (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ FindThatLead has no own-domain `vs ZoomInfo` page; the head-to-head G2 comparison page (https://www.g2.com/compare/findthatlead-vs-zoominfo-sales) returned HTTP 403 across all fallback extractor tiers, so no specific competitor claims were captured.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The Smartest B2B Sales Prospecting Tool for your Business — instantly access thousands of phone numbers and email addresses." Positions as the all-in-one leadgen flow: find lead → send campaign → close deal (source: https://findthatlead.com/en/).
- **Recurring proof points:** "300,000 companies trust FindThatLead", 200M professional profiles + 9M company profiles, 1,376-reviews badge across all product pages, customer testimonial metrics ("+10,000 emails/month", "+50% more meets", "50% open rate"), 99% verification confidence, 30-day data re-verification cycle.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as a "B2B sales prospecting tool" and "all-in-one leadgen tool" — never claims platform / GTM / intelligence-graph language. Self-positioning is a tactical SMB point tool, not a platform.
