# G2 Market Intelligence

## Sub-products

- [[g2-market-intelligence--market-intelligence|G2 Market Intelligence]] — competes with Chorus
- [[g2-market-intelligence--competitive-pulse|G2 Competitive Pulse]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing
- [[g2-market-intelligence--mcp-server|G2 MCP Server]] — competes with APIs & MCP

## Summary

G2 Market Intelligence is G2's vendor-side competitive analytics product — an interactive dashboard that turns G2 review data and Buyer Intent comparison signals into real-time insights on category traffic, satisfaction (NPS, ease of use, etc.), win/loss switching trends, and pricing/contracting benchmarks. It targets B2B software vendors' marketing, product marketing, RevOps, customer marketing, and competitive-intelligence teams (sample customer: Druva's Director of Competitive Intelligence). The most material crossover with ZoomInfo is on the "why-deals-move" / churn-signal layer that ZoomInfo addresses via Chorus + the GTM Context Graph and on intent/ABM signals where G2 Buyer Intent + the new Competitive Pulse (March 2026) overlap with ZoomInfo Marketing. G2 Market Intelligence is a tangential competitor — vendor analytics on G2's marketplace activity, NOT a B2B contact/company database — and is sold only inside the Enterprise plan with no public dollar pricing.

## Product Offerings

### G2 Market Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[g2-market-intelligence--market-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Interactive vendor-facing dashboard that turns G2 review data and buyer behavior signals into real-time competitive insights about a vendor's category, satisfaction scores, win/loss dynamics, and pricing/contracting trends (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).
- **Key features:**
  - Category Overview tab with AI-generated summaries comparing NPS, switching trends, and pricing across all products in a category with at least 10 G2 reviews
  - Traffic Analytics tab combining G2 category traffic and Buyer Intent comparison signals — surfaces top 10 competitors by comparison pageviews and buyer firmographics (country, company size, industry)
  - Satisfaction tab analyzing 10 measures of customer sentiment (NPS, ease of setup, ease of use, quality of support, meets requirements, etc.) plotted against up to five competitors
  - Win/Loss tab using "What did you switch from?" review-form data to show "Products Replacing You" and "Products You Replace" with switching themes
  - Pricing and Contracting tab showing Perceived Cost, Average Discount Given, and Average Contract Length sourced from review-form responses
  - Likes/Dislikes machine-learning theme extraction from free-response review text, plus Feature Ratings panel comparing highest/lowest-rated features against competitors
  - Firmographic filters (company size, industry, category, region) and time-interval controls; exports to XLSX or Google Sheets including raw review-level data
  - AI-generated summary panels (Satisfaction at a Glance, Traffic Analytics Summary, Win Loss Summary, Pricing and Contracting Summary, Category Overview Summary) refreshed daily
- **Source URL:** https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence

### Competitive Pulse (announced March 2026 — expanded market intelligence layer)

> **Wiki:** [[g2-market-intelligence--competitive-pulse]]

- **What it does:** Unified view that combines a customer's CRM opportunity data, G2 Buyer Intent signals, and competitive research behavior to flag at-risk deals and surface Churn Threats when existing customers engage with competitors on G2 (source: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/g2-introduces-innovations-to-help-software-companies-build-trust-and-win-in-the-ai-answer-economy-302725392.html).
- **Key features:**
  - Churn Threats — flags when existing customers show increased engagement with competitors on G2
  - AEO Traffic insights — answer engine optimization analytics measuring engagement from AI-generated answers and conversational search
  - Expanded Buyer Intent signals across G2's marketplace
  - Spend and contract intelligence derived from $100B+ in SaaS purchasing agreements (Data Solutions add-on, geared toward investors)
  - G2 MCP server connecting AI assistants (Claude first) to G2 buyer signals, reviews, research behavior, and competitive insights
- **Source URL:** https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/g2-introduces-innovations-to-help-software-companies-build-trust-and-win-in-the-ai-answer-economy-302725392.html

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Built on first-party G2 review data — 6 million verified reviews and signals from 200M+ annual buyers across the G2 ecosystem (G2 + Capterra + Software Advice + GetApp) (source: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/g2-introduces-innovations-to-help-software-companies-build-trust-and-win-in-the-ai-answer-economy-302725392.html).
- Single source of buyer voice + buyer behavior on G2.com — switching themes, perceived cost, average discount, and contract length come directly from review-form questions, not modeled (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).
- AI-generated summary panels refreshed daily across Satisfaction, Traffic, Win Loss, Pricing, and Category Overview tabs (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).
- Buyer Intent comparison signals power Traffic Analytics — vendors see who their buyers are also comparing them against on G2 (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).
- G2 MCP server (Claude integration) brings G2 buyer signals directly into AI workflows — positioned as "the trust layer" for AI-driven software discovery (source: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/g2-introduces-innovations-to-help-software-companies-build-trust-and-win-in-the-ai-answer-economy-302725392.html).
- Category-level data (10+ reviews per product) enables industry trend analysis beyond a vendor's own competitor list (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus / GTM Context Graph (the "why" behind deals).** G2 Market Intelligence's Win/Loss + Switching Themes + Likes/Dislikes panels surface why customers churn or switch — directly competitive with how Chorus + the GTM Context Graph reasons across conversations to explain deal motion. Both promise insight into "why" deals move sourced from buyer voice rather than CRM stage data alone (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (intent / ABM).** G2 Buyer Intent + Comparison Page Views feed Market Intelligence to show which competitors a vendor's buyers are evaluating in real time. Competitive Pulse (March 2026) explicitly combines CRM opportunity data with G2 buyer intent — overlapping ZoomInfo's intent + ABM workflows (source: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/g2-introduces-innovations-to-help-software-companies-build-trust-and-win-in-the-ai-answer-economy-302725392.html).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio category analytics + AI summaries.** G2's Category Overview and AI-generated summary panels are adjacent to GTM Studio's NL audience building and competitive analytics, but G2 is bounded to its own review and buyer-intent dataset rather than a fused first-party + third-party graph (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).
  - **APIs & MCP.** G2's new MCP architecture ships a Claude integration exposing G2 reviews + buyer signals to AI assistants — adjacent to ZoomInfo's MCP server at mcp.zoominfo.com/mcp, though scoped to G2's own data rather than the full GTM Context Graph (source: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/g2-introduces-innovations-to-help-software-companies-build-trust-and-win-in-the-ai-answer-economy-302725392.html).
- **No overlap:**
  - G2 Market Intelligence has no contact, firmographic, technographic, or B2B database equivalent to ZoomInfo's data foundation (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ phone numbers, 200M+ verified emails). Vendors get aggregate competitor sentiment, not records to prospect into (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/market-intelligence).
  - G2's broader Marketing Solutions stack — Reviews, Profiles, Badges, AI Custom Research interviews, G2 Ads sponsored placements — is a marketplace/reputation play that ZoomInfo does not sell (source: https://sell.g2.com/plans).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The closest signal is G2's existing partnership integration ("G2 + ZoomInfo: G2 Buyer Intent data integrates with ZoomInfo Sales and ZoomInfo Marketing"), positioning the two as complementary rather than head-to-head — but this is not a competitive vs page.
