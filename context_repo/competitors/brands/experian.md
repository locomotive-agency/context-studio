## Summary

Experian is a credit bureau and data, analytics, and decisioning vendor whose primary buyers are commercial credit risk leaders, lenders, and data/IT teams — not GTM sellers. Its closest crossover with ZoomInfo is the Experian Data Quality line (Aperture Data Studio) — a self-service data hygiene, validation, de-duplication, and enrichment platform that overlaps with ZoomInfo Operations, plus the U.S. Business Database / B2B Marketing Solutions which sells human-verified B2B prospect data overlapping with ZoomInfo's data foundation. Tangentially, Experian also competes only on data hygiene and B2B firmographics — it does not sell sales engagement, conversation intelligence, a seller front-end, or an MCP/agent ecosystem, and the bulk of its revenue (credit bureau, fraud, identity, healthcare, workforce) is fully outside ZoomInfo's category.

## Product Offerings

### Experian Aperture Data Studio

> **Wiki:** [[experian--aperture-data-studio]]

- **What it does:** Self-service data quality management platform that combines profiling, cleansing, validation, transformation, enrichment, matching/de-duplication, and monitoring on a single web-based application that runs on-prem, in-cloud, or as a hosted/managed service.
- **Key features:**
  - Drag-and-drop workflows for profile, cleanse, validate, transform, enrich, de-duplicate (no SQL required)
  - Address, email, and phone verification (real-time and batch); multi-country address data
  - Advanced fuzzy matching with rule-based match resolution for de-duplication
  - Customizable data catalogue with data quality monitoring and business impact analysis
  - AI & GenAI automations and SDK for extension
  - Enrichment from Experian's globally curated consumer and business data sets
  - SOC 2 Type 2 certified; deploys on physical hardware, VMs, on-prem, cloud, or hosted/managed
- **Source URL:** https://www.edq.com/data-quality-platform/aperture-data-quality-platform/

### Experian Data Quality (data quality and management solutions)

> **Wiki:** [[experian--data-quality]]

- **What it does:** Umbrella line that analyzes, enriches, profiles, standardizes, cleanses, and monitors customer data — covering data management, data quality, and software solutions tiers.
- **Key features:**
  - Data management — strategy and tooling to unify customer data
  - Data quality — profiling, validation, monitoring
  - Software solutions — customizable to data management needs
  - Address, email, and phone validation services (free lookup tools, USPS Address Validation, NCOA, CASS certification)
  - Pre-built Salesforce integration to validate, standardize, and enrich contact data inside Salesforce Sales/Service Cloud
- **Source URL:** https://www.experian.com/business/solutions/data-quality

### Experian Ascend Platform

> **Wiki:** [[experian--ascend-platform]]

- **What it does:** AI-driven, cloud-based platform that combines Experian's proprietary data, analytics, and credit risk + fraud software for the credit lifecycle (revenue growth, decisioning, market expansion, fraud prevention).
- **Key features:**
  - Analytical Sandbox — cloud analytics environment for BI, visualization, and model development
  - Experian Assistant — GenAI chatbot for natural-language analytics
  - Ascend ML Builder — point-and-click model build/train/test
  - Ascend Ops — model deployment and monitoring (ModelOps)
  - Decisioning engine — component-based automated decisioning
  - Fraud detection sandbox for new fraud signals and models
- **Source URL:** https://www.experian.com/business/products/ascend

### U.S. Business Database / B2B Marketing Solutions (Business Information Services)

> **Wiki:** [[experian--us-business-database]]

- **What it does:** B2B prospecting and marketing data product offering verified U.S. business records used for list generation, append, scoring, prescreen/prequalification, and demand-generation campaigns.
- **Key features:**
  - 16M+ active U.S. businesses (compiled from 6,000+ sources, validated by U.S. call-center verification)
  - 60+ filters for firmographic and trade-credit attributes
  - 17.5MM+ verified businesses with firmographic and credit data via Business TargetIQ
  - Marketing data list generation & append; DEI indicators (minority-led / women-led)
  - Demand gen — email, digital onboarding, display, social campaigns
  - Prescreen and prequalification using commercial + consumer attributes
  - Lead enrichment, look-alike analysis, custom predictive modeling
- **Source URL:** https://www.experian.com/business-information/business-marketing

### Commercial Solutions (Business Information Services suite)

> **Wiki:** [[experian--commercial-solutions]]

- **What it does:** Commercial data + analytics platform for lenders, suppliers, and risk teams across the small-business credit lifecycle.
- **Key features:**
  - 28M+ U.S. businesses in commercial database; 190M+ active commercial business families globally; 225 countries/jurisdictions
  - 47M+ trade records in Experian commercial consortium
  - BusinessIQ 2.0 — web platform or API for credit risk decisions
  - Ascend Commercial Suite — analytics + multi-source data for commercial credit lifecycle
  - Sentinel commercial entity fraud suite for fraud detection on commercial entities
  - SBFE Data (Small Business Financial Exchange) blended with Experian commercial + consumer data
- **Source URL:** https://www.experian.com/business/solutions/commercial-solutions

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations ↔ Experian Aperture Data Studio + Experian Data Quality for Salesforce.** Aperture provides data profiling, cleansing, validation, de-duplication, matching, enrichment, and monitoring — the same capability set ZoomInfo Operations sells as data hygiene + enrichment for CRM. The pre-built Salesforce integration validates and enriches contact data directly inside Salesforce, mapping to ZoomInfo Operations' core CRM-clean use case (source: https://www.edq.com/data-quality-platform/aperture-data-quality-platform/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / Data (Pillar 1) ↔ Experian U.S. Business Database / Business TargetIQ / B2B Marketing Solutions.** Experian sells human-verified B2B prospect data with firmographic + credit attributes (16M+ U.S. businesses; 17.5MM+ verified businesses via Business TargetIQ) for list generation, append, prescreen, lead enrichment, and look-alike modeling — direct overlap with ZoomInfo's company/contact data foundation and marketing audience use cases (source: https://www.experian.com/business-information/business-marketing).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1) ↔ Experian Commercial Solutions.** Experian's commercial database overlaps on company-level firmographic data but is built around credit/risk attributes (trade records, SBFE, prescreen) rather than contact-level GTM signals — overlap on "B2B company data," not on contact reach, intent, or seller workflows (source: https://www.experian.com/business/solutions/commercial-solutions).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (AI-driven data quality) ↔ Experian Ascend Platform.** Ascend is an AI/ML modeling and decisioning platform for credit-lifecycle use cases — adjacent only insofar as both apply AI to first-party data, but Ascend is oriented to credit/fraud decisioning, not GTM data orchestration (source: https://www.experian.com/business/products/ascend).
- **No overlap:**
  - Credit bureau reporting and credit decisioning (Business Credit Reports, BusinessIQ, Small Business Credit, Smart Business Reports, debt collections) — Experian operates as a credit bureau, a regulated data space ZoomInfo does not enter (source: https://www.experian.com/business/solutions/commercial-solutions).
  - Fraud and identity solutions, regulatory compliance, healthcare claims/identity, workforce management (I-9, ACA, tax credits) — none have a ZoomInfo counterpart (source: https://www.experian.com/business/solutions/data-quality).
  - Conversation intelligence, sales engagement, GTM workflow surfaces, and an MCP / AI-agent ecosystem — no Experian property reviewed surfaces equivalents to Chorus, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, or ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP server.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Power your business with trusted data" — Experian Aperture Data Studio combines self-service data quality with globally curated data sets in a single platform so business users can build a consistent, accurate, and holistic view of their consumer data (source: https://www.edq.com/data-quality-platform/aperture-data-quality-platform/).
- **Recurring proof points:** SOC 2 Type 2 certified; 16M+ active U.S. businesses (Business Database) compiled from 6,000+ sources with daily verification; 17.5MM+ verified businesses via Business TargetIQ; 28M+ U.S. businesses, 190M+ active commercial business families globally, 47M+ trade records; customer testimonials from West Yorkshire Police (productivity +600%), Schroders Bank, and Edward Don & Company.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames the category as "data quality management platform" / "data quality software & tools" — built around "trusted data" as a foundational data-engineering capability for business users, not as a GTM intelligence layer. ZoomInfo, by contrast, owns the "Go-To-Market Intelligence Platform" category. The two vendors do not share category framing, even where they share data-hygiene capabilities.
