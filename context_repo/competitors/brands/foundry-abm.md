# Foundry ABM

## Sub-products

- [[foundry-abm--abm-platform|Foundry ABM Platform]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing
- [[foundry-abm--intent|Foundry Intent]] — competes with Intent Data
- [[foundry-abm--sales-acceleration|Foundry Sales Acceleration]] — competes with GTM Workspace

## Summary

Foundry ABM is the Triblio-powered account-based orchestration platform sold by Foundry (the data, media and martech division of IDG, Inc.) to B2B technology marketers and demand gen leaders. It bundles cross-channel ABM execution — display and social advertising, website personalization, 1:1 Smart Pages, intent-based plays and CRM activation — with Foundry Intent (a multi-source intent data product) and a Sales Acceleration module that pushes signals into Salesloft / Outreach. Direct overlap with ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM orchestration, intent data, website visitor identification) and adjacent overlap with the Sales side of GTM Workspace via Foundry Sales Acceleration; Foundry's media, content syndication, and publisher-network businesses sit outside ZoomInfo's footprint. Merged with duplicate input row "foundry" — this dossier is scoped specifically to the Foundry ABM software product, not the broader Foundry media/data parent.

## Product Offerings

### Foundry ABM (account-based orchestration platform, powered by Triblio)

> **Wiki:** [[foundry-abm--abm-platform]]

- **What it does:** Cross-channel account-based marketing orchestration platform that lets marketing and sales run intent-driven campaigns across advertising, web personalization, 1:1 landing pages and CRM activation from a single command center.
- **Key features:**
  - Account-based display and social advertising (cross-channel ad campaigns to target accounts)
  - Website personalization (dynamic content based on visitor firmographics)
  - 1:1 Smart Pages (personalized landing pages per target account)
  - Account-based orchestration / multi-stage automated buyer journeys triggered by intent
  - AI scoring on proprietary, human-verified intent data to prioritize in-market accounts
  - Sales activation plays inside the customer's CRM
  - Social media integrations to keep brand top of mind for prospects
  - Account-level reporting and intent-based lead scoring
- **Source URL:** https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/

### Foundry Intent

> **Wiki:** [[foundry-abm--intent]]

- **What it does:** Multi-source intent data product that captures buying signals from the customer's website, the public web, Foundry's owned media network, and human-verified research and feeds them into the ABM platform for activation.
- **Key features:**
  - Captures intent across the customer's website, public web, industry content, and Foundry's opted-in audience
  - 80M+ intent signals captured per week
  - 150M+ audience members in Foundry's network
  - 7,600+ targeting dimensions including intent, persona, and firmographic points
  - Identifies in-market companies visiting the customer's website (reverse-IP / website visitor identification)
  - Activates intent for ad targeting, demand gen leads, and sales activation
  - Pre-built integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics 365, SugarCRM, Adobe, Google
- **Source URL:** https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/data/intent-data/

### Foundry Sales Acceleration

> **Wiki:** [[foundry-abm--sales-acceleration]]

- **What it does:** Sales-side product layered on the Triblio ABM platform that surfaces best contacts, account research and next best actions for sellers already using Foundry Intent, delivered inside their CRM or sales engagement tool.
- **Key features:**
  - Surfaces top accounts, contacts and next-best-actions to sales reps
  - Delivers Foundry Intent insights directly into the rep's CRM
  - Pushes the best contacts into Salesloft or Outreach for prospecting
  - Automated account research to replace manual prep time
  - Buying-signal alerts at the territory level
- **Source URL:** https://www.demandgenreport.com/industry-news/foundry-launches-sales-acceleration-tool-on-the-triblio-abm-platform/7779/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- "Only ABM platform with proprietary, human-verified intent data and AI scoring" — Foundry pairs the Triblio orchestration engine with first-party publisher data instead of relying solely on third-party intent vendors (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/).
- End-to-end stack combining IDG's quality content, first-party publisher data, marketing services, and Triblio's ABM SaaS — pitched as removing the need to stitch together boutique vendors (source: https://foundryco.com/blog/idg-acquires-triblio-heres-why-it-matters/).
- Foundry's intent data network includes 80M+ signals/week, 150M+ audience members, and 7,600+ targeting dimensions sourced from its owned tech-publisher network (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/data/intent-data/).
- Bundles ABM tactics (advertising, web personalization, 1:1 Smart Pages, sales activation) under one platform, removing the need for separate personalization or intent vendors (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/).
- Sales Acceleration module pushes Foundry Intent insights directly into the rep's CRM, Salesloft or Outreach so reps can action "who's in market right now" without manual research (source: https://www.demandgenreport.com/industry-news/foundry-launches-sales-acceleration-tool-on-the-triblio-abm-platform/7779/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Foundry ABM (the Triblio orchestration platform) competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo Marketing — both cover ABM target-audience building, intent-driven advertising, website visitor identification, 1:1 web personalization, and multi-channel ABM plays (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/). Foundry Intent overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Intent Data layer (Streaming Intent), claiming 80M+ signals/week and 7,600+ targeting dimensions sourced from its owned publisher network (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/data/intent-data/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Foundry's **Sales Acceleration** module — surfacing top accounts, best contacts and next-best actions inside the rep's CRM / Salesloft / Outreach — sits adjacent to ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace seller surface, though Foundry's product is narrower (intent insights pushed into existing tools, not a full prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted-outreach front-end) (source: https://www.demandgenreport.com/industry-news/foundry-launches-sales-acceleration-tool-on-the-triblio-abm-platform/7779/).
- **No overlap:** Foundry runs a tech-media and lead-gen advertising business spanning 10+ owned publisher brands (CIO, CSO, InfoWorld, Computerworld, Macworld, PCWorld, Network World, Tech Advisor, TechHive, ChannelWorld), plus content syndication, branded content / BrandHub / BrandPage, partner marketing, events and affiliate marketing — ZoomInfo does not own publisher media or run paid demand-gen content programs (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/). Foundry also has no CRM data quality / routing equivalent to ZoomInfo Operations, no conversation intelligence equivalent to Chorus, and no APIs & MCP / agent-builder lane.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public Foundry-authored vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced in SERP results;_ the G2 head-to-head URL was blocked (HTTP 403). The TrustRadius vs page (https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/foundry-abm-vs-zoominfo-marketingos) carries third-party reviewer commentary: Foundry ABM reviewers cite orchestration setup, account-level customization and third-party API connectivity; ZoomInfo Marketing reviewers cite campaign-creation speed, reporting clarity, and audience-building from WebSights / intent / workflows. One TrustRadius reviewer explicitly stated about Triblio's category: "None of these tools had ANYTHING EVEN CLOSE to Triblio's orchestration capabilities" — when comparing Foundry ABM against Terminus, 6sense, and Metadata (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/foundry-abm-vs-zoominfo-marketingos).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The account-based orchestration platform — together, sales and marketing can predict and grow pipeline through intent and account-based orchestration campaigns, resulting in more deals won." (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/)
- **Recurring proof points:** G2 market-leader badges in Account-Based Orchestration Platforms; 80M+ intent signals/week; 150M+ audience members; 7,600+ targeting dimensions; 365Talents case study (€1.5M influenced pipeline, average 19 days from ad surge to deal creation in 2022); Foundry research claim of 2.5x campaign efficiency from intent-based targeting; logos including Cisco, Apollo, Avaya, Tipalti, Chargebee, Merkle, NextGen.
- **Tone / category framing:** "Account-based orchestration" — Foundry frames the category as orchestration (cross-channel campaign automation) rather than "ABM platform" or "sales intelligence," and pitches a one-stop end-to-end alternative to stitching together personalization + intent + advertising vendors.
