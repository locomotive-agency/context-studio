## Summary

Kaspr is a B2B contact-data tool sold as a LinkedIn Chrome Extension plus web-app workspace, targeted at SDRs/BDRs, individual contributors, recruiters and founders inside SMBs. It directly overlaps ZoomInfo's Data foundation pillar — selling the same product class (verified phone numbers, emails, company data revealed from LinkedIn) but with explicit European bias, public self-serve pricing, and a free-forever tier. Kaspr is owned by Cognism (acquired April 2022) and is positioned as the "cheaper, easier" alternative to ZoomInfo for buyers who do not need GTM Context Graph reasoning, ABM, conversation intelligence, MCP/agent ecosystem, or enterprise APIs. The vs-ZoomInfo page leads with "no data passport needed for Europe" and a 'free forever with unlimited B2B email credits' wedge.

## Product Offerings

### Kaspr LinkedIn Chrome Extension

> **Wiki:** [[kaspr--linkedin-chrome-extension]]

- **What it does:** Browser extension that reveals B2B phone numbers, emails and company info directly from LinkedIn profiles, Sales Navigator and Recruiter Lite.
- **Key features:**
  - Reveals phone numbers and emails directly on LinkedIn profiles, groups, events and people-search results
  - Works on LinkedIn, Sales Navigator and Recruiter Lite
  - Previews data prefix before a credit is spent
  - Database of 500M+ phone numbers and emails (200M+ profiles, 120M+ European B2B contacts) verified against 120 sources
  - Free plan with unlimited B2B email credits + 5 phone credits/month
  - GDPR and CCPA aligned
- **Source URL:** https://www.kaspr.io/linkedin-chrome-extension

### Kaspr Web App / Lead Management Dashboard

> **Wiki:** [[kaspr--web-app]]

- **What it does:** Centralized workspace to save leads, organize them with lists/tags/notes, track activity, and manage team credit usage.
- **Key features:**
  - Lead lists, tags, tasks, notes/comments and waiting list for unfound contacts
  - Shared credits across all users in a workspace
  - Team activity and usage reports (export on Business+)
  - Custom member permissions (Business+)
  - Integrated dialer calls via Aircall and Ringover
  - Workspace auto-detection by email domain
- **Source URL:** https://www.kaspr.io/pricing

### Enrichment Automations (Bulk + CSV Enrichment)

> **Wiki:** [[kaspr--enrichment-automations]]

- **What it does:** Workflows that enrich contacts in bulk from LinkedIn searches, Sales Navigator, LinkedIn Groups, Events or uploaded CSVs of LinkedIn URLs.
- **Key features:**
  - Enrich from LinkedIn search, Sales Navigator search, Groups and Events
  - CSV upload (LinkedIn URLs) for bulk enrichment
  - Up to 500 / 1,000 / 2,500 contacts per launch (Starter / Business / Enterprise)
  - 3 / 10 / Unlimited workflow launches per month by tier
  - Automatic export to CRM or CSV after enrichment
- **Source URL:** https://www.kaspr.io/pricing

### Kaspr API

> **Wiki:** [[kaspr--api]]

- **What it does:** REST API (access on request) to enrich and sync contact data with custom tools, gated to paid plans.
- **Key features:**
  - Limited API access on Starter (20 req/hr, 60 req/day)
  - Full API access on Business (250 req/hr, 500 req/day)
  - Unlimited API on Enterprise
  - Available "upon request" even on paid plans
- **Source URL:** https://www.kaspr.io/pricing

### Integrations layer

- **What it does:** Native push of enriched LinkedIn contact data to CRMs, sales engagement, dialers and Zapier.
- **Key features:**
  - Native CRM integrations: HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho
  - Sales engagement / email: Lemlist, Brevo
  - Dialers: Aircall, Ringover (call from inside the Kaspr web app)
  - Zapier connector for long-tail apps (Starter+)
  - Field mapping in the dashboard for each integration
- **Source URL:** https://www.kaspr.io/why-kaspr/integrations

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** SaaS, recruitment / staffing (Focus Cloud Group, Uptoo), marketing & growth agencies (Markentive, Splashr)
- **Company size called out:** SMB, individual contributors, mid-market (via Business / Organization tier)
- **Persona / role focus:** SDRs / BDRs, sales reps and Heads of Sales Operations, recruiters (LinkedIn Recruiter Lite users), founders / growth hackers
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Find phone numbers and emails on LinkedIn / Sales Navigator while prospecting
  - Enrich LinkedIn searches and Sales Navigator lists in bulk
  - Sync enriched leads into CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce/Pipedrive/Zoho) and dialers (Aircall/Ringover)
  - Switch from Lusha/ZoomInfo/Apollo for European phone-number coverage
  - Find candidates via LinkedIn Recruiter Lite (recruiters)

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Lemlist, Brevo
- **Data / enrichment:** LinkedIn, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, LinkedIn Recruiter Lite (Business+)
- **Other notable integrations:** Aircall (dialer), Ringover (dialer), Zapier (long-tail apps, Starter+), Kaspr API (on request)

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- European data coverage with no extra "data passport" fee — 120M+ European B2B contacts included in all plans, vs. ZoomInfo's separate Data Passport add-on (source: https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo)
- Free forever plan with unlimited B2B email credits, no credit card required (source: https://www.kaspr.io/pricing)
- Public, self-serve pricing in three currencies with month-to-month or annual options and self-managed auto-renewal toggle (source: https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo)
- Data verified against 120 sources at request time, not pre-stored verification (source: https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo)
- LinkedIn surface coverage broader than ZoomInfo's extension: people search, Groups, Events and Recruiter Lite all supported (source: https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo)
- 24/7 chat support included on every tier (including free); ZoomInfo gates support behind paid packages (source: https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Kaspr's 500M+ phone-number/email database (with 120M+ European B2B contacts) competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1)** foundation (500M contacts, 135M+ verified phone numbers, 200M+ verified emails) (source: https://www.kaspr.io/). Kaspr's Chrome Extension + prospecting workspace overlaps **ZoomInfo Sales** and its Chrome extension; the vs-page even acknowledges "Like Kaspr, ZoomInfo also has a Chrome Extension for prospecting on LinkedIn" (source: https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo). Kaspr's Bulk / CSV Enrichment workflows overlap **ZoomInfo Operations** for CRM enrichment, at smaller scale and without data-warehouse destinations (source: https://www.kaspr.io/pricing).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Kaspr API offers contact enrichment endpoints but is rate-limited (60–500 req/day on paid tiers, unlimited on Enterprise) and "available only upon request" — a thinner surface than ZoomInfo's **APIs & MCP** suite (source: https://www.kaspr.io/pricing). Kaspr's Lemlist / Brevo / Aircall / Ringover integrations touch the sales-engagement layer but do not include Outreach or Salesloft, where ZoomInfo has deeper engagement integrations (source: https://www.kaspr.io/why-kaspr/integrations).
- **No overlap:** Kaspr has no equivalent to ZoomInfo's **GTM Context Graph** (no conversation, intent, or behavioral signal capture); no **GTM Workspace** seller surface or **GTM Studio** marketer / RevOps / GTM-engineer surface; no ABM (**ZoomInfo Marketing**), on-site chat (**ZoomInfo Chat**), or conversation intelligence (**Chorus**); no MCP server or AI-agent ecosystem; and no real technographic depth or first-class intent data — "intent data" is listed as an Enterprise-tier feature only, with no detail provided (source: https://www.kaspr.io/pricing, https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "Kaspr does what ZoomInfo promises to do. But way cheaper, easier, and looks nicer." Plus: "unlike ZoomInfo, you don't need a data passport to access European contacts. Plus, our pricing is public." (source: https://www.kaspr.io/kaspr-vs-zoominfo)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Instant access to accurate European contact data" — a LinkedIn Chrome Extension and web app that gives 200M+ profiles' phone numbers and emails "in seconds" to help SMB sellers, recruiters and founders hit quota without enterprise contracts.
- **Recurring proof points:** G2 4.4/5 from 600+ reviews (750+ cited on homepage); 100K users globally; G2 Spring 2025 badges (Lead Capture Momentum Leader, Lead Capture Leader Small-Business, Lead Intelligence High Performer); Visibly HQ "six discovery calls in the first two weeks" after switching from Lusha; Thibaut Souyris case study citing 38% reply rate and 11% meeting booked rate; Cognism ownership.
- **Tone / category framing:** Self-described as a "prospecting tool for SMBs and individual contributors" — explicitly NOT an enterprise GTM platform. Emphasizes simplicity ("no lengthy onboarding"), free-forever entry, and European data parity as the wedge against ZoomInfo.
