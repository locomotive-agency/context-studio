## Summary

Weflow is a Salesforce-native Revenue AI platform sold to RevOps leaders, CROs, sales managers, AEs, CS, and Business Systems teams at B2B tech companies (100–10,000 employees, 10–1,500 Salesforce seats; 10-user minimum). Its modular product set covers Salesforce activity capture, conversation intelligence, pipeline/deal intelligence, forecasting, and an AI workflow/agent builder, all written into native Salesforce objects. The most material ZoomInfo overlap is with Chorus (conversation intelligence), GTM Workspace (seller-facing pipeline + AI execution surface), and ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data hygiene) — but Weflow has no third-party B2B contact, company, intent, or technographic data and explicitly disclaims sales engagement, email enrichment, lead routing, and any non-Salesforce CRM. Pricing is modular and public ($19–$79/user/month), and Weflow's vs-content targets Gong, Clari, People.ai, and Salesforce EAC rather than ZoomInfo.

## Product Offerings

### Activity & Contact Capture

> **Wiki:** [[weflow--activity-capture]]

- **What it does:** Server-side Salesforce activity sync that auto-syncs emails, meetings, and contacts from Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 directly into native Salesforce objects (Task, Event, EmailMessage, Contact). (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq)
- **Key features:**
  - Writes to native Salesforce objects (Task, Event, EmailMessage, Contact) so data is reportable, automatable, and stays if you uninstall
  - Auto-creation of Contact records and Opportunity Contact Role assignment
  - 24-month historical backfill from Google Workspace or Microsoft 365
  - Email open tracking with last-open-date and open-count fields
  - Optional Gmail and Outlook extensions for in-inbox mapping/override
  - $19/user/month; setup in 20–45 minutes
- **Source URL:** https://www.getweflow.com/faq

### Conversation Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[weflow--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls in 96+ languages, then pushes structured insights directly into Salesforce fields. (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq)
- **Key features:**
  - Recording and transcription across Zoom, Teams, and Meet (98-language transcription)
  - AI summaries, coaching insights, and follow-up emails
  - Automatic field updates for MEDDIC, next steps, close dates, competitor mentions
  - Clips, playlists, topics/trackers with retroactive crawl, coaching scorecards (1–5)
  - Unlimited recordings/transcripts at flat rate
  - $39/user/month
- **Source URL:** https://www.getweflow.com/faq

### Pipeline Intelligence / Deal Intelligence & Forecasting

> **Wiki:** [[weflow--pipeline-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Surfaces deal risks, opportunity progression, and pipeline health on top of Salesforce data, plus forecast submission, roll-up, and AI predictions. (source: https://www.getweflow.com/product/deal-intelligence-forecasting)
- **Key features:**
  - 50+ AI deal risk indicators, velocity insights, signals, and buyer-role detection
  - AI deal score (0–100) per deal with health diagnostics
  - Configurable deal warnings/alerts on stalls and missed milestones
  - 30+ pre-built pipeline analytics (Waterfall, Pacing, stage conversions, Deal Flow)
  - Deal-by-deal forecast submissions with automatic role-hierarchy roll-ups
  - AI forecast prediction (bottom-up, dynamic-weighted, AI prediction with seasonality)
  - Opportunity snapshots tracking week-on-week pipeline changes
  - $39/user/month for Pipeline Intelligence
- **Source URL:** https://www.getweflow.com/product/deal-intelligence-forecasting

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS / tech (primary), cybersecurity, healthcare, fintech, professional services
- **Company size called out:** 100–10,000 employees; 10–1,500 Salesforce seats; 30–300 quota-carrying reps; 10-user minimum
- **Persona / role focus:** Account Executives, Customer Success, Sales Managers, Executives / CROs, RevOps, Sales Enablement, Business Systems teams, SDRs (via configurable activity-capture profiles)
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Automate Salesforce activity/data capture
  - Run pipeline reviews with deal signals and AI insights
  - Forecast submission, roll-up, and AI prediction
  - Conversation intelligence + AI-driven Salesforce field updates
  - Deal hygiene and process compliance (MEDDIC / SPICED / Command of the Message)
  - Track pipeline changes / pacing vs. quota

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce only (exclusive — bi-directional, real-time, two-way sync; respects validation rules, field dependencies, permissions, role hierarchy, custom objects, Cases). Explicitly does not support HubSpot, MS Dynamics, or Pipedrive (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** None — FAQ explicitly says "no email sequences, cadences, or outbound automation. You'll still need Outreach, Salesloft, or a similar tool."
- **Data / enrichment:** None — no third-party data, enrichment, intent, or lead routing.
- **Other notable integrations:** Google Workspace (Gmail/Calendar), Microsoft 365 (Outlook/Calendar), Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Gmail and Outlook browser extensions, Managed Salesforce package on AppExchange, Salesforce Platform license support.

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Salesforce-exclusive architecture: writes activity/conversation/forecast data to native Salesforce objects (Task, Event, EmailMessage, Contact) so data stays queryable, reportable, and yours if you cancel — explicitly contrasted with Gong's Revenue Graph and Salesforce Einstein Activity Capture (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).
- Modular pricing: buy Activity Capture, Conversation Intelligence, Pipeline Intelligence, or Forecasting individually with no platform fee (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).
- Deploy in 2 weeks (Activity Capture in 20–45 minutes) with no professional services cost vs. Clari/Gong 8-week+ implementations and $15K–$75K services fees (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq/weflow-vs).
- Conversation intelligence output writes directly into Salesforce fields (MEDDIC, next steps, close dates, competitor mentions) automatically — reps don't manually update Salesforce after the call (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).
- Cost positioning: ~70–80% lower than Gong's $130K–$200K+/yr 50-user deployments; full AI Revenue Intelligence platform at $79/user/month (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq/weflow-vs).
- Methodology-aware AI field templates (MEDDIC, SPICED, Command of the Message) (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus (ZoomInfo conversation intelligence)** vs **Weflow Conversation Intelligence** — both record, transcribe, summarize, and analyze sales calls; Weflow positions explicitly against Gong/Chorus-class CI tools (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).
  - **GTM Workspace (next-gen seller front-end with prioritized accounts + AI-drafted outreach)** vs **Weflow Pipeline Intelligence + Ask Weflow AI + AI Workflows** — both are seller-facing surfaces consolidating deal signals, AI-drafted follow-ups, and pipeline visibility. Weflow's AI Notetaker / Email Composer / Deal Reviewer / Deal Monitor stack mirrors GTM Workspace's seller-workflow scope (source: https://www.getweflow.com/platform-overview).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing)** vs **Weflow Activity Capture + Managed Salesforce Application** — direct CRM-data-hygiene play overlapping ZoomInfo Operations' CRM data-quality pillar, scoped to Salesforce only (source: https://www.getweflow.com/managed-app).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio (RevOps / GTM-engineer surface — orchestration, plays)** vs **Weflow AI Workflows & Agent Builder** — adjacent to GTM Studio's play-orchestration framing, though scoped to Salesforce/Weflow data only with no third-party prospect graph (source: https://www.getweflow.com/platform-overview).
  - **GTM Context Graph (cross-source reasoning over CRM + conversation + behavioral data)** vs **Weflow Deal Intelligence (50+ signals + AI deal score + AI forecast prediction)** — same conceptual move (blending CRM activity + conversation transcript signals + historical pipeline data), but limited to one customer's first-party Salesforce + call data with no cross-account third-party intelligence layer (source: https://www.getweflow.com/product/deal-intelligence-forecasting).
- **No overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ phone numbers, 200M+ emails, technographics)** — Weflow has no third-party B2B contact, company, intent, or technographic data; FAQ explicitly disclaims data enrichment (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM) / Intent / sales engagement (ZoomInfo Chat, sequences, cadences)** — Weflow's FAQ states it has no sales engagement, sequences, ABM, intent data, or chat (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq).
  - **APIs & MCP (Enterprise API + MCP server for agent-builders)** — no public API or MCP listing surfaced on reviewed Weflow pages.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public Weflow-vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Weflow's vs-content targets Gong, Clari, People.ai, and Salesforce Einstein Activity Capture rather than ZoomInfo. The closest framing: "Weflow builds projections from CRM pipeline data, historical win rates, stage conversion patterns, and activity levels... Weflow does not analyze conversation content. Instead, its AI projections rely on pipeline data, historical win rates, stage conversion patterns, and activity levels." (source: https://www.getweflow.com/faq/weflow-vs).
