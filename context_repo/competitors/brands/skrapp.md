## Summary

Skrapp is a B2B email finder and lead-generation tool aimed at SMB / mid-market sellers, recruiters, and marketers who prospect on LinkedIn and Sales Navigator. Its core offering is verified business emails extracted from LinkedIn profiles and company websites, backed by a 200M+ contact / 40M+ company database refreshed daily, with a Chrome extension as the primary surface. Overlap with ZoomInfo is limited to ZoomInfo's data foundation (verified business emails) and a sliver of ZoomInfo Operations (CRM enrichment via list sync) — Skrapp has no GTM Context Graph, no GTM Workspace/Studio equivalent, no phone-number/direct-dial data, no intent or conversation intelligence, and no MCP/agent ecosystem. Its explicit positioning against ZoomInfo is price-led (transparent self-serve plans vs. quote-based enterprise contracts) plus AI enrichment included by default.

## Product Offerings

### Email Finder

> **Wiki:** [[skrapp--email-finder]]

- **What it does:** Finds verified professional email addresses by name and company using web crawling, pattern recognition, and real-time email verification.
- **Key features:**
  - Combines web crawling, pattern recognition, and real-time verification
  - Returns email plus verification status (Valid / Catch-all)
  - 92% average email search success rate
  - 97% email verification accuracy
  - Database refreshed daily
  - 200M+ B2B contacts and 40M+ company profiles
- **Source URL:** https://skrapp.io/

### LinkedIn Email Finder (Chrome extension)

> **Wiki:** [[skrapp--linkedin-email-finder]]

- **What it does:** Chrome extension that reveals verified business emails from any LinkedIn profile, Sales Navigator search, or LinkedIn Recruiter result.
- **Key features:**
  - Verified against company mail server with 97% verification accuracy
  - AI-enriched role, seniority, and function data on every lead
  - Works on 1st-, 2nd-, and 3rd-degree LinkedIn profiles
  - Bulk extraction up to 1,000 profiles per minute on People Search / saved lists
  - Auto-saves enriched leads back to Sales Navigator lists
  - Compatible with Sales Navigator Core, Advanced, and Advanced Plus
  - Export results to CSV in one click
  - 4.8/5 on Chrome Web Store with 200K+ users
- **Source URL:** https://skrapp.io/linkedin

### Email Extractor (Company website Chrome extension)

- **What it does:** Chrome extension that extracts verified company email addresses, employee contact details, company size, and industry data directly from any company website.
- **Key features:**
  - One-click company email extraction from websites
  - Pulls employee roles and contact details alongside emails
  - Surfaces company size and industry data in-line
  - Capterra 4.5/5, Chrome 4.8/5
- **Source URL:** https://skrapp.io/extension

### Lead Finder / B2B Lead Database

> **Wiki:** [[skrapp--lead-finder]]

- **What it does:** Searchable B2B lead database surfacing 200M+ business profiles for list-building.
- **Key features:**
  - 200M+ business profiles refreshed daily
  - Filter by job title, location, company, industry
  - AI search across the lead database
  - Targeted list export to CSV
- **Source URL:** https://skrapp.io/compare/zoominfo-alternative

### Data Enrichment / Company Search

> **Wiki:** [[skrapp--data-enrichment]]

- **What it does:** Enriches uploaded contact lists with verified emails, firmographic data (industry, size, location, revenue), and individual profile data (job title, seniority, department).
- **Key features:**

  - Firmographic enrichment (industry, size, location, revenue)
  - Profile enrichment (title, seniority, department)
  - AI Fields (buying role, seniority, gender) included by default
- **Source URL:** https://skrapp.io/compare/zoominfo-alternative

### Email Verifier

> **Wiki:** [[skrapp--email-verifier]]

- **Source URL:** https://skrapp.io/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Skrapp competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1)** foundation — specifically the verified-business-email portion. Skrapp claims 200M+ B2B contacts, 40M+ company profiles, and 97% verification accuracy with daily refresh, and pitches itself as the lower-cost source of the same primitive (source: https://skrapp.io/, https://skrapp.io/compare/zoominfo-alternative). The Email Verifier directly competes with ZoomInfo's first-party verification claims.
- **Adjacent overlap:** The LinkedIn Chrome extension + Lead Finder is a lightweight seller surface adjacent to ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace / ZoomInfo Sales prospecting UI but materially narrower (source: https://skrapp.io/linkedin). Bulk CSV enrichment + List CRM Sync to Salesforce / HubSpot / Pipedrive / Zoho is adjacent to ZoomInfo Operations' CRM data-quality use case, but lacks routing, dedup, and governance (source: https://skrapp.io/compare/zoominfo-alternative). The Enterprise REST API is adjacent to ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP surface (source: https://skrapp.io/compare/zoominfo-alternative).
- **No overlap:** No GTM Context Graph (no first-party + conversation + intent + behavioral data fusion), no GTM Studio (no ABM, audience orchestration, marketing automation, RevOps tooling), no conversation intelligence (no Chorus equivalent), no phone numbers or direct dials (a core ZoomInfo data asset Skrapp explicitly does not sell), no MCP server / agent ecosystem, and no technographics across 30K+ technologies (source: https://skrapp.io/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "ZoomInfo locks verified data behind enterprise contracts and complex pricing. Skrapp.io offers transparent plans where every credit counts; no quotes needed. … ZoomInfo's enrichment features come as costly add-ons. Skrapp.io includes AI Fields by default… ZoomInfo is built for large GTM teams; Skrapp.io is designed for speed." (source: https://skrapp.io/compare/zoominfo-alternative). The same page claims a price-per-credit of $0.037 on Skrapp Pro vs. ~$3.00 on ZoomInfo Basic 5K and frames Skrapp as similar lead coverage at "a tiny fraction of price."

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Find and enrich leads. Grow revenue." — get verified business emails, qualified leads, and enriched data at scale, free to start (source: https://skrapp.io/).
- **Recurring proof points:** Trusted by 2M+ sales professionals; trust logos for Adobe, IBM, SAP, Oracle, Microsoft, Social Bloom, Goodera, Dashlane, Boston Wells; 3B+ searches processed; 97% verification accuracy / 92% search success rate; 200M+ B2B leads and 40M+ company profiles refreshed daily; 4.7 average across 900+ reviews on G2, Capterra, and Chrome (4.8/5 on Chrome with 500+ reviews; 200K+ extension users) (source: https://skrapp.io/, https://skrapp.io/extension).
