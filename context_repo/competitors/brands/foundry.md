# Foundry

## Sub-products

- [[foundry--abm|Foundry ABM (Triblio)]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing

## Summary

Foundry is a B2B tech media + intent data + ABM orchestration platform (the rebrand of IDG Communications) selling primarily to B2B technology CMOs, demand-gen, and ABM marketers who target IT decision makers. Its stack pairs an owned editorial network (CIO, CSO, Computerworld, InfoWorld, Network World, etc.) with multi-source intent data (Foundry Intent), an account-based orchestration platform (Foundry ABM, powered by Triblio), and intent-driven advertising (Foundry Ads). The most material overlap with ZoomInfo is on the ABM and intent-data layers — Foundry ABM competes directly with ZoomInfo Marketing, and Foundry Intent competes with ZoomInfo's intent / engagement data layer — while Foundry has no equivalent to ZoomInfo's 500M-contact prospecting DB, GTM Context Graph reasoning, GTM Workspace seller surface, GTM Studio, Chorus, or APIs & MCP for AI agents.

## Product Offerings

### Foundry Intent (intent data)

> **Wiki:** [[foundry--intent]]

- **What it does:** Multi-source B2B intent data product that captures buying signals from a customer's website, the public web, Foundry's owned editorial network, and content syndication, then matches signals to buyers and buying committees for activation.
- **Key features:**
  - Captures intent across customer website, public web, industry content, and Foundry's opted-in audience
  - 80M+ intent signals captured per week
  - 150M+ audience members reachable
  - 7,600+ targeting dimensions (intent, persona, firmographic)
  - Matches signals to buying-committee members for personalized activation
  - Integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics 365, SugarCRM, Google, Adobe
- **Source URL:** https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/data/intent-data/

### Foundry ABM (account-based orchestration platform, powered by Triblio)

> **Wiki:** [[foundry--abm]]

- **What it does:** Account-based marketing orchestration platform that runs cross-channel campaigns (display + social ads, website personalization, 1:1 Smart Pages, sales activation) tied to Foundry intent and CRM signals.
- **Key features:**
  - Cross-channel orchestration across display, social, website personalization, and 1:1 Smart Pages
  - AI-driven account scoring fed by proprietary, human-verified intent data
  - Sales Acceleration module surfaces best contacts and topics inside the rep's CRM
  - Intent-triggered automated buyer journeys
  - Integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, SugarCRM, Marketo (Triblio integrations)
  - G2 leader badges in Account-Based Orchestration Platforms category
- **Source URL:** https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/

### Foundry Ads (intent-driven B2B advertising)

> **Wiki:** [[foundry--ads]]

- **What it does:** Display, native, video, social, and OTT ad programs run against Foundry's owned editorial sites and vetted external inventory, targeted using Foundry intent and 7,600+ targeting dimensions.
- **Key features:**
  - Access to 150M+ audience members and 300M+ global records in the Foundry ecosystem
  - Ad placements adjacent to CIO, CSO, Computerworld, InfoWorld, Network World and other owned sites
  - Pricing on cost-per-click (CPC) or cost-per-viewable-impressions (vCPM)
  - Formats: display banners, native, search, video, social, OTT
  - Outcomes: awareness, engagement, full-funnel activation tied to ABM lists
  - Frequency capping (typical: 3 impressions per person per 8 hours)
- **Source URL:** https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/advertising/

### Editorial brand network (CIO, CSO, Computerworld, InfoWorld, Network World, Macworld, PCWorld, TechHive, Tech Advisor, ChannelWorld + specialty brands)

> **Wiki:** [[foundry--editorial-network]]

- **What it does:** Owned-and-operated tech publishing network that delivers the audience and content adjacency layer underneath Foundry Intent, Foundry Ads, and lead-gen programs; also runs CIO 100, CSO50, and 800+ industry events annually.
- **Key features:**
  - 28M+ monthly visitors across editorial properties
  - 800+ industry-leading events per year
  - CIO reaches 4M+ chief information officers / IT thought leaders across 72 countries
  - Computerworld reaches 12M enterprise IT managers across 47 countries
  - Award programs: CIO 100 and CSO50
  - Foundry stat: "96% of visitors take action toward purchasing after engaging with content"
- **Source URL:** https://foundryco.com/our-brands/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Owned editorial network of 10+ trusted IT-buyer brands (CIO, CSO, Computerworld, InfoWorld, Network World, Macworld, PCWorld, TechHive, Tech Advisor, ChannelWorld) producing 28M+ monthly visitors — a content-and-audience moat no pure data vendor can replicate (source: https://foundryco.com/our-brands/).
- Multi-source intent that combines first-party website signals, Foundry's owned editorial engagement, public-web conversational signals across 5M+ companies, and content-syndication / event behavior — 80M+ intent signals/week (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/data/intent-data/).
- Self-claimed "only ABM platform with proprietary, human-verified intent data and AI scoring that takes away the guesswork of prospecting" (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/).
- Closed-loop bundle: Foundry Intent feeds Foundry Ads (placed alongside owned editorial inventory) and Foundry ABM orchestration in one stack — vs ABM-only vendors that require separate intent and media buys (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/advertising/).
- Built through 4 acquisitions — Triblio (ABM), KickFire (first-party intent), LeadSift (third-party intent), Selling Simplified (B2B leads) — layered on the IDG editorial backbone (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/).
- Rich proprietary research backbone (State of the CIO, AI Priorities, Customer Engagement Study, Role & Influence) used as both buyer-research credibility and as a content-marketing wedge for tech vendors (source: https://foundryco.com/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM) ↔ Foundry ABM (Triblio).** Foundry ABM orchestrates cross-channel display + social ads, website personalization, 1:1 Smart Pages, and intent-triggered automated journeys with CRM integrations to Salesforce, HubSpot, and Marketo — directly competing with ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM orchestration capabilities (the Gartner MQ Leader category ZoomInfo plays in). (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/)
  - **Intent Data (intent / engagement layer) ↔ Foundry Intent.** Foundry Intent claims multi-source intent (first-party + public web + owned editorial + syndication) with 80M+ signals/week and 7,600+ targeting dimensions — competing directly with ZoomInfo's intent and engagement signals layer feeding the GTM Context Graph. ZoomInfo holds Forrester Wave Leader for Intent Data Providers (Q1 2025); Foundry holds a parallel G2 leader position for Buyer Intent Data Providers. (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/data/intent-data/)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace ↔ Foundry Sales Acceleration.** Foundry's Sales Acceleration module surfaces best contacts and topics inside the rep's CRM — adjacent to ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace, but Foundry's lens is intent-derived account prioritization for ABM-aligned reps, not full prospecting / contact discovery against a 500M-contact database. (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/)
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing — demand-gen / digital advertising activation ↔ Foundry Ads.** Foundry runs paid media (display, native, video, social, OTT) directly on owned editorial inventory — ZoomInfo activates audiences into ad networks but does not own publisher inventory, so the overlap is on intent-targeted advertising activation rather than ad-tech parity. (source: https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/advertising/)
- **No overlap:**
  - Foundry-only: owned B2B editorial network (CIO, CSO, Computerworld, InfoWorld, Network World, Macworld, PCWorld, etc.) + 800+ events/year incl. CIO 100 / CSO50 awards programs (source: https://foundryco.com/our-brands/).
  - Foundry-only: content-marketing / brand-experience services (BrandHub, BrandPage, content creation, partner marketing, affiliate marketing) (source: https://foundryco.com/).
  - ZoomInfo-only: GTM Context Graph reasoning layer + GTM Workspace + GTM Studio + APIs & MCP server for AI agents (source: https://foundryco.com/ — no equivalents found in any reviewed Foundry page).
  - ZoomInfo-only: conversation intelligence (Chorus) and CRM data quality / routing / dedupe (ZoomInfo Operations) — Foundry has no parallel products in any reviewed page.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Stories inspire. Data connects." Foundry helps tech marketers connect with and address the full buying team using trusted media, intent data, and content that drives engagement, decisions, and conversions.
- **Recurring proof points:** 28M+ monthly visitors across editorial properties; 800+ industry-leading events/year; 150M+ audience members and 7,600+ targeting dimensions; 80M+ intent signals captured per week; OpenText case study ($2M+ pipeline in 6 months from brand-to-demand program); SugarCRM case study ($9.9M influenced pipeline using Foundry ABM + Bombora + G2 intent).
- **Tone / category framing:** Buyer-aligned, end-to-end tech-marketing platform — combining trusted publisher network, intent data, and ABM orchestration — explicitly NOT a sales-prospecting database. SERP analyst commentary (amediaoperator) frames Foundry's evolution as a TechTarget-style media-meets-martech transformation.
