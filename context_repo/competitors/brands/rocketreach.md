# RocketReach

## Summary

RocketReach is a self-serve "lead intelligence" platform — verified B2B emails, mobile/direct phones, and firmographics across 700M+ profiles — bundled with native outreach (Messages, Sequences, AI Email Writer), automated list refresh (Autopilot), and a Contact Data API. Primary buyers are SDR/AE sales teams, recruiters and executive-search firms, and developers embedding contact data via API; pricing is publicly listed from $25–$128/seat/mo, with custom plans starting at $6K/year. The most material crossover with ZoomInfo is direct on the Data foundation pillar (RocketReach claims 700M contacts vs ZoomInfo's 260M on its own vs-page) and on the Enterprise API; secondary overlap is on intent (Intentsify-powered, 35K+ topics) and on the Workspace-style outreach loop, while RocketReach has no analog to GTM Context Graph, Chorus, GTM Studio, or MCP — and adds a recruiter-focused product suite (Bullhorn ATS integration, NPI healthcare API) that ZoomInfo does not target.

## Product Offerings

### Global Contact Database

- **What it does:** Continuously updated, AI-matched contact database with verified emails and phone numbers across 700M+ professionals and 35–60M companies.
- **Key features:**
  - 700M+ verified professional profiles
  - 60M companies (homepage) / 35M companies (pricing page)
  - 989M emails (homepage stat)
  - 90–98% deliverability on verified emails
  - 98% accuracy claim on A-grade emails (real-time verification)
  - Real-time email prediction + verification on every lookup
  - Aggregates licensed datasets, public info, human-verified data, a data-sharing cooperative, and proprietary email algorithms
  - Unique coverage in healthcare, legal, founders, and technology
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/contact-database

### Create Lead Lists / Prospect Lists

- **What it does:** Bulk list-building and CSV/social-link enrichment so users can generate or complete prospect lists without per-contact lookups.
- **Key features:**
  - Upload a CSV and AI parses columns to enrich
  - Build prospect lists from social links (Twitter, Facebook, etc.)
  - List Generation tool for filtered lookups
  - Competitive bulk rates available on request
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/create-lead-lists

### B2B Intent Data (RocketReach + Intentsify partnership)

- **What it does:** Account-level intent signals — surfaces companies actively researching solutions in your category, paired with RocketReach contact data in one workflow.
- **Key features:**
  - Powered by partnership with Intentsify ("RocketReach Foundry")
  - 35,000+ intent topics, refreshed weekly
  - Intent + 700M verified contacts in one platform
  - Sub-flows for Sales, Demand Gen, ABM, RevOps
  - Pricing starts at $175/mo for the intent + contacts bundle
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/b2b-intent-data

### RocketReach Autopilot

- **What it does:** Automated workflows that build and refresh prospect lists on a daily/weekly/monthly cadence to keep pipeline fresh without manual searches.
- **Key features:**
  - Active autopilots: 5 (Essentials), 10 (Pro), 25 (Ultimate)
  - Daily, weekly, or monthly refresh cadences
  - Discover-and-engage workflows in a few clicks
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/

### Messages (with Sequences and AI Email Writer)

- **What it does:** Native email outreach: send customized emails, automate follow-ups, run sequences, and write copy with an AI email writer — all without leaving RocketReach.
- **Key features:**
  - Send email directly from RocketReach (500/day across all plans)
  - Sequences for multi-step outreach
  - AI Email Writer for drafting copy
  - Real-time engagement tracking, tags, and notes
  - Suppression lists
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/

### Contact Data API

- **What it does:** REST API exposing the same contact + company data that powers the search UI for enrichment and embedding into customer applications/CRMs.
- **Key features:**
  - 4.5B records cited as the addressable corpus on the homepage and API page
  - People Data API and Company Data API endpoints
  - Email Finder API, Firmographic API, NPI API for healthcare
  - Same continuously verified database as the search platform
  - Pricing: included on standard plans; custom packages start at $6K annually
  - "Get running in less than an hour"
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/api

### Browser Extension (Chrome)

- **What it does:** Chrome extension that surfaces verified emails, direct dials, and firmographic data directly from social networking and company sites while browsing.
- **Key features:**
  - 300K+ Chrome Store plugin users (homepage)
  - Real-time contact + company lookup as you browse
  - Available on every paid plan
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/

### Integrations (CRM / sales engagement / ATS)

- **What it does:** Native integrations to push verified contacts and sync data with sales, marketing, and recruiting tools.
- **Key features:**
  - Salesforce (with custom field mapping on Ultimate)
  - HubSpot
  - Outreach
  - Salesloft
  - Bullhorn (recruiting/staffing ATS)
  - Zapier (custom workflows)
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/integrations/

### RocketReach Verify (Beta)

- **What it does:** Standalone email-verification tool, in Beta, that lets users verify emails already in their CRM using the same engine RocketReach uses internally (180M emails verified monthly).
- **Key features:**
  - Currently in Beta / Early Access
  - Verifies emails across the 700M+ profile dataset
  - Pitched as "purpose-built for speed, scale, and precision"
- **Source URL:** https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Public, self-serve pricing from $25/mo with no sales call required — explicitly contrasted with ZoomInfo's gated/expensive pricing in customer testimonials (source: https://rocketreach.co/pricing).
- Claims "3x more contacts" than ZoomInfo with 700M contact records vs ZoomInfo 260M on their own vs-page (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/comparison/rocketreach-vs-zoominfo/).
- Higher G2 user ratings on contact data accuracy (80% vs ZoomInfo 77%), ease of use (90 vs 88), ease of setup (92 vs 88), and ease of admin (95 vs 87) per RocketReach's own framing (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/comparison/rocketreach-vs-zoominfo/).
- Niche depth in healthcare (NPI API + Healthcare Data tier), legal, founders, and technology personas (source: https://rocketreach.co/).
- Bundled outreach: native Messages + Sequences + AI Email Writer + Autopilot at standard pricing — positions RocketReach as a one-stop prospect-to-send tool (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/).
- Intent Data + contact data combined in one tool starting at $175/mo, contrasted with "Intent data alone costs $10K+/year" from standalone vendors (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/b2b-intent-data).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation:** RocketReach Global Contact Database (700M profiles / 60M companies / 989M emails / 98% A-grade email accuracy) competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Data foundation pillar (500M contacts / 100M companies / 200M+ verified emails / 135M+ phones). Same buyers, same deliverable; RocketReach explicitly claims 3x ZoomInfo's contact volume on its vs-page (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/comparison/rocketreach-vs-zoominfo/, https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/contact-database).
  - **Intent / ABM data:** RocketReach's Intentsify-powered B2B Intent Data (35K+ topics, weekly refresh) covers the same prospecting use case as ZoomInfo's intent module — and the page explicitly contrasts itself with "standalone intent data" vendors at "$10K+/year," a category that includes ZoomInfo's intent product historically (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/b2b-intent-data).
  - **Enterprise API:** RocketReach's Contact Data API exposes verified contact + firmographic data via REST (People, Company, Email Finder, Firmographic, NPI endpoints) — the same wedge as ZoomInfo's Enterprise Data API, with developer-friendly inclusion on standard plans plus $6K/yr custom packages (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/api).
  - **Seller front-end (vs ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace):** RocketReach has built native send-engagement on top of its data — Messages, Sequences, AI Email Writer, Autopilot for automated list refresh — mirroring the prospecting + outreach loop GTM Workspace targets, though without conversation intelligence or the GTM Context Graph reasoning layer (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **CRM data quality (vs ZoomInfo Operations):** Salesforce custom mapping on Ultimate plus the RocketReach Verify Beta touch the CRM-hygiene play that ZoomInfo Operations owns — but at narrower scope (no routing, no enrichment orchestration) (source: https://rocketreach.co/pricing, https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/).
  - **ABM-light orchestration (vs ZoomInfo Marketing):** The Intent Data page's "For Account-Based Marketing — Engage the Right Accounts" workflow touches ZoomInfo's ABM space without offering ABM-platform features like ad targeting, journey orchestration, or program reporting (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/b2b-intent-data).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph / Chorus / GTM Studio / MCP:** No surfaced product covers conversation intelligence, first-party CRM-context fusion, natural-language audience builders, an MCP server, or GTM-engineer-facing orchestration. RocketReach's pitch is a unified surface for prospecting + outreach, not a reasoning layer over the customer's first-party data (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/).
  - **Healthcare NPI / credentialing data (competitor-only):** RocketReach sells a dedicated healthcare-provider data product (NPI numbers, specialty, location for billing/credentialing) as both an API endpoint and a feature gated to Ultimate. ZoomInfo's reference docs do not surface an equivalent NPI/credentialing-grade product (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/api).
  - **Recruiting / staffing ATS workflow (competitor-only):** Native Bullhorn integration positions RocketReach for the staffing / executive-search ICP (Ridgeway Partners, Integris, Yeshiva University cited as customers). ZoomInfo's primary ICP framing is GTM (Sales/Marketing/RevOps), not staffing/recruiting workflows (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/products/integrations/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** RocketReach publishes a first-party comparison page titled "Hire or Close Valuable Prospects with RocketReach, Not ZoomInfo." Headline pitches: "3x more contacts and over 650 million technographic details, find almost anyone around the globe"; "Users consistently rate RocketReach's contact data accuracy higher–80% vs 77%, according to G2"; and "Unlike our competitors, RocketReach has flexible subscription plans for both small and large businesses." Customer testimonials lean on price-to-value framing — "I don't need the expansive power of ZoomInfo (nor the big price point) and RocketReach served my needs perfectly" (source: https://rocketreach.co/resources/comparison/rocketreach-vs-zoominfo/).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Verified Contact Data. Real Results. Reach the right decision-makers. Close deals faster. Hire top talent — all with RocketReach, the most intuitive lead intelligence platform" (source: https://rocketreach.co/).
- **Recurring proof points:** 700M profiles / 60M companies / 989M emails (homepage); trusted by 95% of the S&P 500; 30M+ users (varies by page — 26M on homepage); 98% accuracy on A-grade emails; G2 Leader Spring 2026 + Best Estimated ROI Mid-Market + Easiest Admin Mid-Market + Most Implementable; Inc 500 Fastest Growing Private Companies 2026 / Inc Best Workplace 2025; 300K+ Chrome extension users; named customers Google, Morgan Stanley, NBC Universal, Intel, Adobe, Visa, Bombardier, Ticketmaster, Goldman Sachs, KPMG, Coulter Partners (source: https://rocketreach.co/, https://rocketreach.co/pricing).

The recurring counter-positioning theme is accurate, transparent-priced, easy-to-adopt versus expansive but expensive and gated.
