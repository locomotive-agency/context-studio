# Salesforce Agentforce

## Summary

Agentforce is Salesforce's enterprise agentic AI platform, sold to existing Salesforce CRM customers across sales, service, marketing, commerce, IT, and industries verticals, with a free Salesforce Foundations entry tier and three production buying models (Flex Credits, Conversations, per-user). For ZoomInfo, the head-to-head exposure is concentrated in the Agentforce SDR Agent + Prospecting Agent + Sales Coach stack, which Salesforce explicitly positions as the #1 ZoomInfo alternative on `/compare/zoominfo-competitors/` even though ZoomInfo simultaneously ships a Revenue Agent for Agentforce on Salesforce AppExchange. Agentforce competes on agentic execution, MCP/A2A ecosystem, and CRM-native context — not on third-party B2B contact data, where Salesforce still partners with ZoomInfo. Sibling product to `salesforce-sales-cloud` (Sales Cloud parent), `salesforce-einstein` (predictive AI layer), and `salesforce-marketing-cloud` (marketing automation).

## Product Offerings

### Agentforce (core platform)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-agentforce--agentforce-core]]

- **What it does:** Build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents at scale on the Salesforce platform; agents work across customers, suppliers, and employees 24/7 by reasoning over CRM data, Data 360, and connected systems.
- **Key features:**
  - Atlas Reasoning Engine — breaks down prompts into sub-tasks, plans actions, and executes autonomously
  - Agentforce Builder — unified drafting, testing, and deployment with low-code canvas, document-like editor, and pro-code script view
  - Agent Script — pairs deterministic workflows with LLM reasoning for hybrid reasoning agents
  - Agentforce Voice — AI-powered voice agents across phone, web, and mobile
  - Intelligent Context — extracts and structures information from unstructured/multimodal sources for grounding
  - Einstein Trust Layer — dynamic grounding, zero data retention, toxicity detection, and guardrails
  - AgentExchange marketplace — pre-built agents, connectors, and templates from Salesforce partners
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/

### Pre-built / out-of-the-box agents

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-agentforce--prebuilt-agents]]

- **What it does:** Salesforce ships role-specific Agentforce agents that customers can deploy across Sales, Service, Marketing, Commerce, and IT.
- **Key features:**
  - SDR Agent — engages prospects 24/7, answers product questions, handles objections, and books meetings using CRM and external data
  - Sales Coach — runs personalized role-play sessions for sellers using Salesforce data and generative AI
  - Service Agent — replaces traditional chatbots; resolves service issues without preprogrammed scenarios
  - Merchandiser — assists ecommerce merchandisers with site setup, promotions, product descriptions, insights
  - Buyer Agent / Personal Shopper — B2B buying assistance and ecommerce concierge
  - Campaign Optimizer / Campaign Agent / Journey Agent — automates the full marketing campaign lifecycle
  - Prospecting Agent — autonomously enriches leads and delivers prioritized account lists to sellers
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/

### Agentforce 360 Platform (formerly Salesforce Platform)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-agentforce--agentforce-360-platform]]

- **What it does:** Underlying platform that powers Agentforce — combines metadata, governance, MuleSoft (integration), Data 360 (data), and Einstein Trust Layer (security) on a single foundation.
- **Key features:**
  - MuleSoft Agent Fabric — agent-to-agent (A2A) and system-to-agent communication with governed access to enterprise data and APIs
  - Flow Orchestration — low-code multi-step automation across apps, agents, and systems
  - Open standards: MCP (Model Context Protocol), A2A, REST APIs
  - 200+ pre-built templates, topics, and actions
  - Data 360 — unified customer profile across Sales, Service, Marketing, Commerce, Industries
  - Tableau embedded analytics across Customer 360 apps
  - Apex code, Prompt Builder, low-code Agent Builder for customization
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/platform/agentforce-platform/

### Agentforce Sales (vs ZoomInfo positioning)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-agentforce--agentforce-sales]]

- **What it does:** Salesforce's sales-intelligence-and-prospecting variant of Agentforce, positioned in `/compare/zoominfo-competitors/` as the #1 ZoomInfo alternative; combines Sales Cloud with Data 360 and Prospecting Agent.
- **Key features:**
  - Data sourcing via Data 360 — unifies internal and external data into single profile per prospect
  - Predictive lead scoring flags high-intent deals with highest close probability
  - AI Prospecting Agent autonomously enriches leads and delivers prioritized account lists 24/7
  - Out-of-the-box dashboards for pipeline coverage, win rates, deal size
  - Real-time pipeline management — flags stalled deals, accelerates close, identifies future opportunities
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/compare/zoominfo-competitors/

  - Coaching and teaching (sales role-play)
  - Product recommendations / personal shopping
  - Appointment scheduling
  - Marketing campaign creation, optimization, journey design

- **Data / enrichment:** Data 360 (Salesforce's native data platform — formerly Data Cloud); Zero Copy Federation for partner data layers; ZoomInfo (per LinkedIn / AppExchange — see Flags)

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Native to Salesforce CRM — Agentforce is positioned as the only agentic platform unified with Customer 360 metadata, Data 360, and Einstein Trust Layer on one platform (source: https://www.salesforce.com/platform/agentforce-platform/).
- Hybrid reasoning via Agent Script — pairs deterministic workflows with LLM reasoning so "required business logic always runs in sequence" while LLM handles nuance (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).
- Atlas Reasoning Engine — proprietary reasoning engine that breaks down prompts, plans actions, and executes autonomously (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).
- Three flexible buying models (Flex Credits / Conversations / per-user) plus a free Salesforce Foundations entry tier and free Agentforce Developer Edition (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/pricing/).
- AgentExchange marketplace and MuleSoft Agent Fabric for A2A and MCP-based extensibility — open standards-based ecosystem (source: https://www.salesforce.com/platform/agentforce-platform/).
- G2 4.5-star rating; recognized "#1 platform in Agentic AI" per Salesforce homepage (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace (AI agent layer) ↔ Agentforce SDR Agent + Prospecting Agent + Sales Coach.** Agentforce SDR Agent "engages with prospects 24/7, answering questions, managing objections, and scheduling meetings based on CRM and external data." Prospecting Agent "autonomously enriches leads and delivers qualified prospects straight to your team 24/7." This is head-to-head with the AI-agent layer inside ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).
  - **APIs & MCP / universal access pillar ↔ Agentforce MCP server + AgentExchange + MuleSoft Agent Fabric.** Salesforce ships its own MCP server: "Connect your AI agents to external data and tools with Salesforce's Agentforce Model Content Protocol (MCP) servers." MuleSoft Agent Fabric enables "agent-to-agent (A2A) and system-to-agent communication." Both vendors compete on the agent-builder ecosystem and open-standards extensibility (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing ↔ Agentforce Campaign Agent + Journey Agent + Campaign Optimizer.** Salesforce ships pre-built marketing agents that "automate the full campaign lifecycle, using AI to analyze, generate, personalize, and optimize marketing campaigns based on business goals" — direct overlap with ZoomInfo Marketing's audience building and play orchestration (source: https://www.salesforce.com/platform/applications).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) ↔ Agentforce Voice + Sales Coach.** Agentforce Voice delivers "natural, conversational experiences ... with transcription accuracy"; Sales Coach "provides personalized role-play sessions ... using Salesforce data and generative AI." Lighter conversation-intelligence overlap than Gong-class but moves Salesforce into voice + coaching workflows ZoomInfo serves with Chorus (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Agentforce Sales positioned as ZoomInfo alternative.** Salesforce's own `/compare/zoominfo-competitors/` page lists Agentforce Sales as the #1 ZoomInfo alternative, even though Salesforce simultaneously is the #1 CRM ZoomInfo integrates with (source: https://www.salesforce.com/compare/zoominfo-competitors/).
  - **Data layer positioning.** Data 360 unifies first-party data plus partner data and is described as "a single source of truth" — adjacent to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph framing — but Data 360 does not ship Salesforce-owned third-party B2B contact data at ZoomInfo's scale (source: https://www.salesforce.com/platform/applications).
- **No overlap:**
  - Third-party B2B contact + company data foundation at ZoomInfo's scale (Salesforce relies on partner data, including ZoomInfo, for verified contacts/intent — Salesforce does not sell a 500M-contact / 100M-company database).
  - Service Cloud / IT helpdesk / commerce / field service / healthcare / banking-specific autonomous agents (transaction disputes, claims, patient data management, retail merchandising, IT troubleshooting) — domains ZoomInfo's GTM platform does not enter.
  - Native CRM and full Customer 360 application suite (Service, Marketing, Commerce Clouds) — Salesforce sells the underlying CRM, helpdesk, marketing automation, and commerce platform; ZoomInfo is intelligence layered on top of CRMs, not a CRM itself.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "Sales teams often choose Agentforce Sales for its AI-powered capabilities, powerful Prospecting Agent, and predictive insights. ... While many organizations trust ZoomInfo, its price can often limit adoption. Some ZoomInfo competitors offer similar features at lower and more flexible price points." (source: https://www.salesforce.com/compare/zoominfo-competitors/)

Build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents 24/7 across customers, suppliers, and employees on a single trusted platform.
