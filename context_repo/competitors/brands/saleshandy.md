## Summary

Saleshandy is an all-in-one cold email outreach platform sold to SDRs, lead-gen agencies, founders, and recruitment agencies at SMB/agency scale. It bundles a B2B Lead Finder (852M+ contacts / 42M+ companies) with multichannel sequences, an email verifier, a dialer, deliverability tooling, and a built-in CRM — explicitly positioned as a consolidation play against teams stitching ZoomInfo + Outreach/Salesloft + a verifier together. Versus ZoomInfo, the meaningful overlap is narrow: Lead Finder competes with ZoomInfo's Pillar 1 data layer, and the Outreach engine touches the engagement surface that ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace addresses. There is no overlap with the GTM Context Graph, GTM Studio (ABM/marketing), Chorus-style conversation intelligence, intent data, website visitor tracking, or TalentOS.

## Product Offerings

### Outreach (Cold Email Sequences)

> **Wiki:** [[saleshandy--outreach]]

- **What it does:** Multi-step, multichannel cold email outreach engine with AI sequence generation, conditional follow-ups, sender rotation across unlimited email accounts, and a unified inbox.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Copilot generates a full multi-step sequence from an offer + audience prompt
  - Multichannel sequences across email, phone, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, and manual tasks
  - Conditional branching / subsequences based on opens, clicks, replies
  - Merge tags, variable tags, and Spintax for at-scale personalization
  - Sender rotation across unlimited connected email accounts to protect deliverability
  - Unified inbox with AI reply categorization (interested / not interested / meeting booked / OOO)
- **Source URL:** https://www.saleshandy.com/outreach

### Lead Finder (B2B Database)

> **Wiki:** [[saleshandy--lead-finder]]

- **What it does:** B2B contact and company database advertised as 852M+ verified contacts across 42M+ companies, with AI-search and waterfall enrichment across 9 data providers.
- **Key features:**
  - 852M+ contacts and 42M+ companies (homepage and Lead Finder page)
  - 75+ search filters plus AI Search natural-language prompts
  - Real-time waterfall enrichment across 9 premium data providers
  - CSV upload + enrichment with verified emails and phone numbers
  - "Net New" tab to filter out previously-pulled contacts and avoid wasted credits
  - Direct push of enriched leads into email sequences, LinkedIn, or dialer campaigns
  - REST API endpoints for programmatic enrichment (`/v1/enrich/company`, `/v1/enrich/contact`)
- **Source URL:** https://www.saleshandy.com/lead-finder

### Email Verifier

> **Wiki:** [[saleshandy--email-verifier]]

- **What it does:** Free email-list verifier that runs syntax, domain, MX, catch-all, and SMTP checks; advertised at 98%+ accuracy.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-layer verification: syntax, domain status, MX records, catch-all, live SMTP
  - Bulk CSV upload or single-email check
  - Results labeled Valid / Risky / Invalid
  - 98%+ accuracy claim
  - Sold standalone as a free tool and as a paid add-on ($15 per 5,000 recipients) inside Outreach plans
- **Source URL:** https://www.saleshandy.com/email-verifier

### Dialer

> **Wiki:** [[saleshandy--dialer]]

- **What it does:** Built-in calling for sales teams: buy phone numbers, place calls, and get AI call summaries inside Saleshandy.
- **Key features:**
  - Buy phone numbers and dial directly from the platform
  - AI call summaries
  - Unlimited recording storage on the Starter tier
  - Dialer credits sold standalone or as add-ons that never expire
- **Source URL:** https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing

### Saleshandy CRM

> **Wiki:** [[saleshandy--crm]]

- **What it does:** Lightweight built-in CRM with Kanban and list views to manage prospects and deals without leaving Saleshandy.
- **Key features:**
  - Unlimited prospects
  - Kanban and list views
  - Custom pipelines and stages
  - Currently "Free for limited time" on Starter ($19/mo regular monthly price)
- **Source URL:** https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing

### Email Infrastructure / Deliverability Stack

> **Wiki:** [[saleshandy--email-infrastructure]]

- **What it does:** Add-on that buys domains and mailboxes pre-configured with SPF, DKIM, DMARC, plus warm-up and inbox-placement testing to keep cold sends out of spam.
- **Key features:**
  - Buy domains + mailboxes with auto-configured SPF/DKIM/DMARC
  - Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, and Azure mailbox support
  - Automatic background warm-up
  - Domain-health, DKIM/DMARC checks, and tiered inbox-placement testing (Free / Starter / Pro / Scale)
  - Annual Flexi tier offers free mailbox replacement (requires new domain at $14/yr)
- **Source URL:** https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- All-in-one outbound stack — Lead Finder + Outreach + Verifier + Dialer + Deliverability + CRM in one product, framed as "stop juggling 5 tools for outreach" (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/).
- Unlimited email accounts on every Outreach tier (including the $25/mo Starter), with built-in sender rotation — a direct shot at per-seat or per-mailbox pricing models (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing).
- Pay-only-for-verified credit model in Lead Finder: "1 credit = 1 verified result. $0 when we can't verify," with unused-credit rollover (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/lead-finder).
- Email Infrastructure as a buy-and-configure add-on — domains and mailboxes with SPF/DKIM/DMARC pre-set, so customers can scale cold sending without sourcing infrastructure separately (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing).
- Self-serve, transparent SaaS pricing starting at $25/mo with a 7-day free trial — explicitly contrasted by Saleshandy's own marketing against ZoomInfo's "$15K+/year, quote-based, 1-year contracts" framing (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/blog/zoominfo-review/).
- Native MCP server (`saleshandy.com/mcp`) and developer CLI listed in the product footer, signaling AI-agent-friendly access on top of the standard REST API (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1) ↔ Saleshandy Lead Finder.** Lead Finder advertises 852M+ contacts across 42M+ companies with 75+ filters and waterfall enrichment, framed by Saleshandy as a larger and cheaper substitute (their own ZoomInfo review claims 700M+ Saleshandy contacts vs. "235M+ contacts" on ZoomInfo) (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/lead-finder; https://www.saleshandy.com/blog/zoominfo-review/).
  - **GTM Workspace (sales engagement surface) ↔ Saleshandy Outreach.** Saleshandy explicitly attacks the gap that ZoomInfo "doesn't offer native outreach features," selling its multichannel sequences, AI Copilot, sender rotation, and unified inbox as a one-platform replacement for ZoomInfo + a standalone sales-engagement tool (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/blog/zoominfo-review/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + enrichment APIs) ↔ Saleshandy enrichment REST API + CSV enrichment.** Saleshandy exposes `/v1/enrich/company`, `/v1/enrich/contact`, and CSV-based enrichment — far narrower than ZoomInfo Operations' deduplication/standardization suite, but it touches the same "enrich my CRM with verified contact data" problem space (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/lead-finder).
  - **APIs & MCP ↔ Saleshandy MCP server + developer CLI + REST API.** Saleshandy lists Model Context Protocol, developer docs, and a CLI alongside its REST endpoints — same access-lane archetype, scoped only to its outbound + enrichment surface rather than a full GTM context graph (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing).
- **No overlap:**
  - GTM Context Graph / first-party data fusion across CRM + conversations + intent — Saleshandy is purely a third-party prospect database + outbound execution layer (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/).
  - Conversation intelligence (Chorus equivalent) — only AI call summaries on the Dialer; no deal-coaching analytics, scorecards, or org-wide call search (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing).
  - ABM / marketing orchestration (ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio) — Saleshandy targets sales, agencies, and recruiters; not marketing or RevOps personas (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/industries/saas).
  - Intent Data / buying signals — Saleshandy's own ZoomInfo comparison concedes "Intent Data & Buying Signals … Not included natively"; "50+ buying signals" inside Lead Finder are firmographic filters, not a Forrester-Wave-leader intent-data product (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/blog/zoominfo-review/).
  - WebSights / IP-to-company resolution — not advertised; their ZoomInfo review lists this as a ZoomInfo capability without a Saleshandy counterpart (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/blog/zoominfo-review/).
  - Talent / recruitment data platform (TalentOS) — Saleshandy targets recruitment agencies as a cold-email channel only, with no candidate-database, layoff-signal, or ATS-integration features (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/industries/recruitment-agencies).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "ZoomInfo is an overpriced tool that offers a lot of extra features you might never use to justify its high license costs … Saleshandy gives you access to a larger database of 700M+ professional contacts, more than twice as many search credits, and unused credit rollover for just $24/month (annual billing)." (source: https://www.saleshandy.com/blog/zoominfo-review/) — Note: this is Saleshandy's own self-published comparison, not third-party research.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Your all-in-one outreach platform to book meetings every day" — stop juggling five tools (data, sequencing, deliverability, calling, CRM) and run cold outbound end-to-end from one product.
- **Recurring proof points:** Trusted by 25,000+ businesses worldwide; ~1,713 verified reviews across G2 (4.6/5, 773), Trustpilot (4.7/5, 743), and Google (4.3/5, 197); 92% email verification rate on first try (HighTouch testimonial); 23% higher email accuracy vs. Apollo (Slyce Software side-by-side test); 98% verification accuracy and <3% bounce rate claimed for Lead Finder waterfall; founded 2015 and "serves over 10,000 customers"; ranked top-5 Outreach & Sales Engagement Software by G2.
- **Tone / category framing:** "All-in-one cold outreach platform" — explicitly the consolidator (Lead Finder + Outreach + Verifier + Dialer + Inbox Placement + CRM), not a sales-intelligence or GTM platform.
