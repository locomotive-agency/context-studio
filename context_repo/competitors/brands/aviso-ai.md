## Summary

Aviso AI is an end-to-end AI revenue / forecasting / agentic-GTM platform sold to CROs, RevOps leaders, and frontline sales teams at mid-market and enterprise B2B companies. Its core wedge is forecasting (98%+ accuracy claim, native consumption-based forecasting) plus a conversation-intelligence module (MIKI assistant, smart transcripts, emotion analysis) and an agentic layer (AI Avatars, 50+ task agents, no-code Agent Studio) — putting it in direct overlap with ZoomInfo's Chorus, GTM Context Graph, and the GTM Workspace seller surface. Aviso explicitly positions itself as a tech-stack consolidator, naming Clari, BoostUp, Gong, People AI, Outreach, and Salesloft as displacement targets, but it is not a third-party B2B data provider and has no public APIs/MCP or ABM-platform offering. ## Product Offerings

### Revenue Forecasting

> **Wiki:** [[aviso-ai--revenue-forecasting]]

- **What it does:** AI-driven forecasting that auto-rolls up forecasts across reps and teams, ingests CRM and non-CRM data, and supports both ACV and consumption (usage-based) revenue models.
- **Key features:**
  - Auto-roll-up of forecast across reps, categories, and teams with best-case and most-likely numbers
  - Winscore Explanations score deals using CRM data, multi-channel engagement, conversations, and historical trends
  - Consumption forecasting that translates product usage into ARR/MRR alongside traditional models
  - Customizable forecasting hierarchies independent of CRM hierarchy
  - 98%+ forecast accuracy claim ("Get Nearly 100% Forecasting Accuracy" headline)
  - Bidirectional CRM sync; unifies multiple CRM instances and data lakes
- **Source URL:** https://www.aviso.com/product/revenue-forecasting

### Conversation Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[aviso-ai--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Captures and analyzes 1,000+ signals from sales calls (online and in-person) to produce smart transcripts, sentiment, emotion analysis, and next-best-actions.
- **Key features:**
  - Smart Transcripts with talk/listen ratios, key topics, competitor mentions, objections, next-best-steps
  - Emotion analysis using text + acoustic features (pitch, range, speech rate, intensity)
  - Aspect-based sentiment analysis and buyer-intent mining
  - Real-time in-call coaching with recommended responses and contextual pop-ups
  - Earnings-call analysis with keyword trend and Generative-AI summarization
  - Aviso Notetaker for Zoom, MS Teams, Google Meet (auto-recording and transcription)
  - 1-click AV recording for offline / in-person meetings
  - "Ask MIKI Anything": NLP query layer over call data
- **Source URL:** https://www.aviso.com/product/conversation-intelligence

### Relationship Intelligence (Aviso Engage)

> **Wiki:** [[aviso-ai--relationship-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Maps buyer-side stakeholder relationships, surfaces multi-threading gaps, and pushes AI nudges so reps engage the right contacts at the right cadence.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-threaded deal management with Champion B/C tracking
  - Relationship Graphs showing every seller-buyer engagement and A/B/C engagement scores
  - Email sentiment mining: emails sent, competitor mentions, objections, topics
  - Auto-population of new stakeholders into rep flows
  - Winscore Insights tying relationship-building to deal win probability
  - Bi-directional CRM sync for contacts and post-meeting tasks/events
- **Source URL:** https://www.aviso.com/product/relationship-intelligence

### Sales Engagement

> **Wiki:** [[aviso-ai--sales-engagement]]

- **What it does:** Native multi-channel prospecting and outreach module with AI-personalized sequences, AI Co-Pilots, and a Virtual SDR Agent.
- **Key features:**
  - Virtual SDR Agent: autonomous prospecting agent trained on intent data, CRM data, and external sources
  - AI Personalizer with Agentic Research Workflow extracting account insights from 10-Ks, earnings calls, news, and CRMs
  - Multi-channel sequences (email, LinkedIn, call, SMS) with A/B testing
  - DISC personality + LinkedIn profiler insights for contact-level personalization
  - Lead Score, ICP Score, and Intent Score for high-intent prospect targeting
  - Sequence and people analytics dashboards
- **Source URL:** https://www.aviso.com/product/sales-engagement

### Agentic AI Platform / MIKI

> **Wiki:** [[aviso-ai--miki]]

- **What it does:** Aviso's agentic layer: real-time AI Avatars, 30+ out-of-the-box agentic workflows, 50+ task-based revenue agents, and a no-code GTM Agent Studio orchestrated by MIKI.
- **Key features:**
  - MIKI agentic orchestrator (NLP query layer; can update CRM, change deal stages, revise commit accuracy)
  - Real-Time AI Avatars: role-specific agents for human-like deal/prospect interactions
  - 30+ pre-built agentic workflows and 13-week cadences for BDR-to-CRO roles
  - 50+ task-based agents for revenue use cases (objections, buyer insights, org changes)
  - No-Code GTM Agent Studio with chained actions and adaptable workflows
  - Customer Success virtual CSM avatar and renewal/retention AI cadences
- **Source URL:** https://www.aviso.com/

### Aviso Platform (Revenue Operating System)

> **Wiki:** [[aviso-ai--platform]]

- **What it does:** Underlying data and ML infrastructure that powers every Aviso product — single pane across CRMs, time-series database, AutoML, real-time streaming, and CRM writeback.
- **Key features:**
  - Aviso One: unifies multiple CRM instances (Salesforce, Dynamics) into a single forecasting view
  - Aviso Replay: proprietary time-series database tracking every change across objects and fields
  - Aviso Learn: AutoML pipeline (feature selection, hyperparameter tuning, model training)
  - Aviso Graph: GDPR/CCPA-compliant relationship graph (no cross-tenant data sharing)
  - Aviso LiveStream: real-time streaming sync across CRM and other tools
  - Aviso CRM Writeback: auto-update of deal details, activities, and contacts back to CRM
  - Aviso Connect: plug-and-play API layer to extend to other datasets
  - Aviso Mobile App: forecasting, pipeline, deal guidance, CI on the go
- **Source URL:** https://www.aviso.com/platform

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Time-Series AI Engine (Aviso Replay) that goes back 8 quarters and tracks every field change across connected datasets — pitched as differentiator vs CRM-only sales analytics tools (source: https://www.aviso.com/solution/use-case/unified-revenue-operations).
- Consumption / usage-based revenue forecasting natively combined with ACV forecasting — explicitly called out in the New Relic case study where "other vendors like Clari and Anaplan" could not meet requirements (source: https://www.aviso.com/case-study/new-relic-chooses-aviso-ai-for-accurate-forecasting-of-its-consumption-based-revenue).
- Aviso Graph is GDPR/CCPA compliant and does NOT share data across tenants (positioned against People.ai) (source: https://www.aviso.com/platform).
- Multi-CRM consolidation: Aviso One unifies multiple instances of Salesforce or Dynamics into a single forecasting view, claiming up to 30-50% spend reduction by retiring unused CRM licenses (source: https://www.aviso.com/platform).
- Full agentic stack — MIKI orchestrator + AI Avatars + 50+ task-based agents + no-code GTM Agent Studio — positioned as "consolidate tech stack with one Agentic AI platform" (source: https://www.aviso.com/).
- 98%+ forecast accuracy and ~25% rep time-back claims, plus willingness to buy out prospects' existing vendor contracts (source: https://www.aviso.com/pricing).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence):** Aviso markets itself as "#1 Conversation Intelligence" with 1,000+ signals, smart transcripts, emotion/sentiment analysis, real-time in-call coaching, and a notetaker for Zoom/MS Teams/Google Meet — feature-for-feature overlap with Chorus (source: https://www.aviso.com/product/conversation-intelligence).
  - **GTM Context Graph (deal/forecast reasoning):** Aviso's pitch is reasoning over CRM + conversation + activity + relationship signals to explain why deals move and produce forecast explanations — same problem space ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph addresses (source: https://www.aviso.com/solution/use-case/unified-revenue-operations).
  - **GTM Workspace (seller surface / next-best-action):** Aviso pitches a "single pane of glass" for reps/managers/CROs with deal acceleration and next-best-action guidance, with prospects citing they "haven't logged into SFDC in months" — competing for the seller's primary surface (source: https://www.aviso.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (sales engagement / SDR prospecting):** Aviso ships a native sales-engagement module with multi-channel sequences, an AI personalizer pulling from earnings calls/10-Ks, and a Virtual SDR Agent (source: https://www.aviso.com/product/sales-engagement).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing:** Aviso's Marketing Intelligence matches leads to SDRs and integrates with 6sense, Marketo, and Pardot — adjacent but lacks ABM platform depth (source: https://www.aviso.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality, routing):** Aviso CRM Writeback auto-updates deal details/activities/contacts and Aviso One unifies multiple CRM instances — adjacent to ZoomInfo Operations' hygiene/enrichment but driven by activity capture rather than third-party B2B data (source: https://www.aviso.com/platform).
- **No overlap:**

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "One Agentic AI Platform for Modern GTM Teams to Predict, Guide, and Simplify Every Revenue Action" — pitched as an end-to-end AI revenue platform that consolidates the GTM tech stack and delivers ~98% forecast accuracy.
- **Recurring proof points:** 98%+ forecast accuracy and 99.5% platform uptime; 450+ revenue teams (Honeywell, NetApp, New Relic, RingCentral, LaunchDarkly); 40% increase in average win rate; 70%+ improvement in net new revenue per AE; +36% net new revenue per rep, +12% win rate, +16% avg deal size, -23% sales cycle time; New Relic 99%+ ACR forecast accuracy (chosen over Clari and Anaplan); customers explicitly citing displacement of Clari and BoostUp.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as an "End-to-End AI Revenue Platform" / "AI Revenue Operating System" / "Agentic AI Platform" — explicitly positioned to consolidate tools, not as a point solution. Repeatedly contrasts itself against CRM-only analytics, point CI tools (Gong, Chorus), and forecasting incumbents (Clari, BoostUp).
