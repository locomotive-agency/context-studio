## Summary

Woodpecker is a cold email outreach and sales engagement tool aimed at SMB sales teams, lead-generation agencies, and recruiters running outbound campaigns. Its core overlap with ZoomInfo is narrow but real: Woodpecker competes with ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace's outbound-execution surface and lightly with the Data foundation via its built-in Lead Finder (1B+ B2B records), but it has no conversation intelligence, intent data, ABM orchestration, or GTM Context Graph equivalent. Pricing is fully public, usage-based ($4 per 100 contacted prospects), with unlimited mailboxes and team members at no extra cost — a model that contrasts directly with ZoomInfo's seat-based enterprise contracts. Woodpecker's own ZoomInfo-competitors blog frames the brand as "great for larger enterprises and seasoned sales teams" but ill-suited to small businesses focused on cold email.

## Product Offerings

### Woodpecker Cold Email

> **Wiki:** [[woodpecker--cold-email]]

- **What it does:** Cold email outreach automation that sends sequenced emails, follow-ups, and protects deliverability for B2B prospecting.
- **Key features:**
  - Up to 16-step campaigns combining emails, LinkedIn manual tasks, and phone calls
  - Inbox rotation across multiple connected mailboxes (no per-slot pricing)
  - Centralized inbox auto-sorting replies into 'interested', 'auto-replied', 'bounced', 'out-of-office' folders
  - Adaptive Sending detects email-provider limits and throttles automatically
  - Free deliverability monitor, free warm-up, free catch-all email verification
  - Built-in AI interest-level detection on replies; A/B testing up to 5 versions
  - Snippet-based personalization with empty-custom-field detection (skips send if blank)
  - Send-in-prospect-timezone, GDPR + CCPA compliance, ISO + CASA tier-2 certified
- **Source URL:** https://woodpecker.co/features/

### Woodpecker Agency

> **Wiki:** [[woodpecker--agency]]

- **What it does:** Multi-client workspace management for lead-generation and outreach agencies running campaigns on behalf of clients.
- **Key features:**
  - All clients on one dashboard with overview of every client's results
  - One-click login between client workspaces
  - Separate account data per client for safety
  - Clients can manage their own email connections; view-only access option
  - Notifications for all client accounts in one place
  - $27/month per active client; white-label add-on $5/month per active client
- **Source URL:** https://woodpecker.co/features/

### Lead Finder

> **Wiki:** [[woodpecker--lead-finder]]

- **What it does:** Built-in B2B contact-discovery feature that surfaces verified emails from a 1B+ record database for cold outreach.
- **Key features:**
  - 1+ billion record B2B database for prospect search
  - 400 Lead Finder credits included monthly free
  - Additional credits start at $28/month per 2,000 credits
  - Per-credit billing for verified email addresses ('Risky' or 'Guess' results not charged)
- **Source URL:** https://woodpecker.co/

### LinkedIn outreach automation

> **Wiki:** [[woodpecker--linkedin-outreach]]

- **What it does:** Add-on that automates LinkedIn DMs, connection requests, and profile visits inside Woodpecker campaigns.
- **Key features:**
  - Native LinkedIn automation rolled out in 2025
  - Combine with email and call steps in one multichannel campaign
  - Priced separately at $29/month per LinkedIn account connected
- **Source URL:** https://woodpecker.co/pricing/

### Domains, inboxes, and dedicated infrastructure

> **Wiki:** [[woodpecker--api-mcp]]

- **What it does:** Buy or connect domains and email accounts for sending; optional dedicated servers (Infraforge) for advanced users; native MCP Server / API / CLI.
- **Key features:**
  - Buy domains and emails with SPF, DKIM, DMARC pre-configured
  - Email addresses (Google or Microsoft) at $6/month each
  - Email addresses (Maildoso or Mailforge) at $4/month each
  - New domains ~$1/month, billed annually
  - Dedicated servers (Infraforge) at $59/month per server
  - API, webhooks, MCP server, CLI, and integrations bundled at $20/month
- **Source URL:** https://woodpecker.co/pricing/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- No per-email-slot pricing — connect unlimited mailboxes and team members for free; charge is per contacted prospect (source: https://woodpecker.co/pricing/).
- Free deliverability extras — free warm-up, free catch-all email verification, free deliverability monitor included on every paid account (source: https://woodpecker.co/pricing/).
- Adaptive Sending detects email-provider limits in real time and throttles to prevent blocks, imitating manual sending cadence (source: https://woodpecker.co/features/).
- Dedicated agency mode with per-client workspaces, one-click client login, and optional white-label (source: https://woodpecker.co/features/).
- Up to 16-step multichannel campaigns mixing email, LinkedIn manual tasks, and phone calls (source: https://woodpecker.co/features/).
- Native MCP Server exposed inside the standard $20/month API bundle — one of the few outreach tools shipping MCP support (source: https://woodpecker.co/pricing/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Woodpecker's Cold Email + LinkedIn automation directly overlaps the outbound-execution surface inside GTM Workspace — both products run sequenced multichannel outreach for SDR/AE workflows (source: https://woodpecker.co/features/). The built-in Lead Finder (1B+ B2B record database) places it in lightweight overlap with ZoomInfo's Data foundation, though scale and verification depth are not comparable to ZoomInfo's 500M contacts / 100M companies / 200M+ verified emails (source: https://woodpecker.co/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Woodpecker exposes a native MCP Server alongside its API/CLI as a $20/month add-on, mirroring ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP positioning but limited to outreach-execution data rather than a broader GTM Context Graph (source: https://woodpecker.co/pricing/). Free catch-all email verification + deliverability monitor touches a narrow slice of what ZoomInfo Operations provides at the CRM data-quality layer (source: https://woodpecker.co/pricing/).
- **No overlap:** No conversation intelligence (no equivalent to Chorus — Woodpecker tracks manual call tasks only); no buying-intent or third-party signal data; no ABM / GTM Studio / RevOps marketing-automation surface; no first-party + third-party data fusion or "why deals move" GTM Context Graph reasoning (source: https://woodpecker.co/features/, https://woodpecker.co/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** From Woodpecker's own ZoomInfo-competitors blog: ZoomInfo "might overwhelm new users with its complex interface and occasionally slow performance with large datasets. It may be great for larger enterprises and seasoned sales teams, but small businesses might find it less suited to their needs and could consider Zoominfo competitors for simpler, more cost-effective solutions... If cold email outreach forms the core of your strategy, consider Woodpecker." (source: https://woodpecker.co/blog/zoominfo-competitors/) — note this is a listicle that positions Woodpecker as #1, not a dedicated vs-page; see flags.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "All you need to cold email in one place" — send cold emails, free email verification, free warm-up, buy pre-configured domains and emails, run multichannel email + LinkedIn campaigns (source: https://woodpecker.co/).
- **Recurring proof points:** ProSales Connection's "best open rate was 83.3% with a 38.9% reply rate" on a 130-prospect campaign; Veth Group "set up about 1,000 appointments a month with Woodpecker... over 150 in a given month for a client for half the cost of an SDR"; ProfitWell CEO endorsement on personalized one-to-one scaling; ISO certified, CASA tier-2 certified, GDPR + CCPA compliant, Google compliant partner (oAuth) (sources: https://woodpecker.co/blog/case-study-prosales-connection/, https://woodpecker.co/pricing/, https://woodpecker.co/features/).
- **Tone / category framing:** Positions narrower than ZoomInfo's all-in-one GTM platform — "Tools for Cold Email, Sales & Agency Outreach." Plain, practitioner-friendly tone aimed at SMB founders, agencies, and individual reps rather than enterprise RevOps.
