## Summary

Qualified is an Agentic Marketing Platform anchored by Piper, an AI SDR Agent that converts inbound buyers across the website and email inbox, sold primarily to B2B Marketing and Demand Gen leaders at mid-market and enterprise SaaS companies. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo Chat (website conversational layer) and the AI-SDR/seller-agent narrative inside GTM Workspace — but is inbound-only, depends on partners (Demandbase, 6sense, Salesforce, even ZoomInfo) for account/contact data, and does not sell a B2B data foundation or developer/MCP layer. Salesforce signed a definitive agreement to acquire Qualified on Dec 17, 2025 (expected close in Q1 of Salesforce FY27), so positioning will fold into Agentforce; that pending event is the biggest near-term variable in any ZoomInfo vs Qualified comparison.

## Product Offerings

### Piper the AI SDR Agent

> **Wiki:** [[qualified--piper-ai-sdr]]

- **What it does:** Always-on AI SDR agent that autonomously engages, qualifies, nurtures, and converts inbound buyers across the website and email inbox 24x7 (source: https://www.qualified.com/ai-sdr).
- **Key features:**
  - Piper Conversations — live chat, voice, and video on the website
  - Piper Email — bespoke AI-generated nurture and follow-up emails
  - Piper Meetings — instant scheduling from website forms, chat, or email
  - Piper Offers — personalized marketing offers served to the buying committee
  - Piper for Slack — two-way collaboration and notifications inside Slack
  - PiperX — face-to-face video conversations with a human-like avatar
  - Multi-language agent (Enterprise tier) and multiple agent profiles (Ultimate tier)
- **Source URL:** https://www.qualified.com/ai-sdr

### Qualified Agentic Marketing Platform

> **Wiki:** [[qualified--agentic-marketing-platform]]

- **What it does:** Underlying platform that powers Piper — synthesizes GTM data and uses ML reasoning to plan, decide, and act on buyer engagement in real time (source: https://www.qualified.com/platform).
- **Key features:**
  - Agent Spotlight (decisioning, reasoning, planning visibility)
  - Agent Studio (onboarding, training, content, goals, guardrails, evaluation, coaching)
  - Visitor 360 / segmentation with waterfall enrichment
  - Account segmentation, qualification, routing, workflow
  - Reporting & analytics (native + Salesforce)
  - Enterprise APIs (Enterprise tier); multiple websites/brands and production instances (Ultimate)
  - Third-party research intent signals (Enterprise tier)
- **Source URL:** https://www.qualified.com/platform

### Agentic Solutions (Nurture, PLG, Outbound, ABM)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Chat (data-powered website chat) ↔ Piper Conversations / Qualified AI Chatbots.** Both engage website visitors in real time and route high-intent traffic to sales reps; Qualified positions Piper as the agentic replacement for traditional B2B chatbots/conversational marketing tools (source: https://www.qualified.com/ai-sdr).
  - **GTM Workspace AI SDR / agentic seller layer ↔ Piper the AI SDR Agent.** Qualified frames Piper as an autonomous AI SDR that "thinks, reasons, and strategizes" to convert inbound buyers — the same agentic-seller positioning ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace uses for the seller front-end. Qualified is inbound-website-centric; ZoomInfo extends to outbound and multi-channel (source: https://www.qualified.com/ai-sdr).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM) / GTM Studio orchestration ↔ Qualified for ABM / Agentic Nurture / Piper for Demandbase.** Qualified pitches account-based engagement and nurture flows that overlap with ZoomInfo's ABM and marketing orchestration, but depends on Demandbase / 6sense for target-account data rather than owning the data layer itself (source: https://qualified.com/integrations/demandbase).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing) ↔ Qualified Platform routing/workflow + Visitor 360 enrichment.** Qualified does account segmentation with "waterfall enrichment" and routing/workflow inside its platform — ZoomInfo Operations covers the same need at a CRM layer with deeper data depth (source: https://www.qualified.com/platform).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) ↔ Piper Conversations transcript + Agent Spotlight visibility (lightweight).** Qualified records and surfaces buyer-Piper conversations for review/coaching but does not handle Zoom/sales-call recording — Gong is integrated for that workload (source: https://www.qualified.com/platform).
- **No overlap:**
  - **B2B contact and company data foundation.** Qualified does NOT sell verified contact data, company graphs, technographics, or human-researcher-curated profiles; it depends on data partners (ZoomInfo is even listed as an integration on 8x8's case-study sidebar) and on first-party Salesforce data for enrichment (source: https://www.qualified.com/customers/8x8).
  - **Outbound prospecting / cold-outreach as a primary motion.** Piper is positioned for INBOUND demand conversion ("Engage and convert inbound buyers at scale"). Qualified for Outbound exists as a packaged solution but is not the headline product. ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace and ZoomInfo Sales serve outbound prospecting natively (source: https://www.qualified.com/ai-sdr).
  - **Developer / API / MCP agent-builder ecosystem.** Qualified offers enterprise APIs at the Enterprise tier but no MCP server, no developer-focused "AI agent builder" category presence — ZoomInfo has a public MCP server (mcp.zoominfo.com/mcp) and a full Enterprise API suite (Data, GTM Workspace, Marketing, Platform) (source: https://www.qualified.com/pricing).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Qualified maintains vs-pages for Drift and 1mind but does not publish a direct vs-ZoomInfo page; SERP returns instead pointed to G2, TrustRadius, and Slashdot third-party comparison shells.
