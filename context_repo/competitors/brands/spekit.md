## Summary

Spekit is an AI-powered revenue / sales enablement platform — branded the "Rep Acceleration Platform" — built for sales enablement, product marketing, RevOps, and revenue leaders on high-growth B2B teams. It combines a governed GTM knowledge engine, agentic AI coaching, automated Deal Rooms, and a unified deal-context layer (Salesforce + Gong) delivered primarily through a Chrome Extension that overlays content, coaching, and follow-up actions inside the tools reps already use. Crossover with ZoomInfo is narrow and adjacent: Spekit's coaching/summary/follow-up workflow grazes Chorus and the GTM Workspace surface, while its category — sales enablement, training, and digital adoption — is a space ZoomInfo does not sell into; Spekit, in turn, is not a B2B data provider. Spekit's homepage even shows ZoomInfo as a Spekit customer logo.

## Product Offerings

### GTM Knowledge Engine

> **Wiki:** [[spekit--knowledge-engine]]

- **What it does:** Governed, AI-powered content management system that centralizes GTM content (products, messaging, plays, competitive intel) and feeds it into every rep-facing surface (AI Sidekick, Learning Paths, Deal Rooms).
- **Key features:**
  - AI Content Builder — generates new content, transforms existing docs, auto-updates "Speks" when source material changes
  - Governance Dashboard — monitors freshness, flags outdated material, audits what reaches reps
  - Auto-Tagging — AI categorizes and indexes content automatically
  - Connectors to Google Drive, SharePoint, and Confluence
  - Knowledge Checks — embedded quizzes surfaced in the flow of work
  - Learning Paths — structured onboarding and reinforcement journeys from the same content
  - Flexible CMS — modular, reusable content blocks
  - Analytics tying content engagement and Knowledge Check performance to Salesforce closed-won outcomes
- **Source URL:** https://www.spekit.com/platform/knowledge-engine

### Agentic Coaching & Actions (AI Sidekick)

> **Wiki:** [[spekit--ai-sidekick]]

- **What it does:** AI orchestration layer that proactively detects what a rep is doing in real time and delivers coaching, content recommendations, or automated actions (drafts emails, builds Deal Rooms, generates exec summaries) inside the rep's existing tools (source: https://www.spekit.com/platform/agentic-coaching).
- **Key features:**
  - Proactive coaching based on deal context and GTM motions
  - Context-aware content and messaging recommendations
  - Auto-drafted follow-ups grounded in Gong call intelligence and Salesforce opportunity data
  - Auto-creates Deal Rooms preloaded with deal-specific content
  - Generates executive summaries from call intelligence
  - Just-in-time enablement inside Salesforce, Gmail, Gong, and other tools reps already use
  - Surfaces battlecards, exit criteria, or play guidance based on rep activity
- **Source URL:** https://www.spekit.com/platform/agentic-coaching

### Personalized Buyer Experiences (Deal Rooms)

> **Wiki:** [[spekit--deal-rooms]]

- **What it does:** Automatically generates branded, deal-specific digital sales rooms tailored to the account, stakeholders, and stage, with tracked engagement signals.
- **Key features:**
  - Customized, fully-tracked Deal Rooms
  - Deal-aware content recommendations and AI-assisted content creation
  - Messaging always aligned to GTM strategy
  - Revenue-linked buyer engagement insights
  - Real-time notifications when buyers engage
- **Source URL:** https://www.spekit.com/platform

### Unified Deal Context

> **Wiki:** [[spekit--unified-deal-context]]

- **What it does:** Connects Salesforce opportunity data and Gong call intelligence into a single deal view that follows the rep wherever they work (CRM, email, call tools).
- **Key features:**
  - Connects Salesforce opportunity data and Gong call intelligence into a single deal view
  - Recognizes the active opportunity automatically and delivers relevant guidance
  - Surfaces deal context wherever reps work, including CRM, email, and call tools
  - Domain-detection in email matches the active opportunity
  - Fuels Sidekick's coaching recommendations with grounded, deal-specific context
- **Source URL:** https://www.spekit.com/platform

### Embedded Enablement / Spekit Hub / Learning Paths / Analytics

> **Wiki:** [[spekit--embedded-enablement]]

- **What it does:** Supporting feature surfaces — in-app guidance via the Chrome Extension (Embedded Enablement), the admin/control center (Spekit Hub), structured learning journeys (Learning Paths), and reporting tied back to Salesforce pipeline.
- **Key features:**
  - Chrome Extension delivers content, answers, and coaching directly inside any browser-based tool
  - Spekit Hub — admin control center for managing knowledge, configuring coaching, and measuring impact
  - Learning Paths built from the same governed knowledge content reps see daily
  - Analytics connect content engagement and Knowledge Check performance to Salesforce closed-won outcomes
- **Source URL:** https://www.spekit.com/platform

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) — adjacent only:** Spekit consumes Gong call intelligence rather than recording calls itself, but Spekit's auto-generated call summaries, follow-up drafting, and coaching from conversation context overlap with Chorus's coaching/insights workflow. Evidence: "Spekit executes tasks on the rep's behalf: creating a Deal Room preloaded with deal-specific content, generating an executive summary from call intelligence, or drafting a follow-up email from live deal context." (source: https://www.spekit.com/platform/agentic-coaching)
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end):** Spekit's AI Sidekick + Embedded Enablement + Deal Rooms compete for the same "one place for the rep to operate" surface — but delivered as a Chrome Extension overlay across Salesforce, Gmail, Gong, etc. rather than as a destination app. Evidence: "Install a single extension and Spekit automatically surfaces the coaching, content, knowledge, and automated actions to 10x your GTM team, anywhere buying conversations happen." (source: https://www.spekit.com/platform/integrations)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio (marketer / RevOps surface):** Spekit Hub + the Knowledge Engine compete for the same enablement-team / RevOps configuration surface, but for content + coaching orchestration rather than audience building / play orchestration: "The control center for enablement teams. Manage knowledge, configure coaching, and measure impact." (source: https://www.spekit.com/platform/knowledge-engine)
  - **GTM Context Graph:** Both pitch a contextual reasoning layer; Spekit's "Unified Deal Context" is materially narrower (Salesforce + Gong) versus ZoomInfo's data + conversation + intent + behavioral fusion across third-party + first-party sources. Evidence: "Connects Salesforce opportunity data and Gong call intelligence into a single deal view … Fuels Sidekick's coaching recommendations with grounded, deal-specific context." (source: https://www.spekit.com/platform)
- **No overlap:**
  - **Competitor-only — sales enablement / training / digital adoption core:** governed CMS + AI content generation + Learning Paths + Knowledge Checks + Deal Rooms; ZoomInfo does not sell a sales enablement product.
  - **Competitor-only — in-app digital-adoption / embedded guidance via Chrome Extension** (Spekit's lineage as a digital adoption platform for Salesforce). ZoomInfo does not embed walkthrough/coaching overlays in other vendors' UIs.
  - **Brand-only — B2B contact/company data foundation, intent data, Forrester Wave Leader status for Intent, and APIs/MCP for agent builders.** Spekit is not a data provider; it consumes data from Salesforce/Gong rather than supplying contact data. Evidence: TrustRadius vs page rates Spekit "0/0 Ratings" on every Prospecting and Sales Intelligence Data Standards feature (Advanced search, List quality, Contact information, Company information, etc.) while ZoomInfo Sales is rated 8.0–8.1, reflecting the category mismatch (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/spekit-vs-zoominfo-sales).
  - **Note:** ZoomInfo appears as a Spekit customer logo on the AI page, underscoring Spekit's category-adjacent (not head-to-head) position relative to ZoomInfo.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The TrustRadius head-to-head is third-party-authored and largely highlights category mismatch — Spekit is rated "0/0" across Prospecting, Sales Intelligence Data Standards, and Data Augmentation features (because it doesn't sell those), while ZoomInfo Sales scores 8.0–8.1. Spekit's adjacent self-description on the comparison: "Spekit is a just-in-time enablement engine that embeds answers, coaching, and sales content directly in the tools revenue teams use." (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/spekit-vs-zoominfo-sales)
