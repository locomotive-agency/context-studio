# Ampliz

## Summary

Ampliz is a B2B sales intelligence and contact data provider with two flagship vertical/regional product lines — Healthcare Intelligence (5.38M HCPs, 8,203 hospitals) and APAC Intelligence (36M+ business profiles across 20 APAC countries) — plus a Chrome extension (SalesBuddy) and an Enterprise tier advertising 400M+ records. It targets sales, marketing, and recruiter buyers in healthcare, software/IT, manufacturing, education, and APAC-region B2B prospecting, and explicitly positions itself as the user-friendly, transparently priced alternative to ZoomInfo. Most material crossover with ZoomInfo: Ampliz's Enterprise + SalesBuddy stack is a direct functional competitor to ZoomInfo Sales' data foundation and prospecting workflow, and its Bombora-sourced intent data + technographics overlap with ZoomInfo's intent/technographic layer — but Ampliz has no GTM Context Graph equivalent, no public APIs & MCP surface, and no GTM Workspace/Studio next-gen front-end.

## Product Offerings

### APAC Intelligence

- **What it does:** Regional B2B intelligence database focused on Asia-Pacific decision-makers, with country-level email lists across 20 APAC countries.
- **Key features:**
  - 36M+ business profiles across 20 APAC countries
  - Country-segmented email lists with disclosed counts (India 6.87M, Malaysia 856K, Singapore 575K, Indonesia 776K, Vietnam 668K, Australia 434K, Japan 250K, etc.)
  - APAC-targeted data insights covering 196+ countries
  - Industry-focused insights (IT, education, manufacturing)
  - Job-title-based intelligence to reach C-level executives and SME owners
  - GDPR and CCPA aligned
- **Source URL:** https://www.ampliz.com/apac-intelligence

### Healthcare Intelligence

- **What it does:** Vertical healthcare data product covering hospitals, physicians, and healthcare decision-makers for medical device, pharma, and healthcare-recruitment buyers.
- **Key features:**
  - 5,384,164 HCPs and 8,203 hospitals (database snapshot)
  - 10 million executive contacts
  - 25+ search filters for segmented data output
  - Customer footprint analytics with intent-driven data
  - Use cases: medical device manufacturers, pharma, healthcare recruitment firms, medical reps, diagnostic centers
  - GDPR-aligned data policies
- **Source URL:** https://www.ampliz.com/healthcare-intelligence

### Ampliz SalesBuddy

- **What it does:** Chrome browser extension that surfaces company, contact, and contextual intelligence on the page the user is browsing, plus a prospect-fit score against the user's defined ICP.
- **Key features:**
  - Prospect-fit score against user-defined ICP (no credits required, available on all plans)
  - Filters by seniority level and department to find decision-makers
  - Preview phone, email, and LinkedIn before spending credits
  - Manual contact-sourcing requests when a site has no contact details
  - Contextual Intelligence requests (technology stack, funding info, contact location) — manually sourced by the Ampliz team
  - Database backing: 120M+ contacts, 21M+ direct dials, 7M companies, 95% accuracy claim
- **Source URL:** https://www.ampliz.com/ampliz-salesbuddy/

### Ampliz Enterprise

- **What it does:** Bulk B2B prospecting tier giving enterprise teams export-ready access to Ampliz's global database with advanced filters and compliance support.
- **Key features:**
  - 400M+ contact records and 9M+ companies (Enterprise-tier framing)
  - Advanced filters: Company, Name, Department, Seniority, Title, Location, City/State
  - Bulk export to CSV / CRM
  - Region-specific compliance cross-checks performed by Ampliz team
  - Add-on intelligence via Custom Data and Ampliz Enhance modules
  - Prospect recent-activity refresh date shown alongside profile
- **Source URL:** https://www.ampliz.com/ampliz-enterprise

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Ampliz Enterprise + SalesBuddy directly compete with ZoomInfo Sales' data foundation and prospecting workflow — Ampliz Enterprise advertises "400Million+ records" and 9M+ companies for global prospecting (source: https://www.ampliz.com/ampliz-enterprise), and SalesBuddy is a Chrome extension explicitly positioned as a real-time prospecting tool, the same job ZoomInfo Sales does. On data foundation specifically, Ampliz competes head-to-head on contact-data scale at smaller numbers than ZoomInfo's headline figures (Ampliz cites 120M / 150M / 400M contacts across different pages — see flags) (source: https://www.ampliz.com/our-data, https://www.ampliz.com/ampliz-salesbuddy/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Ampliz's intent + technographic layer (Bombora-sourced) touches ZoomInfo's intent / technographic positioning, but at much narrower scale and without ZoomInfo's Forrester-Wave-leader proprietary signals (source: https://www.ampliz.com/resources/ampliz-vs-zoominfo/). Ampliz Enhance and Custom Data add-ons perform a database-enrichment job similar to ZoomInfo Operations, though without CRM data-quality automation, deduplication, or routing (source: https://www.ampliz.com/ampliz-enterprise).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph** — no conversation intelligence, no Chorus/Workbounce equivalent, no agentic "why deals move" reasoning layer on any reviewed Ampliz page.
  - **GTM Workspace / GTM Studio / APIs & MCP** — Ampliz lists "Contact API Coming Soon" on every page footer; no public API, no MCP server, no agent-builder positioning.
  - **ABM platform / play orchestration (ZoomInfo Marketing)** — Ampliz markets to marketers but only as a list-segmentation tool; no audience builder, no orchestrated plays, no Gartner MQ ABM-leader positioning.
  - **Healthcare HCP/NPI vertical depth** is competitor-only — Ampliz publishes a dedicated Healthcare Intelligence product, NPI lookup, and physician-specialty directories that ZoomInfo does not market at this granularity (source: https://www.ampliz.com/healthcare-intelligence).
  - **APAC regional depth via country-segmented lists** is also competitor-only — Ampliz publishes per-country APAC counts that ZoomInfo's broader global coverage does not surface as a discrete product.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** From Ampliz's vs-ZoomInfo article: _"While ZoomInfo offers a wealth of data and features, it falls short in terms of user-friendliness compared to Ampliz. One of the main drawbacks of ZoomInfo is its complex interface, which can be overwhelming for users... Ampliz's commitment to data accuracy is evident in its approach to data enrichment. By prioritizing human-verified data, Ampliz ensures higher reliability and accuracy in its insights. In contrast, ZoomInfo's reliance on machine-processed data may raise concerns about the precision of its information."_ (source: https://www.ampliz.com/resources/ampliz-vs-zoominfo/). Three core attack lines: ease of use, human-verified accuracy vs ZoomInfo's machine-processed data, and price transparency vs ZoomInfo's tier-gating. ICP comparison: shared = Sales and Marketing in mid-market/enterprise B2B; competitor-only = healthcare-vertical buyers, APAC regional buyers, and Recruiters as a first-class persona; ZoomInfo-only = RevOps / GTM Engineers, Developers / AI agent builders (via APIs & MCP), and Account Management / CS.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Data to power your business — fresh, reliable, accurate B2B and Healthcare data" — positioned against ZoomInfo on price transparency, ease of use, and human-verified accuracy (source: https://www.ampliz.com/, https://www.ampliz.com/resources/ampliz-vs-zoominfo/).
- **Recurring proof points:**
  - Trusted by 5000+ businesses (homepage)
  - 120M+ contacts, 21M+ direct dials, 7M companies (SalesBuddy/Enterprise)
  - 400M+ records (Ampliz Enterprise tier)
  - 5.38M HCPs, 8,203 hospitals, 10M executive contacts (Healthcare)
  - 36M+ APAC business profiles across 20 countries
  - 95% data accuracy guarantee + 800+ data experts verifying records
- **Tone / category framing:** Sales intelligence / B2B data provider with two flagship verticals (Healthcare, APAC). Positions itself as the user-friendly, transparently priced alternative to ZoomInfo — never claims an "AI GTM Platform" category and does not market a reasoning/context layer.
