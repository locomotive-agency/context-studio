# LeadGenius

## Sub-products

- [[leadgenius--precision-data|LeadGenius Precision Data]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)
- [[leadgenius--ds-research-services|LeadGenius DS Research Services]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

LeadGenius is a managed B2B contact-data provider selling custom-sourced, on-demand "Precision Data" to MOPs, SOPs, RevOps, ABM/ABS, and enterprise sales teams across global and niche markets. The platform pairs AI with Human-in-the-Loop researchers and ships data through dedicated white-glove delivery pods rather than a self-serve UI, explicitly contrasting itself with "static, one-size-fits-all databases." The most material brand overlap is direct against ZoomInfo's Data foundation and ZoomInfo Operations: LeadGenius's Precision Data Platform competes on contact-to-company match rate, verified email accuracy, and enrichment rate, and Contact Monitoring overlaps Operations on CRM-decay management; LeadGenius authors a vs-ZoomInfo article claiming 48% lower accuracy-adjusted total cost of ownership. Pricing is gated; a third-party Vendr buyer guide benchmarks the average annual contract at ~$22,000.

## Product Offerings

### Precision Data Platform (Enterprise B2B Data Platform)

- **What it does:** Custom, on-demand B2B contact-data foundation that activates real-time, contextual intelligence at enterprise scale, sourced via AI + Human-in-the-Loop and delivered through dedicated white-glove pods.
- **Key features:**
  - Global-Scale Infrastructure with in-region sourcing teams across EMEA, LATAM, APAC
  - Tailored data architectures aligned to a customer's segmentation and workflows
  - Advanced privacy framework (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD) embedded in every workflow
  - Enterprise-validated person data verified through trusted public sources
  - Signal-rich enrichment beyond static firmographics
  - Seamless delivery and refresh flexibility tied to GTM cycles
  - Dedicated white-glove delivery pod per enterprise engagement
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadgenius.com/product

### Contact Coverage & Buying Committees

- **What it does:** Custom-sourced, live, on-demand contact data covering the full 5–10 stakeholder buying committee account-by-account, including hard-to-cover global and niche markets.
- **Key features:**
  - Full buying-committee visibility per target account
  - Coverage in EMEA, LATAM, APAC and niche industries (hospitality, construction, franchises, brick-and-mortar, SMBs)
  - AI + Human-in-the-Loop sourcing instead of a static database
  - On-demand custom sourcing per account
  - Targeted at ABM and ABS teams
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/contact-coverage-buying-committees

### Contact Activation

- **What it does:** Distributes the customer's content through a global publisher network and delivers back opted-in, in-market contacts with verified buying signals.
- **Key features:**
  - Global publisher network for content distribution
  - Opted-in, GDPR-compliant lead delivery
  - Buying-signal enrichment on every contact
  - Reignites stalled opportunities via fresh engagement signals
  - Lead-scoring inputs based on real intent rather than form-fills
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/contact-activation

### Contact Monitoring

- **What it does:** Continuously tracks role changes, promotions, and champion movement on the customer's existing contacts and surfaces them as buying signals.
- **Key features:**
  - Tracks role changes, new hires, and promotions
  - Flags champion movement when an advocate switches companies
  - Refresh runs on the customer's chosen cadence
  - Identifies tenured stakeholders who influence procurement
  - Worldwide automatic monitoring
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/contact-monitoring

### Advanced Contact Tags

- **What it does:** Enriches contacts with skills, tools, and technographic signals beyond job titles, so prospecting can target real expertise and influence rather than keyword-matched titles.
- **Key features:**
  - Skills- and tool-expertise tags beyond job titles
  - Technographic signals on the contact (not just account) level
  - Pre-built tags to reduce manual list-building
  - Surfaces hidden buyers whose titles don't match expected keywords
  - Personalization by skills/tools to lift open and reply rates
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/advanced-contact-tags

  - Operating compliantly under GDPR / LGPD / CCPA
  - Skill- and tool-based targeting beyond job titles

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- AI + Human-in-the-Loop sourcing — pairing repeated on every solutions page and contrasted with "static databases" (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/).
- On-demand, custom-sourced data instead of a pre-built database — contacts sourced "in real time, uniquely for your use case" (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/).
- Global coverage in EMEA, LATAM, APAC and niche segments (hospitality, construction, franchises, brick-and-mortar, SMBs) where "others fall short" (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/contact-coverage-buying-committees).
- Customer retains full data ownership — "LeadGenius takes a different approach by allowing customers to retain full ownership of all sourced data," positioned against "leased data models like those used by ZoomInfo" (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/resources/the-true-cost-of-ownership-why-leadgenius-outperforms-zoominfo).
- Compliance-first framing (GDPR, LGPD, CCPA) embedded in every workflow rather than an add-on (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/product).
- Dedicated "white-glove delivery pod" attached to every enterprise engagement — managed-service layer rather than self-serve (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/product).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation ↔ LeadGenius Precision Data Platform / Advanced Contact Tags.** LeadGenius positions itself as "the data foundation for global go-to-market teams" and competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo on contact-to-company match rate, verified email accuracy, and enrichment rate (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/product, https://www.leadgenius.com/resources/the-true-cost-of-ownership-why-leadgenius-outperforms-zoominfo).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations ↔ LeadGenius Contact Monitoring.** Pitched as continuous monitoring that "keeps your CRM fresh" and surfaces buying signals like role changes, promotions, and champion movement — same job-to-be-done as ZoomInfo Operations' enrichment and decay-management plays (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/contact-monitoring).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM / demand gen) ↔ LeadGenius Contact Activation.** Distributes the customer's content through a global publisher network and delivers opted-in, in-market contacts back to sales — a content-syndication / activation play that touches ZoomInfo Marketing's demand-gen surface but is not a full ABM platform (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/contact-activation).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace ↔ LeadGenius Contact Coverage & Buying Committees.** Targets ABM/ABS teams with full buying-committee visibility — touches the same prospecting workflow as the GTM Workspace, but ships as data deliverables and reports rather than a seller front-end UI (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/solutions/contact-coverage-buying-committees).
- **No overlap:**
  - No conversation intelligence (no Chorus equivalent — no call recording, transcription, or coaching surface on any reviewed page).
  - No GTM Studio equivalent (no natural-language audience builder, no play orchestration layer, no marketer/RevOps front-end UI).
  - No MCP server / agent-builder ecosystem — no developer or AI-agent surface referenced.
  - No website-chat product (no equivalent to ZoomInfo Chat).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** From LeadGenius's own vs-ZoomInfo article: "Despite its higher initial cost, LeadGenius's total accuracy-adjusted costs are about 48% lower than those of ZoomInfo when you consider downstream effects… LeadGenius mitigates this risk with a 94% confirmed contact-to-company match rate—significantly higher than ZoomInfo's 76%… an 89% match rate when combining contact-to-company matches with verified email accuracy, compared to ZoomInfo's meager 54%" (source: https://www.leadgenius.com/resources/the-true-cost-of-ownership-why-leadgenius-outperforms-zoominfo). The article also leans on a "leased data" framing — claiming ZoomInfo customers lose access to data when the contract ends, while LeadGenius customers "retain full ownership of all sourced data." A second LeadGenius-authored comparison page (/resources/zoominfo-alternative) was Cloudflare-blocked and could not be extracted — see flags.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Real-time, compliant B2B contact data — powered by AI + Human-in-the-Loop, with global coverage and flexible delivery — sold as "Precision Data" that is custom-sourced rather than pulled from a static database.
- **Recurring proof points:** "150+ customers" cited on the homepage; LeadGenius's own match-rate, email-accuracy, and enrichment-rate numbers vs. ZoomInfo (94% vs. 76%, 89% vs. 54%, 63% vs. 29%); repeated stat tiles "400+ / 42 / 3 Billion+" appear on every solutions page but their labels were not surfaced in the extracted content — see flags.
- **Tone / category framing:** "Global B2B Contact Data & Buyer Intelligence for Revenue Teams" — explicitly contrasted with "static, one-size-fits-all databases" and framed as a managed data partner with white-glove pods, not self-serve software.
