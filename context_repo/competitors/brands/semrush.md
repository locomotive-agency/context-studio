## Summary

Semrush is an AI-powered digital marketing / SEO and AI-search visibility platform sold to SEOs, content marketers, PPC managers, social/local marketers, and enterprise SEO leadership. Its overlap with ZoomInfo is tangential rather than direct: Semrush operates on search demand and AI-search visibility signals, while ZoomInfo operates on B2B contact, account, and intent data — different ICPs (SEO/digital marketing teams vs B2B GTM teams). The clearest signal that this is not a real GTM-platform competitor: ZoomInfo itself is a Semrush Enterprise customer, with a CRO testimonial featured on the Semrush homepage citing "+373% increase in share of voice" (source: https://www.semrush.com/). Semrush is now an Adobe company per a 2026 acquisition banner, which may push it further into Adobe Experience Cloud / customer-experience territory rather than B2B sales intelligence.

## Product Offerings

### Semrush One

> **Wiki:** [[semrush--semrush-one]]

- **What it does:** Unified subscription combining traditional SEO (rankings, backlinks, site audit) and AI Visibility (LLM rankings, prompt tracking) in one plan.
- **Key features:**
  - Websites to monitor (5/15/40 by tier)
  - Daily Google rankings (500 / 1,500 / 5,000 keywords)
  - Daily AI prompt rankings (50 / 100 / 200 prompts)
  - AI-ready Site Audit
  - Historical SEO data (Pro+ and above)
  - Content optimization and keyword cannibalization analysis
  - MCP access (expanded MCP and API on Advanced)
- **Source URL:** https://www.semrush.com/pricing/semrush-one

### SEO Toolkit (SEO Classic)

> **Wiki:** [[semrush--seo-toolkit]]

- **What it does:** Standalone SEO suite covering keyword research, competitor analysis, position tracking, backlinks, and site audit.
- **Key features:**
  - Keyword research and Keyword Magic Tool (27B+ keywords)
  - Competitor analysis tools and Keyword Gap
  - Position Tracking (500–5,000 keywords daily by tier)
  - Backlinks database (43T+ backlinks)
  - Site Audit (technical SEO)
  - MCP Access
  - API access (Business tier only)
- **Source URL:** https://www.semrush.com/pricing/seo/

### Keyword Research Toolkit

> **Wiki:** [[semrush--keyword-research]]

- **What it does:** Set of 6 tools for SEO keyword discovery, gap analysis, and rank-tracking inputs.
- **Key features:**
  - Keyword Overview (volume, intent, KD, CPC, SERP features)
  - Organic Rankings research
  - Keyword Magic Tool (27B+ keyword toolbox)
  - Keyword Gap (benchmark up to 5 profiles)
  - Keyword Strategy Builder (pillar/subpage planning)
  - Organic Traffic Insights (Google Analytics integration)
- **Source URL:** https://www.semrush.com/features/keyword-research-toolkit

### AI Visibility Toolkit

> **Wiki:** [[semrush--ai-visibility-toolkit]]

- **What it does:** Tracks how a brand appears in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, SearchGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) and benchmarks share of voice vs competitors.
- **Key features:**
  - Prompt-level visibility tracking
  - AI market share and sentiment analysis
  - Brand visibility tracking across AI models
  - Persona-based prompt generation (Enterprise AIO)
  - 239M+ LLM prompts in database (per stats panel)
- **Source URL:** https://www.semrush.com/

### Semrush Enterprise (SEO + AIO + Site Intelligence + Datos + MFour + Insights24)

> **Wiki:** [[semrush--enterprise]]

- **What it does:** Modular enterprise marketing intelligence platform spanning SEO, AI search optimization, large-scale technical site audits, panel/journey data (Datos), surveys (MFour), and social listening (Insights24).
- **Key features:**
  - 25B+ keywords, 43T+ backlinks, 800M+ domains, 100M+ AI prompts
  - Coverage of 140+ markets / 142 geo databases
  - Crawling for up to millions of pages with JS and Shadow DOM support
  - Audits for accessibility, compliance, and QA
  - Daily privacy-safe consumer panel data (Datos)
  - Real-time consumer survey data (MFour)
  - Live social/conversation monitoring across 20+ markets (Insights24)
  - Role-based workspaces and free guest users
- **Source URL:** https://enterprise.semrush.com/

### Other Toolkits (Traffic & Market, Content, Local, Social, Advertising, AI PR)

- **What it does:** Modular toolkits sold separately or via Semrush One; cover traffic/market intelligence, AI content creation, local SEO, social scheduling, paid advertising research, and AI-era PR.
- **Key features:**
  - Traffic & Market: competitor traffic distribution, audience insights
  - Content Toolkit: AI-driven content briefs, scoring, AI Article Generator
  - Local Toolkit: Google Business Profile, GBP AI Agent, Listing Management
  - Social Toolkit: Social Poster, Social Tracker, Social Analytics, influencer discovery
  - Advertising Toolkit: Ads Launch Assistant, PPC Keyword Tool
  - AI PR Toolkit: media discovery, AI press-pitch generation
- **Source URL:** https://www.semrush.com/features/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Semrush sells SEO and AI-search visibility intelligence; none of the reviewed product pages indicate B2B contact data, direct dials, account-level intent, sales engagement, conversation intelligence, or GTM-orchestration surfaces that compete head-to-head with any ZoomInfo product line. (See flag `manual_review:tangential_overlap_only`.)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM/demand gen) ↔ Semrush Enterprise (SEO + AIO + Insights24 + Datos):** both pitch marketing leadership on competitive intelligence and audience insight, but Semrush operates on search/AI-search demand signals and consumer-panel data, while ZoomInfo Marketing operates on B2B account/contact intent and ABM orchestration. ZoomInfo is itself a Semrush Enterprise customer per the homepage testimonial, suggesting these tools are complementary rather than substitutive (source: https://www.semrush.com/).
  - **APIs & MCP ↔ Semrush MCP and API:** Semrush Pro tier ships "MCP Access" and Semrush One Advanced has "expanded MCP and API data access," overlapping the MCP/agent ecosystem framing — though Semrush MCP exposes SEO data, not B2B firmographic / contact data (source: https://www.semrush.com/pricing/seo/).
- **No overlap:** B2B contact and company data (emails, direct dials, technographics); sales prospecting / SDR workflows; conversation intelligence (no Chorus-style call recording or analysis surfaced); GTM Workspace / GTM Studio surfaces (seller and RevOps front-ends). Conversely, all of Semrush's core surfaces — search/AI visibility tracking, GEO, LLM share-of-voice, link building, technical SEO crawls, social posting, local SEO/GBP, AI PR — have no equivalent in ZoomInfo's reviewed product lines (source: https://www.semrush.com/features/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results — Semrush does not publish a `vs ZoomInfo` page (third-party comparisons exist on Software Advice and TrustRadius but not on Semrush's own domain). ZoomInfo's CRO endorses Semrush on the Semrush homepage as a marketing partner, which underlines the non-rivalrous positioning. See flag `vs_brand_missing`.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Be found everywhere search happens — the leading platform to grow and measure brand visibility across virtually every digital channel"; "Your edge to win every search" uniting SEO and AI visibility on 17 years of search intelligence.
- **Recurring proof points:** ZoomInfo homepage testimonial — James Roth, CRO at ZoomInfo: "Semrush for Enterprise has been a game-changer for our marketing teams... +373% increase in share of voice"; Picsart customer story (124% impressions growth, 20% increase in clicks, 4 hrs saved/week); Bajaj Finserv (260% share of voice growth); Square (10X productivity); customer logos including Shopify, Decathlon, Booking.com, P&G, Samsung, FedEx, Amazon, GE, Airbnb, Netflix, TikTok, Dropbox, Salesforce, Hertz, LG, Disney, HSBC.
- **Tone / category framing:** AI-powered digital marketing platform / SEO + AI search visibility platform — explicitly framed as "unifying SEO authority and AI visibility," not as a B2B sales or GTM tool. Post-Adobe acquisition messaging ties Semrush to "AI-driven customer experience" inside Adobe.
