# Breeze Intelligence

- [[breeze-intelligence--enrichment|Breeze Intelligence — Data Enrichment]] — competes with CRM Enrichment

## Summary

Breeze Intelligence is HubSpot's productized rebrand of Clearbit (acquired Dec 2023) — a B2B data-enrichment + buyer-intent layer that sits inside HubSpot's Smart CRM and is gated behind a paid HubSpot Hub subscription. It is sold to marketing operations, RevOps, demand-gen, and sales teams already on HubSpot, and overlaps ZoomInfo's Data foundation, ZoomInfo Marketing (intent + ABM audiences), ZoomInfo Operations (CRM enrichment + routing), and — via the broader Breeze AI surface — GTM Workspace / GTM Studio agent surfaces. Note: this is a sibling overlap with the existing `clearbit.md` dossier — Clearbit was extracted there as a stand-alone product; this dossier captures the post-acquisition HubSpot-native packaging and the new Breeze AI agent surface area. No first-party "Breeze Intelligence vs ZoomInfo" comparison page surfaced in HubSpot-owned content during this run.

## Product Offerings

### Breeze Intelligence — Data Enrichment

- **What it does:** Real-time enrichment of contact and company records inside HubSpot's Smart CRM with firmographic and technographic context — industry, revenue, employee count, technologies installed, job title, location, and social profiles (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/data-enrichment).
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time CRM enrichment, typically within seconds (minutes for larger sets)
  - Over 100 million company domains and 380 million email addresses indexed
  - 100+ B2B data points per company/contact, including technologies installed, employee count, revenue, location
  - Continuous refresh and verification with dynamic quality scoring and human QA
  - Configurable overwrite/keep/blank field handling per attribute
  - Global coverage in any language (parity with US per HubSpot's internal analysis)
- **Source URL:** https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/data-enrichment

### Breeze Intelligence — Buyer Intent (Reveal)

- **What it does:** Identifies which companies visit a customer's website using IP intelligence and surfaces ICP-matched, in-market accounts plus pricing-page visit signals (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit).
- **Key features:**
  - IP intelligence to identify companies visiting the website
  - Visitor dashboard highlighting ICP-matched accounts
  - Pricing-page visit detection and visit frequency
  - Filter by channel source
  - Determine likely product/solution interest based on engagement
- **Source URL:** https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit

### Breeze Intelligence — Form Shortening

- **What it does:** Dynamically shortens marketing forms by hiding fields whose values can be enriched from the email address, increasing conversion while preserving downstream data coverage (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit).
- **Key features:**
  - Dynamic form-field reduction keyed off the visitor's email
  - Auto-fill of enrichable fields so reps still receive complete records
  - Retains scoring and routing fidelity with shorter forms
- **Source URL:** https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit

### Breeze Intelligence — Target Markets / Audience Building

- **What it does:** Builds B2B target-market audiences from HubSpot's company database (formerly Clearbit's), going beyond static lists to find every company that matches ICP criteria for activation (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit).
- **Key features:**
  - Build audiences from global B2B company data
  - Filter by technology tags, employee range, business model, and more
  - Used for ABM list building and ad audience activation
- **Source URL:** https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit

### Breeze Assistant

- **What it does:** An in-product AI companion that pulls from CRM data, the customer's knowledge base, and HubSpot Academy to give role-specific answers and guidance — distinct from Breeze Intelligence but part of the same Breeze AI surface area (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence).
- **Key features:**
  - Embedded across HubSpot desktop and mobile
  - Pulls from CRM data, knowledge base, and HubSpot Academy
  - Role-aware answers (marketing/sales/service)
  - Available alongside custom assistants in Breeze Studio
- **Source URL:** https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence

### Breeze Agents (Customer / Prospecting / Data / Customer Health / Company Research / Closing)

- **What it does:** Specialized AI agents that run end-to-end workflows across marketing, sales, and customer service. Adjacent product line to Breeze Intelligence — both consume HubSpot Credits (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents).
- **Key features:**
  - Customer Agent — resolves customer inquiries across support channels with handoff guardrails
  - Prospecting Agent — surfaces in-market accounts on buying signals, builds contact lists, drafts personalized outreach for rep review
  - Data Agent — answers questions about customers by analyzing CRM data, conversations, documents, and the web
  - Customer Health Agent (Beta) — evaluates account health and surfaces talking points + drafted outreach
  - Company Research Agent (Beta) — pulls insights from a target company's website, recent news, and CRM
  - Closing Agent (Beta) — surfaces deal blockers and engages buyers from approved content
  - Available across Starter, Professional, and Enterprise; Customer/Prospecting/Data agents consume HubSpot Credits
- **Source URL:** https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS / Software (dominant); financial technology (Agicap); education (Morehouse College, Kaplan); customer service organizations (RADD, ResellerRatings).
- **Company size called out:** SMB / Starter, mid-market, Enterprise — HubSpot explicitly tiers by "Small Businesses & Startups" (Starter Customer Platform) and "Enterprises" (Enterprise Customer Platform).
- **Persona / role focus:** Marketing operations, sales operations / RevOps, demand generation, sales reps consuming routed leads + agent-drafted outreach, customer service / support managers, CRM administrators / Smart CRM owners.
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - CRM/contact and company record enrichment
  - Form shortening + form conversion optimization
  - Anonymous-website-visitor identification (buyer intent)
  - ABM target-market list building
  - AI-drafted prospect outreach (via Prospecting Agent)
  - Customer service automation (via Customer Agent)

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** HubSpot Smart CRM (native — Breeze Intelligence is consumed inside HubSpot only).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** HubSpot Sales Hub (native).
- **Data / enrichment:** Self-contained — Breeze Intelligence IS the enrichment surface. Pulls from public info, third-party sources, and the web; 100M+ company domains and 380M email addresses indexed (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/data-enrichment).
- **Other notable integrations:** Marketing Hub (native form-shortening + audience activation); Service Hub (native); Content Hub (native); Data Hub / Data Studio (credit-consuming syncs); HubSpot Marketplace and Breeze marketplace for AI agents; Solutions Partner Program and Technology Partner Program ecosystems (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Built natively into HubSpot's Smart CRM — no separate seat to buy and no integration to maintain. Enrichment, intent, form-shortening, and AI agents all sit in the same workflow surface (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/data-enrichment).
- Inherits Clearbit's data foundation: 100M+ company domains, 380M email addresses indexed, continuously refreshed with dynamic quality scoring and human QA (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/data-enrichment).
- Bundled into the Breeze AI umbrella alongside Breeze Assistant, Breeze Agents, Breeze Studio, and the Breeze marketplace — a single agentic-platform story rather than a stand-alone data SKU (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence).
- Consumption pricing via HubSpot Credits, not per-seat licenses — credits are shared across all usage-based Breeze features and reset monthly (source: https://knowledge.hubspot.com/account-management/understand-hubspot-credits-and-billing).
- Trust-and-safety surface (model cards, AI trust page, opt-out and deletion mechanisms for individuals in the commercial database) is positioned as a differentiator against general-purpose AI tooling (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents).
- Umbrella ROI proof: "129% more leads, 36% more deals, 37% improvement in ticket closure rates" after one year on HubSpot — used as the platform-wide outcome story Breeze inherits (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation (contacts, companies, technographics)** — Breeze Intelligence's enrichment surface (100M+ company domains, 380M email addresses indexed, 100+ attributes per record) competes directly with ZoomInfo's contact + company + technographic data foundation. The job-to-be-done (CRM enrichment of leads/contacts/accounts) is identical (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/data-enrichment).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM, intent, target-account audiences)** — Breeze Intelligence Reveal converts anonymous website visitors into ICP-matched buying signals; Target Markets builds B2B audiences from the same database for ABM activation — same job-to-be-done as ZoomInfo's WebSights/Intent Data + ABM audience surfaces (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing) and ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (AI prospecting)** — Data enrichment "updates, corrects, or even adds information to contact records" and routes via Smart CRM. Prospecting Agent "surfaces in-market accounts based on buying signals, builds contact lists, and equips reps with personalized outreach ready to review and send" — overlapping ZoomInfo Operations' CRM data hygiene plus ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace's AI prospecting (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents).
  - **GTM Workspace and GTM Studio (seller and marketer agent surfaces)** — Breeze Studio is HubSpot's "control center for tailoring AI teammates"; Breeze marketplace is the "discovery layer for AI tools"; agents include Customer, Prospecting, Data, Customer Health, Company Research, Closing — overlapping GTM Workspace (seller agent surface) and GTM Studio (marketer / RevOps agent surface) (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Conversation intelligence (Chorus)** — The Breeze umbrella page lists "Conversation Intelligence — capture insights from customer conversations and use them to coach your team" as a popular feature. Breeze Intelligence proper does not include conversation intelligence, but the broader Breeze/HubSpot suite does (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence).
  - **APIs & MCP** — HubSpot exposes a Developer Tools surface and an ecosystem marketplace; reviewed pages do NOT mention an MCP server for Breeze Intelligence. Adjacent to ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP access lane, but not a direct match (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence/breeze-ai-agents).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Powerful AI, effortlessly simple — Breeze is HubSpot's collection of AI tools, built into the customer platform to help your marketing, sales, and service teams get more done." Breeze Intelligence specifically promises a CRM that "gets smarter" through enrichment, buyer intent, and form shortening (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/artificial-intelligence and https://offers.hubspot.com/emea-meet-breeze-intelligence).
- **Recurring proof points:** 100M+ company domains and 380M email addresses indexed; 288,000+ HubSpot customers in 135+ countries; "129% more leads, 36% more deals, 37% improvement in ticket closure rates" after one year on HubSpot; Agicap saved 750 hours weekly across 150+ sales reps and increased deal velocity 20% with Breeze; Sandler "25% more engagement and 4x sales leads"; Kaplan "30% reduction in customer service response times."
- **Tone / category framing:** HubSpot frames Breeze Intelligence as the data layer that powers the broader Breeze AI surface — explicitly NOT a stand-alone B2B data SKU. The category language is "Smart CRM / agentic customer platform," not generic data terminology. Breeze Intelligence inherits Clearbit's brand legitimacy (logo wall: Greenhouse, Gong, Asana, Frame, Segment) but the post-acquisition pitch is platform consolidation, not best-of-breed data.
