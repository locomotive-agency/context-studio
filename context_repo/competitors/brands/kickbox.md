## Summary

Kickbox is an email verification and deliverability platform for email marketers, lifecycle/CRM managers, and developers — a narrow, single-purpose tool that cleans existing email lists and validates addresses at point-of-capture. Overlap with ZoomInfo is limited to a single dimension: email-address hygiene, where Kickbox competes head-to-head with NeverBounce (a ZoomInfo company) and indirectly with the verified-email layer of ZoomInfo's Data foundation and Operations modules. Kickbox does not source net-new contact data, company firmographics, intent, technographics, or any GTM intelligence, so it does not compete with ZoomInfo Sales, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, or the GTM Context Graph. Pricing is fully public, credit-based pay-as-you-go starting at $5 per 500 verifications.

## Product Offerings

### Email List Verification

> **Wiki:** [[kickbox--list-verification]]

- **What it does:** Bulk cleanup of email lists/databases to reduce bounce rates and protect sender reputation before a send.
- **Key features:**
  - Smart verification classifying emails as Deliverable, Risky (disposable, role-based, accept-all), or Undeliverable
  - Detection of disposable/temporary email domains
  - Detection of role-based addresses (info@, sales@, etc.)
  - Identification of accept-all domains
  - Typo and syntax error detection
  - Average ~1 hour to verify 100,000 addresses
  - Free re-credit when result is "unknown"
- **Source URL:** https://kickbox.com/

### Email Verification API

> **Wiki:** [[kickbox--verification-api]]

- **What it does:** Real-time email verification at the point-of-capture (webforms, signup, mobile app, POS) plus batch verification of up to 1M addresses per job.
- **Key features:**
  - Single Email Verification API returning deliverable/undeliverable/risky/unknown plus reason
  - Batch Verification Job API supporting up to 1,000,000 addresses per job
  - Sendex score (proprietary email-quality metric)
  - Did-you-mean typo suggestions
  - Free Sandbox mode that does not deduct credits
  - Verification Dashboard with real-time response monitoring
  - Auto-Recharge for credit replenishment
  - 95% accuracy guarantee
- **Source URL:** https://kickbox.com/email-verification-api/

### Deliverability Consulting

> **Wiki:** [[kickbox--deliverability-consulting]]

- **What it does:** Human-led service to diagnose and fix email deliverability issues — authentication, DNS, SSL, header structure, and blacklist remediation.
- **Key features:**
  - Email authentication, DNS, SSL, and domain application audits
  - Header structure review
  - Inbox placement and engagement remediation
  - Blacklist avoidance
  - Ongoing strategic guidance and support
- **Source URL:** https://kickbox.com/

### Free Tools (Email Verifier, Spam Checker, Disposable Email Checker, Email Quality Score)

> **Wiki:** [[kickbox--free-tools]]

- **What it does:** Standalone web utilities for ad-hoc email checks, spam testing, and disposable-domain lookups; primarily lead-gen surfaces.
- **Key features:**
  - Single-address Email Verifier (100 free credits per new account)
  - Email Spam Checker / spam-tester
  - Disposable Email Checker
  - Email Quality Score lookup
- **Source URL:** https://kickbox.com/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Sendex score — a proprietary email-quality metric returned alongside deliverability verdict, positioned as more granular than competitors' binary results (source: https://kickbox.com/comparisons/).
- The only email-verification platform on the market to offer professional deliverability consulting — one-on-one expert audits of authentication, DNS, SSL, and headers (source: https://kickbox.com/comparisons/).
- Trusted by ESPs and respected M3AAWG member; explicitly positions against vendors who "broker email addresses for spam" (source: https://kickbox.com/comparisons/).
- Transparent pay-as-you-go pricing with no minimums; credits refunded for "unknown" results (source: https://kickbox.com/pricing/).
- Over 26 billion emails verified; uses 40+ billion email signals daily for accuracy (source: https://kickbox.com/pricing/).
- Free Sandbox mode for API testing without deducting paid credits; setup in under 5 minutes (source: https://kickbox.com/email-verification-api/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Kickbox's Email List Verification and Email Verification API compete head-to-head with NeverBounce (a ZoomInfo company) and adjacently with the email-verification component of **ZoomInfo Operations** and the verified-email layer of ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1)** foundation. Both vendors classify addresses as deliverable, risky, or invalid (source: https://kickbox.com/comparisons/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Kickbox functions as a hygiene layer for **ZoomInfo Marketing**-style send programs by feeding clean lists into Marketo, HubSpot, Klaviyo, Braze, Iterable, and Customer.io. This intersects ZoomInfo's "200M+ verified business emails" data-quality positioning, but Kickbox only validates emails the customer already has — it does not source new contacts (source: https://kickbox.com/email-marketing/).
- **No overlap:** Net-new B2B contact data, company firmographics, technographics across 30K+ technologies, intent data, conversation intelligence (**Chorus**), ABM orchestration, sales engagement, prioritized-account execution (**GTM Workspace**), natural-language audience building (**GTM Studio**), and the **GTM Context Graph** reasoning layer. None of these capabilities appear on any reviewed Kickbox page (source: https://kickbox.com/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No dedicated Kickbox-vs-ZoomInfo page exists. The only direct callout is a one-line jab on the comparisons page targeting NeverBounce: "GDPR compliant & CCPA Compliant, but owned by data broker ZoomInfo" (source: https://kickbox.com/comparisons/). It is a trust/reputation attack rather than a feature comparison.

- **Recurring proof points:** 150,000+ senders, 26B emails verified, 40B daily email signals, 95% API accuracy guarantee (99.5% claimed for marketing tier), customer logos including Reddit, Kayak, Capital One, HelloFresh, Burberry, Sonos, Zynga, Expedia, Delivery Hero, and case-study metrics (Reddit: 12M undeliverables identified; The Zebra: 98.7% delivery rate).
