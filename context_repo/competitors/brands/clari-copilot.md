# Clari Copilot

## Sub-products

- [[clari-copilot--conversation-intelligence|Clari Copilot (Conversation Intelligence & Coaching)]] — competes with Chorus
- [[clari-copilot--call-recording|Clari Copilot Sales Call Recording]] — competes with Chorus

## Summary

Clari Copilot is a conversation intelligence and sales-coaching module (formerly Wingman) sold to VP/Director of Sales, sales-enablement, and RevOps buyers as part of Clari's Revenue Orchestration Platform. Its scope is call recording, transcription, real-time battlecards, deal-risk signals from calls, and CRM auto-capture — putting it head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Chorus (and the conversation-context capture pillar of the GTM Context Graph). Reviewers consistently choose Copilot over Chorus and Gong on price and ease-of-onboarding rather than on feature breadth, while admitting feature parity at the CI core. Copilot does not compete with ZoomInfo's data foundation, prospecting universe, marketing/ABM, or APIs/MCP — it is a sales-only motion layered on top of the customer's first-party calls.

## Product Offerings

### Clari Copilot (Conversation Intelligence & Coaching)

> **Wiki:** [[clari-copilot--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered conversation intelligence platform that records, transcribes, and analyzes every sales call, then feeds buyer signals into Clari's revenue process for forecasting, coaching, and CRM auto-capture (source: https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/).
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time call recording and transcription (audio + video) with post-call review at up to 2x playback
  - Live battlecards / monologue alerts that prompt reps in-call to navigate objections, competitor mentions, and pricing pushback
  - Smart Call Summaries with AI-generated notes, action items, follow-up emails, and key moments (pricing, pain points, next steps)
  - Game Tapes — curated playlists of winning sales calls used for onboarding and async coaching ("A Netflix for sales")
  - Call Scorecards and team performance metrics (talk/listen ratio, longest monologue, interactivity, engaging questions)
  - Deal Central — early-warning system for at-risk deals using buyer signals from calls
  - Automatic CRM activity capture (intent, objections, contacts, next steps) feeding back to Salesforce / HubSpot / Pipedrive
  - Smart Chapters and Smart Playbooks that surface objections and reinforce winning behavior across the team
- **Source URL:** https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/

### Clari Copilot Sales Call Recording

> **Wiki:** [[clari-copilot--call-recording]]

- **What it does:** The call-capture layer of Copilot — automatically records, transcribes, and analyzes every sales call (audio and video) and stores them in a searchable cloud library for sharing and coaching (source: https://www.clari.com/call-recording-software/).
- **Key features:**
  - Automatic recording of every video and audio sales call
  - Searchable call logs with filters by category, topic, CRM deal stage, and call duration
  - AI-identified key moments — pricing, pain points, next steps — auto-tagged in transcripts
  - Shareable recordings and call snippets via direct link for async coaching
  - Call analytics: talk/listen ratio, longest monologue, rep interactivity, engaging questions
  - 2x speed playback for fast review
- **Source URL:** https://www.clari.com/call-recording-software/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Real-time battlecards and monologue alerts during the live call rather than post-call only — "Win while the call is happening, not after it's over." (source: https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/)
- Tight coupling to Clari's Revenue Orchestration Platform — Copilot signals feed directly into pipeline inspection, forecasting, and Deal Central rather than living as a standalone CI silo (source: https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/).
- Cost-positioned as the affordable CI option — TrustRadius reviewers repeatedly cite "much cheaper than competitors" (Gong, Chorus) as the deciding factor (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/chorus-ai-vs-wingman).
- Game Tapes — curated "Netflix-for-sales" playlists of winning calls; cited as cutting new-rep ramp from 1 month to 2 weeks (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/chorus-ai-vs-wingman).
- TrustRadius score 9.1/10 across 120 reviews; "100% would buy again" and "100% delivers good value for the price" on the comparison page versus Chorus 95% / 96% (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/chorus-ai-vs-wingman).
- Free trial available (Chorus has none per the same listing); no seat minimum cited by some reviewers — easier entry point for SMB / first-time buyers (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/chorus-ai-vs-wingman).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Clari Copilot competes head-to-head with **Chorus** in the Conversation Intelligence category — both record/transcribe/analyze sales calls, ship coaching playlists, surface deal-risk signals, and auto-capture call activity to CRM (TrustRadius runs the head-to-head explicitly: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/chorus-ai-vs-wingman). Copilot's pitch — "Buyer signals that actually inform your revenue process… Copilot captures buyer signals and sends them directly into Clari to sharpen forecasting and pipeline inspection" — directly contests ZoomInfo's **GTM Context Graph** conversation-context-capture pillar (source: https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Copilot sits next to Clari's Groove (sales engagement) and Guide (AI Action Hub) modules in the Sales Engagement & Productivity solution — collectively encroaching on **GTM Workspace's** seller-front-end "next-best-move" surface (source: https://www.clari.com/solutions/ai-sales-engagement-productivity). Copilot's CRM auto-capture of intent/objections/contacts/next steps is also adjacent to **ZoomInfo Operations'** broader CRM data-quality scope (source: https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/).
- **No overlap:** Copilot ships **no third-party B2B contact/company database, no intent signals, no technographics, and no prospecting universe** of its own — it is purely first-party (the customer's own calls). Clari leans on partner data (ZoomInfo and LinkedIn surfaced inside Groove) for prospect context (source: https://www.clari.com/solutions/ai-sales-engagement-productivity). No Copilot-specific API or MCP server is documented on the reviewed pages, and there is no marketing/ABM or developer/agent-builder ICP — areas ZoomInfo serves through GTM Studio, ZoomInfo Marketing, and APIs & MCP.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No Clari-authored vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced in SERPs. The closest signal is the third-party TrustRadius head-to-head, where reviewers repeatedly chose Wingman/Copilot over Chorus on price and ease: _"Wingman, from what we could tell, has very similar functionality to both of these tools but at a much lower price point"_ and _"Wingman is much more comparable to Gong and was so easy to onboard, integrate, and get up and running."_ On feature parity one reviewer noted, _"each of the competitors didn't have wildly different offerings… we really just needed a tool to help with a share of voice and rep training"_ (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/chorus-ai-vs-wingman). See flag `vs_brand_missing`.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "AI-driven Conversation Intelligence — Conversations that win more deals. Every call is revenue waiting to be unlocked. Clari Copilot gives sellers the AI edge to win deals faster and leaders the visibility to run revenue smarter." (source: https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/)
- **Recurring proof points:** TrustRadius 9.1/10 across 120 reviews; 100% would buy again; 100% delivers good value for the price; customer logos including Hitachi Vantara, Genesys, Iron Mountain, Informatica, New Relic, Confluent, Fortinet, UKG, UiPath; quoted outcome "increase win rates by 20%+ with real-time coaching and predictive insights"; UiPath outcome of "15% fewer slipped deals, 10% higher renewals" using the broader Clari platform; parent-Clari pricing page proof points: "448% Return on Investment," 30% increase in first meetings booked, 15% faster deal cycles, 22% reduction in rep onboarding time.
