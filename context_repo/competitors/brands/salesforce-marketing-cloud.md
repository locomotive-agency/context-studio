## Summary

Salesforce Marketing Cloud is an enterprise marketing cloud and B2B marketing automation suite (Marketing Cloud Engagement, Account Engagement / Pardot, Personalization, and Marketing Intelligence) sold to B2B marketers, demand gen, marketing ops, and RevOps inside the broader Salesforce Customer 360 platform. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio on lead scoring, ABM dashboards, and audience activation, but operates on first-party CRM and engagement data — it does not sell a third-party B2B contact database, intent data, or conversation intelligence, so it is structurally complementary to ZoomInfo's data foundation and GTM Context Graph rather than a full substitute. Salesforce now markets the product line as "Agentforce Marketing" and pushes "+ editions" (Account Engagement+, Engagement+, Personalization+, Intelligence+) that layer Agentforce autonomous agents onto existing subscriptions. No dedicated Salesforce-authored vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced; the two are most often deployed together (ZoomInfo data feeding Salesforce activation). Sibling page to `competitors/salesforce-sales-cloud.md`, `competitors/salesforce-agentforce.md`, and `competitors/salesforce-einstein.md`.

## Product Offerings

### Marketing Cloud Account Engagement (formerly Pardot)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--account-engagement]]

- **What it does:** B2B marketing automation platform built on Salesforce CRM that aligns marketing and sales to generate, nurture, score, and convert leads into pipeline.
- **Key features:**
  - AI-powered lead scoring and grading (Einstein Lead Scoring, Behavior Scoring)
  - Engagement Programs / lead nurturing with automation rules
  - Email marketing, forms, landing pages, and dynamic content
  - B2B Marketing Analytics with Multi-Touch Attribution and Einstein Attribution
  - Account-Based Marketing dashboards and account-based scoring
  - Native Salesforce CRM sync (leads, contacts, opportunities, campaigns)
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/b2b-automation/

### Account Engagement+ (next-gen B2B add-on)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--account-engagement]]

- **What it does:** A "+" edition that layers Agentforce campaign creation and next-gen Marketing Cloud capabilities on top of an existing Account Engagement subscription.
- **Key features:**
  - Agentforce Campaign Creation (natural-language briefs, content, segments, journeys)
  - Includes Marketing Cloud Growth or Advanced
  - Lead nurturing and scoring
  - B2B Marketing Analytics
  - Einstein content generation for email copy, subject lines, forms, and landing pages
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/b2b-automation/pricing/

### Marketing Cloud Next (Growth & Advanced Editions)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--marketing-cloud-next]]

- **What it does:** Agentic, cross-channel marketing automation platform built natively on the Salesforce platform, designed for both B2B and B2C journeys with autonomous AI agents.
- **Key features:**
  - Agentforce Campaign Creation with autonomous AI agents
  - Multi-channel journeys (email, SMS, WhatsApp, web)
  - Forms and landing pages
  - Path Experimentation (Advanced Edition)
  - Two-way / Unified Conversations for SMS and WhatsApp
  - Distributed Marketing and Alerts; Engagement History dashboards
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/marketing-cloud-editions/pricing

### Marketing Cloud Engagement (legacy MCE / B2C cross-channel)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--engagement]]

- **What it does:** Scale and automation engine for email, mobile, and web journeys; the legacy ExactTarget-derived B2C side of Marketing Cloud, now sold as Pro+ / Corporate+ "+" editions to layer agentic capabilities.
- **Key features:**
  - Bulk email at scale (Pro+ tier ships 2.5M email messages, 15K contacts)
  - Mobile (SMS, push) and web messaging
  - Journey Orchestration
  - Data Cloud credits and Data 360 unification
  - Agentforce campaign creation when on a "+" edition
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/engagement/pricing

### Salesforce Personalization (formerly Marketing Cloud Personalization / Interaction Studio)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--personalization]]

- **What it does:** Real-time 1:1 cross-channel personalization with autonomous decisioning across web, mobile app, and email.
- **Key features:**
  - Agentforce Personalization Decisioning
  - Web, mobile app, and email personalization
  - ML & rules-based product and content recommendations
  - Experimentation
  - Unified customer profile, 50M personalization credits at base tier
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/personalization/pricing

### Marketing Intelligence (analytics)

> **Wiki:** [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--marketing-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Agentic marketing analytics that connects, harmonizes, and visualizes campaign and paid media data to optimize ROI.
- **Key features:**
  - Agentforce Paid Media Optimization
  - AI campaign summaries
  - Data enrichment and out-of-the-box visualizations
  - Automated data management (Intelligence+ tier)
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/analytics/pricing

- Agentforce autonomous AI agents that build briefs, content, segments, and journeys from natural-language prompts (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/)
- Gartner positioned Salesforce as a Leader in the 2025 Magic Quadrant for B2B Marketing Automation Platforms (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/b2b-automation/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM) / GTM Studio ↔ Marketing Cloud Account Engagement + Account Engagement+** — Account Engagement positions itself as "the only marketing automation platform built on the world's #1 CRM" and explicitly markets ABM dashboards, account scoring, and lead nurture, the same buying center ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio targets for marketers, demand gen, and RevOps audience-building and play orchestration (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/b2b-automation/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (demand-gen / lead scoring) ↔ Account Engagement Einstein Lead Scoring & Grading + Multi-Touch Attribution** — Salesforce pitches AI-powered lead scoring and grading with attribution and ROI reporting as the way to align marketing and sales, directly competing with ZoomInfo Marketing's audience scoring and prioritization workflows (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/lead-generation/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end) ↔ Marketing Cloud Engagement + Distributed Marketing and Alerts** — MCE / Engagement Pro+ surfaces sales alerts and distributed marketing flows that nudge sellers off marketing engagement signals; adjacent to ZoomInfo's seller workflow but driven by first-party engagement data, not third-party B2B intelligence (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/engagement/pricing).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (data quality + routing) ↔ Salesforce Data Cloud / Data 360** — Data 360 unifies first-party customer data inside Salesforce and powers Account Engagement's segmentation; complementary to but adjacent with ZoomInfo Operations' role of cleansing, enriching, and routing CRM records (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/).
- **No overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ phone numbers, technographics across 30K+ technologies)** — Salesforce Marketing Cloud and Account Engagement do not sell a third-party B2B contact/company database. They activate first-party data captured via forms, journeys, and CRM and require an external provider (often ZoomInfo) to fill the top-of-funnel data gap (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/b2b-automation/).
  - **ZoomInfo GTM Context Graph / intent / Chorus conversation intelligence** — Reviewed pages do not surface intent data, third-party buying signals, or conversation intelligence as Marketing Cloud capabilities. Salesforce's "context" is first-party engagement history within Data Cloud (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/).
  - **ZoomInfo APIs & MCP server for AI agents** — Salesforce offers Agentforce/Agent Builder for building agents on Salesforce data, but reviewed pages do not surface a comparable third-party B2B data API or MCP endpoint for outside tools to consume contact/company data (source: https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Only third-party head-to-heads (SoftwareReviews, SoftwareAdvice) appeared; Salesforce does not author a vs-ZoomInfo page because the two products are largely complementary (activation layer vs data layer).
