## Summary

Oliv AI is an AI-native revenue intelligence platform selling specialized AI agents (Deal Driver, Forecaster, Coach, Researcher, Meeting/Deal Assistant, CRM Manager, Pipeline Tracker) to sales managers, AEs, and RevOps. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace (seller front-end), Chorus (conversation intelligence), and ZoomInfo Operations (CRM hygiene), but does not compete on B2B contact/company data — it integrates with ZoomInfo and SalesIntel for prospecting. Pricing is modular and transparent, starting at $19/user/month, positioned as the agentic alternative to Gong + Clari rather than a database play.

## Product Offerings

### Oliv Revenue Intelligence Platform

> **Wiki:** [[oliv-ai--platform]]

- **What it does:** AI-native revenue intelligence layer that connects to CRM, email, calendar, calls, and meetings to capture deal data and serve as the data layer Oliv's AI agents act on.
- **Key features:**
  - Tracks 100+ data points per deal aligned to sales methodologies (MEDDIC, SPICED, BANT)
  - Auto-generated Deal Scorecard previewing deal health and obstacles
  - Buying Committee map with stakeholder pain points and objections
  - Imports CRM stage process, entry/exit criteria, and qualification rules and automates them
  - Two-way CRM integration (bidirectional sync with Salesforce, HubSpot)
  - Runs 100+ models trained specifically on revenue data
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/sales

### Deal Driver (AI agent for sales managers)

> **Wiki:** [[oliv-ai--deal-driver]]

- **What it does:** Tracks deals, flags risks, sends pipeline updates, and loops sales managers in at key moments.
- **Key features:**
  - Morning brief correlating rep calendars with deal stages
  - Attends meetings and flags deal updates throughout the day
  - Daily next-step reminders pushed to reps
  - Sunset summary recapping the day's meeting outcomes
  - Weekly pipeline update to sales managers
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/blog/oliv-ai-agents-for-sales-teams

### Forecaster (AI agent)

> **Wiki:** [[oliv-ai--forecaster]]

- **What it does:** Builds an accurate, logic-driven forecast by analyzing each opportunity's activity, free from optimism bias.
- **Key features:**
  - Updates the "core four" (deal stage, forecast stage, close date, amount) after every meeting
  - Weekly forecast review with reps every Thursday
  - Weekly forecast one-pager delivered Monday morning
  - Roll-up and aggregated forecasting
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/blog/oliv-ai-agents-for-sales-teams

### Coach (AI agent)

> **Wiki:** [[oliv-ai--coach]]

- **What it does:** Creates personalized coaching plans for each rep and helps managers set weekly and monthly focus areas.
- **Key features:**
  - Evaluates four skills: conversational skills, emotional intelligence, meeting management, deal management
  - Per-deal coaching plan generation
  - Weekly rep one-pager with skill analysis and improvement plan
  - Monthly manager one-pager per rep with prescribed action plan
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/blog/oliv-ai-agents-for-sales-teams

### Researcher / Research Assist (AI agent)

- **What it does:** Produces deep account dossiers with buyer pain points, decision maps, and tailored outreach in minutes.
- **Key features:**
  - Aggregates company and contact insights from multiple sources
  - Highlights gaps in account data for the rep to fill
  - Builds account maps in ~15 minutes
  - Extracts insights from rep-provided source material with one click
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/blog/oliv-ai-agents-for-sales-teams

### Meeting Assistant / Deal Assistant (AI agent)

> **Wiki:** [[oliv-ai--meeting-assistant]]

- **What it does:** Automates meeting prep, live note-taking, action-item assignment, and follow-up emails.
- **Key features:**
  - Live in-meeting note-taking
  - Automated meeting prep briefs
  - Action-item assignment to reps
  - Auto-drafted follow-up emails
  - Real-time Answer Assistant during calls
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/pricing

### CRM Manager (AI agent)

> **Wiki:** [[oliv-ai--crm-manager]]

- **What it does:** Auto-creates and enriches accounts/contacts, updates fields, and creates deals based on qualification criteria — keeping CRM hygiene end-to-end.
- **Key features:**
  - Auto-creation of accounts and contacts from conversations
  - Field-level updates from meeting/email signals
  - Deal creation triggered by qualification criteria
  - Bidirectional sync with Salesforce and HubSpot
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/pricing

### Pipeline Tracker (AI agent)

- **What it does:** Calls reps every evening to update their pipeline hands-free; syncs notes, dates, and stages back to CRM instantly.
- **Key features:**
  - Voice-driven pipeline updates
  - Logs unrecorded meetings with rep voice context
  - Instant CRM sync of notes, dates, and stages
- **Source URL:** https://www.oliv.ai/pricing

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- AI-native architecture: "runs 100+ models trained specifically on revenue data, so every signal is structured, not just summarized" (source: https://www.oliv.ai/)
- Process Graph encodes ICP, qualification rules, and approval flows so agents act with company-specific context, not as generic LLMs (source: https://www.oliv.ai/)
- Modular agent purchasing — "Add agents one at a time, where the ROI is clearest. No buying the whole suite on day one. Start at $19/user/month." (source: https://www.oliv.ai/)
- In-house GTM Engineers (forward-deployed) handle deployment and configuration — no SIs, no vendor risk (source: https://www.oliv.ai/)
- Free migration from Gong, Avoma, Fireflies, or Clari with full data transfer — recordings, transcripts, notes, metadata (source: https://www.oliv.ai/pricing)
- Voice-first pipeline updates — Pipeline Tracker calls reps each evening to capture pipeline hands-free (source: https://www.oliv.ai/pricing)

  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence)** vs Oliv Meeting Insights pack + Meeting Assistant — Custom Meeting AI Scorecards, Real-time Answer Assistant, talk-pattern skills, auto call scoring, and Topic Intelligence are direct CI features (source: https://www.oliv.ai/pricing).

- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph** vs Oliv's Process Graph + 100+ revenue-tuned models — Oliv's Process Graph encodes ICP, qualification rules, and approval flows for agents, mirroring ZoomInfo's "reasoning across CRM + conversation + intent + behavioral data" narrative without the underlying third-party B2B data scale (source: https://www.oliv.ai/).
