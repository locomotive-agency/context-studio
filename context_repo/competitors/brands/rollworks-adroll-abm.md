# RollWorks (AdRoll ABM)

## Sub-products

- [[rollworks-adroll-abm--abm-platform|AdRoll ABM (formerly RollWorks)]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing
- [[rollworks-adroll-abm--adroll-dsp|AdRoll (Cross-Channel DSP)]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing
- [[rollworks-adroll-abm--privacy-consent|Privacy & Consent / Cookie Consent Banner]] — competes with ZoomInfo Operations

## Summary

RollWorks is an account-based marketing and advertising platform sold to B2B demand-gen, ABM, and lifecycle marketers — and as of August 2025 it was officially rebranded to AdRoll ABM under parent NextRoll, with rollworks.com redirecting most product traffic to adroll.com. The product fuses ABM intelligence (an 18M-company B2B database, ICP/Journey-Stage modeling, intent signals via Bombora and G2) with a cross-channel demand-side platform that activates display, native, video, mobile, social, and connected-TV ads against target accounts. This puts RollWorks in direct overlap with ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM workflows and audience activation, partial overlap with ZoomInfo's data-foundation pitch (though at materially smaller scale: 92M contacts vs ZoomInfo's 500M), and outside ZoomInfo's footprint on programmatic ad execution and in-product cookie-consent management. No public RollWorks-vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced in research. This dossier preserves the legacy RollWorks framing as a duplicate input — see also `competitors/adroll-abm.md` for the unified AdRoll umbrella view.

## Product Offerings

### AdRoll ABM (formerly RollWorks)

> **Wiki:** [[rollworks-adroll-abm--abm-platform]]

- **What it does:** Account-based marketing and advertising platform that connects buyer intelligence to multi-channel advertising so B2B marketers can identify, target, engage, and measure high-fit accounts in one application.
- **Key features:**
  - Proprietary database of 18 million vetted B2B companies for ICP modeling and account-list creation (source: https://help.rollworks.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041349152-What-is-Account-Based-Marketing).
  - Buyer-insights engine built on "trillions of proprietary data points," 2.6 billion digital identities, and 92 million contacts (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
  - Command Center that tracks buyer signals in real time and recommends next best steps — add to CRM, build account/contact lists, send priority lists to sales.
  - Journey Stages tracking (Unaware, Aware, Engaged, Opportunity-to-Revenue) with intent + engagement signal classification.
  - InIQ AI buyer-insight engine and BidIQ AI bid-optimization engine (NextRoll trademarks) powering audience selection and ad bidding.
  - Cross-channel ad activation across display, native, video, mobile, social, and connected TV from one platform.
  - Predictive intent targeting using firmographic, technographic, job-title, search-keyword, and ICP-fit signals plus partner intent from G2 and Bombora.
  - Revenue Impact Report and Advertising Engagement Report tying ABM/ad activity to pipeline and closed-won revenue.
- **Source URL:** https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform

### AdRoll (digital advertising platform)

> **Wiki:** [[rollworks-adroll-abm--adroll-dsp]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered, full-funnel cross-channel advertising platform sold alongside AdRoll ABM for retargeting, brand awareness, and conversion campaigns across the open web and walled-garden social.
- **Key features:**
  - Cross-channel campaign management (display, native, video, mobile, social, connected TV) from a single automation builder.
  - AI lookalike, contextual, third-party (2,000+ syndicated audiences), and first-party CRM/website/ecommerce audience targeting.
  - Retargeting and brand-awareness campaigns with monthly recurring ad credits in paid packages.
  - Cross-channel attribution add-on tying conversions back to channels and journeys.
  - Social ads add-on covering Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Pinterest from the same DSP.
  - Self-service or managed-service delivery models with strategic and creative support.
- **Source URL:** https://www.adroll.com/solutions/cross-channel-advertising

### Privacy & Consent / Cookie Consent Banner

> **Wiki:** [[rollworks-adroll-abm--privacy-consent]]

- **What it does:** Built-in TCFv2.0-approved cookie consent management platform plus AdRoll's cookieless / Privacy-Sandbox-tested AI bidding strategy.
- **Key features:**
  - TCFv2.0-approved Consent Management Platform (IAB EU framework compliant).
  - AI bidding engine re-engineered for cookieless contexts; tested in Google Privacy Sandbox PAAPI and ARA.
  - Interoperability with addressability solutions like LiveRamp RampID and 33Across Lexicon ID.
  - Partnerships with Audigent and Experian to enrich first- and third-party targeting data.
- **Source URL:** https://www.adroll.com/features/consent-management

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- "AdRoll ABM seamlessly combines a precise B2B audience segmentation and targeting solution with a cross-channel demand-side platform into a single application" — ABM intelligence plus an in-house DSP, powered by InIQ and BidIQ (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/account-based-marketing).
- Ad-led, advertising-first ABM positioning: "Advertising is your largest budget item, and we know ads better than anyone" — competes from a DSP/programmatic heritage rather than a sales-data heritage (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
- All-in pricing claim: "Most ABM platforms tack on additional fees for audiences, integrations, and measurement capabilities. Our packages include everything you need…for one price" (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
- Walled-garden + open-web orchestration: "Most DSPs are unable to target users across the open web to social media sites — but we're not most DSPs" — unified targeting across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest plus open programmatic (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/social-web-orchestration).
- Internal performance claims: "5X more ROI than other ABM platforms" and "find accounts that are 4X more likely to convert" (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
- Privacy-first AI bidding tested in Google Privacy Sandbox (PAAPI, ARA) plus a TCFv2.0 cookie consent banner shipped in-product (source: https://www.adroll.com/features/consent-management).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** AdRoll ABM competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo Marketing (legacy ABM module) and the ABM workflows inside GTM Studio — both run account targeting, intent-based account scoring, journey-stage tracking, and sales hand-offs, with AdRoll ABM additionally activating programmatic and social ads (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform). The 18M-company / 92M-contact / 2.6B-identity data foundation also positions AdRoll ABM as a smaller-scale alternative to ZoomInfo's data foundation pitch (source: https://help.rollworks.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041349152-What-is-Account-Based-Marketing). Intent signals via Bombora and G2 directly compete with ZoomInfo's proprietary intent layer (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/cross-channel-advertising).
- **Adjacent overlap:** AdRoll ABM Workflows and the Command Center next-best-action engine orchestrate audience and account hand-offs across Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Pardot, and Eloqua — adjacent to ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio orchestration but anchored on ad campaigns rather than seller workflow (source: https://help.rollworks.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002360631-Integrations).
- **No overlap:** AdRoll runs its own programmatic / cross-channel demand-side platform (DSP) across display, native, video, mobile, CTV, plus walled-garden social — ZoomInfo does not operate an ad bidder/DSP (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/cross-channel-advertising). AdRoll also ships an in-product TCFv2.0 cookie consent CMP that ZoomInfo's GTM platform does not include (source: https://www.adroll.com/features/consent-management). The sibling AdRoll product sells into B2C — Retail, Ecommerce, Hospitality, Finance, D2C, Nonprofit — well outside ZoomInfo's B2B-only GTM scope (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/account-based-marketing). And there is no equivalent of Chorus / ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace seller surface; AdRoll ABM only integrates with Outreach and Salesloft as third-party endpoints.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The account-based marketing platform built to scale." AdRoll ABM (formerly RollWorks) connects data, teams, and tools so B2B marketers can speed sales cycles and close high-value deals — with multi-channel ads as the activation layer (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
- **Recurring proof points:** Snowflake (50% new opportunity rate; 75% lift in SDR-booked meetings; 3X meeting rate); Total Expert (93% of priority SAM accounts reached; 100% closed-won attribution); PitchBook (CPC down 37.9%; sales cycle 14.9% faster); G2 badges — Leader, Easiest Setup, Users Love Us, Best Estimated ROI, High Performer; 33 published case studies on FeaturedCustomers (Honeywell, Coupa, Dialpad, NS1, Blackbaud, Aircall, Personify) (sources: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/account-based-marketing, https://www.featuredcustomers.com/vendor/adRoll-abm/case-studies).
- **Tone / category framing:** Positions itself as "an account-based marketing and advertising solution" that fuses ABM intelligence with a B2B-focused DSP — explicitly not a contact-data vendor. Heavy emphasis on multi-channel advertising as the differentiator vs ABM-only or data-only competitors.
