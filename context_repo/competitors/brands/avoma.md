# Avoma

## Sub-products

- [[avoma--ai-meeting-assistant|AI Meeting Assistant]] — competes with Chorus

## Summary

Avoma is an all-in-one AI meeting platform sold to mid-market sales, RevOps, and customer success teams that bundles an AI Meeting Assistant with modular Conversation Intelligence, Revenue Intelligence, and Scheduler/Lead Router add-ons. Its conversation-derived "sales intelligence" framing puts it in direct overlap with ZoomInfo's Chorus module on call recording, AI scorecards, coaching, deal risk, and AI-assisted forecasting (source: https://www.avoma.com/conversation-intelligence, https://www.avoma.com/revenue-intelligence). Pricing is fully public with a recorder-only seat model and unlimited free viewers — Avoma's pricing page calls out Chorus by name and frames itself as the "fair, flexible, and affordable" alternative to "$120/seat, min 3 years" enterprise CI tools (source: https://www.avoma.com/pricing). Avoma sells no third-party B2B data, ABM, or MCP/agent-builder surfaces, so it does not touch ZoomInfo's Data foundation, GTM Studio, or APIs/MCP layers.

## Product Offerings

### AI Meeting Assistant

> **Wiki:** [[avoma--ai-meeting-assistant]]

- **What it does:** End-to-end AI meeting assistant that automates recording, transcription, AI-generated notes, follow-up emails, and CRM updates across 70+ languages.
- **Key features:**
  - Automatic video recording across Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, WebEx, GoToMeeting (bot-less native + bot-based)
  - Real-time transcription in 70+ languages with multi-speaker identification
  - AI summary notes with key takeaways, action items, and 40+ default Smart Topics
  - 10+ default note templates (Demo, MEDDIC, SPICED, SPIN, 1:1) plus custom templates
  - AI-generated follow-up emails sent from native email integration
  - Auto-save notes to CRM records (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive, Copper)
  - "Ask Avoma" AI Copilot at meeting and global level
  - AI-generated Smart Chapters and topic-detection bookmarks
- **Source URL:** https://www.avoma.com/

### Conversation Intelligence (add-on)

> **Wiki:** [[avoma--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Add-on that automates call scoring, real-time agent assist, and coaching analytics on top of the meeting assistant.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Scorecards with auto-scoring for 100% call coverage
  - Real-time Answer Assistant with battle-tested Answer Cards for objection handling
  - AI coaching recommendations and coaching scorecards
  - Custom Smart Trackers (semantic) and Custom Keyword Trackers
  - Talk-pattern insights (talk time, talk speed, filler words, sentiment heatmap)
  - Smart phrase tracking with email/Slack alerts for deal/churn risk and feature requests
  - Member and Scorecard Performance Trend Dashboards
  - Top Themes (questions, pain points, feature requests)
- **Source URL:** https://www.avoma.com/conversation-intelligence

### Revenue Intelligence (add-on)

> **Wiki:** [[avoma--revenue-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** AI-driven pipeline review, deal risk alerts, sales methodology tracking, forecasting, and win-loss analysis.
- **Key features:**
  - Dealboard for pipeline review with 2-way CRM field updates
  - AI-powered Deal and Churn Risk Alerts using conversation sentiment + CRM engagement
  - AI Sales Methodology Tracker (MEDDIC, SPICED, Sandler, BANT, custom)
  - Roll-up & aggregated forecasting with deal health scores
  - AI Win-Loss Deal Analysis across meetings, calls, emails
  - Pipeline and forecasting reports (high-risk pipeline, weekly/monthly movement)
  - Revenue "Ask Avoma" Copilot across CRM Deal & Account conversations
- **Source URL:** https://www.avoma.com/revenue-intelligence

### Scheduler & Lead Router

> **Wiki:** [[avoma--scheduler]]

- **What it does:** Sales call scheduling software with instant booking links, no-show reminders, calendar sync, CRM activity logging, and lead-routing rules.
- **Key features:**
  - Unlimited 1:1, group, and round-robin scheduling pages
  - Two-way sync with Google Calendar and Outlook
  - Multi-step automated reminder sequences with one-click rescheduling
  - Auto-log every booking, reschedule, cancellation in HubSpot and Salesforce
  - Embeddable scheduling pages for emails, websites, signatures
  - Advanced routing rules, inbound form qualification, outbound SDR-to-AE handoff (Lead Router add-on)
  - Weighted distribution and availability-based lead assignment
- **Source URL:** https://www.avoma.com/product/scheduler

- Public, self-service pricing across all tiers with a 14-day no-credit-card trial that includes all add-ons — explicit jab at "Talk-to-Sales only" enterprise CI tools (source: https://www.avoma.com/pricing).
- Native Scheduler + Lead Router replaces ChiliPiper-class point solutions inside the same platform (source: https://www.avoma.com/product/scheduler).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Avoma's Conversation Intelligence add-on overlaps directly with **Chorus** on call recording/transcription, AI scorecards, real-time agent assist, talk-pattern insights, and theme tracking (source: https://www.avoma.com/conversation-intelligence). Avoma's Revenue Intelligence add-on overlaps with ZoomInfo's seller-side deal intelligence — Chorus deal-level insights and the **GTM Workspace** deal/forecast surface — on pipeline review, deal-risk alerts, AI-assisted forecasting, win-loss analysis, and an "Ask Avoma" deal copilot (source: https://www.avoma.com/revenue-intelligence). Avoma's pricing page compares itself by name against Chorus on cost and feature parity.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Avoma's Scheduler & Lead Router (inbound form qualification, weighted distribution, SDR-to-AE handoff) is adjacent to **GTM Studio / ZoomInfo Operations** routing logic, but Avoma's framing is scheduler-first rather than full RevOps orchestration (source: https://www.avoma.com/product/scheduler). Avoma's CRM auto-sync of conversation-derived signals (objections, next steps, methodology adherence) is a thin first-party signal layer adjacent to the **GTM Context Graph**, but it does not include third-party B2B data, contacts, intent, or technographics (source: https://www.avoma.com/use-case/sales-intelligence-platform).
- **No overlap:** Avoma sells no third-party B2B contact, company, technographic, or intent data — the entire **Data (Pillar 1)** layer (500M contacts / 100M companies / phone / email / technographics) is absent (source: https://www.avoma.com/blog/sales-intelligence). Avoma sells no ABM, audience-building, or marketing orchestration capabilities — **GTM Studio's marketing/ABM surface** is unmatched (source: https://www.avoma.com/). Avoma offers API + Webhooks at Organization+ tier but no public MCP server or agent-builder positioning — **APIs & MCP** ecosystem is unmatched (source: https://www.avoma.com/pricing).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Avoma's pricing-page comparison table positions Avoma against Chorus on Monthly Cost/Seat ("$120, min 3 years") and Upfront Costs, framing Avoma as "Fair, flexible, and affordable pricing without compromising advanced functionalities" vs competitors where "You pay a fortune for the brand name and end up not using advanced functionalities" (source: https://www.avoma.com/pricing). No dedicated `/vs/zoominfo` or `/vs/chorus` long-form page surfaced.
