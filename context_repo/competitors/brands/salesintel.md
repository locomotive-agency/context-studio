# SalesIntel

**G2:** ⭐ 4.4 / 5 — 315 reviews  <!-- g2-injector -->

## Summary

SalesIntel is a B2B sales intelligence and "Signal-First Agentic Pipeline Generation Platform" that sells primarily to CROs, BDR/AE teams, demand-gen marketers, and RevOps. Its core surfaces — human-verified contact and technographic data, Signal360 (predictive + demand-capture intent), VisitorIntel website de-anonymization, GTMCanvas no-code workflow builder, ProspectConnect sequencing, and AdsIntel ABM ads — overlap directly with ZoomInfo's Data foundation, GTM Context Graph signals, GTM Workspace seller front-end, GTM Studio marketer/RevOps front-end, and Operations CRM enrichment. The vendor explicitly positions against ZoomInfo on price simplicity ("one price for one platform" with unlimited credits) and human-verified data quality, and offers a "Guided Transitions" service to migrate ZoomInfo customers. It does not appear to compete in conversation intelligence (Chorus), website chat, or the MCP/agent-builder ecosystem.

## Product Offerings

### Signals (Signal360)

- **What it does:** Tracks 30+ buying-signal categories across predictive (future intent) and demand-capture (active evaluation) signals across a customer's TAM.
- **Key features:**
  - 30+ signal categories: funding, M&A, leadership changes, hiring surges, job changes, technographic shifts, website visits, intent surges
  - 60,000+ intent topics powered by Bombora and SalesIntel PredictiveIntel
  - PredictiveIntent AI suppression filters out illogical matches by technographic, size, and industry; only 60+ confidence-score signals surface
  - Native sync to Salesforce and HubSpot; signal-triggered sequences in Outreach and Salesloft
  - Webhooks and API for custom signal routing
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/products/buying-signals

### Data (Contact + Firmographic + Technographic)

- **What it does:** AI plus human-verified B2B contact, firmographic, technographic, and intent data layer used to power every product, with research-on-demand for gaps.
- **Key features:**
  - 200M+ verified B2B contacts; 54M+ verified mobile numbers
  - 95% accuracy guarantee with human verification by 2,000+ researchers
  - 42,000+ technologies tracked for technographic intelligence
  - Research on Demand: research consultants fulfill a custom data request every 45 seconds
  - RevDriver+ Chrome extension for prospecting on LinkedIn, Sales Navigator, or any company website
  - SOC 2 certified, GDPR and CCPA compliant
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/b2b-contact-data/

### VisitorIntel (Web Visitor Identification)

- **What it does:** De-anonymizes website traffic against a 22M+ company database and pushes identified accounts into the GTM stack for retargeting and outreach.
- **Key features:**
  - Match site traffic to a 22M+ company database
  - Filter identified accounts by ICP fit; score by engagement depth and return frequency
  - Surface buying-committee members alongside account-level data
  - Real-time alerts for first ICP visits (BDRs) and open-opportunity revisits (AEs)
  - Native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Marketo
  - Push visitor audiences to LinkedIn and Google Ads for ABM retargeting
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/products/identify-website-visitors

### GTMCanvas

- **What it does:** No-code agentic workflow builder that turns detected signals into automated multi-step campaigns and rep-handed action lists.
- **Key features:**
  - Drag-and-drop automation for any GTM workflow (list building, account research, outreach sequencing, CRM updates)
  - Predictive signals trigger awareness/relationship-building campaigns
  - Demand-capture signals launch immediate engagement sequences
  - AI agents handle repetitive prospecting tasks; reps step in for human touch
  - No-code workflow construction
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/

### RepIntel (Rep Dashboard)

- **What it does:** Daily prioritized account briefing that surfaces a next-best-action list and signal-driven context for each rep.
- **Key features:**
  - AI-ranked daily priority list based on signals + firmographics
  - Per-account context (why they matter, what changed)
  - One-click routing to sequences
  - Direct CRM sync
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/solutions/sales

### ProspectConnect (Sales Engagement / Sequencing Platform)

- **What it does:** Multi-channel sequencing engine for signal-triggered outreach to the buying committee.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-channel sequences across email, call, LinkedIn, and SMS
  - Auto-enroll signal-qualified accounts or use a rep-review queue
  - Dynamic personalization tokens populated with company, role, and signal info
  - Engagement and outcome reporting
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/solutions/sales

### AdsIntel (B2B Advertising Platform)

- **What it does:** ABM advertising layer that runs ads to accounts showing buying signals.
- **Key features:**
  - Account-based ad targeting on LinkedIn, Facebook, Google, and PPC
  - Audiences built from intent-firing accounts and identified site visitors
  - Retargeting Ads add-on
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/pricing/

### FormsIntel (Lead Capture Forms)

- **What it does:** Enriches every form submission with full firmographic and contact data and surfaces buying-committee members.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time firmographic and contact enrichment on form fill
  - Builds a buying committee from a single submission
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/pricing/

### ICPIntel (ICP & Lookalike Modeling)

- **What it does:** Validates signal-firing accounts against the customer's ICP and scores accounts for fit; supports lookalike prospecting.
- **Key features:**
  - ICP DNA analysis and lookalike prospecting
  - Auto-scoring of every account for fit
  - Buying-committee mapping for ICP-matching accounts
- **Source URL:** https://salesintel.io/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- "Signal-First Agentic Pipeline Generation Platform" — claims to be the first to fuse predictive (future-pipeline) and demand-capture (in-market) signals on one platform via Signal360 (source: https://salesintel.io/).
- Human verification at scale — 2,000+ human researchers and a 95% data accuracy guarantee, plus the largest human-verified mobile-number list in the industry (54M+ verified mobiles) (source: https://salesintel.io/).
- Research on Demand — custom data requests fulfilled by a researcher team "every 45 seconds" to fill database gaps (source: https://salesintel.io/b2b-data/).
- Unlimited usage pricing model — unlimited export and enrichment credits per user per month under a single platform price (source: https://salesintel.io/pricing/).
- PredictiveIntent AI filters — proprietary suppression of irrelevant matches based on technographic overlap, company size, and industry; "800M+ false positives suppressed"; only signals scoring 60+ confidence surface (source: https://salesintel.io/products/buying-signals).
- Guided Transitions — onboarding service explicitly designed to migrate ZoomInfo customers without selling downtime (source: https://salesintel.io/salesintel-vs-zoominfo/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - SalesIntel Data (200M+ contacts, 54M+ mobiles, 42K+ technologies, 95% human-verified) directly competes with ZoomInfo's Data foundation; SalesIntel positions its data as the equal-or-better layer with "one price for one platform and unlimited data" (source: https://salesintel.io/b2b-contact-data/).
  - SalesIntel RepIntel + ProspectConnect + RevDriver+ Chrome extension overlap with ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace as the seller front-end for prospecting and signal-driven priorities (source: https://salesintel.io/solutions/sales).
  - The ABM Suite (AdsIntel + VisitorIntel + FormsIntel + ICPIntel) overlaps with ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio across ABM ads, website-visitor identification, form enrichment, retargeting, and ICP modeling (source: https://salesintel.io/pricing/).
  - Data Enrichment + GTMCanvas + native CRM integrations overlap with ZoomInfo Operations on CRM data quality and routing — the Salesforce integration page describes auto-enrichment, de-duping, and scheduled bulk enrichment to "avoid data decay" (source: https://salesintel.io/platform/revops/integrations/salesforce/).
  - Signal360 (Bombora + PredictiveIntel across 30+ categories and 60,000+ intent topics) overlaps with ZoomInfo's intent / GTM Context Graph signal layer; SalesIntel explicitly frames competing intent providers as "only capturing later-stage intent" (source: https://salesintel.io/products/buying-signals).
- **Adjacent overlap:** SalesIntel APIs & Webhooks (a /platform/revops/integrations/api/ surface plus webhook delivery) touches ZoomInfo's Enterprise API surface but no MCP server or agent-protocol ecosystem was found on reviewed pages (source: https://salesintel.io/platform/revops/integrations/salesforce/).
- **No overlap:**
  - No conversation intelligence equivalent to Chorus on reviewed pages — no call recording, transcription, or deal-intelligence surface mentioned.
  - No website-chat product equivalent to ZoomInfo Chat; VisitorIntel handles identification but not conversational website chat.
  - No MCP server, agent-protocol, or developer-marketplace surface comparable to mcp.zoominfo.com / Claude directory listing.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "Quality data is table stakes. You deserve more. ... With SalesIntel, you pay one price for one platform and get access to unlimited data, unlimited data enrichment, and unlimited customer support. ... We do the heavy-lifting to work around ZoomInfo's contractual challenges to ensure you are set up for success without missing a day of selling." (source: https://salesintel.io/salesintel-vs-zoominfo/)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Turn buying signals into pipeline that converts. AI agents that find, engage, and convert your best accounts. Agents do the repetitive work, while your team focuses on closing." (source: https://salesintel.io/)
- **Recurring proof points:** 5,000+ customers; logos including Fluor, IQVIA, CohnReznick, Cisive, Solix, DISA, 360; 200M+ verified contacts, 54M+ mobile numbers, 95% accuracy; Alleyoop case study showing 35% connect rate vs ZoomInfo 16% / Cognism 8% in a 1,000-contact head-to-head test; Maestro Group $500K SOW closed in first 6 months after leaving ZoomInfo; TALON 25% higher connect rate after migrating from ZoomInfo; G2 "Account Intelligence Leader," "Momentum Leader," and "Higher Performer Small Business" badges (sources: https://salesintel.io/, https://salesintel.io/pricing/, https://salesintel.io/salesintel-vs-zoominfo/).
- **Tone / category framing:** Pipeline Generation Platform / "Signal-First Agentic Pipeline Generation Platform" — explicitly anti-"data provider" framing. Positions itself as a complete GTM solution that consolidates "four or five separate tools" (intent + firmographics + contact data + engagement + workflow automation) into one (source: https://salesintel.io/).
