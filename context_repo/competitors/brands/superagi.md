# SuperAGI

## Sub-products

- [[superagi--supersales|SuperAGI SuperSales]] — competes with GTM Workspace
- [[superagi--super-app|SuperAGI Super App]] — competes with GTM Studio
- [[superagi--digital-workers|SuperAGI Digital Workers]] — competes with GTM Workspace

## Summary

SuperAGI sells an "AI Super App for Work" / "All-in-One AI GTM Platform" of 50+ AI-native apps and digital workers, targeting Founders, SDRs, AEs, Sales Leaders, RevOps, and Marketers across SMB through Enterprise. Direct overlap with ZoomInfo runs through SuperSales (AI sales engagement + AI SDR), an AI-native CRM, an agent-curated lead database (275M+ leads / 75M+ accounts; 450M+ verified contacts cited on the ZoomInfo Alternative page), conversation intelligence, intent signals, and SuperMarketing — explicitly framed as "ZoomInfo wasn't built for the Agentic CRM era". Beyond GTM, SuperAGI extends into Voice, Customer Support, HR, Legal, Ecommerce, Vibe Coding, and a Humanoid SDR managed service that ZoomInfo does not target. Pricing is publicly listed and credit-based starting at $150/mo.

## Product Offerings

### SuperSales (AI Sales Platform)

- **What it does:** All-in-one AI Sales Platform pitched to "grow your revenue, without the hassle of hiring large sales team" via multi-agent automation across engagement, CRM, and data (source: https://web.superagi.com/supersales/).
- **Key features:**
  - AI Outbound/Inbound SDRs that plan, prioritize, research, engage, and update CRM autonomously
  - Sequences, Dialer (power + parallel), Meetings, Conversations, Email Warmup Infra
  - Account / Pipeline / Lead Management plus mobile app
  - Buyer Intent & Signal, Contact & Account Search, Data Enrichment
  - Agent Builder + Next Best Actions for native multi-agent automation
  - Persona coverage spans Founders, SDRs, Sales Leaders, RevOps, AEs, Marketers
- **Source URL:** https://web.superagi.com/supersales/

### AI-Native CRM (SuperSales CRM)

- **What it does:** AI-native system of record for contacts, companies, deals, and tasks; pitched as a "CRM Built Ground Up for Human and AI Agents Alike" (source: https://web.superagi.com/crm/).
- **Key features:**
  - AI suggests next steps, optimizes workflows, and enhances team collaboration
  - Automatic data enrichment of contact and lead profiles
  - Intelligent task prioritization across entities
  - LinkedIn Chrome plugin to add leads directly into the CRM, email or call
  - Granular targeting with lookalike audiences and real-time intent signals
  - 300+ integrations to power the sales pipeline
  - Mobile app to manage contacts, companies, leads, and deals on the go
- **Source URL:** https://web.superagi.com/crm/

### Prospecting Agent

- **What it does:** Autonomous agent that finds high-quality leads matching a defined ICP from SuperAGI's curated lead database in seconds (source: https://web.superagi.com/product-update/introducing-prospecting-agent/).
- **Key features:**
  - Define ICP criteria; AI autonomously finds matching leads
  - Direct CRM integration — no manual entry or third-party tools
  - Agent-curated lead database of 275M+ leads and 75M+ accounts
  - Pairs with AI SDR for personalized outreach
- **Source URL:** https://web.superagi.com/product-update/introducing-prospecting-agent/

### SuperMarketing (AI-Native Omnichannel Marketing Automation)

- **What it does:** AI-native marketing automation pitched to deliver 1:1 personalization across email, SMS, WhatsApp, Mobile, Facebook, Google, and other channels (source: https://web.superagi.com/supermarketing/).
- **Key features:**
  - Channels: Email, SMS, Web Push, WhatsApp, Mobile SDK
  - Customer engagement: Automations, Campaigns, Forms, Workflow Automation
  - Personalisation & Customer Data: Dynamic Customer Segmentation, Multi-variant Testing, Web SDK event tracking, Customer Data Platform (CDP)
  - Analytics: Funnel, Cohort, Session, Engagement, Website Event Tracking
  - LLM-driven dynamic customer journeys (vs. static automation flows)
  - Marketing Agents that design social media visuals, manage email campaigns, and personalize interactions
- **Source URL:** https://web.superagi.com/supermarketing/

### Humanoid SDR (Humex)

- **What it does:** Managed Sales Development service that pairs trained human SDRs with SuperAGI's AI-native GTM stack — competitor pitches "up to 4x higher productivity" and "10x more cost-effective" vs. traditional in-house SDR teams (source: https://web.superagi.com/humanoid-sdr/).
- **Key features:**
  - 100% autonomous sales execution from prospecting to follow-ups
  - Multi-channel mastery across email, LinkedIn, calls, and in-meeting engagement
  - "Top 1% Human SDRs" recruited, trained, and deployed by SuperAGI
  - Runs on AI Outbound/Inbound SDRs, AI Journey, AI Dialer, Signals, Agent Builder
  - Scales without hiring cycles or onboarding delays
- **Source URL:** https://web.superagi.com/humanoid-sdr/

### AI Super App / App Marketplace (50+ AI-Native Apps)

- **What it does:** Single platform combining 50+ AI-native apps and Digital Employees spanning Sales, Sales Data, Automations, BI & Analytics, Marketing, Customer Support, Meetings, Ecommerce, Vibe Coding, Voice, HR, Legal — installable from a marketplace (source: https://superagi.com/).
- **Key features:**
  - Sales apps: CRM, Cold Outreach (AI SDR), Sequences, Sales Dialer, Business Phone, Library, CPQ (coming soon), Invoices
  - Sales Data apps: Prospect, Data Enrichment, Anonymous Website Visitors, Signals
  - Marketing apps: Campaigns, Unibox, Ecommerce Marketing (Contlo)
  - Voice apps: AI Business Receptionist, Voice Agents
  - Vibe Coding: Vibe Coder, Mobile App Builder (alpha)
  - Digital Employees — "autonomous AI teammates that think, act, execute, and improve"
  - Developer Center: publish apps and Digital Employees to the SuperAGI marketplace; community apps available
- **Source URL:** https://superagi.com/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

This is the load-bearing section. Compared against ZoomInfo's positioning anchors in `CONTEXT.md` (all-in-one AI GTM Platform; Data + GTM Context Graph + Universal Access; primary product lines including Sales, Marketing, Operations, Chorus, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, APIs & MCP).

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace ⇄ SuperSales + Prospecting Agent + AI SDR.** SuperSales pitches autonomous prospecting, multi-channel engagement, and seller workflows from one surface — directly competitive with ZoomInfo's "prioritized accounts + AI-drafted outreach + execution in one surface" framing (source: https://web.superagi.com/supersales/).
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation ⇄ Agent-curated lead database (275M+ leads / 75M+ accounts; 450M+ verified contacts).** SuperAGI's ZoomInfo Alternative page directly checks "B2B Lead Database — 450M+ verified global contacts ready to engage" against ZoomInfo, putting it in head-to-head data competition (source: https://web.superagi.com/zoominfo-alternative/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio ⇄ AI-Native CRM data enrichment + Routing Forms + Workflows.** ZoomInfo Alternative page rows out Routing Forms, AI-Powered Data Enrichment ("AI-based duplicate detection, fuzzy matching, or auto-merging of CRM records"), and Workflows (source: https://web.superagi.com/zoominfo-alternative/).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) ⇄ AI-Driven Conversation Intelligence.** ZoomInfo Alternative page rows out "Native real-time coaching, sentiment analysis, or objection handling AI" (source: https://web.superagi.com/zoominfo-alternative/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM) ⇄ SuperMarketing (omnichannel marketing automation, segmentation, journey orchestration, CDP).** Includes Account Based Intent Signal called out on the ZoomInfo Alternative comparison (source: https://web.superagi.com/supermarketing/, https://web.superagi.com/zoominfo-alternative/).

- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **APIs & MCP ⇄ MCP Market + Developer Center.** SuperAGI surfaces an MCP Market (i.web.superagi.com/mcp) and Developer Center to publish apps and digital employees — adjacent to ZoomInfo's MCP server / API ecosystem play but anchored on SuperAGI's own apps rather than a B2B data/intelligence layer (source: https://superagi.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales — buyer intent / website visitor ID ⇄ Anonymous Website Visitors + Signals + Account Based Intent Signal.** SuperAGI ships native intent surfaces; the ZoomInfo Alternative page lists "Account Based Intent Signal" as a ZoomInfo Add-on while SuperAGI shows it native — adjacent rather than identical-scope intent coverage (source: https://web.superagi.com/zoominfo-alternative/).

- **No overlap:** Vibe Coding (Vibe Coder, Mobile App Builder), Voice Agents / AI Business Receptionist, HR (Attendance, ATS, Time Off, Performance), Legal (E-Sign), Ecommerce (Products catalog, Contlo marketing), Customer Support (Live Chat, Tickets, Knowledge Base), Project Management, and SuperCoder (custom RAG / Customer360 software dev — fintech and pharma case studies). These cross-vertical apps live outside ZoomInfo's GTM Platform scope (source: https://superagi.com/, https://web.superagi.com/case-studies/).

- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** SuperAGI explicitly opens with "ZoomInfo wasn't built for the Agentic CRM era" and frames four ZoomInfo failure modes — "Signals Need Action: Contacts, intent, and visitor ID don't book meetings… that work lives outside ZoomInfo," "Rules, Not Agents: Workflows update fields and route. They don't research accounts, write buyer-aware messages, or run the outreach for you," "Fragmented Stack: Dialer here, ads there, sequences elsewhere," and "Weak Attribution: You see activities, not outcomes" (source: https://web.superagi.com/zoominfo-alternative/). The page then runs a side-by-side feature matrix across Outreach, Data & Lead Management, Buyer Intent, Conversation Intelligence, Marketing, I-Assistant, and Support — checking SuperAGI native vs. ZoomInfo add-on / limited / missing in multiple rows.
