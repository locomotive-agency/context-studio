## Summary

Revenue Grid is a Salesforce-native revenue intelligence platform that pairs automatic activity capture (email, calendar, meetings) with AI-driven sales engagement (Revenue Signals, Deal Guidance, Sales Sequences) for sales, RevOps, and Salesforce-IT buyers — with explicit FinServ and other regulated-industry positioning. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph activity-capture layer (the Chorus / Workbounce-style first-party signal substrate) and with the seller-execution surface of GTM Workspace, particularly for Salesforce-only enterprises. Where it has no overlap is the entire ZoomInfo data foundation (no third-party contacts, intent, or technographics), GTM Studio / ABM, and the APIs/MCP agent-builder surface.

## Product Offerings

### RG Capture — Activity Capture / Salesforce + Email & Calendar Integration

> **Wiki:** [[revenue-grid--activity-capture]]

- **What it does:** Automatically captures emails, meetings, contacts, tasks, and attachments from Outlook and Gmail into Salesforce, with configurable rules and unlimited data retention.
- **Key features:**
  - Automatic or semi-automatic capture of emails, meetings, contacts, tasks, and attachments from Outlook and Gmail into Salesforce
  - Capture rules let admins decide what is logged, how it is stored, and what is excluded
  - Sync to standard or custom Salesforce objects with configurable field mapping
  - Unlimited / indefinite data retention (no 24-month cap)
  - Backfill / historical synchronization of past emails and meetings
  - Calendar sync including delegated and shared calendars; personal events excluded by default
  - SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified; cloud, private cloud, or on-premises deployment
  - Multi-geo Microsoft 365 support; designed to support 100K+ users
- **Source URL:** https://revenuegrid.com/product/salesforce-and-email-and-calendar-integration

### RG Capture — Inbox Sidebar

> **Wiki:** [[revenue-grid--inbox-sidebar]]

- **What it does:** An Outlook/Gmail sidebar that lets reps view, edit, and manage Salesforce data and activities without leaving the inbox.
- **Key features:**
  - Create and save emails to specific Salesforce records
  - Use Salesforce templates in emails
  - Create and edit Salesforce objects directly in the sidebar
  - Real-time alerts for every email click or open (Magic Pixel)
  - Insights on who opens emails, where, and on what device
  - Share availability or "Book me" page from the sidebar for meetings
  - Log tasks and events into Salesforce without switching tabs
- **Source URL:** https://revenuegrid.com/product/email-sidebar

### RG Capture — Data Capture

- **What it does:** The capture engine framing — keeps Salesforce data accurate, duplicate-free, and consistent by auto-syncing all client interactions into the CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Auto or semi-auto sync of emails, meetings, contacts, attachments
  - Decide what to capture, how to store it, and what to exclude
  - Inbox sidebar to access and edit data from email
  - Smart scheduling links for prospects
  - All captured data stored directly in Salesforce for analytics/audit
  - No 24-month retention cap
  - Multi-geo Microsoft 365 support; 100K+ user scale
- **Source URL:** https://revenuegrid.com/product/data-capture/

### RG Inspect — True Pipeline, Sales Forecasting, Team Analytics

> **Wiki:** [[revenue-grid--rg-inspect]]

- **What it does:** Pipeline visibility, AI-driven forecasting based on real deal-closure odds, and rep activity analytics — surfaced from captured CRM activity.
- **Key features:**
  - True Pipeline: see which deals are progressing or stalling
  - Sales forecasting based on real odds of deals closing
  - Team analytics: bird's-eye view of rep activity
  - Vendor claims 90% forecast accuracy and 400% more activity in CRM
  - Performance analytics correlating activity with outcomes
- **Source URL:** https://revenuegrid.com/solutions/revenue-intelligence-software/

### RG Engage — Sales Sequences, Deal Guidance, Revenue Signals, Meetings Assistance, Intel Assistance

> **Wiki:** [[revenue-grid--rg-engage]]

- **What it does:** Sales engagement suite — multichannel automated sequences, AI deal guidance, real-time signals, meeting prep/capture, and external intel feeds.
- **Key features:**
  - Multichannel automated sales sequences
  - Revenue Signals: real-time AI alerts on deals at risk, silent deals, or stalling deals
  - AI Deal Guidance — recommends next-best actions based on deal context, playbooks, and communication insights
  - Engagement timeline per opportunity: last touch, meetings, emails, transcripts, upcoming steps with AI-generated stage summaries
  - Relationship mapping to identify decision-makers, champions, and gaps
  - Guidance delivered in Microsoft Teams, Slack, and Salesforce
  - Signals can be sourced from Zoom, Eventbrite, Stripe and other external tools
  - Meetings Assistance covers prep, execution, and follow-up
- **Source URL:** https://revenuegrid.com/product/deal-guidance

### RG Direct — Mentor

> **Wiki:** [[revenue-grid--rg-mentor]]

- **What it does:** AI chat assistant ("RG Mentor") that delivers in-CRM guidance for better win rates and shorter sales cycles.
- **Key features:**
  - RG Mentor: AI chat assistant delivering instant answers and guidance
  - Real-time assistance to prioritize the day, delegate manual tasks, and recommend next best steps
  - Surfaces deal context and risks at point-of-need
- **Source URL:** https://revenuegrid.com/pricing/

### Revenue Grid for Enterprise

- **What it does:** Enterprise-tier deployment positioning — customizable platform for large revenue teams with security, support, and Salesforce Community license coverage.
- **Key features:**
  - Coordinates and aligns diverse revenue teams with AI-driven insights at every stage
  - Customizable platform, products, and deployment to match enterprise requirements
  - Best-rated support
  - All Salesforce Community / Experience Cloud licenses supported
  - Acts only as a means of transfer — does not store Salesforce or personal data
  - Vendor-claimed metrics: 33% more data in CRM, 24% more CRM usage, 10 hours saved/employee/week, 24% higher engagement response, 34% higher employee satisfaction
- **Source URL:** https://revenuegrid.com/product/enterprise

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Salesforce-native architecture with Managed Package, custom object sync, and unlimited data retention — positioned directly against Einstein Activity Capture's 6-/24-month cap (source: https://revenuegrid.com/product/salesforce-and-email-and-calendar-integration).
- Cloud, private cloud, or on-premises deployment options — uncommon in the activity-capture / revenue-intelligence category and aimed at regulated FinServ buyers (source: https://revenuegrid.com/product/salesforce-and-email-and-calendar-integration).
- Revenue Signals: AI-driven, contextual alerts that act as "guided selling" — delivered inside Slack, Teams, Outlook, Gmail, and Salesforce (source: https://revenuegrid.com/product/revenue-signals/).
- Acts only as a means of data transfer — does not store Salesforce or personal data outside the customer's instance, marketed as a security advantage (source: https://revenuegrid.com/product/enterprise).
- 20+ years on the market with 800,000–900,000 users and consistent G2 Leader since 2018; vendor positions itself as "first-to-market revenue intelligence" (source: https://revenuegrid.com/solutions/revenue-intelligence-software/).
- Public per-seat pricing ($30 / $49 / $149) with founder pricing for early AI access — comparatively transparent vs. enterprise revenue-intelligence peers like Gong and Clari (source: https://revenuegrid.com/pricing/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph (activity-capture component) and Chorus / ZoomInfo Operations** vs. **RG Capture (Activity Capture, Data Capture, Inbox Sidebar):** Revenue Grid auto-captures 100% of emails, meetings, contacts, tasks, and attachments from Outlook/Gmail into Salesforce — the same first-party-signal substrate ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph promises (built on Chorus + Workbounce as context-capture engines). Revenue Grid claims "100% data accuracy" and a "unified intelligence platform: call recording, activity capture, pipeline management, AI insights, and more in one platform" (source: https://revenuegrid.com/solutions/revenue-intelligence-software/).
  - **GTM Workspace / ZoomInfo Sales** vs. **RG Engage + RG Direct (Mentor):** Revenue Grid pitches reps a single surface that recommends next-best-actions, drafts follow-ups, summarizes deal status, and surfaces signals — directly equivalent to the "prioritized accounts + AI-drafted outreach + execution in one surface" framing for GTM Workspace (source: https://revenuegrid.com/product/deal-guidance).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + activity sync)** vs. **RG Capture / Salesforce + Email & Calendar Integration:** Indefinite retention, configurable capture rules, custom-object sync, SOC 2 Type II + ISO 27001 + private-cloud / on-prem deployment overlap with ZoomInfo Operations' CRM-data-quality and routing positioning, particularly for Salesforce-centric enterprises (source: https://revenuegrid.com/product/salesforce-and-email-and-calendar-integration).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence)** vs. **RG Engage / Deal Guidance — engagement timeline with meeting transcripts and AI-generated stage summaries:** Revenue Grid surfaces transcripts and AI summaries inside the deal timeline but does not pitch a standalone conversation-intelligence recorder; it is consuming conversation context rather than generating it (source: https://revenuegrid.com/product/deal-guidance).
  - **GTM Studio (RevOps / GTM-engineer orchestration)** vs. **Core Signals & Logic Apps configuration (paid services add-on):** Revenue Grid offers Logic Apps for cloud workflow orchestration and custom Core Signals build-out as a paid service — adjacent to GTM Studio's natural-language audience building and play orchestration, but delivered as professional-services configuration rather than a self-serve marketer/RevOps surface (source: https://revenuegrid.com/pricing/).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Data foundation (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ phones, 200M+ emails, technographics):** Revenue Grid has no third-party B2B contact/company database, no intent data, and no technographics — exclusively first-party-data on customer email/meeting/CRM activity (source: https://revenuegrid.com/solutions/revenue-intelligence-software/).
  - **APIs & MCP (Enterprise APIs + MCP server in Claude directory):** Reviewed pages do not promote a public Enterprise API, an MCP server, or AI-agent-builder integration paths beyond Slack/Teams Signals delivery (source: https://revenuegrid.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / ABM (audience building, intent data):** No ABM, intent, or marketer-orchestration surface in the reviewed product set; marketing buyers are not a stated ICP (source: https://revenuegrid.com/solutions/revenue-intelligence-software/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Revenue Grid publishes vs. Einstein Activity Capture, vs. Salesforce Inbox, and a "vs. Competitors" hub, but no first-party `revenuegrid.com` page comparing themselves to ZoomInfo was returned by SERP. Third-party sources (G2 "Chorus by ZoomInfo vs. Revenue Grid", Cuspera "ZoomInfo InboxAI vs. RevenueGrid Inbox") exist but are aggregator pages, not Revenue Grid claims.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Transform relationship data into revenue growth" — automatically and securely capture every customer interaction into Salesforce, then turn that activity into AI-guided next-best-actions, signals, and forecasts.
- **Recurring proof points:** 800K–900K Salesforce users globally; "consistently G2 Leader since 2018"; vendor metrics of 90% forecast accuracy, 400% more activity in CRM, 30+ hours saved per person monthly, and 14% higher win rate; named customers Slalom (3x contacts auto-created, $30M increase), Vapotherm (761 days saved, 110K emails captured), Bessemer Trust (60% improvement), Customertimes (30–40% time saved on pipeline reports), Morgan & Morgan (15–20% caseload increase), CAPIS; Microsoft endorsement from Rob Howard (Director, Office 365 Ecosystem); SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, CCPA compliance posture; $20M Series A funding.
- **Tone / category framing:** "Revenue Intelligence" / "Revenue Operations and Intelligence" platform with "first-to-market" framing (per ZoomInfo company-entry SERP snippet); also positions itself as "the most comprehensive and secure activity capture solution on the market" and the leading Salesforce-native alternative to Einstein Activity Capture, Salesforce Inbox, and (per blog content) Gong/Clari for Salesforce teams.
