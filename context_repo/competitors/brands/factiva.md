## Summary

Factiva is a Dow Jones-owned business news aggregation and corporate-research database — pitched at corporate communications, competitive-intelligence, consulting, financial, and library/government research teams — not at GTM sellers or marketers. Overlap with ZoomInfo is tangential: Factiva sells premium licensed editorial news (33K sources, 1.4B-article archive) plus 35M company profiles and 70M executive bios that surface in a "Business Development" use case and integrate into Salesforce as news/trigger feeds, but Factiva does not sell verified contact data (phone, email, direct dials), intent signals, conversation intelligence, ABM, or any GTM execution surface (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/). The most defensible overlap is at the API layer: Factiva's Retrieval API and AI News Feed compete with ZoomInfo APIs & MCP as a *content* source for agent builders building LLM/RAG workflows (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/products/feeds-and-apis/). Pricing is fully gated behind contact-sales — see flags.

## Product Offerings

### Factiva (research platform)

> **Wiki:** [[factiva--platform]]

- **What it does:** Global news monitoring research platform combining premium news, company profiles, and executive bios across 33,000 sources, 200 countries, and 33 languages.
- **Key features:**
  - 33,000+ premium news sources spanning 200 countries and 33 languages
  - 35M company profiles and 70M executive bios
  - 400K news articles ingested daily
  - Factiva Smart Summary (GenAI-powered article summarization with citations)
  - AI Assistant grounded in licensed content with direct source links
  - Personalized news tracking by topic, industry, and company
- **Source URL:** https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/products/factiva/

### Factiva AI Research Solutions

> **Wiki:** [[factiva--ai-research-solutions]]

- **What it does:** Licensed news content packaged for use in customer GenAI/RAG workflows to improve output accuracy, reduce hallucinations, and provide citable sources.
- **Key features:**
  - Content licensed for GenAI use with copyright compliance
  - Near-real-time feeds and APIs for RAG workflows
  - 8,000+ sources, 160+ countries, 25+ languages provisioned for AI
  - RAG model retrieval options that keep customer data secure
  - Conversational AI combining proprietary data with licensed news
  - Sentiment, entity recognition, and event-detection patterns
- **Source URL:** https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/products/ai-research-solutions/

### Factiva Feeds & APIs

> **Wiki:** [[factiva--feeds-and-apis]]

- **What it does:** Programmatic delivery of Factiva premium news and data into customer platforms, AI models, and workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Factiva AI News Feed (rights-cleared content for AI/GenAI projects)
  - Factiva Retrieval API for LLM RAG workflows
  - Database of 1.4B+ articles, growing by ~1.5M articles per day
  - Real-time ingestion optimized for text mining and automation
  - Dow Jones Developer Platform with documentation and solution patterns
  - Datasets across Industries, Compliance, Events, Indicators, Markets, People, Companies, and News
- **Source URL:** https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/products/feeds-and-apis/

### Factiva Curated Newsletters (Dow Jones Curation Services / My Company Today / Dow Jones Direct)

> **Wiki:** [[factiva--curated-newsletters]]

- **What it does:** Editor-curated news briefings and licensed content packages distributed via newsletter, email, intranet, RSS, or mobile.
- **Key features:**
  - My Company Today: customizable daily executive briefings
  - Dow Jones Direct: licensed WSJ, Barron's, MarketWatch content for marketing reuse
  - Curation by dedicated Dow Jones editorial team
  - Brand-customizable templates (logo, colors, tone)
  - Delivery via email, intranet, RSS feed, or mobile
- **Source URL:** https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/products/curated-newsletters/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Factiva does not sell into ZoomInfo's primary product lines (Data (Pillar 1), GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, APIs & MCP, Chorus, ZoomInfo Marketing, ZoomInfo Operations, ZoomInfo Chat). Its category is corporate-research / news-intelligence, not GTM data.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - *ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace prospecting* vs *Factiva Business Development use case* — Factiva pitches "Boost sales prospecting, identify contacts and grow your sales pipeline with a global news database and intelligence on millions of companies and executives," with Salesforce/CRM news integration as the wedge (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/use-case/business-development/). It is a news-trigger and executive-research feed, not a verified-contact prospecting tool.
  - *Data (Pillar 1) (firmographics)* vs *Factiva company / executive profiles* — 35M company profiles and 70M executive bios, but corporate-research depth (financials, board members, executive lists), not contact/dial data (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/).
  - *APIs & MCP for AI agents* vs *Factiva Retrieval API + AI News Feed* — Factiva positions its rights-cleared news as content for LLM/RAG workflows, making it an alternative *content* source for agent builders rather than a GTM-data source (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/products/feeds-and-apis/).
- **No overlap:**
  - *Competitor-only*: Premium editorial news archive (WSJ, Barron's, MarketWatch, Dow Jones Newswires) and 1.4B-article corpus — ZoomInfo does not sell news content (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/use-case/corporate-communications-reputational-risk-management).
  - *Competitor-only*: Corporate communications / reputational risk monitoring and PR research (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/use-case/corporate-communications-reputational-risk-management).
  - *Competitor-only*: Editor-curated newsletters and content licensing for marketing reuse (Dow Jones Direct) (source: https://www.dowjones.com/business-intelligence/factiva/products/curated-newsletters/).
  - *Brand-only*: Verified phone/email/direct-dial contact data, intent data, conversation intelligence (Chorus), ABM orchestration, RevOps data quality / routing, GTM Studio, GTM Workspace, MCP-based agent surface — Factiva surfaces none of these.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ TrustRadius's third-party comparison frames Factiva as "a data service that helps companies identify opportunities, accelerate decisions and manage a business's reputation," while ZoomInfo Sales is "a modern go-to-market platform for B2B companies." The TrustRadius prospecting / sales-intelligence feature ratings for Factiva are entirely blank (0 of 0 ratings) across advanced search, lead identification, list quality, ICP targeting, contact information, and Salesforce integration — users do not evaluate Factiva on those GTM-data dimensions, while ZoomInfo Sales scores 7.3-8.6 there (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/factiva-vs-zoominfo-sales). See `vs_brand_missing` flag.

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Global news and data monitoring, now with GenAI" — Factiva combines thousands of premium global news and data sources with the speed of GenAI to surface insights, track executive moves, and identify trends faster.
- **Recurring proof points:**
  - 33,000+ sources, 200 countries, 33 languages, 400K articles daily
  - 35M company profiles and 70M executive bios
  - 1.4B+ article archive growing by ~1.5M articles per day
  - Owned by Dow Jones / News Corp; ISO 27001 certified
  - TrustRadius score 8.1/10 (28 reviews); Likelihood to Recommend 9.0
  - Premier publication brands: WSJ, Barron's, MarketWatch, Dow Jones Newswires, IBD
- **Tone / category framing:** "The world's most trusted business intelligence at your fingertips" — explicitly positioned as premium news / corporate research / GenAI content licensing, not as a GTM or sales-intelligence platform.
