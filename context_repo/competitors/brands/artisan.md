# Artisan

## Sub-products

- [[artisan--ava|Ava — Autonomous AI BDR]] — competes with GTM Workspace
- [[artisan--sales-automation|Sales Automation Platform]] — competes with GTM Workspace
- [[artisan--b2b-data|Artisan B2B Data]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

Artisan sells an autonomous AI BDR ("Ava") to sales leaders at startups, SMBs, and enterprises looking to scale outbound without growing BDR headcount. Ava bundles a 250M+-contact B2B database, intent signals, multi-channel sequencing, deliverability, a power dialer, autonomous reply handling, and meeting booking under a single credit-based subscription. That puts Artisan in direct overlap with ZoomInfo Sales (data + intent + WebSights) and the seller front-end ZoomInfo ships through GTM Workspace — Artisan's pitch is that GTM Workspace–style assistants "still require manual execution" while Ava executes the entire outbound motion end-to-end. There is no overlap with ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, APIs/MCP, ABM advertising, or RevOps data-quality surfaces.

## Product Offerings

### Ava — Autonomous AI BDR / AI Sales Agent

> **Wiki:** [[artisan--ava]]

- **What it does:** Single autonomous AI agent that runs the full outbound motion end-to-end: sources leads, enriches them, sends personalized multi-channel sequences, A/B tests messaging, handles replies and objections, and books meetings on reps' calendars.
- **Key features:**
  - Searches 250M+ verified B2B contacts across 200+ countries and prioritizes by intent signals like funding rounds and hires
  - Personalized multi-channel sequences across email and social (LinkedIn) with native dialer queue for reps
  - Continuous A/B (and "A/Z") testing of subject lines, structure, CTAs, and tone with auto-volume shifting
  - Autonomous reply handling, objection handling, and meeting booking on reps' calendars
  - Sends from rep mailboxes with bi-directional CRM sync (Salesforce, HubSpot, custom via API)
  - Self-healing mailboxes and built-in deliverability monitoring at scale
  - CRM reactivation campaigns for MQLs, closed-lost, churned, and event leads
  - Cross-sell / upsell campaigns using CRM and product-usage data via webhooks
- **Source URL:** https://www.artisan.co/ai-sales-agent

### Sales Automation Platform (Ava's outbound suite)

> **Wiki:** [[artisan--sales-automation]]

- **What it does:** All-in-one outbound automation platform that bundles data, enrichment, sequencing, deliverability, A/B testing, dialer, and CRM sync into a single autonomous system marketed as a BDR-stack replacement.
- **Key features:**
  - B2B data + waterfall enrichment across 22+ data sources built in
  - Autonomous campaign generation and message writing (no templates or variables)
  - Intent-driven cold outbound triggered on funding rounds, hires, and web activity
  - Website visitor de-anonymization (5 credits per identified visitor)
  - Human power dialer ($67/seat/mo) plus AI dialing copilot and AI follow-ups
  - Slack performance updates and human-in-the-loop escalation rules
  - Outbound in 50+ languages
  - Approval gates: tone/CTA/length locks, budget caps, and reply-sensitivity controls
- **Source URL:** https://www.artisan.co/products/sales-automation

### B2B Data / Contact Database

> **Wiki:** [[artisan--b2b-data]]

- **What it does:** Bundled contact database that powers Ava's outreach, with verified emails, direct dials, firmographics, technographics, and intent signals on 250M+ B2B professionals across 200+ countries.
- **Key features:**
  - 250M+ B2B professional contacts; "billions of verified data points"; 200+ countries covered
  - Demographic, firmographic, technographic, and intent data on every contact
  - Waterfall enrichment across multiple best-in-class providers checked sequentially
  - Continuously verified and refreshed (positioned against "aging" databases)
  - Email enrichment costs 2 credits; phone enrichment costs 10 credits
  - Database is included in the platform, not a separate subscription
- **Source URL:** https://www.artisan.co/data/b2b-data

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS, Events, Meal delivery / consumer-tech B2B, Point-of-sale / fintech (SumUp), Business services
- **Company size called out:** Startups, SMBs, Enterprise (dedicated `/solutions/*` pages for each)
- **Persona / role focus:** BDRs / SDRs (replaced or augmented), AEs, account managers (AMs), Heads of Growth / Sales leaders, founders running early outbound
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Cold outbound — net-new prospect targeting via funding/hiring/intent signals
  - CRM reactivation — MQLs, closed-lost, churned, and event leads
  - Cross-sell / upsell — outreach to existing customers using CRM + product-usage data
  - Outbound launch for startups with no BDR team
  - Scaling outbound at enterprises without adding BDR headcount

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** HubSpot (Intern+), Salesforce (Employee+), custom CRMs via API (Enterprise)
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Native multi-channel sequencer (email + social) is built in; native power dialer ($67/seat/mo) and AI dialing copilot
- **Data / enrichment:** Waterfall across 22+ sources (per Artisan's own description); the "GTM stack" page shows ZoomInfo, BigQuery, HubSpot, Salesforce, and Google as ecosystem logos
- **Other notable integrations:** Slack, Google calendar (meeting booking), webhooks as data source (Employee+), BigQuery (shown in GTM stack visual)

- Bundles a 250M+-contact B2B database with sequencing, deliverability, dialer, and reply handling under one credit-based subscription rather than separate add-ons (source: https://www.artisan.co/data/b2b-data)

- White-glove enterprise deployment with deployment strategist, forward-deployed engineer, and outbound campaign specialist; SOC 2 Type II + SSO/SAML for the enterprise tier (source: https://www.artisan.co/solutions/enterprise)
- Customer proof centered on volume + cost-per-lead: SaaStr 6,800+ emails / 3.6% positive reply; CookUnity 100K+ emails at $45 CPL; SumUp 400K+ emails at $52 CPL; RAISE "20x ROI" / 5-BDR-equivalent automation (source: https://www.artisan.co/customers)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace AI agent layer** — Ava is positioned as a fully-autonomous successor to GTM Workspace–style AI assistants. Artisan's own ZoomInfo review states the AI sales agent "still requires manual execution… Artisan's AI BDR Ava… finds leads, enriches profiles, sends emails, and manages follow-ups—all on autopilot." (source: https://www.artisan.co/blog/zoominfo-review)
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (B2B contact data + intent)** — the dedicated `/data/b2b-data` page sells a 250M+ contact database with verified emails, direct dials, firmographics, technographics, and intent (funding/hiring/web activity) (source: https://www.artisan.co/data/b2b-data).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace outreach automation** — Artisan bundles multi-channel outreach automation, A/B testing, deliverability, and a power dialer (source: https://www.artisan.co/products/sales-automation).
  - **Website visitor identification (ZoomInfo WebSights)** — pricing page lists "Identify website visitor — De-anonymize a person on your website, including their email address. 5 credits" as a built-in capability across paid plans (source: https://www.artisan.co/pricing).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio (marketer/RevOps play orchestration)** — Ava runs CRM-reactivation, cross-sell, and upsell campaigns using CRM data + webhooks, brushing against orchestration use cases without being a peer to GTM Studio's audience-builder + play-orchestration surface (source: https://www.artisan.co/products/sales-automation).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence)** — Artisan adds telephony with an AI dialing copilot and follow-ups, adjacent to but not a substitute for ZoomInfo's conversation intelligence (no transcript analysis, deal-risk scoring, or coaching surfaces called out) (source: https://www.artisan.co/pricing).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph** — Artisan does not market a graph/context layer fusing CRM + conversation + intent + behavioral data to explain "why" deals move; the pitch is execution (Ava), not reasoning (source: https://www.artisan.co/).
  - **APIs & MCP for any tool/agent** — no public API or MCP server surfaced on reviewed pages; integrations are limited to CRMs, Slack, calendar, and webhooks (source: https://www.artisan.co/ai-sales-agent).
  - **ABM advertising / display orchestration** — outbound is email + social (LinkedIn) + phone only; ZoomInfo Marketing's ad-targeting and ABM journey orchestration has no Artisan equivalent (source: https://www.artisan.co/products/sales-automation).
  - **CRM data quality / RevOps tooling (ZoomInfo Operations)** — Artisan syncs to CRMs and enriches contacts but does not market deduplication, routing, scoring, or governance peer to ZoomInfo Operations (source: https://www.artisan.co/solutions/enterprise).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "Due to ZoomInfo's enterprise-only pricing model and steep learning curve, it's worth considering a more AI-first, easy-to-use sales automation tool like Artisan." Artisan's side-by-side claims '300M+ globally enriched, AI-refreshed' contacts vs ZoomInfo's '260M+ (US-heavy)', frames ZoomInfo Sales / intent / WebSights as paid add-ons vs. Artisan-included, and contrasts ZoomInfo's "manual sequences and dialer" with "Ava handles email and social media outreach fully" (source: https://www.artisan.co/blog/zoominfo-review).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Hire Ava. The autonomous AI BDR. She finds leads, sends personalized outreach, handles objections and books meetings at a fraction of the cost of a human BDR." Positions a single AI employee as a replacement (not assistant) for human BDR teams, with all the data/sequencing/deliverability infrastructure built in.
- **Recurring proof points:** SaaStr (Jason Lemkin) "replaced our outbound sales team with Artisan" — 6,800+ emails, 3.6% positive reply rate, "$100K deals daily"; CookUnity 100K+ emails at $45 CPL, saves reps a full day/week of research; SumUp 400K+ emails at $52 CPL; RAISE / Chain of Events "20x ROI" automating 5 BDRs with one employee; Zirtual tripled outbound leads, 80 positive replies in 3 months; press in Forbes, NYT, TechCrunch, Inc., Business Insider, BBC; SOC 2 Type II certified; viral "Stop Hiring Humans" ad campaign.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as an "AI employee" platform — not a copilot or assistant — running "autonomous outbound." Treats sales intelligence (à la ZoomInfo), enrichment (à la Clay), sequencing (à la Outreach/Salesloft), and signals as a stack to be replaced by one agent.
