# Valley AI

## Sub-products

- [[valley-ai--linkedin-outbound|Valley AI LinkedIn Outbound]] — competes with GTM Workspace

## Summary

Valley AI is a signal-based AI SDR / LinkedIn-outbound platform sold to B2B sales leaders, founders, and lead-gen agencies — a narrow point-solution rather than a GTM platform. Valley targets the warm-outbound use case ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace also serves (prioritized accounts + AI-drafted outreach), but does it on a single channel (LinkedIn) and with no underlying verified B2B database, no marketing/ABM surface, and no public APIs/MCP. Crossover with ZoomInfo is real but channel-narrow: Valley competes with the seller "draft me a personalized outreach" workflow, not with the data foundation, GTM Studio, Operations, or developer ecosystem. No direct Valley-vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced; ZoomInfo appears in Valley's testimonials only as part of customer stacks Valley claims to outperform on meeting-booking ("we have Outreach, HubSpot, Seamless, but Valley has booked us more meetings…").

## Product Offerings

### Valley (core platform)

- **What it does:** AI LinkedIn outbound engine that captures warm signals (profile views, post engagers, company-page visitors, competitor followers), enriches and scores leads against ICP, deep-researches prospects across 60+ data points, drafts messages in the user's voice, and sends connection requests / InMails inside LinkedIn safety limits.
- **Key features:**
  - Signal capture — LinkedIn profile viewers, company-page visitors, post engagers, profile followers, website visitors
  - ICP enrichment + auto-scoring; the engine "deletes the noise" so users only message the top 20% fit
  - Deep research across 60+ data points (blogs, newsletters, podcasts, funding rounds, job changes, mutual connections)
  - Sender tone replication / voice cloning for personalization
  - Native LinkedIn execution: connection requests + InMails on dedicated IPs, 1,000–1,200 messages/seat/month inside LinkedIn limits
  - Reply management plus manual or autopilot mode
  - 5x money-back guarantee if a LinkedIn account is suspended because of Valley
  - Slack notifications for website visitors
- **Source URL:** https://www.joinvalley.co/product

### Valley Studios (Done-For-You)

- **What it does:** Managed-service tier where Valley's team runs the entire outbound operation — from ICP targeting through booked meetings — for the customer (source: https://www.joinvalley.co/pricing).
- **Key features:**
  - Fully managed outbound — no hiring, training, or managing required by the customer
  - Listed at $1,499/mo (waitlist; "currently not accepting more users")
  - ICP targeting + campaign management handled by Valley team
- **Source URL:** https://www.joinvalley.co/pricing

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS (developer tools, product-led growth — e.g. Bolt.new, Tacnode); GTM / lead-gen agencies; recruitment & staffing; finance, insurance & advisory; B2B consulting firms; growth-stage SaaS.
- **Company size called out:** Solo founders / SMB (e.g. Linarca single-seat, $10K+ ACV); small-to-mid B2B sales teams; GTM agencies managing 5+ client accounts (Scale tier 10–50 seats).
- **Persona / role focus:** VP Sales / CRO; founding & enterprise AEs; sales / SDR leaders; founders running their own outbound; agency owners / fulfillment leads; Director of Growth Marketing.
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Convert warm LinkedIn signals (profile viewers, post engagers, competitor followers) into booked meetings
  - Replace the Sales Navigator + Clay + ChatGPT + HeyReach/Phantombuster + Zapier "duct-tape stack"
  - Scale agency client outbound without scaling headcount (one platform per client)
  - Convert product-led signups (e.g. Bolt.new's 15–20K daily registrants) into enterprise pipeline with a small sales team
  - Run hyper-personalized warm outbound for founder-led / solo SMB GTM motions

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce, HubSpot — referenced in stack-integration blog content but not confirmed as native first-party connectors on the product or pricing pages (see flags).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Instantly.ai — the Valley-recommended companion for cold email since Valley itself is LinkedIn-only (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/blog/valley-ai; corroborated by a Valley LinkedIn post titled "How Valley integrates with Instantly.ai for better outreach").
- **Data / enrichment:** Built-in lead enrichment ("unlimited" across all tiers, replaces Clay in Valley's pitch); Sales Navigator CSV import for custom lead lists.
- **Other notable integrations:** LinkedIn (native execution layer for connection requests + InMails); Slack (visitor notifications).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Signal-based warm outbound — only messages prospects already interacting with the user (profile views, post engagement, company-page visits, competitor followers) instead of cold lists (source: https://joinvalley.co/).
- Headline reply-rate claim — 15–45% reply rates vs. a stated 1–2% industry average for templated LinkedIn automation (source: https://joinvalley.co/).
- Tone / voice replication on top of 60+ data-point research per prospect, drafted in the user's writing style rather than mail-merge templates (source: https://www.joinvalley.co/product).
- LinkedIn account safety — dedicated IPs per account, native execution inside LinkedIn limits, plus a 5x money-back guarantee if Valley causes account suspension (source: https://joinvalley.co/).
- Performance guarantee on Growth — "10 meetings booked in 90 days or Valley works for free" (conditional on 600+ messages/seat/month) (source: https://www.joinvalley.co/pricing).
- Stack-consolidation positioning — one platform replacing Sales Navigator + Clay + ChatGPT + HeyReach + Zapier (~$750/mo) (source: https://joinvalley.co/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Valley overlaps GTM Workspace's "prioritized accounts + AI-drafted outreach" workflow. Customer testimonials position Valley as displacing the meeting-booking job currently shared across Outreach, HubSpot, and Seamless ("we have Outreach, HubSpot, Seamless, but Valley has booked us more meetings than anything else") (source: https://joinvalley.co/). Channel scope differs: Valley is LinkedIn-only, where GTM Workspace draws from the GTM Context Graph across CRM + conversations + intent + behavioral data and isn't channel-restricted.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Valley's per-prospect lead enrichment + ICP scoring touches the same "right-person" job ZoomInfo's data foundation solves, but Valley does not maintain a persistent verified database — it scrapes/researches from public LinkedIn signals at message-time (source: https://www.joinvalley.co/product). Its "60+ data point" deep research narrative rhymes with Chorus / Workbounce context-capture, but Valley's context is web/research-based, not CRM/conversation-based.
- **No overlap:** Verified B2B contact database at scale (no equivalent of ZoomInfo's 500M contacts / 100M companies / direct dials / technographics — source: https://joinvalley.co/); marketing / ABM orchestration (no GTM Studio analog — source: https://www.joinvalley.co/product); APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem (no public API or MCP server mentioned anywhere on reviewed pages); cold email + multi-channel sequences (LinkedIn-only by design — source: https://www.salesforge.ai/blog/valley-ai); CRM data quality + routing (no ZoomInfo Operations equivalent on reviewed pages).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ ZoomInfo appears in Valley testimonials only as a peer the customer already owned alongside Outreach and HubSpot — Valley's named-competitor messaging targets HeyReach, Expandi, LinkedIn Helper, Phantombuster, Clay + ChatGPT stacks, not ZoomInfo.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The first LinkedIn automation platform for warm outbound. Capture signals, research leads, and book meetings — automatically." (source: https://joinvalley.co/)
- **Recurring proof points:** 300+ teams; 15–45% reply rates vs. 2% industry average; Bolt.new (7-figure pipeline + 25 enterprise demos in 60 days, two-person sales team); ThinkFish (380–400 meetings/month across 50 seats); Smallest.ai ($2M+ enterprise pipeline / 8 enterprise meetings/month); GGWP ($4M pipeline); WeLaunch ($5M client ARR). Operator quotes from Garrett Marker (CRO @ Brex), Jeff Becker (ex-Head of Sales @ LinkedIn), Director of Strategic Sales @ Miro, Owen Fitzgerald (CEO @ Ridge), Roberto Arrieta (Founder @ Linarca).
- **Tone / category framing:** "Signal-based warm outbound" / "next-generation AI SDR" — explicitly framed against template-based LinkedIn automation tools (HeyReach, Expandi, LinkedIn Helper, Phantombuster) and the "Clay + ChatGPT + Sales Nav + sender tool" duct-tape stack. Tone is operator-led, sharp, and product-page-heavy on testimonials and dollar-metric outcomes.
