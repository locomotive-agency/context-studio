## Summary

Similarweb is a digital data intelligence platform sold to B2B sales teams (SDRs, AEs, CSMs, RevOps), digital marketers, ecommerce category managers, and hedge fund analysts; its Sales Intelligence suite is the direct ZoomInfo overlap. The pitch is web-traffic-derived prospecting plus 470M GDPR/CCPA-compliant contacts, three native AI agents (Prospecting, Outreach, Meeting Prep), and native Salesforce/HubSpot enrichment with API + MCP access. Material brand-overlap observation: Similarweb directly competes with ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace and the underlying Data foundation, but its Web/App/Retail/Stock Intelligence suites sell into categories ZoomInfo does not address, while ZoomInfo retains conversation intelligence (Chorus) and a deeper GTM-engineer/MCP narrative that Similarweb does not match.

## Product Offerings

### Sales Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[similarweb--sales-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Helps B2B sales teams find, qualify, and prioritize accounts by turning digital signals (traffic trends, buyer intent, tech changes, news activity) into prospecting and outreach actions.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Prospecting Agent — natural-language ICP search that returns prioritized companies and contacts
  - Contacts Data — 100M+ contacts (FAQ cites 470M total) with direct phone numbers and verified emails across 190+ countries, sourced GDPR/CCPA-compliant
  - Sales Signals — buyer intent, traffic shifts, technology changes, news events, and ad-network activity
  - AI Outreach — generates personalized emails/InMail from buyer/market data
  - AI Meeting Prep — auto-builds tailored briefs from digital signals and competitive insights
  - Sales Chrome Extension — surfaces firmographics, technographics, AI-generated insights, and contact details on any site
  - CRM enrichment + native HubSpot and Salesforce integrations
  - API and MCP access for enrichment automation
- **Source URL:** https://www.similarweb.com/corp/sales/

### Lead Generator (Sales Intelligence sub-product)

> **Wiki:** [[similarweb--lead-generator]]

- **What it does:** Lead-generation workflow inside Sales Intelligence that finds ready-to-act buyers using real-time web/app data, intent, and 50+ firmographic/technographic filters.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Prospecting Agent driven by natural-language descriptions of the ICP
  - Real-time intent signals (traffic spikes, tech-stack changes) to surface high-intent accounts
  - Decision-maker targeting with role/seniority filters and direct dials, business emails, LinkedIn profiles
  - Mobile app data layered with web data for buyer qualification
  - Saved Searches and Static/Dynamic Lists with email alert frequencies (Daily/Weekly/Monthly)
  - Native HubSpot and Salesforce integrations for real-time CRM enrichment
  - Export to Excel/CSV or via API/MCP
  - Multi-vendor contact-data waterfall model with accuracy scoring per contact
- **Source URL:** https://www.similarweb.com/corp/sales/lead-gen/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Similarweb Sales Intelligence competes head-to-head with **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace** on prospecting, qualification, and AI-drafted outreach (source: https://www.similarweb.com/corp/sales/). Similarweb's contacts data (100M+ surface, 470M FAQ-cited, 190+ countries, GDPR/CCPA-compliant) is positioned as a substitute for **Data (Pillar 1)** — direct phones, business emails, firmographics/technographics (source: https://www.similarweb.com/corp/sales/). Native Salesforce/HubSpot CRM enrichment and AI Lead Scoring overlap **ZoomInfo Operations** (source: https://www.similarweb.com/packages/sales/). API + MCP access on Business and Enterprise tiers overlaps **APIs & MCP**, with the differentiator that Similarweb sells the API standalone (source: https://www.similarweb.com/packages/sales/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Sales Signals (buyer intent, traffic shifts, technology changes, news, ad-network activity) and Web Intelligence touch **ZoomInfo Marketing's** intent and competitive-analysis surface without being a one-to-one substitute (source: https://www.similarweb.com/corp/sales/). Similarweb's "true 360 degree view of all digital activity" framing parallels **ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph** narrative but is signal-led rather than CRM/conversation-led — it does not unify first-party CRM, conversation, or behavioral data the way ZoomInfo claims to (source: https://www.similarweb.com/corp/teams/sales/).
- **No overlap:** Similarweb sells four product surfaces ZoomInfo does not — **Web Intelligence** (SEO/PPC/keyword research), **App Intelligence** (mobile app benchmarking), **Retail Intelligence** (Amazon SKU analytics), and **Stock Intelligence** (alternative data for hedge funds) (source: https://www.similarweb.com/). Conversely, ZoomInfo's **Chorus** (conversation intelligence) and **ZoomInfo Chat** categories did not surface on any reviewed Similarweb page (source: https://www.similarweb.com/corp/sales/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The G2 vs page returned a 403 block on extraction. Reddit/Vendr threads from SERP suggest buyers position Similarweb as a signal-led alternative when web-traffic data matters more than raw contact volume, but no Similarweb-authored vs page was extractable.
