## Summary

Monday CRM is an AI-first, no-code sales CRM built on the monday.com Work OS, sold to SMB and mid-market revenue teams (with an Ultimate tier for enterprise) that want lead management, visual pipelines, outreach, and post-sale workflows on one customizable surface. It is structurally complementary to ZoomInfo — Monday is a CRM of record + workflow layer, while ZoomInfo is the GTM intelligence + data graph that feeds it (Monday lists ZoomInfo as an enrichment integration on its own marketplace). Direct overlap is narrow but real: Monday's AI Lead Agent + AI Sales Agent and AI Notetaker compete adjacent to ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace and Chorus, and its Crunchbase-powered enrichment overlaps Operations-style data hygiene at the CRM layer only. Monday explicitly pitches its CRM as a ZoomInfo replacement in its "15 ZoomInfo alternatives" blog: "a CRM with built-in data enrichment and prospecting tools can replace it entirely" (source: https://monday.com/blog/crm-and-sales/zoominfo-alternatives/).

## Product Offerings

### monday CRM (core platform)

> **Wiki:** [[monday-crm--core]]

- **What it does:** AI-first, no-code CRM that lets revenue teams manage the full sales cycle — leads, contacts, accounts, deals — and post-sale workflows on a customizable visual platform built on the monday.com Work OS.
- **Key features:**
  - Customizable visual pipeline with drag-and-drop deal stages and no-code customization
  - Emails & Activities timeline with two-way Gmail/Outlook sync, AI email composer, mass emails
  - Sequences for multi-step outreach (Pro+) and Quotes & Invoices generation tied to CRM boards
  - Sales analytics dashboards with sales pipeline, sales funnel, leaderboard, and deal-stages widgets
  - Code-free automations (lead assignment, follow-ups, conditional status changes, round-robin)
  - Sales forecasting (Pro+) and lead scoring + team goals + account management (Ultimate)
  - iOS / Android mobile CRM with offline access, in-app calling, and call summaries
  - 500+ integrations (per homepage) / 150+ apps (per /crm/features)
- **Source URL:** https://monday.com/crm

### monday CRM AI Agents (AI Lead Agent + AI Sales Agent)

> **Wiki:** [[monday-crm--ai-agents]]

- **What it does:** Native AI agents that source/qualify leads against an ICP and run initial outreach (phone calls, SMS) so reps focus on closing rather than prospecting.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Lead Agent: sources, adds, and enriches prospects to your ICP on a recurring schedule
  - AI Sales Agent: phone calls and SMS for initial outreach and discovery, with handoff to reps
  - Multiple agents can be created with separate goals and instructions
  - Credit-based pricing (e.g. 10 credits per lead for AI Lead Agent)
  - AI Notetaker for live-call transcription, summaries, and action items
  - AI Sidekick (lite on Standard/Pro, plus on Ultimate) for context-aware assistance
- **Source URL:** https://support.monday.com/hc/en-us/articles/8675571451282-Unique-features-found-only-in-monday-CRM

### monday campaigns

> **Wiki:** [[monday-crm--campaigns]]

- **What it does:** Built-in marketing platform that lets users build, launch, and optimize on-brand email campaigns directly inside monday CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - On-brand email campaign creation
  - Campaign launch and optimization inside the CRM
  - Connects campaign performance back to deals/accounts in monday CRM
- **Source URL:** https://monday.com/crm

### Data Enrichment (Crunchbase-powered + marketplace data partners)

> **Wiki:** [[monday-crm--data-enrichment]]

- **What it does:** Auto-enriches accounts/leads with firmographic data from Crunchbase natively, with optional marketplace integrations to third-party contact-data vendors (ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Lusha) when contact-level enrichment is needed.
- **Key features:**
  - Crunchbase-powered native enrichment app (firmographic data)
  - Marketplace integrations for ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Lusha for contact / direct-dial / email enrichment
  - Auto-enrichment from website forms, social ad campaigns, and other lead sources
  - Chrome extension for adding contacts/accounts directly from Gmail
- **Source URL:** https://monday.com/crm/overview/integrations

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- "monday CRM adapts to your workflow, not the other way around" — set up in minutes, customize fields, stages, and automations without IT or developers (source: https://monday.com/crm).
- Built on the broader monday.com Work OS, supporting both pre-sale and post-sale processes (lead → renewal) on one platform — the FAQ explicitly contrasts this with HubSpot/Pipedrive (pre-sale only) and Salesforce/Zoho ("force you to adapt to them") (source: https://monday.com/crm).
- AI-native CRM with native AI Lead Agent + AI Sales Agent (phone/SMS), AI Notetaker, AI email composer/Sidekick, and AI timeline summaries (source: https://support.monday.com/hc/en-us/articles/8675571451282-Unique-features-found-only-in-monday-CRM).
- Public, transparent pricing starting at $12/seat/month with a 14-day free trial — explicitly contrasted with "request a demo" pricing as a red flag in their ZoomInfo-alternatives blog (source: https://monday.com/blog/crm-and-sales/zoominfo-alternatives/).
- Quoted customer outcomes: 80% increase in opportunity capture, 26X ROI from accurate data, 3,500+ hours saved by automating reporting and data entry (source: https://monday.com/crm).
- Built-in marketing platform (monday campaigns) inside the CRM for email campaign creation, launch, and optimization — extends past traditional CRM scope (source: https://monday.com/crm).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing) ↔ monday CRM Data Enrichment + automations + round-robin** — Crunchbase-powered firmographic enrichment, Merge Duplicates, lead routing automations, and conditional status changes overlap Operations' remit at the CRM record layer (source: https://monday.com/crm/overview/integrations; https://support.monday.com/hc/en-us/articles/8675571451282-Unique-features-found-only-in-monday-CRM). Monday does not, however, run a cross-system enrichment graph the way ZoomInfo Operations does.
  - **GTM Workspace (seller surface) ↔ monday CRM AI Lead Agent + AI Sales Agent** — Monday positions its native agents as a built-in alternative to a separate prospecting tool, framing them in their own blog as a way to "replace a separate prospecting and enrichment platform" (source: https://monday.com/blog/crm-and-sales/zoominfo-alternatives/).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) ↔ monday AI Notetaker** — AI Notetaker joins meetings, transcribes in real time, and produces summaries / action items inside monday CRM. Lightweight CI overlap with Chorus, but without Chorus's deal-coaching, trackers, and forecast modeling depth (source: https://support.monday.com/hc/en-us/articles/8675571451282-Unique-features-found-only-in-monday-CRM).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio ↔ monday campaigns** — built-in email campaign creation, launch, and optimization inside the CRM. Overlaps marketing execution, but lacks first-party intent data, ABM audience building, and the GTM Context Graph reasoning ZoomInfo Marketing/Studio centers on (source: https://monday.com/crm).
  - **Data (Pillar 1) (contact + company data) ↔ Crunchbase enrichment + ZoomInfo/Clearbit/Lusha marketplace integrations** — Monday CRM does NOT operate its own B2B contact/company database. Native enrichment is firmographic only (Crunchbase), and contact-level enrichment is offloaded to third parties — including, notably, ZoomInfo itself, which monday lists as a marketplace integration with the description "feed contact info into CRM automatically" (source: https://monday.com/crm/overview/integrations).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph** — no equivalent. Monday CRM is a system of record + workflow surface; it does not run a cross-customer B2B intelligence graph or process 1.5B+ data points daily, and there is no "reasons across CRM + conversations + intent + behavioral signals" analog (source: https://monday.com/crm).
  - **APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem with proprietary B2B intelligence** — monday.com has its own MCP server and Agent Factory, but they expose monday's work-management data, not a B2B contact/company graph. There is no equivalent to ZoomInfo's Enterprise APIs (Data, Marketing, Platform) for intelligence access (source: https://monday.com/crm).
  - **Reverse direction:** monday.com's broader Work OS sells into project management, IT service desks (monday service), dev / R&D (monday dev), HR, and operations use cases — entirely outside ZoomInfo's GTM remit (source: https://monday.com/crm).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No dedicated `monday CRM vs ZoomInfo` comparison page surfaced in SERP results; the strongest signal is monday's own "15 ZoomInfo alternatives for sales teams who need real accuracy" blog: _"Finding a ZoomInfo alternative isn't about swapping one contact database for another. It's about getting your team back to what actually drives revenue: conversations with real prospects through effective outbound calling. … unified platforms like monday CRM that combine prospecting, outreach, and deal management in one place. … a CRM with built-in data enrichment and prospecting tools can replace it entirely, saving you from juggling multiple subscriptions and siloed data."_ (source: https://monday.com/blog/crm-and-sales/zoominfo-alternatives/). The third-party Software Advice comparison rates monday CRM 4.7/5 (460 reviews) vs. ZoomInfo Sales 4.1/5 (317 reviews) on Value-for-Money and Customer Support (source: https://www.softwareadvice.com/compare/200338-ZoomInfo-sales/vs/348745-monday-crm/).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The only AI-first CRM your team will love" — grow revenue faster with an intuitive CRM built on code-free automations and intelligent workflows; the three pillars are "sell smarter, predict better, move faster."
- **Recurring proof points:** 80% increase in opportunity capture (EAC); 26X ROI from accurate data and consistent execution (Freedom); 3,500+ hours saved by automating reporting and data entry (Cenversa); 1,000+ G2 reviews with G2 Momentum Leader / Sales Performance Management Leader badges; 250,000+ customers worldwide on the broader monday.com platform (Canva, Coca-Cola, Lionsgate, HoltCat, Oxy logos); customer testimonials from The Shark Group, Inteli-k, IntoWork, CarbonWeb, ODUGlobal.
- **Tone / category framing:** AI-first CRM / unified revenue platform that adapts to your workflow rather than forcing teams into a rigid CRM template — explicitly contrasted with Salesforce, Zoho ("force you to adapt to them"), HubSpot, and Pipedrive (which "[don't] support … pre- and post-sales processes on one platform").
