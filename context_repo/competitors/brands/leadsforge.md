# Leadsforge

## Sub-products

- [[leadsforge--ai-lead-search|Leadsforge AI Lead Search Engine]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

Leadsforge is an AI lead-generation tool sold to SMB sales reps, SDRs, founders, and small agencies as a chat-based alternative to traditional B2B contact databases. It overlaps with ZoomInfo's Data foundation and ZoomInfo Sales prospecting workflow — claiming 500M+ contacts surfaced via natural-language ICP search and waterfall enrichment — but at $49/month on a credit-based plan rather than ZoomInfo's $15K+ annual contracts. It sits inside the broader Salesforge "Forge stack" (Mailforge, Primeforge, Warmforge, Agent Frank), so most depth comes from sibling products rather than Leadsforge itself, and the product is described as still pre-launch on multiple reviewed pages.

## Product Offerings

### Leadsforge — AI Lead Search Engine

> **Wiki:** [[leadsforge--ai-lead-search]]

- **What it does:** Chat-based prospecting tool that translates a plain-language ICP description into a verified contact list across 500M+ profiles using waterfall enrichment (source: https://www.leadsforge.ai/).
- **Key features:**
  - Conversational/chat-based ICP search (describe ICP in natural language; no filters or technical skills required)
  - Waterfall data enrichment across multiple data sources for emails, LinkedIn profiles, and mobile phone numbers
  - Search across 500M+ contacts
  - Company lookalike finder (input customer or competitor domains/names)
  - Competitor company-page follower discovery to surface high-intent prospects
  - Built-in intent signals and lead qualification via custom prompts
  - One-click export to Salesforge sequences or CSV; native CRM integrations (HubSpot, Salesforce, Folk, Breakcold, Attio)
  - 100 free credits on signup; SOC2 compliant; API access
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadsforge.ai/, https://www.salesforge.ai/directory/sales-tools/leadsforge, https://coldemailkit.com/tools/leadsforge

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Chat-like ICP definition replaces filter dashboards — "like ChatGPT, but for finding leads"; no technical skills required (source: https://www.leadsforge.ai/)
- Waterfall enrichment across multiple data sources in a single workflow improves fill rates vs single-source tools (source: https://coldemailkit.com/tools/leadsforge)
- Credit-based pricing starting at $49/month with 100 free credits and non-expiring credit rollover — third parties describe this as a 25x–65x cheaper price point than ZoomInfo (source: https://prospeo.io/s/leadsforge-vs-zoominfo)
- Tight one-click integration with Salesforge sequences plus the broader Forge stack (Mailforge, Primeforge, Warmforge, Agent Frank) for end-to-end outbound (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/integrations/leadsforge)
- Native lookalike search and competitor-follower discovery as ICP-expansion features (source: https://www.leadsforge.ai/)
- SOC2 compliant (source: https://www.leadsforge.ai/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Leadsforge directly competes with ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1)** and **ZoomInfo Sales** prospecting UI. The product claims 500M+ contacts with email, LinkedIn, and phone-number lookup via waterfall enrichment — the same job ZoomInfo Sales performs for SDRs and AEs, only via a chat interface (source: https://www.leadsforge.ai/, https://coldemailkit.com/tools/leadsforge).
- **Adjacent overlap:** When Leadsforge is paired with sibling Forge products (Salesforge sequences + Agent Frank as an AI SDR + Mailforge/Warmforge for deliverability), the combined stack approximates a workspace-style outbound surface adjacent to **GTM Workspace** — but Leadsforge by itself does not draft or execute outreach. Leadsforge also advertises "built-in intent signals & lead qualification via custom prompts," touching ZoomInfo's intent layer, though Prospeo describes these as "basic signals" and not depth-comparable to ZoomInfo's Bombora-powered intent (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/integrations/leadsforge, https://prospeo.io/s/leadsforge-vs-zoominfo).
- **No overlap:** Leadsforge does not sell into **GTM Studio** (marketer/RevOps audience orchestration, ABM, marketing automation), conversation intelligence (Chorus), CRM data quality / routing (ZoomInfo Operations), website chat (ZoomInfo Chat), or the **GTM Context Graph** reasoning layer — there is no first-party CRM/conversation/behavioral fusion product on any reviewed page (source: https://www.leadsforge.ai/, https://www.salesforge.ai/directory/sales-tools/leadsforge).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public Leadsforge-authored comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The strongest third-party framing comes from Prospeo: "LeadsForge wins on accessibility, price, and UX simplicity. ZoomInfo wins on data depth, intent signals, integrations, and workflow breadth. … A scooter vs a semi truck. … LeadsForge starts around $49/month on a credit-based model… ZoomInfo starts at $14,995/year." (source: https://prospeo.io/s/leadsforge-vs-zoominfo)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "#1 Search Engine To Find The Best Leads" — automate prospecting and waterfall data enrichment with one simple chat-like interface that requires no technical skills (source: https://www.leadsforge.ai/).
- **Recurring proof points:** 500M+ contacts; 4.6/5 G2 rating (homepage); 4.5/5 G2 from 12 SMB reviews + 4.0/5 Trustpilot from 11 reviews per third-party comparison; 100 free credits on signup; credits don't expire; SOC2 compliant; listed on the ZoomInfo App Marketplace per third-party source.
- **Tone / category framing:** AI-driven lead generation platform / "Search Engine For Leads" — positioned at the SMB / sub-$100/month end of the prospecting market, explicitly framed as a lighter, conversational alternative to enterprise data platforms rather than a full GTM platform.
