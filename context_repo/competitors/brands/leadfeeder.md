# Leadfeeder

## Sub-products

- [[leadfeeder--visitor-id|Leadfeeder (Visitor ID)]] — competes with WebSights
- [[leadfeeder--dealfront|Dealfront]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing

## Summary

Leadfeeder is a website-visitor identification platform now part of **Dealfront** (the European GTM platform). Its central pitch is "Identify up to 45% of companies visiting your website" — direct overlap with ZoomInfo WebSights. Leadfeeder publishes an explicit `/blog/comparisons/leadfeeder-vs-zoominfo/` post that names ZoomInfo as targeting "larger enterprises with broader data and integrations but opaque credit-based pricing and higher costs," vs. Leadfeeder's positioning for "SMBs thanks to transparent, low-cost plans, always-free option, live support, and practical tracking features." Public pricing is the strongest differentiator: Leadfeeder offers an always-free tier (7 days of data, limited features) and a Paid plan starting at **$99/month** for up to 50 leads, vs. ZoomInfo's reported $5K-$45K/year per the same blog. Leadfeeder does not compete on B2B contact data depth — it is a visitor-identification + ICP-targeting layer first.

## Product Offerings

### Web Visitors (visitor identification)

- **What it does:** Identifies up to 45% of companies visiting a website by enriching IP traffic with B2B firmographic data; the flagship product.
- **Key features:**
  - Identify up to 45% of anonymous visiting companies
  - Real-time visitor feed
  - Filter by ICP fit, geography, behavior
  - "Turn web visits into leads" use case
  - GDPR-compliant European-data focus
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/web-visitors/

### Solutions: Web Visitor Tracking

- **What it does:** Visitor-tracking solution that turns anonymous traffic into qualified accounts and CRM-ready leads.
- **Key features:**
  - Up to 45% of companies identified
  - Built-in CRM sync (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, MS Dynamics)
  - Real-time visitor stream
  - Segment + filter incoming traffic
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/solutions/web-visitor-tracking/

### Buying Intent Signals

- **What it does:** Surfaces buying-intent signals from website visitor behavior + Dealfront's intent dataset; helps sales prioritize accounts showing real activity.
- **Key features:**
  - Behavioral intent signals from website visits
  - Dealfront intent dataset integration
  - Account-level intent scoring
  - Sales prioritization workflow
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/solutions/buying-intent-signals/

### Leadfeeder AI (AI Insights)

- **What it does:** AI layer that summarizes visitor behavior, recommends next-best-actions, and produces account-level insights.
- **Key features:**
  - AI-generated visitor summaries
  - Next-best-action recommendations
  - ICP Insights for account fit
  - Browser extension for in-workflow access
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/features/ai-insights/

### ICP Insights / Build Audiences

- **What it does:** Identifies and targets ICP-matched companies — claims "drive up to 3× more ICP companies to website."
- **Key features:**
  - ICP-fit company identification
  - Audience targeting for outbound
  - Marketing campaign segmentation
  - Increase lead quality up to 30% (claimed)
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/features/icp-insights/ (referenced; not extracted)

### Optimize Campaigns

- **What it does:** Campaign optimization based on visitor data — claims "reduce cost per qualified lead by up to 35%."
- **Key features:**
  - Cost-per-qualified-lead reduction
  - Campaign optimization workflows
  - Marketing-side activation of visitor data
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/solutions/optimize-campaigns/ (referenced)

### [[leadfeeder--dealfront|Dealfront / Leadfeeder integration]]

- **What it does:** Leadfeeder is now positioned within Dealfront's broader European GTM platform — combined visitor identification + B2B data + intent + outbound.
- **Key features:**
  - Part of Dealfront's GTM platform
  - European data focus
  - GDPR + EU privacy compliance
  - Cross-product workflows
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Cross-industry; case studies feature Custobar (€180K pipeline from website intent) and a customer roster across European SMB through mid-market.
- **Company size called out:** SMB through Mid-market; the vs-page explicitly says Leadfeeder "wins for SMBs."
- **Persona / role focus:** Marketing leaders + demand-gen, SMB sales teams; "Generate high quality leads" (Marketing) and "Accelerate your pipeline" (Sales).
- **Use cases promoted:** Turn web visits into leads (45% identification); power sales intelligence (75% conversion rate improvement claim); build audiences (3× more ICP companies); prioritize top accounts (30% lead quality lift); optimize campaigns (35% CPL reduction).

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce (dedicated `/integrations/salesforce`), HubSpot, Pipedrive, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Zoho.
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Outreach, Salesloft (referenced via integrations).
- **Data / enrichment:** Dealfront B2B data; not a standalone contact database.
- **Other notable integrations:** Google Ads, Zapier, Slack, browser extension for in-workflow access. Google Ads integration positions Leadfeeder data as a paid-media targeting source.

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Up to 45% of website visitors identified by company — Leadfeeder's flagship metric (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/web-visitors/).
- Always-free tier (7 days data, limited features) — vs. ZoomInfo's "Contact Sales" gating (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/pricing/).
- Public pricing starting at $99/month — most transparent pricing in the visitor-ID category (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/blog/comparisons/leadfeeder-vs-zoominfo/).
- European data focus + GDPR-by-default compliance — strong fit for EU GTM teams uncomfortable with US-data-vendor licensing (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/).
- Custobar customer story: €180K pipeline from website intent (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/customers/custobar/).
- Browser extension for in-workflow access — distinctive UX layer.

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (WebSights / website visitor identification) ↔ Leadfeeder Web Visitors.** This is the core head-to-head — both pitch IP-to-company resolution as the primary product. Leadfeeder claims 45% identification rate; ZoomInfo's WebSights claims comparable but quote-only commercial terms. (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/web-visitors/, https://www.leadfeeder.com/blog/comparisons/leadfeeder-vs-zoominfo/)
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing intent / Buyer Intent ↔ Leadfeeder Buying Intent Signals.** Both surface visitor-behavior-derived intent at the account level. (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/solutions/buying-intent-signals/)
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing ABM advertising ↔ Leadfeeder ICP Insights / Audience Building + Google Ads integration.** Both activate B2B data for paid-media targeting. (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/features/icp-insights/)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace seller surface ↔ Leadfeeder Sales solutions + browser extension.** Leadfeeder feeds visitor intelligence into sales workflows but does not host a full prospecting workspace.
  - **ZoomInfo Operations ↔ Leadfeeder CRM sync.** Both syncs data into CRM but Leadfeeder's role is narrower (visitor data, not full enrichment).
- **No overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (Pillar 1)** — Leadfeeder doesn't sell a B2B contact database; relies on Dealfront's data layer.
  - **ZoomInfo Chorus** — no CI product.
  - **ZoomInfo APIs & MCP / agent ecosystem at the data layer** — Leadfeeder has no MCP analogue.
  - **Sales engagement / sequencing** — Leadfeeder integrates with these tools but doesn't replace them.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Direct from the vs-blog: _"Leadfeeder wins for SMBs thanks to transparent, low-cost plans, always-free option, live support, and practical tracking features; ZoomInfo targets larger enterprises with broader data and integrations but has opaque, credit-based pricing and higher costs."_ The blog also hosts a side-by-side feature + pricing + integration comparison that explicitly declares Leadfeeder the winner on pricing transparency and a tie on integrations. (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/blog/comparisons/leadfeeder-vs-zoominfo/)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Turn web visits into leads. Identify up to 45% of companies visiting your website."
- **Recurring proof points:** 45% visitor identification rate; 75% conversion rate improvement; 3× ICP companies driven to website; 30% lead quality lift; 35% CPL reduction; Custobar €180K pipeline.
- **Tone / category framing:** "B2B website visitor tracking tool" — Leadfeeder deliberately stays in a focused product category vs ZoomInfo's all-in-one frame. The Dealfront merger is positioned as a European GTM platform play; Leadfeeder retains its standalone branding for now.

## Flags & Limitations

- vs_brand_present — Leadfeeder publishes a strong vs-ZoomInfo blog with explicit verdict ("Leadfeeder wins for SMBs"). The blog uses third-party-sourced pricing claims for ZoomInfo ($5K–$45K/year) which may not reflect actual deal terms.
- manual_review:company_snapshot_partial — Leadfeeder's founding date, HQ, and Dealfront combined-entity employee size were not surfaced on reviewed pages.
- manual_review:dealfront_ownership — Leadfeeder is now part of Dealfront. Strategic future of Leadfeeder as a standalone brand is unclear; some product surfaces may consolidate into Dealfront.
