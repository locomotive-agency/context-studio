## Summary

Dealfront is a European, GDPR-anchored B2B "lead generation engine" — IP-based website visitor identification plus a 60M-company / 400M-contact prospecting database, native B2B display ads, AI-prompted ICP scoring, and trigger-event intent — sold to marketing, sales/SDR and RevOps teams. As of 2026 the brand has been re-unified under Leadfeeder; `www.dealfront.com` 301-redirects to `leadfeeder.com/dealfront/`, the legal entity remains Dealfront Group GmbH, and every page now banners "Dealfront is now Leadfeeder." Direct overlap with ZoomInfo is heaviest on ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM + visitor data), the Data foundation (smaller scale, EU-skewed), and the Intent Data + WebSights surfaces; Dealfront does not compete in conversation intelligence (Chorus), the APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem, or unified GTM Context Graph reasoning, but it does run a native B2B display-ad platform that ZoomInfo does not. No public Dealfront-vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced in research — see flags.

## Product Offerings

### Web Visitors

- **What it does:** B2B website visitor identification — reveals which companies are visiting the website and pushes high-fit accounts into the CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - IP-to-company identification, including identification of remote-worker traffic
  - Identifies up to 45% of companies visiting the website
  - Real-time alerts (Slack or email) when target accounts engage with key pages
  - 50+ behavioral and firmographic filters to qualify visitors
  - Bot/ISP/low-quality traffic filtering with a daily ML-refreshed IP-to-company database
  - Automatic CRM sync of identified companies
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/web-visitors/

### Target

- **What it does:** Prospecting database for building company and contact lists outside of website-visitor traffic.
- **Key features:**
  - Global database of 60M companies and 400M verified contacts
  - 100+ firmographic, technographic, and behavioral filters
  - Decision-maker search by job role, seniority, and location
  - Reveals and exports emails, phone numbers, and LinkedIn profiles
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/

### Campaigns (Promote)

- **What it does:** Native B2B display-advertising module — runs IP-targeted programmatic display ads at named accounts and ICP audiences built inside the platform.
- **Key features:**
  - IP-based display ads to specific company audiences
  - LinkedIn audience activation with claimed >90% audience match
  - Ad-campaign attribution back to website visits
  - Automatically retargets open CRM opportunities
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/

### Lists

- **What it does:** Account-list management for prioritizing and acting on best-fit accounts identified via web visits or list-building.
- **Key features:**
  - Custom feeds (e.g., companies visiting pricing pages, ad campaigns)
  - Automatic CRM/sales-team sharing of feeds
  - Hottest leads bubble to the top of the list
  - Email or Slack alerts when specific companies visit
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/

### Leadfeeder AI

- **What it does:** AI layer that connects buyer fit, intent, contacts, and signal alerts so sales and marketing can prioritize accounts most likely to convert via natural-language prompts.
- **Key features:**
  - Natural-language Q&A on visitors
  - AI Company Insights — ICP-fit answers
  - AI List Enrichment — score companies 0–10 against custom prompts (up to 50,000 records per list)
  - Contact discovery by described role rather than literal job title
  - Custom alert prompts (e.g., "Alert me on Series B funding")
  - Free single-record CRM sync without consuming enrichment credits
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/features/ai-insights

### Buying Intent Signals (Trigger Events)

- **What it does:** Third-party trigger-event monitoring layered on first-party website intent — flags companies hitting predefined buying signals such as hiring, funding, expansion, partnerships, and website updates.
- **Key features:**
  - 33 predefined Trigger Events including job offers, financials, partnerships, website updates
  - First-party website intent + third-party trigger events combined
  - Real-time alerts when target accounts hit chosen signals
  - ICP-aware account clustering ("active focus", "future pipeline", "ICP cluster")
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadfeeder.com/solutions/buying-intent-signals

- Free-forever Lite tier (up to 100 companies/month) — public, low-friction entry point at €0 (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/pricing/)
- Transparent EUR-denominated company-tier pricing — 12 published tiers for Web Visitor Identification (€99–€1,199/mo) plus a Platform plan starting at €399/mo (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/pricing/)
- Native B2B display-advertising module ("Promote" / Campaigns) with IP-targeting and >90% LinkedIn audience match (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/)
- Natural-language AI layer (Leadfeeder AI) for ad-hoc visitor questions, ICP scoring, and custom-prompt alerting (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/features/ai-insights)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM) + WebSights (website visitor data)** — Dealfront's flagship Web Visitors + Campaigns (Promote) modules align directly with ZoomInfo Marketing's website-visitor + ABM activation use cases (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/web-visitors/).
  - **Data foundation (contacts + companies)** — Dealfront's Target database of 60M companies / 400M contacts competes directly with ZoomInfo's data foundation, though at smaller stated scale and skewed toward "Comprehensive European B2B data" (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/).
  - **Intent Data + ZoomInfo Sales (intent + prospecting workflows)** — Dealfront's 33 Trigger Events plus AI-prompted alerting competes with ZoomInfo's intent + signal stack and the AI agent layer surface inside GTM Workspace (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/solutions/buying-intent-signals).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Dealfront's marketing solution promotes natural-language audience building and CRM-/ad-channel activation — adjacent to GTM Studio's natural-language audience and play orchestration — but Dealfront does not pitch a unified GTM Context Graph reasoning across CRM + conversations + intent (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/solutions/marketing).
- **No overlap:**
  - Native B2B display advertising (Campaigns / Promote) — ZoomInfo does not operate its own programmatic display-ad platform, while Dealfront sells ad serving directly inside the same product that does identification and prospecting (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/dealfront/).
  - EUR-denominated, EU-hosted, GDPR-Legitimate-Interest legal framing — ZoomInfo does not lead with EU-residency / GDPR Legitimate-Interest as a top-line differentiator (source: https://www.leadfeeder.com/gdpr/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Reviewed Dealfront/Leadfeeder pages do not include a `dealfront.com/vs/zoominfo` (or equivalent) page.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Turn your website into a lead generation engine" — reveal companies visiting your site, prioritize the ones showing real buying intent, and activate them via CRM, alerts, and B2B display ads, all GDPR-compliant and EU-hosted.
- **Recurring proof points:** 15,000+ companies use Dealfront/Leadfeeder; identify up to 45% of companies from website traffic; up to 75% MQL-to-SQL conversion-rate lift; up to 35% reduction in cost per qualified lead; 4-minute time-to-value from setup to first identified company; named customers Custobar (€180K pipeline), Reachdesk, Schneider Electric, AutomatiKing, DNA, and Wolters Kluwer.
- **Tone / category framing:** Positions itself as the "lead generation engine" for the B2B website. The site explicitly leads with the website-as-growth-channel narrative ("Your Website Is Your Best Growth Channel: It's Time We Built for That") and positions ICP-fit visitor identification as the entry point for both sales and marketing motions.
