## Summary

Acxiom is a consumer / customer data and identity company (owned by IPG) selling to B2C marketers, CMOs, and martech/CDP owners at large enterprise brands across automotive, healthcare, pharma, financial services, retail, travel, and telecommunications. Its portfolio centers on Real ID identity resolution, third-party consumer data (InfoBase, Personicx, audience packages), customer data management services, and vendor-agnostic CDP / martech / Salesforce implementation services — all positioned around "people-based marketing" rather than B2B GTM. Crossover with ZoomInfo is tangential: only the data-foundation pillar overlaps directionally (consumer attributes vs. B2B contacts/companies) and the identity-graph plumbing is similar in concept but applied to a different ICP. Acxiom does not compete with ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, Chorus, APIs & MCP, or any seller-facing module.

## Product Offerings

### Real ID / Real Identity (Identity solutions)

> **Wiki:** [[acxiom--real-id]]

- **What it does:** Connected consumer identity solution that recognizes and connects people across online and offline touchpoints to power people-based marketing.
- **Key features:**
  - 2.6B connected consumers globally; 260M digitally addressable US consumers
  - 5T+ records processed per month; 10K+ unique attributes
  - Recognizes unauthenticated website/app visitors without extra tags
  - Up to 1.7x greater reach of first-party data on top DSPs
  - Interoperable with other leading identifiers and major DSPs
  - Native integrations with leading data clouds and marketing platforms
  - Brand-controlled first-party identity graph using Real ID as foundation
  - Winner of 2024 MarTech Breakthrough Award
- **Source URL:** https://www.acxiom.com/identity-resolution/real-id/

### Consumer Marketing Data (InfoBase / Personicx / third-party data)

> **Wiki:** [[acxiom--consumer-marketing-data]]

- **What it does:** Third-party consumer data, segments, and audiences brands use to enrich profiles, target, and personalize across channels.
- **Key features:**
  - 260M digitally addressable US consumers; 2.6B reachable people globally (~68% of digital population)
  - 10K+ unique consumer attributes
  - InfoBase consumer insights database (positioned as "gold standard")
  - Personicx segmentation across lifestyle and behavioral dimensions
  - Attribute categories: demographic, socioeconomic, behavioral, interests, attitudinal, financial, health
  - Pre-built seasonal audience data packages (e.g. Game Day, Back to School)
  - 35+ markets covered globally
  - Tied to Real ID for connected, ethically sourced data
- **Source URL:** https://www.acxiom.com/customer-data/

### Customer Data Management

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Acxiom is a B2C consumer-data and identity company; it does not sell into any ZoomInfo product line on a head-to-head substitute basis. (See `manual_review:icp_mismatch` flag.)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Data foundation:** Acxiom Consumer Marketing Data (260M US consumers, 10K+ attributes) is conceptually parallel to ZoomInfo's Data (Pillar 1) (500M B2B contacts, 100M companies, 30K+ technographics) — but with a wholly different audience: consumer attributes (financial, health, demographic, attitudinal) versus B2B firmographics, contacts, direct dials, and emails (source: https://www.acxiom.com/customer-data/).
  - **Identity stitching of first- and third-party data:** Acxiom Real ID stitches first-party customer data with third-party reference data — directionally similar plumbing to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, but applied to consumer (B2C) identity rather than B2B account/contact reasoning (source: https://www.acxiom.com/identity-resolution/real-id/).
  - **CRM data quality / enrichment:** Acxiom's Customer Data Management services touch the same problem space as ZoomInfo Operations (data hygiene, standardization, identity matching, enrichment), but Acxiom delivers as a managed service for consumer marketing databases, not a self-serve B2B SaaS module (source: https://www.acxiom.com/data-management/).
- **No overlap:**
  - GTM Workspace (seller front-end, AI-drafted outreach, prioritized accounts) — Acxiom has no AE/SDR-facing prospecting workspace; their "Prospecting Solutions" line is consumer-acquisition data, not B2B SDR/AE tooling (source: https://www.acxiom.com/solutions/).
  - Chorus / conversation intelligence — no call recording, transcription, or rep-coaching capability surfaced (source: https://www.acxiom.com/solutions/).
  - APIs & MCP for AI agents / GTM engineers — no public-facing developer API, MCP server, or self-serve integration surfaced; activation is through partner integrations and managed services (source: https://www.acxiom.com/).
  - Data clean rooms / collaborative services — Acxiom-only line, not a ZoomInfo product (source: https://www.acxiom.com/solutions/).
  - Salesforce implementation, martech services, agency/SI work — Acxiom services business; ZoomInfo does not sell SI services (source: https://www.acxiom.com/solutions/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._
