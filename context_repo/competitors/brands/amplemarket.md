# Amplemarket

## Sub-products

- [[amplemarket--ai-platform|Amplemarket Duo (AI Copilot family)]] — competes with GTM Workspace
- [[amplemarket--data|Amplemarket Searcher / Data]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1) / ZoomInfo Sales

## Summary

Amplemarket is an all-in-one AI sales engagement platform sold to sellers (AEs, SDRs, BDRs), sales leaders, founders, RevOps, and marketers — predominantly at SMB and mid-market technology / SaaS companies, with growing enterprise traction. It overlaps ZoomInfo's Data foundation (200M+ contacts, weekly refresh), the seller-front-end role of ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (Duo Copilot + Searcher + Multichannel Sequences), and ZoomInfo's intent / signals layer (20+ first- and third-party signal types including Slack communities, G2 reviews, and ingested Demandbase / 6sense intent). The most direct head-to-head pitch (sourced from Amplemarket's first-party `/competitors/zoominfo` page) is that Amplemarket replaces a "$50K-100K+ ZoomInfo stack" (data + Outreach + warmup + social tool + dialer) at "73-79% lower stack cost" with a 5-tool deliverability suite ZoomInfo lacks.

## Product Offerings

### Duo Copilot (and Duo product family — Copywriter, Inbox, Voice)

- **What it does:** AI sales agent that captures contact-level buying signals across the web/social/CRM, researches accounts, and prepares hyper-personalized multichannel outreach sequences for human reps to approve or edit (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/duo).
- **Key features:**
  - Contact-level signal tracking (job changes, G2 review activity, Slack community posts, social engagement, website visits, closed-lost reactivation, Champion job moves, competitor research)
  - Auto-generates multichannel sequences (email + call + social) tailored per signal and persona
  - Duo Copywriter — drafts personalized first-touch emails using intent-based hooks
  - Duo Inbox — auto-drafts reply emails for every prospect message (Elite-tier add-on)
  - Duo Voice — AI-cloned voice messages for social DMs at scale
  - Learns from rep feedback (dismissed leads, edited messages) and applies learnings across signals/personas
  - Human-in-the-loop by design — never sends without rep approval
- **Source URL:** https://www.amplemarket.com/duo

### Searcher (AI lead generation + B2B database)

- **What it does:** Natural-language lead-generation surface backed by Amplemarket's B2B database — describe a target ICP in plain text and AI returns matching contacts and accounts ranked by buying signals (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/searcher).
- **Key features:**
  - Natural-language prompt-to-lead-list (e.g., "Sales Directors in NYC working in startups <$10M revenue")
  - 200M+ verified global contacts with weekly refresh of 70M+ contacts (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/competitors/zoominfo)
  - <3% average email bounce rate; 96.5% phone number accuracy
  - GDPR, CCPA, SOC 2 certified
  - 30+ ICP filters; bulk export of up to 2,500 leads from social posts / events / ads
  - AI surfaces decision-makers showing real buying signals as the prompt is typed
- **Source URL:** https://www.amplemarket.com/searcher

### Intent Signals

- **What it does:** Tracks 20+ types of first- and third-party buying signals across Slack communities, G2, job changes, company activity, CRM, website activity, product usage, events, paid campaigns, email, and external intent providers — and triggers Duo-prepared outreach when signals fire (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/signals).
- **Key features:**
  - Slack community member + message tracking (signal source ZoomInfo does not cover)
  - Competitor G2 review tracking (negative reviews trigger competitive outreach)
  - Custom CRM signals — point Duo at any Salesforce / HubSpot CRM report or field as a signal source
  - Website activity signals: high-intent page visits, abandoned form, account-level interest surge, content downloads
  - Product-usage signals: trial ending, feature adoption velocity, freemium usage spike / drop
  - External-intent ingestion: "Intent from Demandbase, 6sense and more" — third-party intent stored in CRM can trigger Amplemarket outreach
- **Source URL:** https://www.amplemarket.com/signals

### Multichannel Sequences (engagement + Unibox)

- **What it does:** AI-built multichannel outreach sequences across email, phone, social DMs, WhatsApp/iMessage, and parallel-dialer calls; conditional logic adapts per prospect; Unibox centralizes all reply threads (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/multichannel-selling).
- **Key features:**
  - AI-assisted sequence builder (target + goal + structure → multichannel draft in seconds)
  - Conditional steps (e.g., "no email → send social message"; "has phone → call next")
  - Email + phone + social + WhatsApp + iMessage channels in one sequence
  - Parallel Dialing — connect external dialer to run calls alongside email / social
  - A/B testing of variations across templates
  - Unibox — centralized multi-channel inbox for replies (email + social DMs)
  - Templates marketplace and reusable best-performing sequences
- **Source URL:** https://www.amplemarket.com/multichannel-selling

### Deliverability Optimization (Domain Health Center, Booster, Mailbox Recommendation, Spam Checker)

- **What it does:** End-to-end email deliverability suite — monitor mailbox health, warm up new mailboxes, run inbox-placement tests, and recommend the best mailbox for each campaign (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/pricing).
- **Key features:**
  - Domain Health Center — monitor all mailboxes / domains in one place
  - Deliverability Booster — AI email warmup network
  - Inbox Placement Tests / Email Spam Checker
  - Mailbox Recommendation — pick the optimal mailbox per send
  - Multiple mailboxes per user (2 on Startup, 4 on Growth, 6+ on Elite)
  - Positioned as something ZoomInfo lacks entirely — "5-tool deliverability stack where ZoomInfo has zero" (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/competitors/zoominfo)
- **Source URL:** https://www.amplemarket.com/pricing

- Contact-level (vs account-level) buying signals — 100+ contact-level signal types tracked vs ZoomInfo Copilot's company-level focus (job moves only at contact level) (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/competitors/zoominfo).
- End-to-end deliverability stack baked in (Domain Health Center, Booster, Inbox Placement Tests, Mailbox Recommendation) — explicitly framed as a category ZoomInfo does not cover (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/competitors/zoominfo).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation** — Searcher / B2B database (200M+ contacts, 70M+ weekly refresh, <3% bounce, 96.5% phone accuracy) directly attacks ZoomInfo's Data foundation pillar with comparative claims on bounce rate, refresh cadence, and phone accuracy (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/searcher).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (legacy classic UI) + GTM Workspace (next-gen seller front-end)** — Duo Copilot + Searcher + Multichannel Sequences are positioned as the seller's daily front-end (prioritized accounts, AI-drafted outreach, multichannel execution) — the exact category framing GTM Workspace targets. The vs-page explicitly compares Amplemarket to GTM Workspace (with AI agents) feature-by-feature (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/competitors/zoominfo).
  - **Chorus / intent + signals layer** — Amplemarket's Signals product tracks 20+ first- and third-party intent signal types and explicitly lists "Intent from Demandbase, 6sense and more" as ingested external sources, directly competing with ZoomInfo's intent-data leadership (Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader) and with Chorus as a conversation-driven signal source (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/signals).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM)** — Amplemarket markets a /amplemarket-for-marketers persona track ("boost conversions / multiply growth") that consumes intent signals and routes high-intent leads to reps — adjacent to ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM playbook, but Amplemarket has no native ad-platform audience push surfaced on reviewed pages (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing)** — Bi-directional Salesforce / HubSpot sync, Custom CRM signals against any field, real-time email validation, and data enrichment touch the CRM-hygiene job ZoomInfo Operations sells, but Amplemarket positions this as "fuel the outbound" rather than "manage the CRM" (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/competitors/zoominfo).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio** (natural-language audience building + ABM play orchestration) — no NL audience-builder for marketers/RevOps/GTM-engineers comparable to GTM Studio surfaced on reviewed pages; Amplemarket's NL prompt is in Searcher (lead generation) for sellers, not a marketer/ops orchestration surface (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/searcher).
  - **APIs & MCP (Enterprise API + MCP server)** — no public developer API, MCP server, or "Ask AI"-style developer surface listed on reviewed pages. Amplemarket has /ai/chatgpt, /ai/claude, /ai/perplexity, /ai/gemini, /ai/grok marketing landing pages but these are "Ask AI about Amplemarket" pages, not a developer/agent API (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence — call recording / transcription / coaching)** — Duo Voice generates outbound AI voice messages for social DMs; no call-recording, transcription, or call-coaching product surfaced. Both vendors have a dialer per the vs-page Outbound Dialer comparison, but Amplemarket has no CI product (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/duo).
  - **ZoomInfo Chat (data-powered website chat)** — no website-chat product surfaced on reviewed pages.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** From Amplemarket's first-party `/competitors/zoominfo` page: "Looking for the best ZoomInfo alternative? 73-79% lower stack cost replacing ZoomInfo + 4-5 tools. <3% email bounce vs ZoomInfo's reported 15%. 70M+ data refreshes weekly vs ZoomInfo's monthly updates. 100+ contact-level signals against ZoomInfo's account-level. 5-tool deliverability stack where ZoomInfo has zero." Followed by: "$15K-40K+ for data alone — and you still need Outreach ($15K+), a social tool, a warmup tool, and a dialer. Most teams pay $50K-100K+ for the full ZoomInfo stack. ... Save ~$216K/yr — A 25-user ZoomInfo stack runs $296K-$373K/yr across 5-6 tools. Amplemarket replaces them all at ~$80K/yr" (source: https://www.amplemarket.com/competitors/zoominfo). The pitch is entirely cost-consolidation + data-quality framed; it does not engage ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, Workbounce, Chorus, GTM Studio, GTM Workspace, or APIs/MCP positioning by name.
