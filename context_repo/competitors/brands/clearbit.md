## Summary

Clearbit was acquired by HubSpot in November 2023 and is in active transition to **HubSpot Breeze Intelligence**, the rebrand under HubSpot's Breeze AI platform. Standalone Clearbit free tools are being sunset (April 30, 2025: Free Platform, Weekly Visitor Report, TAM Calculator, Clearbit Connect, Slack Integration; December 1, 2025: Logo API). Clearbit publishes a dedicated `/lp/use-cases/clearbit-vs-zoominfo` page that frames the difference as "Clearbit integrates with all your MarTech stack" vs "ZoomInfo MarketingOS designed for use with ZoomInfo's own RevOS Suite" — the positional pitch is openness vs. ecosystem lock-in. Functionally, Clearbit is now best understood as Breeze Intelligence inside HubSpot: company + contact enrichment with 40+ firmographic / demographic / technographic attributes, form shortening, ad targeting, intent-based outreach, and lead scoring/routing. Pricing as a standalone product is being phased out in favor of HubSpot's tier model + HubSpot Credits for AI features.

## Product Offerings

### Data Enrichment

- **What it does:** Company and contact data enrichment for CRM and MAP records — global coverage, normalized fields, role/seniority standardization, corporate hierarchies.
- **Key features:**
  - Global coverage across countries and languages
  - 40+ firmographic, demographic, technographic attributes (per Breeze Intelligence transition note)
  - Best-in-class industry classification (6-digit NAICS, GICS, SIC)
  - Corporate hierarchies (parent + subsidiaries)
  - Normalized role + seniority
  - Continuous data refresh option in Breeze Intelligence
- **Source URL:** https://clearbit.com/solutions/data-enrichment

### Ad Targeting + Conversion Tracking

- **What it does:** Personalize ad campaigns and improve performance using firmographic + intent data; native conversion tracking with Facebook + Google.
- **Key features:**
  - Personalize ad campaigns based on firmographic data
  - Include or reject prospects based on company-fit factors
  - Conversion tracking with Facebook + Google
  - Reduce ad spend on poor-fit prospects
- **Source URL:** https://clearbit.com/solutions/ad-targeting

### Intent-Based Outreach (Buying Intent + Visitor Tracking)

- **What it does:** Identifies anonymous website visitors, scores them against ICP, and triggers personalized outreach via email and dynamic website changes.
- **Key features:**
  - IP-intelligence-based visitor identification
  - Visitor dashboard highlights ICP-matched companies
  - Hyper-personalized email + dynamic website experiences
  - Real-time intent signal capture
- **Source URL:** https://clearbit.com/solutions/intent-based-outreach

### Forms (Form Shortening + Enrichment)

- **What it does:** Reduce form-fill friction by enriching email addresses into full company + contact context — only requires fields Clearbit can't enrich.
- **Key features:**
  - Dynamic form shortening
  - Auto-enrichment from email address alone
  - 100% data coverage on lead capture
  - Works with HubSpot, Marketo, Pardot
- **Source URL:** https://clearbit.com/lp/use-cases/clearbit-for-forms

### Lead Scoring + Routing

- **What it does:** Real-time lead scoring + assignment to the right rep based on enriched data — corporate hierarchy + ICP-fit + intent.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time lead scoring
  - Automated routing rules
  - Multi-factor scoring (fit + intent + behavior)
  - CRM-native assignment
- **Source URL:** https://clearbit.com/lp/use-cases/clearbit-vs-zoominfo

### Breeze Intelligence (HubSpot integration — successor brand)

- **What it does:** The post-acquisition product surface — Clearbit data + intent inside HubSpot's CRM, available as a Breeze AI agent and as native enrichment.
- **Key features:**
  - 40+ firmographic / demographic / technographic attributes
  - Continuous CRM record refresh
  - Native to HubSpot Smart CRM (no separate UI)
  - Buyer intent inside HubSpot
  - TAM market sizing
- **Source URL:** https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Cross-industry; case-study customers include Greenhouse, Livestorm.
- **Company size called out:** SMB through Mid-market — Clearbit's data was popular with companies that didn't want ZoomInfo enterprise contracts.
- **Persona / role focus:** Marketing operations, demand-gen marketers, RevOps; less seller-focused than ZoomInfo.
- **Use cases promoted:** Data enrichment for CRM hygiene; ad targeting based on company fit; identify-and-engage anonymous website visitors; form shortening for higher conversion; lead scoring + routing.

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** HubSpot (now native via Breeze Intelligence), Salesforce.
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Marketo (legacy), Pardot (legacy).
- **Data / enrichment:** Clearbit IS the enrichment layer (now Breeze Intelligence).
- **Other notable integrations:** Facebook Ads, Google Ads, HubSpot Smart CRM (native), Zapier-style connectors via HubSpot ecosystem. Less emphasis on standalone integrations post-acquisition.

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- "Integrates seamlessly with all your current — and future — MarTech stack applications" — explicit pitch against ZoomInfo's "designed for use with ZoomInfo's own RevOS Suite" (source: https://clearbit.com/lp/use-cases/clearbit-vs-zoominfo).
- Native HubSpot integration via Breeze Intelligence — Clearbit data is now a first-class HubSpot product, not a third-party integration (source: https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit).
- Form shortening ("Dynamic Form Shortening") — Clearbit's core demand-gen UX innovation, copied widely (source: https://clearbit.com/lp/use-cases/clearbit-for-forms).
- Strong industry-classification depth (6-digit NAICS, GICS, SIC) and corporate hierarchies (source: https://clearbit.com/).
- Marketing-ops orientation — Clearbit's pitch is to marketers and ops teams rather than sellers, a deliberate counter to ZoomInfo's seller-first frame.
- Free tools as a historical lead-gen flywheel — though sunsetting, this established Clearbit's SMB / dev-friendly reputation that ZoomInfo never had.

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (Pillar 1) ↔ Clearbit Data Enrichment.** Direct head-to-head on B2B contact + company data. Clearbit's vs-page acknowledges ZoomInfo has a larger contact database but argues Clearbit's smaller-but-more-precise approach plus integrations openness is a better fit for marketing teams. (source: https://clearbit.com/lp/use-cases/clearbit-vs-zoominfo)
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (FormComplete + WebSights) ↔ Clearbit Forms + Visitor Identification.** Direct overlap on form enrichment + anonymous-visitor identification. Clearbit's form-shortening UX predates and arguably inspired ZoomInfo FormComplete. (source: https://clearbit.com/lp/use-cases/clearbit-for-forms)
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM advertising) ↔ Clearbit Ad Targeting.** Both pitch advertising-side activation of B2B data — Clearbit native to Facebook + Google ads. (source: https://clearbit.com/solutions/ad-targeting)
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data hygiene) ↔ Clearbit Enrichment + Lead Scoring/Routing.** Both target CRM hygiene + automated routing.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo APIs ↔ Clearbit API (legacy) + Breeze Intelligence in HubSpot.** Clearbit had a strong developer-friendly API reputation pre-acquisition; that strategic surface is now folded into HubSpot's developer story.
- **No overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace seller surface** — Clearbit has no seller-facing prospecting workspace (unlike LeadIQ or Apollo).
  - **ZoomInfo Chorus** — no CI product.
  - **ZoomInfo APIs & MCP / agent ecosystem at the data layer** — Clearbit's developer story is deprecated; Breeze AI agents are the successor surface.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Direct framing on `/lp/use-cases/clearbit-vs-zoominfo`: _"Clearbit: Integrates seamlessly with all your current – and future – MarTech stack applications. Popular with marketing and ops teams using company fit and intent to focus their funnel. Free tools and flexible pricing make it easy to start and grow with Clearbit."_ vs. _"ZoomInfo MarketingOS: Designed for use with ZoomInfo's own RevOS Suite. Popular with outbound sales teams who use their phone numbers for prospecting. License-based pricing and strict contact terms."_ The framing positions ZoomInfo as a closed ecosystem with restrictive licensing vs Clearbit/HubSpot as an open MarTech enrichment layer. (source: https://clearbit.com/lp/use-cases/clearbit-vs-zoominfo)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Clearbit has joined HubSpot. A data foundation you can build on" — the homepage explicitly leads with the HubSpot acquisition.
- **Recurring proof points:** Customer logos include Greenhouse, Livestorm; vs-page leans heavily on differentiator framing rather than customer numbers.
- **Tone / category framing:** Clearbit is in active brand transition. The standalone Clearbit identity is being absorbed into HubSpot's Breeze Intelligence product. The `/lp/use-cases/clearbit-vs-zoominfo` framing positions Clearbit as the marketing-team-friendly, integrations-open alternative to ZoomInfo's outbound-sales-team-and-licensing-heavy MarketingOS.
