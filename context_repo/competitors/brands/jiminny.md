## Summary

Jiminny is a conversation and revenue intelligence platform aimed at mid-market and growth-stage revenue teams (sales reps, managers, execs, RevOps, and CS), offering AI call recording, transcription, coaching, and pipeline insights on top of the customer's own CRM and conversation data. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Chorus module (conversation intelligence and coaching) and touches the seller-execution surfaces of ZoomInfo Sales / Workspace, but does not sell B2B contact data, intent, ABM orchestration, or APIs/MCP — the data foundation under Jiminny is first-party conversations plus CRM, not a third-party B2B graph. Jiminny publishes transparent seat-based pricing ($0 / $42 / $83 per month) and emphasizes coaching, ease of adoption, and Customer Success as differentiators against Gong-class enterprise tools and Chorus.

## Product Offerings

### Conversation Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[jiminny--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered platform that records, transcribes, and analyzes sales and customer calls (phone and video) plus emails, surfacing insights for revenue teams.
- **Key features:**
  - Instant AI deal & call insights via "Ask Jiminny" on any call or multi-call deal
  - Automatic CRM logging of calls, transcripts, and insights
  - Proactive deal risk alerts for stalled deals and missing next steps
  - Team performance benchmarking and coaching opportunity surfacing
  - Native Salesforce and HubSpot apps plus integrations with other CRMs
  - 60+ language transcription support
- **Source URL:** https://jiminny.com/product/conversation-intelligence

### Revenue Intelligence (also branded as GTM Intelligence in nav)

> **Wiki:** [[jiminny--revenue-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Software that turns conversation and CRM data into pipeline visibility, forecasting, and risk alerts for sales reps, managers, and execs.
- **Key features:**
  - Deal intelligence with stage progression, forecast categories, and trigger detection
  - Ask Jiminny natural-language Q&A across calls, deals, and pipeline
  - Native Salesforce and HubSpot CRM sync (plus other CRMs)
  - At-risk deal alerts triggered by competitor mentions, missing next steps, or cold communication
  - GTM insights for Product and Marketing teams from searchable conversation data
  - Data-driven sales forecasting based on real-time activity
- **Source URL:** https://jiminny.com/product/revenue-intelligence

### AI Notetaker

> **Wiki:** [[jiminny--ai-notetaker]]

- **What it does:** AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, summarizes, and surfaces action items from sales and customer calls, team huddles, one-on-ones, and coaching sessions.
- **Key features:**
  - Instant call summaries and AI-generated action items
  - High-accuracy speaker-distinguished transcription
  - Ask Jiminny anything (timeline, competitors, objections) across meetings
  - In-person recording via mobile app and desktop upload
  - Native Salesforce and HubSpot apps for in-CRM access
  - Integrations with Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zapier, and dialers
  - GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2 compliance with EU or US data residency
  - 60+ languages auto-detected and transcribed
- **Source URL:** https://jiminny.com/product/ai-notetaker

### Sales Coaching (feature)

> **Wiki:** [[jiminny--sales-coaching]]

- **What it does:** Coaching workflow built on top of conversation data — call libraries, scorecards, and team-level dashboards for revenue leaders to scale rep development.
- **Key features:**
  - Instant AI feedback with real-time improvement tips for reps
  - Call Libraries & Playlists highlighting best practices and winning techniques
  - Customizable performance metrics and coaching frameworks
  - Trend analysis across individual reps and teams
  - Coaching scorecards and structured feedback loops
  - Custom visibility settings for coaching confidentiality
- **Source URL:** https://jiminny.com/features/sales-coaching

### Pipeline Management (feature)

- **What it does:** Deal Insight dashboard that combines CRM deal data with conversation intelligence to track pipeline health, run pipeline reviews, and surface deal risks.
- **Key features:**
  - Deal Insight Dashboard visualizing stages and progress
  - Proactive risk alerts on individual deals
  - Drill-down into individual calls tied to a deal
  - Pipeline reviews with conversation-context insights
  - Automated Call Scoring
  - CRM integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Close, Bullhorn, Copper
- **Source URL:** https://jiminny.com/features/pipeline-management

### Competitor Intelligence (feature)

> **Wiki:** [[jiminny--competitor-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Surfaces when, how, and why competitors come up on customer calls, with custom keywords, alerts, and CRM-side win/loss analysis.
- **Key features:**
  - Keyword and theme detection for competitor names, products, and pricing comparisons
  - Custom nudges and alerts when competitors are mentioned on a call
  - Voice-of-customer feedback for Product Marketing
  - Pull competitor mentions into CRM opportunity records
  - Win/loss rate analysis based on competitor engagement
  - Competitor-specific dashboards for leadership
- **Source URL:** https://jiminny.com/features/competitor-intelligence

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Jiminny's Conversation Intelligence + AI Notetaker compete head-to-head with **Chorus** — same surface area (call recording, transcription, deal-risk alerts, coaching workflows). Jiminny's own Gong-competitors blog acknowledges this by framing Chorus as the alternative whose "primary strengths lie in data sharing and integration with ZoomInfo" (source: https://jiminny.com/blog/gong-competitors). Sales Coaching and Pipeline Management features also overlap with the coaching and deal-execution facets of ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (source: https://jiminny.com/features/sales-coaching).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Jiminny's Revenue Intelligence (labeled "GTM Intelligence" in nav) ingests conversation + CRM activity to score pipeline health, forecast, and surface deal risk — adjacent to ZoomInfo's **GTM Context Graph** reasoning layer, but Jiminny operates entirely on first-party conversation + CRM data and provides no third-party B2B data foundation (source: https://jiminny.com/product/revenue-intelligence). Auto-logging calls, transcripts, and AI notes into CRMs is adjacent to ZoomInfo Operations' CRM hygiene pitch, scoped to conversation-derived data only (source: https://jiminny.com/faq).
- **No overlap:** Jiminny does not sell B2B contact data, company firmographics, technographics, intent data, ABM orchestration, website chat, or APIs/MCP for agent builders — all areas ZoomInfo covers. Jiminny's data foundation is the customer's own conversations + CRM, with no third-party B2B database (source: https://jiminny.com/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No dedicated `Jiminny vs ZoomInfo` page surfaced within the 10-URL cap (a `/chorus-alternative` page is linked in nav but not extracted). The closest competitor-authored framing is from Jiminny's Gong-competitors blog: "Chorus AI's primary strengths lie in data sharing and integration with ZoomInfo, enabling teams to achieve seamless cross-functional alignment across sales and marketing... [Jiminny is for] sales teams prioritizing intuitive, scalable insights across conversations coaching, great value and expert Customer Success." (source: https://jiminny.com/blog/gong-competitors).
