## Summary

Snov.io is an SMB-focused email finder + cold outreach automation platform that bundles a 500M+ B2B email database, a 7-tier email verifier, mailbox warm-up infrastructure, multichannel email + LinkedIn drip campaigns, and a free built-in sales CRM into one credit-based subscription (source: https://snov.io/). It targets solopreneurs, startups, lead-gen / SEO / digital marketing agencies, and mid-market sales teams running cold outbound — explicitly pitched as a cheaper, simpler ZoomInfo alternative for those segments (source: https://snov.io/zoominfo-alternative). Direct overlap with ZoomInfo lands on the Data foundation (verified emails / company profiles), the Data API surface, and the GTM Workspace seller front-end (drip campaigns + LinkedIn automation + free CRM); Snov.io has no comparable GTM Context Graph, intent, ABM, or RevOps orchestration story. Snov.io adds a deliverability infrastructure layer (SPF/DKIM/DMARC setup, warm-up, placement score) and a free CRM that ZoomInfo does not productize.

## Product Offerings

### Email Finder

> **Wiki:** [[snov--email-finder]]

- **What it does:** Finds and verifies B2B email addresses by name, domain, company name, or LinkedIn profile, with web app, Chrome extension, and bulk search modes.
- **Key features:**
  - Database of 500M+ B2B email addresses with smart AI search
  - Search by name, domain, bulk domain, company name, or LinkedIn URL
  - Email Finder Chrome extension (4.9 on Chrome Web Store, 400,000+ users)
  - Bulk Email Finder (CSV upload of names + domains)
  - Pre-verified emails claimed at 98% accuracy
  - 50 free email searches per month on the Trial plan
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/email-finder

### Email Verifier

> **Wiki:** [[snov--email-verifier]]

- **What it does:** Cleans email lists with multi-tier verification before sending to keep bounce rates low.
- **Key features:**
  - 7-tier email verification
  - 98% accuracy rate
  - Graylisting bypass
  - Bounce risk detection
  - Bulk Email Verifier
  - Email Verifier API
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/pricing

### Outreach-ready Email Infrastructure / Email Accounts

> **Wiki:** [[snov--email-infrastructure]]

- **What it does:** Sets up and warms up email infrastructure (domains, mailboxes, SPF/DKIM/DMARC) for cold outreach at scale.
- **Key features:**
  - Domains and mailboxes setup
  - SPF, DKIM, DMARC configuration
  - Automated inbox warming with unlimited warm-up volume
  - Premium pool warm-up and specific domain warm-up
  - Bulk email account management (Custom Ultra)
  - Done-for-you email setup add-on
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/

### Drip Campaigns / Multichannel Outreach

> **Wiki:** [[snov--drip-campaigns]]

- **What it does:** Automated multi-step email and LinkedIn sequences with personalization, A/B testing, and a shared reply inbox.
- **Key features:**
  - Multichannel campaigns (email + LinkedIn in one flow)
  - Unlimited follow-ups, senders, and campaigns
  - Drag-and-drop campaign builder with template library
  - AI Studio Persona-driven Email Builder and AI Email Writer
  - A/B testing, dynamic personalization, spintax
  - Unibox shared inbox, reply sentiment analysis, mailbox rotation
  - Calendly booking detection and high-bounce safeguard
  - Supports Gmail, Outlook, Exchange, and SMTP senders
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/email-drip-campaigns

### LinkedIn Automation

> **Wiki:** [[snov--linkedin-automation]]

- **What it does:** Automates LinkedIn touches such as profile views, likes, connection requests, and follow-ups inside outreach sequences.
- **Key features:**
  - Profile views, likes, connection requests, and follow-ups on autopilot
  - Sold as an add-on at $69/mo per LinkedIn slot ($62/mo annual)
  - LinkedIn Prospect Finder Chrome extension
  - LinkedIn Search inside Snov.io workspace
  - Combines with email in one multichannel campaign
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/pricing

### Sales CRM

> **Wiki:** [[snov--sales-crm]]

- **What it does:** Free built-in sales CRM for managing leads, deals, tasks, and pipelines alongside Snov.io's outreach tools.
- **Key features:**
  - Free with every plan including Trial
  - Multiple pipelines with up to 100 stages
  - Deal stage management and deal loss reason tracking
  - Tasks management and shared team task calendar
  - Google Calendar sync
  - Unlimited team seats and shared notes/templates
  - Automated data capture with deal timeline
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/sales-crm

### Email Finder API

> **Wiki:** [[snov--email-finder-api]]

- **What it does:** REST API for embedding Snov.io email lookup, domain search, and prospect enrichment into apps and workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - REST API with domain search, email finder, and bulk lookup
  - Rate limit capped at 60 requests per minute
  - Get profile with email, get prospect with URL endpoints
  - Free to start with any Snov.io account
  - Separate Email Verifier API also offered
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/email-finder-api

### Deliverability toolkit

- **What it does:** Monitors and protects email reputation with deliverability checks, spam diagnostics, and DNS/blacklist tooling.
- **Key features:**
  - Deliverability and placement check
  - Spam word checker
  - Custom tracking domain
  - DNS record checker
  - Sender reputation check and blacklist checker
  - Smart alerts and recommendations
- **Source URL:** https://snov.io/pricing

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1)** vs Snov.io Email Finder + B2B Lead Database — Snov.io advertises a 500M+ verified email database and 50M+ company profiles with smart AI search and search by name/domain/company/LinkedIn, competing with ZoomInfo's contact + company data layer at the SMB end of the market (source: https://snov.io/email-finder).
  - **APIs & MCP (Data API)** vs Snov.io Email Finder API + Email Verifier API — Snov.io exposes REST endpoints for domain search, email finder, profile lookup, and bulk lookup (60 req/min), positioned for embedding into apps and agent workflows (source: https://snov.io/email-finder-api).
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end)** vs Snov.io Drip Campaigns + LinkedIn Automation + Sales CRM — Snov.io frames itself as "everything you need to scale cold outreach, all in one CRM platform" with multichannel email + LinkedIn campaigns, an AI Email Writer / Persona-driven Email Builder, Unibox, and built-in CRM, overlapping the seller-front-end use case (without ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph reasoning layer) (source: https://snov.io/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations** vs Snov.io 7-tier Email Verifier and 2-way HubSpot/Pipedrive sync — Snov.io verifies emails before send and pushes/pulls verified contacts in/out of HubSpot and Pipedrive, touching the data-quality + CRM enrichment space at the email/contact level only (no routing, dedupe, or full CRM hygiene) (source: https://snov.io/integrations).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing** vs Snov.io Drip Campaigns + AI Studio Persona-driven Email Builder — drip + multichannel campaigns and the AI Studio email builder cover personalized B2B nurture sequences, adjacent to marketing orchestration but without account-based intent, ABM advertising, or web personalization (source: https://snov.io/email-drip-campaigns).
- **No overlap:**
  - Email deliverability infrastructure: SPF/DKIM/DMARC setup, mailbox warm-up, premium warm-up pool, Email Placement Score and Domain Health Checker — Snov.io owns a cold-email deliverability layer ZoomInfo does not productize (source: https://snov.io/zoominfo-alternative).
  - Snov.io explicitly markets a free, built-in sales CRM with multi-pipeline deal management, while ZoomInfo's CRM-adjacent product (Operations) is data quality on top of the customer's CRM, not a CRM itself (source: https://snov.io/sales-crm).
  - Affiliate program, Snovio Academy education content, and an aggressive freemium / 50-free-credits-per-month flywheel aimed at solopreneurs and very small SMBs — segments ZoomInfo de-prioritizes (source: https://snov.io/pricing).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "Why choose Snovio over ZoomInfo? ... Snov.io shines with its seamless integration with over 5,000 apps, user-friendly interface, and competitive pricing, making it a fantastic alternative to ZoomInfo. ... Snov.io's flat-rate pricing makes it an excellent choice for startups, medium-sized businesses and sales and marketing agencies." (source: https://snov.io/zoominfo-alternative). The page also frames Snov.io as cheaper ("$189 per 20,000 credits" vs ZoomInfo's "$4,900 per 5,000 emails") with unlimited data export, multichannel automation, and a built-in CRM that ZoomInfo lacks.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Lead generation and multichannel outreach automation platform that gets replies — find leads with AI, set up email infrastructure with unlimited mailboxes and warm-ups, and run email and LinkedIn campaigns on autopilot, all in one CRM platform.
- **Recurring proof points:** 400,000+ users; 4.5/5 on G2; 4.5 on Capterra; 4.7 on Trustpilot; 4.9 on Chrome Web Store; "trusted by 300,000 companies in 180+ countries" (homepage) and "over 3,000,000 users"; logo wall featuring Google, Salesforce, Dropbox, Payoneer, SendPulse, DocuSign, Zendesk, Freshworks, Comcast; customer outcome metrics (40% more leads, 3x ROI, 60K leads found, 110% revenue increase, 30% more meetings, 80% average response rate); 98% email accuracy claim with 7-tier verification; GDPR / CCPA / LOA compliance.
- **Tone / category framing:** Positions itself as an "all-in-one" outreach automation and lead generation platform / "CRM platform" for SMB cold outreach, explicitly framed as a cheaper, simpler ZoomInfo alternative for startups, agencies, and mid-sized businesses.
