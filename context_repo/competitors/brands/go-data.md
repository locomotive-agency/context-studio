# Go Data (CIENCE GO Data)

## Sub-products

- [[go-data--database|Go Data Database]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

CIENCE GO Data is a B2B contact database (750M+ records claim; 300M on third-party listings) sold inside CIENCE's broader managed-GTM bundle of data + AI outreach + outsourced SDR teams, targeting B2B sales and marketing leaders from SMB to enterprise. Its direct overlap with ZoomInfo is the data foundation — CIENCE explicitly pitches "the same contact coverage at better accuracy" with a 95%+ email deliverability claim — while its biggest no-overlap surface is managed SDR-as-a-Service (a service motion ZoomInfo does not sell). Pricing is partially public: GTM setup $5,000 one-time, dedicated GTM team $2,000/mo, and a graph8 platform license at $499/mo with 75k credits, but the GO Data subscription itself is gated behind a demo call.

## Product Offerings

### GO Data

- **What it does:** A B2B contact database with verified emails, direct dials, and firmographic / technographic filters; positioned as a data subscription without per-record fees.
- **Key features:**
  - 750M+ verified B2B records (cience.com claim) / 300M records per Capterra and G2 listings (source: https://www.cience.com/go-data-sales-leads/, https://www.capterra.com/p/265351/GO-Data/)
  - 95%+ verified email deliverability vs. claimed industry average of 70-80% (source: https://www.cience.com/go-data-sales-leads/)
  - Filter by job title, seniority, industry, company size, revenue range, geography, and tech stack
  - Real-time verification with continuous refresh; processes 1B+ data points daily
  - Free sample data pull with no credit card
  - Export to CSV or sync directly to HubSpot, Salesforce, and 50+ tools
  - Records continuously consumed and validated by CIENCE's outsourced SDR campaigns (real-world deliverability check)
- **Source URL:** https://www.cience.com/go-data-sales-leads/

### B2B Data (Sales Data Solutions)

- **What it does:** The umbrella sales-data product line covering professional profiles and company records with bespoke enrichment and multi-source verification.
- **Key features:**
  - 200M+ professional profiles, 30M+ company records
  - Demographic, firmographic, technographic, and psychographic data layers
  - Multi-source verification with continuous monitoring
  - Bespoke data enrichment tailored to client ICPs
  - 1B+ data points processed daily
  - AI-driven QA combined with trained human researchers
  - 90%+ data accuracy claim across data points
- **Source URL:** https://www.cience.com/b2b-data/

### Audience Data

- **What it does:** Targeted datasets unifying B2B and B2C personas to power multi-channel campaigns on LinkedIn, Meta, Instagram, and ad platforms.
- **Key features:**
  - Unified B2B and B2C persona data
  - Reach beyond LinkedIn into Meta/Instagram/social ad platforms
  - AI- and human-enriched audience segments
  - Privacy-compliant data management
  - Improved attribution and analytics across channels
- **Source URL:** https://www.cience.com/audience-data/

### AI Outreach (graph8 Infrastructure)

- **What it does:** Multi-channel outreach orchestrated by AI across email, phone, LinkedIn, and ads, sold as a platform license under the graph8 brand.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-channel campaigns: email, phone, LinkedIn, ads
  - AI personalization at scale, human-orchestrated
  - Visitor deanonymization and data enrichment built in
  - Email/call/automated workflow management
  - Cited result: 500%+ appointment growth (SV Insight)
- **Source URL:** https://www.cience.com/go-data-sales-leads/

### Managed SDR (Outbound, Inbound, Local)

- **What it does:** SDR-team-as-a-service: trained SDRs trained on the client's ICP that book qualified meetings; available in outbound, inbound, and locally-targeted variants.
- **Key features:**
  - Outbound SDR — multi-channel outreach + sales playbooks
  - Inbound SDR — MQL-to-SQL nurturing and lead qualification
  - Local SDR — region-specific data and outreach
  - Performance-based per-meeting pricing (typically 4× ROI benchmark)
  - Cited results: 600+ qualified leads/month (BigBox); 4,000+ leads in 4 months (August Ash)
  - SDR Marketplace add-on: hire trained SDRs at cost (Levels 1-3, Offshore $1,500-$3,500/mo or US/Western Europe $3,500-$5,500/mo)
- **Source URL:** https://www.cience.com/

### Go-To-Market Services (GTM Set Up, Scaled Outbound, Enterprise Teams, Additional Channels)

- **What it does:** Full-service GTM consulting and execution covering strategy, scaled outbound infrastructure, enterprise team augmentation, and add-on channels.
- **Key features:**
  - GTM Set Up — 5-day sprint, complete campaign and infrastructure setup ($5,000 one-time)
  - Scaled Outbound — high-volume outbound systems
  - Enterprise Teams — dedicated resources for large teams
  - Additional Channels — physical mail and syndicated content
- **Source URL:** https://www.cience.com/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Managed SDR + data + AI outreach delivered as one bundled GTM-as-a-service offer; data alone is one of three pillars (source: https://www.cience.com/go-data-sales-leads/).
- Real-world data validation: contact records are continuously consumed and validated by CIENCE's own outsourced SDR campaigns rather than synthetic verification only (source: https://www.capterra.com/p/265351/GO-Data/).
- Performance-based per-meeting pricing model (typically 4× ROI benchmark) instead of pure seat-based licensing; month-to-month contracts (source: https://www.cience.com/pricing/).
- Transparent SDR Marketplace with 100% pass-through pricing — Levels 1-3, Offshore vs US/Western Europe rate cards published openly (source: https://www.cience.com/pricing/).
- 5-day GTM sprint setup at a flat $5,000 (source: https://www.cience.com/pricing/).
- graph8 platform license at $499/mo bundles 75k credits, AI outreach, visitor deanonymization, and data enrichment in one stack (source: https://www.cience.com/pricing/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** GO Data and the broader B2B Data product compete head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Data foundation (500M contacts / 100M companies / 200M+ verified emails). CIENCE claims 750M records, 200M+ professional profiles, 30M+ companies, and 95%+ email deliverability vs. industry average of 70-80% (source: https://www.cience.com/go-data-sales-leads/, https://www.cience.com/b2b-data/). Audience Data also touches ZoomInfo Marketing's multi-channel orchestration territory (source: https://www.cience.com/audience-data/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** The graph8 Infrastructure platform license overlaps with parts of ZoomInfo's GTM Studio (orchestration, AI outreach) and ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality / enrichment), but is bundled with managed services rather than sold as a self-serve workspace (source: https://www.cience.com/pricing/, https://www.cience.com/b2b-data/).
- **No overlap:** Managed SDR-as-a-Service (Outbound, Inbound, Local SDR teams + the SDR Marketplace) is a service motion ZoomInfo does not sell (source: https://www.cience.com/). The CIENCE Partner Program (white-label CIENCE branding for partner agencies) and "Additional Channels" (direct mail, syndicated content) are also outside ZoomInfo's scope (source: https://www.cience.com/partners/, https://www.cience.com/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No dedicated vs-page surfaced; the competitor's pitch is a single FAQ on the GO Data sales-leads page: "CIENCE delivers the same contact coverage at better accuracy — with 95%+ email deliverability vs. the industry average of 70-80%. For teams who want to go further, the graph8 platform adds AI-powered outreach and managed SDR services in one system." (source: https://www.cience.com/go-data-sales-leads/)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "B2B Lead Generation That Books Qualified Sales Meetings — Complete GTM System. Month-to-Month. Performance-Based." Data + AI outreach + managed SDRs as one bundled service.
- **Recurring proof points:** 2,500+ B2B clients across 250+ industries; named customers Okta (1M contacts enriched), Microsoft, Google, Uber, Yamaha, Salesforce, US Bank, LexisNexis, Shutterstock; 4.8/5 G2 / 4.4 Clutch / 4.8 UpCity, Crowdreviews, GoodFirms / 4.3 TrustRadius; 10% conversion rate (Turn Technologies); 4,000+ leads in 4 months (August Ash); 600+ qualified leads/month (BigBox); 500%+ appointment growth (SV Insight); 95%+ email deliverability; 90%+ accuracy; 1B+ data points processed daily.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as a "complete GTM system" / "B2B GTM & Lead Generation" service rather than a SaaS category — explicitly positions data as one component of an integrated services offering, with managed SDR labor as the differentiator vs. self-serve data tools.
