## Summary

Fireflies is an AI meeting-assistant / conversation-intelligence platform sold self-serve to individuals, professional teams, and Fortune 500 IT buyers, with use-case landing pages for sales, recruiting, marketing, product research, engineering, healthcare, and other verticals. Direct ZoomInfo overlap is narrow but pointed: Fireflies competes head-to-head with Chorus on transcription, conversation analytics, and CRM-logging, and touches ZoomInfo Sales on AI-drafted follow-ups and meeting-driven CRM hygiene — but it carries zero B2B contact, company, intent, or technographic data and has no equivalent of the GTM Context Graph or GTM Studio. Fireflies' published pitch against Chorus leans on price (Free/Pro $10/Business $19/Enterprise $39 per seat vs. Chorus's reported $8,000+ annual contracts), language coverage (100+ languages vs. "English only"), and breadth of post-meeting AI workflows (200+ AI Skills, MCP server for Claude/Devin/ChatGPT). Treat the vs-Chorus claims as competitor-authored — they live on Fireflies' own blog.

## Product Offerings

### AI Meeting Assistant (Fred)

> **Wiki:** [[fireflies--ai-meeting-assistant]]

- **What it does:** Records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings across video-conferencing platforms via a notetaker bot, Chrome extension, mobile app, or desktop app.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Note Taker Bot (invite fred@fireflies.ai or autojoin via calendar)
  - Transcription in 100+ languages with auto-language detection
  - Claimed 95% transcription accuracy
  - Speaker recognition / speaker tags
  - AI summaries with overview, bullet points, action items, custom notes
  - AskFred AI assistant for question-answering across past meetings
  - Meeting search down to specific sentence and timestamp
  - Audio/video file upload for transcription (MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A)
- **Source URL:** https://fireflies.ai/

### Conversation Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[fireflies--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Analytics suite that extracts speaker, topic, and sentiment insights from recorded meetings to coach teams and surface trends.
- **Key features:**
  - Speaker talk-time tracking
  - Talk-to-listen ratio, longest monologues, filler-word counts
  - Sentiment analysis
  - Topic trackers (competitors, objections, pricing keywords)
  - Question detection and key topic detection
  - Team analytics dashboard for admins (Business+ tier)
- **Source URL:** https://fireflies.ai/conversation-intelligence

### Live Assist

> **Wiki:** [[fireflies--live-assist]]

- **What it does:** Real-time AI assistant that surfaces suggestions, coaching prompts, and answers during a live meeting.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time suggestions during meetings
  - Live coaching prompts
  - On-call answers (AskFred for live Google Meet)
- **Source URL:** https://fireflies.ai/

### Chrome Extension

> **Wiki:** [[fireflies--chrome-extension]]

- **What it does:** Browser extension that records, transcribes, and summarizes Google Meet calls without a bot joining the meeting.
- **Key features:**
  - Botless capture of Google Meet sessions
  - Live transcription as you speak
  - Auto-speaker identification
  - Auto-language detection
  - AskFred for Web — ask questions about live calls or web content
  - Captures and summarizes Loom and Soapbox videos
- **Source URL:** https://fireflies.ai/product/chrome-extension

### AI Skills Store (200+ Apps)

> **Wiki:** [[fireflies--ai-skills-store]]

- **What it does:** Library of pre-built AI skills that automate post-meeting workflows for specific roles (sales, recruiting, marketing, product, engineering).
- **Key features:**
  - Sales skills: Objection Handler, Sales Scorecards, Competitor Mentions, Deal Risk Assessment, Purchase Intent Detector
  - Marketing skills: Content Calendar Generator, Campaign Review Summarizer, Social Media Content Drafting
  - Recruiting skills: Candidate Sentiment Analyzer, Culture Fit Extractor, Candidate Generic Debrief
  - Customizable Super Summaries templates per industry / meeting type
  - 200+ apps total
- **Source URL:** https://fireflies.ai/

### Fireflies MCP Server

> **Wiki:** [[fireflies--mcp-server]]

- **What it does:** Model Context Protocol server that exposes Fireflies meeting insights to external AI tools (Claude, Devin, ChatGPT).
- **Key features:**
  - One-click connection to Claude, Devin, ChatGPT
  - Bring meeting insights into AI agent workflows
- **Source URL:** https://fireflies.ai/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Chorus (ZoomInfo conversation-intelligence module) — Fireflies competes head-to-head on transcription, conversation analytics (talk-to-listen, sentiment, topic trackers, sales coaching), CRM logging, and meeting summaries; both record customer calls and surface coaching insights for sales managers (source: https://fireflies.ai/blog/chorus-vs-fireflies). Secondary overlap with ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace on AI-drafted follow-up emails, CRM auto-fill from meeting notes, and meeting recaps for sellers (source: https://fireflies.ai/use-cases/sales).
- **Adjacent overlap:** ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing) — Fireflies syncs meeting summaries, action items, and tasks back to Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, but only for meeting-derived data, not contact/account enrichment (source: https://fireflies.ai/integrations). APIs & MCP — Fireflies offers public API access on every paid tier (including Free) and an MCP Server connecting to Claude, Devin, ChatGPT — same agent-builder ecosystem play, but limited to meeting data, not B2B contact/company data (source: https://fireflies.ai/).
- **No overlap:** Fireflies sells zero B2B contact, company, phone-number, email, intent, or technographic data — no equivalent of ZoomInfo's Data (Pillar 1), GTM Context Graph (no cross-source CRM + intent + behavioral fusion), or GTM Studio (no audience building, ABM orchestration, or ad targeting) (sources: https://fireflies.ai/, https://fireflies.ai/conversation-intelligence, https://fireflies.ai/use-cases/marketing). Conversely, Fireflies sells into recruiting / HR (Greenhouse / Lever / BambooHR ATS sync), product & UX research, engineering stand-ups, healthcare HIPAA meetings, podcasting, real-estate, and VC pitch capture — markets ZoomInfo does not target (source: https://fireflies.ai/use-cases/recruiting).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** From the Fireflies-authored vs-Chorus blog: "Why pay more for the Chorus when you can dance to the same tune with Fireflies? ... Fireflies offers robust conversation analytics and topic tracking for a fraction of the cost (just $684 for three seats) [vs Chorus's reported $8,000 annually for three users]. ... If highly detailed sales analytics are critical, Chorus.ai has a clear edge. However, if budget, flexibility, collaboration, and security matter more, Fireflies.ai is the clear winner." Pitch concedes Chorus has deeper sales analytics and pre-built sales reports but argues Fireflies wins on price, language coverage, customization, security (end-to-end encryption, zero data retention, BAA with OpenAI), and integration breadth (source: https://fireflies.ai/blog/chorus-vs-fireflies).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The #1 AI Assistant For Your Meetings — transcribe, summarize, search, and analyze all your team conversations" across any video-conferencing platform (source: https://fireflies.ai/).
- **Recurring proof points:** "Used across 1 million+ companies" (homepage), "Trusted by 200,000+ sales teams," "300,000+ marketing teams," "200,000+ hiring teams"; G2 4.8/5 with multiple G2 leader badges (AI Meeting Assistants, Conversation Intelligence, Sales Coaching); customer logos Assembly AI, Emaar, Leonardo AI, Penn; sales-impact claims of "30% faster deal closures," "10 hrs/week extra selling time," "50% more CRM data entry"; SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, HIPAA, zero data retention, signed BAA with OpenAI.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as an "AI meeting assistant" / "AI notetaker" that bundles conversation intelligence as one of many post-meeting workflows — not as a standalone CI platform. Explicitly contrasts with Chorus (deeper sales analytics) and competes on price, breadth (200+ AI Skills), language coverage, and security.
