# D&B Hoovers

## Sub-products

- [[d-b-hoovers--platform|D&B Hoovers Platform]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales
- [[d-b-hoovers--data-cloud|D&B Data Cloud (D-U-N-S Number)]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

D&B Hoovers is a B2B sales intelligence and sales-acceleration platform sold by Dun & Bradstreet to sales, marketing, and RevOps teams across SMB, mid-market, and enterprise — anchored on D&B's commercial Data Cloud (330M+ companies, 520M+ contacts) and the D-U-N-S Number identifier rather than on an AI-GTM-platform framing. The product overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's data foundation and the legacy ZoomInfo Sales prospecting UI, with adjacent overlap into ZoomInfo Operations (via the D&B Connect Salesforce integration) and ZoomInfo Marketing (via D&B Rev.Up ABX bundled alongside Hoovers). It does not compete with ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, APIs & MCP / agent-builder lane, or Chorus conversation intelligence, while it sells into adjacent D&B territory ZoomInfo does not — credit/risk decisioning and government-contracting compliance verification.

## Product Offerings

### D&B Hoovers (core sales intelligence platform)

- **What it does:** B2B sales acceleration tool that combines Dun & Bradstreet's commercial database with company/contact targeting filters, news triggers, and AI-powered prospect scoring to help sales teams build prospect lists and engage decision-makers.
- **Key features:**
  - 330M+ companies and 520M+ contacts in the database
  - 70M+ emails, 60M+ direct dials, 190M+ decision-makers
  - AI-powered Prospect Scoring to identify accounts matching ideal-customer profile
  - News Triggers / opportunity triggers for timely engagement
  - 130+ filters (Essentials) / 175+ filters (CIAL D&B partner page) for company search
  - Buyer intent data layered into prospect targeting
  - Comprehensive corporate hierarchy / family-tree views
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-hoovers.html

### D&B Hoovers Essentials (SMB tier)

- **What it does:** Self-serve, credit-based subscription tier of D&B Hoovers aimed at small and mid-sized businesses, with a public per-month plan and an annual plan, plus a free first month / free trial.
- **Key features:**
  - Monthly plan: $49/month for 150 Company Credits + 150 Contact Credits
  - Annual plan: $529/year for 1,800 Company Credits + 1,800 Contact Credits
  - Per-contact pay-as-you-go option ("Pay per contact")
  - First month free; cancel anytime
  - 130+ filters and dynamic list updates against the D&B Data Cloud
  - News alerts, opportunity triggers, conversation starters on priority accounts
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-hoovers-smb.html

### D&B Healthcare Insights (D&B Hoovers vertical add-on)

- **What it does:** An add-on layered into D&B Hoovers that delivers healthcare-facility-specific data — bed counts, physician specialty, payor mix, procedure volumes — for sales/marketing teams selling into healthcare.
- **Key features:**
  - Coverage of Hospitals & Health Systems, Physician Groups, Long-Term Care, Connected Care (ACOs/HIEs/Payors), Clinics, Imaging Centers, Surgery Centers
  - Healthcare-specific data attributes: bed counts, physician specialty, operating room size, payor mix, net patient revenue, procedure volumes, accreditations
  - Segmentation & territory targeting tooling
  - Account-level intelligence: primary specialties, staffing numbers, total visits/admissions, affiliations
  - Episodic care and Medicare Shared Savings Program data attributes
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/featured/dnb-hoovers-healthcare-insights.html

### D&B Technology Insights (D&B Hoovers vertical add-on)

- **What it does:** Vertical add-on referenced on the homepage that surfaces tech-stack / IT product insights for prospects, helping sellers personalize outreach to tech buyers.
- **Key features:**
  - Tech-product / IT-stack visibility on customers and prospects
  - Filtering on tech-stack characteristics for ICP targeting
  - Pipeline qualification using tech signals
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-hoovers.html

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Sits on Dun & Bradstreet's commercial Data Cloud — distinct heritage as a data-first vendor going back to 1841, with "90% of the Fortune 1000" cited as Data Cloud users
- D-U-N-S Number as a unique business identifier underpinning hierarchies, enrichment, and data governance — explicitly cited as foundational by First Horizon Bank, Algolia, and Dassault Systèmes case studies
- Vertical-specific add-on datasets — D&B Healthcare Insights and D&B Technology Insights — layered directly into the Hoovers UI rather than sold as separate platforms
- Public, self-serve SMB pricing tier ($49/mo Essentials) with a free first month and pay-per-contact option, sitting alongside enterprise/quote-based contracts
- AI-powered Prospect Scoring positioned as a way to find accounts that look like existing ideal customers
- TrustRadius 2025 Buyer's Choice and multiple G2 Leader badges (Small Business, Mid-Market, Asia, India regional leaders)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** D&B Hoovers competes directly with ZoomInfo's Data foundation (500M contacts, 100M companies, 200M+ verified emails, 135M+ verified phone numbers) and the legacy **ZoomInfo Sales** classic prospecting UI. The D&B Hoovers homepage frames the product as "Your Sales Accelerator" powered by the Data Cloud with 330M+ companies and AI-powered Prospect Scoring — head-to-head with ZoomInfo Sales' contact/company database and prospecting workflow (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-hoovers.html). Adjacent direct overlap with **ZoomInfo Operations**: D&B Connect for Salesforce + the D&B Hoovers Salesforce Lightning integration cover CRM data quality, match rules, and enrichment — Algolia case study calls D&B Connect "core for data matching and standardization" with Hoovers data added into Salesforce "with the click of a button" (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/customer-stories/algolia.html).
- **Adjacent overlap:** D&B Rev.Up ABX is positioned alongside Hoovers in the First Horizon Bank bundle for ABM-style targeting, segmentation, fit-and-intent modelling, and Visitor Intelligence — adjacent to **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM)** in the same buyer pocket (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/customer-stories/first-horizon-bank.html). D&B Hoovers' buyer-intent data — described as "industry-leading intent data" on the CIAL D&B partner page — is adjacent to ZoomInfo's **Intent Data** (Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader) (source: https://en.cialdnb.com/products/dnb-hoovers).
- **No overlap:** Credit, finance, and risk decisioning (D&B Finance Analytics, D&B Credit Insights, D-U-N-S–based business credit scores) is core to D&B's broader portfolio — Gartner names "business decisioning data and analytics" as D&B's primary focus — and ZoomInfo does not sell credit decisioning, PAYDEX scores, or credit-to-cash workflows (source: https://www.gartner.com/reviews/product/d-and-b-hoovers). D&B is also used for government-contracting compliance — Dassault reports an "87% reduction in the number of company records needing a manual check for corporate identity and location" via D&B verification (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/customer-stories/dassault-systemes.html). On the other side, no reviewed D&B Hoovers page references a conversation-intelligence product equivalent to Chorus, an AI-agent / GTM-engineering / play-orchestration framing equivalent to GTM Workspace or GTM Studio, or an APIs & MCP server / agent-builder lane (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-hoovers.html).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Two third-party comparison URLs were attempted — G2's `compare/dun-bradstreet-d-b-hoovers-vs-zoominfo-sales` was blocked (HTTP 403 across both Jina and the requests + curl_cffi fallback tiers). D&B does not author a first-party `vs ZoomInfo` page on dnb.com.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "D&B Hoovers is Your Sales Accelerator" — speed up sales prospecting and spend more time closing using rich audience targeting backed by the Dun & Bradstreet Data Cloud.
- **Recurring proof points:** 330M+ companies, 520M+ contacts, 70M+ emails, 60M+ direct dials, 190M+ decision-makers; "90% of the Fortune 1000" trust the D&B Data Cloud; TrustRadius 2025 Buyer's Choice; G2 Leader (Small Business, Mid-Market, Asia, India regional badges); named customers First Horizon Bank, Dassault Systèmes, Algolia, Trend Micro, Dan Martinez & Associates. Vendor-stated CIAL D&B partner-page metrics: "fill the pipeline 3x faster", "59% more qualified leads", "60% boost in closed deals."
- **Tone / category framing:** Sales intelligence / sales acceleration — "shortcut to reach buyers and win deals" and "B2B sales intelligence." Anchored on "data + tool" rather than "all-in-one AI GTM Platform" framing. Strong emphasis on data heritage (1841, D-U-N-S Number, Fortune 1000 reach) and vertical depth over agent / MCP / GTM-engineering messaging.
