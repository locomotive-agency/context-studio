# Agent Frank

## Sub-products

- [[agent-frank--ai-sdr|Agent Frank AI SDR]] — competes with GTM Workspace

## Summary

Agent Frank is an autonomous AI SDR product built by Salesforge that targets sales leaders, growth/GTM engineers, and outbound agencies running B2B motions with $5K–$100K ACVs into startups, SMBs, and mid-market accounts. It overlaps directly with GTM Workspace by automating prospecting, multichannel email + LinkedIn outreach, and meeting booking on autopilot — with Salesforge's own ZoomInfo-alternatives blog explicitly framing Leadsforge + Agent Frank as a ZoomInfo replacement (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/blog/zoominfo-alternatives). Agent Frank is the AI-SDR product within the broader Salesforge "Forge Stack." It does not overlap with ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, ZoomInfo Marketing, ZoomInfo Operations, or Chorus, and explicitly excludes Fortune 500 / enterprise procurement motions from its target ICP.

## Product Offerings

### Agent Frank (AI SDR)

- **What it does:** Autonomous AI SDR that prospects, writes and sends multichannel outreach (email + LinkedIn), follows up, and books meetings 24/7 from a knowledge base provided by the buyer.
- **Key features:**
  - Auto-Pilot and Co-Pilot modes (fully autonomous vs human-in-the-loop review)
  - 24/7 automated prospecting using continuous ICP-based search via Leadsforge (500M+ contact search engine)
  - Native multichannel outreach across email and LinkedIn (no LinkedIn Sales Navigator integration required)
  - 20+ supported languages including American English, German, French, Japanese, Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese, Italian, Polish
  - Knowledge-base-driven personalization using uploaded product/brochure files plus website content; 9 selectable tonalities
  - Three configurable goals: click-out, send meeting link, or receive meeting link
  - Dedicated Account Manager and shared Slack channel for every customer
  - Bundled complimentary Salesforge workspace and built-in Warmforge email warm-up at no extra cost
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank

### Salesforge platform (host platform for Agent Frank)

- **What it does:** Sales execution platform with unlimited mailboxes and unlimited LinkedIn senders that hosts Agent Frank's work and human-led campaigns side-by-side; flat plans rather than per-seat.
- **Key features:**
  - Unlimited mailboxes and unlimited LinkedIn senders (no seat-based pricing)
  - Conditional multichannel sequences combining email and LinkedIn touchpoints
  - Primebox unified inbox for managing all email + LinkedIn replies in one place
  - Smart mailbox rotation and dynamic IP rotation to protect deliverability
  - Sentiment Analysis on inbound replies for automatic triage
  - Overdrive mode for 1:1 AI personalization at scale
  - A/B testing and multi-language sequences (Growth plan)
  - Knowledge Base for AI-generated reply suggestions
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforge.ai/solutions/sales

### Email infrastructure add-ons (Infraforge / Megaforge)

- **What it does:** Optional dedicated sending infrastructure that pairs with Agent Frank to maximize deliverability — required to process active contacts at scale.
- **Key features:**
  - Infraforge — private email infrastructure starting at $33/month (annual billing) for 10 mailboxes; offers SSL, domain masking, multi-IP provisioning
  - Megaforge — premium multi-ESP infrastructure built specifically for Agent Frank, starting at $69/month for 20 mailboxes split across Gmail, Outlook, Mailforge, Infraforge sending ~15 emails/day each (300/day total)
  - Auto-distributes sending so if one ESP burns, another picks up
  - Two-week automated mailbox warm-up via Warmforge included at no extra cost
  - Dedicated IPs and isolated sequencer fingerprint available for agency tenants
  - Connect-your-own-mailboxes option supported as an alternative
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS; outbound lead-gen agencies; recruiting / staffing; professional services with $5K–$100K ACVs
- **Company size called out:** Solo founders, startups, SMB, mid-market (Fortune 500 / six-to-seven-figure deal sizes are explicitly listed under "Not-so-good fit")
- **Persona / role focus:** Sales / SDR leaders, outbound agency owners, growth teams, GTM engineers, B2B founders running their own outbound
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Replace or augment human SDR teams with autonomous outbound (24/7 prospecting + sending)
  - Scale multichannel email + LinkedIn outreach without seat-based cost penalties
  - Run client outreach across multiple brands inside one platform (agencies)
  - Automate prospecting + personalization while reps focus on closing
  - Generate pipeline at lower CAC than the "classic" SDR cost stack (claim: $8K/mo vs $499/mo, "16x reduction")

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** None required — Agent Frank is explicitly positioned as "no need to integrate with Salesforce or any other CRM" (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/comparison/agent-frank-vs-agentforce). No native Salesforce, HubSpot, or MS Dynamics sync surfaced on reviewed pages.
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Self-contained — Agent Frank executes outreach inside Salesforge rather than handing off to Outreach or Salesloft.
- **Data / enrichment:** Leadsforge (sister product) — natural-language ICP search engine over a 500M+ contact pool, used as Agent Frank's lead source; bring-your-own contacts (CSV upload) supported.
- **Other notable integrations:** Infraforge / Mailforge / Primeforge / Warmforge — internal email infrastructure stack (not external integrations); bring-your-own Gmail and MS365 mailboxes supported.

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Bundled email infrastructure (Infraforge / Megaforge) and Warmforge warm-up come as part of the Salesforge ecosystem rather than requiring third-party deliverability tooling (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank).
- Native LinkedIn outreach without LinkedIn Sales Navigator or third-party automation tools — email and LinkedIn execute in one workspace (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/comparison/agent-frank-vs-agentforce).
- Flat-rate pricing ($499/mo Tier 1) with predictable per-active-contact economics, contrasted against Agentforce's $2-per-conversation usage pricing and AiSDR's $900/mo flat tier (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/comparison/agent-frank-vs-aisdr).
- Unlimited LinkedIn senders and unlimited mailboxes, explicitly positioned against seat-based pricing that "punishes growth" (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank).
- Dedicated Account Manager + shared Slack channel included for every customer — a managed-service layer wrapped around the AI agent (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank).
- 20+ languages supported natively (German, French, Japanese, Spanish, Brazilian Portuguese, Polish, etc.) for global outbound (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Agent Frank competes head-to-head with **GTM Workspace** (the seller-facing prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted outreach + execution surface) by covering "everything from finding prospects, crafting emails and following up, right up to booking meetings" autonomously (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank). It also overlaps **ZoomInfo Sales** (legacy classic prospecting UI + AI assistant layer) — Salesforge's own blog frames Leadsforge + Agent Frank as a paired ZoomInfo replacement that automates "prospecting and outreach automatically" (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/blog/zoominfo-alternatives).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Touches **ZoomInfo's data foundation (Pillar 1)** through Leadsforge's "500M+ Contacts Search Engine," but does not claim multi-source verification, technographic enrichment, intent signals, or research-team-validated data depth — data is treated as a feeder utility, not a standalone product (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank). Touches **GTM Studio** through the Solutions/Growth page targeting "GTM engineers" with "a reliable execution layer that runs without constant manual intervention," but limited to outbound execution rather than full play orchestration (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/solutions/growth).
- **No overlap:** No **GTM Context Graph** equivalent — no conversation intelligence, no first-party CRM intelligence layer, no Chorus/Workbounce-style context-capture surfaced on any reviewed page. Agent Frank is explicitly outbound-only, "won't handle customer service requests or marketing campaigns" (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/comparison/agent-frank-vs-agentforce), so no overlap with **ZoomInfo Marketing** (ABM) or **ZoomInfo Operations** (CRM data quality / routing). The "Not-so-good fit" list explicitly excludes Fortune 500 targeting, six-to-seven-figure deal sizes, and long RFP cycles (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/agent/frank), so no overlap with ZoomInfo's enterprise motion. No developer **APIs & MCP** surface accessible — no MCP server surfaced on reviewed pages.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "ZoomInfo provides static lists that require manual filtering and verification… Leadsforge offers all key features that automates the entire process — from finding the right prospects to verifying contact data in real-time… pairs with Agent Frank by Salesforge, an AI SDR that handles prospecting and outreach automatically." (source: https://www.salesforge.ai/blog/zoominfo-alternatives) — note the pitch attacks ZoomInfo's prospecting workflow (static lists, manual filtering, opaque pricing) without engaging ZoomInfo's three-pillar narrative (Data depth, GTM Context Graph, Universal Access).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The #1 AI SDR" — built by a former VP of Sales — that runs 24/7 prospecting + multichannel outreach to book meetings, freeing humans to close deals at lower overhead than a traditional SDR seat (claim: "16x lower cost" vs $8K/mo classic SDR stack).
- **Recurring proof points:**
  - G2 4.9 rating cited on the product page
  - "16x cost reduction" claim vs $8,000/month classic SDR cost stack ($6,500 salary + $500 tools + $500 data + $500 admin)
  - Customer case study: VAI Consulting (Ryan Van Hout, Partner & Co-Founder)
  - Implied throughput: 1,000 active contacts produces 2,000–2,500 new leads and 6,000–7,500 personalized emails per month
  - Founder credibility framing — Frank Sondors (former Googler / VP Sales)
- **Tone / category framing:** Positions itself as "The #1 AI SDR" in the AI-SDR-agent category alongside 11x.ai/Alice, AiSDR, Reply.io Jason AI, and Salesforce Agentforce. Treats data providers (ZoomInfo, Apollo, Cognism) as upstream feeders that Leadsforge + Agent Frank replace end-to-end rather than as direct peers.
