## Summary

Cognism is a premium B2B sales-intelligence platform whose central pitch is European data quality and compliance, framed explicitly against ZoomInfo. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Data foundation (Pillar 1) and Operations enrichment, but does not compete on GTM Workspace, Chorus, or APIs/MCP — Cognism's positioning is "best-in-class data that strengthens existing systems rather than replaces them," which is a deliberate counter to ZoomInfo's all-in-one platform frame. Their dedicated `/cognism-vs-zoominfo` page is the most directly competitive vs-page in the entire Tier 1 set, with switching-customer testimonials claiming 98% match rate vs. ZoomInfo's 72%, 22% vs. 14% call connect rate, and ROI in 8 weeks. Pricing is a transparent two-tier (Standard / Pro) Sales Prospecting product plus separate CRM Enrichment and Data-as-a-Service add-ons.

## Product Offerings

### Sales Intelligence (Sales Prospecting)

- **What it does:** Contextual access to B2B account and contact intelligence surfaced inside CRM, LinkedIn, and web workflows, with phone-verified mobile numbers and AI-powered natural-language search.
- **Key features:**
  - Phone-verified mobile numbers (Cognism's primary differentiator)
  - Verified emails, job titles, work history, technographics
  - Cognism AI (Company Research, Persona Builder, natural-language Search)
  - Browser extension for LinkedIn, Sales Navigator, Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, websites
  - Native CRM and engagement integrations (Salesforce two-way sync, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Pipedrive, Outreach, Bullhorn, Salesloft)
  - Performance dashboards
  - Compliance baked in: GDPR / UK GDPR, SOC 2, TPS / DNC screening across 15 countries
  - Account Manager + Live Chat support
- **Source URL:** https://www.cognism.com/

### CRM Enrichment

- **What it does:** Standalone or add-on product that finds, enriches, and refreshes incomplete or stale CRM records on a subscription + credits model based on database size.
- **Key features:**
  - Identify incomplete / outdated CRM records
  - Enrich priority records with verified contact data
  - Keep contacts current as people move roles or companies
  - Subscription + credits pricing tied to database size and enrichment method
- **Source URL:** https://www.cognism.com/pricing

### Data-as-a-Service (DaaS)

- **What it does:** API or bulk-delivery access to Cognism data outside the platform — for embedding into internal systems, enrichment, and routing processes.
- **Key features:**
  - API or bulk delivery
  - Pricing scales with delivery method, volume, and selected fields
  - Designed for teams building data into their own tools
- **Source URL:** https://www.cognism.com/

### Cognism Intent Data

- **What it does:** Buyer-intent layer powered by Bombora plus Cognism's own real-time signals (hiring, funding/M&A, job changes, technographics).
- **Key features:**
  - Bombora intent across 12 core topics + 100+ additional topics
  - Real-time signals: hiring activity, funding events, technographic shifts
  - Surfaces directly in prospecting workflow (not a separate dashboard)
- **Source URL:** https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Cross-industry; case studies span tech, recruitment (Bullhorn), professional services, training (Sparta Global), e-commerce (Openprise, SaleCycle).
- **Company size called out:** SMB, Mid-Market, Enterprise — explicit homepage segmentation.
- **Persona / role focus:** Revenue Operations, SDR Managers, Heads of Revenue Operations, Sales Leaders.
- **Use cases promoted:** EU/UK prospecting where ZoomInfo's coverage is "below par"; CRM data hygiene / enrichment; embedding data into internal tools via DaaS; replacing ZoomInfo when GDPR exposure is a concern.

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce (two-way sync), HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Pipedrive — native, included as standard.
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Outreach, Salesloft, Bullhorn — native.
- **Data / enrichment:** Bombora intent (data partner). API-based and CSV on-demand enrichment.
- **Other notable integrations:** LinkedIn / Sales Navigator / Recruiter (browser extension); Zapier (workflow); company websites (browser extension).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Phone-verified mobile numbers with on-demand verification service — Cognism's flagship data claim, framed against ZoomInfo's "confidence score" labeling (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo).
- European / UK data quality and global coverage — explicitly positioned as the alternative when ZoomInfo's EMEA data is insufficient (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo).
- DNC / TPS screening across 15 countries by default (UK TPS/CTPS, US, Canada, Australia, plus 10 EU registers) — vs. ZoomInfo's claim of DNC checking in 8 countries (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo).
- "Best-in-class data that strengthens existing systems" framing — explicit counter-positioning to ZoomInfo's all-in-one consolidation pitch (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo).
- AI tools (Company Research, Persona Builder, Search) included in all packages for every user — framed against ZoomInfo's "AI email writer and Copilot at additional per-seat charge" (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo).
- Integrations included as standard across all packages, not gated as add-ons.

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (Pillar 1) ↔ Cognism Sales Intelligence + DaaS.** Direct head-to-head on B2B contact / company data. Cognism's pitch is regional accuracy advantage in Europe; switching-customer quotes claim 98% Cognism match rate vs 72% ZoomInfo and 22% vs 14% call connect rate. (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo)
  - **ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio (data ops) ↔ Cognism CRM Enrichment.** Standalone enrichment product mirrors ZoomInfo Operations' core CRM-hygiene use case. (source: https://www.cognism.com/pricing)
  - **ZoomInfo Intent ↔ Cognism Intent (Bombora + signals).** Both layer intent on top of contact data. Cognism uses Bombora; ZoomInfo's intent is "believed to rely on bidstream data" per Cognism's vs page. (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo APIs ↔ Cognism Data-as-a-Service.** Both expose data programmatically; Cognism does NOT have an MCP server or AI-agent integration story analogous to ZoomInfo's MCP / Claude directory listing.
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace / GTM Studio / Chorus / WebSights / Marketing.** Cognism explicitly does not compete on these — it is a data + intent + enrichment layer, not a workflow / engagement / conversation-intelligence platform. ZoomInfo's all-in-one platform breadth has no Cognism analogue. (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo)
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Cognism authors a dedicated `/cognism-vs-zoominfo` page (one of the most direct vs-pages in the Tier 1 set). Sample claims, with attribution: _"Cognism blew ZI out of the water. Cognism's match rate was 98% vs. 72% by ZoomInfo, and we found that Cognism returned a ton more phone numbers. When we called them to verify the numbers, the call connect rate was 22% with Cognism and 14% with ZoomInfo."_ — Amanda Newman, SDR Manager @ User Evidence. _"Our legal team reviewed ZoomInfo's GDPR standards in Europe, which were not up to scratch. As a result, they removed the European team's access to the platform, which massively impacted our sales output."_ — Pierre Bourguignon, SDR Manager @ Cloudreach. (source: https://www.cognism.com/cognism-vs-zoominfo)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Become fluent in your market" — premium sales intelligence with phone-verified data, AI-powered search, and embedded compliance.
- **Recurring proof points:** 85% data accuracy claimed by switching customers; 22% pipeline growth quarterly; ROI in 8 weeks; £903K pipeline generated (Bright Network); 33% pipeline increase in 3 months (User Evidence); 100-150 leads/week/rep (Cloudreach); 40% of outbound pipeline attributed to Cognism (pricing-page customer quote).
- **Tone / category framing:** "Best-in-class data" vs. "all-in-one platform" — Cognism rejects the platform-consolidation pitch and positions data quality + integration into existing stacks as the operating model. The vs-ZoomInfo page calls out that ZoomInfo "expanded largely through acquisition, assembling a broad go-to-market platform... For some organisations, this offers convenience and scale. For others, particularly those operating across multiple regions, it can introduce added complexity and cost as capabilities are accessed through layered add-ons."
