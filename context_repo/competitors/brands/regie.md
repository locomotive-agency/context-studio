## Summary

Regie.ai is an AI Sales Engagement Platform (positioning itself as "the world's only AI SEP") that targets SDRs, AEs, RevOps and marketing inside mid-market and enterprise B2B SaaS, pairing autonomous prospecting agents with human reps for outbound execution. Its RegieOne product unifies enrichment, dialing, email, social sequencing and analytics — the same seller surface ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace and legacy Copilot/Sales target — and ships 220M+ contacts plus 100+ built-in signals inside the platform, putting it in direct overlap with ZoomInfo's data + AI-prospecting layers. It does not yet expose a marketer/RevOps studio (parallel to ZoomInfo GTM Studio) or a public API/MCP for agent builders, leaving ZoomInfo's universal-access pillar largely uncontested. The most material competitive observation is that Regie.ai actually integrates ZoomInfo as one enrichment source while simultaneously selling a stack designed to replace it for outbound execution.

## Product Offerings

### RegieOne (AI SEP platform)

> **Wiki:** [[regie--regieone]]

- **What it does:** Unified AI sales engagement platform that combines enrichment, dialing, email, social sequencing and reporting in one workflow, pairing AI agents with human reps for prospecting (source: https://www.regie.ai/).
- **Key features:**
  - Static and dynamic (intent-based) multi-channel sequencing agents
  - Prompt-based steps and BYO list support
  - 1st and 3rd party intent prioritization with custom fit/intent scoring
  - Power dialer plus multi-room virtual Sales Floor with chat and screen share
  - AI message creation, AI persona library and CMS, Copilot Chrome extension
  - Power socializer for LinkedIn outreach
  - Native CRM and Sales Engagement Platform integrations
  - Outcome, messaging, agent and rep activity analytics with pipeline attribution
- **Source URL:** https://www.regie.ai/pricing

### Prospecting Agents / Auto-Pilot (Force Multiplier Rep)

> **Wiki:** [[regie--prospecting-agents]]

- **What it does:** Autonomous AI agents that source, enrich, research, prioritize, and warm up prospects, then hand off ready-to-talk leads to reps; positioned as the AI-SDR / force-multiplier tier above the AI SEP (source: https://www.regie.ai/pricing).
- **Key features:**
  - Autonomous discovery and enrollment of new prospects
  - 120,000 AI + enrichment credits included (1k accounts / 4k contacts)
  - Custom enrichment waterfalls across leading vendors
  - Bounce and job-verification checks
  - Research agents and "Reasons to Engage" generation
  - Parallel dialer up to 9 lines with AI voicemails and coaching
  - 10 mailboxes plus domains with warming
  - Targeting and agent prospect-acquisition analytics
- **Source URL:** https://www.regie.ai/pricing

### AI Dialer

> **Wiki:** [[regie--ai-dialer]]

- **What it does:** Signal-prioritized parallel dialer with virtual Sales Floor, AI scripts, custom voicemail drops and automated CRM follow-up; sold only as part of an Agents package (source: https://www.regie.ai/features/call).
- **Key features:**
  - Parallel dialing — up to 9 numbers at once
  - Signal-based prioritization analyzing 150+ signals
  - Agent-driven lead warm-up before dial
  - Waterfall enrichment across leading vendors free of charge
  - Virtual Sales Floor with manager listen-in and live whisper coaching

- **Source URL:** https://www.regie.ai/features/call

### Email Agents

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Positions as "the world's only AI SEP" — replaces dozens of prospecting tools (dialer, data enrichment, signals/intent, email/social messaging, SEP) in one platform (source: https://www.regie.ai/).
- Pairs autonomous AI agents with reps in a "left-brain / right-brain" division of labor, claiming 4–6x SDR-equivalent output from Auto-Pilot (source: https://www.regie.ai/use-cases/named-account-signals).
- Bundles 220M+ verified contacts and waterfall enrichment into the platform with no per-contact fees, undercutting standalone enrichment vendors (source: https://www.regie.ai/features/enrichment).
- Continuously monitors 100+ signals (10-Ks, BuiltWith, news, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, CRM) plus unlimited custom signals to trigger plays autonomously (source: https://www.regie.ai/pricing).
- Public per-seat pricing ($180 / $499) with 10- or 5-seat minimums — rare transparency in the AI-SDR category (source: https://www.regie.ai/pricing).
- Holds a foundational patent for AI agents in go-to-market; reported customer outcomes include 69% more accounts touched per SDR and 21% more outbound meetings booked (source: https://www.regie.ai/use-cases/known-buyer-movement).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** RegieOne's AI SEP plus Prospecting Agents go head-to-head with **GTM Workspace** and the **ZoomInfo Sales** seller front-end — Regie unifies enrichment, dialing, email and sequencing in one rep-facing surface and explicitly markets replacement of the SEP/dialer/enrichment stack (source: https://www.regie.ai/). Auto-Pilot autonomously sources, enriches, prioritizes and engages prospects with signal-driven sequencing — the same AI-prospecting workflow GTM Workspace's AI-agent layer sells (source: https://www.regie.ai/pricing). Regie's 220M+ contacts plus 100+ built-in signals also substitute for ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1) + Intent Data** at the execution layer, even though Regie also resells ZoomInfo Sales as one of several enrichment sources (source: https://www.regie.ai/features/enrichment).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Regie's AI Dialer ships call transcription, AI analysis, manager listen-in / whisper, and AI coaching that touch **Chorus**'s conversation-intelligence turf without being a full CI platform (source: https://www.regie.ai/features/call). The website-pixel + Behavioral Intent + Named Account Signals plays overlap **ZoomInfo Marketing**'s ABM and de-anonymization workflows (source: https://www.regie.ai/use-cases). Bounce/job verification, deduplication, and continuous refresh are adjacent to **ZoomInfo Operations** data-quality, scoped to outbound (source: https://www.regie.ai/features/enrichment).
- **No overlap:** No marketer / RevOps NL-audience-building studio comparable to **GTM Studio** (source: https://www.regie.ai/use-cases). No public **APIs & MCP** server for any-tool / agent-builder access — Regie integrates with partner APIs but does not expose its own developer/agent API (source: https://www.regie.ai/integrations). No standalone conversation-intelligence product (Chorus equivalent) outside the dialer (source: https://www.regie.ai/features/call).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The closest direct claim is a featured customer quote on the Known Buyer Movement page: "If you asked 'ZoomInfo or Regie.ai? Pick one.' I would choose Regie.ai. This is an essential tool for any seller. I used to spend 45 minutes writing emails that never got read. Now I spend 3 minutes, and my emails get read!" — Anthony T., Enterprise Account Manager (source: https://www.regie.ai/use-cases/known-buyer-movement).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Unify your entire GTM motion in one platform — enrichment, dialing, email, sequencing, reporting — with AI agents paired to reps so small teams reach 3–5x more accounts without adding headcount.
- **Recurring proof points:** G2 Spring 2025 Grid Leader, Momentum Leader, Most Implementable Mid-Market, Users Love Us; named customers Reputation, Arcadia, BILL, Bitdefender, Commvault, Wrike, First National Capital, Session AI; reported outcomes 69% more accounts touched per SDR, 4–6x SDR-equivalent output, 21% more outbound meetings booked; foundational AI-agents patent (Oct 2025); $30M Series B (Feb 2025); GTM Partners ROI study (May 2024).
- **Tone / category framing:** "AI SEP" — autonomous prospecting agents on top of a Sales Engagement Platform; positions as the consolidator that replaces the legacy stack of dialer + data + intent + SEP.
