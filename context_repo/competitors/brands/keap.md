## Summary

Keap is a small-business CRM and sales/marketing automation platform (formerly Infusionsoft) sold almost exclusively to solopreneurs, coaches, consultants, franchisees, and very small service businesses — an ICP that does not overlap with ZoomInfo's enterprise GTM buyer. Its core surfaces (contact management, drag-and-drop automation, email/SMS broadcasts, landing pages, quoting/invoicing/payments, appointment booking) compete adjacently with ZoomInfo Marketing's automation features and ZoomInfo Sales' pipeline UI but lack any third-party B2B data, Context Graph, or enterprise-tier orchestration. Keap was acquired by Thryv Holdings on 2024-10-29 for $80M, and the brand is mid-merger — some Keap pages now serve under Thryv's CMS and link to learn.thryv.com. No Keap-owned vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced in SERP, so head-to-head pitch quotes are not available.

## Product Offerings

### Keap CRM (Small Business CRM)

> **Wiki:** [[keap--crm]]

- **What it does:** Centralizes contacts, communications, and lead/deal tracking for small businesses with built-in automation for follow-up.
- **Key features:**
  - Contact management with company records, tags, lists, filters, and custom fields
  - Lead scoring and lead source tracking
  - Sales pipeline tracking with pipeline automation
  - Customizable dashboard and per-contact automation history
  - Mobile app (US, AU, CA, UK, NZ) for contacts/appointments/tasks on the go
  - Gmail and Outlook sync for 1:1 emails and calendar
- **Source URL:** https://keap.com/solutions/best-crm-for-small-businesses

### Sales & Marketing Automation

> **Wiki:** [[keap--sales-marketing-automation]]

- **What it does:** Drag-and-drop Easy Automations plus an Advanced Automations builder that automate lead capture, follow-up, nurture, and post-purchase workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Easy Automations (preset templates) and Advanced Automations builder for custom multi-branch flows
  - Library of Proven Automation Templates spanning marketing, sales, service, and operations (cart abandonment, quiz/assessment, hiring, new-hire onboarding, etc.)
  - AI Automation Assistant to generate full campaigns; AI Content Assistant to draft emails
  - Triggers from forms, tags, behavior, or third-party app actions
  - Self-reported customer outcomes: 39% revenue increase, 53% more leads, 10 hrs/week saved (Keap Customer Survey 2020)
  - Zapier integration (4,000+ apps); homepage cites 5,000+ third-party integrations
- **Source URL:** https://keap.com/solutions/automation

### Email & Text Marketing

> **Wiki:** [[keap--email-text-marketing]]

- **What it does:** Sends broadcasts, 1:1 messages, automated nurture campaigns, and SMS/voice broadcasts to segmented audiences.
- **Key features:**
  - Drag-and-drop email builder with ready-made templates
  - Email broadcasts, 1:1 emails, newsletters, and triggered automated emails
  - Email deliverability health dashboard
  - 1:1 texts, text broadcasts, automated text messaging, and reply handling (US only)
  - Dedicated business phone line for calls and texts (US/Canada)
  - SMS pricing tiers: Tier 1 included (500 msgs / 100 voice min); upgrades $24-$279/mo; overage $0.015/msg, $0.01/min
- **Source URL:** https://keap.com/marketing/email-marketing

### Lead Capture & Landing Pages

> **Wiki:** [[keap--lead-capture-landing-pages]]

- **What it does:** Builds branded forms, landing pages, and multi-page sales funnels that capture leads and trigger automations.
- **Key features:**
  - Drag-and-drop landing page builder with branded templates
  - Web forms that trigger automations on submission
  - Multi-page sales funnels with linked pages
  - Internal forms for team data collection
  - Lead source tracking on every captured contact
- **Source URL:** https://keap.com/pricing

### Quoting, Invoicing & Payments

> **Wiki:** [[keap--quoting-invoicing-payments]]

- **What it does:** Sends quotes, one-time/recurring invoices, and accepts payments natively (Keap Pay) or via PayPal/Stripe/Eway.
- **Key features:**
  - Quotes that convert to invoices with one click
  - Checkout forms with order bumps and promo codes
  - Native payment processor (Keap Pay) plus PayPal, Stripe, Eway support
  - Recurring subscription billing and billing automation for past-due reminders
  - Credit-card-expiration reminders and automated payment retries
- **Source URL:** https://keap.com/pricing

### Reporting

> **Wiki:** [[keap--reporting]]

- **What it does:** Tracks sales, marketing, and automation performance via dashboards, sales reports, and an advanced reporting hub.
- **Key features:**
  - Sales reports by day/week/month with period-over-period comparison
  - Forecasting for payments, receivables, and failed invoices
  - Automation performance reports including stage-by-stage contact flow
  - Reporting hub with pinning of most-used reports (Keap Max/Ultimate only)
  - Graphly third-party integration for advanced visualization
- **Source URL:** https://keap.com/solutions/reporting

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Coaching/consulting, health & wellness, travel agencies, marketing agencies, independent retail (e.g. pharmacies), home services (cleaning, lawn care, painting, pools), real estate, franchises, music/creator businesses, nonprofits (source: https://keap.com/customer-stories)
- **Company size called out:** Solopreneurs and very small businesses (typical reference profile: 2 users / 1,500 contacts); multi-location SMBs and franchises via a separate Keap-for-franchises offering (source: https://keap.com/pricing, https://keap.com/)
- **Persona / role focus:** Small business owner / founder, solo coach or consultant, marketing manager inside an SMB, franchisee operator, implementation/automation partner agency (source: https://keap.com/, https://keap.com/customer-stories)
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Lead capture from website/social with automated follow-up
  - Nurture and conversion of leads into paying customers
  - Quoting, invoicing, and recurring payment collection
  - Appointment booking and reminders
  - Customer reactivation, referral, and review-request automations

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Native — Keap CRM is the platform itself; no inbound CRM-to-CRM sync mentioned on reviewed pages
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Native dialer / business phone line via Keap Business Line (US/Canada); 1:1 + automated SMS broadcasts (US only)
- **Data / enrichment:** _Not surfaced on reviewed pages — Keap does not market third-party contact enrichment._
- **Other notable integrations:** Zapier (4,000+ apps; homepage cites 5,000+ integrations); Gmail and Outlook (calendar + 1:1 email sync); PayPal, Stripe, Eway (payment processors); QuickBooks (accounting sync); Google My Business (review automation); Graphly (third-party reporting); open API + webhooks for custom connections (source: https://keap.com/pricing, https://keap.com/solutions/automation)

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Single-plan pricing — Keap pitches one platform plan that includes every feature ("Say goodbye to feature-based plans") rather than tiered feature gating common in HubSpot/Salesforce (source: https://keap.com/pricing)
- Required, paid implementation package — every new account is required to buy implementation services covering business mapping, done-for-you automations, data import, and recurring strategy calls (source: https://keap.com/pricing)
- Library of Proven Automation Templates spanning marketing, sales, service, and operations (e.g. cart abandonment, quiz/assessment, hiring, new-hire onboarding) shipped in-product (source: https://keap.com/pricing)
- Lifecycle Automation framework — proprietary methodology for SMB growth (collect, convert, create raving fans) embedded in product navigation and education (source: https://keap.com/)
- Bundled Customer Success Manager + 24/7 chat + US-based phone support included with every subscription (source: https://keap.com/pricing)
- Self-promoted "#1 HubSpot Alternative" with a dedicated comparison link in the global header (source: https://keap.com/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Keap does not compete head-to-head with any ZoomInfo product line — it has no third-party B2B database, no contact/company data, no firmographics, no intent data, no Context Graph, and no enterprise GTM orchestration tier.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Keap's email + marketing automation surface (broadcast/triggered emails, nurture campaigns, lead capture forms, landing pages) is adjacent to **ZoomInfo Marketing**'s automation features but at the SMB end (no ABM, no intent-driven targeting). Keap CRM + sales pipeline + sales automation is adjacent to **GTM Workspace**'s seller front-end, but Keap explicitly does NOT find or supply leads — its FAQ states "Keap does not 'find' leads for you" (source: https://keap.com/solutions/automation).
- **No overlap:** ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1)** foundation (500M contacts, 100M companies, technographics, intent), **GTM Context Graph**, **GTM Studio**, **Chorus** conversation intelligence, and the **APIs & MCP** server are entirely outside Keap's scope. Conversely, Keap sells in spaces ZoomInfo does not enter: native invoicing/payments (Keap Pay), recurring subscription billing, an appointment scheduler, and a dedicated business phone line for SMB owners.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The only Keap-vs-ZoomInfo content in SERP came from third-party listicles (findstack, sourceforge, softwareworld, syncgtm) which fall under CONTEXT.md's forbidden LLM-aggregator category and were not extracted.
