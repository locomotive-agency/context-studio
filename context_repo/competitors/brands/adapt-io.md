# Adapt.io

## Sub-products

- [[adapt-io--lead-builder|Adapt Lead Builder (Adapt Data OS)]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales
- [[adapt-io--enrichment-api|Adapt Enrichment & API]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

Adapt.io is a B2B lead-intelligence / contact-data platform (operated by SLI Technologies, Inc.) that sells primarily into SMB and mid-market sales and marketing teams via a 250M+ verified contact database, a Chrome extension, an enrichment engine, and a REST API. Its overlap with ZoomInfo is concentrated on the data-foundation and ZoomInfo Sales / Marketing / Operations layers — it is a substitute for prospecting, list building, CRM enrichment, and the Enterprise API, but does not market a GTM Context Graph, conversation intelligence, MCP server, or third-party intent data. Adapt's own ZoomInfo-alternative page leans on price, faster implementation, transparent credit-based pricing, and a 3,000+ tech-sector industry taxonomy as its anti-ZoomInfo wedge.

## Product Offerings

### Prospecting (Adapt Data OS)

- **What it does:** Lead discovery and list-building over a 250M+ verified B2B contact database with advanced filtering, Chrome extension, and CRM export.
- **Key features:**
  - Adapt Tech Sector — proprietary mapping across 3,000+ specialized tech sectors (e.g. Fintech SaaS, HRTech SaaS, eCommerce SaaS) for ICP targeting
  - Funding Insights — surfaces decision-makers in recently funded companies
  - Job Change Alerts — flags contacts who recently moved into new roles
  - Advanced filters across job title, department, level, employee count, revenue, and location
  - Chrome extension to find verified contact data from LinkedIn or company websites
  - List building with one-click CRM export and ABM list building (upload company website lists)
  - Lead scoring and prioritization to rank top leads
- **Source URL:** https://www.adapt.io/platform/prospecting

### Data Enrichment

- **What it does:** Keeps lead and CRM data fresh by appending and standardizing 50+ data attributes across CSV, CRM, and API workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - CSV Enrichment — upload data files and receive enriched output
  - CRM Enrichment — sync and auto-enrich the CRM
  - API Enrichment — scale data ops via the Adapt API
  - 50+ enrichment attributes including direct dial, mobile number, department, level, revenue, employee count, industry, and geography (city / state / country / zip)
  - Real-time data updates triggered by role changes or relocations
  - Lead scoring and routing powered by enriched attributes
- **Source URL:** https://www.adapt.io/platform/enrichment

### Adapt API

- **What it does:** REST API surface for embedding Adapt's B2B contact and company data into external systems and product workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Prospect API — query the 150M+ profile contact / company dataset for verified emails and phone numbers
  - Enrich API — fill data gaps in the CRM with demographic / firmographic attributes
  - Refresh API — webhook-driven automatic updates in real time
  - Use cases: automated lead generation, CRM enrichment, lead scoring, product integration, data validation / cleansing
  - API access is gated to the Custom tier per the pricing page (source: https://www.adapt.io/pricing)
- **Source URL:** https://www.adapt.io/platform/api

### Email Finder

- **What it does:** Standalone tool to discover and verify B2B email addresses of decision-makers, with bulk export and Chrome extension.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time email verification (built-in ZeroBounce integration per https://www.adapt.io/alternatives/best-zoominfo-alternative)
  - Bulk email search with one-click API export
  - Hyper-targeted email list building with segmentation
  - Chrome extension to capture emails from LinkedIn and company websites
  - Stated 90%+ accuracy on B2B contact data per the page FAQ
  - Adapt Alerts to keep email lists fresh and prevent data decay
- **Source URL:** https://www.adapt.io/email-finder

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation** — Adapt Data OS (250M+ verified contacts, direct dials, mobile numbers, technographics, 50+ enrichment attributes) is a direct substitute for ZoomInfo's data layer (source: https://www.adapt.io/platform/prospecting).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (legacy seller-facing prospecting UI)** — Adapt's prospecting platform + Chrome extension targets Sales Reps, AEs, and Sales Leadership with the same job-to-be-done (source: https://www.adapt.io/solutions/sales).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations** — Adapt's enrichment engine plus the Refresh API (webhook-driven CRM updates) and Lead Scoring & Routing use case overlaps the data-hygiene + routing scope of Operations (source: https://www.adapt.io/platform/enrichment).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing** — Adapt for Marketers explicitly addresses demand gen, email marketers, and marketing leaders with hyper-targeted list building, LinkedIn Ads targeting, and CRM enrichment (source: https://www.adapt.io/solutions/marketing).
  - **ZoomInfo Enterprise API** — the Adapt API (Prospect / Enrich / Refresh) is functionally analogous, though access is gated to the Custom tier (source: https://www.adapt.io/platform/api).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Adapt offers signal-driven prioritization (Funding Insights, Job Change Alerts, Lead Scoring & Routing) that touches ZoomInfo's prioritization story but does not claim a unified GTM Context Graph fusing CRM, conversations, intent, and behavioral signals — adjacency, not substitution (source: https://www.adapt.io/platform/prospecting).
- **No overlap:**
  - No GTM Context Graph or multi-source GTM-reasoning layer surfaced.
  - No conversation intelligence product (no Chorus analogue).
  - No website-chat / data-powered chat product (no ZoomInfo Chat analogue).
  - No MCP server, agent-builder, or LLM-tool ecosystem.
  - No third-party intent / buyer-signal product (Adapt's "signals" are first-party — funding events and job changes).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "ZoomInfo's enterprise-level pricing can be prohibitive. Adapt offers comparable functionality at a fraction of the cost. Plus, our streamlined implementation process gets you up and running in less than 24 hours – no lengthy onboarding or complex configurations… Adapt features built-in ZeroBounce email verification and 3,000+ industries for laser-sharp ICP targeting." (source: https://www.adapt.io/alternatives/best-zoominfo-alternative)

ICP comparison — shared: Sales (AEs / SDRs / Leadership), Marketing & Demand Gen. Competitor-only: Recruiters (called out on /email-finder). ZoomInfo-only: RevOps / GTM Engineers, Account Management / Customer Success, and Developers / AI Agent Builders (no MCP or agent persona is named anywhere on Adapt's reviewed pages).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "B2B Lead Intelligence Platform — Prospecting made easy with our chrome extension. Find verified contact data directly from LinkedIn or the company website. Trusted by 500K+ users globally." Umbrella product name is "Adapt Data OS." (source: https://www.adapt.io/, https://www.adapt.io/pricing)
- **Recurring proof points:** 250M+ verified contacts; trusted by 500K+ users / 250,000 businesses; G2 rating 4.6/5 across 2,777 reviews; customer outcomes including 30% email-success uplift (PeoSpectrum), 40% prospecting productivity (Practical Business Improvements), 25% cold-prospecting revenue lift (Kinetic), and 15x pipeline-velocity returns (Rise Against Hunger); 90%+ stated email accuracy. (source: https://www.adapt.io/alternatives/best-zoominfo-alternative, https://www.adapt.io/email-finder, https://www.adapt.io/)
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames the category as "B2B Lead Intelligence Platform" / "Adapt Data OS" — a prospecting + enrichment + API stack, deliberately positioned beneath ZoomInfo's "all-in-one platform" framing on price and simplicity rather than scope.
