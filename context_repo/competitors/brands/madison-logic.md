## Summary

Madison Logic is an enterprise Account-Based Marketing (ABM) platform that sells intent data, content syndication, and multi-channel ABM media activation (display, CTV, audio, LinkedIn) to mid-market and enterprise B2B marketers (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/). It overlaps with ZoomInfo's Marketing/ABM module and intent data product line, but Madison Logic is media-buying-led — operating as a DSP that runs paid ads against named accounts — which sits adjacent to ZoomInfo's GTM intelligence model. Madison Logic and ZoomInfo announced a strategic partnership in August 2025, so the relationship is both partner and competitor in the ABM space (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/press-release/madison-logic-zoominfo-partnership/). Pricing is gated; third-party estimates put mid-market full-platform contracts at $120K–$250K/year and enterprise at $400K–$800K+/year (source: https://aeroleads.com/blog/madison-logic-pricing-breakdown-product-compared-2026/).

## Product Offerings

### ML Insights

> **Wiki:** [[madison-logic--ml-insights]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered intent data product that captures, scores, and prioritizes engagement signals across accounts and buying-group personas to identify in-market accounts.
- **Key features:**
  - 500M+ monthly proprietary engagement signals feeding account scoring
  - 485M+ professional network for buying-group identification
  - ICP filters, technographics, and purchase-propensity scoring
  - First- and third-party data aggregation across content engagement and cross-channel activity
  - AI-powered next-best-action recommendations across personas and content
  - Real-time refresh of data, scores, and priorities for adaptive targeting
- **Source URL:** https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/ml-insights/

### ABM Content Syndication

> **Wiki:** [[madison-logic--abm-content-syndication]]

- **What it does:** Distributes gated content (whitepapers, reports, webinars) across Madison Logic's publisher network to generate verified, intent-qualified leads from target accounts.
- **Key features:**
  - Comprehensive global publisher network for in-market account reach
  - Single Touch, Double Touch, and BANT qualification programs
  - 3-step proprietary lead verification (client parameters, 6 data-point check, manual review)
  - GDPR-compliant opt-in lead delivery within 45 minutes
  - AI-validated multi-stage process for data accuracy and quality engagement
  - Buying-group-targeted content aligned to purchase triggers
- **Source URL:** https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/abm-content-syndication/

### ABM Display Advertising

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (legacy ABM module / GTM Studio audience-building) vs Madison Logic ML Platform.** Madison Logic positions itself as the "all-in-one ABM platform" for B2B marketers, directly competing with ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM and audience-targeting use case (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Intent Data (Data foundation / GTM Context Graph) vs ML Insights.** Both sell B2B intent; Madison Logic's intent comes from an owned publisher network with content-engagement signals while ZoomInfo's is multi-source. ZoomInfo is a Forrester Wave Leader for Intent Data Providers — Madison Logic is the most direct intent-data competitor in the ABM-media stack (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/ml-insights/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing/Operations attribution vs ML Measurement.** ML Measurement provides cross-channel pipeline attribution and CRM-integrated revenue reporting, overlapping with ZoomInfo Marketing's campaign attribution (source: https://aeroleads.com/blog/madison-logic-pricing-breakdown-product-compared-2026/).

- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data (Pillar 1) (firmographics, contacts) vs ML Insights account/persona targeting.** Madison Logic uses firmographics and technographics for ABM targeting but does NOT sell raw contact data, direct dials, or verified emails. Adjacent rather than substitute (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/ml-insights/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace vs ML Platform sales-enablement features.** Madison Logic surfaces account signals into Gong (AI-personalized follow-up emails, call scripts) but does not provide a seller front-end like ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace; pitch is marketing-led (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/integrations/).

- **No overlap:**
  - **Owned ABM media buying — display, CTV, audio, LinkedIn paid ad activation.** Madison Logic operates as a demand-side platform (DSP) running paid media; ZoomInfo does not sell display/CTV/audio media inventory (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/abm-display-advertising).
  - **B2B content-syndication lead generation as a CPL service.** ZoomInfo does not sell cost-per-lead content syndication from a publisher network; Madison Logic prices content syndication at $60–$150 per verified lead (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/abm-content-syndication/).
  - **Embedded managed-service ABM strategy.** Madison Logic includes hands-on ABM strategist services as part of the engagement (Program Planning, Enablement, Content Strategy, LinkedIn Strategy); ZoomInfo is a SaaS platform and does not productize embedded strategists (source: https://www.madisonlogic.com/company/why-madison-logic).

- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Madison Logic does not publish a vs-ZoomInfo page; the most prominent ZoomInfo-related content on madisonlogic.com is a partnership announcement (https://www.madisonlogic.com/press-release/madison-logic-zoominfo-partnership/, Aug 2025) framing the two as collaborating on B2B audience planning, prospecting, and marketing attribution rather than competing.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Build the brand that drives demand" — own the buying journey by creating lasting impact at every interaction with high-value buying groups through data-driven ABM. Frames as the all-in-one ABM platform combining intent data, content syndication, display, CTV, audio, and LinkedIn Ads.
- **Recurring proof points:** 500+ leading brands; 415M+ decision-makers reached; named customers including Salesforce, T-Mobile, Adobe, Schneider Electric, Akamai, Shell Lubricant Solutions, AgentSync, Vonage, CrowdStrike, Endava, Marketo, Genesys, Panasonic; AgentSync 116% ROI / $9.6M pipeline; Endava 30x ROI; Amilia 746% over pipeline target; platform metrics 32% engagement lift / 17% pipeline increase / 28% faster pipeline velocity; 30+ G2 badges; 485M+ professionals across 128 verticals; 500M+ monthly engagement signals; 20 years of ABM data/AI.
- **Tone / category framing:** Anchors itself as "#1 in data-driven ABM" — frames the category as Account-Based Marketing media activation rather than GTM intelligence or sales data. Positions ABM as a marketer-led discipline owning the buying journey, not a sales prospecting motion.
