## Summary

Klenty is an AI sales engagement platform sold to SMB and mid-market sales teams (SDRs, AEs, Heads of Business Development) that bundles multichannel cadences, a parallel/power dialer, and AI agents for account research and email drafting (Agentic Cadences, SDRx). It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo GTM Workspace and Copilot AI SDR on the seller-execution surface and with Chorus on conversation intelligence (AI Call Coaching Suite / Call IQ). Klenty has no marketer / RevOps front-end, no GTM Context Graph equivalent, and no MCP / agent-builder API surface, so the overlap is concentrated in the seller cadence + dialer + AI SDR lane. Pricing is public and seat-based ($35–$99/user/month) with a 14-day free trial.

## Product Offerings

### Sales Engagement Platform (multi-channel cadences)

> **Wiki:** [[klenty--sales-engagement-platform]]

- **What it does:** Plans, executes, and tracks outbound and inbound sales sequences across email, calls, LinkedIn, SMS, and WhatsApp from one platform (source: https://www.klenty.com/feature/multi-channel-outreach/).
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-channel sequences across email, dialer, text, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp
  - Email personalization with placeholders, liquid templates, video, and AI-researched openers
  - Inbox auto-rotation and email warmup for primary-tab deliverability
  - Triggers and Actions to automate CRM workflows based on engagement
  - SmartLists, A/B testing, and a multi-channel inbox to consolidate prospect communications
  - Bidirectional CRM sync with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, MS Dynamics
- **Source URL:** https://www.klenty.com/feature/multi-channel-outreach/

### Agentic Cadences

> **Wiki:** [[klenty--agentic-cadences]]

- **What it does:** AI agents research accounts, write 1:1 emails, refresh stale templates, and orchestrate personalized multi-channel cadences for every prospect, putting reps in an approval role rather than execution role (source: https://www.klenty.com/feature/agentic-cadences).
- **Key features:**
  - AI Research Feed pulls account/prospect insights from 150+ public data sources and LinkedIn into prospect fields
  - Tailored cadences per account/prospect rather than standardized templates
  - AI-written cold emails and follow-ups using sales frameworks
  - AI Variables that insert researched subject lines, openers, body copy, and CTAs
  - Auto-enrollment of unresponsive prospects in AI-written nurture cadences
  - Positioning shift "from order takers to approvers" of AI-executed work
- **Source URL:** https://www.klenty.com/feature/agentic-cadences

### Sales Dialer Suite (Parallel Dialer, Power Dialer, Click-to-Call)

> **Wiki:** [[klenty--sales-dialer]]

- **What it does:** Multi-mode sales dialer that lets reps make up to ~350 calls/hour with parallel dialing, AI voicemail/IVR detection, AI note-taking, and CRM auto-update (source: https://www.klenty.com/sales-dialer/pricing/).
- **Key features:**
  - Parallel Dialer dials 5 prospects simultaneously up to ~350 calls/hour
  - Power Dialer dials full lists sequentially in one click
  - AI Voicemail Detection, AI IVR Detection, AI Outcome Detection
  - One-click Voicemail Drop and call recordings
  - Local Dial and number auto-rotation with reputation management
  - VoIP virtual numbers at $1.15/month per number; ~$0.021/min US calling rate after free credits
- **Source URL:** https://www.klenty.com/sales-dialer/pricing/

### AI Call Coaching Suite (Call IQ)

> **Wiki:** [[klenty--call-iq]]

- **What it does:** Conversation intelligence layer that ingests sales calls and produces transcripts, summaries, scorecards, and coaching insights against an ingested talk track (source: https://www.klenty.com/sales-engagement-platform/pricing/).
- **Key features:**
  - Live Transcription, AI Conversation Tracking, AI Topics
  - AI Battlecards surfaced live during calls
  - AI Objection Detection and AI Call Scorecard
  - Coaching Summary and Talk Track Tables to enforce cold-calling methodology
  - Free Call IQ trial — analyze 250 minutes of meetings
  - Call Transcription requires AI Coaching add-on at $40/user/month
- **Source URL:** https://www.klenty.com/sales-engagement-platform/pricing/

### AI Account Research / AI List Builder (SDRx)

> **Wiki:** [[klenty--sdrx]]

- **What it does:** AI agent (SDRx) that builds an ICP-matched account list from a natural-language prompt and runs account research across 150+ data sources to produce a tailored value proposition per account (source: https://www.klenty.com/).
- **Key features:**
  - Natural-language ICP description produces a pre-validated account list
  - Account research across 150+ public data sources
  - Tailored value proposition generated per account
  - Insights piped directly into prospect fields for cadence personalization
- **Source URL:** https://www.klenty.com/

### CRM Acceleration (Salesforce / HubSpot / Pipedrive / Zoho / MS Dynamics)

> **Wiki:** [[klenty--crm-acceleration]]

- **What it does:** Native bidirectional CRM integration that lets reps run cadences, import/export prospects, and sync emails/calls/tasks without leaving their CRM (source: https://www.klenty.com/crm-acceleration/salesforce).
- **Key features:**
  - Salesforce plugin compatible with Lightning and Classic — execute cadences without leaving Salesforce
  - Import any list/report from Salesforce; auto-import new contacts every few hours
  - BCC CRM email sync and real-time activity logs across emails, calls, tasks
  - Sales Stage Automation — start/stop cadences as deals move stages
  - Bidirectional sync with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, MS Dynamics
- **Source URL:** https://www.klenty.com/crm-acceleration/salesforce

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS, sales-led tech, mid-market services (named customers include First Due, Plauti, BlueInk, Group GTI, Double the Donation, Synup, NetReputation, Azeus Convene, GoShare, LearnWorlds, PipeCandy).
- **Company size called out:** SMB and mid-market. Klenty's pricing page claims "sales teams of 10 or 10,000," but the third-party FoxReach comparison pegs Klenty as "mid-size sales teams" against ZoomInfo's "enterprise" framing.
- **Persona / role focus:** SDRs / BDRs (heavy cold-callers), Account Executives running outbound cadences, Heads of Sales Development / VPs of Business Development, Sales Operations.
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Outbound prospecting (cold email + cold calling cadences)
  - Inbound lead follow-up automation from CRM leads
  - Account-Based Selling (Plus tier adds account prioritization, account panel, account-based execution)
  - AI SDR / agentic cadence execution to free reps from research and email drafting
  - Sales call coaching / conversation intelligence (Call IQ)

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce (Lightning + Classic plugin), HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, Microsoft Dynamics 365 (source: https://www.klenty.com/crm-acceleration/salesforce).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Klenty itself is the SEP; competes via /compare pages against Outreach, Salesloft, Nooks, Orum, JustCall.
- **Data / enrichment:** Bundled phone+email credits at the Plus tier (4,000 credits); AI Account Research pulls from 150+ public data sources and LinkedIn (source: https://www.klenty.com/feature/agentic-cadences).
- **Other notable integrations:** Zapier (Starter tier), Google / Microsoft auth for sign-up, BCC CRM email sync, public API for custom workflows.

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Agentic Cadences — AI agents research accounts and write 1:1 emails so reps approve rather than execute every step (source: https://www.klenty.com/feature/agentic-cadences).
- Parallel Dialer reaches up to ~350 calls/hour by dialing 5 prospects simultaneously with AI voicemail/IVR detection (source: https://www.klenty.com/sales-dialer/pricing/).
- True multi-channel coverage including WhatsApp, called out by FoxReach as a differentiator vs. ZoomInfo (source: https://www.foxreach.io/tools/compare/klenty-vs-zoominfo).
- Pricing transparency at $35–$99/user/month with no required enterprise contract; a customer testimonial frames it as "a simple decision" vs. SalesLoft or Outreach (source: https://www.klenty.com/sales-engagement-platform/pricing/).
- Self-described as "the only Sales Engagement Platform built to simultaneously capture active buyers and nurture passive buyers" (source: https://www.klenty.com/sales-engagement-platform/pricing/).
- 24/5 customer support with a 2.5-minute average response time; SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and HIPAA badges (source: https://www.klenty.com/sales-engagement-platform/pricing/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace ↔ Klenty Sales Engagement Platform + Agentic Cadences.** Klenty positions itself as the seller execution surface for multichannel outreach with AI-drafted emails, prioritized accounts, and cadence execution from one platform — the same scope GTM Workspace claims as the next-gen successor to ZoomInfo Sales (source: https://www.klenty.com/feature/agentic-cadences).
  - **Chorus + GTM Workspace AI SDR ↔ Klenty AI Call Coaching Suite (Call IQ) + SDRx.** Klenty's AI Call Coaching Suite delivers transcripts, summaries, AI Battlecards, AI Call Scorecards, and AI Objection Detection — the same conversation-intelligence + coaching scope Chorus owns. SDRx and Agentic Cadences play the AI SDR role that ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI agent layer does (source: https://www.klenty.com/sales-dialer/pricing/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (legacy ZoomInfo Engage) ↔ Klenty SEP tiers (Starter / Growth / Plus).** SourceForge maintains a "Klenty vs ZoomInfo Engage" comparison and Cuspera maintains "Klenty vs ZoomInfo InboxAI" — both vendors are competing for the same legacy sales-engagement footprint (source: https://sourceforge.net/software/compare/Klenty-vs-ZoomInfo-Engage/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Data foundation (500M contacts, 200M+ verified emails) ↔ Klenty AI List Builder + 4,000 bundled credits at Plus.** Klenty exposes a small data layer through AI List Builder and bundled credits, but does not maintain a comparable B2B database; FoxReach explicitly contrasts Klenty's lack of "largest B2B contact database" against ZoomInfo (source: https://www.foxreach.io/tools/compare/klenty-vs-zoominfo).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing) ↔ Klenty CRM Acceleration (Triggers and Actions, Sales Stage Automation, bidirectional sync).** Adjacent automation layer but not a replacement for Operations' routing and dedupe scope (source: https://www.klenty.com/crm-acceleration/salesforce).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio (marketer / RevOps / GTM-engineer front-end).** Klenty has no marketer or RevOps surface — pages address sellers, SDR managers, and Sales Ops only.
  - **GTM Context Graph (proprietary B2B data fused with first-party CRM/conversation/behavioral signals, 1.5B+ data points/day).** No data-graph or intent-data layer surfaced on Klenty pages.
  - **APIs & MCP (Data, Marketing, Platform).** Klenty exposes a basic API but no MCP server or agent-builder ecosystem positioning.
  - **Bombora-style intent data and ABM advertising.** Not surfaced on reviewed Klenty pages.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No first-party Klenty vs ZoomInfo page surfaced in SERP; the closest is a third-party FoxReach comparison: "Klenty focuses on mid-size sales teams running structured multi-channel outreach cadences, while ZoomInfo is designed for enterprise sales and marketing teams who need comprehensive B2B data at scale. Klenty is more affordable at $50/mo vs Custom." (source: https://www.foxreach.io/tools/compare/klenty-vs-zoominfo)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "AI Sales Engagement Platform — Open More Conversations. Build More Pipeline." Klenty places dials, improves connect rates, manages multi-channel sequences, and handles admin so reps focus on conversations.
- **Recurring proof points:** 5,000+ fast-growing sales teams; G2 4.6/5; First Due 60→200 converted meetings in 8 months; Double the Donation 93% outbound revenue jump; BlueInk 100% increase in meetings booked in a single quarter and 97% increase in open rates; Plauti 40% increase in LinkedIn execution; Group GTI 233% increase in booked meetings.
- **Tone / category framing:** Self-positioned as the "AI Sales Engagement Platform" and "the only SEP that simultaneously captures active buyers and nurtures passive buyers." Frames the AI shift as moving reps from "order takers" to "approvers" of AI-executed work.
