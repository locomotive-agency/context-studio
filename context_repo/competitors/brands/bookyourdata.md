# BookYourData

## Sub-products

- [[bookyourdata--paygo-database|BookYourData Pay-As-You-Go Database (Prospector + Verification + Enrichment)]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales

## Summary

BookYourData is a pay-as-you-go B2B contact data and sales intelligence vendor targeting SMB sales teams, agencies, recruiters, and founders who want verified lead lists without a subscription. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Data pillar and the classic ZoomInfo Sales prospecting workflow — Prospector + Email Verification + Technographics ship a similar contact-export job-to-be-done — but the pitch is explicitly cost and simplicity ("$799 one-time for 5k contacts vs ZoomInfo starting from $15k/year"). It does not build the GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, conversation intelligence, or APIs & MCP / agent surfaces ZoomInfo's three-pillar narrative requires.

## Product Offerings

### Prospector

- **What it does:** Self-serve list builder over a 250M-contact, 30M-company B2B database with real-time email verification.
- **Key features:**
  - Access to 250M+ verified B2B contacts across 200+ countries
  - 100+ search filters (industry, job title, location, company size, role)
  - Real-time email verification on every export
  - 100+ data attributes per contact and company record
  - Mobile direct dials (50M+) and personal/work emails
  - Bulk lookup and instant CSV/Excel download or direct CRM push
  - Job posting and last-funding-round filters for targeting hiring/funded companies
- **Source URL:** https://www.bookyourdata.com/platform/prospector

### Email Verification

- **What it does:** Built-in 8-step real-time verification engine that validates every email at export to guarantee 97% accuracy.
- **Key features:**
  - 8-step real-time verification triggered at export
  - Catch-all email verification (vendor claims industry-first)
  - Pay only for deliverable emails — invalid records refund the credit
  - Built into every plan (no add-on fee)
  - 97% accuracy guarantee or credits returned
- **Source URL:** https://www.bookyourdata.com/platform/email-verification

### Email Finder

- **What it does:** Single-record email lookup tool that returns verified email addresses by company name, domain, or contact details.
- **Key features:**
  - Find emails by company name, domain/URL, or contact details
  - 97% accuracy guarantee
  - Pay-only-for-results pricing model
  - Backed by the 250M+ contact database
- **Source URL:** https://www.bookyourdata.com/platform/email-finder

### Data Enrichment

- **What it does:** Enriches user-supplied lists with 100+ contact and company attributes via CRM connectors, CSV upload, or REST API.
- **Key features:**
  - CRM enrichment for Salesforce and HubSpot (auto-sync incomplete records)
  - CSV enrichment — upload partial lists and add missing fields
  - REST API enrichment for systems integration
  - Email enrichment (upload an email list, retrieve names/titles/phones/social)
  - Contacts enhancement (names + companies → full contact info)
  - Company enhancement (URL → firmographics, employee count, sales volume)
  - Pay-only-for-verified-matches model
- **Source URL:** https://www.bookyourdata.com/platform/data-enrichment

### Technographics

- **What it does:** Filter prospects by the 60K+ web technologies a company uses or plans to implement.
- **Key features:**
  - 60K+ tracked web technologies
  - Strategic lead targeting by tech stack used or planned
  - Smart exclusion filters (drop companies on competing tech)
  - Chrome extension surfaces a company's tech stack while browsing
  - Bundled into the standard product (no higher pricing tier required)
- **Source URL:** https://www.bookyourdata.com/platform/technographics

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Pay-as-you-go credit model with credits that never expire — explicit anti-subscription positioning vs ZoomInfo, Cognism, Apollo, Lusha (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/pricing)
- 97% data-accuracy guarantee with credit refund for invalid records; vendor cites 50-60% as industry average (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/pricing)
- Built-in 8-step real-time email verification on every export, including catch-all email verification — claimed industry-first (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/platform/email-verification)
- All enterprise features (technographics, API, data enrichment, unlimited team seats) included at every tier — no feature gating (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/pricing)
- Transparent self-serve pricing table from $0 (free trial) through $24,999 (2.5M credits) with no "Contact sales" wall (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/pricing)
- G2 4.9/5 rating (370 reviews) with 2025 G2 badges including Best Usability, Easy to Use, High Performer, Momentum Leader (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation (500M contacts / 200M+ verified business emails / 30K+ technographics)** vs Prospector + Email Verification + Technographics — BookYourData claims 250M+ B2B contacts, 150M+ business emails, 50M+ mobile direct dials, 30M+ companies, and 60K+ tracked technologies, directly competing on contact volume and tech-stack tracking (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (classic prospecting UI / contact list building)** vs Prospector — same job-to-be-done (filter and export verified contact lists); BookYourData explicitly positions itself as the SMB/startup alternative to enterprise SalesOS (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/blog/bookyourdata-vs-zoominfo).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality / enrichment)** vs Data Enrichment (CRM, CSV, API) — BookYourData ships direct CRM auto-enrichment for Salesforce and HubSpot plus a REST API, overlapping the Operations module for SMB buyers (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/platform/data-enrichment).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end with AI-drafted outreach)** — BookYourData stops at handing the verified list to the rep's CRM; it does not author or sequence outreach. It feeds a workflow GTM Workspace owns end-to-end (source: https://intercom.help/bookyourdata/en/articles/14170023-what-crm-integrations-does-bookyourdata-support).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph / first-party-data unification** — no reasoning layer over CRM + conversation + intent. BookYourData is a list export tool, not a context graph (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/blog/bookyourdata-vs-zoominfo).
  - **Conversation intelligence (Chorus equivalent)** — not surfaced anywhere; vs-page concedes ZoomInfo has "call analysis" that BookYourData lacks (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/blog/bookyourdata-vs-zoominfo).
  - **GTM Studio (NL audience building / play orchestration for marketers, RevOps, GTM engineers)** — no marketer/RevOps front-end. Vs-page positions ZoomInfo as "best for large enterprises" on this dimension (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/blog/bookyourdata-vs-zoominfo).
  - **APIs & MCP / agent ecosystem** — no MCP or agent-builder support; their public API and Chrome extension are flagged "Coming Soon" on the product nav (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/).
  - **Advanced AI / GTM Workspace AI agent layer equivalent** — vs-page admits "Offers minimal AI features" vs ZoomInfo's "various AI features like advanced AI for intent data, lead scoring, and enrichment" (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/blog/bookyourdata-vs-zoominfo).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "BookYourData offers simple and practical features like filters, targeting, and instant downloads, while ZoomInfo has advanced automation like sales triggers and intent signals that can be complex to set up and are not suitable for businesses that just need contact information. Sample price for 5k contacts: BookYourData $799 one-time fee vs ZoomInfo starting from $15k/year." (source: https://www.bookyourdata.com/blog/bookyourdata-vs-zoominfo).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** The only B2B data platform with pay-as-you-go pricing, never-expiring credits, and a 97% accuracy guarantee — "laser-targeted leads on demand, without subscriptions, at 1/4 the cost of other providers."
- **Recurring proof points:**
  - G2 4.9/5 stars (370 reviews) — "#1 Highest Rated Sales Intelligence Software by G2" (vendor claim)
  - G2 2025 badges: Best Usability, Easiest to Use, High Performer, Momentum Leader, Best Meets Requirements
  - 100,000+ clients since 2015 (data-enrichment page)
  - 250M+ contacts, 150M+ business emails, 50M+ mobile direct dials, 30M+ companies, 60K+ technologies tracked
  - Customer quotes from Creative Edge (AU) and IRise Realty Corp explicitly naming ZoomInfo and Lusha as the alternatives BookYourData beat
  - Best Price Guarantee — beat any US provider with 95%+ accuracy
- **Tone / category framing:** B2B data platform / sales intelligence software. Self-positioning is "pay-as-you-go email database provider" explicitly contrasted with "subscription-based providers like ZoomInfo, Cognism, Apollo, Lusha" — they own the cost-flexibility frame, not the platform-depth frame.
