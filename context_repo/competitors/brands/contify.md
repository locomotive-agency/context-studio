## Summary

Contify is an AI-native Market & Competitive Intelligence (M&CI) platform built for Strategy / MI/CI analysts and secondary Product, Marketing, and Sales buyers in enterprise accounts. It monitors 1 Mn+ news, web, social, and internal sources across 700k+ companies and 117+ languages, then surfaces decision-ready insights through Athena AI, dashboards, newsletters, and a Business News API. Overlap with ZoomInfo is concentrated in the signal/context layer (Athena AI + Gong/Fireflies/SharePoint ingest mirrors GTM Context Graph + Chorus) and in sales battlecards / account triggers (vs ZoomInfo Sales); it does not compete on B2B contact data, phone numbers, emails, or technographics. Contify positions in the Gartner CI&MI category — recognized as a 2026 Visionary — rather than the GTM / sales-intelligence category ZoomInfo owns.

## Product Offerings

### M&CI Platform

> **Wiki:** [[contify--m-and-ci-platform]]

- **What it does:** AI-native market and competitive intelligence platform that collects, analyzes, and delivers ready-to-use insights from global and proprietary internal sources, customized for every team, function, and strategic priority.
- **Key features:**
  - Athena AI — agentic insights engine with Business Facts, Automated Insights, and Ask Athena
  - Dashboards with auto-updating battlecards, trend analysis, and 50+ pre-configured templates
  - Newsletters with auto-summarized updates, automated alerts, and custom branded templates
  - Newsfeeds (Global + Curated) with smart filters by company, business event, content type, and location
  - Custom sources, custom taxonomy/tags, and custom fact & insight prompts
  - Multilingual coverage of 117+ languages with auto-translation to English
  - Coverage of 700,000+ companies across 100+ industry segments
  - Internal-source ingestion: SharePoint, Gong/Fireflies call transcripts, Slack, MS Teams, Email
- **Source URL:** https://www.contify.com/platform/

### Athena AI

> **Wiki:** [[contify--athena-ai]]

- **What it does:** Proprietary agentic AI insights engine that transforms unstructured market updates into structured, verified insights and answers ad-hoc business questions grounded in curated intelligence datasets.
- **Key features:**
  - Business Facts: 30+ auto-extracted business facts including product launches, fundings, and M&As
  - Automated Insights: continuously updating insights generated from external + internal intel with tailored prompts
  - Ask Athena: on-demand research assistant for ad-hoc intelligence queries
  - Real-time Dashboards: charts, battlecards, and insight widgets that auto-update; embeddable in CRM/KMS/ERP
  - Quoted outcomes: 50% improvement in competitive awareness, 30% reduction in research time, 10x faster insight generation
- **Source URL:** https://www.contify.com/athena-ai/

### Business News API

> **Wiki:** [[contify--business-news-api]]

- **What it does:** Developer-facing API delivering GenAI-enriched, structured business news data on companies, industries, and topics for embedding into apps, CRMs, BI tools, and LLM pipelines.
- **Key features:**
  - 1 Mn+ curated business-relevant sources, including 250k+ non-English
  - 700k+ companies tracked globally; 900k+ API calls/day; 2.5 Mn+ stories/week
  - Auto-translated content from 117+ languages and 190+ nations
  - Entity, event, and sentiment tags across 50+ business activities (funding, partnerships, regulatory)
  - AI-generated key highlights per story; deduplication; ML noise filtering with human-in-loop validation
  - REST/JSON endpoints with 15+ metadata fields and webhook delivery
  - 99.9% uptime SLA; configurable rate limits per tier
  - Use cases include AI/LLM training, company intelligence feeds, BI/analytics, risk analysis, research enrichment
- **Source URL:** https://www.contify.com/news-api/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph + Chorus (ZoomInfo) ↔ Athena AI + internal-source ingestion (Contify):** Contify ingests Gong/Fireflies sales-call transcripts and internal docs (SharePoint, Slack, MS Teams, email) to capture "competitor mentions, buyer problems, product discussions, and key trends" — the same conversation-context fusion thesis ZoomInfo pitches with the GTM Context Graph + Chorus (source: https://www.contify.com/integrations/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (account intelligence / sales triggers / battlecards) ↔ Contify Account & Sales Intelligence:** Contify pitches sales teams on auto-updated battlecards, key-account triggers (leadership changes, funding, M&A, expansions), and competitor pricing-change alerts — directly overlapping with ZoomInfo Sales' account-intelligence and signal use cases (source: https://www.contify.com/functions/account-intelligence-sales-team/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **APIs & MCP ↔ Contify Business News API:** Contify ships a developer-facing Business News API (700k+ companies, 900k+ calls/day, REST/JSON, 99.9% SLA) for embedding company/industry news into apps, CRMs, BI, and LLM pipelines — adjacent to ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP but news/signals-shaped instead of contact/firmographic-shaped (source: https://www.contify.com/news-api/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (intent / signal layer) ↔ Contify M&CI Platform:** Contify monitors competitor website changes, M&As, partnerships, regulatory filings, and event participation across 1 Mn+ sources — adjacent intent/signal coverage, but sourced from public web/news rather than B2B intent providers and behavioral data (source: https://www.contify.com/platform/).
- **No overlap:**
  - Patent-filings monitoring, regulatory-compliance tracking, custom analyst-curated intelligence newsletters, and bundled analyst-managed services are core Contify workflows that ZoomInfo does not offer — these are MI/CI-team workflows, not seller workflows (source: https://www.contify.com/functions/competitive-intelligence-product/).
  - Contify does not sell B2B contact data, verified phone numbers / direct dials, verified emails, or technographics (negative-space: ZoomInfo's 500M contacts / 135M phones / 200M emails are not a Contify offering) (source: https://www.contify.com/platform/).
