## Summary

Bombora is a B2B Intent-data and audience provider selling primarily to B2B marketing, sales, advertising, and agency teams; its flagship Company Surge® Intent data is sourced from a proprietary Co-op of 200+ publishers and 5,500+ B2B media sites. The most material overlap with ZoomInfo is squarely on the Intent/data layer — Bombora positions itself as "the leader in B2B Intent data" and is cited by Forrester as the "gold standard" for account-level intent, directly contesting the Intent component of ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph. Critically, Bombora is "a data company, not a platform" — it has no contact database, no seller workspace, and no conversation-intelligence layer, and instead pushes signals into other vendors' surfaces (HubSpot, Salesforce, Apollo, 6sense, Demandbase). Pricing is fully gated and Bombora does not publish a vs-ZoomInfo comparison on its own properties.

## Product Offerings

### Company Surge® Intent data

> **Wiki:** [[bombora--company-surge]]

- **What it does:** Bombora's flagship B2B Intent product that flags accounts actively researching topics relevant to a customer's products, derived from the Bombora Data Co-op.
- **Key features:**
  - 20,100+ Company Surge® Intent topics tracked across the B2B web
  - Buying-stage mapping (early / mid / late research) per account
  - Patented Intent-identification methodology using AI, ML, LLMs, and NLP — not just keyword correlation
  - Account-specific relative scores derived from historical baselines
  - Patented composite identity resolution (beyond IP-to-domain matching)
  - 100+ integrations across ad-tech, martech, sales management, and CRM platforms
  - Privacy-first / consent-driven collection (GDPR, CCPA; IAB TCF v2.2)
- **Source URL:** https://bombora.com/intent/

### Identity & Enrichment (Visitor Insights)

> **Wiki:** [[bombora--identity-enrichment]]

- **What it does:** Identifies and enriches anonymous website visitors using a composite deterministic + probabilistic methodology, fueling first-party data activation.
- **Key features:**
  - Patented composite methodology fusing deterministic, behavioral, and IP-to-company data
  - Proprietary work-from-home model for probabilistic identity
  - Real-time delivery into CDPs, CMSs, chatbots, and other site-personalization tools
  - Visitor-level firmographic, demographic, and behavioral enrichment
  - Retargeting audiences from identified site visitors via Bombora's DSP/SSP partners
  - Customer-owned first-party data with no usage limitations imposed by Bombora
- **Source URL:** https://bombora.com/identity-and-enrichment/

### Premium B2B Digital Audiences

> **Wiki:** [[bombora--digital-audiences]]

- **What it does:** Pre-built and custom B2B audiences activatable across DSPs, SSPs, and DMPs for targeted advertising campaigns.
- **Key features:**
  - 600+ pre-built B2B audiences ready to activate
  - 6,350 firmographic, B2B demographic, and behavioral attributes for custom audiences
  - Audiences claim to be 5x larger than other B2B providers across key segments
  - Activation across all leading DSP, SSP, and DMP platforms
  - Curated Ecosystem Audiences from partners (Crunchbase, Definitive Healthcare, G2, HG Insights)
  - Layerable with Company Surge® Intent for in-market account targeting
- **Source URL:** https://bombora.com/digital-audiences/

### Campaign Measurement (B2beacon™)

> **Wiki:** [[bombora--b2beacon]]

- **What it does:** B2B-centric digital campaign measurement that reports reach and engagement at the account, buying group, and persona level without requiring an ABM platform subscription.
- **Key features:**
  - Granular performance measurement at account, buying-group, and persona level
  - Standardized metrics across the platforms, channels, and ad formats B2B advertisers use
  - No upfront commitment or ABM-platform subscription required
  - Log-level campaign measurement to justify future ad investment
  - Integrated with Bombora's Insights suite dashboards
- **Source URL:** https://bombora.com/digital-audiences/

### Insights Suite

> **Wiki:** [[bombora--insights-suite]]

- **What it does:** Visualization layer that turns Bombora's Intent, Visitor Insights, and B2beacon™ data into intuitive dashboards (Visitor Insights, Market Insights, B2beacon™) without requiring a dedicated data analyst.
- **Key features:**
  - Visitor Insights dashboards — de-anonymized site visitors with firmographic + Intent enrichment
  - Market Insights — longitudinal view of trends across the B2B web
  - B2beacon™ campaign-performance visualizations
  - Account-level Intent shifts mapped to sales cycle stages
  - Topic-level engagement segmentation for content and messaging strategy
- **Source URL:** https://bombora.com/insights-suite/

### Bombora Data Co-op

- **What it does:** The proprietary B2B publisher cooperative that powers all Bombora data products — direct-tag relationships with thousands of B2B media destinations.
- **Key features:**
  - 200+ publishers and brands; 5,500+ B2B media sites (1,740+ added in 2024)
  - Billions of monthly consumption events, millions of unique domains tracked
  - 86% of Co-op data exclusively shared with Bombora for deriving Intent
  - Proprietary JavaScript tag deployed on Co-op member pages (gated, paywalled, and open content)
  - Consent-driven collection compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and IAB TCF v2.2
  - Direct contractual relationships with each Co-op member
- **Source URL:** https://bombora.com/co-op/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Proprietary B2B Data Co-op of 200+ publishers and 5,500+ media sites with 86% of data exclusively shared with Bombora for Intent derivation — competitors cannot replicate this signal source. (source: https://bombora.com/co-op/)
- Direct-tag data collection (proprietary JavaScript on Co-op member pages) instead of scraping or bidstream inference — collects consent on 100% of events. (source: https://bombora.com/intent/)
- Patented Intent-identification methodology using account-specific historical baselines plus AI/ML/LLM/NLP for relevance, not keyword correlation. (source: https://bombora.com/intent/)
- Positioned as "a data company, not a platform" — explicitly designed to be deployed inside customers' existing tech stacks via 100+ integrations. (source: https://bombora.com/)
- Forrester Wave-cited "gold standard" for account-level intent (quoted on the Intent product page). (source: https://bombora.com/intent/)
- B2beacon™ — claimed as the only B2B digital audience provider offering campaign-by-campaign measurement at account/buying-group/persona level without an ABM platform subscription. (source: https://bombora.com/digital-audiences/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Intent Data and the Intent layer of the GTM Context Graph** vs. **Bombora Company Surge® Intent data** — both identify in-market accounts via topic-level research signals; Bombora explicitly claims "gold standard" for account-level intent, contesting the Intent component of ZoomInfo's three-pillar narrative. (source: https://bombora.com/intent/)
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM audiences and digital advertising activation)** vs. **Bombora Premium B2B Digital Audiences + Curated Ecosystem Audiences** — 600+ pre-built audiences and 6,350 attributes activate across DSPs/SSPs/DMPs for the same ABM and digital-targeting use cases. (source: https://bombora.com/digital-audiences/)
  - **ZoomInfo Operations identity-resolution and visitor-deanonymization** vs. **Bombora Identity & Enrichment (Visitor Insights)** — Visitor Insights de-anonymizes ~97% of unidentified site traffic and pushes data into CDPs/CRMs/chatbots. (source: https://bombora.com/identity-and-enrichment/)
- **Adjacent overlap:** B2beacon™ campaign measurement touches the same ad-performance / attribution workflows ZoomInfo Marketing reports on, but Bombora positions B2beacon™ as a stand-alone alternative to ABM-platform measurement. (source: https://bombora.com/digital-audiences/)
- **No overlap:**
  - **B2B contact database / direct dials / verified emails** — Bombora is explicit that it is a data signal provider, not a contact database; homepage states "We are a data company, not a platform." No contact records, dialer integrations, or email-validation features surfaced. ZoomInfo's 500M-contact / 135M-phone foundation has no Bombora equivalent. (source: https://bombora.com/)
  - **Seller workspace / outreach UI (analogous to GTM Workspace, Chorus)** — Bombora has no seller front-end product, no AI-drafted outreach, and no conversation-intelligence equivalent of Chorus. (source: https://bombora.com/)
  - **Publisher-side monetization via Co-op membership** — Bombora sells into B2B publishers as Co-op members; ZoomInfo does not target publishers as a buyer persona. (source: https://bombora.com/)
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No public Bombora-owned vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced. The only direct comparison content recovered is a single Gartner Peer Insights review snippet stating Bombora's intent data "comes from a consortium of B2B websites and seems to be more predictive of surging research on topics than the intent add-ons you get with contact data providers." (source: https://www.gartner.com/reviews/market/revenue-data-solutions/compare/bombora-vs-zoominfo) — see flags.
