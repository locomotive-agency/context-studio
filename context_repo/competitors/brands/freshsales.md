# Freshsales

## Summary

Freshsales is an AI-powered sales CRM built by Freshworks, sold primarily into startup, SMB, and mid-market sales teams (with an enterprise tier) as the system of record for contacts, pipeline, and multichannel customer engagement. It is structurally complementary to ZoomInfo rather than a direct competitor: Freshsales does not sell B2B contact data, intent, technographics, or a context graph — those are exactly the gaps ZoomInfo's data foundation and GTM Context Graph fill, which is why Freshsales appears in Freshworks' own marketplace as a ZoomInfo Sales integration target. The adjacency is on the seller-experience layer (Freddy AI for lead scoring, deal insights, and sales emails) and on the marketer layer via Freshsales Suite (CRM + marketing automation), where it touches GTM Workspace and GTM Studio surfaces from a CRM-of-record posture.

## Product Offerings

### Freshsales (Sales CRM)

> **Wiki:** [[freshsales--sales-crm]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered sales CRM that helps teams capture, qualify, route, and track leads, manage pipeline, and close deals using a 360° customer view and Freddy AI.
- **Key features:**
  - Kanban view for Contacts, Accounts, and Deals
  - Built-in Chat, Email, and Phone (cloud telephony)
  - Contact lifecycle stages and auto profile enrichment
  - Sales sequences (multi-step email / call / SMS automation)
  - Multiple sales pipelines and deal management
  - Freddy AI: contact scoring, deal insights, sales emails, deduplication, forecasting
  - Workflows (basic and advanced) plus auto-assignment / territory management
  - Curated and custom reports / dashboards
- **Source URL:** https://www.freshworks.com/crm/sales/

### Freshsales Suite

> **Wiki:** [[freshsales--suite]]

- **What it does:** Combined CRM + marketing automation product that unifies sales and marketing teams on a single 360° customer view, adding journeys, segmentation, and multichannel campaigns to the core CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - All Freshsales CRM features plus marketing automation
  - Marketing journey builder with triggers, actions, controls, delays
  - Segments and marketing lists (demography, geography, behavior)
  - Visual drag-and-drop email builder
  - Landing pages and web forms
  - Conversion-rate optimization (heatmaps, session replay, A/B testing)
  - Multichannel engagement: Phone, Email, WhatsApp, Chat, SMS
  - AI chatbot / lead-generation bot
- **Source URL:** https://www.freshworks.com/crm/suite/

### Freddy AI (embedded across Freshsales)

> **Wiki:** [[freshsales--freddy-ai]]

- **What it does:** Generative-AI assistant inside Freshsales that scores leads, drafts emails, surfaces priority deals, deduplicates records, and provides forecasting insights.
- **Key features:**
  - Contact scoring
  - Sales emails by Freddy AI (drafting, rephrasing, expanding text)
  - Deal insights — which deals to focus on
  - Deduplication of leads / contacts
  - Out-of-office detection on incoming emails
  - Forecasting insights (Enterprise tier)
- **Source URL:** https://www.freshworks.com/crm/features/

### Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) add-on

> **Wiki:** [[freshsales--cpq]]

- **What it does:** Add-on that lets sales reps generate branded documents like invoices, quotes, onboarding documents, and contracts directly from the CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Generate quotes, invoices, contracts, onboarding documents
  - 1 CPQ license included with the Growth plan
  - Additional licenses at $19/user/month
  - Linked to deal records inside Freshsales
- **Source URL:** https://www.freshworks.com/crm/pricing/

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Self-positions as the CRM of record. A HubSpot data sync app is published in the Freshworks Marketplace (per SERP listing); migration paths from Salesforce / HubSpot are messaged on comparison pages.
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Built-in email, phone (cloud telephony), chat, SMS, WhatsApp Business, Apple Business Chat; sales sequences are native to Freshsales; Slack for contextual collaboration (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/features/).
- **Data / enrichment:** Auto-profile enrichment from social and publicly listed information is the only native enrichment surface — there is no third-party B2B data product (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/features/).
- **Other notable integrations:** Freshdesk (Freshworks customer support), Freshsuccess, Freshworks Marketplace (100+ apps including Mailchimp, DocuSign, Facebook, Slack), webhooks, REST APIs, Gmail and Outlook add-ons, Google Maps (mobile), Microsoft Teams and Google Workspace (per Lalamove case study).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Transparent, low-cost public pricing with a free tier for up to 3 users and a $9/user Growth tier — substantially below enterprise data / intelligence platforms (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/pricing/).
- Freddy AI is woven through the product (contact scoring, deal insights, sales emails, deduplication, forecasting) rather than sold as a separate tier (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/features/).
- Built-in cloud telephony, chat, WhatsApp Business, Apple Business Chat, and SMS as native CRM channels — not bolt-on integrations (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/features/).
- Sales + marketing unification via Freshsales Suite (CRM + marketing automation in one product) rather than separate Marketing / Sales SKUs (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/suite/).
- Positioned for ease of use and fast time-to-value: 21-day free trial, no credit card, friendly onboarding, in-app guided tours (source: https://www.freshworks.com/products/what-is-freshsales/).
- Trusted by 74,000+ businesses worldwide with named customers across geographies — Lalamove, Blue Nile, Sify, Chargebee, TaylorMade, RingCentral, Trainline (source: https://www.freshworks.com/products/what-is-freshsales/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** None. Freshsales is a CRM of record; it does not compete head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Data foundation, GTM Context Graph, or APIs & MCP. Freshworks even publishes a "ZoomInfo Sales" integration in its own marketplace (per SERP) so customers can pipe ZoomInfo data into Freshsales.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end, prioritized accounts + AI-drafted outreach):** Freshsales offers AI-drafted prospecting emails, sales sequences, lead scoring, and deal-prioritization recommendations from inside its own CRM (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/features/).
  - **GTM Studio (marketer / RevOps front-end):** Freshsales Suite is explicitly framed as "CRM plus Marketing Automation" with journey builders, segmentation, landing pages, and multichannel campaigns, which touches the same marketer / RevOps surface as GTM Studio (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/suite/).
  - **Chorus / ZoomInfo Chat:** Freshsales includes built-in website chat, AI lead-generation bots, and visitor intent tracking — adjacent to ZoomInfo's chat / engagement surfaces but not equivalent to conversation-intelligence recording (source: https://www.freshworks.com/crm/features/).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1)** (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ verified phones, 200M+ verified emails, 30K+ technographics) — Freshsales does not sell B2B contact / company data.
  - **GTM Context Graph** — no equivalent intelligence layer that fuses third-party intent + first-party CRM + conversations; Freddy AI works on the customer's own first-party CRM data.
  - **APIs & MCP** — Freshsales offers REST APIs and webhooks for CRM data movement, but no MCP server or agent-builder distribution model.
  - **ZoomInfo Operations** — no separate CRM data-quality / orchestration product (auto-assignment and territory management are bundled inside the Pro tier).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The closest signal is Freshworks publishing a ZoomInfo Sales app in its marketplace, framing ZoomInfo as a data provider that enriches Freshsales rather than as a competitor.

  - Lalamove: 24% increase in sales conversions, 58% improvement in customer engagement

  - Humn: 10X more customers acquired; admin task time dropped 20%
