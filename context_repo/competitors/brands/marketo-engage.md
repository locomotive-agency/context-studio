# Marketo Engage

> **Edge case — Adobe-owned:** Marketo Engage is wholly owned by Adobe (NASDAQ: ADBE), acquired from Vista Equity Partners for $4.75B in 2018. It sits inside Adobe Experience Cloud alongside Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer B2B Edition, and Workfront — competitive positioning should treat this as Adobe's full-stack B2B marketing play, not a standalone vendor.

## Summary

Marketo Engage is Adobe's B2B marketing automation platform sold to mid-market and enterprise demand-gen, marketing ops, and ABM teams as a campaign-execution hub for email, web, mobile, chat, webinars, and ad-network audience activation. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio (campaign orchestration, ABM, lead scoring, sales-marketing alignment) and with ZoomInfo Chat via Adobe Dynamic Chat, while Sales Insight surfaces buyer-engagement signals inside the CRM in territory adjacent to ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace. Marketo does NOT compete on ZoomInfo's third-party B2B data foundation or GTM Context Graph — in fact it lists ZoomInfo as a profile-enrichment integration partner (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/integrations.html). Pricing is fully gated across all four packages (Growth, Select, Prime, Ultimate).

## Product Offerings

### Marketo Engage core platform

> **Wiki:** [[marketo-engage--core-platform]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered B2B marketing automation platform for planning, executing, and measuring omnichannel campaigns — from batch email to automated nurture to multi-step ABM campaigns.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-step campaign canvas with marketer-defined rules and triggers
  - Lead and account scoring based on demographic, firmographic, and behavioral data
  - Bi-directional native CRM sync (Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, Veeva)
  - Engagement Programs with Streams to nurture leads (up to 25 Streams per program)
  - Workspaces & Partitions for multi-team data segregation
  - Custom Objects/Fields, Sandbox, Smart Lists, segmentation
- **Source URL:** https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo.html

### AI & Agentic Capabilities

> **Wiki:** [[marketo-engage--ai-agentic]]

- **What it does:** AI features inside Marketo Engage to automate marketing operations, generate content, optimize journeys, and assist users — many features marked "Coming soon" on the feature page.
- **Key features:**
  - Lead list import enrichment to flag duplicates and standardize values (coming soon)
  - Program validation against best practices and naming conventions (coming soon)
  - Smart List creation from natural-language prompts (coming soon)
  - Generative AI authoring for email copy, subject lines, and Firefly-powered images
  - Image-to-HTML converter for emails
  - AI Assistant for product knowledge — in-product Q&A grounded in Adobe documentation (coming soon)
  - Journey creation and optimization via agentic AI (coming soon)
- **Source URL:** https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/agentic-ai.html

### Sales Intelligence & Engagement (Sales Insight)

> **Wiki:** [[marketo-engage--sales-insight]]

- **What it does:** Surfaces buyer engagement data inside the CRM so sales reps can prioritize leads and have timely conversations.
- **Key features:**
  - Lead scoring with cold-lead indicators (negative points for unsubscribes, non-engagement)
  - Smart grid timeline of lead/contact/account/opportunity activity
  - Account and opportunity "best bets" watch lists
  - Lead feed with instant email/mobile notifications
  - Insights dashboard inside CRM (Dynamic Chat insights, interesting moments)
  - Sales Insight Actions for direct seller engagement
  - Email alert tokens triggered by defined activities
- **Source URL:** https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/sales-intelligence-engagement.html

### Omnichannel Engagement

> **Wiki:** [[marketo-engage--omnichannel-engagement]]

- **What it does:** Centralized hub to orchestrate cross-channel campaigns across email, web, mobile, chat, events, webinars, ads, and social.
- **Key features:**
  - Drag-and-drop email designer with generative AI authoring
  - Interactive Webinars (native, powered by Adobe Connect) with polling, surveys, Q&A
  - Third-party webinar integrations (Zoom, ON24, GoToWebinar, Cvent)
  - Landing pages and forms with personalization based on Marketo data
  - SMS text messaging and mobile push (iOS/Android SDK)
  - Display ad audience activation to Google, LinkedIn, Facebook, X
  - Social channel integration (Facebook, LinkedIn, X)
- **Source URL:** https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/omnichannel-engagement.html

### Adobe Dynamic Chat

> **Wiki:** [[marketo-engage--dynamic-chat]]

- **What it does:** Generative AI-powered conversational marketing tool embedded in Marketo Engage with a natural-language interface trained on the customer's brand and products.
- **Key features:**
  - Two-way chat between visitors and live sales agents
  - AI-generated conversation summaries and assisted responses for sales agents
  - Self-service / self-qualification flow for buyers
  - Engaged Conversations limits: 10K/mo Standard, 100K/mo Prime
- **Source URL:** https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/dynamic-chat.html

### Marketo Measure

> **Wiki:** [[marketo-engage--measure]]

- **What it does:** Multi-touch attribution add-on that measures the impact of campaigns, content, and channels on pipeline and revenue.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-touch attribution across campaigns, content, channels
  - Granular reporting by channel and pipeline stage
  - Available as add-on; included in Ultimate package; tiered Standard / Performance / Performance Plus
- **Source URL:** https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/marketo-measure.html

### Adobe Experience Platform Agent Orchestrator (cross-Adobe)

> **Wiki:** [[marketo-engage--agent-orchestrator]]

- **What it does:** Adobe's AI agent orchestration layer powering agents across Experience Platform, including Marketo Engage workflows for planning, audiences, content, journeys, experience management, and data/insights.
- **Key features:**
  - Conversational AI Assistant interface for prompting agents
  - Reasoning engine that interprets prompts and plans steps
  - Knowledge base providing context for role-specific prompts
  - Agent Composer for monitoring and customizing agents
  - Coordinates Adobe agents (Brand Concierge, LLM Optimizer, Journey Optimizer Experimentation Accelerator, Experience Manager Sites Optimizer)
  - Adobe Marketing Agent for ChatGPT Enterprise
- **Source URL:** https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-platform/agent-orchestrator.html

- Deep integration with Adobe Experience Cloud (Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer B2B Edition, Workfront, Experience Manager, Analytics, Target) — Marketo is part of Adobe's full CX stack rather than a standalone tool (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/integrations.html)
- Native Interactive Webinars built on Adobe Connect — webinar platform inside the marketing automation tool with no extra integration required (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/omnichannel-engagement.html)
- Named a Leader in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for B2B Marketing Automation Platforms (and 2024) (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo.html)
- Adobe Marketing Agent for ChatGPT Enterprise — Marketo workflows accessible through ChatGPT via Agent Orchestrator (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-platform/agent-orchestrator.html)
- Deep enterprise customization: Workspaces, Partitions, Sandbox, Custom Objects (50M-250M records depending on tier), Performance Plus throughput up to 15x Standard (source: https://helpx.adobe.com/legal/product-descriptions/adobe-marketo-engage---product-description.html)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio** ↔ Marketo Engage core platform + Sales Insight + ABM (Target Account Management add-on). Marketo handles ABM, lead nurturing, multi-touch attribution, and sales-marketing alignment — the same surface ZoomInfo Marketing/GTM Studio targets for marketers, RevOps, and GTM engineers (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo.html).
  - **ZoomInfo Chat** ↔ Adobe Dynamic Chat. Generative-AI conversational marketing tool with self-qualification flows and live agent handoff (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/dynamic-chat.html).
  - **GTM Workspace** ↔ Sales Insight (Smart grid, lead feed, instant notifications, Sales Insight Actions inside CRM). Surfaces buyer engagement, lead scoring, account watch lists, and triggered alerts inside the CRM (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/sales-intelligence-engagement.html).
  - **APIs & MCP** ↔ Marketo Engage REST API + webhooks; Adobe Experience Platform Agent Orchestrator (Adobe Marketing Agent for ChatGPT Enterprise). Agent-builder / external-agent ecosystem support (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/experience-platform/agent-orchestrator.html).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations** — Marketo exposes data normalization, deduplication, and lead-list import enrichment (largely "coming soon" AI features) plus Workspaces/Partitions, but does not provide ZoomInfo's 500M-contact / 100M-company third-party data foundation for CRM enrichment (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/agentic-ai.html).
  - **Chorus** — Marketo integrates with Gong and ships AI-summary capability for Interactive Webinars recordings, but does not own a conversation-intelligence engine; touches the space via partnership rather than direct product (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/integrations.html).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1)** (500M contacts, 100M companies, 200M+ verified emails, 135M+ phone numbers, technographics) — Marketo Engage is a marketing-execution platform and does not sell a third-party B2B contact/company database. Marketo lists ZoomInfo as a profile-enrichment integration partner (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/integrations.html).
  - **GTM Context Graph** — Marketo's data layer is the customer's own first-party engagement and CRM data; not a fused third-party-plus-first-party intelligence graph that explains why deals move (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo.html).
  - **Intent Data and technographics** — no native intent or technographic data layer in Marketo; relies on integrations (e.g., 6sense, Demandbase, Bombora via partner ecosystem) for intent signals (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/integrations.html).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The G2 head-to-head page (https://www.g2.com/compare/adobe-marketo-engage-vs-zoominfo-marketing) returned an HTTP 403 block; Adobe does not maintain a Marketo-vs-ZoomInfo page on business.adobe.com. Adobe's app exchange instead lists ZoomInfo as an enrichment integration ("Up-level your Marketo Engage database with ZoomInfo"), framing the relationship as complementary rather than competitive.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Marketo Engage is a complete AI-powered marketing automation platform that helps teams scale personalized buyer engagement and grow predictable pipeline and revenue." Attract the right buyers, nurture them across channels and journeys, keep sales and marketing in sync, and deliver insights that maximize marketing impact on revenue (source: https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo.html).
- **Recurring proof points:** BNY Mellon — reduced time per email campaign by 37%; Alma — $4M in additional revenue; PriceFx — increased revenue 28x and pipeline 24x; Oppenheimer — reduced campaign costs by 400% per prospect; Qualcomm — improved lead quality and scoring by 40%; Leader in 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for B2B Marketing Automation Platforms.
- **Tone / category framing:** B2B marketing automation platform / AI-powered marketing automation; positioned within Adobe Experience Cloud as the campaign-management product alongside Journey Optimizer (B2C) and Journey Optimizer B2B Edition (buying groups). Heavy "AI & agentic" surface on the homepage with many features still marked "Coming soon" on dedicated feature pages.
