## Summary

Informatica Data Quality is an enterprise data quality, observability, and cleansing service inside the Informatica Intelligent Data Management Cloud (IDMC), now owned by Salesforce after the $8B acquisition closed on November 18, 2025 (source: https://www.salesforce.com/news/press-releases/2025/11/18/salesforce-completes-acquisition-of-informatica/). Its primary buyers are Chief Data Officers, IT, Data Architects, and Application Owners across regulated enterprises — a tangential overlap with ZoomInfo, where head-to-head competition exists only between Informatica Cloud Data Quality and ZoomInfo Operations on CRM B2B record cleansing, deduplication, and standardization. Informatica also partners with ZoomInfo via a published ZoomInfo B2B Data Connector in the Informatica Marketplace, indicating an enrichment-relationship rather than a substitution-relationship for ZoomInfo's Data foundation. Informatica sells none of ZoomInfo's seller-facing GTM Workspace, prospecting, or conversation intelligence products.

## Product Offerings

### Data Quality and Observability

> **Wiki:** [[informatica-data-quality--data-quality]]

- **What it does:** Profiles, cleanses, standardizes, deduplicates, and observes enterprise data so AI and business applications can operate on trusted, fit-for-purpose data.
- **Key features:**
  - Automated, scalable data profiling for continual analysis and problem detection
  - Data cleansing, standardization, and address verification
  - Prebuilt AI-powered rules and accelerators that auto-generate common data quality rules across virtually any source
  - Data observability across data, pipelines, and business lenses
  - Named a Leader for the 17th time in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Augmented Data Quality Solutions
- **Source URL:** https://www.informatica.com/products/data-quality.html

### Cloud Data Quality (CDQ)

> **Wiki:** [[informatica-data-quality--cloud-data-quality]]

- **What it does:** Cloud-native data management service that quickly identifies, fixes, and monitors data quality problems in business applications using a no-code visual environment.
- **Key features:**
  - Profiling wizard experience with intelligent insights
  - Automated issue identification and rule generation
  - Elastic, high-performance trusted data pipelines
  - Iterative data discovery and observability
  - Rich set of transformations — cleansing, standardization, verification, deduplication
  - AI-driven insights and anomaly detection
  - No-code visual environment with free trial
- **Source URL:** https://www.informatica.com/products/data-quality/cloud-data-quality-radar.html

### CLAIRE AI

> **Wiki:** [[informatica-data-quality--claire-ai]]

- **What it does:** Informatica's metadata-driven AI engine that powers automation, conversational data access, and autonomous agents across IDMC — the underlying intelligence behind Data Quality automation.
- **Key features:**
  - CLAIRE Agents — autonomous agents that discover data, build pipelines, and proactively fix data quality issues
  - CLAIRE GPT — natural-language conversational interface for self-service data discovery, analysis, and execution
  - CLAIRE Copilot — in-workflow AI guidance for data professionals
  - Available at no additional cost to eligible IDMC customers through January 31, 2027
  - Forrester TEI: $63.6M total financial impact over five years; 70% faster decision-making; 50% lower data security risk
- **Source URL:** https://www.informatica.com/platform/claire-ai.html

### Agentic Data Management (use case)

> **Wiki:** [[informatica-data-quality--agentic-data-management]]

- **What it does:** Positions IDMC + CLAIRE as the trusted-data foundation for enterprise AI agents, including agent engineering, orchestration, and governance.
- **Key features:**
  - Trusted, governed data for AI agents
  - AI and CLAIRE agents for automating data operations
  - AI Agent Engineering — build, connect, orchestrate, and manage custom AI agents
  - Multi-agent workflow orchestration
  - Governed-data trust for third-party agents
- **Source URL:** https://www.informatica.com/use-cases/agentic-data-management.html

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Informatica Cloud Data Quality vs ZoomInfo Operations — both profile, cleanse, standardize, deduplicate, and verify CRM records; ZoomInfo's own Pipeline blog lists Informatica Data Quality among "Top 10 Data Cleansing Tools for B2B Teams in 2026" and the two products are repeatedly compared on G2, TrustRadius, PeerSpot, SoftwareReviews, and TopAdvisor (source: https://www.informatica.com/products/data-quality/cloud-data-quality-radar.html).
- **Adjacent overlap:** (1) Informatica's Marketplace lists a ZoomInfo B2B Data Connector positioned as data-enrichment for Informatica-resolved customer records — a partnership rather than a head-to-head substitute for ZoomInfo's Data (Pillar 1) foundation (source: https://marketplace.informatica.com/listings/cloud/connectors/zoominfo-b2b-data-connector.html). (2) CLAIRE AI / Agentic Data Management and ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph share a "trusted enterprise context for AI" narrative, but Informatica is metadata-driven across all enterprise data while ZoomInfo's Context Graph is GTM-specific and fuses third-party B2B data with first-party CRM/conversation/behavioral signals (source: https://www.informatica.com/use-cases/agentic-data-management.html).
- **No overlap:** Informatica's broader IDMC stack — MDM & 360, Data Catalog, Data Governance & Privacy, Data Marketplace, API & App Integration, AI Agent Engineering — has no ZoomInfo counterpart. Informatica's industry/vertical use cases (Cloud Modernization, Regulatory Compliance & ESG, Supply Chain Optimization, Higher Education, Healthcare, Government, Manufacturing, Energy & Utilities) and CDO/IT-buyer focus sit outside ZoomInfo's GTM-team buyer base. Informatica also sells nothing in ZoomInfo's seller-workflow space — no prospecting, no dialer, no conversation intelligence, no AI-drafted outreach, no GTM Workspace equivalent (source: https://www.informatica.com/products/data-quality.html).
