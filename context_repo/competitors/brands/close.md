## Summary

Close is an SMB / inside-sales CRM with built-in calling, email, SMS, and an embedded AI sales agent (Chloe), targeted at founders and sales teams under ~100 reps. It overlaps with ZoomInfo only in narrow seller-execution territory: the Chloe + native communication stack touches GTM Workspace's "front-end for sellers" and Chorus's basic conversation-intelligence value, while ZoomInfo Operations is partially redundant for SMBs that adopt Close's native routing/dedupe. Close has no third-party B2B data foundation, no GTM Context Graph equivalent, and no Marketing/ABM/RevOps modules — so it is structurally complementary, not competitive, with ZoomInfo's core platform.

## Product Offerings

### Close CRM (core platform)

> **Wiki:** [[close--crm]]

- **What it does:** All-in-one sales CRM that combines lead/contact management, multi-channel communication, automation, and reporting in a single tool aimed at small and scaling sales teams.
- **Key features:**
  - Lead and contact management with custom fields, custom activities, custom objects, and custom pipeline views
  - Multi-pipeline opportunity tracking with Pipeline Guidance for stalled deals
  - Smart Views (dynamic, saved, shareable lead lists)
  - Built-in forms (no-code lead capture with field mapping and spam protection)
  - Centralized multi-channel inbox (calls, emails, SMS, tasks)
  - Built-in reporting and dashboards (activity, opportunity funnels, sent email, email sequence)
  - REST API, webhooks, and event log API access on all paid tiers
- **Source URL:** https://close.com/crm

### Communication (Power Dialer & Calling, Email, SMS)

> **Wiki:** [[close--communication]]

- **What it does:** Native voice, email, and SMS layered into the CRM — calls, sequences, and texts run inside Close without third-party dialers or email plugins.
- **Key features:**
  - Built-in VoIP calling and global numbers in 200+ countries (port-in supported)
  - One-Click Calling logged automatically to the lead
  - Power Dialer for sequential outbound calling
  - Predictive Dialer (multi-line, on Scale plan) connecting reps only on answer
  - Two-way email sync with bulk and individual sending and AI Email Assistant
  - Two-way SMS plus automated SMS steps inside Workflows
  - WhatsApp integration and call recording (unlimited on Scale)
- **Source URL:** https://close.com/communication

### Chloe (AI sales agent)

> **Wiki:** [[close--chloe]]

- **What it does:** Embedded AI sales agent inside Close that calls inbound leads in seconds, runs structured discovery, books meetings, and logs the entire interaction back into the CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Auto-calls every inbound lead in seconds and runs natural-language qualification
  - Books meetings or routes qualified leads to a live rep with full context
  - Sends follow-ups based on call transcripts; updates deal stage automatically
  - Generates AI summaries with full transcripts, action items, and next steps
  - Re-engages cold leads sitting in the CRM and surfaces warm responses to reps
  - Enriches contact fields from LinkedIn and the public web
  - Free during early Beta; only telephony usage fees billed
- **Source URL:** https://close.com/chloe

### Sales Automation / Workflows

> **Wiki:** [[close--workflows]]

- **What it does:** Multi-channel automated sequences and triggered workflows for follow-up, qualification, task creation, and lead routing.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-channel Workflows (email + SMS + tasks) with templates, timing, and triggers
  - Automated follow-ups, qualifying sequences, task creation, and lead routing
  - AI Email Assistant on Growth and Scale tiers
- **Source URL:** https://close.com/pricing

### Reporting & Coaching

> **Wiki:** [[close--reporting-coaching]]

- **What it does:** Sales dashboards, leaderboards, and call coaching for individual and team performance.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time dashboards and pipeline tracking
  - Activity-based reporting and opportunity funnel reports
  - Call recording and call coaching (unlimited recording on Scale)
  - Email sequence reporting and sent-email reporting
- **Source URL:** https://close.com/

### Developer Platform & MCP

> **Wiki:** [[close--developer-mcp]]

- **What it does:** REST API, webhooks, and an MCP server for connecting Close data to AI agents and external tools.
- **Key features:**
  - REST API + webhooks (developer.close.com)
  - Close MCP server for compatible AI models / agents
  - Native integrations with ChatGPT and Claude
- **Source URL:** https://close.com/integrations

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Not industry-restricted on the homepage; customer logos span e-commerce (Reverb, Natural Dog Company, SimpleTire), education (Flatiron School, ClassDojo), security (Paladin Security), HR/services (Same Page HR, HR Covered), and tour/booking SaaS (FareHarbor, Hipcamp) (source: https://close.com/customers).
- **Company size called out:** Solo founders and individual sellers (Solo plan); small businesses and startups; scaling sales teams under ~100 reps — Close's own /compare page states "For sales teams of <100 people" (source: https://close.com/compare).
- **Persona / role focus:** Founders and co-founders running early sales motion; inside-sales reps and SDRs; sales leaders / VPs of Revenue at SMBs; customer success and account management teams (Sisu, OutboundSync, Sales Transformation Group case studies cite CS use).
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Inside sales (high-velocity calling and follow-up)
  - Outbound sales sequences (multi-channel workflows)
  - Inbound lead qualification (now powered by Chloe)
  - Tool consolidation — replacing Salesforce/HubSpot/Pipedrive plus separate dialer/email tools
  - Renewal management and re-engagement of cold leads

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- All-in-one CRM with calling, email, SMS, forms, and meetings built natively — "no integrations" required for core communication (source: https://close.com/communication).
- Chloe AI sales agent lives inside the CRM (not a bolted-on voice tool) and works with Close data from day one with no integration sync (source: https://close.com/chloe).
- Transparent self-serve pricing starting at $9/mo (Solo) with no contracts — explicitly contrasted on /compare against Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, Salesloft "sales call required, complex pricing with hidden costs" (source: https://close.com/compare).
- Free customized onboarding, free migrations, and dedicated customer success on all paid plans (source: https://close.com/compare).
- Explicitly positions itself for sales teams under 100 reps; notes it is NOT designed for outside-sales mapping/routing or marketing/landing-page features (source: https://close.com/compare).
- Public Close MCP server plus ChatGPT and Claude integrations — unusual ecosystem depth for an SMB CRM (source: https://close.com/integrations).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No direct overlap with any ZoomInfo product line._ Close is a CRM-of-record with execution tooling; ZoomInfo does not sell a CRM. Their offerings sit on opposite sides of the workflow.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end):** Close + Chloe is the seller front-end for SMB inside-sales teams — pipeline, calling, email/SMS, and an embedded AI agent that drafts outreach and books meetings. Buyers comparing "all-in-one selling surfaces" will see Close as a low-end alternative to GTM Workspace, even though Close is structurally a CRM-of-record (source: https://close.com/chloe).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence):** Close generates AI summaries of every call (transcripts, action items, key moments) and offers unlimited call recording on Scale, duplicating the basic value of Chorus for inside-sales conversations — though without Chorus's deal-coaching/forecasting depth (source: https://close.com/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing):** Close handles routing, duplicate-merge, and field enrichment as native CRM features. For SMBs choosing Close, ZoomInfo Operations becomes redundant for that segment (source: https://close.com/crm).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1) (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ phone numbers, 200M+ emails, technographics):** Close has no third-party B2B contact database, intent data, or technographics. Lead enrichment via Chloe pulls "from LinkedIn and the web" on a per-lead basis — not a standing data asset. Close customers still need a data provider (ZoomInfo, Apollo, Clay) to source leads (source: https://close.com/).
  - **GTM Context Graph:** No equivalent intelligence/context layer. Reporting is operational (activity, funnels, email sequences) — not a "why deals move" reasoning system across CRM + intent + behavioral signals (source: https://close.com/pricing).
  - **GTM Studio / ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM):** Close explicitly states on /compare it is "not the best-suited for organizations needing built-in proposal generators, product/inventory management, or marketing features like landing pages or forms"; no ABM, intent-data orchestration, or display advertising functionality is referenced anywhere (source: https://close.com/compare).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Close's own /compare hub lists CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Copper, HighLevel) and sales engagement (Outreach, Apollo, Salesloft) but does NOT list ZoomInfo. The Close-vs-ZoomInfo G2 page returned a 403 to both Jina and the curl_cffi fallback. See flags.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The Sales CRM That Does the Work For You" — a CRM that doesn't just store data, it acts on it via built-in calling/email/SMS and the Chloe AI sales agent. Hero copy: "This CRM calls your leads for you" (source: https://close.com/).
- **Recurring proof points:**
  - 10,000+ customers
  - 2B+ sales interactions
  - 4.8 G2 rating
  - "50% increase in booked appointments per week" (FollowUp.com); "$100,000+ saved in tech costs switching from Salesforce" (Sisu); "5x ROI / 80% reduction in CRM costs" (Hownd)
  - "9 out of 10 new users start using Close to place calls, send emails + texts, or host a meeting within their first week"
  - Public customer list: FareHarbor, Hipcamp, WorkOS, Flatiron School, Reverb, SimpleTire, Paladin Security, Natural Dog Company, ClassDojo
- **Tone / category framing:** "Sales CRM" built specifically for small and scaling sales teams — explicitly positioned as a "system of action" (vs Salesforce/HubSpot framed as "systems of record") with all sales-execution tools native, not integrated.
