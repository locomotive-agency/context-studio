## Summary

Klue is a competitive enablement platform that combines competitive intelligence and win-loss analysis into one product, sold primarily to product marketing, competitive intelligence, and sales enablement leaders at mid-market and enterprise B2B companies. Its core surface is "Compete Agent," an AI operative that auto-generates competitor profiles, distributes Deal Tips inside Slack/Salesforce, and ties battlecard adoption to revenue impact — overlapping ZoomInfo only at the Chorus/GTM Workspace seller-surface layer where competitor-mentions on calls trigger in-deal coaching. Klue does not pitch itself as a B2B contact-data substitute for ZoomInfo Sales, and a TrustRadius comparison places the two products in different categories rather than as direct alternatives. The most material crossover is at the conversation-intelligence-to-battlecard pipeline: Klue ingests Gong/Chorus calls and pushes recommendations to sellers, the same workflow ZoomInfo positions Chorus + Workspace to own end-to-end.

## Product Offerings

### Klue Compete (Competitive Intelligence) / Compete Agent

> **Wiki:** [[klue--compete-agent]]

- **What it does:** Automatically collects, curates, and shares competitor intel and delivers AI-driven deal-based competitive insights to sellers in the tools they already use (source: https://klue.com/compete-agent).
- **Key features:**
  - Compete Agent — AI "Research Analyst" and "Deal Assistant" that auto-generates competitor profiles, refreshes intel every 24 hours, and proactively sends Deal Tips to sellers
  - Knowledge Hub — uploads internal docs, GTM, and product context to drive consistent messaging across the compete program
  - Dynamic battlecards for Tier 1 competitors, with Klue Reports for adoption and revenue-influence tracking
  - Ask Klue — instant, sourced answers to competitive questions inside Slack, Salesforce, and Klue
  - Competitor tracking across websites, pricing, product changes, news, reviews, plus Klue Insights from G2 reviews and win-loss interviews
  - Browser extensions (Chrome, Edge), iOS/Android apps, and email digests for distribution
  - Auto Insights — auto-generates competitive content that fuels research, supports sellers in live deals, and serves as a trusted source for an internal LLM
- **Source URL:** https://klue.com/compete-agent ; https://klue.com/competitive-intelligence-software ; https://klue.com/ai

### Klue Win-Loss

> **Wiki:** [[klue--win-loss]]

- **What it does:** Captures and analyzes buyer and seller feedback across deals (via AI interviews, live expert interviews, or imported interviews) to surface why deals were won or lost and feeds that intel back into battlecards, Deal Tips, and Ask Klue (source: https://klue.com/win-loss).
- **Key features:**
  - AI Interviewer — short async conversations for seller deal debriefs and field pulse
  - Live Expert Interviews conducted by Klue's interviewers, analysts, and writers for strategic deals
  - "Bring Your Own Interviews" — auto-analyzes interviews captured in any other tool
  - Auto AI Analysis — organizes every interview into summaries, win/loss themes, competitive mentions, buyer quotes, and executive summaries
  - Insight categories across the customer journey: buyer motivations, evaluation criteria, competitive drivers, sales experience, product fit, retention signals
  - Win-Loss insights flow automatically into battlecards, Deal Tips, and Ask Klue (consolidated CI + Win-Loss platform claim)
- **Source URL:** https://klue.com/win-loss

### Klue Dynamic Battlecards

> **Wiki:** [[klue--battlecards]]

- **What it does:** Authoring + distribution surface for competitor-specific battlecards used by sellers in live deals; embedded in Slack, Salesforce, Highspot, Seismic, Showpad, and browser extensions.
- **Key features:**
  - Dynamic battlecards for Tier 1 competitors with Knowledge Hub-driven consistent messaging
  - Klue Reports for battlecard adoption and revenue-influence tracking
  - Embedded distribution into Slack, Salesforce, Microsoft Teams, Highspot, Seismic, Showpad, SharePoint
  - Browser extensions (Chrome, Edge), iOS / Android apps, email digests
- **Source URL:** https://klue.com/competitive-intelligence-software

### Ask Klue (in-deal AI assistant)

> **Wiki:** [[klue--ask-klue]]

- **What it does:** Provides instant, sourced answers to seller competitive questions inside Slack, Salesforce, and Klue — used live during competitive deals.
- **Key features:**
  - Instant sourced answers to competitive questions
  - Embedded in Slack, Salesforce, and the Klue app
  - Powered by Klue's Knowledge Hub and Auto Insights
  - Tied to Deal Tips so sellers get proactive recommendations against competitor mentions
- **Source URL:** https://klue.com/compete-agent

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Klue overlaps ZoomInfo at the Chorus + GTM Workspace seller-surface layer. Klue analyzes Gong/Chorus call recordings, detects competitor mentions, and proactively pushes Deal Tips and Ask Klue answers into Salesforce/Slack — the same in-deal coaching motion ZoomInfo positions Chorus + GTM Workspace battlecards to fill (source: https://klue.com/compete-agent).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Klue's "one-platform" intelligence layer that ingests calls, CRM, G2 reviews, and win-loss interviews to drive deal-level recommendations is conceptually adjacent to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph reasoning across CRM + conversations + intent + behavioral signals — but Klue's scope is competitor-only, not the full GTM context (source: https://klue.com/ai). Klue Reports also tie CI engagement to pipeline and revenue, overlapping how ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio reports on play and program influence (source: https://klue.com/compete-agent).
- **No overlap:** Klue sells a structured Win-Loss interview program (AI interviewer + live expert interviewers + bring-your-own interviews) as a managed service that ZoomInfo does not offer (source: https://klue.com/win-loss). Klue's core authoring surface — Knowledge Hub, dynamic battlecards, competitor profiles — is built for PMM/CI leads as the economic buyer; ZoomInfo does not market a dedicated PMM battlecard authoring product (source: https://klue.com/competitive-intelligence-software). Conversely, Klue does not sell B2B contact data, intent data, ABM orchestration, prospecting workflows, or developer APIs/MCP — the bulk of ZoomInfo's product surface area.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No public Klue-vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced on klue.com. The closest public head-to-head is TrustRadius, which places the two products in different categories: Klue is summarized as "competitive intelligence software designed to help marketing and enablement teams collect, curate, and deliver intel to enterprise sales teams," while ZoomInfo Sales is summarized as "a modern go-to-market platform for B2B companies" used to find buyers based on ICP and contact data; aggregate scores list Klue at 6.6/10 (16 reviews) vs ZoomInfo Sales at 8.2/10 (1,868 reviews) (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/klue-vs-zoominfo-sales).
