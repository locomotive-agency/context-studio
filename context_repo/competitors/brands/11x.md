# 11x

**G2:** ⭐ 4.8 / 5 — 65 reviews  <!-- g2-injector -->

- [[11x--platform|11x Platform]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

11x sells "autonomous AI digital workers" for sales, RevOps, and GTM teams — primarily Alice (an AI SDR that runs end-to-end outbound) and Julian (an AI voice agent that answers and qualifies inbound calls). Its primary buyers are revenue leaders at growth-stage and enterprise companies whose pipeline targets are outpacing SDR headcount. The most material crossover with ZoomInfo is direct: Alice substitutes for the AI SDR layer and the AI-drafted-outreach + execution surface that GTM Workspace anchors, while 11x's "Real-Time Company and Lead Database" is positioned as a replacement for the contact-data layer ZoomInfo's data foundation provides. Where 11x does not compete is the breadth of ZoomInfo's data (500M contacts, technographics, multi-source verification), the GTM Studio NL-audience builder, and the APIs/MCP agent-builder ecosystem.

## Product Offerings

### Alice — Outbound digital worker (AI SDR)

> **Wiki:** [[11x--alice]]

- **What it does:** Autonomous AI SDR that runs the entire outbound motion: prospecting from live signals, prospect research, multi-channel personalized outreach, follow-up, and continuous optimization (source: https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice).
- **Key features:**
  - Prospecting from live signals (job changes, funding rounds, tech-stack shifts, hiring patterns)
  - Per-prospect AI research and message writing tailored to role, company, and context
  - Multi-channel sequences across email, social, and phone with automated timing and follow-up
  - Continuous optimization on reply and meeting outcomes (not opens)
  - Built-in plays: outbound lead generation, lead nurture, lead reactivation, retargeting website visitors, competitive takeout, closed-lost, PLG conversion, event registration / follow-up / no-show
  - Proprietary deliverability engine and mailbox management
- **Source URL:** https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice

### Julian — Inbound digital worker (AI Phone Agent)

> **Wiki:** [[11x--julian]]

- **What it does:** AI voice agent that picks up inbound calls in real time, qualifies prospects on the conversation, and books meetings or transfers to a rep with full context (source: https://www.11x.ai/).
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time inbound call answering with no hold queues or voicemail
  - Conversational qualification and meeting booking on the call
  - Routing/transfer to the right rep with full context
  - Speed-to-lead in seconds (callback before the prospect closes the tab)
  - Use cases: speed-to-lead, inbound lead qualification, automated meeting scheduling, customer onboarding operations, customer expansion
- **Source URL:** https://www.11x.ai/

### 11x Platform — Find & Research / Engage & Convert / Measure & Scale

> **Wiki:** [[11x--platform]]

- **What it does:** Underlying platform powering Alice and Julian: data and lead generation, research/messaging, outreach/workflows, inbound handling, analytics/deliverability, and integrations (source: https://www.11x.ai/).
- **Key features:**
  - Real-Time Company and Lead Database with live web search and AI prospecting
  - Website Visitor Tracking and Signals & Triggers feeds
  - Deep Research, AI Personalization, Playbooks, Knowledge Base for messaging
  - Multi-Channel Sequences, Sequence Builder, Smart Replies, Multi-Lingual Outreach, Consented Outbound Calling
  - Intelligent Lead Routing and Inbound Lead Qualification
  - Analytics, Deliverability & Mailbox Health, Call Deliverability & Number Management
  - Native CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho), Slack, G2, and an open API
- **Source URL:** https://www.11x.ai/

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Native bi-directional Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and Zoho; other CRMs via API (source: https://www.11x.ai/platform/integrations/crm-integration).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Built-in multi-channel sequencer; Alice's "Before/After" diagram explicitly positions 11x as a replacement for standalone sales-engagement tools (source: https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice).
- **Data / enrichment:** Built-in Real-Time Company and Lead Database with live web search and AI prospecting; 11x positions this as their own data layer rather than relying on a third-party enrichment provider (source: https://www.11x.ai/).
- **Other notable integrations:** Slack, G2, open API for pull/push and trigger orchestration; calendar and telephony integrations referenced in product copy and the CRM-sync illustration (source: https://www.11x.ai/platform/integrations/crm-integration).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Positions Alice as a "digital worker" replacing an entire SDR role, not a tool an SDR uses — "Alice outperforms your best SDR at 11x the scale" (source: https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice).
- Consolidates contact data, sales engagement, intent, automation, research/personalization, and email warmup into one digital worker; the Alice page explicitly diagrams "Before Alice" stacks (Outreach, Clay, Jasper, Instantly, etc.) being replaced (source: https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice).
- Voice AI is a first-class channel via Julian — real-time inbound call answer + outbound calling, with proprietary call deliverability and number management (source: https://www.11x.ai/).
- Live-signal prospecting (job changes, funding, tech-stack shifts, hiring) instead of static contact databases (source: https://www.11x.ai/products/alice/outbound-lead-generation).
- Native bi-directional CRM sync without middleware; auto-creates CRM records when Alice or Julian discovers new contacts (source: https://www.11x.ai/platform/integrations/crm-integration).
- Quantified customer outcomes: 1.5x qualified meetings (Checkr), 700% ROI headline, $1M+ pipeline / 35% of pipeline within 3 months (canibuild, MMB Networks), $100M+ revenue generated for customers (source: https://www.11x.ai/case-study/checkr, https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **GTM Workspace AI SDR layer.** Alice "runs your entire outbound motion, from prospecting and personalization to follow-up" so reps "never touch a cold list" — a direct substitute for the AI-drafted-outreach + execution surface ZoomInfo positions in GTM Workspace (the AI-agent layer) (source: https://www.11x.ai/products/alice/outbound-lead-generation).
  - **Data foundation (contacts, companies, signals).** 11x markets a "Real-Time Company and Lead Database" plus "Live Web Search and AI Prospecting" filtering "millions of signals across 50+ data sources" — competing with the contact/company/signal layer ZoomInfo's Data (Pillar 1) provides (source: https://www.11x.ai/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations + ZoomInfo Chat (CRM data quality, routing, inbound capture).** Julian handles inbound calls and qualification; the CRM Integration page describes auto-creating contacts, bi-directional Salesforce/HubSpot sync, and intelligent lead routing — the same operational job ZoomInfo Operations and ZoomInfo Chat cover (source: https://www.11x.ai/platform/integrations/crm-integration).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence).** Julian writes "full transcripts, replies, call status, outcomes" back to the CRM — adjacent to Chorus's CI space, though 11x emphasises autonomous calling rather than coaching, scorecards, or forecasting (source: https://www.11x.ai/platform/integrations/crm-integration).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM, intent activation).** 11x's Marketing & Growth solution turns demand into pipeline via signal-based outbound — adjacent to ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM/intent activation, but without an ABM advertising surface (source: https://www.11x.ai/).
- **No overlap:**
  - APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem for third-party developers — 11x exposes a single integration API, not an MCP server or agent-builder marketplace.
  - GTM Studio's natural-language audience building and play orchestration for marketers/RevOps/GTM engineers — 11x has no equivalent NL audience-builder workspace; the end product is a digital worker, not a builder surface.
  - Conversation intelligence as coaching/forecasting (Chorus depth) — Julian transcripts are operational, not deal-coaching analytics.
  - The 20-year B2B data scale ZoomInfo describes (500M contacts, 100M companies, 30K+ technographics, 95% accuracy) — 11x's data layer is real-time aggregated and sized to feed Alice, not sold as a comparable contact/company database.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The URL https://www.11x.ai/sales-tools/zoominfo, which Google returned as a "Top 10 ZoomInfo Alternatives" snippet, currently redirects to the 11x homepage with identical content and no comparison material.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Digital workers, Human results" — autonomous AI digital workers that execute, learn, and optimise across every customer touchpoint so revenue teams "scale pipeline without scaling headcount" (source: https://www.11x.ai/, https://www.11x.ai/solutions/growth).
- **Recurring proof points:** $70M–$75M raised from a16z, Benchmark, 20VC, HubSpot Ventures and others; customer logos including Xerox, Checkr, Armanino, Rillet, Metaview, UST, Leica, Rho, HiddenLayer, Ouster, canibuild, Questex, and Goldman Sachs; 1.5x qualified meetings (Checkr), 700% ROI headline, $500K pipeline + 3.2x email reply rate (Checkr), $1M+ pipeline in first 3 months (canibuild), 35% of pipeline generated within 3 months (MMB Networks), $100M+ revenue generated for customers, SOC-2 Type 2 and CASA badges (source: https://www.11x.ai/, https://www.11x.ai/case-study/checkr, https://www.11x.ai/worker/alice).
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames the category as "autonomous AI digital workers" / "AI-native digital workforce." Explicitly contrasts with traditional sales tools and positions Alice as a replacement for the entire pre-existing GTM stack — contact data, sales engagement, intent, automation, research/personalization, and email warmup all consolidated into one worker.
