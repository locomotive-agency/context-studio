# IntentData.io

- [[intentdata-io--contact-level-intent|IntentData.io Contact-Level Intent Data]] — competes with Intent Data

## Summary

IntentData.io is a B2B contact-level buyer intent data provider targeting demand-gen marketers (CMOs, Directors of Demand Gen) and B2B sales leaders (VP Sales, BDRs/AEs). Its core pitch is contact-level signals — named individual + job title attached to each intent action — vs the account-only model used by most third-party providers. Direct overlap with ZoomInfo sits inside Pillar 1 (Data foundation): the intent layer (where ZoomInfo is the Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader) and technographics, with IntentData.io's "Contextual Technographic Data" competing against ZoomInfo's 30K+ technology coverage. It does not sell a contact/company database, a seller workspace, conversation intelligence, or APIs/MCP — it ships data into the buyer's Salesforce / Marketo / HubSpot / Terminus stack rather than offering its own surface.

## Product Offerings

### Contact-Level Intent Data

> **Wiki:** [[intentdata-io--contact-level-intent]]

- **What it does:** Delivers contact-level (named individual + job title) third-party buyer intent signals harvested from publicly available open-web content rather than account-only signals.
- **Key features:**
  - Named contact + job title attached to each intent signal (vs account-level only from typical third-party providers)
  - Signals across "Engaged with: Competitor", Industry News, Influencer, Events, Followed & Follower, and Key Related Contacts
  - Account-level signals: New Office, New Product, Recent Funding Event, Hiring by Role, Change of Key Position
  - Open-web sourcing positioned as GDPR- and ICO-compliant
  - "Streams" feature splits results into subsets routed to CRM, BDRs, CSV exports, or paid-social custom audiences
  - Configurable tracking of competitor engagement, target accounts, customers, influencers, and events as triggers
- **Source URL:** https://www.intentdata.io/

### Contextual Technographic Data (TechnoIntent)

> **Wiki:** [[intentdata-io--technointent]]

- **What it does:** Blends technographic signals (web tags + parsed job postings) with contact-level intent data to indicate not just which software a company runs but whether they are actively researching alternatives.
- **Key features:**
  - Combines tag-tracking with parsing of structured and unstructured job-posting data
  - Covers backend software beyond martech / salestech
  - Renewal Disruption play: flags accounts in research mode 9–10 months into competitor contracts
  - Knockout play: surfaces accounts engaging with content about a competitor's known weaknesses
  - Product Launch play: ICP-matched accounts with no current solution and active problem research
  - Market research output: penetration, churn rate, and persona engagement by geography and industry
- **Source URL:** https://www.intentdata.io/contextual-technographic-data-incorporates-intent-signals

### Prospect Data Integrations

> **Wiki:** [[intentdata-io--prospect-data]]

- **What it does:** Pushes contact-level prospect records and intent signals into CRMs and marketing-automation platforms via a native Salesforce integration plus form-push delivery to systems with integrated form functionality.
- **Key features:**
  - Native Salesforce integration
  - Form-push delivery into Marketo, HubSpot, and any CRM/MAP with integrated forms
  - Selective import controls (specify lead types in vs CSV download) to manage CRM contact costs
  - Process integrations with Terminus (ABM), LinkedIn, Twitter, Drift, Triblio, Predictable Revenue
  - Real-time delivery for BDR / AE / CS alerts
  - Activation coaching for best-practice integration with existing sales / marketing processes
- **Source URL:** https://www.intentdata.io/prospect-data

- Open-web sourcing positioned against bidstream / publishing-coop / IP-based providers; claims GDPR and ICO compliance (source: https://www.intentdata.io/prospect-data)
- Granular Key Terms (up to 100 in Enterprise) instead of broad "topics" as used by publishing-coop providers like Bombora (source: https://www.intentdata.io/intent-data-for-marketing)
- Contextual Technographic Data combining web tags with parsed job-posting signals to surface software in active disruption — covers backend software beyond martech/salestech (source: https://www.intentdata.io/contextual-technographic-data-incorporates-intent-signals)
- Streams feature lets buyers route different signal subsets to different destinations (CRM vs CSV vs BDR queue vs paid-social custom audience) (source: https://www.intentdata.io/buyer-intent-data-pricing)
- Sells configurable signal types per tier (Engaged with Competitor, Influencer, Events, Industry News, Followed & Follower, Key Related Contacts) rather than a black-box score (source: https://www.intentdata.io/buyer-intent-data-pricing)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Intent Data (Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader for Intent Data Providers) vs IntentData.io Contact-Level Intent Data.** Both sell third-party buyer intent signals to B2B sales and marketing teams; IntentData.io explicitly differentiates by claiming contact-level (named person + title) granularity vs account-only providers, putting it in direct competition with ZoomInfo's intent product within the same Forrester intent-provider category (source: https://www.intentdata.io/).
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation — technographics (30K+ technologies) vs IntentData.io Contextual Technographic Data (TechnoIntent).** TechnoIntent blends web-tag tracking with parsed job-posting signals to deliver technographic insight including backend software — a direct competitive offering inside the same data-foundation pillar (source: https://www.intentdata.io/contextual-technographic-data-incorporates-intent-signals).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (legacy ABM) and GTM Studio (orchestration) vs IntentData.io Prospect Data integrations + ABM/demand-gen activation services.** IntentData.io ships data into Salesforce / Marketo / HubSpot / Terminus and offers paid ABM-strategy / BDR-coaching add-ons. It does not sell its own ABM platform or seller workspace; it pushes data into the buyer's existing stack — adjacent to ZoomInfo Marketing/Studio rather than a substitute (source: https://www.intentdata.io/prospect-data).
  - **APIs & MCP vs IntentData.io CRM/MAP form-push and native Salesforce integration.** IntentData.io's distribution is integration-first into existing CRMs/MAPs; no public API or MCP server documented on the reviewed pages — architecturally distinct from ZoomInfo's API + MCP access lane (source: https://www.intentdata.io/prospect-data).
- **No overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo contact / company database (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ phones, 200M+ emails)** — IntentData.io does not sell a standalone B2B contact / company database; it positions itself as a signal provider attached to publicly observed individuals (source: https://www.intentdata.io/).
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end) and GTM Studio (marketer / RevOps front-end)** — IntentData.io has no proprietary seller or marketer workspace UI; data is consumed inside the buyer's existing CRM / MAP / ABM tools (source: https://www.intentdata.io/prospect-data).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) and ZoomInfo Chat** — no first-party conversation intelligence or website chat product; conversational-marketing use case explicitly requires a separate Drift subscription per the pricing page (source: https://www.intentdata.io/buyer-intent-data-pricing).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Vs-ZoomInfo content surfaced only via third-party aggregators (sourceforge.net, slashdot.org), not first-party IntentData.io comparison pages.
