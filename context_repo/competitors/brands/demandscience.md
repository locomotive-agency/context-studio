# DemandScience

## Sub-products

- [[demandscience--intent-graph|DemandScience IONIC Identity Graph + Intent Data]] — competes with Intent Data
- [[demandscience--labs-services|DemandScience Labs (Managed Demand Generation)]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing

## Summary

DemandScience is a B2B managed demand-generation services vendor — "Precision Performance Marketing as a Service" — selling intent data, an identity graph, and a Labs services team that runs ABM, content syndication, BANT lead-gen, programmatic ads, and managed email on the customer's behalf. Its primary buyer is the marketing/demand-gen leader (VP Marketing, Director of Demand Gen, Director of ABM), with marquee logos including Salesforce, Microsoft, Adobe, AWS, and Capgemini. The most striking ZoomInfo overlap is on the marketer-facing surface: DemandScience's Demand + Advertising + Labs offering competes directly with ZoomInfo's ABM and demand-gen orchestration, and its 247M-contact IONIC Identity Graph plus HG-Insights-powered Winnable Account System overlap with ZoomInfo's Data and Intent Data pillars. Crucially, DemandScience explicitly positions itself as complementary, not a replacement — its vs-ZoomInfo page is a partnership/"Better Together" pitch rather than a displacement pitch.

## Product Offerings

### Ionic — Intelligence & Orchestration Layer

- **What it does:** Software intelligence layer that filters signals for quality, connects data across channels, and uses small language models plus machine learning to recommend next-best actions across DemandScience programs.
- **Key features:**
  - Validated account and contact data with verified buyer signals
  - Custom small-language AI models with always-on machine learning
  - Cross-channel audience translation (one audience, every channel)
  - Expert-guided activation via the Labs team layered on top
  - Maintains the IONIC Identity Graph: 247M+ verified global contacts across 100+ countries
  - Underpins every other DemandScience program (Demand, Advertising, Data, Web, Outreach, Events, Studio)
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/solutions/ionic

### Labs — Managed Services Team

- **What it does:** Expert services team of strategists, campaign managers, and channel specialists that runs DemandScience campaigns end-to-end on the customer's behalf — no platform learning curve required.
- **Key features:**
  - End-to-end orchestration from persona definition to multi-channel activation
  - Real-time campaign adaptation as signals and performance shift
  - Hands-on execution with no new platforms or extra customer lift
  - Specialists across media, data, personalization, SEO, web optimization, and content
  - Faster speed-to-pipeline without internal tech ramp-up
  - Operational scale without adding customer headcount
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/solutions/labs

### Demand — Lead Generation Solutions

- **What it does:** Suite of lead-gen products (Content Syndication, HQLs, BANT) that delivers verified, opted-in leads aligned to the customer's ICP via multi-channel outreach.
- **Key features:**
  - Content Syndication — promote opted-in content to verified decision-makers
  - Highly Qualified Leads (HQLs) — sales-ready leads matching the customer ICP
  - BANT Leads — qualified by Budget, Authority, Need, and Timing before handoff
  - Multi-channel approach combining email, phone, and content distribution
  - Network access to 17M+ companies and 225M+ decision-makers
  - Verified, accurate contact data so teams don't waste cycles on bad leads
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/solutions/demand/

### Content Syndication

- **What it does:** Distributes customer-supplied content to verified decision-makers and delivers opted-in, fully compliant leads with guaranteed cost-per-lead pricing.
- **Key features:**
  - Access to 247M+ verified professionals in 100+ countries
  - Predictable, transparent guaranteed CPL — no surprises
  - GDPR-, CCPA-, and TCPA-compliant lead delivery
  - Multi-channel reach: owned media brands, targeted email, and phone outreach
  - Lead replacement guarantee if a lead doesn't meet customer criteria
  - Plug into existing workflows with no new tools required
  - Dedicated Customer Experience Manager per account
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/products/content-syndication

### Visitor ID (VID) Tag

- **What it does:** JavaScript tag deployed via tag manager that identifies which companies are visiting a customer's site and produces account-level behavioral intelligence.
- **Key features:**
  - Identifies industry, account, and firmographic data on anonymous site visitors
  - Segments visitors by TAL membership, ICP match, paid-campaign participation, and intent
  - Tracks onsite engagement, intent, and conversion performance per account
  - Single asynchronous JavaScript tag (no site performance impact)
  - GDPR / CCPA compliant; integrates with consent management tools
  - Pulls in DemandScience ad-platform segments and CRM data
  - Feeds insights directly into DemandScience-managed multi-channel programs
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/products/visitor-id

### Buyer Intent Data

- **What it does:** Multi-source intent feed designed to surface businesses already expressing interest in a topic so customers can engage purchase-ready prospects earlier.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-source intent combining engagement signals across channels
  - Real-time buying signals to prioritize active prospects
  - Topic-level early-engagement detection
  - New-market identification (uncovers prospect groups customers may have missed)
  - G2 badges: "Best Estimated ROI — Small Business", "Fastest Implementation", "Best Support — Mid-Market", "Easiest to Do Business With — Enterprise"
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/data/intent-data/

### Winnable Account System (WAS)

- **What it does:** Account-scoring framework powered by HG Insights and GTM Fabric that combines technographic intelligence, IT-spend data, competitive context, and TrustRadius buyer-community signals to score accounts for switchability/displaceability — going beyond topic-level intent.
- **Key features:**
  - Propensity-Based Audience Activation — scores accounts for conversion propensity using HG Insights technographic + IT-spend + competitive data
  - In-Market Buyer Activation — uses HG Insights TrustRadius signals to flag committees actively comparing vendors right now
  - Both products can run independently or combined as a dual-filter
  - Designed to identify which intent accounts are "truly winnable" vs merely "in-market"
  - Powered by HG Insights + GTM Fabric partnership (announced 2026)
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/

### Content-IQ (AI Visibility)

- **What it does:** Patented content architecture and AI visibility system that builds "machine-trustable authority networks" so customer brands surface in both traditional search results and LLM/AI responses.
- **Key features:**
  - U.S. Patent No. 11,468,139 (cited verbatim on the vs-ZoomInfo page)
  - Topic ecosystem mapping and content opportunity scoring
  - Optimizes content to be selected by AI systems and LLMs, not just Google
  - Sub-products: AI Visibility, Studio, Web Personalization (under the Content-IQ umbrella)
  - Positioned as a "visibility layer ZoomInfo has no equivalent for"
- **Source URL:** https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce (AppExchange listing "DemandScience Ads & Sales Experiences (ABM Insights)"); HubSpot (App Marketplace listing "DemandScience ABM").
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** No formal integration claim, but on the vs-ZoomInfo page DemandScience positions managed email as a top-of-funnel program that feeds INTO ZoomInfo Engage workflows rather than replacing them.
- **Data / enrichment:** HG Insights (powers Propensity-Based Audience Activation and In-Market Buyer Activation); TrustRadius buyer-community signals (via HG Insights); GTM Fabric (jointly powers the Winnable Account System); The Trade Desk (programmatic display delivery).
- **Other notable integrations:** LinkedIn Ads (managed); Meta Ads (managed); Connected TV / streaming audio (managed); standard tag managers (for VID deployment); consent management tools (for VID GDPR/CCPA compliance).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Sells managed services, not a self-serve platform — Labs runs campaigns on the customer's behalf so "you don't need another tool to manage" (source: https://demandscience.com/).
- Guaranteed CPL on Content Syndication with a lead-replacement promise if leads don't meet customer criteria (source: https://demandscience.com/products/content-syndication).
- Patented Content-IQ AI-visibility system (U.S. Patent No. 11,468,139) targeting both human SEO and LLM/AI selection (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
- Winnable Account System layers technographic + IT-spend + competitive context (via HG Insights) on top of intent — claims to identify "switchability," not just topic-level interest (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
- IONIC Identity Graph: 247M+ verified global contacts across 100+ countries, sourced and maintained independently (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
- Built-in GDPR / CCPA / TCPA compliance for every lead delivered, with explicit opt-in capture (source: https://demandscience.com/products/content-syndication).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio vs. DemandScience Demand + Advertising + Labs** — both are marketer-facing surfaces for ABM, intent-driven campaigns, content syndication, programmatic display, and managed email. DemandScience explicitly frames itself as the execution layer on top of intelligence (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
  - **Data foundation (intent + technographics + contacts) vs. DemandScience IONIC Identity Graph + Buyer Intent Data + HG-Insights-powered Winnable Account System** — DemandScience maintains its own 247M-contact graph and intent feed and supplements ZoomInfo data "to maximize match rates" (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations / website-visitor identification vs. DemandScience VID (Visitor ID) tag** — both products solve account-level identification of anonymous web traffic; DS even lists "Website visitor identification: matching anonymous traffic to accounts" as something ZoomInfo "does well," confirming the overlap (source: https://demandscience.com/products/visitor-id).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing programmatic ad targeting vs. DemandScience managed display, CTV, audio, LinkedIn, and Meta** — DS is a fully managed service running against ZoomInfo audiences via The Trade Desk, an adjacent execution layer rather than a self-serve substitute (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace AI agent layer intent surfacing vs. DemandScience BANT-Qualified Leads** — DS BANT confirms Budget / Authority / Need / Timing via outbound phone before handoff — a service layer that feeds ZoomInfo data customers human-qualified leads (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
- **No overlap:**
  - Patented AI-visibility / LLM-readability content architecture (Content-IQ, U.S. Patent No. 11,468,139) — DS explicitly states "ZoomInfo has no equivalent content visibility layer" (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
  - Done-for-you content production (Studio: creative, copy, design, translation) — content/asset creation is outside ZoomInfo's product scope (source: https://demandscience.com/).
  - Done-for-you events execution (executive roundtables, virtual summits, field events) — DS Events handles recruitment through logistics; not a ZoomInfo product line (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).
  - Pure pay-per-lead / CPL-guaranteed lead generation as a service — DS Content Syndication is sold on guaranteed CPL with a lead-replacement promise; ZoomInfo does not sell a CPL-guaranteed lead procurement model (source: https://demandscience.com/products/content-syndication).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Unusual for the ZoomInfo competitor set, DemandScience pitches a **"Better Together" partnership, not a displacement**. From the vs-ZoomInfo page: _"ZoomInfo gives you the intelligence: who's in market, who's at the right accounts, who fits your ICP. DemandScience takes that intelligence and executes the programs that turn it into pipeline — and adds two things ZoomInfo doesn't provide: Winnable Account targeting that identifies which accounts are actually switchable, and patented Content-IQ AI visibility so those accounts can find you through search and AI too."_ The FAQ on the same page asks "Does DemandScience replace ZoomInfo?" and answers _"No. These are complementary products serving different functions."_ DS reframes the choice from substitution to "your ZoomInfo investment, working harder" — selling itself as the execution layer that monetizes a ZoomInfo seat (source: https://demandscience.com/resources/comparisons/demandscience-vs-zoominfo/).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Precision Performance Marketing as a Service — Optimize Demand for Pipeline, Not Activity. Run precision ABM and demand programs using verified install intelligence and buying signals to deliver expert content, activation, and measurable pipeline, without adding another platform."
- **Recurring proof points:** 900+ leading companies trust DemandScience (Google, Salesforce, Microsoft, Adobe, AWS, Zoom, Shopify, Dow Jones, Schneider Electric, Capgemini, Accenture, Red Hat, Siemens AG); Forcura case study (141% of SQL goal, 48% of pipeline created by marketing, 219 MQLs from content syndication in 2 weeks); homepage stat "417% boost to pipeline + 39% shorter sales cycle"; "3% ad fraud rate vs 20% industry average"; G2 quadrant awards across SMB, Mid-Market, and Enterprise; U.S. Patent No. 11,468,139 for Content-IQ; 247M+ contacts in IONIC Identity Graph across 100+ countries.
- **Tone / category framing:** "Marketing Data Mirage" — anti-narrative to "activity that looks like progress but doesn't deliver pipeline." DS is positioned as the antidote to MarTech sprawl: verified buyer signals + resonant reach + effortless orchestration + continuous optimization, combining "Human + AI." Category descriptor used most often: "Precision Performance Marketing as a Service."
