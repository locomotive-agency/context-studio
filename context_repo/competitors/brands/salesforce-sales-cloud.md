## Summary

Salesforce Sales Cloud is the dominant CRM platform — by an enormous margin the largest seller-workflow surface in the market — with the same competitive relationship to ZoomInfo as HubSpot: complementary at the infrastructure layer, increasingly competitive at the AI-agent layer. Salesforce's strategic move is **Agentforce**, a platform for building / deploying / managing AI agents at scale, combined with **Einstein** AI integrated across Sales Cloud. The combination creates direct competition with ZoomInfo's "team of AI agents" framing inside GTM Workspace and ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph reasoning layer. Pricing is fully public and tiered: Starter $25 / Pro $100 / Enterprise $175 / Unlimited $350 / **Agentforce 1 Sales $550** USD/user/month — with the Agentforce 1 Sales tier explicitly framed as "The complete Sales CRM with built-in AI and unified data. One platform that does it all." Salesforce does NOT compete on B2B contact data (Pillar 1) — the entire ZoomInfo + Apollo + Cognism + Lusha + LeadIQ ecosystem exists to enrich INTO Salesforce. The relationship is co-existence with rising AI-agent competition.

## Product Offerings

### Sales Cloud (core CRM)

- **What it does:** The core seller CRM — accounts, contacts, deals, opportunities, pipeline, forecasting, reporting, with Einstein AI integrated across.
- **Key features:**
  - Accounts + contacts + leads + opportunities (CRM record of truth)
  - Pipeline management + forecasting
  - Sales process automation
  - Activity capture
  - Reporting + dashboards
  - Mobile app
  - Customizable via Lightning + APIs
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/sales/cloud/guide/

### [[salesforce-sales-cloud--einstein|Einstein AI for Sales]]

- **What it does:** Salesforce's AI layer for sales — predictive lead scoring, opportunity scoring, conversation insights, AI-drafted emails. Unified across Sales Cloud editions.
- **Key features:**
  - Predictive lead + opportunity scoring (Unlimited tier)
  - Einstein Activity Capture
  - Einstein Conversation Insights (Conversation Intelligence)
  - AI-drafted email + summarization
  - Pipeline inspection and deal insights (Enterprise+)
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/artificial-intelligence/

### [[salesforce-sales-cloud--agentforce|Agentforce (AI Agent Platform)]]

- **What it does:** Salesforce's enterprise AI-agent platform — build, test, deploy, manage, and orchestrate AI agents at scale across Sales, Service, Marketing, Commerce.
- **Key features:**
  - Enterprise agent build/deploy/manage lifecycle
  - "Humans + applications + AI agents + data" unification
  - Agentforce Labs for experimental features
  - 24/7 agent operation for customers, suppliers, employees
  - Multi-agent orchestration
  - Customer roster includes OpenTable and other enterprise customers
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/

### [[salesforce-sales-cloud--agentforce|Agentforce 1 Sales (top tier)]]

- **What it does:** Highest Sales Cloud tier — combines complete Sales CRM with built-in AI and unified data + Agentforce agent platform. "One platform that does it all."
- **Key features:**
  - Everything in Unlimited
  - Built-in Agentforce agents
  - Unified Salesforce data + AI
  - Predictive AI
  - Conversation Intelligence
  - Advanced security + governance
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/sales/pricing/

### AI Sales Agent (specialized)

- **What it does:** Specialized AI sales agent for autonomous prospecting, qualifying, and follow-up — dedicated landing page indicates this is a packaged offering separate from general Agentforce.
- **Key features:**
  - Autonomous prospecting workflows
  - Lead qualification
  - Follow-up automation
  - Trained on Sales Cloud data
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/sales/ai-sales-agent/

### [[salesforce-sales-cloud--einstein|Conversation Intelligence (Einstein Conversation Insights)]]

- **What it does:** CI capability inside Sales Cloud at Enterprise+ tiers — call recording, AI summary, deal insights.
- **Key features:**
  - Call recording + transcription
  - AI-powered conversation summaries
  - Deal-momentum signals
  - Coaching insights
  - Available Enterprise tier and above
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesforce.com/sales/pricing/

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Cross-industry; Salesforce serves every B2B vertical with industry clouds (Financial Services, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Retail, etc.).
- **Company size called out:** SMB (Starter) through Enterprise (Unlimited / Agentforce 1 Sales).
- **Persona / role focus:** Sales leaders, AEs, Sales Managers, RevOps, IT/Admins, CIOs/CSOs.
- **Use cases promoted:** Pipeline management; forecasting; deal management; AI-augmented selling; agent-orchestrated sales workflows; enterprise CRM as record of truth.

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce IS the CRM — every other vendor in the GTM stack integrates INTO Salesforce.
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Outreach (deep), Salesloft (deep), HubSpot (cross-CRM), and every major engagement vendor.
- **Data / enrichment:** ZoomInfo, Apollo, Cognism, Lusha, LeadIQ, Clearbit/Breeze, Clay all integrate into Salesforce — Salesforce is the destination for all B2B data layers.
- **Other notable integrations:** AppExchange (10,000+ apps); Slack (Salesforce-owned); MuleSoft (Salesforce-owned); Tableau (Salesforce-owned); every major SaaS via API/native integration.

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- The largest seller workflow surface in the market — Salesforce is the CRM layer the entire B2B GTM stack assumes (source: https://www.salesforce.com/sales/cloud/guide/).
- Agentforce as a strategic AI-agent platform — Salesforce's most aggressive AI-platform play in 2026 (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).
- Public, transparent five-tier pricing with explicit $ amounts at every tier — vs. ZoomInfo's "Contact Sales" gating (source: https://www.salesforce.com/sales/pricing/).
- Free trials on every tier including Agentforce 1 Sales (source: https://www.salesforce.com/sales/pricing/).
- AppExchange — 10,000+ certified apps, the largest enterprise marketplace.
- Owned ecosystem stack: Slack + MuleSoft + Tableau + Heroku + Salesforce Genie / Data Cloud — unified data foundation across teams.
- Agentforce Labs — explicit positioning of experimental + bleeding-edge AI capabilities (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace seller surface ↔ Sales Cloud + Einstein.** Sales Cloud IS the seller workspace for the vast majority of B2B sellers. ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace pitch is "ZoomInfo's data and AI native to a sales-specific UI"; Salesforce's pitch is "the CRM record of truth + AI on top." Many ZoomInfo customers run BOTH — ZoomInfo to enrich Salesforce. (source: https://www.salesforce.com/sales/cloud/guide/)
  - **ZoomInfo GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2 reasoning) ↔ Salesforce Einstein + Data Cloud + Agentforce.** Direct overlap on AI-driven reasoning across CRM data. Salesforce has the structural advantage of owning the CRM record. ZoomInfo's pitch is that the Context Graph reasons across third-party + first-party data combined; Salesforce's Einstein reasons within the Salesforce data graph. (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/)
  - **ZoomInfo Chorus ↔ Einstein Conversation Insights.** Direct CI overlap. Einstein Conversation Insights is bundled into Enterprise tier+; Chorus is sold standalone or with ZoomInfo. (source: https://www.salesforce.com/sales/pricing/)
  - **ZoomInfo "team of AI agents" framing ↔ Salesforce Agentforce.** Both vendors landed on packaged AI-agent platforms. Salesforce's Agentforce is more enterprise-platform-grade (build/deploy/manage lifecycle, multi-agent orchestration, marketplace); ZoomInfo's agents are inside GTM Workspace. (source: https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/)
  - **ZoomInfo Pipeline / Forecasting ↔ Salesforce Sales Cloud forecasting + pipeline management.** Direct overlap.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio ↔ Salesforce Data Cloud / MuleSoft for data ops.**
- **No overlap:**
  - **B2B contact data (Pillar 1)** — Salesforce does NOT maintain a third-party B2B contact database. The entire ZoomInfo + Apollo + Cognism + Lusha + LeadIQ ecosystem exists because Salesforce CRM data is necessarily incomplete on its own. Salesforce relies on partners.
  - **ZoomInfo APIs & MCP / agent-data lane** — Salesforce has APIs but the agent ecosystem is Salesforce-internal (Agentforce). No publicized cross-platform MCP server.
  - **Native intent data** — Salesforce relies on partners for intent.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public Salesforce-authored vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced._ The strategic framing is co-existence — Salesforce wants ZoomInfo (and competitors) to enrich into Salesforce. The competitive pressure is at the AI-agent layer where Agentforce + Einstein increasingly delivers capabilities ZoomInfo also delivers (CI, AI agents, predictive scoring) but inside Salesforce's data graph. The Agentforce 1 Sales tier at $550/user/month is the most aggressive bundling move against the "data + AI agent" sales pitch ZoomInfo also makes.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The complete Sales CRM with built-in AI and unified data. One platform that does it all." (Agentforce 1 Sales tier.) Agentforce platform pitch: "Build, deploy, and manage AI agents at scale."
- **Recurring proof points:** Customer stories include OpenTable, Asymbl, and thousands more enterprise customers; AppExchange ecosystem; Gartner/Forrester CRM leadership year-over-year.
- **Tone / category framing:** "Enterprise agentic AI solution" — Salesforce is leaning heavily on the "agentic" framing and positioning Agentforce as the platform layer for AI agents across customer + employee + supplier interactions. The Agentforce 1 Sales tier explicitly positions $550/user/month as the all-in-one bundle that includes the agent platform — direct competition with ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace + AI agents narrative.
