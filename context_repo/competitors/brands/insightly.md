## Summary

Insightly is an SMB and mid-market CRM platform sold to sales, marketing, operations, and product teams across agencies, consulting, finance, healthcare, manufacturing, professional services, and solar. The product line bundles CRM, Marketing automation, Service (ticketing), and AppConnect (no-code workflow automation) on a shared data layer, with an AI Copilot layered into the CRM (source: https://www.insightly.com/). Crossover with ZoomInfo is almost entirely complementary rather than competitive — Insightly is the system of record that ingests B2B data, and Insightly publishes a dedicated AppConnect integration that pulls ZoomInfo company/contact data into Insightly records (source: https://www.insightly.com/integrations/zoominfo/). No public Insightly-vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced in SERP.

## Product Offerings

### Insightly CRM

> **Wiki:** [[insightly--crm]]

- **What it does:** Modern, customizable CRM that centralizes lead, contact, and pipeline data with workflow automation and embedded project management.
- **Key features:**
  - Lead and contact management with kanban pipeline tracking
  - Workflow automation and validation rules
  - Products, price books, and quotes (configure quotes from opportunity records, generate PDFs)
  - Project & task management (convert closed opportunities into projects)
  - Customizable layouts, custom fields, and advanced permissions
  - Bidirectional email sync and customer email management
  - Performance dashboards & reporting (up to 100 dashboards on Professional, unlimited on Enterprise)
  - SOC 2 Type II, EU/US Privacy Shield + GDPR, HIPAA compliance
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightly.com/crm/

### Insightly Marketing (Marketing Automation)

> **Wiki:** [[insightly--marketing]]

- **What it does:** Marketing automation for email, journeys, landing pages, and prospect scoring built on the same platform as the CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Email marketing with drag-and-drop templates and transactional emails
  - Landing pages and embedded forms
  - Journey builder for multi-step email sequences
  - Dynamic list segmentation by firmographic and demographic data
  - Lead/prospect scoring and grading
  - A/B and multivariate testing
  - Dashboards with UTM tracking, multi-touch attribution, ROI tracking
  - Native sync with Insightly CRM
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightly.com/marketing/

### Insightly Service (Support and Ticketing)

> **Wiki:** [[insightly--service]]

- **What it does:** Customer service software for help desk and ticket management built on the same platform as Insightly CRM and Marketing.
- **Key features:**
  - Comprehensive ticket editor with SLA tracking and management
  - Branded public portals for customer ticket submission and knowledge access
  - Knowledge articles with version tracking and bulk updates
  - Community forums for customer feature requests
  - Customer support insights dashboard (open / unsolved / pending tickets by hour, weekday, month)
  - Auto-generate tickets from contact records, email, or web forms
  - Configurable email settings and macros
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightly.com/service/

### AppConnect (Workflow Automation / Integration Platform)

> **Wiki:** [[insightly--appconnect]]

- **What it does:** Low-code/no-code integration platform that builds drag-and-drop workflows between Insightly and 2,000+ third-party apps without writing code.
- **Key features:**
  - Drag-and-drop workflow designer; no servers or IT involvement
  - 2,000+ pre-built connectors (Microsoft, DocuSign, Shopify, WordPress, PandaDoc, NetSuite, QuickBooks)
  - Field mapping, data transformation, and calculations
  - Scheduling and trigger-based deployment
  - Task-based pricing tiers (25K / 100K / 250K tasks per month)
  - Categories include Sales & Marketing, Customer Service, Finance, HR, Operations, AI/ML, Productivity
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightly.com/appconnect/integrations/

### AI Features / Insightly Copilot

> **Wiki:** [[insightly--copilot]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered assistant inside the CRM that automates record management, drafts emails, and surfaces context with conversational lookup.
- **Key features:**
  - Copilot conversational lookup across contacts, deals, and tasks via natural language
  - AI-generated email replies with CRM context
  - AI email summaries pulled from Contacts, Leads, and Organizations
  - Streamlined record management — add, update, link records via chat
  - Smart communication assistance (drafts, follow-ups, suggested next steps)
  - Available on Professional and Enterprise CRM plans
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightly.com/crm/ai-crm/

### Insightly REST API

> **Wiki:** [[insightly--rest-api]]

- **What it does:** Third-generation REST + JSON API for embedding CRM functionality into custom applications and integrations.
- **Key features:**
  - REST + JSON conventions with a web-based sandbox
  - Client SDKs for Java, .NET, and Python
  - Swagger support for auto-generated SDKs in other languages
  - Use cases: custom reporting, smart lead capture & sync, workflow automation triggers
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightly.com/api-for-developers/

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Insightly is itself the CRM. The site advertises a "How to switch from Salesforce" path, and AppConnect can sync data with other CRMs (source: https://www.insightly.com/).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Apollo, Gong, Calendly (listed in AppConnect catalog).
- **Data / enrichment:** ZoomInfo — Insightly publishes a dedicated AppConnect integration page describing how AppConnect "will initiate a search in ZoomInfo and will return companies and contact details to enrich your CRM data" (source: https://www.insightly.com/integrations/zoominfo/).
- **Other notable integrations:** Microsoft, Google, Gmail, Slack, Jira, DocuSign, PandaDoc, Shopify, Magento, NetSuite, QuickBooks Online, Xero, Workday, ADP Workforce Now, Asana, Okta, WordPress, Microsoft Power BI, Drift, Unbounce. AppConnect lists 2,000+ connectors across 13 categories spanning 28 catalog pages (source: https://www.insightly.com/appconnect/integrations/).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Unified suite — CRM + Marketing + Service + AppConnect on one platform with shared data, marketed as "Insightly All-in-One" (source: https://www.insightly.com/pricing-plans/).
- Affordability and SMB price point — CRM and Service Plus tiers both start at $29/user/month (source: https://www.insightly.com/pricing-plans/).
- Low-code customization — validation rules, custom fields, and workflow automation that sales ops can configure without third-party developers (source: https://www.insightly.com/crm/).
- Project management built into the CRM — opportunities convert into projects with milestones for post-close visibility (source: https://www.insightly.com/crm/).
- AppConnect as a no-code integration engine with 2,000+ connectors and task-based pricing (source: https://www.insightly.com/appconnect/integrations/).
- Insightly Copilot — conversational AI assistant for record lookup, email drafting, and CRM data entry (source: https://www.insightly.com/crm/ai-crm/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Insightly does not sell B2B data, conversation intelligence, intent, or an ABM/GTM execution layer; it is the underlying CRM/marketing/service system, not an intelligence-on-top-of-CRM platform.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Insightly CRM and Insightly Marketing are adjacent to ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace and GTM Studio surfaces in that both sit in front of sellers and marketers, but Insightly is the system of record that GTM intelligence platforms enrich rather than a competitor to the GTM Context Graph (source: https://www.insightly.com/crm/, https://www.insightly.com/marketing/). The dedicated Insightly + ZoomInfo integration page explicitly frames the relationship as complementary: "use ZoomInfo's B2B database to ensure your customer data is fresher and deeper. AppConnect will initiate a search in ZoomInfo and will return companies and contact details to enrich your CRM data" (source: https://www.insightly.com/integrations/zoominfo/).
- **No overlap:** Insightly does not sell a B2B contact/company data foundation, conversation intelligence, intent data, an MCP server / AI-agent ecosystem, or seller workspace built on a context graph. Conversely, ZoomInfo does not sell help-desk/ticketing software (Insightly Service) or post-deal project management (Insightly CRM project module) (sources: https://www.insightly.com/service/, https://www.insightly.com/crm/, https://www.insightly.com/integrations/zoominfo/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The only Insightly page directly referencing ZoomInfo is the AppConnect integration page, which is partnership-framed, not competitive.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Grow faster with the Modern CRM teams love" — sell smarter, grow faster, and build lasting customer relationships, with an "Insightly All-in-One" bundle spanning CRM, Marketing, Service, and AppConnect (source: https://www.insightly.com/).
- **Recurring proof points:** Customer outcomes including Sigma Equipment 10% revenue growth, Sport Court 242% growth, Sullivan & Stanley 2x annual revenue, Hurst Review 20% savings, and PCG Digital 40% cost reduction; awards including TrustRadius Buyer's Choice 2026, TrustRadius Top Rated 2025, G2 High Performer 2025, G2 Users Love Us 2025, and Capterra Shortlist 2026; an independent ROI study cited as showing faster go-live and lower TCO than two unnamed competitors; SOC 2 Type II / GDPR / HIPAA compliance (sources: https://www.insightly.com/, https://www.insightly.com/crm/).
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as "The Modern CRM" inside Unbounce's Go-to-Market Solutions for "mid-market teams that want to convert more customers with less complexity." Emphasizes ease of use, affordability, and unified data over enterprise depth (sources: https://www.insightly.com/, https://www.insightly.com/appconnect/integrations/).
