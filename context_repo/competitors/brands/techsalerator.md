# Techsalerator

## Sub-products

- [[techsalerator--data-marketplace|Techsalerator Data Marketplace]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

Techsalerator is a global B2B + B2C data marketplace and data-licensing vendor (founded August 2020, Fort Lauderdale FL) selling raw firmographic, contact, technographic, consumer, and alternative datasets — not a workflow platform. Its closest overlap with ZoomInfo is the underlying Data foundation: 320M+ companies and 200M+ direct emails position it as a data-source alternative to ZoomInfo's contact graph, but it markets to a much broader buyer set including investors, KYC/AML teams, AI training data buyers, and real-estate/location-intelligence shops. There is no GTM Workspace / GTM Studio / Context Graph / Chorus / MCP equivalent — the product is data licensing plus a Shopify-style data shop with public per-city prices ($350-$400). Techsalerator's vs-ZoomInfo blog post pitches "superior data quality, quantity, and global coverage" and accuses ZoomInfo of regional gaps and data duplication.

## Product Offerings

### B2B / Business Data

- **What it does:** Bulk firmographic and contact records across global businesses, sold as licensable datasets (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/our-solutions).
- **Key features:**
  - 320M+ businesses across 190-219 countries
  - "200 fields per business" on the Solutions page; 50+ fields on the firmographic dataset
  - Sub-products: Firmographic, Entity Relationship, Business Contact, Direct Contact of Business Titles (200M+ direct emails, 140M+ personal phones), Financial, Technographic (100M+ technologies), ESG (30,000 public companies, 450+ fields, 4,000+ ETFs), Funding (220,000+ events)
  - 200+ industries covered
- **Source URL:** https://www.techsalerator.com/our-solutions

### B2C / Consumer Data

- **What it does:** Consumer demographic, behavioral, and contact records sourced locally across countries (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog).
- **Key features:**
  - 2 billion consumer records worldwide (homepage); locally sourced consumer data in 144 countries (Solutions page)
  - People Demographic Data: 1B+ people across 150+ countries, 30+ demographic attributes
  - People Lifestyle and Behavior Data: 1B+ people, 100+ lifestyle attributes
  - People Contact Data: 600M+ direct emails across 45+ countries
  - KYC Identity Data: 2B people across 120+ countries, 150+ fields
- **Source URL:** https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog

### API Solutions

- **What it does:** Programmatic search and retrieval of records for individuals, professionals, and businesses with automated lookups (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/our-solutions).
- **Key features:**
  - Search across individuals and professionals in a "speedy, flexible and automated manner"
  - Marketed as ad-free environment for automated lookups
  - Positioned as a delivery method alongside dataset licensing
- **Source URL:** https://www.techsalerator.com/our-solutions

### Alternative / Geospatial / Asset Datasets

- **What it does:** Tailored alternative datasets beyond business and consumer data, including geospatial, real estate, market data, and news/job feeds (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog).
- **Key features:**
  - Weather Data: 80,000 locations, 100+ signals
  - POI Data: 160M+ places across 100+ countries
  - Real Estate Data: 80,000 locations, 100+ signals
  - Product Data: 1M+ products across 200+ countries, 13,000 sites
  - Corporate Actions: 300M+ businesses, 5-year history
  - End-of-Day Prices: 1,125,419+ securities, 5-year history
  - News Events: 7.2M+ events, 7-year history
  - Job Openings: 174M+ openings, 7-year history
- **Source URL:** https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog

### Techsalerator Data Shop (datashop)

- **What it does:** E-commerce marketplace ("first centralized data marketplace from a single provider") selling pre-packaged city- and country-level datasets at fixed list prices via a Shopify storefront (source: https://www.techsaleratordatashop.com/).
- **Key features:**
  - Browseable storefront across Business Data, Consumer Data, Firmographic Data, POI Data
  - Per-city/region datasets sold at fixed list prices ($350-$400 typical for B2B city packs)
  - Cart, wishlist, account login, and "Search for data → Make your purchase → Gain insights" self-service flow
  - Headline claim: 320M business records, 900M consumer records, plus alternative datasets
- **Source URL:** https://www.techsaleratordatashop.com/

### Data Marketplace (techsalerator.com)

- **What it does:** Country-by-country marketplace surface listing every nation with available local datasets (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-marketplace).
- **Key features:**
  - All 200+ countries listed with flag-icon entry points
  - Covers every territory from Afghanistan to Western Sahara, including non-sovereign territories
  - Acts as a country-routed alternative entry to the Data Shop and bespoke licensing flow
- **Source URL:** https://www.techsalerator.com/data-marketplace

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Single-vendor centralized data marketplace spanning B2B, B2C, geospatial, alternative assets, and KYC in one catalog — positioned as the "first centralized data marketplace from a single provider" (source: https://www.techsaleratordatashop.com/).
- Truly global coverage — 190-219 countries listed, with the Data Marketplace surfacing every individual country page from Afghanistan to Western Sahara (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-marketplace).
- B2C scale (2B consumer records, 600M+ direct emails) is unusual relative to ZoomInfo's B2B-first positioning (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog).
- E-commerce purchasing model for per-city/region datasets at fixed $350-$400 list price — buy without sales contact (source: https://www.techsaleratordatashop.com/).
- ESG dataset (30,000 public companies, 450+ fields, 4,000+ ETF listings) and end-of-day securities prices (1.12M+ securities) place Techsalerator in financial-data-vendor territory adjacent to FactSet/Refinitiv (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog).
- 250+ pre-mapped use cases across the catalog (from AML compliance to brand equity analysis) frame Techsalerator as a "find-the-dataset-for-your-question" provider rather than a workflow tool (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/use-cases).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation** (500M contacts, 100M companies, technographics across 30K+ technologies) vs. Techsalerator B2B Data — Firmographic, Contact, Direct Contact of Business Titles, Technographic. Techsalerator pitches "320M+ companies", 200 fields per business, 200M+ direct emails, 140M+ personal phones, and 100M+ technologies as a direct alternative to ZoomInfo's data foundation (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog).
  - **ZoomInfo APIs** (Enterprise API: Data, Marketing, Platform) vs. Techsalerator API Solutions. Techsalerator markets an API delivery layer for searching and retrieving individual/professional records programmatically; positioned alongside dataset licensing (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/our-solutions).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations** (CRM data quality, enrichment, routing) vs. Techsalerator B2B Contact + Firmographic + Technographic feeds. Techsalerator positions itself as a "pull data, enrich your CRM" source — the data layer feeding into the operations workflows ZoomInfo Operations productizes — but does not ship CRM enrichment workflows, dedupe rules, or routing engines (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/use-cases/customer-data-integration-for-a-unified-view-of-customers).
- **No overlap:**
  - No conversation-intelligence / Chorus equivalent — no recording, transcription, or coaching product on any reviewed page.
  - No GTM Workspace / GTM Studio equivalent — Techsalerator surfaces only data licensing and an e-commerce data shop; there is no prioritized-accounts surface, AI-drafted outreach, audience builder, or play orchestration.
  - No GTM Context Graph equivalent — Techsalerator sells static datasets and APIs; there is no first-party-data fusion, intent reasoning layer, or "why deals move" capability described.
  - No MCP server / agent-builder ecosystem surfaced.
  - Conversely, Techsalerator sells extensive B2C, ESG, Real Estate, POI, End-of-Day Prices, Weather, KYC, and Job-Openings datasets — territory ZoomInfo does not address as a B2B GTM platform (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/data-catalog).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "Techsalerator stands out as the superior alternative, particularly for organizations that prioritize data quality, quantity, and global coverage. ZoomInfo's database has limitations in certain regions and industries, making it less reliable for companies with diverse international or niche market needs… Techsalerator's data is continually updated and verified, offering businesses confidence that they are working with the most current, reliable data available." (source: https://www.techsalerator.com/post/zoominfo-vs-techsalerator)
