## Summary

InsightSquared is a revenue intelligence and sales-forecasting platform aimed at mid-market and high-growth B2B sales, RevOps, marketing, and customer-success leaders, now operating as part of Mediafly's Intelligence360 / Revenue360 suite after the December 2021 acquisition. Its core surfaces — Conversation Intelligence, Activity Capture, AI Sales Forecasting, RevOps Dashboards, Interactive Reporting, and Guided Selling — overlap most directly with ZoomInfo's Chorus and the conversation/activity-fed reasoning of the GTM Context Graph. Unlike ZoomInfo, InsightSquared has no third-party B2B contact, company, technographic, or intent data product and no public APIs/MCP surface, so it competes on top of customer first-party data rather than as a data foundation. Public pricing is gated, and the live Pricing nav routes to mediafly.com — treat the standalone site as a partially deprecated marketing surface for a Mediafly product line.

## Product Offerings

### AI Sales Forecasting

> **Wiki:** [[insightsquared--ai-sales-forecasting]]

- **What it does:** Automates and validates forecast submissions with machine learning to predict close confidence and ideal-customer-profile fit on every deal.
- **Key features:**
  - Automated forecast submission across the team, replacing Excel rollups
  - Confidence to Close machine learning score on every deal
  - Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) score for opportunity scoring
  - Forecast slicing by region, line of business, product, month, or quarter
  - Forecast Summary report flagging missing or outdated data
  - Override tracking and historical forecast change records
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/sales-forecasting/

### RevOps Dashboards

> **Wiki:** [[insightsquared--revops-dashboards]]

- **What it does:** Pre-built and customizable dashboards that align sales, marketing, and customer success teams around a single revenue view.
- **Key features:**
  - 350+ out-of-the-box reports and dashboards
  - Self-service report building with no-code customization
  - Real-time updates synced from CRM activity
  - Segmented views by role, team, region, or business line
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/

### Interactive Reporting / Pipeline Management

> **Wiki:** [[insightsquared--interactive-reporting]]

- **What it does:** Pipeline review and risk-detection reports that score deals on close confidence and ICP fit.
- **Key features:**
  - Confidence-to-Close machine learning model on every opportunity
  - ICP model identifying deals matching profiles of past wins
  - Funnel progression and goal-mapping reports
  - Exception and risk detection (stalled deals, single-threaded deals)
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/interactive-reporting/

### Activity Capture

> **Wiki:** [[insightsquared--activity-capture]]

- **What it does:** Automatically captures sales activities (emails, meetings, demos) from every team member touching a deal and writes them back to the CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Automated email + meeting + demo capture across the buying group, not just the deal owner
  - CRM write-back enriches lead, opportunity, account, and contact records
  - Captures activity for sales engineers, overlays, and CSMs (not only the AE)
  - 15-month look-back window for historical activity baselining
  - Single-threaded / missing decision-maker detection on key deals
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/activity-capture/

### Conversation Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[insightsquared--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Records, transcribes, and analyzes customer calls and meetings, feeding the data into machine-learning forecasting and coaching models.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time call recording and transcription
  - Searchable on-demand library of meeting transcripts
  - Playlists for coaching specific roles, new hires, or teams
  - Skill-level reporting (pricing discussions, objection handling, value prop, demos)
  - Competitive mention tracking and win/loss correlation
  - Direct integration with forecasting and reporting engines
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/conversation-intelligence/

### Guided Selling

> **Wiki:** [[insightsquared--guided-selling]]

- **What it does:** An automated assistant that surfaces in-the-moment next-best-actions for reps based on best-practice playbooks and machine-learning patterns.
- **Key features:**
  - 120+ pre-configured Actions plus customizable rep playbooks
  - Actions customizable by user, role, opportunity type, stage, or region
  - Real-time prompts when prospects go dark or data hygiene gaps appear
  - Actions Engine + Reporting to track Action completion and impact
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/guided-selling/

### Solutions Packages (Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Staffing analytics)

- **What it does:** Vertical / functional analytics packages layered on top of the core platform for specific revenue functions.
- **Key features:**
  - Sales Analytics — sales-funnel visualization by company, product line, time period, and rep
  - Marketing Analytics — campaign and lead-source attribution
  - Customer Success Analytics — renewal and engagement tracking
  - Staffing Analytics — packaged analytics for staffing firms
- **Source URL:** https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence)** vs **InsightSquared Conversation Intelligence** — record, transcribe, and analyze every customer call; on-demand searchable transcript library; coaching playlists; competitive-mention tracking (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/conversation-intelligence/).
  - **GTM Context Graph (reasoning across CRM + conversations + activity)** vs **InsightSquared Revenue Intelligence Platform** — Confidence to Close and ICP models reason across activity capture, conversation intelligence, and CRM data; same "explain why deals move" framing (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality)** vs **InsightSquared Activity Capture** — automated activity write-back addressing under-reporting and CRM hygiene gaps (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/activity-capture/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (seller-facing AI guidance)** vs **InsightSquared Guided Selling** — AI-powered next-best-action prompts customized by role and stage; 120+ pre-configured Actions playbook (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/guided-selling/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM and marketing analytics)** vs **InsightSquared Marketing Analytics** — campaign attribution from initial contact to signed contract (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace AI forecasting** vs **InsightSquared AI Sales Forecasting** — ML-validated forecasting with ICP and Confidence to Close scoring competes with ZoomInfo's pipeline-prediction surface, though ZoomInfo's forecasting story leans on its own GTM Context Graph plus CRM signals (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/sales-forecasting/).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Mediafly content engagement / sales enablement** — page-level content view tracking, share-tracking, value calculators are sales-enablement plays ZoomInfo does not directly own (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/blog/mediafly-acquiring-insightsquared/).
  - **InsightSquared Staffing Analytics** — staffing-firm vertical analytics package that ZoomInfo does not market (source: https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/).
  - **ZoomInfo B2B data foundation** (500M contacts, 100M companies, 200M+ verified emails, 135M+ phone numbers, technographics across 30K+ technologies) — InsightSquared has no third-party B2B contact, company, technographic, or intent data product; it sits entirely on top of customer first-party data.
  - **ZoomInfo APIs & MCP server / agent-builder ecosystem** — no public API, MCP, or developer agent-builder offering surfaced on extracted pages.
