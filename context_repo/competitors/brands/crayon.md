## Summary

Crayon is a competitive intelligence / battlecard enablement platform sold to Product Marketing and CI leads at mid-market and enterprise B2B companies. Its core surface is monitoring competitor digital footprints, mining first-party signals from calls and Slack, and pushing battlecards/answers into Salesforce, Slack, and Teams (source: https://www.crayon.co/, https://www.crayon.co/product/enable). It is tangential to ZoomInfo — a different category — but adjacent to ZoomInfo's Chorus + GTM Workspace seller surface where battlecard-style enablement also lives, and ZoomInfo itself is a published Crayon customer (source: https://www.crayon.co/case-studies-list/zoominfo).

## Product Offerings

### Crayon AI (Sparks + Crayon Answers)

> **Wiki:** [[crayon--crayon-ai]]

- **What it does:** AI layer that aggregates competitor data, scores its importance, and turns it into seller-ready content and conversational answers across the Crayon stack.
- **Key features:**
  - Sparks agent: AI-research tool that runs scheduled SWOT, win/loss, and call-analysis tasks across compete data
  - Crayon Answers: Gen-AI compete assistant inside Slack/Teams that returns immediate answers to competitor questions
  - Competitive signal mining across Gong, Chorus, Slack, Teams, Google Drive, OneDrive, and Clozd
  - Automatic tagging into 80+ subcategories, NLP sentiment grading, ML-driven importance scoring, and anomaly detection
  - Crayon MCP Server and AI-ready APIs that pipe validated compete content into ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Glean, Coveo, and Guru
- **Source URL:** https://www.crayon.co/product/crayon-ai

### Aggregate

> **Wiki:** [[crayon--aggregate]]

- **What it does:** Captures competitive data from competitors' digital footprints plus first-party deal/seller signals.
- **Key features:**
  - Monitors competitor websites, pricing pages, news, support docs, and customer reviews for before/after changes in real time
  - Daily morning email summarizing the most important insights from the prior day
  - GTM Insights pulls intel from call recordings (Gong, Chorus, Clari) and Slack
  - One-click ingestion of win/loss interview and survey findings into enablement materials
- **Source URL:** https://www.crayon.co/product/aggregate

### Organize

> **Wiki:** [[crayon--organize]]

- **What it does:** Structures inbound competitive intel into searchable, labeled, and template-driven workflows so it becomes usable enablement content.
- **Key features:**
  - Sparks pre-built and custom prompt templates that run against hundreds of data points and can be scheduled
  - Call Clips that surface competitive moments from call recordings and summarize them
  - Saved Searches that filter competitive noise into a curated, automated stream
  - Labels that categorize insights for retrieval and routing to stakeholders
- **Source URL:** https://www.crayon.co/product/organize

### Enable (Battlecards & Compete Hub)

> **Wiki:** [[crayon--enable]]

- **What it does:** Distributes competitive insights and battlecards into the tools sellers already use.
- **Key features:**
  - Battlecards embedded in Salesforce, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Highspot, Seismic, Showpad, and SharePoint
  - Compete Hub for tailored, account/territory-specific competitive updates
  - Crayon Answers Gen-AI assistant for instant responses to compete questions
  - Sparks-powered battlecard refresh that keeps content updated automatically
  - Field-intel submission loop via Slack or in-app for sellers to feed insights back
- **Source URL:** https://www.crayon.co/product/enable

### Measure

> **Wiki:** [[crayon--measure]]

- **What it does:** Reports on the ROI of the competitive-intelligence program by tying battlecard usage to pipeline and revenue outcomes.
- **Key features:**
  - Win/loss trend analysis with win rates by competitor and reasons for wins/losses
  - Competitive Pipeline view that flags compete risk in open opportunities
  - Seller Engagement metric correlating rep win rate to battlecard views
  - Influenced revenue reporting tied to battlecard usage
  - Rep-level leaderboard plus engagement tracking on newsletters, Compete Hub posts, and Crayon Answers queries
- **Source URL:** https://www.crayon.co/product/measure

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Purpose-built CI platform with five named modules (Aggregate, Organize, Publish, Enable, Measure) plus a Crayon AI overlay — narrower and deeper than CI features bundled inside larger sales tools (source: https://www.crayon.co/).
- Crayon MCP Server and AI-ready APIs let validated battlecard/CI content ground responses in ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Glean, Coveo, and Guru (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/crayon-ai).
- Sparks AI agent runs scheduled prompts (SWOT, win/loss, call analysis) over hundreds of competitive data points and pushes results into battlecards (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/crayon-ai).
- Influenced-revenue reporting that ties battlecard views and rep engagement to closed-won pipeline (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/measure).
- Battlecards embed natively in Salesforce, Slack, Teams, Highspot, Seismic, Showpad, and SharePoint so reps consume CI in the tools they already use (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/enable).
- Customer-cited results: 40% increase in battlecard adoption (Alteryx); 22% increase in competitive win rate (Salsify); $6M influenced revenue in <1 year (Cognism); win rate doubled and one top-competitor win rate tripled to 95% within 6 months (Allego) (source: https://www.crayon.co/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** None. Crayon is a different category — competitive intelligence and battlecard enablement, not a Go-To-Market Intelligence Platform. Crayon's homepage frames it as "Competitive Intelligence Software" that "monitors your competitors and enables your sales team with real-time intelligence" (source: https://www.crayon.co/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Chorus / GTM Workspace seller surface:** Crayon mines call recordings from Gong/Chorus/Clari and pushes battlecards/Crayon Answers into Salesforce/Slack/Teams — the same in-the-flow seller surface ZoomInfo addresses with Chorus + GTM Workspace, but Crayon is a curation/enablement layer rather than a data + workflow stack (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/enable).
  - **GTM Context Graph (signal aggregation):** Crayon's Aggregate module ingests competitor digital signals plus first-party signals (calls, Slack, win/loss) — adjacent to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph framing, but scoped to competitor intelligence rather than full GTM reasoning across the customer journey (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/aggregate).
  - **APIs & MCP:** Both ship MCP servers exposing their content to AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot). Crayon's surface is competitive content; ZoomInfo's is GTM data — same access pattern, different payloads (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/crayon-ai).
- **No overlap:**
  - Competitor monitoring of websites, pricing pages, news, support docs, and reviews is not part of ZoomInfo's stack (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/aggregate).
  - Battlecard authoring, Compete Hub, win/loss analysis, and influenced-revenue measurement of a CI program are Crayon-native (source: https://www.crayon.co/product/measure).
  - Crayon sells no B2B contact/company/intent data graph — no equivalent to ZoomInfo's 500M contacts / 100M companies / 135M phones / 200M emails / technographics data foundation (source: https://www.crayon.co/).
  - Crayon has no execution workspace for outreach, audience building, play orchestration, or routing (no equivalent of GTM Workspace or GTM Studio) (source: https://www.crayon.co/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Crayon publishes a ZoomInfo *customer* case study, not a vs/comparison page, framing the two as complementary: "ZoomInfo has integrated Crayon with Slack, which allows the company to instantly give the entire organization access to intel" (source: https://www.crayon.co/case-studies-list/zoominfo).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Drive More Competitive Wins with Automated Insights — Crayon AI mines intel from your data, creates instant content, and enables your reps with talk tracks and sales plays that actually help them close more deals." (source: https://www.crayon.co/)
- **Recurring proof points:**
  - ZoomInfo case study: 90% of 1,000+ ZoomInfo sales team interacts with CI deliverables; Slack integration cited (source: https://www.crayon.co/case-studies-list/zoominfo)
  - Alteryx — 40% increase in battlecard adoption
  - Cognism — $6M in influenced revenue in less than a year
  - Salsify — 22% increase in competitive win rate
  - Allego — sales win rate doubled in under 6 months; top-competitor win rate tripled to 95%
  - Reputation, Affinity, Oyster testimonials for Sparks and Crayon Answers
- **Tone / category framing:** Crayon positions as the "Competitive Enablement Platform" / "Competitive Intelligence Software" — a vertical CI category that captures, organizes, publishes, enables, and measures competitive intelligence, distinct from broader GTM or sales intelligence platforms.
