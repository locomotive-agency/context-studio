# RB2B

**G2:** ⭐ 4.9 / 5 — 95 reviews  <!-- g2-injector -->

## Summary

RB2B is a person-level US website visitor identification tool aimed at SMB/mid-market founders, demand-gen leads, agencies, and SDR teams who want to deanonymize site traffic. It directly overlaps ZoomInfo's WebSights/Website Visitor Tracking module but executes one notch deeper — surfacing names, job titles, LinkedIn URLs, and verified business emails of the people (not just companies) on US traffic, pushed to Slack in under five minutes. RB2B's contrarian "Free Forever" plan, fully transparent self-serve pricing ($0–$199/mo), and Slack-first workflow position it as the cheap, narrow, fast counterpart to ZoomInfo's broad GTM platform. It has no contact database, no buyer intent layer, no conversation intelligence, and no person-level coverage outside the US.

## Product Offerings

### RB2B Person-Level Website Visitor ID

> **Wiki:** [[rb2b--person-level-visitor-id]]

- **What it does:** Identifies the individual US-based people visiting your website (name, job title, LinkedIn URL, business email) in real time and pushes them to Slack, CRM, or other tools.
- **Key features:**
  - Person-level (contact-level) identification for US visitors via a proprietary publisher network using 1st/3rd-party cookies, device IDs, and IP addresses
  - Identifies 70-80% of US website traffic (claimed) on the Pro+ Premium Resolution tier
  - 5-minute pixel install in site header
  - Real-time Slack and Microsoft Teams alerts with LinkedIn profile, name, title, and verified business email
  - Hot Pages (high-intent page tagging) and Hot Leads (ICP filtering) on Pro and Pro+
  - Pageview history, session recap, and repeat visitor tracking
  - Domain/URL exclusion lists and ICP-based filtering to keep the CRM clean
  - SOC2 Type II certified, registered data broker in CA, TX, VT, OR; CCPA/CPRA compliant
- **Source URL:** https://www.rb2b.com/lead-generation-software

### Company-Level ID (Global, via Demandbase partnership)

> **Wiki:** [[rb2b--company-level-id]]

- **What it does:** Identifies the companies (not individuals) visiting your website worldwide through a Demandbase Reverse-IP Lookup partnership, for non-US traffic and free-tier users.
- **Key features:**
  - Global company-level resolution (not person-level) for GDPR-compliant identification
  - Available on Free plan (150 monthly resolutions) and across all paid tiers
  - Firmographic data: company name, industry, size, location
  - Requires a consent-management tool when deployed globally
- **Source URL:** https://www.rb2b.com/

- Free Forever plan with no credit card and no contract, 150 monthly company-level resolutions — contrarian to enterprise visitor-ID vendors (source: https://www.rb2b.com/pricing).
- 5-minute pixel install with publicly listed flat monthly pricing ($0–$199 self-serve; no "talk to sales" barrier) (source: https://www.rb2b.com/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** RB2B's Person-Level Website Visitor ID + Company-Level ID overlaps directly with ZoomInfo Marketing's WebSights / Website Visitor Tracking module, but executes at the person level rather than company level. RB2B's own ZoomInfo review article frames ZoomInfo's three BI data tiers as "Company Contacts, Buyer Intent, Website Visitor Tracking" and explicitly positions RB2B as the way to add person-level resolution on top of ZoomInfo (source: https://www.rb2b.com/learn/zoominfo-review). RB2B's real-time Slack/Teams alerts + Hot Leads + Hot Pages also overlap with ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace's signal alerting and SDR prioritization, though from a much narrower contact graph (source: https://www.rb2b.com/lead-generation-software).
- **Adjacent overlap:** RB2B routes identified visitors into HubSpot/Salesforce/webhook workflows, lead scoring, and rep alerts — adjacent to (not a substitute for) ZoomInfo Operations' broader data-quality, dedupe, and routing platform (source: https://www.rb2b.com/integrations). RB2B appends business emails to identified visitors but only for the small subset of people who visited the site — narrow vs. ZoomInfo's 200M+ verified business email database covering the broader TAM (source: https://www.rb2b.com/pricing).
- **No overlap:** Outbound prospecting database / TAM search (RB2B has no contact database to search outside identified visitors); GTM Context Graph / 1.5B daily data points / third-party Buyer Intent / Conversation Intelligence (Chorus); ABM advertising, multi-channel ad targeting, and form enrichment (no RB2B counterpart); Talent / TalentOS / candidate prospecting; international person-level identification (RB2B is US-only by design with IP ringfencing); APIs / MCP for AI agents / developer ecosystem (none surfaced) (sources: https://www.rb2b.com/learn/zoominfo-review , https://www.rb2b.com/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** From RB2B's "An In-Depth ZoomInfo Review" article: _"RB2B is the only FREE tool that provides person-level visitor information, so you don't have to guess who the decision maker is. … Many users, myself included, feel that ZoomInfo's pricing is quite steep for what it offers. … Even their ZoomInfo Lite plan, offered for free in exchange for your email contact data, raises questions about data privacy and value exchange."_ (source: https://www.rb2b.com/learn/zoominfo-review). Note: this is a ZoomInfo review with an RB2B add-on pitch, not a dedicated head-to-head comparison page.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Identify the 70-80% of your US website visitors that today go anonymous — at the person level, with names, LinkedIn, and business emails delivered to Slack in under 5 minutes, with a Free Forever plan.
- **Recurring proof points:** SOC2 Type II compliant; G2 4.5 stars / 275+ reviews; customer testimonials from SAS, ColdIQ, Rebar ($27K ARR closed in days, 10x ROI), Seso, Mixmax ($100K opportunity), Sendspark, Distribute.so, Default, Reply, Biscred (11 demos from 100 enriched visitors); Demandbase data partnership for global company-level ID; registered data broker in CA, TX, VT, OR.
- **Tone / category framing:** RB2B positions itself as "the simplest, fastest, easiest, and least expensive path to the website visitor signal" — explicitly contrarian to enterprise sales-intelligence platforms, framing ZoomInfo as expensive, broad, and slow vs. RB2B's narrow, fast, free signal layer.
