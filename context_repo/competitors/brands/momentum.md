## Summary

Momentum is an AI Revenue Orchestration Platform that targets RevOps, Sales, and CS leaders at mid-market and enterprise B2B companies, sitting between the call recorder and Salesforce to extract structured deal data from conversations and run Slack-native execution workflows. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Chorus (conversation intelligence), ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data hygiene via MEDDIC/MEDDPIC Autopilot and Retropilot batch backfill), and the GTM Workspace seller surface (Slack Deal Rooms + Notifications + Slack-to-Salesforce approvals), but does not compete on third-party B2B contact, company, or intent data — it operates only on the customer's first-party CRM and conversation data and explicitly integrates with Chorus, Gong, and Clari Copilot rather than displacing them. Momentum was acquired by Salesforce on March 2, 2026 (definitive agreement Feb 18, 2026) to feed Agentforce 360 and Slackbot with conversational ingestion, which materially shifts its long-term competitive trajectory toward the Salesforce/Slack stack.

## Product Offerings

### Deal Execution Agent

> **Wiki:** [[momentum--deal-execution-agent]]

- **What it does:** Automates post-call admin for AEs and CSMs by writing call summaries, action items, and ~98% of CRM field types directly into Salesforce.
- **Key features:**
  - MEDDPIC / MEDDIC Autopilot — automated CRM field updates from calls
  - AI Summaries pushed to Salesforce notes
  - AI-suggested tasks and email follow-ups
  - Autopilot Classic + Autopilot Batch (Retropilot) for backfilling historical CRM fields at scale
  - Ask Momentum — natural-language Q&A across calls, accounts, deals
  - Sync to Snowflake for downstream analytics
- **Source URL:** https://www.momentum.io/

### Customer Retention Agent

> **Wiki:** [[momentum--customer-retention-agent]]

- **What it does:** Detects churn risk, sentiment, and product-feedback signals from CS calls and triggers automated retention workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Churn Detection Signals with instant notifications
  - Upsell signal detection
  - AE-to-CSM handoff context
  - Sentiment Analysis on customer interactions
  - Automated retention workflows
  - Executive churn & retention briefs
- **Source URL:** https://www.momentum.io/customer-retention-agent

### AI Sales Coaching Agent

> **Wiki:** [[momentum--ai-sales-coaching-agent]]

- **What it does:** Provides AI-driven, real-time coaching scores and competency reviews based on individual call recordings.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Coaching Score per call
  - Competency Reviews and methodology briefs
  - Individual call scoring + commenting
  - Coaching Agent aggregate dashboard
  - Team Performance Briefs
- **Source URL:** https://www.momentum.io/ai-sales-coaching-agent

### AI CRO (Executive Insights)

> **Wiki:** [[momentum--ai-cro]]

- **What it does:** Generates executive-level briefs analyzing accounts, reps, and pipeline to inform leadership decisions.
- **Key features:**
  - Executive CRO Briefs on pipeline performance and account health
  - Pipeline forecasting briefs
  - Closed Lost / Won analysis
  - AI Signals across accounts and reps
  - Ask Momentum on calls + accounts
- **Source URL:** https://www.momentum.io/executive-insights

### Workflow Automation (Slack Deal Rooms + Notifications)

> **Wiki:** [[momentum--workflow-automation]]

- **What it does:** No-code workflow builder that turns Slack into the GTM command center — auto-creates Deal Rooms, routes Salesforce alerts, and lets reps approve / update Salesforce from Slack buttons.
- **Key features:**
  - AI-powered Deal Rooms per opportunity
  - Account Rooms for ongoing customer projects
  - Smart Notifications from Salesforce triggers
  - Slack-to-Salesforce approvals and pipeline updates
  - Microsoft Teams support (announced)
- **Source URL:** https://www.momentum.io/notifications

### Conversation Intelligence Add-On

> **Wiki:** [[momentum--conversation-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Add-on (+$30/user/mo) that records, transcribes, and summarizes Zoom / Google Meet / Teams calls and stores them in a video library.
- **Key features:**
  - Note-taker bot for Google Meet
  - Auto-recording bot for Zoom
  - Centralized video library with playlists and snippet sharing
  - SmartClips — AI-generated short video highlights
  - 99+ language translation of summaries
  - Account Briefs with stakeholder mapping
- **Source URL:** https://www.momentum.io/pricing

### Deep Research

- **What it does:** Analyzes all deal data across conversations and CRM to surface insights across the full customer journey.
- **Key features:**
  - Cross-journey deal analysis combining CRM + conversation data
  - Annual platform license + usage-based credits pricing
- **Source URL:** https://www.momentum.io/deepresearch

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Slack-native command center: Deal Rooms spin up automatically and reps can update Salesforce directly from Slack buttons (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
- Retropilot batch mode analyzes thousands of historical calls to backfill MEDDIC and Salesforce fields at scale — not just go-forward capture (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
- Positions itself as a "revenue orchestration layer" that sits across the GTM stack rather than as a standalone conversation intelligence tool: "Momentum goes beyond conversation intelligence" (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
- Claims ~98% CRM field-type coverage for automated extraction from calls (source: https://www.momentum.io/pricing).
- Layered interoperability: integrates with Gong, Chorus, and Clari Copilot — explicitly designed to layer on top of incumbent CI tools instead of replacing them (source: https://www.momentum.io/integration).
- Acquired by Salesforce (Feb 18, 2026 announcement; closed Mar 2, 2026) to feed Agentforce 360 and Slackbot with conversational data ingestion (source: https://www.salesforce.com/news/stories/salesforce-signs-definitive-agreement-to-acquire-momentum/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence)** vs Momentum's Conversation Intelligence Add-On + AI Summaries — Momentum records, transcribes, and summarizes Zoom/Meet/Teams calls and pushes summaries to Salesforce, the same core CI workflow Chorus serves. Momentum even integrates directly with Chorus as an interoperability play (source: https://www.momentum.io/integration).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing)** vs MEDDPIC Autopilot + Retropilot (CRM hygiene) — Momentum's headline pitch is that it "puts CRM hygiene on autopilot" by extracting structured fields from conversations and writing them to Salesforce, including historical batch backfill. Direct overlap with Operations' CRM data quality role (source: https://www.momentum.io/pricing).
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end)** vs Slack Deal Rooms + Notifications + Deal Execution Agent — Momentum positions Slack as the seller's command center for prioritized deals, automated outreach drafting (email follow-ups, action items), and execution against Salesforce — the same seller execution surface ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace targets (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio (orchestration / play building)** vs no-code Workflow Automation builder — Momentum offers a no-code workflow builder for triggers/actions/conditions across GTM tools, adjacent to GTM Studio's play orchestration but oriented around post-sale Slack/CRM execution rather than marketer-led audience building (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (AE prospecting + coaching)** vs AI Sales Coaching Agent + AI CRO Briefs — Momentum coaches reps and gives CROs pipeline briefs, adjacent to ZoomInfo Sales/GTM Workspace's AE workflows but driven by call data rather than third-party contact + intent data (source: https://www.momentum.io/ai-sales-coaching-agent).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1)** (500M contacts, 100M companies, 200M+ verified emails, 135M+ phones, technographics) — Momentum has no third-party B2B contact, company, or technographics dataset; it operates exclusively on the customer's first-party CRM and conversation data (source: https://www.momentum.io/).
  - **Intent Data (Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader)** — Momentum does not provide third-party intent or buying signals; AI Signals are derived from internal calls and CRM events (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
  - **ABM / marketing orchestration (ZoomInfo Marketing)** — Momentum has a Marketing Leader persona but no ABM platform, ad targeting, or audience-building product (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
  - **APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem** — no public developer API or MCP server surfaced on the reviewed pages.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public first-party comparison page surfaced in SERP results — see flags._ Momentum's only direct mention of ZoomInfo on its own pages is to celebrate native integration with Chorus and Salesloft, not to differentiate against ZoomInfo as a competitor (source: https://www.momentum.io/blog/integrations-salesloft-chorus).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "AI Revenue Orchestration Platform that captures unstructured data from every customer interaction, routes insights at speed, and automates execution across your revenue systems" — first call to renewals (source: https://www.momentum.io/).
- **Recurring proof points:** Customer roster — Zscaler, Ramp, 1Password, Demandbase, Postman, Alation, Contentful, ScyllaDB, Treasure Data, ElevenLabs, Jasper, Sentry; reported outcomes of 3–10 hours saved per rep per week, faster deal cycles, cleaner CRM data; G2 Leader (AI Sales Assistant), Highest User Adoption, Best Meets Requirements badges; SOC 2, GDPR, OpenAI Zero Retention Data Policy compliance (source: https://www.momentum.io/momentum-faq).
- **Tone / category framing:** "AI Revenue Orchestration Platform" / "Enterprise Listening for Your Entire GTM Team" — explicitly positions itself as the orchestration layer across the GTM stack, not a CI replacement, not a CRM, not an enablement tool.
