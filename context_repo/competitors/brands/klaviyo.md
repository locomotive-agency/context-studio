## Summary

Klaviyo is a B2C CRM and AI marketing-automation platform built for ecommerce / DTC brands and B2C marketers — its primary surfaces are email, SMS, RCS, WhatsApp, mobile push, and AI-driven customer service for shoppers. It is tangential, not directly overlapping, with ZoomInfo: their ICPs (B2C lifecycle marketers, retail / restaurant / wellness operators) do not intersect ZoomInfo's B2B sales, RevOps, ABM, or GTM-engineer audiences. The closest crossover is conceptual — Klaviyo Data Platform (KDP) plays a CDP role parallel in shape to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, and Klaviyo ships an MCP Server analogous to ZoomInfo's MCP — but the underlying data is first-party consumer data, not B2B account/contact intelligence. No public Klaviyo-vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced from Klaviyo's own properties.

## Product Offerings

### Klaviyo Marketing (marketing automation)

> **Wiki:** [[klaviyo--marketing]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered marketing automation that delivers email, SMS, WhatsApp, RCS, mobile push, social, and reviews from a single B2C CRM, triggered off real-time customer behavior.
- **Key features:**
  - Email marketing with drag-and-drop editor and templates
  - SMS, RCS, WhatsApp, and mobile push channels
  - Pre-built and custom flows (welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase)
  - Segmentation engine using behavioral, transactional, and engagement signals
  - AI-powered subject line generator and content personalization
  - Reviews and social marketing modules
  - Composer (Beta): AI agent that builds full on-brand campaigns from a prompt
- **Source URL:** https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/marketing-automation

### Klaviyo Data Platform (KDP) — built-in CDP

> **Wiki:** [[klaviyo--data-platform]]

- **What it does:** Unifies behavioral, transactional, and engagement data into a single real-time customer profile with identity resolution and predictive AI baked in, then activates that data inside Klaviyo Marketing and Service without external syncs.
- **Key features:**
  - Unified customer profiles with lifetime data retention
  - 350+ pre-built integrations and APIs for data ingestion
  - Identity resolution, identity stitching, probabilistic matching
  - Predictive AI: churn risk, predicted next-order date, customer lifetime value (CLV)
  - Custom objects (households, subscriptions, stores) for flexible data modeling
  - Advanced KDP add-on: data warehouse syncing (e.g., Snowflake), data transformation, custom monitors
  - ISO 27001 certified, SOC 2 Type 2 audited; CCPA / GDPR compliant
- **Source URL:** https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/customer-data-platform

### Klaviyo AI (K:AI)

> **Wiki:** [[klaviyo--ai]]

- **What it does:** Suite of AI agents and 40+ AI features inside Klaviyo — Marketing Agent generates campaigns, Customer Agent resolves support and sells 24/7, plus image Remix, channel affinity, and an MCP Server for agentic integrations.
- **Key features:**
  - Marketing Agent — generates on-brand campaigns, flows, and forms from a website URL
  - Customer Agent — resolves 65% of customer questions autonomously and recommends products in chat
  - Composer (Beta) — prompt-to-campaign content agent
  - Image Remix — natural-language image editing inside the Klaviyo editor
  - Channel affinity — picks the channel each profile is most likely to engage on
  - Klaviyo MCP Server (developer-facing) plus ChatGPT app and Claude Connector
- **Source URL:** https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/ai

### Klaviyo Service (K:Service)

> **Wiki:** [[klaviyo--service]]

- **What it does:** AI-first customer service product line — Customer Hub (self-service portal), Helpdesk (ticketing / inbox), and Customer Agent — all sharing the same customer profile as marketing.
- **Key features:**
  - Customer Hub: self-service portal with order tracking, account management, "1-click Buy again"
  - Helpdesk: shared inbox, ticket management, personalized macros, multi-agent support
  - Customer Agent for autonomous resolution and product recommendations across web chat, SMS, email (WhatsApp coming soon)
  - Seamless live-agent handoff with full conversation context
  - Pre / post-purchase resolutions: order status, returns, subscriptions
- **Source URL:** https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/customer-service

### Email marketing (channel product)

> **Wiki:** [[klaviyo--email-marketing]]

- **What it does:** Email marketing channel inside Klaviyo Marketing — personalized campaigns triggered from KDP profile data with templates, AI assist, and ecommerce-native automations.
- **Key features:**
  - Drag-and-drop email editor with templates
  - Behavioral, transactional, and engagement-based personalization
  - Pre-built ecommerce flows (abandoned cart, browse abandonment, post-purchase, win-back)
  - AI for email marketing (subject lines, content, send-time optimization)
  - Built-in deliverability tooling and benchmarks
- **Source URL:** https://www.klaviyo.com/products/email-marketing

### SMS marketing (channel product)

> **Wiki:** [[klaviyo--sms-marketing]]

- **What it does:** Business SMS / bulk SMS sending tied to the same customer profile as email, with mobile-message credits, two-way conversations, and AI-assisted copy.
- **Key features:**
  - Bulk SMS plus two-way conversational SMS
  - Mobile messaging credits (carrier-fee-transparent pricing model)
  - Compliance tooling for SMS opt-in / opt-out
  - AI for SMS marketing (copy generation)
  - Native expansion to RCS for Business
- **Source URL:** https://www.klaviyo.com/products/sms-marketing

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Positions itself as "the autonomous B2C CRM" — system of record + system of action purpose-built for B2C / ecommerce, not a general CRM (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/).
- CDP is built-in (KDP), not a separate product or third-party stitch — unifies data and activates it inside the same UI for marketing and service (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/customer-data-platform).
- AI agents (Marketing Agent, Customer Agent, Composer) span both marketing creation and customer service resolution in one platform; Customer Agent claims 65% autonomous resolution (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/ai).
- Deep ecommerce-native integrations with Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, WooCommerce, plus 350+ marketplace apps and an MCP server for agentic workflows (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/).
- Scale claims: 8B customer profiles, 1.6B average API calls/day, 3.7B average events/day; 193,000+ brands in 100 countries (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/).
- Free tier with full functionality (≤250 profiles, ≤500 emails/mo, 150 mobile credits/mo) — strong SMB / startup acquisition motion (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/pricing).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No direct head-to-head product overlap with ZoomInfo's primary product lines._ Klaviyo does not sell B2B contact data, sales intelligence, ABM, intent data, conversation intelligence, or GTM seller tooling. (See `manual_review:icp_mismatch` flag.)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Klaviyo Data Platform (KDP) vs ZoomInfo GTM Context Graph / Data (Pillar 1)** — Klaviyo's KDP unifies behavioral, transactional, and engagement signals into one customer profile with identity resolution; conceptually parallel in shape to ZoomInfo's **GTM Context Graph** fusing first-party CRM/conversation/behavioral data, but Klaviyo's profile graph is consumer-level and ZoomInfo's is B2B account/contact level (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/customer-data-platform).
  - **Klaviyo Marketing + KDP vs ZoomInfo GTM Studio** — both give marketers a front-end to build segments / audiences and orchestrate multi-channel campaigns from unified profile data, but Klaviyo's audience is B2C consumers and ZoomInfo **GTM Studio** targets B2B accounts/contacts (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/marketing-automation).
  - **Klaviyo APIs + MCP Server vs ZoomInfo APIs & MCP** — Klaviyo ships an MCP Server (developers.klaviyo.com/en/docs/klaviyo_mcp_server), a ChatGPT app, and a Claude Connector — same agentic-ecosystem direction as ZoomInfo's **APIs & MCP** at mcp.zoominfo.com/mcp, applied to ecommerce/B2C agents instead of GTM agents (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/).
- **No overlap:**
  - B2B contact and company data (500M contacts, 100M companies, phone/email/technographics) — Klaviyo's data layer is the customer's own first-party shopper data only.
  - **GTM Workspace** / sales prospecting front-end and sales-engagement workflows for AEs/SDRs — Klaviyo has no seller-facing UI, no prospecting, no outbound sales workflow.
  - ABM platforms / intent data / RevOps data quality and routing — Klaviyo never references accounts, intent, or RevOps; ICP is B2C marketers and ecommerce operators.
  - Conversation intelligence (**Chorus**) — Klaviyo's Customer Agent handles shopper chat, but does not record or analyze sales calls.
  - B2C consumer marketing channels (email, SMS, RCS, WhatsApp, mobile push, web chat to shoppers) — ZoomInfo does not sell into the B2C/ecommerce marketing-automation category; Klaviyo's core surface area sits entirely outside ZoomInfo's market.
  - AI-powered B2C customer service (Customer Hub, Helpdesk, Customer Agent for shoppers) — ZoomInfo does not offer customer service tooling or shopper self-service.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Klaviyo's footer "Why Klaviyo" comparison links target Mailchimp, Postscript, Attentive, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and Listrak — not ZoomInfo. (See `vs_brand_missing` flag.)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The autonomous B2C CRM — AI marketing & service to grow relationships." Unifies customer data and automates email, SMS, RCS, WhatsApp, and mobile push to drive personalized, revenue-generating campaigns (source: https://www.klaviyo.com/).
- **Recurring proof points:** 193,000+ brands in 100 countries; 8B customer profiles, 1.6B avg API calls/day, 3.7B avg events/day; Daily Harvest (-18% tech costs), Marc Fisher (-20% time to execute), Dollar Shave Club (-30% TCO), Tatcha (+20% YoY ecommerce revenue), Princess Polly (2.8X global revenue growth), ThirdLove (40k+ Customer Hub self-service interactions, 15x SMS ROI), 2xist (56.1x platform ROI Q1); customer logos include CorePower Yoga, Glossier, Mattel, The Body Shop, True Food Kitchen, Unilever, Vans, Vuori; G2 Leader Mid-Market Winter 2026, Capterra Shortlist, Software Advice Front Runners 2026.
- **Tone / category framing:** Defines the category as the "autonomous B2C CRM" — explicitly differentiates from general CRMs, point-solution ESPs, and traditional CDPs by combining system-of-record + system-of-action for B2C; consistently leads with "B2C" to anchor the ICP.
