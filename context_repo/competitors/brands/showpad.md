## Summary

Showpad is an AI-native revenue effectiveness / sales enablement platform sold to enablement, revenue, and marketing leaders at field-sales-driven enterprises in regulated, physical-product industries (medical devices, manufacturing, chemicals, CPG). Its four-pillar suite — Sales Readiness, Content Management, Buyer Engagement, and Analytics & Insights — is unified by GenieAI, an AI core grounded in customer-approved content via a Data + Trust governance layer (source: https://www.showpad.com/platform-overview). For ZoomInfo, the material overlap is narrow but real: Showpad's Field Meeting AI, Roleplay AI, and Genie Assistant compete with Chorus's conversation/coaching role and the GTM Workspace seller surface, while Showpad ships zero third-party B2B data, intent, or technographic intelligence and therefore does not touch ZoomInfo's Data foundation, GTM Context Graph, or APIs/MCP layer.

## Product Offerings

### Sales Readiness

> **Wiki:** [[showpad--sales-readiness]]

- **What it does:** Onboards, trains, and coaches sellers via courses, knowledge checks/certifications, and AI roleplay so they show up prepared for buyer conversations.
- **Key features:**
  - Training Library and Courses
  - Roleplay AI sales simulator that adapts to objections
  - Knowledge checks and certifications
  - My Team Manager hub for sales managers
  - Authoring AI for one-click voice narration, captions, notes, and translation
- **Source URL:** https://www.showpad.com/platform-overview

### Content Management

> **Wiki:** [[showpad--content-management]]

- **What it does:** Centralizes, governs, and surfaces marketing-approved sales content (spec sheets, pricing, proof points) across mobile, offline, and browser surfaces.
- **Key features:**
  - Content Library, Pages, Collections
  - AI-Powered content governance
  - AI-Powered Search and AI-Powered Asset FAQs
  - AI-Powered Page Builder
  - Compliance-approved materials with versioning
  - Mobile, offline, and browser access
- **Source URL:** https://www.showpad.com/platform-overview

### Buyer Engagement

> **Wiki:** [[showpad--buyer-engagement]]

- **What it does:** Provides digital sales rooms (Shared Spaces) and field-sales tooling so buying committees can collaborate with sellers and stakeholders can see content engagement.
- **Key features:**
  - Shared Spaces (digital sales rooms) with two-way buyer collaboration
  - Mutual Action Plans
  - Buyer Uploads
  - Video messages
  - 3D and AR asset hosting for product demos
  - Field Meeting AI converts in-person meetings into CRM notes and follow-ups
  - Shared Space templates
- **Source URL:** https://www.showpad.com/buyer-engagement

### Analytics & Insights

> **Wiki:** [[showpad--analytics-insights]]

- **What it does:** Surfaces patterns across content usage, learning completion, and buyer behavior to attribute revenue impact and forecast more reliably.
- **Key features:**
  - Question-Based Analytics
  - Analytics AI
  - Engagement analytics tying seller and buyer activity to revenue
  - Shared Spaces Management Dashboard
  - CRM auto-logs of buyer signals
- **Source URL:** https://www.showpad.com/platform-overview

### GenieAI

> **Wiki:** [[showpad--genieai]]

- **What it does:** Showpad's AI core/layer built on the customer's proprietary content with a Data + Trust governance layer; powers Genie Assistant, Showpad Agents, Roleplay AI, Authoring AI, and Field Meeting AI.
- **Key features:**
  - Genie Assistant (chat answers from verified content, AI-drafted emails, next steps)
  - Showpad Agents — out-of-the-box and custom AI agents for compliance checks, deal updates, content creation
  - Vision Intelligence
  - Voice (Expert tier)
  - Custom agents (Expert tier)
  - Data + Trust Layer for explainable, auditable, source-backed AI
- **Source URL:** https://www.showpad.com/genieai

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) and the GTM Workspace seller surface vs. Showpad Sales Readiness + GenieAI:** Showpad Field Meeting AI "capture[s] in-person conversations and meeting notes as CRM data," Roleplay AI "creates confident sellers with a sales simulator that responds like real buyers," and Genie Assistant gives sellers "instant answers from verified content, AI-drafted emails and briefs, and next steps for every deal — without ever leaving Showpad." That is the same conversation-capture + coaching + seller-execution pattern Chorus and the GTM Workspace deliver, but driven from the customer's internal content rather than ZoomInfo's external GTM data (source: https://www.showpad.com/genieai).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio vs. Showpad Content Management + Buyer Engagement:** Showpad's content engine, Shared Spaces (digital sales rooms), Mutual Action Plans, and engagement analytics support marketing-to-sales handoff and buying-committee orchestration — adjacent to GTM Studio's audience-building/play orchestration, but from the sales-content/enablement side rather than the demand-gen side (source: https://www.showpad.com/buyer-engagement).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations vs. Showpad CRM auto-logs and Field Meeting AI:** Showpad's Salesforce/Dynamics integrations "automatically log every buyer interaction — including email opens, views, and shares — directly to the corresponding Contact, Lead, or Opportunity record." Adjacent to Operations' CRM-data role but for engagement signals rather than firmographic data (source: https://www.showpad.com/buyer-engagement).
- **No overlap:**
  - Showpad ships zero third-party B2B contact, company, intent, or technographic data — no equivalent to ZoomInfo's Data (Pillar 1) (500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ phones, 200M+ emails) or to the GTM Context Graph's third-party + first-party fusion. Showpad's AI is grounded entirely in customer-approved first-party content via the Data + Trust Layer (sources: https://www.showpad.com/platform-overview/integrations, https://www.showpad.com/genieai).
  - Showpad has an open API + SDK and Developer Portal but no MCP server or AI-agent-builder ecosystem listing surfaced on reviewed pages — no equivalent to ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP lane (source: https://www.showpad.com/platform-overview/integrations).
  - Conversely, Showpad's 3D/AR asset hosting and field-meeting capture for physical-product industries is a category ZoomInfo does not address (source: https://www.showpad.com/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The G2 head-to-head URL (chorus-by-zoominfo-vs-showpad-eos) returned HTTP 403; Showpad's footer instead promotes "Showpad vs. Seismic" and "Showpad vs. Highspot," indicating Seismic and Highspot — not ZoomInfo — are the head-to-head competitors Showpad pitches against.
