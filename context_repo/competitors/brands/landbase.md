# Landbase

## Sub-products

- [[landbase--gtm2-omni|Landbase GTM-2 Omni]] — competes with GTM Workspace

## Summary

Landbase is an agentic-AI Go-to-Market platform — pitched as "the first AI-Native Compound Startup in the GTM space" — that sells RevOps, Sales, Marketing, and Growth teams a multi-agent system (GTM-2 Omni) running on a 300M-contact / 24M-company B2B database with 1,500+ enrichment fields. The crossover with ZoomInfo is direct and broad: Landbase's B2B Database competes with ZoomInfo's Data foundation, Agentic Search and the RevOps solution overlap GTM Studio's natural-language audience build, the Sales solution overlaps GTM Workspace's prioritized-accounts wedge, and Signals overlap the GTM Context Graph's intent reasoning layer. Landbase explicitly positions against ZoomInfo on its own blog ("Unlike ZoomInfo's static database approach, Landbase creates genuine autonomous systems that continuously learn and optimize ... 4-7x higher conversion rates and 80% cost reduction"). Pricing is gated — only plan names (Free / Premium / Enterprise) are public.

## Product Offerings

### GTM-2 Omni (multi-agent GTM platform)

- **What it does:** Multi-agent system that orchestrates the full GTM workflow autonomously — campaigns launch in minutes, costs drop ~80%, and conversion rates lift 4-7x versus traditional approaches.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-agent architecture (Strategy, Research, SDR, RevOps, IT Manager agents)
  - Trained on 50M+ GTM campaigns and 175M+ sales conversations
  - Campaigns launch in minutes instead of months
  - Up to 80% reduction in operational costs and 4-7x conversion lift
  - GTM-2 Omni next-generation model (replaces GTM-1 Omni) launched Oct 2025
  - Recommends campaign strategies for human approval (human-in-the-loop)
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/about

### Agentic Search

- **What it does:** Natural-language B2B audience builder — describe your ICP in plain language and Landbase returns a verified, scored, CRM-ready account list.
- **Key features:**
  - Natural-language ICP queries (no filters or dropdowns)
  - Translates prompts into structured qualification criteria
  - Matches across 300M+ contacts and 1,500+ enrichment fields
  - Queries firmographic, technographic, intent, hiring, and live web sources simultaneously
  - Cross-references and dedupes records across sources
  - Exports directly to HubSpot, Salesforce, or CSV with verified contacts attached
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/products/agentic-search

### B2B Database

- **What it does:** Continuously verified B2B database of 300M+ contacts and 24M+ companies with 1,500+ enrichment fields per account, including firmographic, technographic, and intent data.
- **Key features:**
  - 300M+ verified contacts with verified emails and direct dials
  - 85M+ direct dial phone numbers
  - 24M+ company records with firmographic and technographic data
  - 1,500+ enrichment fields including funding, hiring, tech stack, intent
  - 97%+ deliverability rate; continuous re-verification using live signals
  - Stale records flagged and refreshed before reaching the user
  - Searchable via Agentic Search (natural language)
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/products/b2b-database

### Lookalikes

- **What it does:** Find companies similar to your best customers using AI-driven lookalike modeling, called out in the homepage capabilities and product nav.
- **Key features:**
  - Lookalike analysis built off customer data and web signals
  - Surfaces high-fit accounts not findable manually
  - 5x TAM expansion claim on the homepage
  - Lives in the Landbase product nav alongside Agentic Search and Signals
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/

### Signals

- **What it does:** Real-time buyer-intent and growth-signal detection — funding, hiring, tech adoption, leadership changes, news, and social activity.
- **Key features:**
  - Funding-round tracking and growth signals
  - Hiring trend detection (key roles, headcount changes)
  - Tech stack adoption and usage patterns
  - Live web enrichment from news and press releases
  - Continuous signal refresh feeding scoring and prioritization
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/products/b2b-database

### AI Qualification

- **What it does:** AI agents verify and score accounts automatically against custom fit questions, signals, and attribute checks — replacing manual list scrubbing.
- **Key features:**
  - Custom fit-question evaluation per account
  - Multi-criteria checks (funding, headcount, CRM stack, geo, hiring signals)
  - Tier-1/Tier-2/Tier-3 scoring output, decision-ready
  - 50% fit-accuracy lift claim on homepage
  - Reasoning surfaced per account ("why this account qualifies")
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/

### AI Agents (Research Agent)

- **What it does:** Specialized research agents that gather hard-to-find market insights, perform account research, and feed downstream qualification and outreach.
- **Key features:**
  - Listed in product nav as "AI Agents — Gather hard-to-find market insights"
  - Research Agent footer link points to /products/research-agent
  - Feeds Strategy / SDR / RevOps agents in the multi-agent system
  - Continuous monitoring and re-evaluation of accounts and signals
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/products/b2b-database

### Sales solution (account prioritization & outreach prep)

- **What it does:** Sales-team workflow surface that delivers prioritized, qualified accounts with full context (hiring, funding, tech, decision makers) and verified contacts ready for sequencing.
- **Key features:**
  - Prioritized target accounts ranked by fit and signals
  - Qualification reasons surfaced per account
  - Account context: hiring, funding, tech stack, decision makers
  - Verified emails and phone numbers delivered to CRM or sequencing tools
  - No manual enrichment dependency
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/solutions/sales

### RevOps solution (TAM mapping, qualification, CRM delivery)

- **What it does:** RevOps system surface for defining ICP in natural language, mapping full TAM, qualifying every account, and delivering enriched data to CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Natural-language ICP definition shared across teams
  - Full TAM coverage view (in-CRM vs discovered vs coverage gap)
  - Continuous TAM updates as the market evolves
  - Auto-qualification with consistent criteria across teams
  - Qualified-and-enriched CRM sync (HubSpot, Salesforce)
  - Eliminates manual data reconciliation
- **Source URL:** https://www.landbase.com/solutions/revops

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation:** Landbase B2B Database (300M+ contacts / 24M+ companies / 85M+ direct dials / 1,500+ enrichment fields / 97%+ deliverability) competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Data foundation pillar (500M contacts / 100M companies / 135M phones / 95% accuracy). Same buyers, same deliverable. (source: https://www.landbase.com/products/b2b-database)
  - **GTM Studio (vs Agentic Search + RevOps solution):** Both pitch natural-language audience building and TAM/ICP operationalization for RevOps. Agentic Search lets users "describe your ICP in plain language" and returns verified accounts ready for CRM — the same wedge as GTM Studio's marketer/RevOps/GTM-engineer front-end. (source: https://www.landbase.com/products/agentic-search, https://www.landbase.com/solutions/revops)
  - **GTM Workspace (vs Sales solution + GTM-2 Omni):** Sales solution surfaces prioritized target accounts ranked by fit and signals, qualification reasons, account context (hiring, funding, tech, decision makers), and verified contacts ready for sequencing — the same prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted outreach + execution surface as GTM Workspace. (source: https://www.landbase.com/solutions/sales)
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (vs RevOps solution):** RevOps page pitches "Move from market to pipeline without manual work" with auto-qualified accounts synced to HubSpot/Salesforce, continuous data freshness, and enrichment across 1,500+ fields — same CRM data-quality + routing use case. (source: https://www.landbase.com/solutions/revops)
  - **GTM Context Graph (vs Signals + multi-source search):** Signals product cross-references firmographic, technographic, intent, hiring, leadership-change, news, and social signals to score and prioritize accounts; the company-data page leads with "Static contact lists are a relic. Today's top sellers use dynamic company data as their maps, identifying critical buying signals like new hires or competitive changes" — explicit reasoning-on-signals overlap with the GTM Context Graph. (source: https://www.landbase.com/company-data, https://www.landbase.com/products/b2b-database)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Sales engagement category (Outreach / Salesloft territory ZoomInfo partners with):** Landbase runs end-to-end outbound including domain buying, email authentication, DMARC, warmup, and multivariate testing in-platform — overlapping the engagement category ZoomInfo treats as a partner ecosystem (Martal case study lists 13 tools replaced including "email sending, SMTP deliverability, list validation, email warmup, auto-dialing"). (source: https://www.landbase.com/martal-case-study)
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) — training-data adjacency:** Landbase's GTM-2 Omni is trained on 175M+ sales conversations as part of the Applied AI Lab — touches the conversation-intelligence training-data territory but is not a CI product itself. (source: https://www.landbase.com/about)
- **No overlap:**
  - **Email infrastructure (domain buying + DMARC + warmup):** Landbase handles outbound infrastructure setup as a built-in feature; ZoomInfo's reference docs do not list this as a ZoomInfo product surface. (source: https://www.landbase.com/martal-case-study)
  - **AI-Native Compound Startup positioning replacing 15+ vendors:** Landbase's stated GTM motion is to consolidate the entire outbound stack (data + sequencing + CRM + deliverability) into one platform — beyond ZoomInfo's all-in-one GTM Platform scope which still partners on engagement. (source: https://www.landbase.com/about)
  - **Agency Network channel:** Landbase Agency Network partners (e.g., Martal Group) deliver outbound services to clients on Landbase — explicit agency channel motion not mirrored in ZoomInfo's reference docs. (source: https://www.landbase.com/martal-case-study)
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Landbase's own blog post "8 Best ZoomInfo Alternatives for B2B Sales Intelligence in 2026" frames the buy directly: _"Unlike ZoomInfo's static database approach, Landbase creates genuine autonomous systems that continuously learn and optimize... ZoomInfo provides contact information requiring manual outreach, Landbase's agentic AI identifies prospects autonomously, crafts personalized messages, and manages multi-channel campaigns 24/7. This results in 4-7x higher conversion rates and 80% cost reduction compared to traditional database tools"_ (source: https://www.landbase.com/blog/zoominfo-alternatives). The post lists ZoomInfo limitations as "data accuracy issues, limited AI automation, complex interface, hidden costs, poor customer service" and ranks Landbase as the #1 ZoomInfo alternative.
