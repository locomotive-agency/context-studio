## Summary

Melissa is a 40-year-old data-quality, address-verification, and identity-verification (eIDV / KYC) vendor that sells to data/IT, RevOps, compliance, direct-mail, and e-commerce buyers across SMB through enterprise. It is tangential to ZoomInfo: the only direct overlap is with ZoomInfo Operations on CRM data hygiene (Salesforce / Microsoft Dynamics cleansing, dedupe, and append), while the bulk of Melissa's catalog — postal/CASS/NCOA mailing tools, KYC/AML identity verification, property/hazard data, and B2C demographics — sits outside ZoomInfo's GTM Intelligence category. Melissa publishes transparent per-API and per-credit pricing (PAYG from $40 and subscriptions from $5,145–$16,000+ per year), a posture ZoomInfo does not match. It does not surface a head-to-head ZoomInfo comparison page on its own domain.

## Product Offerings

### Address Verification (Global Address Verification, US Address Verification, Express Entry / Address Autocomplete)

> **Wiki:** [[melissa--address-verification]]

- **What it does:** Verifies, corrects, standardizes, and autocompletes U.S., Canadian, and international postal addresses at point of entry or in batch.
- **Key features:**
  - USPS CASS & DPV Certified and Canada Post SERP Certified
  - Coverage in 240+ countries and territories
  - Address autocomplete (Express Entry) for forms / checkout
  - Batch address cleansing for entire lists
  - Address Verification APIs to embed validation in apps
  - GovCloud Address Suite (FedRAMP authorized) for U.S. public sector
- **Source URL:** https://www.melissa.com/data-quality

### Data Quality Suite / Personator Consumer (contact data hygiene)

> **Wiki:** [[melissa--data-quality-suite]]

- **What it does:** End-to-end profile, cleanse, verify, match/dedupe, and enrich tooling for names, addresses, phones, and emails — the "full spectrum" DQ workflow.
- **Key features:**
  - Profiling — analyze data to track quality over time
  - Cleansing & standardization with machine learning + custom rules
  - Name Verification (parse / standardize first & last names)
  - Phone Verification (mobile + landline, U.S. + international)
  - Email Verification (validate before send)
  - MatchUp (dedupe) — combine duplicate records into one golden record
  - Personator Consumer Cloud API: address verification + name-to-address matching + change-of-address ($12,300/yr for 1M records)
- **Source URL:** https://www.melissa.com/data-quality

### Personator Identity (eIDV / KYC Suite)

> **Wiki:** [[melissa--personator-identity]]

- **What it does:** Electronic identity verification suite for KYC/AML compliance, fraud prevention, and customer onboarding across 250 countries.
- **Key features:**
  - Identity, ID & document verification
  - Mobile Identity Verification via Mobile Network Operator (MNO) sources in 14+ countries
  - PEP, Watchlist & Sanctions screening
  - SSN Name Match (U.S.)
  - Age Verification (date-of-birth checks for age-restricted commerce)
  - Business Verification (KYB)
  - OFAC / Watchlist screening via Personator Cloud
- **Source URL:** https://www.melissa.com/identity-verification

### Data Enhancement & Appends

> **Wiki:** [[melissa--data-enhancement]]

- **What it does:** Enriches consumer and business records with demographic, firmographic, geographic, and property attributes plus contact-record completion.
- **Key features:**
  - Consumer Demographic Data (financial, household, lifestyle)
  - Business Firmographic Data — industry, revenue, location, sales volume on 25M+ U.S. businesses (BusinessCoder Cloud API)
  - U.S. Property & Mortgage Data — 400+ fields across 140M+ U.S. properties
  - Property Hazard Risk (flood, wildfire, hurricane, earthquake)
  - Geocoding / Reverse Geocoding (lat/long for U.S. + international)
  - IP Location (geolocation from IP)
  - Contact Record Completion (append missing name / phone / email / postal address)
  - Consumer record coverage of 2.1B records
- **Source URL:** https://www.melissa.com/data-enhancement

### Unison (Customer Data Validation Platform)

> **Wiki:** [[melissa--unison]]

- **What it does:** No-code data quality platform to validate, cleanse, and maintain customer data continuously across systems.
- **Key features:**
  - No-programming / no-code DQ workflows
  - Customer Data Management — "best-in-class data quality in one platform"
  - Continuous DQ maintenance vs. one-time cleanse
- **Source URL:** https://www.melissa.com/data-quality

### Mailing Tools (Mailers Online, Listware, NCOA / SmartMover)

> **Wiki:** [[melissa--mailing-tools]]

- **What it does:** USPS- and Canada-Post-certified mailing tools for postal presort discounts, change-of-address processing, and self-service list cleaning.
- **Key features:**
  - Mailers Online — cloud postal presort processing
  - Listware — Excel / Online self-service data cleansing (Microsoft Excel, Microsoft 365, Google Sheets)
  - NCOALink / SmartMover — USPS 48-month change-of-address dataset ($2,205/yr for 1M records)
  - DSF² and PAVE-Certified for postage discounts
  - Suppression (deceased, etc.)
- **Source URL:** https://www.melissa.com/data-quality

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** ZoomInfo Operations (data quality / hygiene / routing) overlaps with Melissa's Data Quality Suite + Personator Consumer + MatchUp (dedupe) + Clean Suite for Salesforce / Dynamics. Melissa positions a "full spectrum" workflow — profile, cleanse, verify, match/dedupe, enrich — for names, addresses, phones, and emails inside Salesforce and Dynamics, the same hygiene workload ZoomInfo Operations targets (source: https://www.melissa.com/data-quality).
- **Adjacent overlap:** ZoomInfo's Data foundation (Pillar 1) (B2B contacts, firmographics, technographics) is touched lightly by Melissa's BusinessCoder Cloud API + Business Firmographics + Contact Record Completion. Melissa offers firmographics on 25M+ U.S. businesses and contact-record completion (append missing name / phone / email / postal address). This is data-append for hygiene, not B2B prospecting / intent — coverage scale and intent signals are far smaller than ZoomInfo's 100M companies / 500M contacts / intent (source: https://www.melissa.com/data-enhancement).
- **No overlap:**
  - Postal / direct-mail (CASS, DPV, NCOA, DSF², MCOA/PCOA, presort) — Melissa has a substantial mailing-tools line (Mailers Online, NCOALink, SmartMover, deceased suppression) that ZoomInfo does not address (source: https://www.melissa.com/data-quality).
  - Identity verification / KYC / AML / age verification / sanctions screening — Melissa sells a full eIDV suite (Personator Identity, mobile-network-operator identity, PEP/watchlist/sanctions, SSN name match, age verification, KYB). ZoomInfo does not sell into KYC/AML (source: https://www.melissa.com/identity-verification).
  - Property / mortgage / hazard-risk and geospatial — Melissa's Property Cloud API, Property Hazard Risk, parcel-boundary shapefiles, and geocoding services target real-estate, insurance, GIS, and logistics buyers, outside ZoomInfo's GTM scope (source: https://www.melissa.com/data-enhancement).
  - Consumer / B2C data and demographics — Melissa explicitly markets consumer demographic enrichment for B2C marketers on 2.1B consumer records; ZoomInfo is B2B-only (source: https://www.melissa.com/data-enhancement).
  - GTM Workspace / GTM Studio (seller and marketer/RevOps execution surfaces) — Melissa has no seller workflow, no AI-drafted outreach, no play orchestration, no conversation intelligence (source: https://www.melissa.com/).
