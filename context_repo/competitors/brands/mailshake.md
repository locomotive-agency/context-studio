## Summary

Mailshake is a cold email outreach and sales engagement tool aimed at SMB sales teams, SDRs, and outbound agencies, sold per-user per-month from $29 to $99 with a public pricing table. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace sequencing surface and the legacy ZoomInfo Sales/Engage cadence functionality, but does not attempt to compete on contact data, intent signals, GTM Context Graph reasoning, or marketer/RevOps tooling. Its only data play is a thin Data Finder add-on at $19/mo with 50–2,500 credits per plan. The vendor's own ZoomInfo-alternatives blog frames ZoomInfo as too expensive (~$250/user/mo) and pitches Mailshake as a low-cost outbound execution layer, not as a data substitute.

## Product Offerings

### Mailshake Sales Engagement Platform (core product)

> **Wiki:** [[mailshake--sales-engagement]]

- **What it does:** Multi-channel outbound sales engagement tool that lets reps build sequences combining cold email, LinkedIn, and phone in a single dashboard.
- **Key features:**
  - Automated email sequences with mail merge personalization
  - Lead Catcher unified reply inbox
  - LinkedIn Automation (messages, profile views, connection requests)
  - Multi-channel outreach across email, phone, and social
  - Pipeline-focused analytics (opens, replies, clicks, lead drivers)
  - SHAKEspeare AI email writer for auto-generated copy
- **Source URL:** https://mailshake.com/

### Mail Merge

> **Wiki:** [[mailshake--mail-merge]]

- **What it does:** Sends one customized email to an entire group via field replacement tags, delivered as 1-1 emails rather than mass marketing blasts.
- **Key features:**
  - CSV-based recipient upload with per-column field replacement
  - Field replacement tags (first name, company, etc.) for per-recipient personalization
  - Manual override to add personalization to individual recipients before launch
  - Scheduled delivery and automated follow-ups based on customizable triggers
  - Native Salesforce and HubSpot integrations plus Zapier
- **Source URL:** https://mailshake.com/features/mail-merge

### Email Deliverability Suite

> **Wiki:** [[mailshake--email-deliverability]]

- **What it does:** Built-in deliverability tooling included with every plan to keep cold outreach landing in the inbox rather than spam.
- **Key features:**
  - Email warm up via SMTP — automated opens, replies, and spam-folder removal
  - Email validation credits to remove high-bounce / spam-trap addresses
  - Email rotation across multiple sending addresses to raise daily volume safely
  - Ready-to-use mailboxes with SPF / MX / DKIM pre-configured
  - In-app spam-score and copy analyzer
  - Email domain setup assistant
- **Source URL:** https://mailshake.com/features/email-deliverability/

### Phone Dialer

> **Wiki:** [[mailshake--phone-dialer]]

- **What it does:** Built-in browser-based VOIP dialer that lets reps insert cold calls into the same multi-channel cadence as email and social tasks.
- **Key features:**
  - One-click click-to-call from browser, task view, or prospect view
  - Power Dialer for auto-advancing through call queues
  - In-call notes and post-call task creation
  - Mix calls with email and social steps in a single cadence
  - Team-level dialer/email/social activity dashboard
  - One phone number per team member
- **Source URL:** https://mailshake.com/features/dialer

### Data Finder

> **Wiki:** [[mailshake--data-finder]]

- **What it does:** Add-on prospect database that lets users find business contact emails by role, location, or company and add them to Mailshake campaigns.
- **Key features:**
  - Filter prospects by role, location, or company
  - 50 free Data Finder credits on Starter / Email Outreach; 2,500 on Sales Engagement
  - Direct push of found prospects into a Mailshake campaign
  - Available as a $19/mo add-on on top of base plans
- **Source URL:** https://mailshake.com/pricing/

- Concierge customer success: 1-on-1 onboarding, weekly live training, Cold Email Masterclass content, personalized campaign reviews (source: https://mailshake.com/use-cases/sales-managers).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Mailshake's core sales engagement platform overlaps with ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace sequencing surface and the legacy ZoomInfo Sales/Engage sequencer — multi-channel cadences across email, phone, and LinkedIn with reply tracking and analytics (source: https://mailshake.com/use-cases/sales/, https://mailshake.com/pricing/). The Sales Engagement plan adds Power Dialer and LinkedIn automation, hitting the same cadence ground ZoomInfo's classic Engage offered.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Mailshake's Data Finder add-on ($19/mo, 50–2,500 credits depending on plan) is a thin contact-lookup feature, not a comparable B2B database to ZoomInfo's Data (Pillar 1) foundation. Mailshake explicitly markets it as "better than ZoomInfo" for users new to cold email but does not claim equivalent scale (source: https://mailshake.com/blog/best-zoominfo-alternatives/).
- **No overlap:** Mailshake sells nothing equivalent to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph (intent / signals / reasoning), GTM Studio (marketer / RevOps / GTM-engineer surface, ABM orchestration), APIs & MCP / agent ecosystem, Chorus conversation intelligence, or ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality and routing). Mailshake is pure outbound execution (source: https://mailshake.com/, https://mailshake.com/features/dialer).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No public `/vs/zoominfo` page exists, but Mailshake's own listicle frames ZoomInfo as overpriced: "ZoomInfo is an excellent solution for gathering sales intelligence data... But there's one big problem. The average monthly fee for one person is $250. That's a risky investment—even for well-established firms." (source: https://mailshake.com/blog/best-zoominfo-alternatives/). The ICP comparison table: shared = Sales (AEs/SDRs) and Marketing; competitor-only = Agency Owners; ZoomInfo-only = Account Management / CS, RevOps / GTM Engineers, and Developers / AI agent builders.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Simple, fast-to-deploy cold email and sales engagement software "purpose-built for deliverability" so reps can send personalized multi-channel sequences (email + phone + LinkedIn) the same day they sign up.
- **Recurring proof points:** 100,000+ sales teams (pricing page) / 60,000+ users (LinkedIn); G2 High Performer Winter 2025 + G2 Small Business Winter 2025 badges; G2 4.7/5 across 252+ reviews per third-party comparisons; named customer outcomes — SaaSMQL ($1M+ revenue), Virtuance (83% open rate), BrandStar (10x email volume), New Reach Marketing ($800K).
- **Tone / category framing:** Self-described as "sales engagement software for modern sales teams" and "AI Powered Sales Engagement & B2B Lead Platform" — leans heavily on cold-email and outbound vocabulary rather than GTM platform language.
