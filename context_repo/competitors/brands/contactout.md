## Summary

ContactOut is a B2B contact-data vendor — a LinkedIn email/phone finder delivered as a Chrome extension, search portal, outreach engine, and API — sold primarily to recruiters and sales reps at SMB through enterprise scale. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Data foundation and ZoomInfo Sales prospecting UI, leading with a personal-email wedge (150M personal emails vs. its claimed 40M for ZoomInfo) and a price/contract counter-pitch ("a quarter the cost", no auto-renewal trap). It does not touch ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace/Studio, conversation intelligence, intent, or MCP/agent-ecosystem capabilities, and the recruiting ICP — the heart of ContactOut's go-to-market — is not a primary ZoomInfo segment. Treat ContactOut as a Tier-1 contact-data competitor with a narrow but sharp wedge against Pillar 1 (Data), and zero overlap with Pillars 2 and 3.

## Product Offerings

### Chrome Extension (LinkedIn email & phone finder)

> **Wiki:** [[contactout--chrome-extension]]

- **What it does:** Browser extension that surfaces verified personal/work emails and direct dials for prospects on LinkedIn (Standard, Recruiter Lite, Recruiter Pro, Sales Navigator) and on any company website.
- **Key features:**
  - Reveal personal emails, work emails, and direct dials directly on LinkedIn profiles
  - "ContactOut Anywhere" — find decision-makers on any company website
  - AI Personalizer for one-click hyper-personalized outreach emails
  - List building — bulk-reveal and save all LinkedIn search results in one click
  - Add prospects to email campaigns in one click
  - Sync prospects to CRM/ATS instantly
- **Source URL:** https://contactout.com/chrome-extension

### Search Portal

> **Wiki:** [[contactout--search-portal]]

- **What it does:** Standalone search UI providing access to a database of 350M professionals across 36–40M companies, with 20+ filters for sales and recruiting prospecting.
- **Key features:**
  - Search 350M professionals with 20+ filters (job title, seniority, department, technologies used, revenue, company size, location, skills, keywords)
  - Bulk save and sync to CRM/ATS
  - Export search results to CSV in one click
  - Add prospects to email campaigns in one click
- **Source URL:** https://contactout.com/search-portal

### Email Campaigns

> **Wiki:** [[contactout--email-campaigns]]

- **What it does:** Multi-stage outbound email tool to nurture prospects and candidates from inside ContactOut.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-stage email campaigns
  - Import prospects in one click
  - Personalize emails at scale (paired with AI Email Writer / AI Personalizer)
  - Open, click, and reply rate tracking
- **Source URL:** https://contactout.com/

### Data Enrichment & ContactSheets

> **Wiki:** [[contactout--data-enrichment]]

- **What it does:** Enriches existing CRM/ATS records with ContactOut's verified contact and company data, including bulk LinkedIn-URL enrichment via spreadsheet upload.
- **Key features:**
  - Bulk enrich candidate profiles by uploading LinkedIn URLs
  - Refresh CRM/ATS records with up-to-date emails, phones, and company data
  - Triple-verified emails and phone numbers (99% confidence claim)
- **Source URL:** https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout

### API

> **Wiki:** [[contactout--api]]

- **What it does:** Programmatic access to ContactOut's contact data via Email Finder API and Enriched Profile API for embedding into customer tools and workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Email Finder API — verified personal and work emails on demand
  - Enriched Profile API — real-time profile and company enrichment
  - Daily refresh of CRM/ATS records
  - Bundled with the Team plan or available standalone (book a call to obtain an API token)
- **Source URL:** https://contactout.com/api-feature

### Cloud Phone Dialer & Gmail Enrichment (auxiliary surfaces)

- **What it does:** Two additional product surfaces listed in the homepage product navigation — a cloud-based dialer for placing calls and a Gmail-side enrichment overlay. Dedicated detail pages were not extracted.
- **Key features:**

  - Gmail Enrichment — listed in the homepage feature menu
- **Source URL:** https://contactout.com/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Triple-verified emails and direct dials with a stated 99% accuracy confidence; data is sourced, stored, and refreshed first-hand with hourly updates rather than from resold lists (source: https://contactout.com/our-data).
- Personal-email coverage as the headline wedge — 150M personal emails vs. ContactOut's claimed 40M for ZoomInfo on the same page; positions ~75% personal-email coverage on LinkedIn (source: https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout).
- Native LinkedIn-overlay Chrome extension that works across LinkedIn Standard, Recruiter Lite, Recruiter Pro, and Sales Navigator, plus "ContactOut Anywhere" for any company website (source: https://contactout.com/chrome-extension).
- Consumption-based "unlimited sales seats for under $299/mo" team offer — pay for credits/leads, not seats — a structurally different commercial model than per-seat sales-intelligence platforms (source: https://contactout.com/sales-users).
- Spreadsheet-based bulk LinkedIn-URL enrichment via ContactSheets — upload LinkedIn URLs, get contact data back, framed as a workflow ZoomInfo does not offer (source: https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout).
- Recruiting/ATS positioning as a primary ICP — direct integrations to Lever, Greenhouse, Bullhorn plus Zapier coverage to "thousands more ATS and CRM connections than ZoomInfo" (source: https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (Pillar 1):** ContactOut's contact database (800M people profiles, 150M personal emails, 200M work emails, 100M direct dials, 40M company profiles) is positioned as a head-to-head replacement, claiming "all the B2B data & emails Zoominfo has — plus personal emails for 75% of professionals" (source: https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (classic prospecting UI):** ContactOut Search Portal + Chrome Extension cover the same prospecting motion with 350M professionals and 20+ filters, framed as "a goldmine for sales & recruitment data... same data Zoominfo offers... lower price" (source: https://contactout.com/search-portal).
  - **APIs & MCP (Enterprise Data API):** ContactOut Email Finder API and Enriched Profile API target the contact-enrichment slice of ZoomInfo's API offering for tool/workflow integration (source: https://contactout.com/api-feature).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / outreach:** Email Campaigns + AI Personalizer / AI Email Writer touch the SDR/marketing engagement category but lack broader marketing automation, ABM, web/chat, or intent capabilities (source: https://contactout.com/sales-users).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations:** Daily CRM/ATS refresh, bulk LinkedIn-URL spreadsheet enrichment, and Salesforce/HubSpot connectors overlap with Operations enrichment use cases — without ZoomInfo Operations' routing, dedupe, or workflow automation depth (source: https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2):** No conversation intelligence, intent data, behavioral signals, or first-party-data unification appear on any reviewed page — ContactOut sells contact lookup, not "why-the-deal-moves" reasoning (source: https://contactout.com/).
  - **GTM Workspace / GTM Studio (Pillar 3):** No prioritized-account orchestration, no AI play surface beyond per-email AI Personalizer, and no RevOps / GTM-engineer ICP (source: https://contactout.com/).
  - **Conversation intelligence (Chorus), web chat (ZoomInfo Chat), intent (Forrester Wave Leader):** None present on the homepage or feature pages (source: https://contactout.com/).
  - **MCP server / agentic ecosystem / Claude directory:** No MCP, agent-builder, or developer-ecosystem positioning — only a traditional REST-style API (source: https://contactout.com/api-feature).
  - **Competitor-only territory — Recruiting / ATS-native workflow:** ContactOut sells aggressively to in-house and agency recruiters with native Lever/Greenhouse/Bullhorn ATS connectors and LinkedIn Recruiter Lite/Pro extension support, a swim lane ZoomInfo does not target as a primary ICP (source: https://contactout.com/recruiters).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "ContactOut as an Zoominfo Alternative — a strong Zoominfo competitor at a quarter the cost. ContactOut has over thrice as many personal emails... Zoominfo's auto-renewal clause... you just might end up paying a hernia-inducing $45k roll-over bill." (source: https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Find anyone's email and phone number 10x faster — the world's most accurate contact data for 350M professionals, at a fraction of ZoomInfo's price.
- **Recurring proof points:** 1.4M users from 76% of Fortune 500 companies; G2 4.6/5 and Chrome Store 4.4/5; G2 Lead Intelligence top-ranked across Lead Validation/Enrichment/Quality/Analysis/Browser Extension (9.0–9.4 vs. ZoomInfo 8.3–8.8 on the same page); triple-verified with 99% confidence; CCPA & GDPR compliant; named customers Microsoft, WarnerMedia, Google, Yelp, HSBC, CNN, Robert Half, Tenstorrent, Société Générale; customer quote — "I switched from Zoominfo to ContactOut's consumption based pricing, and I'm now paying one tenth of the price for similar data."
- **Tone / category framing:** "Lead intelligence" / email finder / B2B contact database — explicitly anti-positioning against ZoomInfo on price, personal-email coverage, integration breadth, and contractual flexibility (no auto-renewal trap). Tone is plain-spoken, occasionally combative ("hernia-inducing $45k roll-over bill", "Push back the bounce backs").
