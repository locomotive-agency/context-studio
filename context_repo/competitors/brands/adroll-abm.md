## Summary

AdRoll ABM is the AdRoll umbrella's account-based marketing platform — the same product formerly sold as RollWorks, officially rebranded under the unified AdRoll brand in August 2025 — sold to B2B demand-gen, ABM, lifecycle, and marketing-ops teams from SMB through enterprise. It packages B2B audience intelligence (an AI ICP/intent engine over 2.6B digital identities and 92M contacts) together with a native cross-channel demand-side platform (display, video, social, mobile, CTV, DOOH) inside one application, putting it in direct overlap with ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM workflows and partial overlap with the GTM Studio orchestration surface — though AdRoll cedes contact-data depth to ZoomInfo (92M vs 260M+) and reframes its win as ad execution rather than data scale. Merged with duplicate input row "RollWorks (AdRoll ABM)" — this dossier focuses on the AdRoll umbrella's ABM offering and the new first-party AdRoll-vs-ZoomInfo comparison page; cross-reference `competitors/rollworks-adroll-abm.md` for the legacy RollWorks framing.

## Product Offerings

### AdRoll ABM (formerly RollWorks)

> **Wiki:** [[adroll-abm--abm-platform]]

- **What it does:** Account-based marketing platform that combines B2B audience segmentation and a cross-channel demand-side platform in a single application for B2B teams to identify, target, engage, and measure high-fit accounts (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/account-based-marketing).
- **Key features:**
  - Buyer-insights engine built on "trillions of proprietary data points," 2.6 billion digital identities, and 92 million contacts to define ICP, surface unknown accounts, and predict purchase likelihood.
  - AdRoll ABM Command Center tracking buyer signals in real time and recommending next best steps — add records to CRM, build account/contact lists, send priority lists to sales.
  - InIQ AI buyer-insight engine and BidIQ AI bid-optimization engine (NextRoll trademarks) powering audience selection and ad bidding.
  - Multi-touch cross-channel ad activation across display, native, video, mobile, social, and connected TV — coordinated from one campaign.
  - Predictive intent targeting using firmographic, technographic, job-title, search-keyword, and ICP-fit signals plus partner intent from G2 and Bombora.
  - Closed-won attribution and revenue-impact reporting that "tie ABM efforts to revenue and pipeline" and "amplify your own CRM reporting with unified data integrations."
  - Native CTV and DOOH execution (positioned as "native on both channels" versus competitors that require third-party routing).
  - One-click HubSpot or Salesforce integration with a pitched "launch in 30 days" via automated playbooks.
- **Source URL:** https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform

### Cross-Channel Advertising (DSP)

> **Wiki:** [[adroll-abm--cross-channel-advertising]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered cross-channel demand-side platform sold alongside AdRoll ABM that runs full-funnel awareness, retargeting, and conversion campaigns across the open web and social, in self-service or managed-service tiers (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/cross-channel-advertising).
- **Key features:**
  - Single campaign across display, native, video, mobile, social, and connected TV with shared bid optimization.
  - AI lookalike, demographic, interest, contextual, and first-party (CRM, website, ecommerce) audience targeting.
  - Brand-safe inventory across millions of websites, apps, and streaming platforms via open auctions and PMP deals.
  - Static image, HTML5, short- and long-duration video, and dynamic ad-format support.
  - Self-service tier with AI assistants or Managed-service tier with dedicated account management and creative.
  - Customer-claimed average 5X return on ad spend.
- **Source URL:** https://www.adroll.com/solutions/cross-channel-advertising

### Social & Web Orchestration

> **Wiki:** [[adroll-abm--social-web-orchestration]]

- **What it does:** Cross-channel orchestration that lets a single AdRoll campaign run across the open web and the major walled-garden social platforms from one DSP (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/social-web-orchestration).
- **Key features:**
  - Audience targeting across the web plus Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Pinterest from one platform.
  - Automation Builder positioned as "the only tool on the market that coordinates cross-channel ad campaigns from one platform."
  - BidIQ AI bidding/optimization engine driving real-time cross-channel optimization.
  - Privacy-first cross-device identity graph that does not collect names, phone numbers, addresses, or DOB; no competitor targeting; no cross-advertiser sharing.
- **Source URL:** https://www.adroll.com/solutions/social-web-orchestration

### Cross-Channel Attribution

> **Wiki:** [[adroll-abm--cross-channel-attribution]]

- **What it does:** Attribution dashboard that deduplicates pixel-tracked conversions across channels and integrates with ads, SMS, ecommerce, CRM, and analytics platforms (source: https://www.adroll.com/features/cross-channel-attribution).
- **Key features:**
  - Deduplicated, pixel-tracked metrics aligned with the customer's preferred attribution model.
  - Funnel visualization showing which channel combinations drive the most conversions and highest AOV.
  - Integrations with ads, SMS, ecommerce store, CRM, and third-party analytics platforms in one place.
  - Customizable dashboard with custom metrics for fees, taxes, and unique KPIs.
  - Sold as an add-on included with the Advanced Package.
- **Source URL:** https://www.adroll.com/features/cross-channel-attribution

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS / software, Finance (e.g. PitchBook), Healthcare technology (e.g. Cohere Health), Manufacturing — and on the broader AdRoll DSP side: Retail, Hospitality, D2C, Nonprofit, Agencies (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
- **Company size called out:** Small Business, Mid Market, and Enterprise — case-study customers include enterprise like Snowflake and mid-market like Total Expert (source: https://www.adroll.com/customers).
- **Persona / role focus:** B2B demand-gen marketers, ABM and lifecycle marketing managers, marketing operations / RevOps, and "lean teams that aim high" (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Identifying and targeting high-fit B2B accounts before competitors do
  - Multi-channel ad orchestration (display, social, video, CTV, mobile) against target account lists
  - Real-time buyer-signal tracking and next-best-action recommendations to sales/CRM
  - Tying ABM activity to pipeline and closed-won revenue
  - Aligning sales and marketing on a shared account list and journey-stage view
  - Scaling 1:1 ABM programs (Snowflake — 2,000+ accounts, 75% increase in SDR-booked meetings)

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Native ABM + advertising in one platform: "seamlessly combines a precise B2B audience segmentation and targeting solution with a cross-channel demand-side platform into a single application" (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/account-based-marketing).
- Native CTV and DOOH execution and BidIQ as a "native, purpose-built" DSP versus competitors' "acquired DSP + TTD-split stacks" (source: https://www.adroll.com/compare/adrollabm-vs-zoominfo).
- Onboarding speed: pitched at "~1 month" with one-click HubSpot/Salesforce install and automated playbooks, versus competitors' "3-6 months (full suite)" (source: https://www.adroll.com/compare/adrollabm-vs-zoominfo).
- Pricing transparency claim: "Stated budget = working media" with "no surcharges, frustrating usage caps, or confusing tier system" (source: https://www.adroll.com/compare/adrollabm-vs-zoominfo).
- Designed for lean B2B teams: predictive AI plus workflow automation handles the hardest parts of ABM so small teams can run sophisticated programs (source: https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform).
- Privacy-first identity graph: cross-device identities without collecting PII, no competitor targeting, no cross-advertiser sharing (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/social-web-orchestration).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** AdRoll ABM directly competes with **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM)** — AdRoll's first-party comparison page is titled "AdRoll ABM vs ZoomInfo MarketingOS" and contrasts the two on Platform Purpose, Optimization Loop, CTV/DOOH execution, Cost Transparency, Ad Infrastructure, Onboarding Speed, and Contact Intelligence (source: https://www.adroll.com/compare/adrollabm-vs-zoominfo). The Command Center plus workflow-orchestration story also overlaps ZoomInfo's **GTM Studio** marketer/RevOps surface — AdRoll explicitly contrasts its "Closed loop — auto feedback" against ZoomInfo's "Manual bridge via GTM Studio."
- **Adjacent overlap:** AdRoll's 92M B2B contacts and 2.6B digital identities, layered with G2 and Bombora intent partnerships, are an adjacent (smaller-scale) version of ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1)** foundation; AdRoll concedes contact-intelligence advantage to ZoomInfo (260M+) on the comparison page and reframes value as ad execution rather than raw data depth (source: https://www.adroll.com/compare/adrollabm-vs-zoominfo). Daily Journey-Stage data export to Salesforce and HubSpot also touches **ZoomInfo Operations'** CRM enrichment role for the marketing side of the funnel.
- **No overlap:** Programmatic media buying — display, native, video, social, mobile, CTV, DOOH inventory under BidIQ — is owned ad infrastructure that ZoomInfo does not have; AdRoll positions ZoomInfo as "an ad module on a data platform" requiring third-party routing for CTV/DOOH (source: https://www.adroll.com/compare/adrollabm-vs-zoominfo). AdRoll also serves Retail, Hospitality, D2C, Nonprofit, and Agency verticals via the AdRoll DSP umbrella, all outside ZoomInfo's B2B-only GTM positioning. Walled-garden social ad management (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest) from one console and a privacy-first cookieless-bidding R&D track are also outside ZoomInfo's product footprint (source: https://www.adroll.com/solutions/social-web-orchestration).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "Don't settle for 'advertising-as-an-afterthought.' Choose the ABM platform that unifies account-based intelligence with world-class execution for measurable revenue impact. ... Disconnected data becomes streamlined results ... Skip the unnecessary surcharges, frustrating usage caps, and confusing tier system you get with ZoomInfo." (source: https://www.adroll.com/compare/adrollabm-vs-zoominfo).
