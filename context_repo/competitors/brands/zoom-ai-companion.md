## Summary

Zoom AI Companion is a productivity AI assistant from Zoom Communications, Inc. (NASDAQ: ZM) — **a separate company from ZoomInfo** — bundled with paid Zoom Workplace plans and aimed at end-user knowledge workers, IT admins, and individual professionals who want meeting summaries, AI note-taking, and an agentic web surface across their existing work apps. Its overlap with ZoomInfo is narrow but real at the conversation-intelligence boundary: AI Companion's meeting summaries plus the Custom AI Salesforce connector (which auto-updates opportunity stage/amount/close date) overlap directly with ZoomInfo's Chorus product. There is no overlap on the B2B data foundation, GTM Context Graph (Zoom reasons across the customer's own meetings/Drive/Slack — not third-party prospect data), or GTM Studio's marketing/RevOps orchestration. AI Companion 3.0 launched 2025-12-15 with a new $10/month standalone plan and a federated AI approach combining Zoom's models with OpenAI and Anthropic.

## Product Offerings

### AI Companion (core)

> **Wiki:** [[zoom-ai-companion--ai-companion-core]]

- **What it does:** Generative AI assistant bundled with paid Zoom Workplace plans that captures meeting context, summarizes conversations, takes notes, drafts content, and surfaces insights across Zoom Meetings, Chat, Mail, and the ai.zoom.us web surface.
- **Key features:**
  - Meeting summaries and in-meeting questions across hosted Zoom Meetings
  - AI note-taking (My Notes) for Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and in-person meetings
  - Smart recording with chapters and next steps
  - Chat compose, chat summarize, sentence completion
  - Web surface at ai.zoom.us with intelligent retrieval across Zoom data plus Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Gmail, Outlook Mail
  - Personal workflows and AI-generated daily reflection reports
  - Federated AI approach combining Zoom's own models with OpenAI and Anthropic
- **Source URL:** https://www.zoom.com/en/products/ai-assistant/

### Custom AI Companion

> **Wiki:** [[zoom-ai-companion--custom-ai-companion]]

- **What it does:** Paid add-on that extends AI Companion with company-specific knowledge, third-party app connectors, custom agents built in Zoom AI Studio, and deep research mode.
- **Key features:**
  - Custom knowledge collections from uploaded data, Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Amazon Q, or Glean
  - Agent builder via Zoom AI Studio (low-code, drag-and-drop) with memory, reasoning, and task orchestration
  - Agent2Agent protocol — AI Companion can collaborate with third-party agents like ServiceNow Now Assist
  - Connectors to Salesforce, ServiceNow, Zendesk, Jira, Asana, Box, Slack, Confluence, Seismic, Notion
  - Deep research mode (10 uses/month on the standalone tier)
  - Custom meeting summary templates and custom dictionaries
- **Source URL:** https://www.zoom.com/en/products/custom-ai/

### My Notes (AI note-taker)

> **Wiki:** [[zoom-ai-companion--my-notes]]

- **What it does:** AI note-taking feature inside AI Companion that automatically captures, summarizes, and extracts action items from any virtual or in-person meeting; works across Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, and other third-party platforms.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time transcription with visible meeting indicator
  - AI-generated summary, key takeaways, and action items after the meeting
  - Cross-platform: Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, in-person
  - 9 languages supported for My Notes; 30+ languages for meeting summaries
  - Send summaries to Slack automatically (paid AI Companion tier)
  - Notes stored in Zoom Hub and convert to collaborative Zoom Docs
  - Manual note enrichment via AI from the generated transcript
- **Source URL:** https://www.zoom.com/en/products/ai-assistant/features/ai-note-taking/

### AI Companion 3.0 (Dec 2025 release)

> **Wiki:** [[zoom-ai-companion--ai-companion-3-0]]

- **What it does:** Latest version positioned as "agentic AI" that goes from "conversation to completion" — supports pre-meeting prep, in-meeting assistance, and post-meeting follow-up via the ai.zoom.us web surface.
- **Key features:**
  - AI-first web interface at ai.zoom.us connecting Zoom meetings, Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Gmail, Outlook, and Slack (Slack requires Custom AI add-on)
  - Daily Reflection Report pulling insights from meetings, documents, and notes
  - Cross Meeting Analyst prompt to identify recurring topics and unresolved issues across meetings
  - Agentic writing mode for side-by-side AI editing
  - Intelligent retrieval that auto-searches connected work content (no manual upload)
  - New AI Companion Basic free tier inside Zoom Workplace Basic
  - New $10/month standalone subscription that does not require a paid Zoom Workplace license
- **Source URL:** https://www.zoom.com/en/blog/zoom-ai-companion-3-0-agentic-ai-conversation-to-completion/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Built natively into Zoom Workplace and bundled at no extra cost with paid Zoom seats — no separate subscription required for core features (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/ai-assistant/).
- Federated AI approach combining Zoom's own LLMs with OpenAI and Anthropic models, selected per task rather than locked to a single vendor (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/blog/zoom-ai-companion-3-0-agentic-ai-conversation-to-completion/).
- Agentic web surface at ai.zoom.us connecting Zoom meetings to Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, Gmail, Outlook, and Slack with intelligent retrieval that auto-searches connected content — no manual uploads (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/blog/zoom-ai-companion-3-0-agentic-ai-conversation-to-completion/).
- Cross-platform AI note-taking — works on Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, and in-person meetings, not just Zoom-hosted calls (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/ai-assistant/features/ai-note-taking/).
- Privacy stance: Zoom does not use customer audio, video, chat, screen sharing, attachments, or other communications-like content to train Zoom or third-party AI models (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/ai-assistant/features/ai-note-taking/).
- Agent2Agent protocol enabling AI Companion to coordinate with third-party agents like ServiceNow Now Assist for cross-system task execution (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/custom-ai/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** **Chorus (ZoomInfo conversation intelligence)** — both products generate post-call AI summaries, action items, and CRM-updatable insights. AI Companion's Custom AI Salesforce connector explicitly updates opportunity stage, amount, and close date in Salesforce, mirroring Chorus's call-summary-to-CRM workflow (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/custom-ai/connectors/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (seller productivity).** AI Companion's sales-persona use cases — pre-call intelligence pulled from Salesforce, real-time deal updates, "Personalize sales outreach" with custom meeting templates — overlap with ZoomInfo's seller workflow but use Zoom's own meeting context as the data source rather than third-party B2B prospect data (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/custom-ai/).
  - **GTM Workspace AI-agent layer / GTM Context Graph (cross-system reasoning).** AIC 3.0's intelligent retrieval reasons across Zoom meetings + Google Drive + OneDrive + Gmail + Outlook + Slack to surface cross-meeting insights, conceptually parallel to GTM Context Graph reasoning across CRM + conversations + intent — but Zoom's data layer is internal collaboration content, not a B2B prospect graph (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/blog/zoom-ai-companion-3-0-agentic-ai-conversation-to-completion/).
- **No overlap:**
  - **B2B prospect data foundation — Data (Pillar 1)** (500M contacts, 100M companies, technographics, Intent Data signals). AI Companion has zero B2B contact/company database; it only operates on data already present in the user's connected work apps.
  - **Marketing / RevOps orchestration** (ZoomInfo Marketing, GTM Studio, ABM, audience building). AI Companion's marketing use cases are limited to social planning, content refresh, and campaign coordination from internal Jira/Slack/Box assets — no audience-building or intent-driven targeting (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/custom-ai/connectors/).
  - **APIs & MCP / external agent-builder ecosystem.** Zoom offers Zoom AI Studio and an agent2agent protocol but no public MCP server akin to mcp.zoominfo.com. Builder ecosystem is limited to Zoom developers and Custom AI connectors (source: https://www.zoom.com/en/products/custom-ai/).
  - **Real-time meeting/UCaaS infrastructure.** Zoom owns the meeting/phone/chat infrastructure that ZoomInfo does not — AI Companion sits as a productivity layer on top of Zoom Workplace.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The closest available comparison is Zoom's own zoom.com/en/compare/zoom-vs-other-ai-tools page, which positions AI Companion against ChatGPT, Microsoft 365 Copilot, Google Gemini, Otter.ai, and Fathom — not against ZoomInfo. The two products are not in the same competitive set; the SERP confusion exists only because both companies' names start with "Zoom."
