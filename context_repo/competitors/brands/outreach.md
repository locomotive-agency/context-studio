## Summary

Outreach has rebranded as the "Agentic AI Platform for Revenue Teams," explicitly leaning into the AI-agents framing with the tagline "You didn't hire enough sellers. Now you don't have to." Outreach competes head-to-head with Salesloft on sales engagement and is the closest direct competitor to ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace seller surface. The platform spans sales engagement, pipeline management, sales forecasting, and revenue intelligence — Outreach reframed its sales-engagement legacy into a broader "Revenue Orchestration" pitch that mirrors Salesloft's category move. Pricing has three tiers (Amplify Core, Amplify Plus, Amplify Pro) — quote-based with both seat-based AND consumption-based components for AI/agent capabilities. Notably, Outreach's own AI sales workflow blog mentions using Outreach + 6sense together — Outreach does not own a contact database and is partner-friendly with data vendors. The split-domain identity (outreach.io for product / outreach.ai for AI-forward marketing + pricing) is a 2026 rebrand artifact.

## Product Offerings

- **Key features:**

  - Specialized AI agents across the revenue motion
  - Spans Sales / Marketing / RevOps / Customer Success
  - Cross-functional revenue platform
- **Source URL:** https://www.outreach.ai/

### Sales Engagement Platform

- **What it does:** Outreach's flagship product — sequencing, dialer, email, and workflow automation to "create quality pipeline and increase productivity."
- **Key features:**

  - Integrated with CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, MS Dynamics)
  - Workflow automation
- **Source URL:** https://www.outreach.io/platform/sales-engagement

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Agentic AI Platform for Revenue Teams. You didn't hire enough sellers. Now you don't have to."
- **Recurring proof points:** Cross-functional trust signal ("Sales, Marketing, Rev Ops and Customer Success teams trust Outreach"); New Agentic AI Innovation Highlights section; case-study evidence at /customers (not extracted in this run).
- **Tone / category framing:** "Agentic AI Platform for Revenue Teams" — Outreach landed on agent-platform framing from a sales-engagement starting point. The "you didn't hire enough sellers" pitch frames AI as a hiring-substitute, which is more aggressive than Salesloft's "productivity" frame and more aggressive than ZoomInfo's "GTM intelligence" frame. Outreach is the rare vendor positioning AI agents as headcount displacement rather than headcount augmentation.
