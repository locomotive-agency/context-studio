## Summary

AlphaSense is an AI-powered **market intelligence and financial research platform** sold primarily to investment analysts, hedge fund / PE / asset management research, corporate strategy, competitive intelligence teams, and consultants — a fundamentally different ICP than ZoomInfo's GTM buyer. Its product surface (Generative Search, Deep Research, Tegus Expert Insights, Financial Data with Canalyst models, Enterprise Intelligence) is built to synthesize premium documents (500M+ filings, broker research, expert transcripts) into analyst-grade deliverables. The overlap with ZoomInfo is narrow and tangential: both pitch an AI intelligence layer that reasons across multiple data sources, but AlphaSense has no verified B2B contact data, no buyer intent, no sales engagement workspace, and no MCP/API ecosystem aimed at GTM execution. Best treated as an adjacent platform that an enterprise CI or strategy team might run alongside ZoomInfo, not a substitute.

## Product Offerings

### Generative Search

> **Wiki:** [[alphasense--generative-search]]

- **What it does:** Multi-agent research system that reasons across qualitative insights, structured financial data, and the customer's internal knowledge to deliver cited, auditable answers in seconds.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-agent research architecture
  - Reasons across qualitative + structured financial data + internal knowledge in one query
  - Sentence-level citations / auditable outputs
  - Built on a 500M+ premium document library
  - Positioned as a replacement for fragmented web search for analysts
- **Source URL:** https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/generative-search

### Deep Research

> **Wiki:** [[alphasense--deep-research]]

- **What it does:** Autonomous AI agent that functions like a team of analysts to generate investment-grade briefings and synthesize insights across hundreds of sources.
- **Key features:**
  - Autonomous multi-step research runs (50–100 searches per run, per ZoomInfo vs page)
  - Builds investment-grade briefings from filings, transcripts, broker research
  - Compresses weeks of analysis into minutes (5–30 min reports)
  - Produces comprehensive cited reports
- **Source URL:** https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/

### Tegus Expert Insights (Expert Transcripts + Expert Calls + Channel Checks)

> **Wiki:** [[alphasense--tegus-expert-insights]]

- **What it does:** World's largest library of investor-led expert interview transcripts plus bespoke expert call services for proprietary qualitative research; flagship differentiator following the Tegus acquisition.
- **Key features:**
  - 250,000+ investor-led expert insights / transcripts
  - 8K+ new transcripts added monthly across 29K+ companies
  - AI-powered summaries, key takeaways, and sentiment analysis on transcripts
  - Bespoke expert call services (custom-sourced experts on demand)
  - Channel Checks for real-time market signals across sectors
  - Talk-to-an-expert workflow on transcripts of interest
  - Autonomous AI Agent Interviewer for channel-check interviews
  - Compliance framework around transcript creation
- **Source URL:** https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/expert-insights/

### Financial Data

> **Wiki:** [[alphasense--financial-data]]

- **What it does:** Suite of clean, auditable financial datasets and purpose-built tools (Canalyst models, screeners, Excel add-in) that unify quantitative analysis with qualitative AlphaSense content.
- **Key features:**
  - Standardized financial statements + forward consensus estimates across 18,000+ public companies
  - 4,100+ Canalyst financial models with company-specific KPIs and segment-level details
  - M&A transaction data (public + private) with AI-generated strategic rationales
  - Funding round data, valuations, ownership, employee counts for private companies
  - Workforce data (headcount, hiring velocity, attrition) for private companies
  - Industry-specific KPIs across 115+ industries (Rule of 40, RevPAR, Load Factor, etc.)
  - M&A Screener with 400+ filters
  - Native Excel add-in with audit links to source
- **Source URL:** https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/financial-data

### Enterprise Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[alphasense--enterprise-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Connects a customer's internal shared drives so memos, meeting notes, decks, and models become searchable alongside AlphaSense's external library.
- **Key features:**
  - Turn-key integrations with SharePoint, Box, Google Drive, Egnyte, OneDrive, Dropbox, Evernote, OneNote, Amazon S3
  - Direct upload and email forwarding for documents
  - GenAI search/summarize/audit across internal + external content
  - One-day deployment claim
  - Private cloud deployment available (Enterprise tier only)
  - LLMs never trained on client data
- **Source URL:** https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/enterprise

### Monitoring (Dashboards + Alerts)

- **What it does:** Customizable dashboards and alerts that present a single view of competitors, industry trends, and macro topics, with mobile-app coverage.
- **Key features:**
  - Customized dashboards tailored to research processes
  - Regular alerts on competitor / industry / macro signals
  - Mobile app for on-the-go tracking
- **Source URL:** https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/

### Workflow Agents

> **Wiki:** [[alphasense--workflow-agents]]

- **What it does:** Custom and scheduled AI agents that automate repeatable research tasks, turning research from manual pull into automated push.
- **Key features:**
  - Custom agent builder
  - Scheduled agents for repeatable tasks
  - Automated push of research outputs to users
- **Source URL:** https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Narrow — only on the **analytical / intelligence layer** between ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph and AlphaSense's Generative Search + Deep Research. Both pitch an AI system that reasons across multiple data sources to surface market context, but they feed different downstream actions: AlphaSense feeds analyst decisions / written research deliverables, ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph feeds prioritized account lists and AI-drafted outreach (source: https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/generative-search). There is no overlap on Data foundation (no contact data), GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, or APIs/MCP.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Competitive intelligence as a use case — AlphaSense is used by enterprise CI teams (Salesforce CI customer is a flagship case study), and ZoomInfo also supports CI / product marketing through technographic + intent data; the buyers can sit in the same building under different mandates (source: https://www.alpha-sense.com/resources/case-studies/salesforce/). Programmatic access — AlphaSense's Ingestion API + embedded widgets are adjacent to ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP, but ZoomInfo's vs page notes AlphaSense's API "centers on bringing content INTO AlphaSense for research, not pushing intelligence OUT to sales and marketing tools" (source: https://pipeline.zoominfo.com/sales/alphasense-vs-contify).
- **No overlap:** AlphaSense-only territories — premium financial document library (500M+ equity research, regulatory filings, earnings transcripts), Canalyst financial models, native Excel valuation tooling, Tegus expert transcripts, expert-call services, financial benchmarking / industry KPI tooling. AlphaSense gaps vs ZoomInfo — no verified B2B contact data, no buyer intent signals, no website visitor identification (no anonymous-traffic resolution), no sales engagement / outreach workspace, no MCP server, no CRM-native seller surface, and no marketing campaign orchestration (source: https://pipeline.zoominfo.com/sales/alphasense-vs-contify).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No AlphaSense-authored vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced in SERP results_ — AlphaSense's `/compare/` section publishes vs PitchBook but no vs ZoomInfo. The only available vs artifact is **ZoomInfo-authored**: "AlphaSense stops at the research deliverable… Neither gives your sellers the verified contacts, buying signals, and AI-driven outreach they need to act on what the intelligence reveals. AlphaSense answers the question 'What's happening in this market?'… ZoomInfo answers 'Which accounts should we pursue right now, and how do we reach them?'" (source: https://pipeline.zoominfo.com/sales/alphasense-vs-contify). Flag added.
