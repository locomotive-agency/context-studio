## Summary

Anaplan is an AI-driven enterprise scenario planning, analysis, and reporting platform sold into the office of the CFO, RevOps/sales-planning leaders, supply chain planners, and HR/workforce teams under the "Connected Planning" thesis. Its sales-side overlap with ZoomInfo is narrow and tangential: Anaplan's Revenue Performance Management suite plans territories, quotas, segmentation, and incentives on top of CRM data the customer already owns — it does not supply the underlying B2B contact, company, intent, or conversation data that defines ZoomInfo's GTM Intelligence Platform. The bulk of Anaplan's footprint (FP&A, financial consolidation, S&OP, supply/demand planning, workforce planning, ICM) sits entirely outside ZoomInfo's category. Anaplan should be treated as an adjacent partner-or-coexist vendor for ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio territory and segmentation workflows, not a substitute.

### Connected Planning Platform

> **Wiki:** [[anaplan--connected-planning-platform]]

- **Source URL:** https://www.anaplan.com/connected-planning/

### Anaplan Intelligence (AI) — Intelligent Role-Based Agents + CoModeler

> **Wiki:** [[anaplan--anaplan-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Predictive, generative, and agentic AI layer with role-specific AI analysts (Finance, Sales, Supply Chain, Workforce) plus CoModeler for natural-language model building.
- **Key features:**
  - Anaplan Sales Analyst — surfaces risks/opportunities, runs scenarios for revenue performance management
  - Anaplan Finance Analyst — automates month-end, spots deviations, manages risk/opportunity
  - Anaplan Supply Chain Analyst — alerts on disruption, runs impact scenarios
  - Anaplan Workforce Analyst — assesses hiring risks, models staffing scenarios
  - CoModeler — natural-language agent that builds, extends, and optimizes Anaplan models
  - Conversational natural-language interface embedded in Anaplan applications
  - Trained on "a decade of planning expertise from deploying millions of models"
- **Source URL:** https://www.anaplan.com/platform/intelligent-role-based-agents

### Anaplan for Sales & Marketing — Revenue Performance Management

> **Wiki:** [[anaplan--revenue-performance-management]]

  - Segmentation & Scoring; Marketing Performance Management; Marketing Finance
- **Source URL:** https://www.anaplan.com/solutions/sales-revenue-performance-management/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Anaplan does not sell B2B contact/company data, intent data, conversation intelligence, prospecting workflows, ABM audience-building, or developer APIs/MCP — none of ZoomInfo's primary product lines (Data foundation, GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, APIs & MCP) have a direct Anaplan substitute.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - ZoomInfo Operations (RevOps / CRM data quality + routing) and the territory/segmentation use cases pulled into GTM Studio overlap with Anaplan's Revenue Performance Management — Territory Planning & Management, Quota Planning, Segmentation & Scoring, GTM Capacity Planning. Anaplan plans on top of CRM data the customer already owns; it does not supply the underlying B2B graph (source: https://www.anaplan.com/solutions/sales-revenue-performance-management/).
  - ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace / GTM Studio sales forecasting and pipeline visibility surfaces overlap with Anaplan Sales Forecasting + the Sales Analyst AI agent — Anaplan models the plan and rolls up forecasts; ZoomInfo executes on the plan via prioritized accounts and AI-drafted outreach (source: https://www.anaplan.com/use-case/sales-forecasting-software/).
  - ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM segmentation) and GTM Studio audience-building touch Anaplan's Marketing Performance Management + Marketing Finance — Anaplan focuses on budget/spend/attribution planning, not audience identification or activation (source: https://www.anaplan.com/solutions/sales-revenue-performance-management/).
- **No overlap:**
  - Anaplan for Finance — FP&A, consolidation, OpEx/CapEx, financial close, disclosure management — sits entirely outside ZoomInfo's GTM platform scope (source: https://www.anaplan.com/solutions/finance-planning).
  - Anaplan Supply Chain — Integrated Business Planning, Demand Planning, Supply Planning, S&OP, Trade Promotion Management, Retail/Merchandise Planning — is not a ZoomInfo category at all (source: https://www.anaplan.com/solutions/sales-operations-planning-software/).
  - Anaplan HR & Workforce — Workforce Planning, Compensation Planning & Modeling, Headcount Expense Planning, Talent Strategy — is outside ZoomInfo's footprint (source: https://www.anaplan.com/).
  - Anaplan Sales Incentives / Incentive Compensation Management (ICM) — sales commission design and payout management is not a ZoomInfo product line (source: https://www.anaplan.com/solutions/sales-revenue-performance-management/).
