# AiSDR

## Sub-products

- [[aisdr--autopilot|AiSDR Sales Autopilot]] — competes with GTM Workspace

## Summary

AiSDR is an AI SDR / sales-autopilot vendor selling a managed AI agent that handles outbound prospecting, multichannel outreach, reply qualification, and meeting booking for SMB and mid-market revenue teams (founders, sales leaders, solo SDRs). Its overlap with ZoomInfo is partial-but-pointed: AiSDR's homepage explicitly names ZoomInfo as one of nine point tools its $900/mo Explore plan claims to replace, positioning itself as an end-to-end seller workflow that bundles a 300M+/700M+ contact database, intent-signal prospecting, and AI message authoring under quarterly billing. AiSDR competes most directly with the prospect-to-meeting slice of GTM Workspace and parts of the Data foundation; it does not compete with Chorus, ZoomInfo's APIs & MCP layer, or ZoomInfo Chat.

## Product Offerings

### AiSDR Platform (full-cycle AI sales agent)

- **What it does:** AI agent that finds in-market buyers, researches each lead, writes personalized outreach across email/LinkedIn/calls, qualifies replies, and books meetings end-to-end.
- **Key features:**
  - Live AI lead search via plain-English ICP description (no static-database lock-in)
  - Real-time deep research per lead (LinkedIn posts, company news, industry signals)
  - Multichannel sequences across email, LinkedIn DMs/InMail/connection requests, and AI call steps via Aircall
  - AI-generated multimedia in messages (AI videos, memes, voice notes)
  - AI-handled reply qualification, objection handling, and meeting booking
  - Domain warmup, mailbox rotation, and 3x bounce checks (~96% deliverability)
  - Two-way HubSpot and Salesforce sync with AI account scoring
- **Source URL:** https://aisdr.com/platform/

### AI Prospecting (Live AI search + database)

- **What it does:** Builds and updates lead lists from real-time public web data plus a built-in contact database (database size cited as 300M+ on /pricing/ and 700M+ on /sales-ai/ and /features/ — see flags).
- **Key features:**
  - Native database (300M+/700M+ contacts depending on page)
  - Sales Navigator real-time LinkedIn data integration
  - Buyer-intent signals: hiring activity, funding rounds, tech stack, search/news mentions, keyword tracking
  - LinkedIn profile-visitor, post-engagement, and keyword tracking
  - Champion tracking and job-change outreach
  - Suppression lists and existing-lead enrichment
- **Source URL:** https://aisdr.com/sales-ai/

### AI Strategist

- **What it does:** Conversational GTM-strategy generator that maps ICP, audience, channels, personas, and a full sequence in roughly 20 minutes; can also diagnose underperforming campaigns.
- **Key features:**
  - Generates 5 tailored GTM campaign ideas from a website URL
  - Outputs ICP, target audience, time-sensitive buyer signals, messaging angle, and full sequence
  - One-click campaign launch from generated plan
  - Used by 80% of AiSDR customers for every new campaign (source: https://aisdr.com/why/)
- **Source URL:** https://aisdr.com/why/

### HubSpot Sales AI SDR (CRM-native module)

- **What it does:** Native two-way HubSpot integration for inbound and outbound automation that uses HubSpot lists, properties, and activity data to drive outreach and sync activity back.
- **Key features:**
  - Two-clicks-to-connect HubSpot setup
  - Active and static list sync; pulls net-new or pushes generated leads
  - Personalization driven by user-selected HubSpot properties
  - AI-powered account scoring inside HubSpot (Grow tier and above)
  - Logs all outreach activity back to CRM; creates contacts and call tasks
  - Works with both inbound follow-ups and outbound campaigns
- **Source URL:** https://aisdr.com/hubspot-sales-ai-sdr/

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** SaaS, Healthcare, E-commerce, Finance (named industry pages); EdTech, Software development, Renewable energy services and Asset Tracking/Logistics (testimonial-level)
- **Company size called out:** Small startups, SMB, mid-market — with explicit "built for" positioning around founders, small revenue teams, and solo SDRs without budget for full SDR seats; explicit "not for" stance against teams that want to "blast their entire TAM" (source: https://aisdr.com/)
- **Persona / role focus:** Revenue/sales leaders, founders/CEOs (founder-led sales), sales managers, SDRs (outbound and inbound), Customer Success, Marketers (lead nurture), and demand-gen/RevOps lighter-touch
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Outbound prospecting and cold outreach automation
  - Inbound lead follow-up and qualification with sub-10-minute response time
  - Lead nurture sequences inside HubSpot
  - AI-assisted GTM campaign planning via the AI Strategist
  - Stack consolidation (replaces lead database + sequencer + email warmup + AI writer + LinkedIn tools)
  - LinkedIn engagement and DM outreach

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Quarterly (not annual) billing with cancel-anytime — explicit jab at AI SDR competitors that lock customers into annual or multi-year contracts (source: https://aisdr.com/pricing/)
- Self-styled as "the ORIGINAL AI SDR" that "measures success in meetings that show up" rather than messages sent (source: https://aisdr.com/why/)
- AI Strategist — conversational generation of full GTM plans (ICP + signals + sequences) in ~20 minutes; reportedly used by 80% of AiSDR customers for every new campaign (source: https://aisdr.com/why/)
- DISC personality analysis on each lead with auto-tuned message tone (assertive vs. analytical etc.) (source: https://aisdr.com/why/)
- White-glove onboarding with a dedicated GTM Engineer; 90% of clients launch in 7 days or less (source: https://aisdr.com/pricing/)
- Multimedia outreach in-platform — AI-generated videos, memes, and LinkedIn voice notes built into the sequence builder (source: https://aisdr.com/platform/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** AiSDR competes head-to-head with the seller-facing slice of **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace** (prospecting + outreach orchestration) and with the **Data** foundation at the small-team end. The homepage diagram "SDR automation that replaces your entire stack" lists "ZoomInfo — Contact data" as one of nine tools the $900/mo Explore plan claims to subsume, alongside Apollo (lead database), Clearbit/Clay (enrichment), Lemlist/Instantly (sequencers), Zerobounce (bounce checking), and Copy.ai (content) (source: https://aisdr.com/). On /sales-ai/, AiSDR markets pulling "unlimited leads from our database of 700M+ contacts" with intent narrowing — directly contesting ZoomInfo's data-depth pitch.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Touches but does not fully substitute **ZoomInfo Marketing** (the platform page positions AiSDR as a "marketer sidekick" running personalized HubSpot-driven nurture sequences and lead reactivation) and **ZoomInfo Operations** (Grow tier ships AI account scoring and two-way Salesforce/HubSpot sync, with enrichment and contact creation — a thin slice of the data-quality/routing job) (source: https://aisdr.com/platform/, https://aisdr.com/pricing/).
- **No overlap:** AiSDR does not compete with **Chorus** (no conversation intelligence, call recording, or deal coaching surfaced — Aircall integration is for outbound dialing only); does not compete with **APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem** (no public API or MCP server surfaced; AiSDR is a managed AI service, not a data layer); does not compete with **ZoomInfo Chat** (no live chat surface — AiSDR de-anonymizes website visitors at Enterprise tier but routes them into outbound sequences) (source: https://aisdr.com/, https://aisdr.com/use-cases/, https://aisdr.com/pricing/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No first-party "AiSDR vs ZoomInfo" page exists. The clearest direct shot is the homepage stack-replacement diagram naming ZoomInfo as "Contact data" inside the bundle AiSDR claims to replace for $900/mo. The pricing page also frames AiSDR against the broader category: "Those tools [Salesloft/Outreach/Instantly] help you send messages. AiSDR helps you decide _what to send_ and _who to send it to_" (source: https://aisdr.com/, https://aisdr.com/pricing/). Third-party Findstack comparison was used as a backstop but yielded thin/duplicated data — see flags.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** An AI SDR that books meetings that actually show up — by combining real-time signal-based targeting, deep per-lead research, and intent-aware multichannel outreach (email + LinkedIn + AI calls) under one roof, replacing 8+ point tools.
- **Recurring proof points:** 250+ companies using AiSDR; 12,000 meetings booked for clients in 2025; customers booked meetings with Disney, Airbnb, Delta, Salesforce, J.P. Morgan, Travelers, Nasdaq, Novartis, Pfizer, Merck, Walmart, Cloudflare, Microsoft, Boeing; 1-3 demos per 100 leads benchmark; ~7.1% average reply rate; 90% of clients live in 7 days or less; YC S23, $3M seed Sep 2023.
- **Tone / category framing:** Calls itself "the ORIGINAL AI SDR" and an "AI sales autopilot" — explicitly positioning itself outside both the sales-engagement category (Outreach/Salesloft) and the lead-database category (ZoomInfo/Apollo/Cognism), with the claim that AiSDR replaces the small-team version of both.
