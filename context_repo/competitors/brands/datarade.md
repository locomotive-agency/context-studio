## Summary

Datarade is a Berlin-based data marketplace (founded 2018) that connects buyers with 2,600+ third-party data providers across 560+ data categories — it is a procurement and discovery layer, not a GTM tool. Its primary buyer is anyone shopping for external data (analysts, RevOps, marketers, AI/ML teams), and its provider-side Data Commerce Cloud SaaS powers the listings. Overlap with ZoomInfo is tangential: Datarade competes at the research/awareness layer when buyers compare B2B contact-data vendors (it ranks Coresignal, Forager.ai, LakeB2B, and Xverum as top ZoomInfo alternatives) and at the AI-ready / MCP / RAG-API surface, but it does not sell unified GTM workflow, intent reasoning, or first-party data of its own.

## Product Offerings

### Datarade Marketplace

> **Wiki:** [[datarade--marketplace]]

- **What it does:** B2B data marketplace where buyers discover, compare, and procure data products from 2,600+ third-party data providers across 560+ data categories (source: https://datarade.ai/).
- **Key features:**
  - Browse 560+ data categories spanning B2B Data, Financial, Geospatial, Consumer, Healthcare, AI Training, and 14+ vertical hubs
  - Free data sample previews and pricing benchmarks across competing providers
  - Free-of-charge to buyers; Datarade is paid by the provider on purchase
  - Complimentary sourcing advice from in-house data acquisition specialists
  - 240+ use-case-tagged search hubs (Lead Generation, CRM Data Enrichment, AI Agent Integration, Algorithmic Trading, etc.)
  - Post-a-data-request workflow that lets providers come to buyers
- **Source URL:** https://datarade.ai/

### Data Commerce Cloud (DCC)

> **Wiki:** [[datarade--data-commerce-cloud]]

- **What it does:** B2B SaaS launched 2022 that helps data providers grow omni-channel data businesses — the seller-side software backing the marketplace (source: https://datarade.ai/company).
- **Key features:**
  - Used by 500 data providers to list and monetize datasets
  - Marketplace listing creation with no tech effort
  - Access to qualified data-buyer budgets
  - B2B intent insights on data buyers visiting the marketplace
  - Hit $1M+ ARR and DCC's 500th customer milestone in 2023
- **Source URL:** https://datarade.ai/company

### AI-Ready Data Search (RAG API, MCP server, AI Agent Integration)

> **Wiki:** [[datarade--ai-ready-data]]

- **What it does:** Curated marketplace surfaces aggregating third-party datasets and APIs marketed for AI workloads — RAG pipelines, MCP servers, and AI-agent integration use cases (source: https://datarade.ai/use-cases/ai-agent-integration).
- **Key features:**
  - RAG API search hub listing 13 third-party RAG-oriented data products from providers including ACNetwork, LakeB2B, Bytemine, FileMarket, MFour, and Lighthouse IP
  - Dedicated MCP-server search lane at https://datarade.ai/search/products/mcp-server
  - AI Agent Integration use-case hub featuring third-party datasets (medical imagery, conversational speech, customer-service audio) from providers such as Datavendor and WiserBrand.com
  - Branded as "AI Data Marketplace" across nav and footer; products tagged "AI-Ready Data Products" and "AI-Ready Data Providers"
- **Source URL:** https://datarade.ai/use-cases/ai-agent-integration

## Pricing

| Tier | Price | What's included | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Buyer access to Datarade Marketplace | Free | Search, comparison, sample previews, sourcing advice; Datarade is paid by the provider when a purchase is made. | https://datarade.ai/ |
| Data Commerce Cloud (provider SaaS) | Contact sales | Provider listing + monetization SaaS; specific tier pricing not published on reviewed pages. | https://datarade.ai/company |

Datarade itself does not charge buyers — its platform-fee model is supplier-side. Per-dataset pricing on the marketplace is set by individual providers; many list "Pricing available upon request" while a minority publish per-row, per-month, or per-purchase rates (e.g. $0.15/row, $25/month, €5,000/purchase) (source: https://datarade.ai/data-categories/pricing-data).

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Cross-industry — financial services, retail/CPG, real estate, healthcare/life sciences, automotive, advertising/martech, AI/ML.
- **Company size called out:** Not explicitly segmented; positions as a global B2B platform with 100K monthly buyer visitors and 500 provider-side SaaS customers (source: https://datarade.ai/company).
- **Persona / role focus:** Data buyers (analysts, data engineers, BI/RevOps, marketers, AI/ML teams); data providers / data businesses listing on the marketplace; investors and corporate-strategy teams sourcing alternative data.
- **Use cases promoted:** Algorithmic Trading, B2B Data Enrichment, Competitor Analysis, Credit Scoring, CRM Data Enrichment, Customer Insights, Footfall Analytics, Lead Generation, Market Intelligence, AI Agent Integration, and Generative AI / LLM Training (source: https://datarade.ai/).

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Largest selection of AI-ready data products and providers — 2,600+ providers, 560+ categories, 240+ use cases (source: https://datarade.ai/).
- Free for buyers with a complimentary sourcing-advisor concierge — provider-funded model removes buyer friction (source: https://datarade.ai/).
- Marketplace transparency: instant data-sample previews and pricing benchmarks across competing providers (source: https://datarade.ai/).
- Provider-side software flywheel via Data Commerce Cloud — 500 SaaS customers and $1M+ ARR feed marketplace inventory (source: https://datarade.ai/company).
- AI/agent positioning: dedicated RAG API and MCP-server search hubs plus an "AI Chat" surface in the primary nav (source: https://datarade.ai/use-cases/ai-agent-integration).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** ZoomInfo's **Data (Pillar 1)** foundation (B2B contacts, companies, technographics) overlaps with Datarade's B2B Data category hubs (Firmographic, Technographic, B2B Leads, B2B Intent, B2B Marketing). Datarade hosts a profile and "Zoominfo Competitors & Alternatives" page that surfaces Coresignal, Forager.ai, LakeB2B, Xverum, and Exellius Systems as alternatives — placing Datarade in the buyer's research path for the same B2B contact/firmographic category ZoomInfo sells (source: https://datarade.ai/data-providers/zoominfo/alternatives).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Datarade's RAG API hub, MCP-server search hub, and AI Agent Integration use-case page sit adjacent to ZoomInfo's **APIs & MCP** / agent-builder ecosystem — but Datarade aggregates third-party data products rather than serving its own first-party data via API. Its B2B Intent Data and Audience Data hubs are also adjacent to **ZoomInfo Marketing** / **GTM Studio**'s intent + audience capabilities, but as a routing layer to third-party suppliers rather than a unified intent product (sources: https://datarade.ai/use-cases/ai-agent-integration, https://datarade.ai/data-categories).
- **No overlap:** Datarade operates a two-sided data-commerce business — buyer-side procurement and provider-side monetization SaaS (Data Commerce Cloud) — and indexes 14+ verticals (financial market data, geospatial, weather, real estate, healthcare, trade, environmental, legal). ZoomInfo neither runs a multi-vendor marketplace nor sells provider-side listing/monetization SaaS, and it does not sell into most of those verticals (source: https://datarade.ai/data-categories).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Datarade's ZoomInfo alternatives page does not pitch Datarade head-to-head; instead it routes buyers to other providers: "Looking for the best B2B Contact Data provider for your business? Find reviews for Zoominfo left by Datarade users, and compare Zoominfo's data products and performance to the most popular competitors and alternatives. Coresignal, Forager.ai, LakeB2B, and Xverum are the top competitors and alternatives to Zoominfo." (source: https://datarade.ai/data-providers/zoominfo/alternatives).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The #1 Global Data Marketplace — find the right data products, effortlessly" from 2,600+ trusted data providers, free of charge to buyers because providers fund the platform on purchase (source: https://datarade.ai/).
- **Recurring proof points:** 2,600+ data providers; 560+ data categories; 240+ use cases; 100K monthly data-buyer visitors; 500 providers using the Data Commerce Cloud B2B SaaS; 30K matches made across 200 countries; $7.5M+ GMV by 2021 and $1M+ ARR by 2023 (sources: https://datarade.ai/, https://datarade.ai/company).
- **Tone / category framing:** "AI Data Marketplace" / "data commerce" — democratizing access to commercial data so it can fuel AI; positioned as transparent, convenient, helpful, and free for buyers.
