## Summary

Metadata is an AI-driven B2B paid-media operating system aimed at demand-gen marketers, marketing leaders, and finance owners of paid budgets. Its core pitch is that AI agents (Bid, Targeting, Analyst, plus campaign-execution agents) automate paid-campaign workflow across LinkedIn, Meta, Google, Reddit (Bing in development), with a patented identity graph (MetaMatch) to bring LinkedIn-grade B2B targeting to non-LinkedIn channels. Direct overlap with ZoomInfo is concentrated in ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio: audience-building from CRM + intent data and activation across paid channels — Metadata explicitly names ZoomInfo as a competitor on its homepage FAQ (source: https://metadata.io/). Metadata sells nothing in the seller, conversation-intelligence, or developer/MCP categories that ZoomInfo also covers.

## Product Offerings

### Metadata Campaigns (Marketing OS)

> **Wiki:** [[metadata--campaigns]]

- **What it does:** Cross-channel paid-campaign automation that launches, optimizes, and reports on B2B paid ads from a single tool.
- **Key features:**
  - Single tool to launch and manage paid campaigns across LinkedIn, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google, Reddit (Bing in development)
  - Campaign Experimentation: launches thousands of multivariate experiments testing audiences, creative, offers, and channels
  - Web Personalization: real-time site personalization for target accounts without engineers
  - Lead Enrichment: enriches form submissions with up to 20 business attributes
  - Revenue Optimization: AI auto-reallocates budget based on CRM pipeline data, not CPL/CPC
  - AI agents (Bid Agent, Targeting Agent, Analyst Agent) automate bidding, audience-building, and reporting
- **Source URL:** https://metadata.io/platform/

### Metadata Audiences (MetaMatch)

> **Wiki:** [[metadata--metamatch]]

- **What it does:** Patented personal-to-corporate identity graph that converts personal emails into B2B-targetable audiences across paid channels.
- **Key features:**
  - Patented personal-to-corporate identity graph matching 1.5B emails to ~800M B2B profiles
  - Audience activation across Facebook, Google Ads, Instagram, LinkedIn
  - Builds audiences from first-party (Salesforce), intent (6sense, G2, Bombora, Qualified), demographic, firmographic, and technographic data
  - 10+ data partners; 22 data sources surfaced for audience-building
  - Audience preview before launch (review match counts, refine criteria, add exclusions)
  - Free trial: 14 days, up to 3 custom audiences
- **Source URL:** https://metadata.io/platform/audience-targeting

### Metadata Insights / Spotlight

> **Wiki:** [[metadata--insights-spotlight]]

- **What it does:** Reporting and attribution layer that ties ad spend to CRM pipeline and revenue across campaigns, channels, audiences, and offers.
- **Key features:**
  - Reporting across campaign names, budget groups, channels, offers, and audiences
  - CRM as source of truth — bi-directional sync with Salesforce/HubSpot
  - Attribution from first impression to closed-won deal
  - Spotlight reporting product referenced in Fingerprint and Docebo case studies
- **Source URL:** https://metadata.io/

### AI Agents (Bid Agent, Targeting Agent, Analyst Agent, MetadataONE)

> **Wiki:** [[metadata--ai-agents]]

- **What it does:** Library of 12+ AI agents that automate bidding, audience-building, creative analysis, and (with MetadataONE) full campaign control via LLM chat.
- **Key features:**
  - Bid Agent: AI-powered LinkedIn bid optimization
  - Targeting Agent: builds audiences across Facebook, Google, Reddit, Instagram via MetaMatch
  - Analyst Agent: turns ad data into real-time insights and reporting
  - Campaign Execution agent: agentic, full-funnel campaign launch
  - MetadataONE (forthcoming): connects to ChatGPT/Claude to plan/launch/optimize campaigns via LLM prompts
- **Source URL:** https://metadata.io/

### Marketing Automation (use case)

> **Wiki:** [[metadata--marketing-automation]]

- **What it does:** Auto-launches and adjusts campaigns across LinkedIn, Meta, Reddit, Google, Bing using real performance signals; pairs CRM data with channel execution.
- **Key features:**
  - Build once, deploy everywhere across all paid channels
  - Group campaigns by objective; optimizer paces budgets toward revenue outcomes
  - Bidirectional CRM sync keeps audiences and goals synchronized
  - Automatic underperformer pausing and budget reallocation
- **Source URL:** https://metadata.io/platform/marketing-automation

### Revenue Optimization (use case)

> **Wiki:** [[metadata--revenue-optimization]]

- **What it does:** Pipeline/revenue-driven budget reallocation that continuously shifts spend toward channels and audiences delivering pipeline, not vanity metrics.
- **Key features:**
  - CRM-plugged optimization on pipeline, revenue, and stage progression
  - AI Agents shift budget toward down-funnel converters
  - 24/7 optimization across channels
  - Real-time, full-funnel reporting tied to revenue
- **Source URL:** https://metadata.io/platform/revenue-optimization

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** ZoomInfo Marketing (legacy ABM module) and GTM Studio (marketer/RevOps front-end) are squarely overlapped by Metadata Campaigns + MetaMatch. Both target B2B marketers running ABM/demand-gen paid campaigns; both build audiences from first-party CRM + intent data (6sense, Bombora, G2) and activate them on paid channels. Metadata explicitly names ZoomInfo as a competitor in its homepage FAQ: "We're often compared to platforms like 6sense, Demandbase, RollWorks, and ZoomInfo" (source: https://metadata.io/). MetaMatch's identity-graph + audience-targeting capability also overlaps ZoomInfo Marketing's audience-building and intent-activation (source: https://metadata.io/platform/audience-targeting).
- **Adjacent overlap:** MetaMatch's ~800M B2B profiles + 1.5B matched emails sit adjacent to ZoomInfo's Data foundation (Pillar 1) but are scoped to ad-channel targeting, not sales prospecting (source: https://metadata.io/). Metadata Lead Enrichment is adjacent to ZoomInfo Operations enrichment, but is form-side / marketing-only and lacks CRM data-quality / routing breadth (source: https://metadata.io/platform/).
- **No overlap:** No seller-facing product (no equivalent to ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace prospecting workflow). No conversation intelligence (no Chorus equivalent). No website chat product of its own — Metadata integrates with Drift/Qualified rather than competing with ZoomInfo Chat (source: https://metadata.io/platform/integrations/). No public API / MCP for external developer-agent ecosystems; MetadataONE is a marketer-facing LLM interface, not a developer surface (source: https://metadata.io/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No public Metadata-authored vs-ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced in SERP results; the G2 comparison page (https://www.g2.com/compare/metadata-io-vs-zoominfo-marketing) was blocked (HTTP 403). Closest direct framing is the homepage FAQ quote above. See flags.
