## Summary

Seismic is an AI-powered sales / revenue enablement platform sold primarily to sales-enablement leaders, CROs, and customer-facing teams at mid-market and enterprise organizations across financial services, technology, manufacturing, and healthcare. It bundles sales content management, training and coaching (Seismic Learning, formerly Lessonly), buyer engagement, programs, and analytics into a single Enablement Cloud, with deep Salesforce + Agentforce integration. Versus ZoomInfo, Seismic is mostly adjacent: it directly overlaps Chorus-style coaching workflows and the seller-front-end content/playbook layer of GTM Workspace, but it does not sell B2B contact/company/intent data and does not compete with the ZoomInfo Data foundation, GTM Context Graph, or APIs/MCP layers — Seismic is itself a ZoomInfo customer per ZoomInfo's published case study (source: https://www.zoominfo.com/about/case-studies/seismic).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) + coaching workflows ↔ Seismic Learning + Coaching & Development.** Seismic Learning provides "AI-powered role-plays," "targeted in-the-moment guidance" for managers, and "AI-driven assessments" to close performance gaps — overlapping Chorus's coaching/training surface (source: https://www.seismic.com/platform/learning/).
  - **GTM Workspace (seller front-end with AI-drafted outreach + execution) ↔ Seismic-in-Salesforce / Agentforce.** Seismic + Salesforce / Agentforce delivers "real-time, AI-powered guidance — surfacing the right content, insights, and actions inside Salesforce to help reps move deals forward," competing with GTM Workspace's seller execution surface for content delivery and playbook guidance (source: https://www.seismic.com/platform/integrations/salesforce/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio (marketer / RevOps orchestration) ↔ Seismic Programs + Content Automation.** Seismic Programs lets enablement and marketing teams "plan, execute, and measure enablement efforts in one seamless workflow" with reporting and CRM integrations — orchestration adjacent to GTM Studio's play orchestration, but content-/training-centric not data-/audience-centric (source: https://www.seismic.com/solutions/teams/sales-enablement/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM) ↔ Seismic for Marketers + Buyer & Customer Engagement.** Seismic markets "AI-powered automation and real-time insights to refine messaging and strategies based on what resonates with customers" for marketing teams — overlaps content-personalization layer of ABM but not audience targeting or intent (source: https://www.seismic.com/platform/enablement-cloud).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1) (500M contacts, 100M companies, technographics)** — Seismic does not sell B2B contact/company/intent data; it organizes and delivers customer-supplied content and integrates with CRM data. ZoomInfo's published case study confirms Seismic is itself a ZoomInfo customer using GTM Workspace for outbound (source: https://www.zoominfo.com/about/case-studies/seismic).
  - **GTM Context Graph (B2B + first-party data fusion)** — Seismic's intelligence layer reasons over Seismic-managed content engagement and CRM data, not over external B2B contact/company/intent data (source: https://www.seismic.com/platform/enablement-cloud).
  - **APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem** — Seismic exposes app integrations via Seismic Exchange and Agentforce, but no public MCP server / data API for agent builders comparable to ZoomInfo's mcp.zoominfo.com (source: https://www.seismic.com/platform/integrations/salesforce/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Public head-to-head pages exist on third-party properties (pipeline.zoominfo.com — a ZoomInfo property; 6sense.com Seismic-vs-Chorus; glimpsehq.io Seismic-vs-ZoomInfo) but no seismic.com-authored vs page. Notably, ZoomInfo's own case study reports Seismic uses ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace for outbound and reportedly attributes 39% of pipeline to ZoomInfo signals (source: https://www.zoominfo.com/about/case-studies/seismic).
