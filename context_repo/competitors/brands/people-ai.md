# People.ai

- [[people-ai--backstory-revenue-answers|Backstory Revenue Answers Platform]] — competes with GTM Context Graph

## Summary

People.ai is an enterprise **Revenue Answers Platform** (rebranding to Backstory in 2026) that automatically captures every email, meeting, call, and chat across a customer's GTM tools and matches the activity to Salesforce/Dynamics/Oracle deals to answer revenue-leader questions about deal risk, pipeline, and forecast. Their primary buyer is the CRO / VP Sales / RevOps leader at large enterprises (1,001+ employees per TrustRadius); customer logos include NVIDIA, OpenAI, Red Hat, HPE, Zscaler, Rubrik, Palo Alto Networks, Verizon, Snowflake, and IBM. The most direct competitive overlap with ZoomInfo is on the **GTM Context Graph** signal/activity layer and **Chorus** conversation intelligence — People.ai's pitch is that their nine-year activity-graph data layer is broader than call-recording-only tools and more accurate than CRM-derived insights. People.ai sells *no* third-party B2B contact database (no overlap with ZoomInfo's Pillar 1 data foundation) and is leadership-focused (no SDR/outbound surface, no marketing/ABM module, no developer/SMB ICP).

## Product Offerings

### Backstory Revenue Answers Platform (formerly People.ai)

> **Wiki:** [[people-ai--backstory-revenue-answers]]

- **What it does:** Captures every customer-facing revenue activity automatically and answers revenue-leader questions ("which deals are at risk, why, what to do next") grounded in the activity graph.
- **Key features:**
  - Automated activity capture across email, calendar, conferencing (Zoom/Teams), Slack, and chat with no rep data entry
  - AI-powered activity-to-deal matching (49 patents awarded, 50+ pending) tying every interaction to the right Salesforce account, opportunity, and contact
  - Engagement signals that flag deals going quiet, single-threaded deals, and missing stakeholders before they slip
  - "Revenue Answers" interface returning direct answers instead of dashboards
  - Configurable matching rules, Agents, signals tuned to the customer's CRM setup, motion, and methodology
  - Multi-CRM support — connects Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and Oracle simultaneously into one activity view
  - 2-4 week deployment with two years of historical activity analyzed on day one
  - Access answers in the web app, embedded in CRM, or via AI tool / MCP integration
- **Source URL:** https://www.people.ai/

### AI-Native Forecasting (launched October 2025)

> **Wiki:** [[people-ai--ai-native-forecasting]]

- **What it does:** Bottoms-up AI forecast that analyzes engagement health, deal velocity, stakeholder involvement, and historical patterns at the individual deal level instead of rolling up rep guesses.
- **Key features:**
  - Bottoms-up deal-level forecast instead of top-down rep aggregation
  - Flags deals where engagement is dropping, stakeholders aren't responding, or activity doesn't match projected close date
  - Reported customer outcome of 20-30% improvement in forecast accuracy (per SaaStr writeup)
  - Grounded in proprietary nine-year activity dataset (billions of touchpoints, 160M+ business contacts)
- **Source URL:** https://www.saastr.com/saastr-ai-app-of-the-week-people-ai-the-answer-platform-that-turns-your-sales-activity-data-into-the-only-forecast-you-can-trust/

### Methodology Enforcement (MEDDPICC and others)

> **Wiki:** [[people-ai--methodology-enforcement]]

- **What it does:** Analyzes deal context to identify where sellers are in the customer's chosen sales methodology and surfaces next-best actions without requiring reps to fill out qualification forms.
- **Key features:**
  - Methodology runs behind the scenes — no extra rep data entry
  - Surfaces gaps in qualification stages (champion, decision criteria, paper process, etc.)
  - Embedded opportunity scorecards inside Salesforce as a playbook for new sellers (per Five9 case study)
- **Source URL:** https://www.saastr.com/saastr-ai-app-of-the-week-people-ai-the-answer-platform-that-turns-your-sales-activity-data-into-the-only-forecast-you-can-trust/

### MCP Integration / AI workflow access

> **Wiki:** [[people-ai--mcp-integration]]

- **What it does:** Exposes People.ai's activity data and revenue intelligence to external AI agents and tools via Model Context Protocol so the data is available "wherever you work."
- **Key features:**
  - Model Context Protocol server announced February 2026 (BusinessWire)
  - Same answers accessible in web app, CRM, or third-party AI tool
  - Positioned for the "agentic AI era" per CEO Jason Ambrose
- **Source URL:** https://www.people.ai/

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Enterprise B2B SaaS, technology / cybersecurity (Palo Alto Networks, Zscaler, Forcepoint, Ping Identity), telecom (Verizon, Five9), cloud / data infrastructure (Snowflake, Databricks, Cohesity, Domino Data Labs).
- **Company size called out:** Enterprise / large enterprise (1,001+ employees per TrustRadius "most used by"); explicitly NOT for SMB or rep productivity.
- **Persona / role focus:** CRO, VP of Sales, RevOps / Revenue Operations, Sales Operations & Enablement, Sales Process Coaches.
- **Use cases promoted:** Pipeline visibility and deal-risk detection; revenue forecasting accuracy; Salesforce CRM hygiene / automated activity capture; methodology enforcement (MEDDPICC) without manual rep input; account / pipeline health and engagement scoring.

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, Oracle (multi-CRM simultaneous support).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Outreach (activity-sync), Groove (activity-sync). Per TrustRadius, People.ai has no native cadence/sequence functionality and is positioned as a complement to engagement vendors, not a replacement.
- **Data / enrichment:** _No native B2B contact database — relies on customer's first-party activity._
- **Other notable integrations:** Gmail, Outlook, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Slack, Model Context Protocol (MCP) server for AI workflow access.

- Activity-to-deal matching accuracy at enterprise scale (49 patents awarded, 50+ pending) — Five9 chose People.ai specifically because matching accuracy was "materially better" than competing vendors (source: https://www.saastr.com/saastr-ai-app-of-the-week-people-ai-the-answer-platform-that-turns-your-sales-activity-data-into-the-only-forecast-you-can-trust/).
- Repositioned as "Revenue Answers Platform" — direct answers to "which deals are at risk" rather than dashboards. Tagline: "Other tools record activity. Backstory drives revenue." (source: https://www.people.ai/).
- 2-4 week implementation with two years of historical activity analyzed on day one, no ramp period (source: https://www.people.ai/).
- Named Visionary in 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Revenue Action Orchestration (source: https://www.saastr.com/saastr-ai-app-of-the-week-people-ai-the-answer-platform-that-turns-your-sales-activity-data-into-the-only-forecast-you-can-trust/).
- Built for revenue leaders making commit decisions, not for individual rep productivity (source: https://www.people.ai/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph (signal / activity layer) ↔ People.ai automated activity capture + activity-to-deal matching.** People.ai "captures every email, meeting, call, and chat across your team, processes that activity against your deal history" and "maps field activity to your open pipeline" — the same first-party activity-context layer ZoomInfo positions as Pillar 2 of the GTM Context Graph (source: https://www.people.ai/).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) ↔ Backstory.** People.ai explicitly frames itself against pure call-recording vendors: "Call recording tools capture transcripts of what was said on calls. Backstory captures everything — calls, emails, meetings, and chat — matches it all to the right deals." (source: https://www.people.ai/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing) ↔ Salesforce CRM hygiene via auto-logging.** Forcepoint quote: "Every customer-facing activity is captured in Salesforce automatically — contact created, tied to the right opportunity, no extra effort from the team. CRM hygiene solved." (source: https://www.people.ai/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace ↔ Backstory revenue answers + methodology enforcement embedded in Salesforce.** TrustRadius compares People.ai head-to-head against ZoomInfo Sales as the relevant alternative; Five9 embeds People.ai opportunity scorecards inside Salesforce (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/people-ai-vs-zoominfo-sales).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio ↔ persona-based marketing insights from activity data.** Five9 used People.ai activity data to discover that IT-persona involvement correlated with larger deal sizes and built a persona-based marketing strategy on it (source: https://www.saastr.com/saastr-ai-app-of-the-week-people-ai-the-answer-platform-that-turns-your-sales-activity-data-into-the-only-forecast-you-can-trust/).
  - **APIs & MCP ↔ People.ai MCP integration.** Both vendors expose data layers via Model Context Protocol; People.ai announced its MCP server in February 2026.
- **No overlap:**
  - **B2B contact data (Data (Pillar 1): 500M contacts, 100M companies, intent data)** — People.ai has no third-party data foundation; their entire value derives from the customer's first-party activity.
  - **Sales engagement / outbound prospecting** — TrustRadius reviewer: "People.ai doesn't have a template email send like a sequence or cadence feature... it is more of a complement to the email automation vendors than a competitor."
  - **SDR / outbound rep tooling** — People.ai is explicitly built for revenue leaders, not individual reps.
  - **Marketing/ABM platform** — no GTM Studio analog.
  - **SMB / mid-market** — TrustRadius "most used by" is enterprise (1,001+ employees) only.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public People.ai-authored vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced._ TrustRadius head-to-head (third-party) positions People.ai as "a revenue intelligence platform" against ZoomInfo Sales as "a modern go-to-market platform... helps find buyers based on Ideal Customer Profiles, advanced company attributes, and accurate contact information in B2B" — implying the two products serve adjacent rather than identical jobs (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/people-ai-vs-zoominfo-sales).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Answers Platform for Revenue Leaders" — capture every revenue activity, connect it to deal history, and deliver direct answers about which deals are at risk, why, and what to do next. "Other tools record activity. Backstory drives revenue."
- **Recurring proof points:** Customer logos NVIDIA, OpenAI, Red Hat, HPE, Zscaler, Rubrik, AMD, Five9, Seismic, Pluralsight, TTEC, Hexagon, Ping Identity, Lily.ai, Forcepoint, Databricks, Elastic, Cyberark, Cohesity, Palo Alto Networks, Verizon, Zoom, Snowflake, IBM; 100,000+ sellers using daily; G2 Enterprise Leader with 600+ reviews; 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant Visionary for Revenue Action Orchestration; Five9 saved sellers 1,000+ hours annually; reported 20-30% forecast accuracy improvement.
- **Tone / category framing:** Repositioning from "revenue intelligence" to "Revenue Answers Platform" — explicitly contrasting with call-recording (Gong-style) and forecasting (Clari-style) point tools by claiming the broader activity-graph data layer. Ambrose framing: "Humans handle the questions and goals. AI handles the answers, actions, and outcomes."
