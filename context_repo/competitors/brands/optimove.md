## Summary

Optimove is a B2C "Customer-Led / Positionless Marketing" platform sold to CRM, customer-marketing, and loyalty leaders at iGaming, retail/eCommerce, and other consumer-facing brands. It pairs a real-time CDP, AI decisioning (OptiGenie agents), multichannel campaign execution, on-site personalization, and gamification under one platform, and pitches a single marketer running campaigns end-to-end without engineering or analyst handoffs. The ICP and data model are fundamentally B2C, so direct overlap with ZoomInfo's B2B GTM platform is minimal — the only material overlap is the orchestration model (Optimove Orchestrate vs. GTM Studio's play building) and multichannel execution (Optimove Engage vs. ZoomInfo Marketing), both of which Optimove operates over consumer profiles rather than B2B account data. Optimove does not sell B2B contact data, GTM Context Graph–style intelligence, seller workspaces, or developer/MCP surfaces, and ZoomInfo does not sell digital web personalization, gamification, or in-play B2C messaging.

## Product Offerings

### Optimove Orchestrate

> **Wiki:** [[optimove--orchestrate]]

- **What it does:** Real-time CDP + AI decisioning + journey orchestration platform that unifies identity, behavior, transactional history, and real-time interactions into one customer profile and decides next-best-action across channels.
- **Key features:**
  - Unified real-time customer profile (identity, behavior, transactional, real-time interactions)
  - OptiGenie agentic AI for proactive/prescriptive insights and self-optimizing journeys
  - Self-service no-code segmentation with hierarchical, dynamic, self-updating audiences
  - Single Marketing View command center across every channel and sending platform
  - Native + API-based integrations, hybrid ETL, and microservices for cross-stack personalization
  - Multi-touch attribution, productized control groups, and incrementality measurement on KPIs like CLTV and revenue lift
- **Source URL:** https://www.optimove.com/platform/orchestrate

### Optimove Engage

> **Wiki:** [[optimove--engage]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered customer engagement platform for designing, executing, and optimizing multichannel campaigns (email, SMS, push, web) with embedded CDP functionality.
- **Key features:**
  - Native channels: email, SMS, mobile push, web, ad networks, WhatsApp
  - Generative AI for on-brand copy, subject lines, and creative assets
  - Embedded CDP that unifies customer data into a single profile
  - Visual journey canvas connecting real-time inbound and outbound interactions
  - 150+ out-of-the-box connectors for data and channel integrations
  - Built-in incrementality, multitouch attribution, and experimentation
  - AI-powered smart content moderation and governance for review/approval
- **Source URL:** https://www.optimove.com/platform/engage

### Optimove Personalize

> **Wiki:** [[optimove--personalize]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered digital personalization engine that tailors web, app, and digital surfaces with real-time content recommendations and dynamic page layouts.
- **Key features:**
  - 20+ AI-driven recommendation models (collaborative filtering, content-based, trending, "customers also bought")
  - Dynamic page-layout personalization that rearranges elements per customer
  - OptiGenie AI on-site search personalization
  - Symmetric on-site + email/app personalized content (web-to-inbox consistency)
  - Layered business rules over AI models (margin, stock, brand constraints)
  - Native A/B/n testing with revenue-tied attribution
- **Source URL:** https://www.optimove.com/platform/personalize

### Optimove AI (OptiGenie)

> **Wiki:** [[optimove--optigenie]]

- **What it does:** Intelligence layer spanning predictive, generative, prescriptive, and agentic AI across the marketing lifecycle — embedded inside Optimove rather than sold as a standalone product.
- **Key features:**
  - Predictive AI for churn, loyalty, and upsell forecasting from first-party customer data
  - Audience Decisioning Agent that surfaces overlooked segments and campaign gaps
  - Campaign Decisioning Agent that auto-deploys next-best-action
  - Content Decisioning Agent for on-brand subject lines, creative, tailored messaging
  - Content Intelligence Agent that personalizes by tone, composition, color preference
  - Journey Decisioning Agent for self-optimizing experiments
  - Insights Agent (chat-based) for KPI-tied campaign idea surfacing
- **Source URL:** https://www.optimove.com/platform/optimove-ai

### Optimove Gamify

> **Wiki:** [[optimove--gamify]]

- **What it does:** AI-orchestrated gamification module — branded mini-games, challenges, leaderboards, and loyalty mechanics that feed engagement data back into the CDP.
- **Key features:**
  - 60+ pre-built games for acquisition, retention, and loyalty
  - Build and deploy branded games across web, app, and in-platform without developer support
  - OptiGenie AI dynamically selects and delivers personalized games inside player journeys
  - Gamified interaction data feeds back into segmentation and personalization
- **Source URL:** https://www.optimove.com/solutions/igaming

- "Positionless Marketing" platform framing — eliminates the traditional marketing "assembly line" so a single marketer can plan, create, and launch a campaign end-to-end (FDJ United testimonial: end-to-end campaign "in under 24 hours") (source: https://www.optimove.com/).
- Forrester Total Economic Impact study cited as evidence of an 88% campaign-efficiency boost (source: https://www.optimove.com/resources/learning-center/customer-data-platform).
- Vertical specialization in iGaming — 56% of EGR Power 50 operators and 70% of the Top Ten are clients; 20+ iGaming-specific AI models; named Gartner Visionary two consecutive years (source: https://www.optimove.com/solutions/igaming).
- Pricing model has no per-seat fees and no data-usage caps — based on volume of customer data, active profiles, and scope of capabilities (source: https://www.optimove.com/platform/orchestrate).
- OptiGenie agentic AI suite spans predictive, generative, prescriptive, and agentic capabilities (Audience, Campaign, Content, Journey, Insights, Content Advisor agents) inside one platform (source: https://www.optimove.com/platform/optimove-ai).
- Snowflake data sharing — clients expose a database replica to Optimove with no ETL required (source: https://www.optimove.com/resources/learning-center/customer-data-platform).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Optimove sells a B2C customer-led marketing / real-time CDP platform; ZoomInfo sells a B2B AI GTM platform built on B2B contact/company data, the GTM Context Graph, and seller/marketer/developer surfaces. None of Optimove's products compete head-to-head with ZoomInfo's data foundation, GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, APIs & MCP, Chorus, or ZoomInfo Operations.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - GTM Studio (orchestration / play building) ↔ Optimove Orchestrate — both are the single command surface where a non-engineering operator builds segments, orchestrates multichannel journeys, and measures incrementality. Optimove's "Single Marketing View ... 360 command center" with "self-service flows from ingestion to no-code segmentation" mirrors GTM Studio's natural-language audience building, but Optimove operates over B2C consumer profiles rather than B2B GTM data (source: https://www.optimove.com/platform/orchestrate).
  - ZoomInfo Marketing (legacy ABM / orchestration module) ↔ Optimove Engage — both deliver multichannel campaign execution (email, SMS, push, web, ads) with attribution and experimentation. ZoomInfo Marketing is account-targeted (ABM display + intent activation); Optimove Engage is consumer-message-volume-priced and built around individual customer profiles (source: https://www.optimove.com/platform/engage).
- **No overlap:**
  - Optimove Personalize — real-time on-site / in-app product recommendations and dynamic page layouts for B2C eCommerce; ZoomInfo does not sell a digital web personalization / recommendation engine (source: https://www.optimove.com/platform/personalize).
  - Optimove Gamify — branded mini-games, leaderboards, and gamified loyalty mechanics (60+ games) for B2C engagement; no analog in ZoomInfo's product line (source: https://www.optimove.com/solutions/igaming).
  - iGaming-specific solution — 20+ iGaming-specific AI models, in-play triggered messaging during sporting events, and bonus/promotion management; ZoomInfo does not sell into iGaming and does not provide in-play / event-driven B2C messaging (source: https://www.optimove.com/solutions/igaming).
  - Real-time B2C CDP — Optimove unifies first-party identity, transactional, behavioral, and real-time interaction data on individual consumers; ZoomInfo's data foundation is third-party B2B contact/company data fused with first-party CRM signals, not a B2C CDP (source: https://www.optimove.com/resources/learning-center/customer-data-platform).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Positionless Marketing" — give a single marketer the data, creative, and optimization powers to execute a campaign end-to-end without handoffs to engineers, creatives, or analysts. Sub-tagline: "Do anything. Be everything." (source: https://www.optimove.com/).
- **Recurring proof points:** Forrester Total Economic Impact study citing an 88% campaign-efficiency boost; Gartner Visionary recognition (two consecutive years per the iGaming page); 56% of EGR Power 50 iGaming operators (and 70% of the Top Ten) are clients; named-customer testimonials from FDJ United, Entain, Kaizen Gaming, Stake, Bet365, Lottomatica, Sisal, BetMGM, MyHeritage, Blain's Farm & Fleet, Varsity Tutors, Jabra, Musti Group; SBC North America, SBC Latino America, and EGR Italy industry awards (source: https://www.optimove.com/, https://www.optimove.com/solutions/igaming, https://www.optimove.com/resources/learning-center/customer-data-platform).
- **Tone / category framing:** Self-described as a "Customer-Led Marketing Platform" / "Positionless Marketing Platform" and a "real-time CDP + Relationship Marketing Hub." Explicitly positions against the "assembly line" model of marketing where engineering, creative, and analytics gate execution.
