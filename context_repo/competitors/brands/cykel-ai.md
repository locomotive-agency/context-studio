# Cykel AI

## Sub-products

- [[cykel-ai--eve-sales|Eve — AI SDR / AI Sales Agent]] — competes with GTM Workspace
- [[cykel-ai--samson-research|Samson — AI Business Analyst / Research Agent]] — competes with GTM Workspace (adjacent)

## Summary

Cykel AI is an agentic "digital worker" platform — three named AI agents (Eve for sales, Lucy for recruitment, Samson for research) running on a TaskOS runtime — sold to founders, sales leaders, and recruitment teams at SMB through enterprise scale. Eve is a direct overlap with ZoomInfo's seller-facing GTM Workspace and the data-foundation pitch: it bundles a 700M-contact dataset, AI personalization, multichannel email + LinkedIn outreach, and meeting booking into one seat-priced product. Cykel's pitch is replacement economics — "$1.63 digital worker daily rate" and a savings calculator showing $110,400 annual savings vs a $120,000 human SDR. Adjacent overlap exists in the Cykel Workflows / TaskOS layer, but Lucy (recruitment) and Samson (analyst reports) sit fully outside ZoomInfo's GTM scope.

## Product Offerings

### Eve — AI SDR / AI Sales Agent

- **What it does:** Autonomous 24/7 AI sales-development agent that finds prospects, researches accounts, writes personalized email + LinkedIn outreach, follows up, and books meetings.
- **Key features:**
  - Lead Research across 700M+ B2B contact profiles to find ICP-matching decision-makers
  - Outbound automation that runs full multichannel campaigns (email + LinkedIn) and tracks engagement
  - GTM AI™ — auto-designs sequences, suggests ICPs, and drafts campaign messaging from a company description
  - AI personalization that researches each prospect and writes custom messages
  - Multichannel outreach managing multiple inboxes and LinkedIn profiles per team
  - Bundled B2B contact data including verified emails and phone-number enrichment (Pro tier and above)
  - Approval workflow — users review prospect lists, message templates, sending limits, and pause/adjust campaigns in real time
- **Source URL:** https://www.cykel.ai/eve

### Lucy — AI Recruiter / AI Agent Recruiter

- **What it does:** Autonomous 24/7 AI recruitment agent that sources candidates from a 300M-profile pool, screens inbound CVs at scale, and runs personalized email + LinkedIn outreach to candidates.
- **Key features:**
  - Deep People Search™ across a 300M-candidate global talent pool with unlimited searches
  - AI screening with customizable, auditable criteria; Applicant AutoScreen™ on Pro tier
  - Multichannel candidate outreach (email + LinkedIn) with multi-sequence personalization
  - ATS integration — Greenhouse, Ashby, Teamtailor, Workday, Pinpoint named; bi-directional sync on Pro tier
  - Search & Rediscovery — chat-based AI search across the customer's ATS data
  - Premium data included — bundled email and phone enrichment across multiple data sources
  - AI Pipeline Intelligence Chatbot trained on workspace and ATS data
- **Source URL:** https://www.cykel.ai/lucy

### Samson — AI Business Analyst / Research Agent (beta)

- **What it does:** AI research agent that produces professional-quality company research and analyst reports for investment, consulting, and advisory teams.
- **Key features:**
  - Researches and writes pro-level company reports
  - Targets investment, consulting, and advisory use cases
  - Currently access-only (Request Access via sales)
  - Launched in beta in 2025
- **Source URL:** https://www.cykel.ai/

### Cykel Workflows / TaskOS

- **What it does:** AI workflow-automation layer (TaskOS) that lets enterprises build and run AI agents that execute multi-step tasks against existing apps via integrations or a Browser Assistant when no API exists.
- **Key features:**
  - AI-powered workflow automation triggered by inputs (instructions, emails, URLs, files)
  - Native integrations with thousands of third-party apps — Google Workspace, Salesforce, and LinkedIn called out by name
  - Browser Assistant for UI automation when no API is available
  - Workflow primitives — composing/sending emails, API integration, data analysis, file generation, web search, AI reasoning steps
  - Co-Pilot AI (assists humans on tasks) and Worker AI (delivers complex tasks unsupervised) framing
- **Source URL:** https://www.cykel.ai/resources/introducing-cykel-workflows

- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Outbound prospecting and meeting booking — "Engage 1k leads/week, 2x your pipeline, Book 25 meetings/month" (source: https://www.cykel.ai/eve)

  - Candidate sourcing and screening at scale
  - Resume / inbound CV screening with auditable AI criteria
  - Company / market research reports (Samson)
  - Cross-tool workflow automation including UI automation via Browser Assistant (Workflows)

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Multi-agent "digital worker" framing — Cykel positions Eve, Lucy, and Samson as named, persona-style AI agents on a single TaskOS platform rather than features inside a tool. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/company)
- Headline replacement economics: "$1.63 digital worker daily rate" and a savings calculator anchored at "$110,400 annual savings" vs a $120,000 human SDR. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/sign-up/eve)
- Bundled data and outreach — one subscription replaces separate B2B data, sourcing, enrichment, and sequencing tools. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/eve)
- Native LinkedIn execution (connection requests + messages) inside the same agent that handles email — Pro tier upsell hinges on LinkedIn outreach. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/pricing)
- Browser Assistant for UI automation when an integration API doesn't exist. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/resources/introducing-cykel-workflows)
- Public-market governance / disclosure (London Stock Exchange listing) — atypical for an early-stage AI SDR vendor. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Eve vs ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace.** Eve covers prospecting, contact discovery, sequencing, and meeting-booking — the same daily seller surface ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace AI agent layer targets — and bundles 700M B2B contacts as the data layer. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/eve)
  - **Eve / Lucy bundled data layer vs ZoomInfo Data Foundation.** Eve cites 700M+ verified B2B contact profiles with email and phone-number enrichment; Lucy cites 300M candidate profiles with personal + professional email enrichment — direct overlap with ZoomInfo's contact-dataset positioning, though Cykel makes no equivalent claim about 95%-accuracy multi-source verification or 300+ human researchers. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/pricing)
  - **GTM AI™ inside Eve vs ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio.** GTM AI™ auto-builds ICPs, designs sequences, and refines campaigns from a company description — the natural-language audience and play building that GTM Studio markets, but scoped to outbound sales. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/eve)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **Cykel Workflows / TaskOS + Browser Assistant vs APIs & MCP.** Cykel sells an agentic workflow runtime with thousands of integrations and UI-level browser automation — adjacent to ZoomInfo's API/MCP positioning. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/resources/introducing-cykel-workflows)
  - **Eve enterprise tier (audit logs, sending limits, approval workflow) vs ZoomInfo Operations / RevOps surface.** Cykel surfaces enterprise security, audit logs, and approval workflows for outbound — adjacent to RevOps requirements but not a CRM data-quality / routing product. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/pricing)
- **No overlap:**
  - Talent acquisition / recruitment automation (Lucy) — ZoomInfo does not sell an AI recruiter or candidate-sourcing product. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/lucy)
  - Investment / consulting / advisory analyst-report generation (Samson) — outside ZoomInfo's GTM-team scope. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/)
  - General workflow automation across non-GTM apps via UI / Browser Assistant — outside ZoomInfo's GTM-platform scope. (source: https://www.cykel.ai/resources/introducing-cykel-workflows)
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Cykel does not publish a direct "Cykel vs ZoomInfo" page; the only Cykel-authored vs-page compares Cykel against Apollo / Clay / Outreach / Salesloft, not ZoomInfo.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Digital workers, 24/7 growth" — named AI agents (Eve, Lucy, Samson) on the TaskOS platform that join your team to handle outbound sales, recruitment, and research at superhuman speed and scale.
- **Recurring proof points:**
  - $1.63 digital-worker daily rate / 5–10x output vs a human SDR
  - Eve case studies: Sundae Bar 3x outbound volume; Beach +248% reply rate; Forces Horizon CEO testimonial
  - "Trusted by 400+ teams" — logos include Shift, Accel-backed companies, Trustpilot, Aero, Doxy.me, Prehype, Klarity, Intuit
  - Lucy claim: 30–50% reduction in time-to-hire, 8+ hours saved per recruiter per week, 90% reduction in resume screening time
  - London Stock Exchange listing as a public-markets credibility signal
  - DeepSeek R1 integrated across the platform
- **Tone / category framing:** "Digital worker platform" — agents that augment (not replace) human teams, sitting on top of an agentic runtime called TaskOS. The voice is anti-SDR-headcount and replacement-economics-heavy.
