## Summary

Terminus is an account-based marketing (ABM) / account-based experience (ABX) platform sold to B2B Marketing leaders, Demand Gen, Customer Marketing, RevOps, and CMOs; it executes account-based advertising, web personalization, chat, and email signature marketing against intent-prioritized target accounts. It was acquired by Demand Science Group on November 12, 2024 and now operates under the DemandScience brand, with terminus.com still serving the legacy ABM product nav while most product child URLs redirect to DemandScience equivalents. Direct overlap with ZoomInfo is concentrated in ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM module / GTM Studio successor) — same category positioning, same multi-channel execution use cases (target account ID, account-based advertising, web personalization, intent prioritization). Terminus does not compete on ZoomInfo's seller surfaces (Sales / Copilot / GTM Workspace), Chorus conversation intelligence, or APIs/MCP, and adds adjacent surface (email signature marketing, post-merger content syndication via DemandScience) that ZoomInfo does not offer.

## Product Offerings

### Terminus ABM Platform (legacy / branded surface)

> **Wiki:** [[terminus-demandscience--abm-platform]]

- **What it does:** Account-based marketing platform that helps B2B teams identify, prioritize and engage best-fit accounts across native channels including ads, chat, web personalization, and email signature.
- **Key features:**
  - Platform Overview — identify, prioritize and engage best-fit accounts (terminus.com/terminus-platform/ legacy nav entry, now redirects to DemandScience Advertising)
  - Prospect Engine — discover unknown, best-fit accounts and contacts
  - Data Studio — target and segment ICP with firmographic, intent, and other data
  - Measurement Studio — measure and optimize pipeline and revenue outcomes
  - GTM Insights — make account insights actionable for Sales and CS
  - Intent Data — prioritize and contextualize outreach based on in-market signals
  - TEAM framework (Target, Engage, Activate, Measure) bridging marketing and sales
  - G2 Leader in ABM for 17 consecutive quarters (per Nov 2024 merger press release)
- **Source URL:** https://terminus.com/demandscience/

### Ad Experiences (Account-Based Advertising)

> **Wiki:** [[terminus-demandscience--ad-experiences]]

- **What it does:** Multi-channel B2B advertising tool delivering display and social ads to verified decision-makers; positioned as the highest-rated B2B advertising tool on the platform.
- **Key features:**
  - Display + Social advertising aimed at verified decision-makers
  - Coverage across display, social, CTV, video, and audio (post-merger, branded as Media Placement & Management)
  - Tethered to first-party intent data and ABM target lists
  - Account-level reporting and in-flight optimization
  - Integration with LinkedIn ad targeting per G2 reviewer testimony
  - Brand-safety and ad-fraud minimization
- **Source URL:** https://terminus.com/ad-experiences/

### Conversational Marketing (Chat Experiences)

> **Wiki:** [[terminus-demandscience--chat-experiences]]

- **What it does:** Native chatbot and live chat product for converting website visitors, integrated with the ABM target-account playbook.
- **Key features:**
  - Native chatbot and live chat
  - ABM-aware routing — different experiences for target vs. non-target accounts
  - Web visitor conversion as part of multi-channel ABM
  - G2 reviewer notes: "Chatbot and ABM display ad platform that works best when tethered to intent data"
- **Source URL:** https://terminus.com/demandscience/

### Email Signature Marketing (Sigstr legacy)

> **Wiki:** [[terminus-demandscience--email-signature-marketing]]

- **What it does:** Turns every employee 1:1 email into a targeted marketing channel via dynamic, recipient-based banner ads in the email signature.
- **Key features:**
  - Dynamic email signature banners synced to ABM target lists
  - Recipient-based (ABM) banners that change per recipient
  - Shuffle banners to rotate creative
  - Listed as a top-mentioned strength in GetApp verified reviews
  - Originated from the Sigstr acquisition prior to the DemandScience merger
- **Source URL:** https://terminus.com/demandscience/

### Web Experiences (Web Personalization)

> **Wiki:** [[terminus-demandscience--web-experiences]]

- **Key features:**

### ABX by DemandScience (post-merger consolidation)

> **Wiki:** [[terminus-demandscience--abx]]

- **Key features:**
  - Concurrent ABM and Syndication activation in one platform
  - Unified reporting across diverse channels
  - Global Audience of 225M+ IT decision makers from 17M+ companies
  - Demand Gen solutions: Content Syndication, HQL, BANT

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Terminus competes head-to-head with **ZoomInfo Marketing** (legacy ABM module / GTM Studio successor) — same category positioning, same multi-channel execution use cases (target account ID, account-based advertising, web personalization, intent prioritization). The SERP returned a dedicated 6sense.com "ZoomInfo vs Terminus" comparison page in the Account Based Marketing category, and SourceForge surfaces a "Terminus vs. ZoomInfo GTM Studio" comparison (source: https://terminus.com/demandscience/). Terminus's **Intent Data + Prospect Engine** also overlap ZoomInfo's Intent Data / buyer-signal layer, and **Measurement Studio + GTM Insights** overlap ZoomInfo Marketing's reporting and orchestration.
- **Adjacent overlap:** **ZoomInfo Chat** vs. Terminus **Chat Experiences** (Conversational Marketing) — both convert anonymous and known website visitors via native chatbot/live chat; ZoomInfo Chat ties to ZoomInfo's contact graph, Terminus Chat ties to its account-list graph (source: https://terminus.com/demandscience/). **ZoomInfo Operations** (CRM enrichment + audience builder) vs. Terminus **Data Studio** + DemandScience Audience Builder (post-merger) — pre-activation enrichment and segmentation overlap, though Terminus's surface is marketing-attached rather than CRM-resident.
- **No overlap:**
  - ZoomInfo's seller-side products (ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace) — Terminus has no AE/SDR-facing prospecting UI; it sells exclusively to marketers and through them to sales/CS as downstream consumers of insights (source: https://terminus.com/demandscience/).
  - Conversation intelligence (Chorus) — no Terminus product covers call recording, deal intelligence, or coaching.
  - APIs & MCP / agent-builder ecosystem — Terminus has no developer-facing API platform or MCP server.
  - Email signature marketing as a paid channel — ZoomInfo does not offer a Sigstr-equivalent product; this is Terminus-only territory.
  - Content syndication and BANT/HQL paid lead delivery (added via the DemandScience merger) — ZoomInfo does not run a syndication network (source: https://demandscience.com/press-releases/terminus/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ No first-party Terminus vs ZoomInfo or DemandScience vs ZoomInfo page surfaced from terminus.com itself; only third-party comparisons (6sense, GetApp, Slashdot, SourceForge, TrustRadius, Exafol) appear.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "You can't afford to miss your pipeline targets, much less revenue goals. Identify and align on your next best customers, prioritize in-market accounts, and execute in the most native channels — including the industry's highest-rated B2B advertising tool. And do it with proven playbooks and expert guidance for every stage of your revenue flywheel." (source: https://terminus.com/demandscience/)
- **Recurring proof points:** G2 Leader in ABM for 17 consecutive quarters; 1,000+ customers including Gainsight, Roche, Dow Jones; 313% ROI over three years (Forrester TEI); 40% reduction in cost per targeted account; 60% more pipeline; 4.5 / 5 average across 140 GetApp verified reviews; $63.4M revenue at acquisition (per Latka company profile cited in SERP).
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as "the leading account-centric platform for revenue growth" — explicitly an ABM/ABX platform first, with a multi-channel execution philosophy ("most native channels") rather than a data-first or AI-first pitch. Post-merger framing emphasizes "account based demand generation" as the new category and ABX (Account-Based Experience) as the next-generation evolution of ABM.
