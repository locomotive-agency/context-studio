## Summary

PrivCo is a U.S.-only private-company financial intelligence database built primarily for investment bankers, PE/VC analysts, M&A teams, and other research-led buyers — not for high-volume B2B prospecting. The overlap with ZoomInfo is narrow: PrivCo competes on the company-data slice of ZoomInfo's data foundation (revenue, EBITDA, valuation estimates on the bootstrapped 80% of U.S. private companies that ZoomInfo's record may not deeply quantify) and offers verified executive contacts geared to deal-sourcing rather than scaled outbound. PrivCo has no equivalent to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, intent, technographics, conversation intelligence, or ABM workflows — its product is a research database with an API, a Microsoft Copilot integration, and a Chrome extension. The third-party comparison surfaced frames the choice as research-vs-execution and explicitly states "PrivCo is built for private-company financial intelligence … while ZoomInfo is built for prospecting and enrichment" (source: https://prospeo.io/s/privco-vs-zoominfo). PrivCo was acquired by Leviathan Gold Ltd in June 2025 (per SERP snippet, page not extracted).

## Product Offerings

### PrivCo Database (client portal)

> **Wiki:** [[privco--database]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered private-company research portal covering 5M+ U.S. private companies with proprietary revenue, EBITDA, and valuation estimates plus verified executive contacts (source: https://www.privco.com/product).
- **Key features:**
  - AI-powered chat search across the entire database, partnered with industry-leading LLMs
  - Proprietary estimation models for revenue, profit, and valuation on bootstrapped / non-VC-backed firms
  - Coverage stats: 5M+ U.S. private companies, $2.5T+ combined revenue, 100K+ M&A deals tracked, 220M+ employees
  - Verified executive contact data (1.9M+ on homepage; 70M+ cited on product page)
  - Advanced filters by ownership, funding, geography, industry, revenue, growth stage
  - PrivCo AI List Builder for prospect / target list creation
- **Source URL:** https://www.privco.com/product

### Private Market Data API & Custom Datasets

> **Wiki:** [[privco--api]]

- **What it does:** RESTful API plus analyst-built custom data exports delivering bulk private-company financial data into client systems via JSON or CSV (source: https://www.privco.com/product/api).
- **Key features:**
  - 146MM+ data points across 1,000 verticals (revenues, EBITDA, valuations, fundings, deals)
  - RESTful API with documented endpoints (e.g. `/v1/companies/search`) and bearer-token authentication
  - Bulk extracts in JSON or CSV
  - Analyst-built custom datasets on request
  - API access gated to the Unlimited / Enterprise tier
- **Source URL:** https://www.privco.com/product/api

### Microsoft Copilot Integration

> **Wiki:** [[privco--copilot-integration]]

- **What it does:** Lets users query PrivCo's private-company database from inside Microsoft Copilot via natural-language prompts (source: https://www.privco.com/product).
- **Key features:**
  - Access PrivCo data inside Microsoft Copilot
  - Natural-language research, analysis, and action on private-company data
- **Source URL:** https://www.privco.com/product

### PrivCo Chrome Extension

> **Wiki:** [[privco--chrome-extension]]

- **What it does:** Browser extension that surfaces PrivCo's private-company financial data on companies as the user browses the web (source: https://www.privco.com/product).
- **Key features:**
  - Discover private companies in PrivCo's database while browsing
  - In-browser access to financial data on private firms
- **Source URL:** https://www.privco.com/product

### Custom Research / Analyst Reports

> **Wiki:** [[privco--custom-research]]

- **What it does:** On-request engagements where PrivCo analysts deliver customized reports on specific companies or market segments (source: https://www.privco.com/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1) (company data) ↔ PrivCo Database.** Both sell company-level B2B data; PrivCo's depth on bootstrapped / non-VC-backed U.S. private firms (revenue, EBITDA, valuation estimates) directly competes with ZoomInfo's company records on companies where ZoomInfo lacks granular financials (source: https://www.privco.com/product).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / contact data ↔ PrivCo executive contacts.** PrivCo's "Sales Teams" and "Business Development" use cases promote prospect-list building and decision-maker outreach using verified executive contacts. The third-party comparison qualifies the overlap as narrow: PrivCo's contacts are "more stable for deal-sourcing workflows where you're tracking the same 50 targets over months, not blasting 5,000 cold emails" (sources: https://www.privco.com/use-cases/sales-teams, https://prospeo.io/s/privco-vs-zoominfo).
- **Adjacent overlap:** APIs & MCP ↔ PrivCo Private Market Data API. PrivCo's REST API with JSON/CSV exports (gated to Unlimited / Enterprise) overlaps the data-delivery channel of ZoomInfo's APIs, but the payload (private-company financials) is different from ZoomInfo's contact / intent / technographic data, and PrivCo has no equivalent to ZoomInfo's MCP server (source: https://www.privco.com/product/api).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph** — PrivCo does not fuse third-party data with first-party CRM / conversation / behavioral signals; it is a stand-alone research database (source: https://www.privco.com/product).
  - **GTM Workspace / GTM Studio** — PrivCo has no execution surface for AI-drafted outreach, prioritized accounts, or marketer audience building / play orchestration (source: https://www.privco.com/product).
  - **Chorus / conversation intelligence, ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM, intent), ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing)** — none of these capabilities appear on PrivCo's extracted pages (source: https://www.privco.com/product).
  - **Global / international company data** — PrivCo is U.S.-only ("PrivCo focuses exclusively on US-based private companies with $1M+ in annual revenue") (source: https://prospeo.io/s/privco-vs-zoominfo).
  - **Investment-banking pitchbook prep, M&A target valuation, PE / VC due diligence, academic research, executive search** — PrivCo sells deeply into financial-research workflows ZoomInfo does not target (source: https://www.privco.com/solutions).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No PrivCo-authored comparison page surfaced; the only vs-content extracted is a third-party prospeo.io comparison: "PrivCo is built for private-company financial intelligence (revenue estimates, valuations, ownership), while ZoomInfo is built for prospecting and enrichment (contacts, intent, technographics, CRM workflows). If you need both research and outbound execution, you'll typically pair a financial tool with a dedicated contact-data provider." (source: https://prospeo.io/s/privco-vs-zoominfo).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Proprietary Financial Intelligence on America's Private Companies" — surface the 80% of the U.S. private market with revenue, EBITDA, valuation estimates and verified executive contacts on bootstrapped and non-VC-backed firms (source: https://www.privco.com/, https://www.privco.com/product).
- **Recurring proof points:** 5M+ private U.S. companies, $2.5T+ combined revenue, 100K+ M&A deals tracked, 220M+ employees, 1.9M+ executive contacts (homepage); 146M+ data points across 1,000 verticals (API page); 16+ years of private-company research; client logos referenced on solutions page (Deloitte, Bloomberg, CNBC, Inc., Forbes); PE-analyst testimonial: "PrivCo's revenue and valuation estimates have been the most consistently accurate of any private company database we've used" (source: https://www.privco.com/use-cases).
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as "The leading private company database for contacts and financial intelligence" — a research and database tool, not a GTM / sales prospecting platform. Tone is research-led ("uncover," "go deeper," "see beyond the obvious"), not execution-led.
