## Summary

Dun & Bradstreet is the canonical commercial-data, master-data and credit/risk-analytics provider — the issuer of the D-U-N-S Number, with a Data Cloud spanning 600M+ organizations and 2B consumers across 250+ markets that supplies finance, procurement, MDM, and sales/marketing teams across SMB through enterprise. Direct overlap with ZoomInfo sits at the firmographic/identity layer (D&B Connect + Data Cloud vs. ZoomInfo's Data foundation and Operations) and at the marketing-execution layer (D&B Marketing Services overlapping ZoomInfo Marketing). The bulk of D&B's revenue lives in adjacent categories ZoomInfo does not address — commercial credit reporting, accounts-receivable automation, supplier and third-party risk, compliance and federal/SLED — and a third-party review frames D&B as "overbuilt and overpriced" for GTM teams that need real-time buying signals. Net positioning: D&B is a foundational data and risk-analytics utility with deep federal/finance roots, while ZoomInfo is a GTM-first platform with reasoning over CRM, intent and conversation context that D&B does not surface.

## Product Offerings

### D&B Connect (Master Data / Data Cloud platform)

> **Wiki:** [[dun-bradstreet--connect]]

- **What it does:** Self-service data management platform that matches, cleanses and enriches first-party company and contact data against the D&B Data Cloud to create a single source of truth.
- **Key features:**
  - Identity resolution: identify and merge like/similar records into a master/golden record
  - Match and enrich first-party data against D&B Data Cloud (600M+ companies, 550M+ contacts, 54M+ linkages, 2,500+ attributes)
  - Multiple delivery modes: API, batch, cloud, iPaaS, and Salesforce integration
  - D&B Connect Discover — unified view of company and contact data with match criteria and identity resolution
  - D&B Connect Manage — AI/ML-driven golden-record creation, accuracy and compliance controls
  - D&B Connect for Salesforce — enrich profiles, prioritize leads and personalize engagement inside the CRM
  - D&B Connect Essentials — simplified package targeted at small to midsize businesses
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-connect.html

### D&B Data Cloud / Master Data Solutions

> **Wiki:** [[dun-bradstreet--data-cloud]]

- **What it does:** Foundational commercial-data and analytics offering used to drive growth, mitigate risk, increase efficiency and reduce costs across the enterprise.
- **Key features:**
  - Data Cloud covering 580M+ entities (master-data page) / 600M organizations and 2B consumers across 250+ markets (homepage)
  - Built around the proprietary D-U-N-S Number as the universal business identifier
  - Topic-based data offerings: Company Entity Resolution, Company Information, Contact Data (email & phone), Derived Attributes, Email Verification, Family Tree linkage
  - Companion products: D&B Direct+ (streaming data API), D&B Analytics Studio (deep data analysis), D&B Connect (platform)
  - Advisory services from solutions architects, data advisors and data strategists
  - Forrester TEI: $11.8M NPV, 208% ROI, payback in just over a year (cited on the page)
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/solutions/master-data.html

### D&B Finance Analytics Credit Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[dun-bradstreet--finance-analytics]]

- **What it does:** Scalable credit-risk-management platform that delivers credit reports and end-to-end credit-to-cash automation backed by D&B's commercial credit data.
- **Key features:**
  - Credit reports and continuous credit monitoring at scale
  - End-to-end credit-to-cash automation
  - Trusted D&B scores (PAYDEX, Failure, Delinquency, Country Risk, Business Disruption Index)
  - Companion: D&B Enterprise Risk Assessment Manager for global finance/credit operations
  - Companion: D&B Receivables Intelligence powered by FIS GETPAID for A/R automation
  - Tiered packaging from $1,200 (Tier 1 Starter) up to $50,000 (Tier 1 Titanium) per the SMB pricing schedule
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-credit-intelligence.html

### D&B Business Information Report (BIR)

- **What it does:** Detailed business credit report on another company — financial scores, payment history and public filings — used to evaluate creditworthiness of customers, suppliers and partners.
- **Key features:**
  - PAYDEX Score plus five other D&B scores and ratings
  - Snapshot (point-in-time) and On Demand (continuous monitoring) versions
  - Up to 12 months of access with unlimited live updates per On Demand purchase
  - Operations, financial-performance and public-filing records
  - Single dashboard view across multiple reports
  - Bundle pricing: Snapshot $139.99–$2,799.99; On Demand $189.99–$3,799.99
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-business-information-report.html

### Supplier Risk / Third-Party Risk Solutions

> **Wiki:** [[dun-bradstreet--supplier-risk]]

- **What it does:** Supplier and supply-chain risk monitoring built on D&B commercial data plus AI agents that predict risk and automate supplier assessments.
- **Key features:**
  - Supplier identification, screening and onboarding
  - Continuous monitoring for sanctions, cyber risk, ESG, ownership and maritime vessel data
  - Diversity and ESG visibility across the supplier base
  - Real-time supplier alerts and automated assessments
  - Tariff/import exposure evaluation tied to current trade policy
  - AI tools/agents that predict risk and automate procurement workflows
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/solutions/risk/supplier-risk.html

### D&B Marketing Services (managed services)

> **Wiki:** [[dun-bradstreet--marketing-services]]

- **What it does:** Done-for-you marketing services where D&B experts plan and execute campaigns using D&B's first-party data paired with the customer's own data.
- **Key features:**
  - Email marketing
  - Social media and display ads
  - Direct mail
  - Omnichannel campaign execution
  - Website design and landing pages
  - Full creative and copywriting services
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-marketing-services.html

### DUNS / SMB credit-builder products

- **What it does:** DUNS Number issuance plus SMB-facing credit-monitoring and credit-building subscriptions that let small businesses see and improve their D&B credit profile.
- **Key features:**
  - D-U-N-S Number lookup and registration (free; required for federal contracting and many vendor-onboarding processes)
  - D-U-N-S Registered Seal — $299.99
  - D&B Credit Insights tiers — Basic ($49/mo or $499/yr), Plus ($149/mo or $1,499/yr)
  - CreditBuilder Plus ($149/mo or $1,499/yr) and CreditBuilder Premium ($199/mo or $1,999/yr)
  - Business Listing Basic ($99/mo or $999/yr) and Business Listing Plus ($119/mo or $1,199/yr)
  - Web Solutions: Email First Year $48 (then $119), Website Builder $399, Website Concierge $849
- **Source URL:** https://www.dnb.com/en-us/utilities/small-business-pricing.html

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1) ↔ D&B Data Cloud / D&B Connect** — D&B Connect advertises 600M+ companies, 550M+ contacts, 54M+ linkages and 2,500+ attributes delivered via API/batch/cloud/iPaaS/Salesforce — directly competing with ZoomInfo's data foundation (500M contacts, 100M companies). (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-connect.html)
  - **ZoomInfo Operations ↔ D&B Connect Discover/Manage/for-Salesforce** — D&B Connect is positioned as a "self-service data management platform" that matches, cleanses, enriches and creates "golden records" inside Salesforce and other systems — the same job as ZoomInfo Operations. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-connect.html)
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing ↔ D&B Marketing Services + Rev.Up ABX** — D&B Marketing Services positions as a marketing-team replacement running email, social, display, direct mail, omnichannel and creative; the syncgtm review references D&B Rev.Up ABX as the ABM platform peer. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-marketing-services.html)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace ↔ D&B Sales & Marketing Solutions** — D&B routes buyers from the homepage to /solutions/grow/sales.html and /solutions/grow/marketing.html for sales intel and marketing optimization, but the dedicated seller front-end (D&B Hoovers) is scoped to a separate dossier. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/)
  - **GTM Context Graph ↔ D&B Data Cloud + Analytics Studio + Scores** — D&B aggregates third-party data and scores into a unified Data Cloud, but reviewed pages do not describe a comparable conversation-intelligence or first-party CRM-fusion reasoning layer; coverage is firmographic + risk-signal weighted rather than context-graph reasoning over CRM/intent/conversation. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/solutions/master-data.html)
- **No overlap:**
  - Commercial credit reporting, PAYDEX, credit-to-cash automation and A/R/collections — D&B Finance Analytics Credit Intelligence, D&B Receivables Intelligence powered by FIS GETPAID, Payment Direct, BIRs and the Tier-1 priced finance suite are not in ZoomInfo's product line. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/solutions/risk/financial-risk.html)
  - Supplier / third-party / supply-chain risk and procurement intelligence — sanctions, ESG, cyber, ownership, maritime vessel data and tariff exposure live entirely outside ZoomInfo's GTM scope. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/solutions/risk/supplier-risk.html)
  - DUNS Number issuance, the Registered Seal and SMB credit-builder products — proprietary D&B identity assets used by federal contractors and vendor onboarding plus SMB credit-monitoring subscriptions; ZoomInfo has no equivalent. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/utilities/small-business-pricing.html)
  - Compliance, ESG, fraud and federal/SLED public-sector solutions — homepage routes buyers to compliance-risk, fraud-risk, sustainability-risk and federal/SLED solution pages, none of which are ZoomInfo categories. (source: https://www.dnb.com/en-us/)
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No dedicated dnb.com vs ZoomInfo page surfaced; closest proxy is the third-party syncgtm review, which positions D&B against modern GTM platforms with: "D&B is the gold standard for enterprise business data. But for GTM teams that need real-time signals and fast time-to-value, it is overbuilt and overpriced. The data tells you what a company is — not when it is ready to buy." (source: https://syncgtm.com/blog/dun-bradstreet-review). See flag `vs_brand_missing`.
