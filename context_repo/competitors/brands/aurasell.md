# Aurasell

## Sub-products

- [[aurasell--ai-crm|Aurasell AI-Native GTM Platform (full CRM)]] — competes with GTM Workspace
- [[aurasell--gtm-os|Aurasell GTM OS (overlay on existing CRM)]] — competes with CRM Enrichment

## Summary

Aurasell is an AI-native CRM + "GTM OS" startup (founded 2024, $30M seed Aug 2025) selling to CROs, sales leaders, and RevOps at startups, SMB, mid-market, and enterprise B2B teams. Its pitch is full-stack tool consolidation — replacing 12–15 separate GTM tools (CRM, sales engagement, conversation intelligence, forecasting, CPQ, data enrichment) with one AI-native platform built on a unified data graph plus a built-in 84M+ account / 950M+ contact database. Direct overlap with ZoomInfo is meaningful: Aurasell's Prospect + Sell modules compete with the GTM Workspace + Sales/data layer, Whisper competes with Chorus, and Manage/Operate competes with ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio. No first-party Aurasell vs ZoomInfo comparison page exists; the competitive framing comes only from third-party research.

## Product Offerings

### AI-Native GTM Platform (full CRM + GTM stack)

- **What it does:** Aurasell's full-stack offering for startups and SMB — an AI-native CRM that replaces a stack of 12–15+ GTM tools with one platform built on a unified, AI-ready data model.
- **Key features:**
  - AI-native CRM as system of record with a unified data graph fed by sales activities (emails, calls, meetings, pipeline updates, quotes)
  - Built-in 84M+ accounts and 950M+ contacts database (Sacra cites 85M companies / 800M contacts)
  - Agentic Workbench surfacing ICP-fit accounts, prioritized contacts, deal risks, and AI-suggested next actions
  - Whisper conversational intelligence agent that captures calls in real time and auto-creates deal data and tasks
  - Built-in CPQ with catalog ingestion, pricing rules, PDF/quote generation, e-signature
  - Forecasting using Monte Carlo simulations that factor in macroeconomic signals + live pipeline
  - Custom AI workflow builder (no-code) and end-to-end agent configuration
  - 220 advertised integrations across CRM, ticketing, file storage, accounting, productivity, telemetry
- **Source URL:** https://www.aurasell.ai/ai-crm-and-gtm

### AI-Native GTM OS (overlay on existing CRM)

- **What it does:** Aurasell's mid-market and enterprise offering — augments the customer's existing Salesforce or HubSpot CRM with an AI-powered GTM operating system instead of replacing it.
- **Key features:**
  - Augments Salesforce and HubSpot rather than replacing them
  - Same Aurasell agentic intelligence + unified data layer applied on top of the existing CRM
  - Targeted at enterprise customers who can't migrate off their system of record
  - Marketed as "World's First AI-Native OS to Run Intelligent GTM Workflows on Any CRM"
- **Source URL:** https://www.aurasell.ai/

### Prospect (use case)

- **What it does:** Pipeline-generation workflow — agents prospect 24/7 across 84M+ accounts and 950M+ contacts, enriching and sequencing automatically.
- **Key features:**
  - ICP / buyer persona builder with deep filters
  - Pipeline agent that finds best prospects, predicts engagement likelihood, schedules conversations
  - AI Smart Columns for instant data enrichment (e.g., security tools used, open AI roles, contact tenure)
  - Automated account research agent that maps account drivers and delivers a tailored value hypothesis
  - AI sequencer that writes, adapts, and personalizes multi-step outreach across email, voice, text
  - Integrated voice dialer with click-to-call, auto call logging, transcripts, AI summaries, follow-up tasks
- **Source URL:** https://www.aurasell.ai/use-case/prospect

### Sell (use case)

- **What it does:** Sales execution workspace — opportunity management, conversational intelligence, AI coaching, and CPQ in one surface.
- **Key features:**
  - Pulse — single command center for pipeline, meetings, tasks, opportunities prioritized by buying signals
  - Automated account research agent for prep and call planning
  - AI-driven outreach across email, LinkedIn, voice
  - Conversational intelligence that captures every call and auto-generates deal signals, actions, tasks
  - Auto-fill of opportunity fields against MEDDPICC, BANT, 3 Whys frameworks
  - CRO-level AI coaching that sharpens execution and lifts win rates
  - AI-powered CPQ that generates quotes inside the deal workflow
- **Source URL:** https://www.aurasell.ai/use-case/sell

### Manage (use case)

- **What it does:** Sales-management workflow — forecasting, scenario planning, deal coaching, sales-process governance, and ready-made analytics for managers and RevOps.
- **Key features:**
  - Forecasting agent that flags risks, spots growth trends, delivers live forecasting intelligence
  - Scenario planning agent factoring in macro/micro business trends
  - Customizable sales process / stage builder per business unit (Commercial, Mid-market, Enterprise)
  - Framework automation for BANT, 3 WHYS, MEDDPICC with field tracking and AI coaching triggers
  - Self-updating opportunities driven by conversations, emails, meetings, and signals
  - Out-of-the-box analytics — pipeline health, stage velocity, win rates, risks, trends — no BI required
- **Source URL:** https://www.aurasell.ai/use-case/manage

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- AI-native architecture — data model designed from scratch to operate with LLMs and agents, rather than AI features bolted onto a 1999-era schema (source: https://sacra.com/research/aurasell/)
- Tool-consolidation pitch — claims to replace 12–15 separate GTM tools with one platform and cut total GTM tech-stack cost by ~50% (source: https://www.saastr.com/ai-app-of-the-week-aurasell-the-ai-native-crm-thats-actually-replacing-15-tools/)
- Two go-to-market modes — "Aurasell becomes your GTM Platform" for startups/SMB OR "Aurasell's GTM OS on Your Existing CRM" for enterprises that keep Salesforce / HubSpot (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/)
- Outcome claims published on homepage — 80% reduction in manual tasks, 35% increase in sales velocity, 50% reduction in ramp time, 2x selling capacity (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/)
- Built-in 84M+ account / 950M+ contact database used directly inside the CRM (eliminates the need for a separate data-vendor seat) (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/use-case/prospect)
- Monte Carlo forecasting that factors in macroeconomic signals plus live pipeline data (source: https://sacra.com/research/aurasell/)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace ↔ Aurasell Prospect + Sell:** Aurasell sells prospecting on top of an in-product B2B database (84M+ accounts / 950M+ contacts) with an AI sequencer that writes/adapts personalized outreach — direct functional overlap with ZoomInfo's data + Workspace seller surface (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/use-case/prospect).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio ↔ Aurasell Manage + Operate:** Aurasell pitches no-code AI workflows, framework governance (MEDDPICC / BANT / 3 Whys), and ready-made analytics for RevOps — overlapping with ZoomInfo Operations + GTM Studio's orchestration role (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/use-case/manage).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) ↔ Aurasell Whisper:** Aurasell's Whisper agent captures every call in real time, transcribes, generates deal signals/tasks, and stores transcripts in opportunity records — same primary job as Chorus (source: https://sacra.com/research/aurasell/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (data + intent + targeting) ↔ Aurasell intent / signal capture:** Aurasell advertises "AI intent & signal capture" and "context-driven value pyramids & hypotheses" built into prospecting flows (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/).
- **Adjacent overlap:** **APIs & MCP / GTM Context Graph ↔ Aurasell unified data graph + custom AI workflow builder.** Aurasell describes a "unified data graph" fed by sales activity that serves as a "single source of truth for AI agents," echoing ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph framing — but Aurasell's graph is internal to its CRM and aimed at operating that CRM's agents, not at exposing reasoning to any tool/agent via APIs/MCP (source: https://sacra.com/research/aurasell/).
- **No overlap:**
  - Aurasell as system-of-record CRM + built-in CPQ + e-signature — ZoomInfo does not sell the CRM record system or CPQ; it overlays existing CRMs (source: https://sacra.com/research/aurasell/).
  - Aurasell built-in voice dialer with auto call logging, transcripts, summaries, follow-up tasks — outside ZoomInfo's scope (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/use-case/prospect).
  - APIs & MCP (universal access lane) — no public Aurasell API or MCP server was found on any reviewed page; ZoomInfo's third pillar (universal access via APIs & MCP) is not addressed by Aurasell's current product surface.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public Aurasell vs ZoomInfo comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Aurasell's only first-party comparison page is vs Salesforce. The closest framing is third-party (Sacra): "Apollo and ZoomInfo offer go-to-market platforms that combine data, sequencing, and light CRM functionality. These platforms compete by providing single-login access and seat-based pricing but restrict users to proprietary data sources, unlike Aurasell's vendor-agnostic approach." (source: https://sacra.com/research/aurasell/).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Make Everyone An Elite Operator" — turn your team into superhuman operators with an AI-native GTM Platform that lets you "Prospect, sell, manage, and operate using one unified AI-native GTM platform" (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/).
- **Recurring proof points:** Customer logos including Xerox, CAST AI, Avo Automation, Mobiclocks, Diligent, CrewAI, Cymulate, Fetch.ai, Outpave, Trust Cloud, TCI Transportation, Revlogic, Smallest AI, Ohio University; named testimonial customers include Ross Calame (EVP Sales, TCI Transportation), Ferréol Hoppenot (EVP Worldwide Sales, CAST AI), Rick Kickert (CEO, Revlogic), George Gilbert (CEO, Mobiclocks), Ashutosh Pani (Marketing Ops, Avo Automation), Dali Rajic (President/COO); outcome claims of 80% reduction in manual tasks, 35% increase in sales velocity, 50% reduction in ramp time, 2x selling capacity, 99.9% uptime; $30M seed Aug 2025 (Next47-led) closed in 28 hours; "15+ Tools in One Platform" feature inventory (source: https://www.aurasell.ai/, https://sacra.com/research/aurasell/).
- **Tone / category framing:** "Go Beyond CRM: Enter the Era of Intelligence." Aurasell frames its category as the "AI-Native CRM + GTM OS" — built post-2022 with AI in the data model rather than bolted on, and pitched as both a full-stack CRM (for startups/SMB) and an OS that sits on top of an existing CRM (for mid-market / enterprise). Their narrative target is tool sprawl: replacing 12–15 fragmented GTM tools with one AI-native system.
