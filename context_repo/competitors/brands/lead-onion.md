## Summary

Lead Onion is a UK-based AI-powered buyer-intent and B2B sales-intelligence platform aimed at SMB-to-mid-market sales, marketing, and RevOps teams that unifies 24 third-party intent sources (Bombora, Foundry, Contanuity, Delivr.ai, PharosIQ, IP Flow, Cognism, G2) and packages them with verified contact data, native cadences, and a Salesforce/HubSpot/Dynamics sync layer. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Pillar 1 data foundation, the GTM Workspace seller front-end, and ZoomInfo's intent layer — but at a much lower entry price ($500/mo Starter) and with a notably smaller data footprint (209M contacts vs ZoomInfo's 500M; 100M emails vs 200M; 110M dials vs 135M). It does not compete on ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, conversation intelligence (Chorus), or developer-facing API/MCP surfaces, and it carries no analyst-validation badges (Wave/MQ/Customers' Choice) on reviewed pages.

## Product Offerings

### Account Intelligence

> **Wiki:** [[lead-onion--account-intelligence]]

- **What it does:** Predict, prioritise, and engage the ~10% of accounts that are actively in-market right now using multi-source intent and 'research surge' analysis (positioned 'for larger GTM teams').
- **Key features:**
  - Research Quadrant — maps each account to one of four buying-journey phases: Initial, Interested, Active, In-Depth
  - Watchlist for tracking key prospect or customer accounts
  - Account-level intent across topics (Bombora / Foundry / Contanuity), competitors, events, technologies, hiring signals, funding, leadership changes, awards, partnerships, new offerings
  - Company enrichment with 30+ firmographic attributes
  - Powered by 24 intent data sources unified into one platform
- **Source URL:** https://leadonion.ai/, https://leadonion.ai/our-data/, https://leadonion.ai/pricing/

### Intent Qualified Leads (IQLs)

> **Wiki:** [[lead-onion--intent-qualified-leads]]

- **What it does:** Convert intent signals into verified, deliverable leads matched to the customer's ICP, with verified email and phone per contact ('for startups & SMEs').
- **Key features:**
  - ICP matching with verified business email + direct dial + LinkedIn profile per contact
  - 99% deliverability claim on contact records
  - Surge-strength + buyer-phase ranking on every IQL
  - Real-time email verification at send-time
  - Auto-routing of IQLs to CRM, cadence, or Slack via Leadflows
  - Contact-level intent powered by Delivr.ai (US contacts) and PharosIQ (download intent)
- **Source URL:** https://leadonion.ai/intent-qualified-leads/, https://leadonion.ai/our-data/

### Website Identification

> **Wiki:** [[lead-onion--website-identification]]

- **What it does:** Turn anonymous website visitors into named companies and verified contacts in real time ('for every business').
- **Key features:**
  - Company-level visitor ID using IP Flow's 1.4B+ B2B IPs
  - Person-level web-visit identification (US contacts) powered by Delivr.ai
  - 20k website lookups included as part of pricing baseline
  - Real-time routing of revealed visitors into CRM or cadence
  - Visitor topic / page-interest signals for prioritization
- **Source URL:** https://leadonion.ai/, https://leadonion.ai/our-data/, https://leadonion.ai/pricing/

### Sales Automation (Cadences + Pipeline)

> **Wiki:** [[lead-onion--sales-automation]]

- **What it does:** Native multi-touch sales engagement and pipeline tracking that auto-enrols revealed in-market leads into outreach.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-channel cadences (email, call, LinkedIn) with A/B testing and dynamic fields
  - Auto-enrolment when account surge thresholds are hit
  - Built-in pipeline view from 'first signal to closed-won'
  - Real-time alerts on buyer-phase change (task / sequence / Slack)
  - Cadences are gated to Growth tier and above (not in Starter)
- **Source URL:** https://leadonion.ai/roles-sales/, https://leadonion.ai/pricing/

### Aimee — AI Buyer Intent Agent

> **Wiki:** [[lead-onion--aimee]]

- **What it does:** Free domain-input intent-discovery agent that scans signals and identifies in-market accounts in under 3 minutes; serves as the entry path to a 7-day trial with 100 free leads.
- **Key features:**
  - Domain-input setup with no credit card required
  - Generates a buyer-intent campaign + targeted contacts + sales cadence in <3 minutes
  - 100 free leads bundled with the 7-day trial
- **Source URL:** https://leadonion.ai/sales-intelligence/, https://leadonion.ai/

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B SaaS / technology services (named customers SecureAuth, Access Intelligence, IT Authorities), Information & Technology Services, Business Services
- **Company size called out:** Startups & SMEs (explicit IQL positioning), SMB to mid-market (Starter $500/mo), larger GTM teams (Account Intelligence positioning, Scale $3,000/mo), Enterprise (POA tier)
- **Persona / role focus:** Sales leaders (Sales Directors, Heads of Sales, AEs/SDRs), Marketing leaders (Heads of Marketing, demand-gen marketers, ABM marketers), RevOps teams
- **Use cases promoted:** Sales Engagement, Sales Intelligence, Account Expansion, Demand Generation, Campaign Optimization, Churn Reduction (using Watchlists to detect competitor-research signals 60 days before cancellation)

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** HubSpot, Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics 365
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Native Lead Onion cadences (multi-channel email / call / LinkedIn); Outreach (referenced as a sync target on the homepage); LinkedIn (push contacts / engagement)
- **Data / enrichment:** Bombora (topic intent), Foundry (topic intent), Contanuity (topic intent across 5,000+ B2B sites), Delivr.ai (person-based intent — US contacts), PharosIQ (download-level contact intent), IP Flow (1.4B+ B2B IPs for visitor ID), Cognism (contact data — direct dials and verified emails), G2 (intent — G2 subscription required)
- **Other notable integrations:** Slack (alerts), Zapier (10,000+ downstream connectors)

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- 24 intent data sources unified into a single platform — sources include Bombora, Foundry, Contanuity, Delivr.ai, PharosIQ, IP Flow, Cognism and G2 — pitched as 'the largest buyer intent data footprint' (source: https://leadonion.ai/our-data/).
- Person-level intent (Delivr.ai-powered) on top of account-level intent — identifies the actual individual researching, not just the company (US contacts only) (source: https://leadonion.ai/our-data/).
- Public, transparent self-serve pricing starting at $500/mo and an Aimee free trial (100 leads, no credit card, <3-min setup) — unusually low entry point for a multi-source intent platform (source: https://leadonion.ai/pricing/, https://leadonion.ai/sales-intelligence/).
- Native sales cadences + pipeline built into the same platform that delivers the intent signal (no separate sales-engagement tool needed at Growth tier and above) (source: https://leadonion.ai/roles-sales/, https://leadonion.ai/pricing/).
- Research Quadrant — proprietary 4-stage account-journey classifier (Initial / Interested / Active / In-Depth) used to route leads into cadences automatically (source: https://leadonion.ai/pricing/).
- Watchlist-based churn-prevention use case — track existing customers for competitor-research signals to intercept churn 60 days before cancellation (source: https://leadonion.ai/roles-revops/, https://leadonion.ai/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1).** Lead Onion's contact-data layer (Cognism-powered) markets the same primitives — 209M contact records, 100M verified emails, 110M mobile numbers, 20M business profiles, 706M tech detections — at materially smaller scale than ZoomInfo's 500M contacts / 200M emails / 135M dials / 30K technographics (source: https://leadonion.ai/our-data/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace.** Lead Onion's 'For Sales' role bundles Account Intelligence + IQLs + native cadences into one seller surface — the same prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted-outreach + execution flow ZoomInfo positions for GTM Workspace (source: https://leadonion.ai/roles-sales/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio.** Lead Onion's 'For Marketers' and Solutions footer (Sales Engagement / Sales Intelligence / Account Expansion / Demand Generation / Campaign Optimization / Churn Reduction) puts it on the same ABM / marketing-orchestration field as GTM Studio (source: https://leadonion.ai/solutions-marketing/, https://leadonion.ai/sales-intelligence/).
  - **Intent Data layer (where ZoomInfo holds Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader status).** Lead Onion's headline category is intent — 'The Premium Intent Platform' — making this the most direct head-to-head positioning conflict (source: https://leadonion.ai/, https://leadonion.ai/our-data/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing).** Lead Onion's RevOps role enriches and syncs accounts/contacts/activity to Salesforce, HubSpot and Slack, but does not surface a routing engine, dedup rules, or workflow-rules builder at Operations' depth (source: https://leadonion.ai/roles-revops/).
  - **ZoomInfo Chat (data-powered website chat).** Lead Onion has Website Identification (visitor reveal at company + person level) but no chat product surfaced in reviewed pages (source: https://leadonion.ai/).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph.** Lead Onion unifies 24 third-party intent sources but does not market a 'context graph' that fuses customer first-party CRM + conversation + behavioral data to explain why deals move; Lead Onion's data narrative is breadth of intent sources, not first-party fusion (source: https://leadonion.ai/our-data/).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence + context capture).** No conversation-intelligence product surfaced — no call recording, transcription, or conversation-analysis offering on reviewed pages.
  - **APIs & MCP server.** No public API, /developers, or MCP surface in nav — only an in-product AI agent ('Aimee') and Zapier as the dev-stack hook (source: https://leadonion.ai/).
  - **Analyst validation.** No Forrester Wave, Gartner Magic Quadrant, Gartner Voice of the Customer, or G2 #1-rankings claims surfaced; proof points are customer testimonials only (SecureAuth, Access Intelligence, IT Authorities).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ A G2 comparison page (https://www.g2.com/compare/lead-onion-vs-zoominfo-sales) exists but was blocked at extraction (403); the SERP snippet paraphrases reviewers as saying 'Lead Onion has a strong focus on real-time intent signals, ZoomInfo Sales outshines it in overall data quality and support' — see flags.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** 'Convert more deals with better data. Access 209M verified leads and companies — powered by real-time account intelligence.' Positioned as an AI-Powered GTM Intelligence Platform unifying 24 intent sources to identify the ~10% of accounts in-market and route verified contacts to outreach.
- **Recurring proof points:** 209M+ verified B2B contacts; 24 intent data sources unified; '40% engagement uplift' (Access Intelligence); '£250k new revenue in 90 days' (SecureAuth); '1,100 new leads in a month' (IT Authorities); 'another million dollars of new pipeline' — Karl Barton, CMO, SecureAuth.
- **Tone / category framing:** Self-described as 'The Premium Intent Platform' and 'AI-Powered GTM Intelligence Platform'. Frames the category around intent breadth ('largest buyer intent data footprint', 'one intent stream' versus 'noise of single-source feeds') rather than data depth or platform breadth. SMB-friendly cost-disruptor framing recurs ('like Apollo on steroids, but without the price tag').
