## Summary

TrustRadius is a B2B buyer intelligence platform serving mid-market and enterprise SaaS vendor marketing, demand gen, and customer marketing teams; its core business is a long-form B2B review marketplace, while `solutions.trustradius.com` monetizes the resulting on-platform buyer research as second-party "downstream" intent data, ICP-qualified intent-driven leads, and embeddable review content. The most material overlap with ZoomInfo is at the intent-data layer (Pillar 1) — TrustRadius explicitly pitches its second-party signals as more accurate than ZoomInfo's third-party intent — plus vendor-side ABM/lead workflows that overlap with ZoomInfo Marketing and Intent-Driven Leads. TrustRadius does NOT provide contact-level data, conversation intelligence, a context graph, or seller execution surfaces, and its own ZoomInfo integration page positions the two as complementary at the account-intent + contact-data layers. TrustRadius was acquired by HG Insights in June 2025 and is now part of HG's Revenue Growth Intelligence suite (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/).

## Product Offerings

### Buyer Intelligence Platform

> **Wiki:** [[trustradius--buyer-intelligence-platform]]

- **What it does:** Vendor-facing platform that elicits long-form, vetted customer reviews on TrustRadius and gives vendors tools to influence the 1M+ monthly self-serve B2B buyers researching their category.
- **Key features:**
  - Custom questionnaires to direct reviewers toward specific use cases and features
  - Average review length over 400 words; reviews vetted by a research team
  - 30% of submitted reviews rejected for fraud or suspicious behavior; 10% rejected for quality reasons
  - TrustRadius Awards driven by customer sentiment and ratings
  - Audience claim: 54% mid-market and enterprise buyers; <20% audience overlap with G2
- **Source URL:** https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/buyer-intelligence-platform

### Downstream Intent Data

> **Wiki:** [[trustradius--downstream-intent-data]]

- **What it does:** Second-party intent signals from in-market buyers researching the vendor, competitors, and category on TrustRadius — surfaced into vendor CRM, ABM, and paid media stacks.
- **Key features:**
  - Second-party (not third-party) intent — signals come directly from TrustRadius on-platform buyer research, framed as more accurate than third-party intent
  - Granular signals: pricing-page views, comparison views, demo views, feature evaluations
  - Salesforce Connector for CRM activation and churn alerts when existing customers research alternatives
  - Integrates into LinkedIn, Demandbase, and 6sense for ABM audience activation
  - Use cases: net-new pipeline, competitive takeout, churn prevention, upsell
- **Source URL:** https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/downstream-intent-data/

### Intent-Driven Leads

> **Wiki:** [[trustradius--intent-driven-leads]]

- **What it does:** Managed lead-generation program that converts in-market accounts on TrustRadius into ICP-qualified, opted-in leads delivered to the vendor's stack.
- **Key features:**
  - ICP filtering against the vendor's target account list
  - Conversion via market reports, customer stories, and product highlights derived from review content
  - Audience claim: 56% plan to make a purchase within 3 months; 75% are director-level and above
  - Cisco case study: 4,000 ICP-matched leads delivered in less than two weeks
  - No bidding/bias model; vendor provides ICP criteria, TrustRadius delivers leads
- **Source URL:** https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/intent-driven-leads

### Content Licensing (TrustQuotes)

> **Wiki:** [[trustradius--content-licensing]]

- **What it does:** Library and embeddable widgets that let vendors syndicate keyword-rich customer review quotes onto their own website, landing pages, and ad creative for SEO and conversion lift.
- **Key features:**
  - TrustQuotes Library to tag and store the strongest customer quotes
  - TrustQuotes for Web widget for embedding on vendor pages
  - QuoteFinder Chrome extension for sales/marketing access to quotes
  - Vendor case study: 33.5% increase in organic traffic; ranked for 52 new keywords (RStudio)
  - Average TrustQuotes-for-Web vendor saves $6,000/month on Google Search CPC
- **Source URL:** https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/content-licensing

### Premium Content & Event Support (add-ons)

> **Wiki:** [[trustradius--premium-content]]

- **What it does:** Add-on packages that turn review content into customer stories, market reports, and ready-to-share social/email assets, plus on-site review-generation support at vendor events.
- **Key features:**
  - Customer story and market report assets derived from review content
  - Competitive intelligence reports
  - On-site review generation at events (written and video reviews; hybrid/virtual/in-person)
  - Ready-to-share customer quote assets for social, web, email
- **Source URL:** https://solutions.trustradius.com/pricing/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data foundation — Intent (Pillar 1) ↔ TrustRadius Downstream Intent Data:** TrustRadius sells second-party downstream intent signals (in-market buyers researching you, competitors, and category) that activate in CRM/ABM/paid media — directly overlapping with ZoomInfo's Intent Data layer (Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader, Intent Data Providers). TrustRadius explicitly frames itself as superior to the "third-party intent data" ZoomInfo Intent Data is built on (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/downstream-intent-data/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (legacy ABM) / GTM Studio audience building ↔ TrustRadius ABM & Lead Generation + Demand Gen Marketers:** TrustRadius positions as a demand-gen and ABM platform that delivers ICP-qualified leads and audiences activated in LinkedIn, Demandbase, 6sense, and Salesforce — overlapping with ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM workflows (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/demand-gen-marketers/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (intent-driven prospecting) ↔ TrustRadius Intent-Driven Leads:** TrustRadius's managed Intent-Driven Leads program delivers opted-in, ICP-qualified leads from accounts researching the vendor or competitors on TrustRadius — competing with the prospecting workflows ZoomInfo Sales sellers run on intent + contact data (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/intent-driven-leads).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + routing) ↔ TrustRadius Salesforce Connector + HubSpot routing:** TrustRadius pushes intent and lead data into CRMs but does not provide CRM data hygiene, deduplication, or routing as a primary function — adjacent connector territory, not a substitute (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/downstream-intent-data/).
  - **Data (Pillar 1) (contact + company) ↔ TrustRadius account-level intent only:** TrustRadius surfaces account-level intent signals but does not provide contact-level decision-maker data — its own ZoomInfo integration page says: "Match the accounts that are researching your product(s) on TrustRadius with decision maker contact information in the ZoomInfo Sales platform" (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/integrations/zoominfo-2/). Complementary at the contact layer; adjacent (not overlapping) at the account-intent layer.
- **No overlap:** Customer review aggregation, custom-questionnaire review generation, TrustQuotes content licensing, on-site review-gen event support, TrustRadius Awards, and Trusted Seller program have no analog in ZoomInfo's GTM platform (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/content-licensing). Conversely, TrustRadius does not provide a context graph reasoning across CRM + conversations + behavioral signals, has no conversation intelligence (Chorus equivalent), no seller-front-end execution surface, no marketer/RevOps orchestration platform, and no public MCP server or AI-agent ecosystem play (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No dedicated TrustRadius-vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced — TrustRadius runs a co-marketed integration partner page instead. The closest direct contrast is in their downstream intent positioning: "Marketers make the mistake of interpreting third-party intent data as indicating that a buyer is in-market. However, by activating second-party, downstream intent data you will reach new, in-market accounts researching your solution, your competitors' solutions, and your technology category on TrustRadius. Our downstream intent signals aren't muddied with other site and blog traffic or third-party sources, making our data more accurate and more useful." (source: https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/downstream-intent-data/)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Win over today's self-serve B2B tech buyer with the most detailed, vetted reviews and second-party downstream intent data — turning on-platform buyer research into pipeline, leads, and content that drives SEO and conversion.
- **Recurring proof points:** 1M+ mid-level/enterprise buyers/month and 12M annual tech buyers; 54% mid-market/enterprise audience with <20% G2 overlap; 400+ word avg review and 11+ min comparison time; Cisco 4,000 leads in <2 weeks; RStudio +33.5% organic traffic and 52 new keywords; avg $6k/mo Google Search CPC savings via TrustQuotes.
- **Tone / category framing:** "Buyer intelligence platform" / "second-party downstream intent data" — explicitly defined in opposition to "third-party intent" (ZoomInfo's framing).
