## Summary

Lead411 is a Boulder, CO B2B sales intelligence and contact data platform aimed at SMB / mid-market sales, marketing, and recruiting teams who want verified emails, direct dials, and growth-intent signals without an enterprise contract. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Data foundation, Operations (CRM enrichment), and the intent layer — selling 450M+ contacts, ~20M company profiles, triple-verified emails, Bombora content intent, growth-event triggers (hiring/funding/exec hires), a 25+ CRM connector library, and an enrichment API. Lead411's vs-ZoomInfo page pitches "Quality over Quantity" — claiming a starting price of $5,000/year (vs. a stated $15,000/year for ZoomInfo), 3-month re-verification cycles vs. 6-month-to-3-year, and unlimited search/export plans. It has no analog to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, Chorus, or MCP server.

## Product Offerings

### Lead Prospecting / B2B Data Provider

- **What it does:** Core verified contact and company database with advanced search, an A.I. dashboard, and growth-intent signals for prospecting.
- **Key features:**
  - Triple-verified email addresses (SMTP + human verification + ESP open validation)
  - Double-verified direct dial and mobile phone numbers with locality match
  - Advanced search filters by industry, company size, revenue, location, title, and technology stack
  - A.I. Dashboard that follows targeted companies/contacts and surfaces engagement signals
  - Growth Intent data from hiring, funding, executive hires, IPO, and new locations
  - International contact and company data
  - 30,000+ tracked technologies for technographic search
- **Source URL:** https://www.lead411.com/data-solutions/lead-prospecting/

### Bombora Buyer Intent Data (within Lead411)

- **What it does:** Adds Bombora-powered topic/content intent on top of Lead411's growth intent so users can target accounts actively researching specific topics.
- **Key features:**
  - Bombora intent topic selection (5 minimum, up to 25 topics)
  - Topics include industry, competitor names, and specific technologies
  - Sort intent results by Lead Score to surface fastest-growing accounts
  - Bundled into Lead411 plans, positioned as cheaper than going direct to Bombora
  - Buyer Intent Data is available on annual subscriptions (per pricing page)
- **Source URL:** https://www.lead411.com/data-solutions/bombora-intent-data/

### Lead Scoring (Growth Intent Algorithm)

- **What it does:** Scores companies based on combined growth indicators (hiring, funding, executive hires) so reps can sort prospects by buying readiness.
- **Key features:**
  - Growth Intent Algorithm aggregates multiple indicators per company
  - Scores surfaced inline in search results (color-coded green)
  - Combines with Advanced Search to filter ICP first, then sort by score
  - Pulls signals such as recent funding, hiring trends, and senior-executive hires
- **Source URL:** https://www.lead411.com/data-solutions/lead-scoring/

### Contact Enrichment / Lead Enrichment API

- **What it does:** CRM data quality product that enriches and re-verifies existing CRM records via prebuilt CRM connectors or a developer API.
- **Key features:**
  - Salesforce, HubSpot, and 25+ CRM integrations available
  - Lead Enrichment API for in-app or in-CRM enrichment
  - Re-verifies emails every 3–6 months on a continuous schedule
  - Live A.I. Research to find an email when one is not in the database
  - Dataset licensing / reseller program offered (contact sales)
- **Source URL:** https://www.lead411.com/data-solutions/contact-enrichment/

### Chrome Extension

- **What it does:** Browser extension that surfaces verified emails, direct dials, and titles inside LinkedIn and company websites and pushes records into the user's CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Reveals work emails, mobile numbers, and titles inline on LinkedIn
  - One-click push of contact/company records to 25+ CRMs
  - Acts as a phone number extractor while browsing company sites
  - Tied into Contact Enrichment API for in-tool exports
- **Source URL:** https://www.lead411.com/chrome-extension/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Triple-verified emails — SMTP + human verification + ESP open-validation, with re-verification every 3–6 months and a claimed <5% bounce rate on verified emails (source: https://www.lead411.com/competitor/zoominfo-competitor-lead411-vs-zoominfo/).
- Unlimited search and export plans on select annual tiers, with no daily/monthly caps (source: https://www.lead411.com/pricing/).
- Bundled Bombora buyer intent data on Pro/annual plans, sold as "much cheaper" than going direct to Bombora (source: https://www.lead411.com/data-solutions/bombora-intent-data/).
- Lower public price point than enterprise-only competitors — vs-ZoomInfo page advertises a $5,000/year starting enterprise seat versus a claimed $15,000/year ZoomInfo entry point (source: https://www.lead411.com/competitor/zoominfo-competitor-lead411-vs-zoominfo/).
- Flexible contracts — monthly billing on some plans and a "no long-term lock-in" claim versus enterprise data providers' multi-year commitments (source: https://www.lead411.com/competitor/zoominfo-competitor-lead411-vs-zoominfo/).
- Growth Intent / Lead Scoring framing — company-event signals (hiring, funding, exec hires, IPO) treated as the primary intent layer, with Bombora content intent layered on top (source: https://www.lead411.com/data-solutions/lead-scoring/).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** A B2B sales intelligence platform that helps teams reach decision-makers faster with real-time verified contact data, growth intent signals, and CRM integrations — positioned as the accurate, affordable, unlimited-export alternative to ZoomInfo.
- **Recurring proof points:** 450M+ contacts and ~20M company profiles; 96% data accuracy / email deliverability claim; triple-verified emails with re-verification every 3–6 months; G2 #1 in ZoomInfo competitors and Apollo competitors lists; customer base spans "fast-growing startups to Fortune 500 teams"; in the contact data space since 2011.
- **Tone / category framing:** Self-described category is "B2B sales intelligence / B2B data provider"; the brand repeatedly self-frames as a "Zoominfo Alternative" rather than defining a new category.
