## Summary

GetProspect is a B2B email finder and LinkedIn-led prospecting tool that bundles a 200M-contact database, bulk enrichment, a free built-in CRM, and a cold email sequencer — sold to SDRs, solopreneurs, and lead-gen agencies on a freemium model that starts at $0 and scales to $399/mo. The narrowest direct overlap with ZoomInfo is on Pillar 1 data (verified business emails, mobile phone numbers, technographics) and on the classic ZoomInfo Sales prospecting workflow, which GetProspect attacks head-on through a dedicated "ZoomInfo alternative" page claiming 84 vs 60 valid emails out of 100 in their own internal test (source: https://getprospect.com/zoominfo-alternative). They have no equivalent of GTM Context Graph (intent, behavioral signal, conversation intelligence) or GTM Studio (ABM, audience orchestration), so the competitive surface is "cheap, transparent SMB email finder" rather than full-stack GTM platform.

## Product Offerings

### Email Finder (web app + LinkedIn Chrome extension)

> **Wiki:** [[getprospect--email-finder]]

- **What it does:** Finds verified business email addresses by name + company / domain, both inside the GetProspect web app and on LinkedIn / Sales Navigator profiles via a Chrome extension.
- **Key features:**
  - LinkedIn Email Finder Chrome extension for one-click email lookup on LinkedIn and Sales Navigator
  - Email lookup by first name + last name + company domain (17 filters)
  - Bulk email finder via domain filter (500/search) or CSV upload (up to 50,000 domains)
  - Reverse email lookup to surface contact name, role, and company from an email
  - Catch-all email verification via proprietary technology
  - 95% data accuracy guarantee with credit refund if lower
  - 50 free valid emails / 100 verifications per month on free plan
- **Source URL:** https://getprospect.com/

### B2B Contact Database

> **Wiki:** [[getprospect--b2b-database]]

- **What it does:** Searchable B2B database used to build prospect lists by ICP filters, with 200M contacts and 26M companies covering 230M corporate emails and 130M mobile phone numbers.
- **Key features:**
  - 200M B2B contacts with emails, 26M companies, 230M corporate emails, 130M mobile phone numbers
  - 8 advanced filters: Job title, Department, Seniority, Location, Industry, Company size, Technologies, Revenue
  - Tracks 3,000 technologies for technographic targeting
  - Bulk save to lists and folders; export up to 50,000 records to CSV for free
  - Recent search history and saved filters
  - 95%+ data accuracy guarantee
- **Source URL:** https://getprospect.com/b2b-contact-database

### Data Enrichment

> **Wiki:** [[getprospect--enrichment]]

- **What it does:** Enriches existing contact or company lists with emails, mobile numbers, and 30+ attributes via single lookup, bulk CSV upload, or API.
- **Key features:**
  - Bulk CSV enrichment (First name + Last name + Company name/domain/LinkedIn URL) across 13 contact attributes
  - Company-level enrichment across 22 attributes (location, description, technologies, social links)
  - One-by-one enrichment by LinkedIn URL or email
  - REST API for programmatic enrichment with JSON requests
  - Native CRM sync to HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho
  - CSV/XLSX export up to 50,000 rows free
- **Source URL:** https://getprospect.com/enrichment

### GetProspect CRM

> **Wiki:** [[getprospect--crm]]

- **What it does:** Free built-in lightweight CRM for organizing scraped/enriched leads into nested folders, dynamic lists, and prospect cards with custom fields.
- **Key features:**
  - Nested folders and dynamic lists for lead segmentation
  - Custom lead fields (dates, text, numbers) used as filter criteria or email variables
  - Prospect cards with company + contact details, activities, notes, prior interactions
  - Multi-workspace team collaboration
  - CSV/XLSX import and export
  - Direct push from LinkedIn/web to CRM lists
- **Source URL:** https://getprospect.com/crm

### Cold Email Software

> **Wiki:** [[getprospect--cold-email]]

- **What it does:** Built-in cold email sender and sequencer for outreach to enriched contact lists, included on the free plan (one sequence) and all paid tiers.
- **Key features:**
  - Cold email sequences against contact lists
  - 97% deliverability via built-in email verification step
  - Up to 100 emails/day per domain recommended to protect sender reputation
  - Available on Free, Starter, and Growth plans
- **Source URL:** https://getprospect.com/

- Free plan with 50 valid emails + 100 verifications + team accounts at no cost; the vs-page calls out ZoomInfo's $1,000+ team-seat surcharge as the contrast (source: https://getprospect.com/zoominfo-alternative)
- Proprietary catch-all email verification surfaces deliverable addresses on domains other tools mark unverifiable (source: https://getprospect.com/pricing)
- All-in-one bundle (email finder + database + enrichment + CRM + cold email) at email-finder pricing — no add-on cost for sequencing or CRM (source: https://getprospect.com/zoominfo-alternative)
- EU-hosted, GDPR-compliant Ukrainian company — explicit EU hosting and DPA on every page (source: https://getprospect.com/pricing)
- Stackable Growth 50k plan (up to 10x) for buyers who need higher volume without an enterprise contract (source: https://getprospect.com/pricing)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **Data (Pillar 1) (200M+ verified emails, 135M+ verified phone numbers, 500M contacts) ↔ GetProspect B2B Contact Database (200M contacts, 230M corporate emails, 130M mobile phone numbers, 26M companies).** GetProspect's database page mirrors ZoomInfo's volumetric Pillar-1 framing — verified emails, mobile phone numbers, technographics — at a fraction of the price (source: https://getprospect.com/b2b-contact-database).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (classic prospecting UI / contact lookup) ↔ GetProspect Email Finder + LinkedIn Chrome extension.** GetProspect explicitly positions as a ZoomInfo alternative for finding prospect emails and runs a 100-prospect head-to-head test on its vs-page (source: https://getprospect.com/zoominfo-alternative).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations ↔ GetProspect Data Enrichment.** Bulk CSV + API enrichment across 30+ attributes touches the CRM-data-quality use case ZoomInfo Operations owns, but at SMB scale and without the dedupe/routing layer (source: https://getprospect.com/enrichment).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales outreach functionality ↔ GetProspect Cold Email module.** GetProspect bundles a sequencer with deliverability checks; same workflow surface as ZoomInfo's outreach features but with no intent-data prioritization (source: https://getprospect.com/).
- **No overlap:**
  - GTM Context Graph / intent / buyer-signal layer — not present in GetProspect's reviewed pages (source: https://getprospect.com/).
  - GTM Studio (ABM, audience orchestration, marketing/RevOps surface) — no equivalent (source: https://getprospect.com/).
  - Conversation intelligence (Chorus) — no call recording, transcription, or analytics (source: https://getprospect.com/crm).
  - Inverse: GetProspect ships a free built-in CRM to capture scraped leads, while ZoomInfo positions as a layer on top of Salesforce/HubSpot/Dynamics rather than as a CRM itself (source: https://getprospect.com/crm).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "GetProspect is the best ZoomInfo alternative because it gives much greater results during the prospect search, and the info is fresher. ZoomInfo lost to a competitor because it gave fewer results, plus they were not entirely accurate." (source: https://getprospect.com/zoominfo-alternative). They reinforce this with an internal test claiming 84 valid emails vs ZoomInfo's 60 out of 100, and call out ZoomInfo's gated pricing and team-seat surcharge as a contrast to their transparent freemium model.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Find verified business emails from LinkedIn or a 200M-contact B2B database, then organize, enrich, and email them — all in one platform with a free plan (paraphrased from https://getprospect.com/).
- **Recurring proof points:** 200M B2B contacts / 230M corporate emails / 26M companies / 130M mobile phone numbers / 3,000 technologies; 95% accuracy guarantee with credit refund; internal head-to-head 84 vs 60 valid emails vs ZoomInfo; 97% cold email deliverability; 1,839 users joined in the last 7 days; free plan with 50 emails + team accounts (sources: https://getprospect.com/, https://getprospect.com/zoominfo-alternative, https://getprospect.com/pricing).
- **Tone / category framing:** Self-positioned as a "B2B platform" that bundles email finder + LinkedIn Chrome extension + B2B database + enrichment + CRM + cold email. On the vs-page they accept the "ZoomInfo alternative" frame and lean on price transparency, EU/GDPR posture, and email-finder accuracy as their wedge.
