# ListKit

- [[listkit--triple-verified-database|ListKit Triple-Verified Database]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales
- [[listkit--dfy-services|ListKit DFY Services]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales

## Summary

ListKit is a credit-based B2B contact-data tool aimed at small-business and agency outbound operators (50-or-fewer-employee shops, founders, cold-email agencies) that pairs a 731M-contact "Triple-Verified" database with done-for-you cold-email and SDR-dialer services. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's data foundation and intent layer, but sells into a much narrower ICP and competes on price + simplicity rather than on platform breadth. Where ZoomInfo positions as an all-in-one AI GTM Platform spanning Workspace, Studio, APIs/MCP, and Chorus, ListKit covers only data + sending — and explicitly leans on a "ZoomInfo locks you in / ListKit lets you scale" pitch with a side-by-side claiming 731M vs 174M leads at $97 vs $399+.

## Product Offerings

### Triple-Verified B2B Lead Database (self-serve platform)

- **What it does:** Searchable B2B contact database that lets users build hyper-targeted prospect lists via a plain-English AI prompt; every contact runs through a "Triple-Verification" engine before export.
- **Key features:**
  - AI Company Search — natural-language prompt interface ("Funded fintech companies with 100+ employees") returns a targeted list
  - 731M Triple-Verified email contacts (626M leads on the triple-verified-leads page)
  - Triple-Verification engine (email validity + domain integrity + recent-activity check) replacing third-party verifiers
  - Unlimited users on every paid plan
  - Waterfall data enrichment (company + lead-level data points priced per credit)
  - List exports ready in under 5 minutes; no export limits
  - GDPR compliant; 5M emails verified monthly; 21B daily buying signals claimed
- **Source URL:** https://www.listkit.io/triple-verified-leads

### Unlimited Intent Data

- **What it does:** Daily-refreshed list of buying-intent leads keyed to a selected intent topic, exported as a list rather than scored inside a CRM.
- **Key features:**
  - Select intent topic, export thousands of intent-matched leads in one flow
  - Daily refresh of the intent-flagged set
  - No export limits on intent leads
  - Positioned as "no cold outreach, just warm leads" — replaces a multi-tool intent + enrich + filter workflow
- **Source URL:** https://www.listkit.io/unlimited-intent-data

### Triple-Verified Phone Numbers / Direct Dials

- **What it does:** Mobile and direct-dial phone numbers added to lead exports for sales teams running cold-call workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Mobile numbers + direct dials surfaced alongside email contacts
  - Phone numbers cost 5 credits per record (per pricing-page calculator)
  - Integrated into the same list-build flow as email leads
  - Marketed against "gatekeepers and landlines"
- **Source URL:** https://www.listkit.io/phone-numbers

### Cold Email Setup (DFY managed sending infrastructure)

- **What it does:** Done-for-you setup and ongoing management of cold-email sending infrastructure — domains, inboxes, scripts, deliverability monitoring — on ListKit's private (non-Google/Outlook) IPs.
- **Key features:**
  - Private "invite-only" sending IP infrastructure separate from Google/Outlook
  - 30 / 60 / 90 inboxes per Base / Gold / Platinum tier
  - 24/7 AI deliverability monitoring with auto inbox replacement
  - Domain + inbox warmup, 3-step sequence creation, monthly script refreshes
  - Up to 5,000 / 10,000 / 15,000 leads contacted per month per tier
  - Up to 15K / 30K / 45K outreach messages/month per tier
  - Client purchases own domains via Porkbun; one-time $1,500 setup fee
- **Source URL:** https://www.listkit.io/cold-email-setup

### Fully Managed Omnichannel Outbound (DFY cold email + cold call agency)

- **What it does:** Managed-service offering where ListKit runs both email and SDR cold-call campaigns end-to-end — list build, scripts, dialing, response sorting, meeting booking — billed quarterly.
- **Key features:**
  - Human SDRs running an AI predictive parallel dialer at 1,500–2,000 dials/day
  - Short 20–30-word AI-personalized cold emails layered on top of dialing
  - Up to 30K / 60K / 120K / 240K leads prospected per quarter (Minimum / Growth / Scale / Enterprise)
  - TCPA-compliant B2B-only dialing with DNC scanning
  - AI response sorting — replies auto-categorized as Interested / Meeting Request / Information Request / Not Interested
  - AI-written personalized ice-breakers per prospect; 24/7 AI deliverability monitoring
  - Meetings booked directly on the client's calendar; no setup fee, billed quarterly
- **Source URL:** https://www.listkit.io/fully-managed-omnichannel-outbound

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** B2B agencies (cold-email / lead-gen agencies), B2B SaaS, professional services firms, e-commerce / DTC brands, real estate marketing firms (case-study mention)
- **Company size called out:** Small business (50 or fewer employees, explicitly self-described G2 segment); founders / solo operators; mid-market via the managed-service tiers up to 240K leads/quarter
- **Persona / role focus:** Founder / agency owner running outbound themselves; SDRs and cold-email operators; sales leaders evaluating in-house SDR vs outsourced
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Building hyper-targeted B2B prospect lists from a plain-English ICP description
  - Running cold-email campaigns at 10K+ sends/month
  - Cold calling decision-makers via direct-dial phone numbers
  - Outsourcing the entire outbound function (email + dialing + meeting booking)
  - Targeting buying-intent leads with daily refreshes

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- "Triple-Verified" lead system (email validity + domain integrity + recent-activity check) marketed as 100% accuracy and ~98% deliverability with no second verification tool needed (source: https://www.listkit.io/triple-verified-leads)
- Self-serve database AND a fully-managed SDR + dialer service sold by the same vendor — buyers can move from $83/mo software to $6K–$28K/mo managed outbound without changing data source (source: https://www.listkit.io/fully-managed-omnichannel-outbound)
- Plain-English / "ChatGPT-for-lists" AI Company Search as the primary list-building UI (source: https://www.listkit.io/)
- Unlimited users on every paid tier — explicitly contrasted with seat-based competitors (source: https://www.listkit.io/pricing)
- Credit-based, contract-free monthly pricing positioned as the anti-annual-contract alternative (source: https://www.listkit.io/comparison-listkit-vs-zoominfo)
- Private cold-email sending infrastructure (own IPs, own domains) separate from Google/Outlook — sold as a deliverability moat (source: https://www.listkit.io/cold-email-setup)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (contacts, companies, phone numbers, business emails)** ↔ ListKit Triple-Verified B2B Lead Database (731M contacts) + Triple-Verified Phone Numbers. ListKit's own /pricing comparison table asserts "Amount of Verified Leads: ListKit 731M vs ZoomInfo 174M" and prices ZoomInfo at $399+ vs ListKit $97 (source: https://www.listkit.io/pricing). The phone-numbers product directly competes with ZoomInfo's 135M+ verified phone numbers / 120M direct dials.
  - **ZoomInfo intent / GTM Context Graph signals** ↔ ListKit Unlimited Intent Data — daily-refreshed buying-intent list keyed to a topic; ships as an exportable list rather than as graph-based scoring inside a workspace (source: https://www.listkit.io/unlimited-intent-data).
- **Adjacent overlap:** ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace (seller front-end with AI-drafted outreach) ↔ ListKit Cold Email Setup + Fully Managed Omnichannel Outbound. ListKit executes outbound for the customer (private IPs, AI-personalized emails, AI dialer, response sorting, calendar booking) — adjacent to GTM Workspace's AI-drafted outreach but delivered as a managed service, not as a self-serve seller surface (source: https://www.listkit.io/fully-managed-omnichannel-outbound).
- **No overlap:**
  - Managed-service SDR + dialer agency model — ZoomInfo doesn't run cold-email campaigns or staff SDR teams for customers; ListKit's $6K–$85K/quarter managed-outbound product has no ZoomInfo counterpart.
  - Private cold-email sending infrastructure (custom domains + "invite-only" IPs) — ZoomInfo doesn't sell sending infrastructure / inboxes.
  - Conversation intelligence (Chorus equivalent) — _No evidence in reviewed pages._
  - ABM / orchestration platform (GTM Studio equivalent) — _No evidence in reviewed pages._
  - Enterprise APIs / MCP server — _No public API or MCP offering surfaced on reviewed ListKit pages._
  - CRM data quality / routing (ZoomInfo Operations equivalent) — _No evidence in reviewed pages._
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "ZoomInfo Locks You In. ListKit Lets You Scale. Zoominfo claims their data is verified. It's not. With ListKit, every lead is Triple-Verified with 100% accuracy." Side-by-side asserts 731M vs 174M leads and $97 vs $399+. Workflow diagram frames ZoomInfo as a 7-step process (page-by-page selection, separate verifier and catch-all verifier purchases, manual sheet handoffs) vs ListKit's 3-step (select → confirm → upload to sender). Listed advantages ListKit checks but ZoomInfo (per their table) does not include: no separate email verification, discounted additional credits, lifetime "never pay for the same lead twice," unlimited users, no charge for invalid emails, ICP insights visualization, priority support (source: https://www.listkit.io/comparison-listkit-vs-zoominfo).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Build hyper-targeted B2B lead lists from a plain-English search, with every contact triple-verified — and, if you want, hand the entire outbound campaign to ListKit to run for you.
- **Recurring proof points:** 731M Triple-Verified email contacts; 5M emails verified monthly; 21B daily buying signals; Trustpilot 4.3/5 (193 reviews); G2 4.8/5 (385 reviews); managed-outbound page cites $1M ARR in 90 days, 1,700+ B2B clients served, 1,500+ dials/day per SDR, 215,983 sent / 5,416 replied / 440 positive customer-campaign result.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames itself as "the fastest way to book B2B sales calls" and "AI built exclusively for list building" — explicitly avoids "sales intelligence platform" / "GTM platform" framing. Positions ZoomInfo as the slow, expensive, contract-locked incumbent and ListKit as the credit-based, cold-outreach-native alternative.
