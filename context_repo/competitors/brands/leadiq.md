# LeadIQ

**G2:** ⭐ 4.2 / 5 — 648 reviews  <!-- g2-injector -->

## Summary

LeadIQ is a Smart B2B Prospecting Platform that competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Data foundation (Pillar 1) on contact data accuracy and seller-workflow simplicity. LeadIQ publishes a dedicated `/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo` page (one of the most explicit vs-pages in the ZoomInfo competitor set) claiming "Top revenue teams trust LeadIQ data quality over ZoomInfo," with G2-sourced ROI of 6 months for LeadIQ vs. 14 months for ZoomInfo. The pitch is real-time data verification + Salesforce-native one-click prospecting + AI-powered account research, positioned as "easier to use than ZoomInfo." LeadIQ does not compete on conversation intelligence, ABM, or marketing-side products. Pricing is fully public, credit-based, scaling from 2,400 to 81,000 credits/year plus an Enterprise tier.

## Product Offerings

### Real-Time Champion Tracking

- **What it does:** Job-change notifications and real-time tracking of sales champions as they move companies — surfaces buying-trigger moments.
- **Key features:**
  - Job change notifications
  - Real-time prospect movement tracking
  - Champion-of-record identification
  - CRM sync for re-engagement workflows
- **Source URL:** https://leadiq.com/

### AI-Powered Account Prospecting

- **What it does:** Automated prospect research across LinkedIn, 10-K filings, and web data — produces account-level intelligence + contact lists for sellers.
- **Key features:**
  - Automated account research (LinkedIn, 10-K filings, web)
  - AI-powered contact suggestions
  - Account-level intelligence
  - Replaces manual prospecting research
  - Sigma case study cited 5x prospecting outreach increase
- **Source URL:** https://leadiq.com/

### Real-Time CRM Enrichment

- **What it does:** One-click capture from LinkedIn / Sales Navigator into Salesforce, HubSpot, and sequencing tools — real-time data verification on delivery.
- **Key features:**
  - One-click capture from LinkedIn to CRM + sequence
  - Real-time email + mobile verification
  - Direct dial + verified business email
  - Native Salesforce + HubSpot enrichment workflows
  - HubSpot Enrichment dedicated product page
- **Source URL:** https://leadiq.com/hubspot-enrichment

### LeadIQ Database Search (Instant Prospecting)

- **What it does:** Database-search interface for finding contacts and companies inside LeadIQ.
- **Key features:**
  - People Search (Email + Mobile Numbers)
  - Company Search
  - Available across all paid tiers
  - Limited Access on lowest tier
- **Source URL:** https://leadiq.com/pricing

### AI Email Writer

- **What it does:** AI-generated personalized cold email drafts based on prospect data + company context.
- **Key features:**
  - AI email writer for personalized cold outreach
  - Powered by prospect + company data
  - Integrated into the prospecting workflow
- **Source URL:** https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo

### Governance Controls for Prospect Data

- **What it does:** Admin controls for governing how reps prospect, capture, and export data — gated to higher-tier plans.
- **Key features:**
  - Governance controls for prospect data
  - Admin oversight of capture workflows
  - Available on Pro/Enterprise tiers
- **Source URL:** https://leadiq.com/pricing

## Target Audience & ICP

- **Industries called out:** Cross-industry; case-study customers include Sigma (data analytics), WalkMe (digital adoption), Optimizely (experimentation), LeanData (lead-to-account routing), Workato (automation), Smartly.io (marketing), Stream, Greenhouse, Planview, Darktrace, Clari (LeadIQ counts Clari among customers), Gong (LeadIQ counts Gong among customers).
- **Company size called out:** SMB through Enterprise; pricing tiers flex from 2.4K credits/year (single user) through Enterprise.
- **Persona / role focus:** SDRs, AEs, Sales Operations, Enterprise Account Managers, Sales Leaders.
- **Use cases promoted:** B2B prospecting; CRM enrichment; LinkedIn-to-CRM workflow; sales sequence creation; champion tracking; AI-driven account research.

## Integrations & Ecosystem

- **CRMs:** Salesforce (deep native), HubSpot (dedicated `/hubspot-enrichment` product page).
- **Sales engagement / outreach:** Salesloft, Outreach, Groove by Clari.
- **Data / enrichment:** Real-time verification via data partnerships + community + proprietary algorithms.
- **Other notable integrations:** Zapier, LinkedIn / Sales Navigator (browser extension), Chrome Extension as primary prospecting surface.

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Dedicated `/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo` page with explicit competitive claims and customer testimonials naming ZoomInfo (source: https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo).
- 6-month vs 14-month time-to-ROI claim from G2 (LeadIQ vs ZoomInfo) — published directly on the vs-page (source: https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo).
- Real-time data verification at the moment of capture, vs. ZoomInfo's batch refresh approach (source: https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo).
- Salesforce-native one-click capture: "I find a lead on SalesNav that I like, I press 1 button, and he is in salesforce AND a sequence" — direct customer quote (source: https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo).
- Public tier-based pricing with a credit slider — vs. ZoomInfo's "Contact Sales" gating.
- Enterprise customer base includes companies that themselves compete with ZoomInfo (Clari, Gong) — strong third-party signal.
- 30-day free trial across any plan (uncommon at the enterprise data-vendor level).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (Pillar 1) ↔ LeadIQ Database + People Search.** Direct head-to-head on B2B contact data accuracy. LeadIQ's testimonials specifically claim better current-data freshness than ZoomInfo. (source: https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo)
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace seller capture surface ↔ LeadIQ Chrome Extension + Salesforce-native capture.** Both deliver in-CRM enrichment from LinkedIn / web. LeadIQ's pitch is one-click simplicity vs ZoomInfo's broader-but-heavier surface. (source: https://leadiq.com/)
  - **ZoomInfo Operations / GTM Studio (data ops + enrichment) ↔ LeadIQ HubSpot Enrichment + Salesforce sync.** Both target CRM hygiene workflows. (source: https://leadiq.com/hubspot-enrichment)
  - **ZoomInfo's AI features (AI email writer, Copilot) ↔ LeadIQ AI Email Writer + AI Account Prospecting.** Both vendors layered AI on top of contact data; LeadIQ's vs-page claims AI features are "Universal access to all data & tools" included in plan, vs ZoomInfo's per-seat Copilot pricing. (source: https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo APIs ↔ LeadIQ API (referenced via integrations).** No publicized MCP server.
- **No overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Chorus (conversation intelligence)** — LeadIQ has no CI product.
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / WebSights / FormComplete** — no marketing-side product.
  - **ABM Platform (Gartner MQ)** — LeadIQ is not in this category.
  - **Standalone DaaS / programmatic data delivery** — not LeadIQ's positioning.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Direct quote from the vs-page: _"Top revenue teams trust LeadIQ data quality over ZoomInfo... I've been a long-time user of ZoomInfo. The verified contacts in LeadIQ save so much time not reaching out to outdated prospects."_ — Enterprise Account Manager, Mid Market. _"LeadIQ > ZoomInfo. It's just EASY. I find a lead on SalesNav that I like, I press 1 button, and he is in salesforce AND a sequence."_ — Enterprise Account Manager. The vs-page also publishes a side-by-side feature table claiming LeadIQ-only advantages on real-time data verification, AI email writer, automated prospect research, and "universal access to all data & tools." (source: https://leadiq.com/compare/leadiq-vs-zoominfo)

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "The Smart B2B Prospecting Platform — Accelerate Revenue with AI-Driven Data."
- **Recurring proof points:** Sigma 5x prospecting outreach; WalkMe 1,000 hours saved per quarter + 19% increase in discovery calls; Optimizely $2M pipeline in one year; LeanData efficiency gains; G2-sourced 6-month vs 14-month ROI vs ZoomInfo.
- **Tone / category framing:** "Smart B2B Prospecting Platform" — LeadIQ deliberately stays narrower than ZoomInfo's "all-in-one GTM platform" frame, instead positioning around prospecting workflow simplicity and data freshness. The vs-page uses customer voice rather than corporate marketing copy, which makes the competitive claims more concrete.
