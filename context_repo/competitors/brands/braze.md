## Summary

Braze is a B2C customer engagement platform (multichannel marketing hub) sold to consumer marketers at retail, fintech, gaming, QSR, media, travel, and on-demand brands — a fundamentally different ICP than ZoomInfo's B2B GTM buyer set. It has no B2B contact database, no seller workflow, no conversation intelligence, and no GTM Context Graph equivalent — its data layer is the customer's own first-party MAUs, billed as Active Users plus Flexible Credits. The only adjacent overlap with ZoomInfo is generic "marketer audience building + paid-media sync" through BrazeAI Operator, Canvas, and Media Audience Sync, but the audiences are logged-in consumer profiles rather than B2B accounts/contacts. Treat Braze as ICP-mismatched competitive context, not as a head-to-head substitute for any ZoomInfo product line.

## Product Offerings

### Braze Platform (overview)

> **Wiki:** [[braze--platform]]

- **What it does:** Vertically-integrated customer engagement platform that unifies first-party customer data, orchestrates real-time multi-step journeys, and delivers cross-channel messaging powered by BrazeAI.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time data unification across data warehouse, digital properties, and backend systems
  - Canvas Orchestration for multi-step cross-channel journeys
  - Cross-channel delivery from one platform
  - Reporting & Analytics with internal AI models
  - BrazeAI integrated across data, journey, content, and decisioning steps
  - Scale claims: 4.5T messages/Canvas actions, 10.2T API calls, 17.9B personalized AI decisions in 2025
- **Source URL:** https://www.braze.com/product/overview

### Braze Data Platform

> **Wiki:** [[braze--data-platform]]

- **What it does:** Real-time first-party customer data layer that unifies, activates, and bidirectionally streams customer data into Braze and back out to analytics tools.
- **Key features:**
  - Direct integrations with data warehouse, digital properties, backend systems
  - Always-on real-time stream that updates segments and personalization
  - Bidirectional data flow into and out of analytics/visualization tools
  - Internal AI models that update as data streams
- **Source URL:** https://www.braze.com/product/overview

### BrazeAI

> **Wiki:** [[braze--brazeai]]

- **What it does:** AI product suite spanning content generation, decisioning, agents, and a conversational operator embedded across the marketer's workflow.
- **Key features:**
  - BrazeAI Operator (Beta) — conversational interface for building campaigns, segments, and queries
  - BrazeAI Decisioning Studio — 1:1 personalization optimized to any business KPI
  - BrazeAI Agent Console — embed AI agents at any step of a customer journey
  - AI content generation tied to brand guidelines and real-time customer data
  - AI Item Recommendations (e.g., out-of-stock email use case)
- **Source URL:** https://www.braze.com/product/brazeai

### Cross-Channel Messaging (Canvas + Channels)

> **Wiki:** [[braze--cross-channel-messaging]]

- **What it does:** Native cross-channel orchestration across email, mobile push, in-app, web, SMS/RCS, WhatsApp, LINE, KakaoTalk, and audience sync to ad platforms.
- **Key features:**
  - Email
  - Mobile App Messaging (push, in-app)
  - Web Messaging
  - SMS / RCS
  - WhatsApp
  - LINE and KakaoTalk (regional channels)
  - Media Audience Sync (paid-media audience activation)
  - Canvas journey orchestration with experimentation
- **Source URL:** https://www.braze.com/

### Canvas Orchestration

> **Wiki:** [[braze--canvas-orchestration]]

- **What it does:** Multi-step cross-channel journey orchestration with experimentation — Braze's flagship workflow surface for marketers.
- **Key features:**
  - Multi-step journey builder
  - Cross-channel reach (email, push, SMS, in-app, web, regional messengers)
  - Experimentation built into journey steps
  - Real-time triggers from streamed first-party data
  - BrazeAI Decisioning Studio for 1:1 personalization at journey steps
- **Source URL:** https://www.braze.com/product/overview

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Real-time stream processing engine vs. batch processing of legacy marketing platforms — Braze explicitly contrasts itself against "batch processing common in legacy tools" (source: https://www.braze.com/resources/articles/braze-pricing)
- Activity-based ("value-based") pricing — billed by Active Users + Flexible Credits + Platform Edition rather than total database size or per-seat licenses (source: https://www.braze.com/resources/articles/braze-pricing)
- Native cross-channel reach including mobile-first and regional channels (WhatsApp, LINE, KakaoTalk, RCS, SMS, push, in-app) from one platform (source: https://www.braze.com/)
- Leader in 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Multichannel Marketing Hubs (third year in a row); Forrester TEI claims "457% ROI and payback in less than six months"; ranked #1 in G2 Push Notification Grid (source: https://www.braze.com/)
- Vertical AI suite (BrazeAI Operator, Decisioning Studio, Agent Console) embedded across data, journey, content, and decision steps — not a single AI bolt-on (source: https://www.braze.com/product/brazeai)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Braze does not sell B2B contact data, GTM Context Graph reasoning, sales seller workspace, RevOps/GTM-engineer surface, or a developer-facing B2B data API/MCP — none of ZoomInfo's six primary product lines are head-to-head substituted by any Braze product.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Generic "marketer audience building and paid-media sync" — Braze Canvas + Media Audience Sync overlap conceptually with ZoomInfo Marketing's audience orchestration, and BrazeAI Operator's natural-language-to-audience UX overlaps conceptually with GTM Studio. In both cases the underlying data layer is fundamentally different: Braze audiences are first-party logged-in consumer MAUs, ZoomInfo audiences are B2B accounts/contacts fused with first-party CRM (sources: https://www.braze.com/, https://www.braze.com/product/brazeai).
- **No overlap:** B2B contact / company / technographic / intent / direct-dial database (no third-party data layer exists in Braze); seller / SDR / AE workspace; conversation intelligence (no Chorus equivalent); CS / account-management surface; RevOps data-quality and routing tools; B2C-only verticals like Gaming, QSR, On Demand, and regional consumer messengers (LINE, KakaoTalk, WhatsApp commerce) where ZoomInfo does not compete (source: https://www.braze.com/, https://www.vendr.com/marketplace/braze).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Braze's named comparison targets are Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Adobe, Iterable, Klaviyo, Pega, Emarsys, and beehiiv — all B2C marketing-cloud peers, not ZoomInfo.

- **Recurring proof points:** 2,600+ brands; 4.5T messages and Canvas actions / 10.2T API calls / 17.9B personalized AI decisions in 2025; Forrester TEI 457% ROI / <6mo payback; 2025 Gartner MQ Leader for Multichannel Marketing Hubs (3rd year); G2 #1 Push Notification Grid; logos include Pizza Hut, Wyndham, MetLife, Delivery Hero, Canva, HBO Max, Chime, Venmo, KFC, Etsy, GAP, Intuit/TurboTax, Wealthsimple (source: https://www.braze.com/, https://www.braze.com/product/overview).
