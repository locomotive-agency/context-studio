## Summary

Sprout Social is a social media management platform serving social-marketing managers, community managers, and social customer-care leads at SMBs, agencies, and enterprises (including Salesforce, Atlassian, HP, UNICEF, and Equifax). Its product spans publishing, engagement (Smart Inbox), listening, analytics, influencer marketing, and employee advocacy — a fundamentally different category from ZoomInfo's B2B GTM intelligence platform. There is no direct overlap with ZoomInfo's product lines: Sprout's "intelligence" reasons over public social conversations, not over verified B2B contact/company data, intent signals, or first-party CRM/conversation data. Treat as an adjacent/tangential competitor at the marketing-persona frame only; the TrustRadius "vs ZoomInfo" page surfaces user reviews of unrelated products rather than a head-to-head competitive pitch.

## Product Offerings

### Engagement (Smart Inbox + customer care)

> **Wiki:** [[sprout-social--engagement]]

- **What it does:** Unified social inbox that consolidates messages, comments, and reviews across networks with AI-driven response assistance, case management, and a Salesforce Service Cloud integration.
- **Key features:**
  - Smart Inbox with custom views, message tagging, and case management
  - Enhance by AI Assist for reply drafting and tone adjustment
  - Message Spike Alerts for surge/crisis detection
  - Salesforce Service Cloud integration (Sprout described as Salesforce's preferred social media management solution)
  - Bot Builder for Twitter DM and Facebook Messenger automation
  - Review Management across Apple App Store, Google Play, Yelp, Trustpilot, Glassdoor, Tripadvisor
- **Source URL:** https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-engagement

### Publishing

> **Wiki:** [[sprout-social--publishing]]

- **What it does:** Plan, schedule, and publish content across networks with AI caption generation, optimal-time scheduling, and approval workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Optimal Send Times — proprietary algorithm using 16 weeks of audience data (claims up to 60% lift in reach) (source: https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-publishing)
  - Collaborative Social Media Calendar across profiles and networks
  - Enhance Post by AI Assist for caption generation
  - SproutLink link-in-bio for Instagram
  - Asset Library with Canva, Bynder, Adobe Experience Manager, Dropbox, Google Drive integrations
  - Campaign Planner and message approval workflows
- **Source URL:** https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-publishing

### Analytics

> **Wiki:** [[sprout-social--analytics]]

- **What it does:** Cross-network social performance reporting that separates organic vs paid and tracks profile, post, hashtag, and competitor metrics.
- **Key features:**
  - Paid Performance Reporting across channels
  - Hashtag Tracking and Profile/Post/Tag/Network reports
  - Competitor Reports for share-of-voice benchmarking
  - Premium Analytics add-on for custom filters, ROI charts, and shareable presentation-ready reports
  - Case Team Activity Report for customer-care productivity
- **Source URL:** https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-analytics

### Listening

> **Wiki:** [[sprout-social--listening]]

- **What it does:** AI-driven social listening that processes billions of unfiltered conversations to surface trends, sentiment, and competitor share-of-voice insights.
- **Key features:**
  - Smart Categories for instant topic identification
  - Sentiment tracking and scoring model
  - Custom Spike Alerts for sudden volume or sentiment changes
  - Trellis AI teammate for plain-language research questions
  - Competitor Comparison and share-of-voice tracking
  - Consumer Research with demographics (age, gender, geo, device)
- **Source URL:** https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-listening

### Influencer Marketing

> **Wiki:** [[sprout-social--influencer-marketing]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered influencer discovery, campaign activation, payments, and reporting; sold separately from the core Sprout plans (no Sprout plan required).
- **Key features:**
  - Discover creators from 10M+ social profiles using smart filters
  - Brand Fit Score and brand-safety scoring
  - End-to-end campaign management
  - Conversion tracking via UTMs and pixels
  - Creator fee payments handled in-platform
- **Source URL:** https://sproutsocial.com/pricing/

### Employee Advocacy (add-on)

- **What it does:** Curates on-brand content for employees to share with their personal social networks, with UTM-based attribution and earned media value calculations.
- **Key features:**
  - Curated content libraries for employee sharing
  - UTM tracking for measurable engagement and sales
  - Earned media value (EMV) calculation
  - Distribution via My Feed, newsletters, Slack, and mobile alerts
  - Send-to-Advocacy from the Compose workflow
- **Source URL:** https://sproutsocial.com/pricing/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** _No evidence in reviewed pages._ Sprout Social does not compete head-to-head with any ZoomInfo product line — Data foundation, GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, APIs/MCP, ZoomInfo Sales, Marketing, Operations, Chorus, or Chat. Sprout sells social media management; ZoomInfo sells B2B GTM intelligence. (See `manual_review:icp_mismatch` flag.)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio (marketer/RevOps front-end of GTM platform):** Sprout publishing/analytics/listening also targets marketing personas, but Sprout's surface is social-channel campaign execution and reporting whereas GTM Studio orchestrates B2B audiences/plays across CRM and intent — the marketer is shared, the workflow is not (source: https://sproutsocial.com/features/).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM):** Sprout Listening + Premium Analytics surface consumer/competitor sentiment and brand-health signals; ZoomInfo Marketing surfaces account-level intent and ABM activation — overlap is at the "marketing intelligence" frame only, not the underlying data layer (source: https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-listening).
  - **Chorus (conversation intelligence) / customer-care surfaces:** Both touch customer-conversation data, but Sprout captures public social conversations while Chorus captures sales-call conversations; no direct substitution (source: https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-engagement).
- **No overlap:**
  - B2B contact and company data (no equivalent to ZoomInfo's 500M contacts / 100M companies / verified emails and phones) (source: https://sproutsocial.com/)
  - Sales prospecting and sales engagement — no SDR/AE prospecting workflow, no AI-drafted sales outreach (source: https://sproutsocial.com/pricing/)
  - GTM Context Graph / first-party CRM + intent + behavioral fusion — Sprout reasons over public social conversations only (source: https://sproutsocial.com/features/social-media-listening)
  - RevOps / data-quality / CRM enrichment / routing — no ZoomInfo Operations equivalent
  - APIs & MCP for AI-agent builders consuming B2B data — Sprout's API serves social workflows, not GTM data exposure (source: https://sproutsocial.com/pricing/)
  - Influencer marketing platform — Sprout sells a standalone Influencer Marketing product (10M+ creator profiles, payments, brand-fit scoring); ZoomInfo does not operate in this category (source: https://sproutsocial.com/pricing/)
  - Employee advocacy — Sprout's Employee Advocacy add-on has no ZoomInfo counterpart
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ The TrustRadius "Sprout Social vs ZoomInfo Sales" page aggregates user reviews across two unrelated product categories rather than presenting a competitive pitch — Sprout reviewers cite ease of social scheduling and reporting while ZoomInfo reviewers cite the contact database and direct-dial accuracy. The two products do not appear in the same buying decision (source: https://www.trustradius.com/compare-products/sprout-social-vs-zoominfo-sales).

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Powerful tools for social media management" — own every social media touchpoint by planning/scheduling content, engaging customers with AI, analyzing performance, and discovering trends (source: https://sproutsocial.com/).
