# GlobalData

## Sub-products

- [[globaldata--sales-intelligence|GlobalData Sales Intelligence]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales
- [[globaldata--research-cloud|GlobalData Research Cloud]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

GlobalData is an LSE-listed (LSE: DATA) enterprise market and sales intelligence vendor selling a vertical-deep, analyst-augmented data platform to commercial and strategy teams at global enterprises. Its Sales Intelligence solution — a Dec 2024 next-gen launch supported by the Sept 2025 "Sam" agentic AI sales analyst — is the relevant ZoomInfo overlap, covering plan/target/enable/engage workflows, AI-drafted account plans and pitches, and buying-intent signals. The crossover is real but partial: GlobalData competes with ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace and on the data-foundation pillar, but its core franchise is industry-vertical research (Pharma, Construction, Healthcare, Oil & Gas, etc.) backed by 800+ analysts, where ZoomInfo does not play. Pricing is gated on GlobalData's own site; the only quantitative benchmark is third-party (Vendr median ~$56K/yr).

## Product Offerings

### Sales Intelligence

- **What it does:** AI-powered, data-driven, expert-curated sales intelligence solution that supports the entire B2B sales workflow across plan, target, enable, and engage stages.
- **Key features:**
  - Plan: market attractiveness analysis and TAM/opportunity assessment with growth, risk and geographic indicators
  - Target: advanced search and data-driven account prioritization to focus on highest-potential opportunities
  - Enable: AI-generated personalized pitches, account plans, talking points, and customizable sales-collateral templates
  - Engage: real-time buying-intent signal alerts and live data on prospects and competitors for timely outreach
  - "Hidden demand" detection across qualitative datasets — patents, company filings, jobs, social media, news
  - Sam, an agentic AI Sales Analyst that plans, reasons, and orchestrates multi-step tasks across the GTM value chain
  - Integrates with the customer's existing tech stack and first-party customer data
  - Won "Data Solution of the Year for Sales" at the 2025 Data Breakthrough Awards
- **Source URL:** https://www.globaldata.com/solutions/sales-intelligence/

### Digital Workers (Sam, Ava, agentic AI suite)

- **What it does:** An ecosystem of agentic AI "digital workers" built on GlobalData's content universe, designed to plug into existing customer systems and automate research and GTM workflows.
- **Key features:**
  - Sam — AI Sales Analyst: plans target accounts, monitors expansion signals, drafts personalized outreach, optimizes campaigns
  - Ava — AI Research Analyst: executes analyst-built research templates, accepts uploaded customer files for personalized context
  - Powered by 260+ proprietary databases, 140+ TB of data, and inputs from 2,000 in-house analysts
  - Connects "effortlessly with existing tools and processes" (no specific integrations named on the page)
  - Enterprise-grade architecture trusted by 4,000 companies globally
- **Source URL:** https://www.globaldata.com/platform/artificial-intelligence/digital-workers

### Strategic Intelligence

- **What it does:** Foresight solution that turns 100+ strategic themes (AI, geopolitics, ESG, etc.) into ranked predictions, theme maps, and sector scorecards for high-stakes strategy decisions.
- **Key features:**
  - Theme maps identifying strategic issues across the next 3-5 years for a given industry
  - Sector scorecards ranking competitors by position across strategic themes
  - Predictive signals surfacing competitor / customer / supplier priorities from hiring, patents, deals
  - Innovation frameworks for spotting emerging tech and ranking start-ups
  - TS Lombard macroeconomic data and analysis (acquired 2022)
  - Thematic reports and analyst briefings for board-ready intelligence
- **Source URL:** https://www.globaldata.com/solutions/strategic-intelligence

### Market Intelligence

- **What it does:** Streamlined market research and industry analysis for go-to-market planning, market entry, and opportunity prioritization.
- **Key features:**
  - Quantitative and qualitative market trend and driver analysis
  - Real-time watchlists with alerts on target-company critical activity
  - AI-enhanced storyboards for pitch decks and whitepapers
  - Country and sector benchmarking with market forecasts
- **Source URL:** https://www.globaldata.com/solutions/market-intelligence

### Proprietary Data Foundation

- **What it does:** Cross-solution data layer that powers every GlobalData product, integrated under a single proprietary taxonomy spanning 50,000 unique nodes across sectors, geographies, and themes.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time data collection from 500,000+ daily intelligence sources
  - Real-time data on 1M+ companies and detailed profiles on 200,000+ companies
  - 10 data categories: asset-level, consumer research, alternative, company, deals, expert insights, macroeconomic, sector & market, industry news, media signals, job postings
  - Intent scoring from a B2B media network with 40M visitors
  - Job-posting data sourced by LinkUp from millions of employer-website postings
  - 100+ datasets available via API and data feeds for systems integration
  - Multi-channel sourcing (network access, client-sharing agreements, primary research, on-the-ground audits)
  - Statistical cross-checks, source-confidence assessments, expert-analyst review, automated validation
- **Source URL:** https://www.globaldata.com/platform/data

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace** vs **GlobalData Sales Intelligence + Sam.** Sales Intelligence supports the entire B2B sales workflow — plan markets, build ICPs, prioritize accounts, generate AI-drafted account plans/pitches, surface real-time buying-intent signals — and Sam is positioned as an agentic AI seller-side workspace. This is the most direct head-to-head with ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace's prioritized accounts + AI-drafted outreach pitch (source: https://www.globaldata.com/solutions/sales-intelligence/).
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation** vs **GlobalData proprietary data platform.** Single taxonomy, multi-source verification, 1M+ companies tracked, alternative data (patents/jobs/social/filings), API & data feeds — overlaps the ZoomInfo data-foundation pillar, though weighted to industry/market/company data rather than ZoomInfo's contact-level depth (people, phones, emails) (source: https://www.globaldata.com/platform/data).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / GTM Studio** vs **GlobalData Sales Intelligence + Market Intelligence.** Sales Intelligence explicitly serves "marketing" and "revenue teams", and Market Intelligence provides AI-enhanced storyboards for pitch decks and whitepapers plus go-to-market planning (source: https://www.globaldata.com/media/corporate/globaldata-launches-next-generation-sales-intelligence-platform/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Operations / intent-data layer** vs **GlobalData buying-signal analytics + "hidden demand" detection.** Custom buying-intent triggers across qualitative datasets are adjacent to ZoomInfo's intent + behavioral signals layer, but GlobalData does not pitch CRM data quality / routing capabilities (source: https://www.globaldata.com/solutions/sales-intelligence/).
  - **ZoomInfo APIs & MCP** vs **GlobalData API & Data Feeds (100+ datasets).** Functionally adjacent for embedding data in customer systems; no MCP server or Claude/agent-builder ecosystem listing surfaced on the reviewed pages (source: https://www.globaldata.com/platform/data).
- **No overlap:**
  - Industry-vertical research / reports (Pharma trials intelligence, Construction projects, Oil & Gas, Medical Devices, Power, Mining, etc.) delivered through industry-specific Intelligence Centers — ZoomInfo does not sell vertical market-research subscriptions (source: https://www.globaldata.com/).
  - Strategic Intelligence (themes/foresight, TS Lombard macro research) is forward-looking strategy / geopolitics / ESG foresight for board-level decisions, outside ZoomInfo's GTM remit (source: https://www.globaldata.com/solutions/strategic-intelligence).
  - Deals Intelligence (M&A, PE, VC deal-flow workflow automation) — sibling solution; ZoomInfo does not sell a deals-flow product (source: https://www.globaldata.com/).
  - Innovation Intelligence (patents, startups, technology landscape) — sibling solution focused on R&D/innovation buyers; outside ZoomInfo's product lines (source: https://www.globaldata.com/).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Better business decisions at the speed of AI" — a single connected intelligence platform combining proprietary data, 2,000 human experts, and AI to power critical decisions across the world's largest industries; for sales specifically, "3X faster revenue velocity" via AI-powered, data-driven, expert-curated Sales Intelligence (sources: https://www.globaldata.com/, https://www.globaldata.com/solutions/sales-intelligence/).
- **Recurring proof points:** Trusted by 4,000 companies globally (Aon, Deutsche Telekom, Hitachi Energy, Tetra Pak, Samsung Biologics, Nestle, Bain & Company, Deloitte, AstraZeneca, Vodafone, Orange, John Lewis); 260+ proprietary databases; 140+ TB of data; 100K+ analyst reports; 500K daily intelligence sources; 2,000 in-house analysts/researchers/consultants, 800 dedicated analysts, 100+ journalists, 250+ AI experts; "Data Solution of the Year for Sales" at the 2025 Data Breakthrough Awards; pilot deployments reported sales-conversion lift, reduced research time, and >$100K savings on TAM-planning costs.
- **Tone / category framing:** Positions itself as "the leading data, analytics, and insights platform for the world's largest industries" — vertical-deep market intelligence, NOT a horizontal B2B contact-data tool. Sales Intelligence is one solution within a broader suite (Strategic / Market / Competitive / Deals / Innovation Intelligence).
