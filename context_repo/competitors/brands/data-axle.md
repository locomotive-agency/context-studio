# Data Axle

## Sub-products

- [[data-axle--genie|Data Axle Salesgenie + Business Data]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales
- [[data-axle--marketing-services|Data Axle Demand Generation + Audience360]] — competes with ZoomInfo Marketing

## Summary

Data Axle is a combined B2B + B2C data and marketing-services company that sells to marketers, sales teams, developers, finance/risk teams, libraries, and nonprofits — anchoring its pitch on "Limitless Data. Measurable Marketing." and a 50-year, human-verified data process rather than on an AI GTM platform framing. Its core Business Data product (90M+ business profiles, 19.5M+ premium-verified, 183M+ multichannel contacts) plus the Salesgenie SMB tool, Audience360 identity layer, Developer Hub APIs, and Data Axle Apps for Salesforce/Microsoft Dynamics overlap directly with ZoomInfo's Data foundation, the legacy ZoomInfo Sales prospecting UI, ZoomInfo Operations data quality, and the APIs & MCP lane. Adjacent overlap exists in ABM/demand gen (Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Intent Data report cited Data Axle as "an intriguing option") but Data Axle sells heavily into spaces ZoomInfo does not: B2C consumer data (250M+ U.S. consumers; 165M households), nonprofit donor co-ops, libraries, business credit, email deliverability, and local listings management.

## Product Offerings

### Business Data

- **What it does:** Verified B2B database of business profiles plus contacts, intent data, and Canadian data, used for prospecting, enrichment, list-building, and audience targeting.
- **Key features:**
  - 90M+ business profiles with 400+ attributes
  - 19.5M+ premium verified business profiles
  - 183M+ multichannel business contacts
  - 300 data experts and ~25M verification calls/year over a 50+ year process
  - Sub-pages for Co-op, Intent Data, and Canadian Data
  - Forrester Wave Q1 2026 Leader for B2B Marketing and Sales Data Providers
  - Promotes the "industry's most complete coverage of small- and medium-sized businesses, freelancers, contractors, businesses operated out of homes and coworking spaces"
- **Source URL:** https://www.data-axle.com/data-solutions/business-data/

### Consumer Data

- **What it does:** Privacy-first B2C consumer database with demographic, psychographic, transactional, digital, and vehicle attributes used for marketing, segmentation, and audience activation across channels.
- **Key features:**
  - 250M+ U.S. consumers; 11M Canadian consumers; 300+ consumer attributes
  - 165M+ households; 110M+ homeowners; 250M email addresses
  - Sub-products: Digital Data, Transaction Data, Vehicle Data, Canadian Consumer Data
  - Sourced from 100+ public and proprietary partners
  - Persona examples include life-event, donor, transactional, and ProfileFuse blended profiles
  - Compliance focus: "Built with privacy at its core"
- **Source URL:** https://www.data-axle.com/data-solutions/consumer-data/

### ProfileFuse / Blended Personas

- **What it does:** Connects business and consumer identities into single profiles so a contact can be recognized as both a B2B decision-maker and an individual consumer.
- **Key features:**
  - Connects B2B contact records to B2C consumer profiles for unified persona view
  - Powers cross-context use cases (e.g., recognizing a webinar signup as a corporate decision-maker)
  - Sits in the "Data Offerings" navigation alongside Business Data and Consumer Data
  - Cited as a differentiator on multiple product pages
- **Source URL:** https://www.data-axle.com/data-solutions/business-data/

### Audience360 (360 Data Management)

- **What it does:** Identity-management and activation layer for 0–3rd party data; the platform-level product Data Axle pitches for enterprises managing complex data ecosystems.
- **Key features:**
  - Unifies 0–3rd party data for accuracy and consistency across platforms
  - Activation across "data without limits" channel matrix: APIs, integrations, installs, digital marketplaces, cloud platforms, MarTech & AdTech
  - Positioned "Best for enterprises managing complex data and ecosystems"
- **Source URL:** https://www.data-axle.com/platforms-products/

### APIs (Developer Hub)

- **What it does:** Eight real-time APIs developers and analysts can use to query, match, and update Data Axle's business and consumer datasets in their own stack.
- **Key features:**
  - 8 APIs: Search, Scan, Insights, Match, Submission, Changes, File Delivery
  - Coverage: 280M+ consumers, 19.5M+ businesses
  - Consumer API plans starting at $25/thousand records (Standard) and $50/thousand records (Enhanced)
  - Business API plans starting at $50/thousand records (Standard) and $75/thousand records (Enhanced)
- **Source URL:** https://www.data-axle.com/data-solutions/apis/

### Salesgenie

- **What it does:** SMB-focused prospecting platform with "unlimited access to millions of leads and built-in outreach tools" — Data Axle's main sales-team-facing product, also positioned as a ZoomInfo alternative for SMBs.
- **Key features:**
  - Unlimited access to millions of business and consumer leads via Data Axle data foundation
  - Built-in outreach tools alongside lead management
  - Subscriptions start at $99/month on a twelve-month basis
  - Salesforce CRM integration for two-way data sync
  - Positioned "Best for distributed enterprise sales teams and small to mid-sized businesses"
- **Source URL:** https://www.salesgenie.com/resources/zoominfo-alternative/

### Content Syndication / Demand Generation

- **What it does:** Managed B2B demand-gen service that promotes a customer's content through CPL and display campaigns and delivers verified, qualified leads with buying-group insights.
- **Key features:**
  - 100% verified leads — every lead personally verified by in-house call-center experts
  - Lead replacement guarantee for out-of-scope leads, valid in 50+ countries
  - Leads enriched with contact name, title, company, industry from Data Axle's business database
  - Buying-group insights delivered with each lead
  - Email sourcing 100% compliant with GDPR and CAN-SPAM
  - Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Intent Data Providers: "an intriguing option"
  - Full-service offering: data, modeling, creative, deployment, post-deployment metrics
- **Source URL:** https://www.data-axle.com/marketing-solutions/demand-generation/

### SignalFuse

- **What it does:** Newly launched real-time intelligence layer that connects first-party data with Data Axle's B2B and B2C profiles and exposes a plain-language AI Copilot.
- **Key features:**
  - "AI-powered, real-time intelligence" positioned as a competitive-speed advantage
  - Connects customer first-party data to Data Axle's B2B and B2C profiles
  - Plain-language Q&A with an integrated AI Copilot
- **Source URL:** https://www.data-axle.com/

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Data Axle's Business Data product (90M+ business profiles, 19.5M+ premium-verified, 183M+ multichannel contacts) competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Data foundation. Data Axle's totals are smaller than ZoomInfo's claimed figures, but it counter-positions on Forrester Wave Q1 2026 Leader status and SMB coverage (source: https://www.data-axle.com/data-solutions/business-data/). **Salesgenie** is the SMB-tier head-to-head against the legacy **ZoomInfo Sales** prospecting UI (source: https://www.salesgenie.com/resources/zoominfo-alternative/). **Data Axle Apps for Salesforce + Microsoft Dynamics** and **Audience360** are direct analogs to **ZoomInfo Operations** (source: https://www.data-axle.com/data-solutions/integrations/). The **Developer Hub APIs** overlap with **APIs & MCP** — though Data Axle has no MCP server / agent-builder framing on reviewed pages.
- **Adjacent overlap:** Data Axle's **Demand Generation / Content Syndication** offering creates "Account Based Marketing (ABM) lists that include active buying signals" — adjacent to **ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM)** (source: https://www.data-axle.com/marketing-solutions/demand-generation/). Both vendors compete in the Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Intent Data Providers report as adjacent **Intent Data** providers.
- **No overlap:** The entire **Consumer Data line** sits outside ZoomInfo's B2B-only scope (source: https://www.data-axle.com/data-solutions/consumer-data/). **Apogee + DonorBase** donor co-ops, **Reference Solutions** for libraries/academia, **Credit Solutions** business-credit reporting, **Inboxable** email deliverability, **Local Listings** management, and **Specialized Marketing Lists** are all product categories ZoomInfo does not sell. No reviewed Data Axle page references conversation intelligence equivalent to **Chorus**, an AI-agent / GTM-engineering / play-orchestration framing equivalent to **GTM Workspace or GTM Studio**, or an **APIs & MCP server / agent-builder lane**. SignalFuse AI Copilot is positioned as a Q&A interface over Data Axle's data, not a conversation-intelligence or GTM-workspace product.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Data Axle does not author a first-party `data-axle.com/vs/zoominfo` page; the closest is Salesgenie's (Data Axle subsidiary) ZoomInfo-alternative page: _"Despite its reputation as a leading provider of business intelligence solutions, ZoomInfo presents multiple challenges for small and medium-sized businesses. In addition to its high-cost entry point, ZoomInfo's platform and services are predominantly tailored to meet the needs of enterprises, resulting in limited scalability and a need for more alignment with SMBs' operational realities... Salesgenie's starting plans are significantly cheaper than ZoomInfo... Salesgenie's pricing structure is clear and straightforward, with no hidden fees or surprise charges."_ (source: https://www.salesgenie.com/resources/zoominfo-alternative/).
