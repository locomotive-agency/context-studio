# Datanyze

## Sub-products

- [[datanyze--chrome-extension|Datanyze Chrome Extension]] — competes with ZoomInfo Sales
- [[datanyze--directory|Datanyze Directory]] — competes with Data (Pillar 1)

## Summary

Datanyze is a budget-tier B2B contact data and technographics tool sold primarily as a Google Chrome extension and self-serve credit-based plan to individual SDRs, AEs, and SMB sales reps prospecting on LinkedIn. The platform overlaps directly with ZoomInfo Sales (LinkedIn contact reveal) and ZoomInfo's technographic data foundation, but does not extend into GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, GTM Context Graph, or APIs & MCP. Notably, Datanyze has been a ZoomInfo subsidiary since the September 2018 acquisition and continues to operate under ZoomInfo ownership — its privacy policy and "Do Not Sell My Info" flow both redirect to zoominfo.com — so this dossier reflects an in-house budget brand rather than a third-party rival.

## Product Offerings

### Datanyze Chrome Extension

> **Wiki:** [[datanyze--chrome-extension]]

- **What it does:** Browser extension that surfaces business contact information (email, direct dial, mobile) directly on LinkedIn profiles and company websites without leaving the browser.
- **Key features:**
  - Reveals email addresses, direct dials, and mobile numbers from LinkedIn profiles
  - Pulls contact data directly inside Google Chrome from any company website
  - Exports profiles directly from Chrome
  - Self-described "data guarantee" refunding inaccurate records
  - Credit-based reveal model (1 credit = 1 contact reveal)
  - Free 10-leads-on-us trial via Chrome Web Store install
- **Source URL:** https://www.datanyze.com/

### Datanyze Directory (technographics + company directory)

> **Wiki:** [[datanyze--directory]]

- **What it does:** Web-based directories of companies, people, and technology market-share data used to identify accounts by industry, country, and tech stack.
- **Key features:**
  - Industry directories spanning 23+ verticals (Software, Finance, Healthcare Services, Manufacturing, Government, Telecom, Insurance, etc.)
  - Per-industry company databases (e.g. Software directory contains 718,496 companies and 8,650,919 contacts)
  - Technographic "Market Share" reports across categories like Web Conferencing, Databases, E-Commerce Platforms, Payment Processing, CDN, Marketing Automation, Email Hosting
  - Tracks technology adds and drops as buying signals
  - Combines web crawling, NLP on unstructured data (job postings, press releases), and third-party providers to detect tech signatures
  - Credits can be spent on the directory pages or via the Chrome extension
- **Source URL:** https://www.datanyze.com/blog/technographic-data

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Browser-native prospecting flow — no separate web app required for contact reveal; works inline on LinkedIn and company websites (source: https://www.datanyze.com/).
- Lower entry price than ZoomInfo Sales (Pro 1 starts at $21/month annual / $29/month monthly) with public credit-based pricing — positioned as the budget alternative (source: https://www.datanyze.com/pricing).
- Self-described "data guarantee" refunding any inaccuracies in the contact database (source: https://www.datanyze.com/).
- Technographics-first messaging — Datanyze frames technographic data (tech adds/drops, NLP on job postings) as a buying-signal layer competitors can't match for SMB-priced tools (source: https://www.datanyze.com/blog/technographic-data).
- Credit-based reveal model with no minimum seats — sold one license at a time; team buyers are explicitly told to buy individual licenses (source: https://www.datanyze.com/pricing).
- Public market-share / technographics directory pages used for SEO-driven inbound, though now blocked behind a captcha for bot scraping (source: https://www.datanyze.com/market-share).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Datanyze's Chrome Extension competes head-to-head with the LinkedIn contact-reveal workflow inside the legacy **ZoomInfo Sales** module — same core capability (verified business email, direct dial, or mobile from a LinkedIn profile), priced at $21–55/mo single-seat instead of multi-seat enterprise (source: https://www.datanyze.com/). Datanyze's technographics directory + market-share content also overlaps with **ZoomInfo's Data foundation (Pillar 1)** (technographics across 30K+ technologies), using the same web-crawl-plus-NLP detection technique (source: https://www.datanyze.com/blog/technographic-data).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Datanyze positions technographics as an enrichment input for ABM, which touches **ZoomInfo Marketing**'s ABM space — but Datanyze stops at the data layer and does not orchestrate plays (source: https://www.datanyze.com/blog/technographic-data).
- **No overlap:** Datanyze sells nothing that competes with **GTM Workspace** (no AI-drafted outreach, no prioritized account workspace), **GTM Studio** (no natural-language audience builder, no play orchestration, no RevOps surface), the **GTM Context Graph** (no CRM/conversation/intent reasoning layer), **APIs & MCP** (no programmatic surface referenced anywhere on reviewed pages), or **Chorus** (no conversation intelligence) (source: https://www.datanyze.com/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ Datanyze does not publish a vs-ZoomInfo page (it is a ZoomInfo-owned subsidiary), and the G2 vs-page (https://www.g2.com/compare/datanyze-vs-zoominfo-sales) was 403-blocked during extraction. Third-party comparison pages consistently frame Datanyze as the budget Chrome extension and ZoomInfo as the enterprise platform.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Find and connect with cold sales prospects within minutes using affordable, accurate contact information from Datanyze" — pitched as affordable + accurate + Chrome-native (source: https://www.datanyze.com/).
- **Recurring proof points:** 63M direct dials; 84M email addresses; 120M people in database; Software industry directory of 718,496 companies / 8,650,919 contacts; "40+ million companies" web/mobile tech choices monitored (per Salesloft marketplace listing); GDPR & CCPA compliant; backed by ML scanning + human verification (sources: https://www.datanyze.com/, https://www.datanyze.com/industries/software, https://marketplace.salesloft.com/partners/datanyze).
- **Tone / category framing:** Datanyze frames itself as a "sales intelligence platform" wedged on technographics + LinkedIn-extension contact reveal — explicitly the budget / SMB lane while ZoomInfo is positioned (by third-party comparison pages) as the enterprise platform. Internal legal furniture redirects to zoominfo.com but the brand surface remains independent.
