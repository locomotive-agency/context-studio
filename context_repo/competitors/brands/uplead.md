## Summary

UpLead is a B2B contact data and lead-generation platform aimed at SMB and mid-market sales teams that want a verified prospect database without an enterprise contract. It directly overlaps with ZoomInfo's Data foundation and ZoomInfo Sales prospecting workflow — selling 180M+ verified contacts, 19M+ companies, real-time email verification, CRM enrichment, intent (Bombora), and an API at price points starting at $99/month. UpLead's vs-page pitches against ZoomInfo on three axes: lower price ($99/mo vs ~$14,995/yr), more concrete accuracy guarantee (95% with credit refunds), and easier onboarding for teams without RevOps support. It has no analog to ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, Chorus, MCP server, or website visitor tracking.

## Product Offerings

### Prospector

- **What it does:** Searches a 180M+ verified B2B contact database with 50+ filters to build laser-targeted prospect lists.
- **Key features:**
  - 180M+ contacts and 19M+ company profiles in 200+ countries
  - 50+ search filters (job title, industry, location, management level, revenue, technographics)
  - Real-time email verification at moment of download
  - Bulk download to CSV or push to CRM
  - Account-Based Marketing list-building workflow
  - Quick search shortcut for narrow ICP queries
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/features/

### Data Enrichment

- **What it does:** Uploads existing CRM/database files and appends 50+ contact and company data points with real-time verification.
- **Key features:**
  - 50+ enrichment fields (contact details, company size, technographics)
  - Bulk processing of uploaded lists
  - Real-time verification of supplied and returned data
  - Available on Plus tier and above
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/data-enrichment/

### Email Verification

- **What it does:** Verifies email addresses in real time at the moment of download or export, with credit refunds on bounces.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time AI-driven validation when leads are unlocked
  - Three-state verification: Valid / Invalid / Accept All
  - Invalid records routed to manual researcher team
  - Credit refund ("Friendly Credits") for bounced emails
  - 95% accuracy guarantee
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/our-data/

### Chrome Extension

- **What it does:** Captures verified contact data directly from LinkedIn profiles and company websites while browsing.
- **Key features:**
  - Pulls verified emails and phone numbers from LinkedIn
  - Surfaces emails and phone numbers from company websites
  - Shows technographic data on visited domains
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/features/

### Email Finder

- **What it does:** Looks up specific email addresses for named decision-makers using domain or company input.
- **Key features:**
  - Account-Based Marketing contact lookup
  - Real-time verification before display
  - Simple-search interface
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/features/

### API

- **What it does:** Real-time B2B data API for enriching CRMs, automating prospecting, and embedding contact data into custom apps.
- **Key features:**
  - Company API (lookup by domain or name)
  - Person API (lookup by email or domain)
  - Combined Person + Company API
  - Prospector API (filter by title, function, level, location)
  - Company Logo API and Company-Name-to-Domain API
  - Full API access on Professional plan
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/features/

### Technographics

- **What it does:** Filters companies by the technologies they use across 16K–27K tracked tech data points.
- **Key features:**
  - 16,000+ technologies indexed (homepage); 27K+ technologies cited on Our Data page
  - Used as a search filter and an enrichment field
  - Available on Plus and above
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/our-data/

### Buyer Intent Data

- **What it does:** Surfaces companies actively researching topics in your category via a Bombora-powered intent feed, layered onto Prospector filters.
- **Key features:**
  - Bombora-sourced intent topics
  - Available on Plus and Professional plans
  - Layered onto 50+ Prospector filters to prioritize in-market accounts
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/uplead-vs-zoominfo/

### Done-for-You Cold Email (managed service)

- **What it does:** Managed outbound service that runs cold-email campaigns powered by UpLead's verified data for B2B teams with $5k+/mo budget.
- **Key features:**
  - Best fit for B2B companies with a proven offer
  - Aimed at teams ready to take booked meetings
  - Minimum $5,000/month outbound budget
- **Source URL:** https://www.uplead.com/

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- 95% data accuracy guarantee backed by credit refunds on bounced emails ("Friendly Credits") (source: https://www.uplead.com/our-data/).
- Real-time email verification performed at the moment of download/export — UpLead's CTO invented email-verification-as-a-service (source: https://www.uplead.com/our-data/).
- Transparent, publicly published pricing starting at $99/month with no annual lock-in on Essentials and Plus (source: https://www.uplead.com/pricing/).
- 8-step data collection and verification pipeline; database refreshed every 90 days (source: https://www.uplead.com/our-data/).
- Self-positions as #1 Highest Rated Lead Intelligence Software on G2 (4.7/5 across 824 reviews) (source: https://www.uplead.com/).
- 7-day free trial includes the full feature set, including competitor intelligence usually limited to top tier (source: https://www.uplead.com/pricing/).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation** ↔ **UpLead Prospector + Our Data** — UpLead's 180M+ contacts / 19M+ companies / 27K+ technologies database is sold as a direct substitute for ZoomInfo's contact database for SMB / mid-market buyers (source: https://www.uplead.com/our-data/).
  - **ZoomInfo Sales (legacy prospecting UI)** ↔ **UpLead Prospector** — same build-targeted-list workflow with 50+ filters, real-time verification, and CRM push (source: https://www.uplead.com/features/).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM enrichment)** ↔ **UpLead Data Enrichment** — uploads CRM files and appends 50+ verified fields (source: https://www.uplead.com/data-enrichment/).
  - **ZoomInfo Enterprise Data API** ↔ **UpLead API** — Company / Person / Combined / Prospector endpoints for embedding B2B data into custom apps (source: https://www.uplead.com/features/).
  - **ZoomInfo intent** ↔ **UpLead Buyer Intent (Bombora)** — UpLead resells Bombora intent topics layered on Prospector filters; UpLead's own vs-page concedes ZoomInfo's intent layer is "more deeply integrated" for ABM (source: https://www.uplead.com/uplead-vs-zoominfo/).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **GTM Studio / ZoomInfo Marketing (ABM orchestration)** — UpLead supports list-building and Bombora intent but does not offer audience orchestration, journeys, or multi-channel campaign execution comparable to GTM Studio (source: https://www.uplead.com/features/).
  - **Done-for-You Cold Email** — UpLead promotes a managed outbound service for $5k+/mo budgets; adjacent to ZoomInfo's data-only positioning rather than a direct product match (source: https://www.uplead.com/).
- **No overlap:**
  - **GTM Context Graph** — no first-party CRM + conversation + behavioral data fusion, no "why deals move" reasoning layer (source: https://www.uplead.com/our-data/).
  - **GTM Workspace** — no priority-account workspace, AI-drafted outreach surface, or unified seller execution UI (source: https://www.uplead.com/features/).
  - **GTM Studio** — no natural-language audience builder, play orchestration, or marketer/RevOps execution surface (source: https://www.uplead.com/features/).
  - **Chorus / conversation intelligence** — no call recording, transcription, or post-call analytics (source: https://www.uplead.com/features/).
  - **MCP server / agent ecosystem** — no MCP server or agent-builder ecosystem; only a marketing "Ask AI for a summary" prompt-copy widget (source: https://www.uplead.com/features/).
  - **Website visitor tracking (de-anonymized)** — UpLead's own vs-page lists this as a ZoomInfo feature it does not match (source: https://www.uplead.com/uplead-vs-zoominfo/).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** "UpLead is significantly cheaper than ZoomInfo. UpLead's Essentials plan starts at $99 per month with no annual contract required. ZoomInfo's entry-level Professional plan starts at around $14,995 per year as an annual commitment. ... UpLead has better data accuracy for most use cases. UpLead guarantees 95% accuracy with real-time email verification at the moment of download and issues credit refunds for any contacts that bounce. ZoomInfo claims accuracy ranging from 75% to 99% depending on the data segment, with weaker accuracy outside North America." (source: https://www.uplead.com/uplead-vs-zoominfo/).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** Real-time verified B2B emails, mobile numbers and intent data — 95% accuracy, transparent pricing, no annual lock-in.
- **Recurring proof points:** 180M+ contacts / 19M+ companies / 27K+ technologies; 95% accuracy guarantee with credit refunds; 4,000+ customers and 4.7/5 across 824 G2 reviews; ROI customer quotes ($400k pipeline / 1,800% ROI at Kyber Security; 12,526% ROI at Whitewater Consulting; 10,469% ROI at Fastsigns; $1M+ in 6 months at Wilmot Modular Structures); CTO invented email-verification-as-a-service; database refreshed every 90 days.
- **Tone / category framing:** Frames the category as B2B lead generation / contact data and a "clean prospecting list" promise. Anti-positions against scraped, low-accuracy databases on one side and against expensive enterprise-only platforms (ZoomInfo by name) on the other.
