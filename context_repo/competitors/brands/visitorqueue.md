## Summary

VisitorQueue is a B2B website visitor identification tool that was acquired by team.blue brand Leadinfo on January 19-20, 2026; visitorqueue.com now redirects to leadinfo.com, so this dossier profiles the combined Leadinfo/VisitorQueue offering as the surviving brand. The platform sells to B2B sales and marketing teams (SMB through mid-market, plus agency partners) and competes directly with ZoomInfo Marketing/WebSights for IP-based visitor identification, and adjacently with ZoomInfo Sales for decision-maker contact data on identified accounts. 6sense's category data shows ZoomInfo at 86.29% market share vs Visitor Queue at 9.54% in the Lead Lists category (source: https://6sense.com/tech/lead-lists/zoominfo-vs-visitorqueue).

## Product Offerings

### Identify (website visitor identification)

> **Wiki:** [[visitorqueue--identify]]

- **What it does:** Reveals the company behind anonymous website visits using IP-based identification, returning company name, viewed pages, and visit details in real time.
- **Key features:**
  - Real-time visitor identification with company details, viewed pages, and behavior
  - Database of 220M+ companies across 195 countries with 45 datapoints per company
  - Automatic bot detection and AI-based traffic filtering
  - Audience Match — machine-learning ICP recognition based on identified visitors
  - Automated lead scoring based on returning sessions and pages viewed
  - Custom segments for visitors who hit pricing or quote pages
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadinfo.com/en/

### Contacts (decision-maker contact data)

> **Wiki:** [[visitorqueue--contacts]]

- **What it does:** Provides decision-maker contact details — LinkedIn profiles, business phone numbers, email addresses, and (on Pro tier) personal mobile numbers — at identified companies.
- **Key features:**
  - Database of 300M+ decision-maker profiles
  - Verified email addresses and (mobile) phone numbers
  - Filter contacts by seniority, role, or location
  - Persona functionality to filter leads by job title or department
  - Mobile phone numbers included in Pro tier only
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadinfo.com/en/solutions/features/b2b-lead-generation-tool

### Autopilot (outreach automation)

> **Wiki:** [[visitorqueue--autopilot]]

- **What it does:** Automates outreach to identified visitors via email and LinkedIn campaigns, with reporting on opens, clicks, and responses.
- **Key features:**
  - Automated LinkedIn connection requests
  - Email outreach automation
  - Add DMUs (decision-making unit members) matching personas
  - Conversion reporting and lead-response tracking
  - Add-on starting at €39/seat/mo
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadinfo.com/en/pricing/

### Leadbot

> **Wiki:** [[visitorqueue--leadbot]]

- **What it does:** On-site chat / appointment-booking widget for converting visitors with quote requests, WhatsApp contact, or direct booking.
- **Key features:**
  - Quote request capture within 10 seconds
  - WhatsApp contact integration
  - Appointment booking
  - Add-on starting at €39/mo for Leadbot Business
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadinfo.com/en/pricing/

### Screen recording & Liquid content

> **Wiki:** [[visitorqueue--screen-recording]]

- **What it does:** Adjacent on-site analytics: session-replay screen recordings (7-14 day retention) and dynamic content personalization based on visitor behavior.
- **Key features:**
  - Session replays of visitor mouse movement and behavior
  - Screen recordings included from Scale package (7-day retention) or Pro (14-day retention)
  - Liquid content add-on for dynamic content personalization (€39/mo)
- **Source URL:** https://www.leadinfo.com/en/pricing/

- **Company size called out:** Freelancers, small businesses, mid-size businesses, enterprises (per Capterra typical-users data)
- **Persona / role focus:** Sales teams, marketing teams, agencies (formal partner program with 2,000+ partners)
- **Use cases promoted:**
  - Identify anonymous B2B website visitors
  - Surface buying-intent signals from on-site behavior
  - Decision-maker outreach via email and LinkedIn

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- 70+ native integrations described as "no workarounds" / direct connections, plus Zapier and Make for thousands more (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/)
- GDPR-first / ISO 27001 certified positioning — explicitly emphasized as European privacy compliance differentiator (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/solutions/features/b2b-lead-generation-tool)
- Transparent, publicly listed tiered pricing in EUR/GBP/USD/DKK with self-serve calculator and 14-day free trial — no payment details required (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/pricing/)
- Database scale claim: 220M+ companies across 195 countries plus 300M+ decision-maker contacts (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/)
- Combined SaaS package: visitor identification + contact data + outreach automation + on-site chat in one platform versus point tools (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/solutions/teams/sales)
- Audience Match — machine learning that identifies ICP based on companies actually visiting the site (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/solutions/teams/sales)

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Leadinfo Identify competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo Marketing / WebSights for IP-based visitor identification (both convert anonymous IP traffic into company-level visitor identification with viewed-pages and behavior data; 6sense lists them in 'Lead Lists', 'Account Based Marketing', 'Lead Capture', 'Marketing Automation Platforms', and 'Sales Intelligence' — source: https://6sense.com/tech/lead-lists/zoominfo-vs-visitorqueue). Leadinfo Contacts (300M+ decision-maker DB with verified emails, business phones, and mobile numbers on Pro) competes with ZoomInfo Sales contact data (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/solutions/features/b2b-lead-generation-tool). Audience Match + automated lead scoring touch ZoomInfo Marketing's intent / behavioral signal layer (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/solutions/teams/sales).
- **Adjacent overlap:** Autopilot (email + LinkedIn outreach automation on identified visitors) is adjacent to ZoomInfo Marketing's orchestration but positioned as a follow-up add-on rather than a multi-channel orchestration suite (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/pricing/). Leadbot is an on-site chat / appointment booking widget — adjacent to ZoomInfo Chat, with WhatsApp and quote requests as the conversion focus (source: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/pricing/).
- **No overlap:** Session-replay screen recordings (7-14 day retention), Liquid content (dynamic on-site content personalization), and industry-vertical packaging for Construction, Machinery, Wholesale, and Staffing & Recruiting fall outside ZoomInfo's product surface (sources: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/pricing/, https://www.leadinfo.com/en/solutions/industry/machinery).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No competitor-authored vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced; Leadinfo's own /compare hub was not in the SERP set, and the wmtips.com VisitorQueue-vs-ZoomInfo-WebSights comparison was bot-blocked. The closest third-party vs-page was 6sense's category page, which states "ZoomInfo has a 86.29% market share in the Lead Lists category... Visitor Queue has a 9.54% market share in the same space" with ZoomInfo at 22,964 customers vs Visitor Queue at 2,540 customers (source: https://6sense.com/tech/lead-lists/zoominfo-vs-visitorqueue).

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Know which company visits your website. Start identifying your B2B website visitors and reach out to them within the same platform. Grow your sales funnel, and generate more and better leads than your competition." Sub-positioning is solving the "invisible majority" of website traffic — 98% of B2B visitors leave without converting (sources: https://www.leadinfo.com/en/, https://www.leadinfo.com/en/blog/team-blue-brand-leadinfo-acquires-visitor-queue/).
- **Recurring proof points:** 8,000+ businesses (homepage); 9,000+ European clients (acquisition press); 15,000+ users; 4.8 rating from 330+ Google reviews / 600+ reviews overall; €235,000 in additional revenue within 6 months (Reason / Mark Graatsma); G2 awards (Momentum Leader, High Performer, Regional Leader, Easiest Setup); named customers ABB, DHL, Brother, Eurofiber, Channable, Bomag, Sendcloud, Visma, Creditsafe.
- **Tone / category framing:** "B2B website visitor identification" / "website visitor intelligence" / "B2B lead generation tool"; Leadinfo frames the category around "turning anonymous traffic into actionable insights" and "a market moving towards fewer, stronger platforms" (consolidation / all-in-one positioning, similar in shape to ZoomInfo's all-in-one GTM platform claim but scoped to visitor identification).
