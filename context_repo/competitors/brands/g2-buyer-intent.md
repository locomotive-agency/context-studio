# G2 Buyer Intent

## Sub-products

- [[g2-buyer-intent--buyer-intent|G2 Buyer Intent]] — competes with Intent Data
- [[g2-buyer-intent--zoominfo-integration|G2 Buyer Intent + ZoomInfo Integration]] — competes with Intent Data

## Summary

G2 Buyer Intent is a first-party buyer-intent data product sold to B2B software vendors (CMO, CRO, Demand Gen, RevOps, Customer/Product Marketing) as an add-on to G2's Brand Professional and Enterprise packages. It captures verified, real-time signals — Profile, Pricing, Comparison, Alternatives, Category, Compare, Sponsored, Licensed, Reference, and Competitive — generated when buyers research a vendor or its competitors on G2.com, and routes them into Salesforce, HubSpot, ZoomInfo, 6sense, Demandbase, Marketo, and Salesloft (source: https://sell.g2.com/data; https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent). It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's Intent Data layer inside the GTM Context Graph and with ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM activation pipes. Unusually for a competitor dossier, G2 is also a publicly announced ZoomInfo partner — the two companies sell a bidirectional integration ("two intent ecosystems in a single platform") that puts G2 signals inside ZoomInfo Sales/Marketing and ZoomInfo Suggested Contacts inside my.G2 (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo).

## Product Offerings

### G2 Buyer Intent

> **Wiki:** [[g2-buyer-intent--buyer-intent]]

- **What it does:** Captures verified, first-party intent signals from over 100M software buyers researching products, categories, and competitors on G2.com, then routes those signals into the customer's sales / marketing / customer success stack (source: https://sell.g2.com/data).
- **Key features:**
  - Verified, real-time intent signals across nine signal types: Profile, Pricing, Alternatives, Category, Compare, Sponsored content, Licensed content, Reference page, Competitive (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent)
  - Buying Stage scoring (Awareness / Consideration / Decision) plus Activity Level scoring (Low / Medium / High), updated daily (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent)
  - ICP filters covering date range, firmographics, geography, target competitors, target categories, and signal types (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent)
  - Detailed company-activity timelines with unique-visitor counts, geolocation, and signal history per buyer (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent)
  - CSV and Detail CSV exports for downstream workflows (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent)
  - Add-on across G2 Brand Professional and Enterprise packages, sold per product profile (source: https://sell.g2.com/plans)
- **Source URL:** https://sell.g2.com/data

### G2 Buyer Intent + ZoomInfo Integration

> **Wiki:** [[g2-buyer-intent--zoominfo-integration]]

- **What it does:** Pipes G2 Buyer Intent signals into ZoomInfo Sales and ZoomInfo Marketing so customers can match high-intent G2 accounts to ZoomInfo contact data and ABM/marketing automation triggers (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo).
- **Key features:**
  - G2 Buyer Intent data updates daily inside ZoomInfo Sales and ZoomInfo Marketing (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo)
  - Layer ZoomInfo firmographic and technographic filters on top of G2 intent to refine ABM segments (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo)
  - ZoomInfo Suggested Contacts in my.G2 — surfaces decision-maker contacts on accounts triggering G2 intent; 50 free contacts per user per month for 6 months (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo)
  - Trigger ZoomInfo Marketing audience updates and ad campaigns when G2 intent fires (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo)
  - Requires G2 Buyer Intent license; the ZoomInfo Sales/Marketing leg requires Advanced+, Elite+, or any ZoomInfo Marketing package (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo)
- **Source URL:** https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Verified first-party intent — signals come directly from buyer behavior on G2.com (profile, pricing, comparison, alternatives, category pages), not inferred third-party clickstream (source: https://sell.g2.com/data).
- Mid- and bottom-funnel intent — G2 frames its data as decision-stage signals because buyers are already on a review/comparison destination (source: https://sell.g2.com/data).
- Compare signals — explicitly identifies which competitors a buyer compared you against on G2's compare pages (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent).
- Dual scoring model — Buying Stage (funnel position) plus Activity Level (engagement intensity), replacing the older single intent score in June 2022 (source: https://documentation.g2.com/docs/buyer-intent).
- Anchored to the G2 marketplace — over 100M software buyers researching on G2 each year, giving signals proximity to actual purchasing decisions (source: https://sell.g2.com/data).
- Tight Salesforce, HubSpot, ZoomInfo, 6sense, Demandbase, and Marketo activation paths — sold as add-on with pre-built ABM and CRM activation pipes (source: https://sell.g2.com/data).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:** Competes head-to-head with ZoomInfo's Intent Data layer inside the **GTM Context Graph** (ZoomInfo's Forrester-Wave-leading Intent Data Provider offering). G2 markets Buyer Intent as "verified, first-party intent data" showing "who's researching your product, category, or competitors in real time" — the same demand-signal layer ZoomInfo positions inside the Context Graph (source: https://sell.g2.com/data). G2 Buyer Intent's ABM Integrations add-on also overlaps with **ZoomInfo Marketing** ABM activation, since G2 ships pre-built activation paths into 6sense, Demandbase, HubSpot, Marketo, LinkedIn, and ZoomInfo itself to "trigger workflows, power lead scoring, and align sales and marketing" (source: https://sell.g2.com/data). The ZoomInfo integration page itself frames the relationship as "two intent ecosystems in a single platform" (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo).
- **Adjacent overlap:** G2's account-prioritization surface in my.G2 (with ZoomInfo Suggested Contacts plugged in) adjoins **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace** without replacing it — sellers see which accounts are researching them on G2 and can pull matching ZoomInfo contacts inline (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo).
- **No overlap:** G2 Buyer Intent does **not** provide B2B contact data (direct dials, verified emails, persona-level records) — it relies on partners like ZoomInfo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, and Bombora for contact resolution (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo). It also does **not** offer conversation intelligence, technographics database, CRM data quality / enrichment, an MCP / AI-agent ecosystem, or a GTM seller workspace UI — none surfaced on the reviewed pages. Sibling G2 products (Reviews, Profiles, Badges, AI Custom Research, Market Intelligence, G2 Ads) sit outside Buyer Intent itself in G2's plan structure (source: https://sell.g2.com/plans).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public comparison page surfaced in SERP results._ G2 publishes a `/zoominfo` integration page rather than a vs-page; the relationship is positioned as complementary partnership ("two intent ecosystems in a single platform"), not a head-to-head pitch (source: https://partnerhub.g2.com/integrations/zoominfo).
