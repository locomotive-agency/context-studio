## Summary

ZeroBounce is an email validation, deliverability, and email-finder platform aimed at email marketers, demand-gen, sales/SDR, and RevOps teams responsible for list hygiene and inbox placement. The product is anchored on a 99.6% accuracy guarantee for bulk and real-time email verification, with adjacent tools for inbox placement testing, server diagnostics, blacklist/DMARC monitoring, AI-driven engagement scoring (Activity Data), and an Email Finder. Most direct ZoomInfo overlap is with **NeverBounce** (a ZoomInfo company) inside the ZoomInfo Operations / data-quality slice — third-party comparisons explicitly bundle the two as alternatives — and a thinner overlap with the email-discovery surfaces inside ZoomInfo Sales / Workspace. ZeroBounce does not compete with ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph, intent data, conversation intelligence, phone/dial data, or GTM Workspace/Studio surfaces.

## Product Offerings

### Email Validation

> **Wiki:** [[zerobounce--email-validation]]

- **What it does:** Cleans and verifies bulk email lists or validates emails one-at-a-time at point of capture, with a 99.6% accuracy guarantee (source: https://www.zerobounce.net/services/email-validation).
- **Key features:**
  - Email Bounce Validator (keep bounce rate under 2%)
  - Abuse Email Detection
  - Spam Trap Detection
  - Disposable Email Check
  - Catch-All Email Check
  - Toxic Domain Verifier
  - MX Record Detection
  - Anti-Greylisting Technology
- **Source URL:** https://www.zerobounce.net/services/email-validation

### Email Validation API / Real-time validator

> **Wiki:** [[zerobounce--validation-api]]

- **What it does:** Real-time email verification API that blocks invalid, risky, or malformed emails at signup forms, lead pages, and any point-of-capture (source: https://www.zerobounce.net/).
- **Key features:**
  - 99.99% API reliability
  - 1.09s average API response time
  - SDKs in 16 languages including Rust, Ruby, Python, JavaScript
  - Auto-validation of new contacts when added to integrated platforms
  - 30+ email types identified
  - Multiple API keys per account
- **Source URL:** https://www.zerobounce.net/

### Email Finder & Domain Search

> **Wiki:** [[zerobounce--email-finder]]

- **What it does:** Finds business email addresses from a person's name and company domain via pattern detection plus 99.6% validation; also surfaces domain email patterns (source: https://www.zerobounce.net/email-validation-pricing).
- **Key features:**
  - Name + company-domain lookup
  - Domain pattern detection
  - Built-in validation of every found address
  - 20 credits per successful query
  - 10 free finder lookups on Freemium tier
- **Source URL:** https://www.zerobounce.net/email-validation-pricing

### AI Email Scoring (Activity Data)

> **Wiki:** [[zerobounce--activity-data]]

- **What it does:** Appends engagement signals to verified email addresses so senders can prioritize "Gold contacts" that recently opened, clicked, or sent (source: https://www.zerobounce.net/services/activity-data).
- **Key features:**
  - Last-known activity period (30/60/90/180/365/365+ days)
  - Activity type — opens, clicks, sends
  - Number of activities count
  - Activity location (inbox vs. web)
  - Available as bulk append or real-time API
  - Gold Recipients identification
- **Source URL:** https://www.zerobounce.net/services/activity-data

### ZeroBounce ONE™ (deliverability suite)

> **Wiki:** [[zerobounce--zerobounce-one]]

- **What it does:** Bundled subscription that combines validation credits with the rest of the deliverability toolkit — Inbox Placement, Email Server Test, Warmup, Blacklist Monitor, DMARC Monitor — at one price (source: https://www.zerobounce.net/zerobounce-one).
- **Key features:**
  - 10,000 validation/scoring credits per month
  - 10,000 Email Finder / Domain searches per month
  - 100 Inbox Placement Tests per month (against 20+ providers)
  - 100 Email Server Tests (200+ checks each)
  - 250 Warmup Seeds per month
  - 10 Blacklist Monitors (scanned every 8 hrs)
  - 1 DMARC Monitor domain
  - Credits never expire; rollover supported
- **Source URL:** https://www.zerobounce.net/zerobounce-one

### Free Email Verifier

- **What it does:** Single-address and small-bulk email verifier on the public site; lead-gen surface for the paid platform (source: https://www.zerobounce.net/free-email-verifier).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **NeverBounce (legacy email validation module of ZoomInfo Operations):** ZeroBounce Email Validation + the real-time API are 1:1 functional substitutes for NeverBounce. Third-party SERP and comparison pages explicitly bundle the two ("NeverBounce a ZoomInfo company vs ZeroBounce" on TrustRadius; the r/SalesOperations thread recommending ZeroBounce because "NeverBounce is expensive, owned by ZoomInfo, and favors Mid-Market"). Source: https://prospeo.io/s/zerobounce-vs-zoominfo.
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality / email-deliverability slice):** ZeroBounce's official Salesforce app validates leads/contacts on record update, supports batch + scheduled validation, and writes results back into the CRM — overlapping the data-hygiene corner of ZoomInfo Operations / RingLead. Source: https://www.zerobounce.net/integrations/salesforce.
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (Pillar 1) (200M+ verified business emails) + Email Finder workflows in ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace** are touched by ZeroBounce's Email Finder + Domain Search, which uses "company domain pattern detection and 99.6% accurate email validation" to surface a person's business email — but does not include a contact database, intent signals, or technographics.
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing engagement signals (intent + engagement)** are adjacent to ZeroBounce Activity Data / AI Scoring, which appends recency-of-engagement metadata (open/click/send activity in last 30-365+ days) to verified emails. It is engagement-only — not topic-level intent, not technographics — but plays in the "who is most active" lane that ZoomInfo Marketing serves with intent data. Source: https://www.zerobounce.net/services/activity-data.
- **No overlap:**
  - GTM Context Graph, Chorus (Conversation Intelligence), GTM Workspace, GTM Studio, APIs & MCP server, technographics across 30K+ technologies, Intent Data signals, and ABM/orchestration are all absent from ZeroBounce. Their product line is validation, finder, scoring, and deliverability monitoring — no contact database, no intent, no conversation intelligence, no orchestration, no AI agent surface, and no MCP server visible on reviewed pages. Source: https://www.zerobounce.net/zerobounce-one.
  - Phone / dial coverage (ZoomInfo's 135M+ verified phones, 120M direct dials) is not part of ZeroBounce's offering — the entire product line is email-channel-only.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** No ZeroBounce-authored vs-ZoomInfo or vs-NeverBounce page surfaced in SERP results. The most-cited third-party comparison (Prospeo) frames the comparison as: _"ZeroBounce is an email verification tool — you hand it a list of email addresses, and it tells you which ones are real. ZoomInfo is a sales intelligence platform that builds the list in the first place... Comparing them head-to-head is like asking whether a hammer is better than a house. The reason this comparison exists at all is that ZoomInfo's data isn't always clean enough to skip verification."_ (source: https://prospeo.io/s/zerobounce-vs-zoominfo). This third-party narrative — that ZoomInfo's "verified" label "doesn't mean what most people assume it means" and customers still need a separate verification tool — is the load-bearing rebuttal directed at ZoomInfo's data-accuracy pillar.
