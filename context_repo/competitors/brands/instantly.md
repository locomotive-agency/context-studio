## Summary

Instantly is an AI cold-email / sales engagement platform with an integrated B2B lead database, email-deliverability infrastructure, and a native sales CRM, sold to cold-email agencies, freelancers, founders, and outbound sales / SDR teams. It overlaps directly with ZoomInfo's data foundation (a 450M+ B2B lead database with waterfall enrichment), the seller-execution slot of GTM Workspace and legacy ZoomInfo Sales (Outreach + AI Sales Agent + AI Reply Agent + Copilot), and the WebSights visitor-identification capability inside ZoomInfo Marketing. Instantly publicly positions itself as a cheaper ZoomInfo alternative on its `/blog/zoominfo-alternatives` page — "You can get sales intelligence alternatives to ZoomInfo, like Instantly B2B finder, at a fraction of the cost." The product surface ZoomInfo does not match is email-deliverability infrastructure (unlimited warmup, SISR dedicated IP rotation, inbox placement testing, Done-For-You / Pre-Warmed inboxes) and a productised agency / freelancer self-service motion with a VIP Managed Services tier ($2K–$10K/mo).

## Product Offerings

### Outreach (Cold Email Outreach)

> **Wiki:** [[instantly--outreach]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered automated cold email sequencing with unlimited sending accounts, smart rotation, and A/Z testing.
- **Key features:**
  - Unlimited email accounts and unlimited email warmup
  - AI-powered email creation: sequences, replies, follow-ups
  - Multichannel campaigns: email, LinkedIn, and webhook sequences
  - SuperSearch filters and lookalike domains for precision targeting
  - Smart analytics with pipeline / opportunities / revenue tracking
  - Unified inbox (Unibox) with AI reply labels
  - Reputation protection and bounce detection
  - API, webhooks, and integrations
- **Source URL:** https://instantly.ai/outreach

### B2B Lead Finder (Lead Database / SuperSearch)

> **Wiki:** [[instantly--b2b-lead-finder]]

- **What it does:** AI-powered B2B contact database with advanced filters, AI search, and waterfall enrichment for building targeted prospect lists.
- **Key features:**
  - 450M+ B2B leads (homepage / pricing) — note: lead-finder page says 160M+ verified contacts and CRM page says 185M
  - 13 filters including title, industry, company size, headcount, revenue, tech stack, keywords, lookalikes
  - Waterfall work-email enrichment with 5+ providers
  - Lookalikes search (enter a client domain to surface similar companies)
  - Keyword search across the database
  - Full profile enrichment (description, headcount, job postings, news, technology, funding)
  - Instantly Web Researcher Agent and AI Email Writer Agent
  - Export to major CRMs and outreach providers
- **Source URL:** https://instantly.ai/b2b-lead-finder

### Sales CRM

> **Wiki:** [[instantly--crm]]

- **What it does:** Native CRM that combines email, calling, SMS, tasks, and LinkedIn connections in one master inbox to manage deals from cold outreach through close.
- **Key features:**
  - Master inbox unifying email, calls, SMS, and tasks
  - AI personalisation analyzing leads to craft personalised messaging
  - Automated salesflows / campaigns
  - In-browser calling and SMS
  - Native connection to Instantly Sending and B2B Lead Finder
  - Unlimited seats on a single subscription
  - Lead-status tracking with custom flows
- **Source URL:** https://instantly.ai/crm

### AI Sales Agent

> **Wiki:** [[instantly--ai-sales-agent]]

- **What it does:** Autonomous AI agent that finds ICP leads, writes personalised messages, follows up, and books meetings after a 30-second URL-based setup.
- **Key features:**
  - 30-second setup by dropping in a URL — no forms or onboarding calls
  - AI reads website to understand product, ICP, and differentiators
  - Autonomous lead finding matched to ICP, role, and intent signals
  - Lead enrichment with verified contact data, firmographics, buying signals
  - Per-prospect personalised outreach (not templates)
  - Automated follow-up sequencing
  - Calendar booking integration
- **Source URL:** https://instantly.ai/ai-sales-agent

### Deliverability & Inbox Placement (Email Warmup, SISR, Inbox Placement testing)

> **Wiki:** [[instantly--deliverability]]

- **What it does:** Deliverability infrastructure including unlimited email warmup, IP / server sharding, and pre-send inbox-placement testing to maximise inbox rates.
- **Key features:**
  - Unlimited email warmup across any provider
  - SISR (Server & IP Sharding and Rotation) — dedicated/private server and IP blocks managed for the user
  - Automatic swap-out of flagged IPs
  - Inbox Placement: deliverability tests across providers, spam-trigger detection, SPF/DKIM/DMARC checks, monitoring against 94 blacklists
  - Done-For-You domains and pre-warmed accounts (DFY: $15/yr/domain + $5/mo/account; Pre-Warmed: $15/yr/domain + $10/mo/account)
  - Reputation protection and global block list
- **Source URL:** https://help.instantly.ai/en/articles/10273259-instantly-plans-overview

### Website Visitors

> **Wiki:** [[instantly--website-visitors]]

- **What it does:** Identifies anonymous US-based website visitors and resolves them to business email or LinkedIn for direct import into campaigns.
- **Key features:**
  - Tracks US-based traffic — business emails, job titles, page-level visit history
  - 1 credit = 1 resolved visitor profile (email and/or LinkedIn)
  - Direct import into Instantly campaigns or lists
  - Hyper Visitors $97/mo and Lightspeed Visitors $397/mo tiers
- **Source URL:** https://help.instantly.ai/en/articles/10273259-instantly-plans-overview

## Differentiators (vs the broader category)

- Unlimited email accounts and unlimited email warmup on every paid Email Outreach plan — uncommon in the cold-email category where competitors typically charge per inbox (source: https://instantly.ai/pricing).
- SISR (Server & IP Sharding and Rotation) on the Light Speed plan automatically swaps flagged IPs and gives users dedicated IP pools without buying private servers or proxies (source: https://instantly.ai/pricing).
- AI Sales Agent with 30-second URL-based setup that autonomously analyses the website, finds ICP leads, writes personalised outreach, and books meetings (source: https://instantly.ai/ai-sales-agent).
- BYO LLM API key plus access to 5 major LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, others) inside Instantly Credits — uncommon in sales engagement tools (source: https://instantly.ai/pricing).
- Public positioning as a cheaper ZoomInfo alternative — "You can get sales intelligence alternatives to ZoomInfo, like Instantly B2B finder, at a fraction of the cost." (source: https://instantly.ai/blog/zoominfo-alternatives/?lng=en).

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Data foundation (Pillar 1) ↔ Instantly B2B Lead Finder / SuperSearch.** Instantly markets a 450M+ B2B contact database with 13 filters, lookalike search, waterfall email enrichment, and full profile / job posting / news / technology / funding enrichment — the same data-foundation slot ZoomInfo fills with its 500M contacts / 100M companies (source: https://instantly.ai/b2b-lead-finder).
  - **ZoomInfo GTM Workspace (seller front-end) / legacy ZoomInfo Sales ↔ Instantly Outreach + AI Sales Agent + AI Reply Agent.** Instantly's outreach + AI agents stack covers the seller workflow ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace owns: prioritized prospects, AI-drafted outreach, sequencing, multichannel campaigns (email/LinkedIn/webhook), and unified reply handling — though tightly scoped to outbound rather than full-funnel (source: https://instantly.ai/outreach).
  - **Outreach / Salesloft category that ZoomInfo competes with via GTM Workspace ↔ Instantly Outreach.** Instantly is fundamentally a sales engagement / cold email platform — direct overlap with the seller-execution layer where GTM Workspace competes with Outreach and Salesloft (source: https://instantly.ai/outreach).
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing (WebSights) ↔ Instantly Website Visitors.** Instantly's Website Visitors product identifies anonymous US-based traffic and resolves it to business email / LinkedIn for direct campaign import — the same job ZoomInfo's WebSights performs (source: https://help.instantly.ai/en/articles/10273259-instantly-plans-overview).
  - **ZoomInfo Operations (CRM data quality + enrichment) ↔ Instantly waterfall enrichment + CRM sync.** Waterfall work-email enrichment with 5+ providers plus full profile / job posting / news / technology / funding enrichment competes with the data-quality use case ZoomInfo Operations sells (source: https://instantly.ai/pricing).
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo CRM-adjacent surfaces (the workspace half of GTM Workspace) ↔ Instantly Sales CRM.** Instantly's standalone CRM (master inbox for email/calls/SMS/tasks/LinkedIn, lead-status flows, in-browser calling) is a real CRM but is sold as a deal-management surface for cold-outreach pipelines — adjacent rather than a head-to-head Salesforce/HubSpot replacement (source: https://instantly.ai/crm).
  - **ZoomInfo APIs / MCP / GTM Studio agentic surfaces ↔ Instantly developer API + AI Sales Agent + AI Reply Agent + automations.** Instantly publishes a developer API and ships several autonomous agents (Sales Agent, Reply Agent, Web Researcher, Email Writer) plus visual automations — adjacent to ZoomInfo's APIs/MCP and GTM Studio play orchestration but limited to the cold-email surface and without a published MCP server (source: https://instantly.ai/marketplace).
- **No overlap:**
  - **Email deliverability infrastructure.** Unlimited email warmup, SISR (dedicated IP rotation), inbox placement testing across 94 blacklists, and Done-For-You / Pre-Warmed domains and inboxes — ZoomInfo does not sell deliverability infrastructure or email-account warm-up as a product (source: https://help.instantly.ai/en/articles/10273259-instantly-plans-overview).
  - **Agency / freelancer self-service motion + VIP Managed Services.** Instantly explicitly targets cold-email agencies, freelancers, and founders with public sub-$50/mo entry pricing and a VIP Managed Services tier ($2K–$10K/mo) with a structured 50/50 revenue split — a self-service and managed-service motion ZoomInfo does not run (source: https://instantly.ai/pricing).
  - **Done-For-You / Pre-Warmed email account marketplace.** Instantly resells warmed-up domains and email accounts ($15/yr per domain + $5–$10/mo per account) — outside ZoomInfo's product surface (source: https://help.instantly.ai/en/articles/10273259-instantly-plans-overview).
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** Instantly does not publish a dedicated `/instantly-vs-zoominfo` comparison page (a vs-Apollo page exists, but no vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced), but its blog explicitly frames Instantly as a cheaper ZoomInfo alternative: _"You can get sales intelligence alternatives to ZoomInfo, like Instantly B2B finder, at a fraction of the cost. ZoomInfo is always a good choice for teams that want the full package. But, if you don't have the budget, only need a list builder, or simply looking for alternatives... Instantly B2B Lead Finder: For finding pre-verified and enriched leads that fit your ICPs."_ (source: https://instantly.ai/blog/zoominfo-alternatives/?lng=en).
