## Summary

Clari is the leading **Revenue Orchestration Platform** — pitched as "Revenue Intelligence Meets Real-Time Action" with 75,000+ teams using Clari Forecast. The product surface spans seven named products: **Capture** (data quality), **Inspect** (opportunity management), **Groove** (sales engagement, acquired), **Align** (buyer collaboration), **Copilot** (conversation intelligence), **Forecast** (forecasting + pipeline), and **Guide** (AI Action Hub for revenue strategy). Clari recently announced a **Clari + Salesloft Forecasting Execution MCP Server** (referenced on the pricing page) — putting Clari in the same MCP-for-sales lane as ZoomInfo, Clay, and Lusha. The most direct competitive overlap with ZoomInfo is on the AI-revenue-platform / forecasting / CI surfaces. Clari does NOT compete on B2B contact data (Pillar 1). Clari Copilot competes directly with Chorus on conversation intelligence; Clari Forecast competes with Salesforce / Gong Forecast on revenue prediction. The "Revenue Orchestration" framing puts Clari in semi-direct rivalry with Salesloft (which uses identical category language) and Gong's "Revenue AI OS."

## Crossover With ZoomInfo

- **Direct overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo Chorus ↔ Clari Copilot.** Direct CI head-to-head. Both pitch real-time conversation analytics + coaching insights. (source: https://www.clari.com/products/copilot/)
  - **ZoomInfo GTM Workspace forecasting / pipeline ↔ Clari Forecast + Inspect.** Direct overlap on revenue forecasting + pipeline management. Clari has the strongest market position in this category. (source: https://www.clari.com/products/forecast/)
  - **ZoomInfo GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2 reasoning) ↔ Clari Revenue Orchestration Platform + Revenue AI Agents.** Both pitch a unified intelligence layer with AI agents. ZoomInfo's Context Graph fuses third-party + first-party data; Clari's platform fuses CRM + activity + conversation + buyer-collaboration data. (source: https://www.clari.com/products/revenue-ai-agents-for-enterprise/)
  - **ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace seller surface ↔ Groove by Clari.** Direct overlap on sales engagement.
  - **ZoomInfo APIs & MCP ↔ Clari + Salesloft MCP Server.** Both ship MCP integrations. Clari's joint Salesloft MCP is a stronger ecosystem play than ZoomInfo's solo MCP. (source: https://www.clari.com/press/clari-salesloft-forecasting-execution-mcp-server/)
- **Adjacent overlap:**
  - **ZoomInfo's "team of AI agents" framing ↔ Clari Revenue AI Agents.** Both vendors converged on AI-agent packaging.
- **No overlap:**
  - **B2B contact data (Pillar 1)** — Clari has none.
  - **ZoomInfo Marketing / WebSights / FormComplete / ABM platform** — Clari has no marketing-side product.
  - **Standalone enrichment / DaaS** — not Clari's positioning.
- **Their pitch against ZoomInfo (if found):** _No public Clari-authored vs-ZoomInfo page surfaced._ Clari competes most directly with Gong and Salesloft on Revenue Orchestration framing. The relationship with ZoomInfo is partial overlap (CI, forecasting, AI agents) but distinct categories overall.

## Messaging & Positioning Notes

- **Top-line value prop (their words):** "Revenue Intelligence Meets Real-Time Action" + "AI Revenue Forecasting Software" + "Forecasting intelligence for every revenue model."
- **Recurring proof points:** 75,000+ teams trust Clari Forecast; Fortinet customer story; LeadIQ + many other vendors as customers.
- **Tone / category framing:** Clari co-owns "Revenue Orchestration" alongside Salesloft — both vendors converged on the same category framing. Clari's strength is Forecast; Salesloft's is Cadence + engagement. The seven-product Clari platform is broader-scoped than Salesloft's Cadence + Conversations + Deals stack. Clari's pitch is "the whole revenue stack in one platform" vs ZoomInfo's "data foundation + AI + access lanes."
