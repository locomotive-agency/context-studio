# Competitive landscape: Intent Data

## Direct competitors (head-to-head)

- [[autobound--signal-intelligence|Autobound Signal Intelligence (Insights Engine + REST API)]] — strength: Real-time timestamped source-attributed signals (not aggregated probabilistic intent); gap: No phone number database — explicitly ceded to ZoomInfo.
- [[bombora--company-surge|Bombora Company Surge Intent]] — strength: Forrester-cited gold standard for account-level intent; gap: No contact database — must be paired with another data source for outreach.
- [[demandscience--intent-graph|DemandScience IONIC Identity Graph + Intent Data]] — strength: Winnable Account System layers technographic + IT-spend + competitive context on top of intent — identifies "switchability" not just topic interest; gap: Smaller absolute scale than ZoomInfo's 500M contacts.
- [[g2-buyer-intent--buyer-intent|G2 Buyer Intent]] — strength: Verified first-party intent from buyers actively on G2.com — not inferred third-party clickstream; gap: Bounded to G2's own marketplace — no cross-web bidstream coverage.
- [[intentdata-io--contact-level-intent|IntentData.io Contact-Level Intent Data]] — strength: Contact-level granularity (named person + title) vs account-only competitors; gap: No published dollar pricing — gated quote-based.
- [[lead-onion--account-intelligence|Lead Onion Account Intelligence]] — strength: 24 unified intent sources pitched as the largest buyer intent data footprint; gap: No Forrester Wave / Gartner MQ recognition for intent (vs ZoomInfo Forrester Wave Q1 2025 Leader).
- [[madison-logic--ml-insights|ML Insights]] — strength: Proprietary publisher network feeding intent — content-engagement signals rather than B2B web-research-only signals; gap: Does not sell raw contact data / direct dials / verified emails (Madison Logic uses firmographics for targeting only).
- [[trustradius--downstream-intent-data|TrustRadius Downstream Intent Data]] — strength: Second-party signals (not third-party) — sourced directly from on-platform buyer research; gap: Account-level only — no contact-level decision-maker data (relies on ZoomInfo for that match).
- [[breeze-intelligence--buyer-intent|Breeze Intelligence — Buyer Intent (Reveal)]] — strength: Native to HubSpot Smart CRM — buying signals route directly into HubSpot workflows and lists; gap: Gated behind paid HubSpot Hub subscription.
- [[builtwith--sales-intelligence|BuiltWith Sales Intelligence]] — strength: Tech-stack movement intent that "firmographic data misses"; gap: Intent signal limited to web-technology stack changes.
- [[dreamdata--signals|Dreamdata Signals]] — strength: Identifies up to 80% of anonymous website visitors via proprietary IP-to-company resolution even without cookies; gap: Surface-area depends on third-party contact data integrations for personal-level signals.
- [[foundry--intent|Foundry Intent]] — strength: Multi-source intent fusing first-party + public web + owned editorial + syndication; gap: No GTM Context Graph reasoning layer or GTM Workspace integration.
- [[foundry-abm--intent|Foundry Intent]] — strength: First-party intent sourced from Foundry's owned tech-publisher network (CIO; gap: Tech-media bias — best for B2B tech vendors; thinner outside that ICP.
- [[influ2--contact-level-intent|Influ2 Contact-Level Intent]] — strength: Contact-level (not account-level) intent attribution; gap: Source mix and topic taxonomy not disclosed on reviewed pages.
- [[salesintel--signal360|SalesIntel Signal360]] — strength: Fuses predictive (future-pipeline) and demand-capture (in-market) signals on one platform via Signal360; gap: Bombora is a third-party intent provider (not proprietary) — same source ZoomInfo and others can also use.
- [[dealfront--connect|Dealfront Connect / Buying Intent Signals]] — strength: Combines first-party website intent with 33 third-party trigger events in one product; gap: No proprietary first-party intent network at ZoomInfo's depth.
- [[hg-insights--intent-driven-leads|HG Intent-Driven Leads]] — strength: TrustRadius community (12M+ annual evaluators) provides verified 2nd-party intent vs. typical 3rd-party signal aggregators; gap: Coverage scoped to TrustRadius-active categories (B2B tech-heavy).
- [[regie--signals|Signals & Use-Case Plays]] — strength: 100+ signal sources packaged into 5+ ready-to-run plays (Named Account Signals; gap: No proprietary intent signal panel like ZoomInfo Streaming Intent / Bombora — relies on aggregating public web + CRM.

## Adjacent competitors (overlap on some features only)

- [[g2-buyer-intent--zoominfo-integration|G2 Buyer Intent + ZoomInfo Integration]] — strength: Bidirectional partnership rather than head-to-head competitor — "two intent ecosystems in a single platform"; gap: Requires both vendors' contracts to unlock full value.
- [[similarweb--web-intelligence|Similarweb Web Intelligence]] — strength: 100M+ website coverage with 10 years of historical data; gap: Web-traffic intent is brand/category-level.
- [[bombora--insights-suite|Bombora Insights Suite]] — strength: No-analyst dashboarding for non-technical marketers; gap: Visualization layer only — depends on Bombora data subscription.
- [[surfe--market-signals|Surfe Market Signals]] — strength: Community marketplace model for shared prospecting playbooks; gap: Not a native first-party intent provider — community-curated lists not real-time intent topics.

## Not actually competitors (reader confusion to address)

- Bombora / G2 Buyer Intent / TrustRadius — third-party intent data sources that feed platforms (including ZoomInfo). Not platform-level competitors.

## Spotlight selection guidance

For articles about B2B intent data or buying signal platforms: spotlight 6sense Predictive Analytics + Demandbase Account Intelligence as the two most-cited rivals. Lean into ZoomInfo's Forrester Wave Leader Q1 2025 status (highest scores across 8 criteria) + the role Intent Data plays as a feeder into GTM Context Graph reasoning — competitors typically isolate intent into a scoring layer where ZoomInfo fuses it into a unified reasoning layer.

For articles about predictive scoring or account intelligence: spotlight 6sense Predictive Analytics + Demandbase Account Intelligence. Lean into the cross-signal fusion (verified data + intent + Chorus + behavioral) that single-dimension predictive models cannot match.
