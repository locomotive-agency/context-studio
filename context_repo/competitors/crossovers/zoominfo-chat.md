# Competitive landscape: ZoomInfo Chat

## Direct competitors (head-to-head)

- [[qualified--piper-ai-sdr|Piper the AI SDR Agent]] — strength: Multi-modal text + voice + video on a single agent (PiperX avatar unique); gap: Inbound-only — Qualified for Outbound is a packaged solution not the headline.
- [[11x--julian|Julian — Inbound AI Phone Agent]] — strength: Voice AI as a first-class inbound channel (most ZI competitors are text-chat only); gap: Transcripts are operational — not deal-coaching analytics like Chorus.
- [[conversica--customer-success-agent|Conversica Customer Service & Retention Agent]] — strength: Customer-success-stage agents (renewal/upsell/billing) — uncommon scope for AI chat agents; gap: Conversica targets the conversational-engagement layer only — does not integrate with ZoomInfo Chat's data-powered visitor identification at scale.
- [[terminus-demandscience--chat-experiences|Terminus Chat Experiences]] — strength: ABM-aware routing — different chatbot experiences for target vs. non-target accounts; gap: Less depth than dedicated CI products — no call recording or deal intelligence.
- [[chili-piper--chat-ai|Chat AI]] — strength: Books meetings directly without a sales rep on-shift; gap: Standalone module priced at $20K/year + Platform Fee — pricier entry.
- [[marketo-engage--dynamic-chat|Adobe Dynamic Chat]] — strength: Embedded inside Marketo Engage (no separate chat tool needed for Marketo customers); gap: Engaged Conversations capped per tier (10K Standard.

## Adjacent competitors (overlap on some features only)

- [[visitorqueue--leadbot|Leadbot]] — strength: WhatsApp contact integration (rare in B2B chat); gap: No conversational AI / GPT-style answers in dossier.

## Not actually competitors (reader confusion to address)

- Customer service chat (Intercom, Zendesk Chat) — different category entirely (post-sale support, not inbound demand-gen).
- Internal chat (Slack, Teams) — not a B2B inbound tool.
