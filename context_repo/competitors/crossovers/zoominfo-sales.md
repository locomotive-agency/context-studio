# Competitive landscape: ZoomInfo Sales

## Direct competitors (head-to-head)

- [[adapt-io--lead-builder|Adapt Lead Builder (Adapt Data OS)]] — strength: Proprietary 3; gap: Only 250M+ contacts vs ZoomInfo's much larger contact universe.
- [[bookyourdata--paygo-database|BookYourData Pay-As-You-Go Database (Prospector + Verification + Enrichment)]] — strength: Pay-as-you-go credit model with never-expiring credits — explicit anti-subscription positioning; gap: Smaller absolute scale than ZoomInfo's 500M contacts.
- [[contactout--chrome-extension|ContactOut Chrome Extension]] — strength: Native overlay for LinkedIn Recruiter Lite/Pro and Sales Navigator (ZoomInfo extension is Sales-Nav focused); gap: No intent or behavioral signals attached to revealed contact.
- [[d-b-hoovers--platform|D&B Hoovers Platform]] — strength: Data heritage going back to 1841 with "90% of the Fortune 1000" as Data Cloud users; gap: Multiple Gartner reviewers flag data freshness/accuracy issues including "outdated.
- [[data-axle--genie|Data Axle Salesgenie + Business Data]] — strength: B2B + B2C combined dataset (only major competitor with both); gap: Smaller B2B contact scale than ZoomInfo (90M business profiles vs 100M companies).
- [[findthatlead--prospector|FindThatLead Prospector + Manager]] — strength: Free-plan entry (10 searches/mo no credit card) — well below enterprise data vendors; gap: 200M contacts vs ZoomInfo's 500M scale.
- [[getprospect--email-finder|GetProspect Email Finder]] — strength: Charges only for valid emails — invalid/not-found lookups don't burn credits; gap: Bounded to LinkedIn-led email-find use case; no intent or buyer-signal layer to prioritize who to find.
- [[globaldata--sales-intelligence|GlobalData Sales Intelligence]] — strength: Industry-vertical depth backed by 800+ dedicated analysts across 22 intelligence centers; gap: No proprietary contact-level depth (no published direct-dial / verified-email scale).
- [[kaspr--linkedin-chrome-extension|Kaspr LinkedIn Chrome Extension]] — strength: 120M+ European B2B contacts at request time with no separate data-passport fee; gap: Smaller US contact scale than ZoomInfo.
- [[lead411--data|Lead411 Data]] — strength: Triple-verification with 3-month re-verification cycles vs claimed 6-month-to-3-year competitor cycle; gap: No GTM Workspace / GTM Studio equivalent — single web app + Chrome extension only.
- [[leadiq--smart-prospecting|LeadIQ Smart Prospecting]] — strength: Streamlined seller workflow at LinkedIn; gap: Smaller dataset than ZoomInfo's 500M contacts.
- [[listkit--triple-verified-database|ListKit Triple-Verified Database]] — strength: Triple-Verification replaces external verifier tools (no separate email-verification spend); gap: Specific CRM integration partners (Salesforce/HubSpot/Dynamics) not enumerated on public pages.
- [[lusha--workspace|Lusha Workspace]] — strength: Public tiered pricing — lower friction than ZoomInfo's quote-based path; gap: Smaller verified contact database than ZoomInfo's 500M.
- [[nimbler--data-platform|Nimbler Data Platform]] — strength: Unlimited users on every account (anti-seat pricing for SMB/agency teams); gap: Pricing inconsistent across third-party catalogs ($100 vs $250 vs $249/mo) — official pricing page is a parking page.
- [[rocketreach--platform|RocketReach Platform]] — strength: Public self-serve pricing from $25/mo (vs ZoomInfo's gated enterprise contracts); gap: No GTM Context Graph reasoning over CRM/conversation/intent/behavioral data.
- [[salesintel--data|SalesIntel Data]] — strength: Largest human-verified mobile-number list in the industry (54M+ verified mobiles); gap: Pricing fully gated to contact form (no public dollar amount).
- [[similarweb--sales-intelligence|Similarweb Sales Intelligence]] — strength: Web-traffic and tech-stack signals as the prospecting trigger (vs. contact-database-only); gap: Contact volume (100M-470M) lower than ZoomInfo's 500M.
- [[uplead--prospector|UpLead Prospector]] — strength: 95% data accuracy guarantee backed by credit refunds on bounced emails ("Friendly Credits"); gap: No GTM Workspace / GTM Studio / Chorus / MCP server equivalents.
- [[amplemarket--data|Amplemarket Searcher / Data]] — strength: Weekly database refresh (70M+ updates/week) — Amplemarket claims this beats ZoomInfo's monthly cadence; gap: 200M+ contacts vs ZoomInfo's 500M — narrower scope.
- [[artisan-ai--data|Artisan B2B Data + Watchtower]] — strength: Watchtower website visitor de-anonymization included on every plan (Artisan claims ZoomInfo gates this behind add-ons); gap: Smaller scale than ZoomInfo's 500M global database (Artisan: 250M-300M depending on page).
- [[linkedin-sales-navigator--advanced-plus|Sales Navigator Advanced Plus]] — strength: CRM data validation directly against LinkedIn — uniquely positioned for CRM hygiene workflows; gap: Still no verified email/direct-dial delivery — only validates LinkedIn profile freshness.
- [[linkedin-sales-navigator--advanced|Sales Navigator Advanced]] — strength: TeamLink reveals warm intros across the LinkedIn graph — capability ZoomInfo Sales does not offer; gap: Still no verified email/direct-dial delivery.
- [[lusha--extension|Lusha Extension]] — strength: Free tier extension drives strong seller adoption; gap: ZoomInfo Sales Chrome extension has deeper enterprise integration + larger dataset behind it.
- [[uplead--data-enrichment|UpLead Data Enrichment]] — strength: Real-time verification of both supplied and returned data (not batch verification); gap: Bi-directional CRM sync gated to Professional tier (Custom pricing only).
- [[visitorqueue--contacts|Contacts (Decision-Maker Data)]] — strength: Bundled with website visitor ID — contacts shown on identified accounts; gap: Mobile numbers gated to Pro tier only.
- [[6sense--sales-intelligence|6sense Sales Intelligence]] — strength: Free tier (50 data credits/month) — lower evaluation friction than ZoomInfo; gap: Smaller verified contact database than ZoomInfo's 500M contacts / 200M+ verified emails.
- [[ampliz--salesbuddy|Ampliz SalesBuddy (Chrome extension)]] — strength: Public self-serve pricing ($0/$50mo/$480yr) — explicitly positioned vs ZoomInfo's gated rate-card; gap: 120M+ contacts vs ZoomInfo's 500M.
- [[aviso-ai--sales-engagement|Sales Engagement]] — strength: Virtual SDR Agent prospects autonomously across intent + CRM + external data; gap: No bundled third-party B2B contact data foundation.
- [[demandbase--sales-intelligence|Demandbase Sales Intelligence]] — strength: Sellers see ABM journey-stage and account intelligence alongside contact data; gap: Smaller verified contact database than ZoomInfo's 500M / 200M+ verified emails.
- [[hg-insights--sales-copilot|HG Sales Copilot]] — strength: Bundled Contact Intelligence with no separate data subscription; gap: Contact dataset positioned as enrichment — not a primary 500M-contact database.
- [[surfe--company-search|Surfe Company Search]] — strength: Daily-refreshed 350M company universe (high freshness cadence); gap: Smaller filter set than ZoomInfo Sales (no Scoops or org-chart depth).
- [[uplead--api|UpLead API]] — strength: Multiple endpoint types (Company / Person / Combined / Prospector / Logo / Name-to-Domain) for varied embedding use cases; gap: Full API access gated to Professional tier (Custom pricing only — "Let's talk").
- [[contify--account-sales-intelligence|Contify Account & Sales Intelligence]] — strength: Battlecards auto-refresh from external news + internal call transcripts (Gong/Fireflies); gap: No underlying contact data to act on triggers (no direct-dials/emails/firmographics).

## Adjacent competitors (overlap on some features only)

- [[activecampaign--sales-crm|Sales CRM]] — strength: Unifies sales + marketing data in one platform — no separate CRM purchase; gap: No third-party contact / company data — only first-party CRM contacts.
- [[findthatlead--email-finder|FindThatLead Email Finder + Verifier + Chrome Extension]] — strength: Three-score verification algorithm with 99% confidence on highest tier and 30-day re-verification cycle; gap: Smaller absolute scale (200M professional profiles vs ZoomInfo's 500M).
- [[kaspr--web-app|Kaspr Web App / Lead Management Dashboard]] — strength: Shared credits + workspace auto-detection for SMB teams onboarding fast; gap: Workspace tooling is much narrower than full ZoomInfo Sales seller UI.
- [[lead411--reach-engagement|Lead411 Reach Engagement]] — strength: 25+ CRM connector library — broader than typical SMB-focused tools; gap: No native sales engagement / sequencing product (relies on Outreach/SalesLoft/QuickMail.io integrations).
- [[listkit--dfy-services|ListKit DFY Services]] — strength: Same vendor for self-serve $83/mo data + $6K-$28K/mo managed outbound (no data-source switch when scaling); gap: Managed-service motion has no ZoomInfo counterpart (so positioning vs ZoomInfo is "do-it-for-you" wedge.
- [[hunter--discover|Hunter Discover]] — strength: Bundled with Email Finder + Email Verifier inside the same platform; gap: Smaller B2B database than ZoomInfo Sales.
- [[rocketreach--autopilot|RocketReach Autopilot]] — strength: Automated list refresh on configurable cadences keeps pipeline fresh without manual searches; gap: Active autopilot count is bounded per tier (no unlimited plan).
- [[trustradius--intent-driven-leads|TrustRadius Intent-Driven Leads]] — strength: Managed program delivering opted-in ICP-qualified leads (no self-serve config required); gap: Lead delivery program rather than seller-led prospecting workflow.
- [[zerobounce--email-finder|ZeroBounce Email Finder & Domain Search]] — strength: Every found email is auto-validated through the same 99.6% verification engine; gap: No underlying B2B contact database — only pattern + validation.
- [[clearout--prospecting|Prospecting (incl. LinkedIn Chrome Extension)]] — strength: Captures email + phone direct from LinkedIn Sales Navigator pages; gap: Self-described as secondary use case — "verification is the main event" (prospeo.io).
- [[contactout--email-campaigns|ContactOut Email Campaigns]] — strength: Bundled with ContactOut data — sellers don't toggle between data and engagement vendors; gap: No dialer or LinkedIn touch as part of sequence (vs Outreach/Salesloft multichannel).
- [[getprospect--cold-email|GetProspect Cold Email]] — strength: Cold-email sequencer bundled with the email finder + database + enrichment — no separate seat license; gap: No intent-data prioritization to choose who to email.

## Spotlight selection guidance

For articles about B2B sales intelligence platforms or contact data tools: spotlight Apollo B2B Data + LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Core or Advanced) + Cognism Pro (the three most-cited rivals across price tiers). Lean into ZoomInfo Sales' verified-data foundation + 500M contacts + 200M+ verified emails + 135M+ verified phone numbers + 300+ human researchers — the structural advantages competitors do not match at scale.

For articles about LinkedIn-integrated prospecting: spotlight LinkedIn Sales Navigator + LeadIQ + Lusha Extension. Frame ZoomInfo Sales as the verified-contact-data layer that complements LinkedIn (most ZoomInfo customers run both).

For articles about EU/GDPR sales intelligence: spotlight Cognism Pro + Cognism Diamond Verified. Lean into ZoomInfo's global compliance footprint (ISO 27001, ISO 27701, SOC 2 Type II, TRUSTe GDPR/CCPA) + international data scale (34M+ international companies, 200M+ international professionals, 45M+ international mobile numbers).
