# Competitive landscape: WebSights

## Direct competitors (head-to-head)

- [[dealfront--web-visitors|Dealfront Web Visitors (Leadfeeder)]] — strength: Free-forever Lite tier (€0/mo) for up to 100 companies/month — public low-friction entry; gap: No proprietary first-party intent at the depth of ZoomInfo's network.
- [[lead-forensics--visitor-id-engine|Lead Forensics Website Visitor Identification engine]] — strength: Proprietary IP-to-company database (vs vendors that resell third-party IP data); gap: Match rate degrades on residential/SMB/remote-worker traffic.
- [[leadfeeder--visitor-id|Leadfeeder (Visitor ID)]] — strength: Free tier — lower evaluation friction than ZoomInfo's quote-based commercial path; gap: Smaller IP-to-organization dataset than WebSights (210M IP-to-org pairings).
- [[rb2b--person-level-visitor-id|RB2B Person-Level Website Visitor ID]] — strength: Person-level (name + email + LinkedIn) resolution rather than company-level only; gap: US-only by design — IP ringfencing means no person-level coverage outside US.
- [[snitcher--platform|Snitcher Platform]] — strength: Self-positions as "The #1 Zoominfo Alternative" with 4.8/5 from 200+ G2 reviews; gap: Identification scoped to companies on the customer's site (no broader 500M-contact database).
- [[visitorqueue--identify|Identify (Website Visitor Identification)]] — strength: European-first GDPR/ISO 27001 visitor-ID positioning; gap: No third-party intent network beyond on-site signals.
- [[clearbit--reveal|Clearbit Reveal]] — strength: Strong API-first developer adoption pre-acquisition; gap: Smaller IP-to-organization dataset than WebSights' 210M IP-to-organization pairings.
- [[lead-forensics--automate|Lead Forensics Automate]] — strength: "Orchestrator" sequencing automates downstream actions on visitor identification; gap: Pricing fully gated — no public dollar amounts.
- [[lead-forensics--essential|Lead Forensics Essential]] — strength: SMB-friendly entry tier with named accounts identified live; gap: No direct CRM integrations — Zapier-only at Essential tier.
- [[rb2b--company-level-id|RB2B Company-Level ID]] — strength: Free tier with 150 monthly company resolutions and no credit card; gap: Company-level only — no person/contact resolution (RB2B routes person-level to its other module).
- [[lead-forensics--b2b-sales-solution|Lead Forensics B2B Sales solution]] — strength: Pipeline re-engagement framing — alerts when stalled deals revisit the website; gap: Use-case packaging only — no separate technology tier from Essential/Automate.
- [[lead-onion--website-identification|Lead Onion Website Identification]] — strength: Person-level web-visit ID (US contacts) on top of company-level reveal; gap: Person-level visitor ID is US-only (Delivr.ai-scoped).
- [[instantly--website-visitors|Instantly Website Visitors]] — strength: Direct import into Instantly outreach campaigns (no integration plumbing); gap: US-only visitor traffic — no international identification.

## Adjacent competitors (overlap on some features only)

- [[snitcher--spotter-api|Spotter API]] — strength: Native GA4 enrichment that injects company names directly into GA4 reports and audiences; gap: Scope ends at analytics enrichment (no CRM-write-back).
- [[snitcher--identity-layer|Identity Layer]] — strength: Retroactive session linking once a person identifies (form / login / email link); gap: Scope ends at identity resolution (no CRM-write-back layer).
- [[snitcher--radar|Radar]] — strength: White-label visitor-ID infrastructure for SaaS platforms; gap: Embedded infra — not a turnkey GTM product for end customers.

## Not actually competitors (reader confusion to address)

- IP geolocation tools (MaxMind, IP2Location) — IP→geo, not IP→organization.
- Web analytics tools (Google Analytics 4, Adobe Analytics) — visitor analytics, not B2B company de-anonymization.

## Spotlight selection guidance

For articles about website visitor identification or inbound de-anonymization: spotlight Leadfeeder + Clearbit Reveal as the two most-cited rivals. Lean into WebSights' 210M IP-to-organization pairings (the largest dataset in this category) + integration with broader ZoomInfo Marketing context (intent + verified contact data + FormComplete + ABM motion) — point-tool visitor-ID products cannot match this depth.

For articles about post-acquisition Clearbit + HubSpot relationships: frame Clearbit Reveal as HubSpot-bound; WebSights remains platform-agnostic.
