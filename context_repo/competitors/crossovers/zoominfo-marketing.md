# Competitive landscape: ZoomInfo Marketing

## Direct competitors (head-to-head)

- [[6sense--abm-platform|6sense ABM Platform]] — strength: Native ABM advertising as a first-class channel (display + retargeting orchestration); gap: No conversation intelligence equivalent to Chorus.
- [[activecampaign--marketing-automation|Marketing Automation]] — strength: Goal-driven autonomous marketing — describe outcomes and AI agents build/launch; gap: No third-party B2B intent / contact data layer feeding audiences.
- [[adroll-abm--abm-platform|AdRoll ABM (formerly RollWorks)]] — strength: Native ABM intelligence + DSP execution in one application — not "ad module on a data platform"; gap: 92M contacts vs ZoomInfo 260M+ — concedes contact-data depth.
- [[demandbase--one|Demandbase One]] — strength: "Demandbase One" platform consolidation pitch; gap: Pricing is fully quote-based — no public dollar amounts.
- [[dreamdata--audience-hub|Dreamdata Audience Hub]] — strength: LinkedIn company match rates above 90% vs industry 29-62%; gap: BYO third-party contact data — does not sell its own database.
- [[foundry--abm|Foundry ABM (Triblio)]] — strength: Closed-loop bundle — intent feeds ABM orchestration feeds Ads on owned editorial inventory in one stack; gap: No 500M-contact prospecting database underneath.
- [[foundry-abm--abm-platform|Foundry ABM Platform]] — strength: Triblio orchestration heritage with proprietary first-party publisher intent baked in; gap: No public pricing — every deal goes through sales.
- [[hubspot--marketing-hub|Marketing Hub]] — strength: Most-used SMB/mid-market marketing platform; gap: ABM motion less rigorous at enterprise scale than ZoomInfo Marketing's Gartner MQ Leader status.
- [[hubspot-marketing-hub--marketing-hub|HubSpot Marketing Hub]] — strength: AI-native marketing platform with 100+ embedded AI features; gap: No standalone B2B contact database (relies on Clearbit/Breeze Intelligence).
- [[influ2--contact-level-ads|Influ2 Contact-Level Ads]] — strength: Person-level (vs account-level) ad targeting and reporting as the core wedge; gap: Audience source is the customer's CRM — Influ2 sells no underlying B2B database.
- [[marketo-engage--core-platform|Marketo Engage Core Platform]] — strength: 200K records/hour Salesforce sync — industry-leading throughput; gap: No third-party B2B data foundation — relies on customer first-party data + ZoomInfo/Clearbit enrichment partnerships.
- [[metadata--campaigns|Metadata Campaigns (Marketing OS)]] — strength: Single tool to launch and manage all paid B2B ad channels; gap: No first-party B2B contact/intent dataset at ZoomInfo's scale.
- [[rollworks-adroll-abm--abm-platform|AdRoll ABM (formerly RollWorks)]] — strength: In-house DSP/programmatic ad execution under one roof — ZoomInfo has no DSP; gap: Contact-data scale at 92M trails ZoomInfo's 500M.
- [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--account-engagement|Marketing Cloud Account Engagement (formerly Pardot)]] — strength: Only marketing automation platform built on the world's #1 CRM; gap: No third-party B2B contact database — relies on partners (often ZoomInfo) for top-of-funnel data.
- [[terminus-demandscience--abm-platform|Terminus ABM Platform]] — strength: G2 Leader in ABM for 17 consecutive quarters; gap: No seller-side prospecting UI like ZoomInfo Sales/GTM Workspace.
- [[6sense--revenue-marketing|6sense Revenue Marketing Platform]] — strength: Predictive buying-stage prediction (Awareness → Consideration → Decision → Purchase); gap: Pricing is quote-based — no dollar amounts surfaced.
- [[data-axle--marketing-services|Data Axle Demand Generation + Audience360]] — strength: Managed-services execution layer (data + creative + deployment + post-deployment metrics) — not a self-serve platform; gap: No conversation intelligence equivalent to Chorus.
- [[demandbase--abm|Demandbase ABM]] — strength: Native ad-buying platform vs. ZoomInfo's ad partner ecosystem; gap: Less data-centric than ZoomInfo Marketing — leans on advertising orchestration first.
- [[demandscience--labs-services|DemandScience Labs (Managed Demand Generation)]] — strength: Managed-services execution layer — runs campaigns on customer's behalf with no platform learning curve; gap: No self-serve platform — every program is sold as a service engagement.
- [[g2-market-intelligence--competitive-pulse|G2 Competitive Pulse]] — strength: Churn Threats fires when existing customers research competitors on G2 — a churn-signal G2 owns natively; gap: Announced March 2026 — capability and customer adoption are still maturing.
- [[jasper--personalization|Jasper Personalization (incl. ABM)]] — strength: Account-level landing-page generation via Grid; gap: No proprietary B2B firmographic/intent data — depends on customer's Salesforce.
- [[marketo-engage--omnichannel-engagement|Marketo Engage Omnichannel Engagement]] — strength: Native Interactive Webinars (Adobe Connect) — webinar platform inside the MAP; gap: No proprietary contact database — audiences are customer-first-party + integrations.
- [[metadata--metamatch|Metadata Audiences (MetaMatch)]] — strength: Patented identity graph — Metadata claims it's the only platform bringing LinkedIn-grade B2B targeting to Meta and Google Ads; gap: Audience graph scoped to ad-channel targeting.
- [[mutiny--abm|Mutiny for ABM]] — strength: Replaces the design/PMM/web bottleneck for ABM landing pages; gap: No firmographic/intent data foundation — relies on customer's CRM and ZoomInfo WebSights as inputs.
- [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--marketing-cloud-next|Marketing Cloud Next (Growth & Advanced)]] — strength: Built natively on the Salesforce platform with shared metadata + governance; gap: No third-party B2B contact data foundation.
- [[terminus-demandscience--abx|ABX by DemandScience]] — strength: Concurrent ABM + content syndication unified — ZoomInfo does not run a syndication network; gap: Brand consolidation in progress — surfaces split between terminus.com and demandscience.com.
- [[terminus-demandscience--ad-experiences|Terminus Ad Experiences]] — strength: Pitched as "highest-rated B2B advertising tool" per G2; gap: Account-level only — no contact-level ad orchestration.
- [[bombora--digital-audiences|Bombora Premium B2B Digital Audiences]] — strength: Audiences claimed 5x larger than other B2B providers; gap: No native ad-buying surface — must activate via DSP/SSP partner.
- [[dreamdata--b2b-attribution|Dreamdata B2B Attribution]] — strength: Purpose-built for long multi-stakeholder B2B journeys (avg 10 stakeholders / 272 days); gap: Attribution layer only — no audience build/activation surface (sold separately as Audience Hub).
- [[dun-bradstreet--marketing-services|D&B Marketing Services]] — strength: Done-for-you managed-service marketing offering — D&B experts plan and execute campaigns end-to-end; gap: Services-led model.
- [[lead-forensics--b2b-marketing-solution|Lead Forensics B2B Marketing solution]] — strength: First-party intent framing positions higher-confidence than third-party intent (Bombora; gap: Marketing solution is use-case packaging on the same engine — no dedicated marketer studio surface.
- [[oracle-data-cloud--eloqua|Oracle Eloqua]] — strength: Mature enterprise B2B marketing automation with deep lead scoring + nurture; gap: Implementation-heavy compared to modern marketing automation entrants.
- [[dealfront--promote|Dealfront Promote / Campaigns (B2B Display Ads)]] — strength: Native programmatic display-ad platform that ZoomInfo does not operate; gap: Smaller media reach than dedicated ABM ad platforms (Demandbase.
- [[chili-piper--web-experiences|Web Experiences]] — strength: Identity-triggered (not behavior-triggered) experiences; gap: Requires Experiences tier ($42K/year.
- [[terminus-demandscience--web-experiences|Terminus Web Experiences]] — strength: Account-level web personalization tied to ABM target identification; gap: Less mature than dedicated personalization platforms (Mutiny.

## Adjacent competitors (overlap on some features only)

- [[braze--platform|Braze Platform]] — strength: Vertically-integrated B2C engagement platform (data + journey + AI); gap: B2C consumer marketing only — not B2B GTM.
- [[adroll-abm--cross-channel-advertising|Cross-Channel Advertising (DSP)]] — strength: Owned DSP infrastructure (BidIQ) — not acquired/split stack; gap: Outside ZoomInfo's owned product footprint (programmatic media buying).
- [[braze--data-platform|Braze Data Platform]] — strength: Real-time stream processing vs batch unification; gap: First-party data only — no third-party B2B contact / firmographic / intent layer.
- [[insightly--marketing|Insightly Marketing]] — strength: Native sync with Insightly CRM on a shared data layer; gap: No B2B intent data or third-party audience graph.
- [[keap--sales-marketing-automation|Keap Sales & Marketing Automation]] — strength: Library of Proven Automation Templates spanning marketing/sales/service/ops; gap: No ABM or intent-driven targeting at any tier.
- [[madison-logic--abm-content-syndication|ABM Content Syndication]] — strength: CPL pricing model on verified leads ($60–$150 per lead) — unique commercial shape; gap: Pure CPL service — no SaaS workflow / orchestration platform on its own.
- [[optimove--engage|Optimove Engage]] — strength: Embedded CDP inside the engagement platform — no external CDP required; gap: B2C consumer profiles only — no B2B account targeting or ABM display.
- [[rollworks-adroll-abm--adroll-dsp|AdRoll (Cross-Channel DSP)]] — strength: Owns the ad bidder/DSP — ZoomInfo does not operate one; gap: Sibling product sells heavily into B2C verticals (Retail/Hospitality/D2C/Nonprofit) outside ZoomInfo's GTM scope.
- [[foundry--ads|Foundry Ads]] — strength: Direct ad placements on owned editorial inventory (CIO/CSO/Computerworld); gap: Adjacent — ZoomInfo activates audiences into ad networks but does not own inventory; not direct ad-tech parity.
- [[hubspot-marketing-hub--marketing-analytics|HubSpot Marketing Analytics & Dashboards]] — strength: Multi-touch attribution baked into the marketing platform (no add-on); gap: Attribution scope tied to HubSpot-tracked touchpoints.
- [[keap--email-text-marketing|Keap Email & Text Marketing]] — strength: Bundled SMS + voice with email in one platform with a dedicated business phone line; gap: SMS limited to US-only; voice line limited to US/Canada.
- [[leadfeeder--dealfront|Dealfront]] — strength: EU GTM platform positioning; gap: Smaller global scale than ZoomInfo.
- [[leandata--buying-groups|LeanData Buying Groups]] — strength: Built natively inside Salesforce so buying groups inherit existing routing rules and audit; gap: No first-party intent data — relies on customer's existing intent/scoring inputs.
- [[madison-logic--abm-display-advertising|ABM Display Advertising]] — strength: Owns the DSP for display media activation — ZoomInfo does not sell display inventory; gap: Requires media spend on top of platform fees.
- [[mutiny--blueprints|Mutiny Blueprints]] — strength: Pre-built template library spanning marketing AND sales asset types; gap: Templates are asset-format only — no campaign orchestration or media buying.
- [[qualified--agentic-solutions|Agentic Solutions (Nurture, PLG, Outbound, ABM)]] — strength: Packaged plays for specific funnel stages and motions; gap: Outbound is a packaged solution.
- [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--engagement|Marketing Cloud Engagement (legacy MCE)]] — strength: Legacy ExactTarget-derived B2C scale engine; gap: B2C-leaning footprint outside ZoomInfo's B2B-only positioning.
- [[visitorqueue--autopilot|Autopilot (Outreach Automation)]] — strength: Bundled directly with visitor ID — outreach triggers off identified visits; gap: Lighter than dedicated multi-channel orchestration suites.
- [[activecampaign--email-marketing|Email Marketing]] — strength: Per-contact predictive send time (17% higher CTR claim); gap: Email/SMS/WhatsApp channels are first-party-list only — no B2B prospecting.
- [[bombora--b2beacon|Bombora B2beacon Campaign Measurement]] — strength: Only B2B audience provider claiming campaign-by-campaign measurement without an ABM-platform subscription; gap: Measurement only — no audience targeting / orchestration on its own.
- [[braze--cross-channel-messaging|Braze Cross-Channel Messaging]] — strength: Native regional consumer messengers (WhatsApp / LINE / KakaoTalk / RCS); gap: B2C consumer messaging only — no B2B-relevant outbound channels.
- [[hg-insights--market-analyzer|HG Market Analyzer]] — strength: Conversational TAM/SAM/SOM analytics — uncommon vs. flat data exports; gap: Strategy-side surface — does not run campaigns or sequences.
- [[madison-logic--abm-connected-tv|ABM Connected TV]] — strength: Connected-TV inventory is rare in ABM stacks — ZoomInfo does not sell CTV media; gap: Requires significant media spend to deliver enough impressions.
- [[metadata--insights-spotlight|Metadata Insights / Spotlight]] — strength: CRM as source of truth — bi-directional; gap: Reporting layer specific to Metadata's own campaigns — not a general analytics surface.
- [[oracle-data-cloud--responsys|Oracle Responsys]] — strength: B2C cross-channel campaign management at enterprise scale; gap: B2C-focused — limited applicability to B2B GTM scenarios.
- [[salesforce-marketing-cloud--personalization|Salesforce Personalization]] — strength: Real-time 1:1 cross-channel personalization with autonomous decisioning; gap: Tangential to ZoomInfo's B2B GTM core — personalization is a B2C-leaning workflow.
- [[zerobounce--activity-data|ZeroBounce AI Email Scoring (Activity Data)]] — strength: Adds engagement recency to verified emails to prioritize most-active contacts; gap: Engagement-only — no topic-level intent or technographic signals.
- [[braze--canvas-orchestration|Braze Canvas Orchestration]] — strength: Real-time-trigger journey orchestration vs batch journey tools; gap: B2C consumer journeys — not B2B account-based journeys.
- [[contify--marketing-intelligence|Contify Marketing Intelligence]] — strength: Public-web competitor monitoring at 1 Mn+ source breadth; gap: No intent data or first-party visitor signals.
- [[hg-insights--customer-voice|HG Customer Voice (powered by TrustRadius)]] — strength: TrustRadius-verified review sourcing with human moderation; gap: Advocacy/marketing-only category — does not enrich CRM or run campaigns.
- [[madison-logic--abm-linkedin-ads|ABM Social Advertising with LinkedIn Ads]] — strength: Native LinkedIn Ads activation tied to ML Insights intent signals; gap: LinkedIn-only — narrower than full social channel coverage.
- [[metadata--marketing-automation|Metadata Marketing Automation]] — strength: One-click deployment across LinkedIn / Meta / Reddit / Google / Bing; gap: Scoped to paid-media automation.
- [[metadata--revenue-optimization|Metadata Revenue Optimization]] — strength: Optimizes on pipeline + revenue not CPL/CPC; gap: Optimization confined to Metadata-managed paid spend.
- [[monday-crm--campaigns|monday campaigns]] — strength: Email campaigns built directly inside the CRM — no separate MAP needed; gap: Email-only — no paid media / ABM / cross-channel activation.
- [[pipedrive--campaigns|Campaigns by Pipedrive]] — strength: Lives inside the CRM with zero data movement; gap: No ABM or display advertising surface.
- [[qualified--for-demandbase|Piper for Demandbase]] — strength: Joint integration making Piper an AI SDR superagent for ABM motions; gap: Depends on Demandbase as the data layer (no native account / contact data).
- [[madison-logic--ml-measurement|ML Measurement]] — strength: Built specifically for ABM media attribution across display/CTV/audio/LinkedIn/syndication; gap: Attribution model is Madison-Logic-channel-centric — limited visibility into non-ML channels.

## Spotlight selection guidance

For articles in the ABM platforms category: spotlight Demandbase One + 6sense ABM Platform (the two head-to-head Gartner MQ rivals). Lean into ZoomInfo Marketing's Gartner MQ ABM Platforms Leader 2024 + 2025 recognition + the verified data foundation (Pillar 1) underneath the ABM motion — both differentiators that 6sense and Demandbase structurally lack at the same scale.

For articles about predictive marketing analytics: spotlight 6sense Predictive Analytics + Demandbase Account Intelligence. Lean into GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2) as the broader signal-fusion layer.

For articles about inbound marketing or form optimization: spotlight 6sense Inbound Marketing Automation + Clay Inbound Enrichment. Lean into FormComplete + WebSights as the natural ZoomInfo Marketing surfaces to anchor.
