# Competitive landscape: Data (Pillar 1)

## Direct competitors (head-to-head)

- [[builtwith--pro-lead-generation|BuiltWith Pro (Lead Generation)]] — strength: 41-year usage history of web-technology adoption (1985+); gap: Contact data limited to non-EU/CA records (GDPR/CCPA-restricted).
- [[clay--waterfall-enrichment|Waterfall Enrichment]] — strength: Architectural pitch: "why use one database when you can use them all?"; gap: Only as good as plugged-in providers — no inherent verification rigor.
- [[clearbit--breeze-intelligence|HubSpot Breeze Intelligence]] — strength: Bundled inside HubSpot — no separate vendor; gap: Smaller dataset than ZoomInfo Data — Clearbit historically had narrower coverage.
- [[clearout--email-verifier|Email Verifier]] — strength: 99% accuracy claim with AI Verdict; gap: Verification only — no prospecting database scale.
- [[cognism--diamond-verified|Diamond Verified Data]] — strength: Phone-verification rigor on EU mobile data — Cognism's clearest differentiator; gap: Narrow scope (EU mobile data) vs. ZoomInfo's global verified data: 135M+ verified phone numbers.
- [[cognism--pro|Cognism Pro]] — strength: Diamond Verified mobile data is Cognism's strongest differentiator; gap: Still smaller dataset scale than ZoomInfo.
- [[dun-bradstreet--data-cloud|D&B Data Cloud / Master Data]] — strength: Owner of the D-U-N-S Number — the universal business identifier required for U.S. federal contracting; gap: Firmographic + risk-signal weighted rather than CRM/intent/conversation reasoning layer.
- [[emailable--bulk-verification|Bulk Email List Verification]] — strength: Credits never expire on PAYG or subscription (NeverBounce expires at 12 months); gap: No contact discovery — verification only (vs page lists "Database size: N/A").
- [[hunter--email-finder|Hunter Email Finder]] — strength: Public-source attribution per email is rare in the B2B data category; gap: Email-only — no phone.
- [[leadsforge--ai-lead-search|Leadsforge AI Lead Search Engine]] — strength: Chat-style ICP entry replaces filter dashboards — "like ChatGPT for finding leads"; gap: Pre-launch / early-phase status flagged across multiple reviewed sources.
- [[linkedin-sales-navigator--core|Sales Navigator Core]] — strength: Sits on the LinkedIn graph — largest verified people-and-company network globally; gap: No verified email or direct-dial phone delivery — LinkedIn does not surface contact info.
- [[privco--database|PrivCo Database]] — strength: Coverage of bootstrapped / non-VC-backed U.S. private firms ZoomInfo lacks deep financials on; gap: U.S.-only — no international coverage.
- [[seamless-ai--data-engine|Seamless Data Engine (Prospector + Buyer Intent)]] — strength: Public free tier — lowest evaluation friction; gap: Smaller verified dataset than ZoomInfo's 500M contacts.
- [[skrapp--email-finder|Email Finder]] — strength: Free 50-credit/month tier with no card; gap: Email-only — no phone numbers or direct dials.
- [[snov--email-finder|Email Finder]] — strength: 500M+ contact scale at SMB pricing; gap: Email-anchored — no phone numbers / direct dials.
- [[surfe--email-finder|Surfe Email Finder / Waterfall Enrichment]] — strength: Multi-vendor waterfall in a single subscription (15+ providers); gap: Smaller core contact universe than ZoomInfo's 500M with first-party verification.
- [[voila-norbert--email-finder|Email Finder]] — strength: Up to 98% email-finder success rate (Ahrefs 2023 test #1 at 92%); gap: 100M prospect index vs ZoomInfo's 500M+ contacts.
- [[apollo--b2b-data|Apollo B2B Data]] — strength: Free tier with limited credits — lowest evaluation friction in the category; gap: Verification depth lags ZoomInfo's 95%+ first-party verified data and 300+ human researchers.
- [[artisan--b2b-data|B2B Data]] — strength: Database bundled with autonomous outbound — not a standalone purchase; gap: 250M canonical figure trails ZoomInfo's 500M scale (with 300M/350M cited inconsistently elsewhere).
- [[cognism--standard|Cognism Standard]] — strength: GDPR-compliant European data positioning; gap: Smaller dataset than ZoomInfo's 500M contacts.
- [[contactout--search-portal|ContactOut Search Portal]] — strength: Personal-email coverage layered on LinkedIn data; gap: Smaller core dataset than ZoomInfo (350M people / 40M companies vs ZoomInfo 500M / 100M).
- [[getprospect--b2b-database|GetProspect B2B Contact Database]] — strength: Volumetric scale similar to ZI's framing (200M contacts vs ZI's 500M; 130M mobile phones vs 135M; 230M corporate emails vs 200M+) at a fraction of the price; gap: Roughly 40% of ZI's contact scale; weaker enterprise vetting story.

- [[experian--us-business-database|U.S. Business Database / B2B Marketing Solutions]] — strength: Daily U.S. call-center verification of business records; gap: 16M U.S. businesses vs ZoomInfo's 100M+ company graph — narrower scale.

## Spotlight selection guidance

For articles about B2B contact data or data accuracy: spotlight Apollo B2B Data + Cognism Pro/Diamond Verified + LinkedIn Sales Navigator (the three most-cited rivals). Lean into ZoomInfo's structural advantages: 95%+ verified first-party data, 300+ human researchers, 1.5B+ data points processed daily, multi-source verification — none of which competitors match at scale.

For articles about data orchestration vs. single-vendor data: spotlight Clay Waterfall Enrichment as the architectural alternative. Lean into Pillar 2 (GTM Context Graph) — orchestration of providers gives you data; the reasoning layer above data is what ZoomInfo provides.

For articles about EU/GDPR data: spotlight Cognism Diamond Verified. Acknowledge the EU mobile data point honestly; lean into ZoomInfo's broader compliance footprint (ISO 27001, ISO 27701, SOC 2 Type II, TRUSTe GDPR/CCPA) and global scale (34M+ international company profiles, 200M+ international professional profiles, 45M+ international mobile numbers).
