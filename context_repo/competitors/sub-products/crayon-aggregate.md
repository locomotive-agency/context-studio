# Crayon — Crayon Aggregate

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on signal aggregation, scoped to competitor intel)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Captures competitive data from competitors' digital footprints (websites, pricing pages, news, support docs, customer reviews) plus first-party deal/seller signals from Gong/Chorus/Clari calls, Slack, and win/loss interviews — delivered as a daily morning insights email.

## Spotlight when
The article compares signal-aggregation layers across vendors, or argues for compete-shaped vs GTM-shaped intelligence. Aggregate's spotlight is before/after change detection on competitor websites/pricing.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time competitor website/pricing/news monitoring, Daily morning insights email, GTM Insights from calls + Slack, Win/loss ingestion
- Strengths: Before/after change detection; first-party + public-web signal fusion; PMM-shaped digest
- Gaps: No contact/firmographic data; no seller-action layer; compete-scoped vs full-funnel
- Proof quote: "Monitor competitor websites, pricing pages, news, support docs, and customer reviews for changes in real time" — https://www.crayon.co/product/aggregate
