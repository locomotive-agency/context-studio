# GTM Buddy — GTM Buddy Digital Sales Room

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — buyer-side intervention triggers loop back to seller workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Buyer-facing "activation surface" that hosts deal content for prospects and tracks per-stakeholder engagement signals (CFO ROI revisits, procurement page-one stalls, security due-diligence time, late-stage stakeholder entrants) to trigger seller interventions.

## Spotlight when
The article touches on buyer enablement or DSR strategy. The persona-specific tracks and decision-signal alerts are the spotlight differentiators.

## Cite
- Key features: Persona stakeholder tracks, DSR Block Library, Per-stakeholder signal tracking, Decision alerts, Engagement analytics
- Strengths: Per-stakeholder signal tracking; decision alerts; Bizzabo 300+ DSR proof point
- Gaps: Buyer-side scope; no firmographic/technographic layer for buyer accounts; no native e-sign
