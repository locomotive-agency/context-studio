# Lemlist — Lemlist Email Finder & Verifier

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on email-finding / verification — meets the same hygiene need but is a verifier-only layer rather than a primary data foundation)

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Finds and verifies prospect emails by cascading queries across 25+ third-party data providers, billed pay-as-you-go on credits, with double-verification before delivery.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email-finder / waterfall-verification stacks vs. ZoomInfo's data accuracy. Lemlist's strongest angle: ~80% find rate via 25-provider waterfall — a credible alternative for teams that don't want to commit to a full data subscription.

## Cite
- Key features: Waterfall across 25+ providers, pay-only-on-verified-hits credits, double-verification, $5/100 verified emails, 40 free/user/mo, no credit expiration
- Strengths: 25-provider cascade with claimed ~80% find rate; verified-hit-only billing; included free credits
- Gaps: Verifier-only (no first-party data foundation); economics flip at high volume; no direct-dial verification at ZI scale
- Proof quote: "~80% verified-email find rate via waterfall enrichment vs 30-60% single-provider claim" — https://www.lemlist.com/product/email-finder-and-verifier
