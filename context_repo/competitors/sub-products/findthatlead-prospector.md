# FindThatLead — FindThatLead Prospector + Manager

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the seller-facing prospecting + list-building workflow at the SMB end)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Automated B2B prospecting tool — define industry, role, location, or keywords and FindThatLead returns a tailored list of prospects with phone numbers and email addresses, organized inside the built-in Manager CRM.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Sales' classic prospecting UI at the SMB end, especially when discussing free-tier alternatives or all-in-one find-verify-send-manage suites.

## Cite
- Key features: Prospector — define industry/role/location/keywords for tailored prospect lists, 200M professional profiles + 9M company profiles, Real-time email generation, Outputs phone numbers and email addresses, Salesforce/HubSpot/Pipedrive via Zapier, Built-in Manager CRM
- Strengths: Free-plan entry; bundles list-building with Manager CRM + Email Sender drip; $55/mo "most popular" plan
- Gaps: 200M vs ZoomInfo's 500M; no Intent Data/GTM Context Graph; no conversation intelligence; no APIs & MCP; JS-rendered pricing
