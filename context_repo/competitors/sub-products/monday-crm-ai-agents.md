# Monday CRM — AI Agents (AI Lead Agent + AI Sales Agent)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller AI-agent layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Native AI agents that source/qualify leads against an ICP and run initial outreach (phone calls, SMS) — pitched as a built-in alternative to a separate prospecting tool, with credit-based pricing decoupled from seat pricing.

## Spotlight when
The article compares native AI sales agents inside CRMs vs. ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace agent layer. AI Lead Agent + AI Sales Agent are monday's most ZI-overlapping features and the natural call-out when buyers want consolidation.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Lead Agent, AI Sales Agent (phone/SMS), Multiple agents with goals, Credit-based pricing, AI Sidekick
- Strengths: Native CRM agents — no separate tool; credit-based pricing; autonomous outreach
- Gaps: Pulls from third-party data; less mature than ZI's GTM Workspace agents; credit overage risk
