# Keap — Keap Reporting

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the manager-level reporting surface; SMB-scoped, no Context Graph)
**Spotlight rank:** 6

## What it does
SMB reporting on sales pipeline, payments, receivables, and automation performance — with stage-by-stage contact flow analytics and Graphly for advanced visualization.

## Spotlight when
The article compares SMB reporting stacks tied to a CRM — especially when payment/receivables forecasting alongside sales reporting is the differentiator.

## Cite
- Key features: Period-over-period sales reports, payment/receivable/failed-invoice forecasts, automation stage-by-stage flow, Reporting hub (Max/Ultimate), Graphly integration
- Strengths: Payments + receivables forecasting; contact flow analytics; pinnable Reporting hub on top tier
- Gaps: No conversation/intent/account signals; advanced viz requires Graphly; no warehouse sync
- Proof quote: "Tracks sales, marketing, and automation performance via dashboards, sales reports, and an advanced reporting hub" — https://keap.com/solutions/reporting
