# Pipedrive — Pulse

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted outreach surface)

**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
In-CRM prospecting workspace with a real-time sales feed (Follow-ups, Overlooked deals, Opportunities), one-click contact/company enrichment, and nurture sequences — built to keep prospecting inside Pipedrive instead of in a second tool.

## Spotlight when
The article frames "all-in-one CRM with prospecting" against ZoomInfo's data + Workspace stack. Pulse's bundled-enrichment story is its strongest hook.

## Cite
- Key features: Pulse Feed (3 tabs), one-click enrichment, custom lead scoring, sequences with reply/click pauses, Smart Contact Data
- Strengths: Prospecting native to the CRM; enrichment bundled in Premium/Ultimate; public seat pricing
- Gaps: Public-web-only data sourcing; no technographic/intent; lighter sequences than Outreach/Salesloft
