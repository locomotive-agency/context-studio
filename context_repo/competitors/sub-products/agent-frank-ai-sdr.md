# Agent Frank — Agent Frank AI SDR

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace — autonomous prospect + outreach + book-meeting workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous AI SDR that prospects, writes and sends multichannel email + LinkedIn outreach, follows up, and books meetings 24/7 from a buyer-provided knowledge base, with optional human-in-the-loop Co-Pilot mode.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo's AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace to standalone AI-SDR products, especially when discussing autonomous outbound, multilingual outreach, or bundled email infrastructure as a differentiator.

- Strengths: Bundled Infraforge/Megaforge email infrastructure + Warmforge warm-up; native LinkedIn outreach without Sales Nav; flat-rate pricing with unlimited mailboxes and LinkedIn senders
