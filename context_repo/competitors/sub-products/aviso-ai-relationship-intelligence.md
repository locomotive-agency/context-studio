# Aviso AI — Relationship Intelligence (Aviso Engage)

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (direct overlap on relationship/multi-threading reasoning over engagement signals)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Maps buyer-side stakeholder relationships, surfaces multi-threading gaps, and pushes AI nudges so reps engage the right contacts at the right cadence — Aviso's relationship-graph layer.

## Spotlight when
Articles about multi-threading, deal-coverage, or relationship intelligence. Aviso's Champion B/C framework is a useful angle for any "are you covering the buying group" comparison.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-threaded deal management with Champion B/C, Relationship Graphs with A/B/C engagement scores, Email sentiment mining, Auto-population of new stakeholders, Winscore Insights, Bi-directional CRM sync
- Strengths: Champion B/C multi-threading framework with gap detection; A/B/C engagement scoring; auto-population of stakeholders from email/calendar
- Gaps: First-party-signal graph; no third-party org chart layer; no external buyer-intent overlay; not standalone
- Proof quote: "Relationship Graphs showing every seller-buyer engagement and A/B/C engagement scores" — https://www.aviso.com/product/relationship-intelligence
