# Bloomberg — Bloomberg Risk

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (none — financial-services risk modeling, no ZoomInfo overlap)

## What it does

## Spotlight when
Generally do not spotlight; included to round out Bloomberg's product taxonomy. Useful only as evidence of Bloomberg's distance from ZoomInfo's GTM scope.
