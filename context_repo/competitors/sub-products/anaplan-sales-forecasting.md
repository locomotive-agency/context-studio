# Anaplan — Sales Forecasting

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on forecast roll-up + pipeline management)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI-driven sales forecasting solution that delivers real-time pipeline visibility and predictive insights for sellers, sales leaders, and CROs — with forecast connected to incentives and demand forecasts.

## Spotlight when
Comparing forecasting approaches. Anaplan's distinct angle is connecting the sales forecast to incentives and demand forecasts in the same platform — a planning-platform play, not a seller-workflow play.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time forecast roll-up, Territory/quota → pipeline, Pipeline management/deal reviews, Single intelligent forecast across teams, Forecast tied to incentives + demand
- Strengths: Forecast connected to incentives and demand; cross-revenue-model unification; ready-to-deploy app
- Gaps: Lives in Anaplan models not seller workflow; no conversation/activity signals; adjacent to ZI's deal/forecast surface
