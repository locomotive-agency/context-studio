# InsightSquared — Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap — record/transcribe/analyze customer calls with coaching playlists and competitive mention tracking)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Records, transcribes, and analyzes customer calls and meetings, feeding the data into machine-learning forecasting and coaching models — searchable on-demand transcript library, coaching playlists, and skill-level reporting.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation intelligence vendors (Chorus, Gong, Clari Copilot, Avoma) and the integrated-CI-inside-revenue-intelligence positioning.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time recording/transcription, searchable transcript library, coaching playlists, skill-level reporting, competitive mention tracking, native CI→forecasting handoff
- Strengths: Native handoff into forecasting/reporting; skill-level reporting; bundled inside revenue intelligence
- Gaps: Less mature than standalone CI leaders; no MCP/agent surface; first-party models only
