# Marketo Engage — Marketo Engage Core Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on B2B campaign orchestration, lead nurturing, lead scoring, sales-marketing alignment)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered B2B marketing automation platform for planning, executing, and measuring omnichannel campaigns — from batch email to automated nurture to multi-step ABM campaigns — with industry-leading native bi-directional CRM sync.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enterprise B2B marketing automation platforms vs. ZoomInfo Marketing. Marketo's strongest spotlight: 2024 + 2025 Gartner Leader status, deep Adobe Experience Cloud integration, and 200K records/hour SFDC sync — the enterprise-credibility story.
