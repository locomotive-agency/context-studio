# Ampliz — Ampliz Healthcare Intelligence

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — vertical-specialist healthcare dataset that ZoomInfo's general data foundation does not match at this granularity)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Vertical healthcare data product covering 5.38M+ HCPs, 8,203 hospitals, and 10M executive contacts for medical device, pharma, diagnostic, and healthcare-recruitment sales teams.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's data depth in a healthcare-vertical context, or contrasts general-purpose ZoomInfo data against vertical specialists.

## Cite
- Key features: 5.38M+ HCPs and 8,203 hospitals, 10M+ executive contacts, 25+ search filters for segmented data output, NPI lookup and physician-specialty directories, Customer footprint analytics with intent-driven data, GDPR-aligned data policies
- Strengths: Vertical-specialist HCP/hospital coverage deeper than ZoomInfo's general database; NPI lookup + physician-specialty directories; manual sourcing requests for niche contacts
- Gaps: No GTM Context Graph or signal-driven recommendations; no conversation intelligence; no public API or MCP; pricing requires demo
- Proof quote: "5,384,164 HCPs and 8,203 hospitals — purpose-built data for medical device manufacturers, pharma, and healthcare recruitment firms." — https://www.ampliz.com/healthcare-intelligence
