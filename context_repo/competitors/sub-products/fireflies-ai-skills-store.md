# Fireflies — Fireflies AI Skills Store

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap — post-meeting AI workflows, not analytics rollups)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Library of 200+ pre-built AI skills that automate post-meeting workflows for specific roles — sales, recruiting, marketing, product, engineering — with customizable Super Summaries templates per industry / meeting type.

## Spotlight when
The article frames CI breadth across non-sales use cases. AI Skills Store is the spotlight when the angle is "200+ pre-built post-meeting workflows across roles" rather than deep sales analytics.
