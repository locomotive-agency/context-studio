# Regie.ai — AI Dialer

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap — touches CI turf via call transcription / AI coaching but is dialer-first, not a recording library)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Signal-prioritized parallel dialer with virtual Sales Floor, AI scripts, custom voicemail drops, and automated CRM follow-up; sold only as part of an Agents package.

## Spotlight when
The article compares dialer + light CI bundles or covers AI voicemail / parallel-dialer modernization stories. Best used as a counter-example to Chorus's depth.

## Cite
- Key features: Parallel dialing 9 lines, 150+ signal prioritization, Virtual Sales Floor with whisper coaching, Synthetic-voice voicemail drops, Auto post-call notes to CRM
- Strengths: Parallel dialer + Sales Floor combined; rep-voice synthetic voicemails; bundled waterfall enrichment
- Gaps: No standalone CI library or scorecards; cannot be purchased standalone; no deal-risk modeling
