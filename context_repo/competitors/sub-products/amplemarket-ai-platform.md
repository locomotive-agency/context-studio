# Amplemarket — Amplemarket Duo (AI Copilot family)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace — signal-driven, multichannel rep copilot)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI sales agent that captures 100+ contact-level buying signals across web, social, Slack, and CRM, researches accounts, and prepares hyper-personalized multichannel sequences for human reps to approve.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo's AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace to Copilot-style sales platforms, especially when the angle is contact-level (vs account-level) signal coverage or unique signal sources like Slack communities.

## Cite
- Key features: Contact-level signal tracking (job changes, G2 reviews, Slack posts, social engagement), Duo Copywriter for personalized first-touch emails, Duo Inbox for auto-drafted reply emails, Duo Voice for AI-cloned voice messages on social, Auto-generated multichannel sequences per signal/persona, Human-in-the-loop approval flow
- Strengths: Contact-level signals (100+ types) vs typical account-level focus; Slack community tracking as first-party signal source; Duo Voice AI-cloned voice messages for LinkedIn DMs
- Gaps: Human-in-the-loop by design (not fully autonomous); no conversation intelligence; no developer API or MCP; Enterprise tiers gated
