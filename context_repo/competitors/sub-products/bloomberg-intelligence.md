# Bloomberg — Bloomberg Intelligence

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on company-financials side of the data foundation)

**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
In-depth investor-grade research and interactive data on industries, companies, government, credit, litigation and economics — delivered inside the Terminal by 500+ research professionals.

## Spotlight when
The article touches market intelligence / public-company research adjacencies. Bloomberg Intelligence's depth on public-company fundamentals contrasts with ZoomInfo's breadth across private B2B contacts and technographics.

## Cite
- Key features: 10 sectors / 135+ industries; 2,000+ company primers; equity/FICC/credit/ESG/litigation research; multi-source interactive charts; Excel + API export
- Strengths: 500+ in-house analysts; investor-grade depth on financials/debt; Terminal-native experience
- Gaps: Investor focus, not GTM; gated to Terminal seats; no B2B firmographic/technographic enrichment
- Proof quote: "500+ research professionals covering 10 sectors and 135+ industries" — https://professional.bloomberg.com/products/bloomberg-terminal/research/bloomberg-intelligence/
