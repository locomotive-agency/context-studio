# Similarweb — Similarweb Sales Intelligence

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on prospecting + qualification + AI-drafted outreach)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Helps B2B sales teams find, qualify, and prioritize accounts by turning digital signals (traffic trends, buyer intent, tech changes, news activity) into prospecting and outreach actions, with three native AI agents and 100M+ (470M FAQ-cited) GDPR/CCPA-compliant contacts.

## Spotlight when
The article compares signal-led prospecting (web traffic, tech changes, ad-network activity) vs. ZoomInfo's contact-database + intent-data motion. Strongest spotlight is the web/app traffic signal as the prospecting trigger.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Prospecting Agent, 100M+ contacts (470M FAQ), Sales Signals, AI Outreach, AI Meeting Prep, Chrome Extension, Salesforce + HubSpot CRM enrichment, API + MCP
- Strengths: Web-traffic and tech-stack signals as prospecting trigger; three native AI agents on Similarweb data; mobile app data layered with web for B2B qualification
- Gaps: Lower contact volume (100M-470M vs. ZI 500M); no conversation intelligence; signal-led not CRM/conversation-led context; no GTM Workspace fusion
