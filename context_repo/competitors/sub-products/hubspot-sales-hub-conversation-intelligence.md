# HubSpot Sales Hub — HubSpot Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on call recording, transcription, and rep coaching)

**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Built-in call recording, transcription, and coaching layer inside Sales Hub Pro+ — captures call stats, tracked terms, and AI-generated insights, with Enterprise adding AI transcript enrichment.

## Spotlight when
The article compares Chorus alternatives or evaluates whether teams need a standalone CI vendor. HubSpot CI's spotlight angle is "free with Sales Hub Pro+" versus paying for a dedicated CI platform.

## Cite
- Key features: Call recording and transcription (750-1,500 hrs/account/month), Tracked terms, Call insights and statistics, Coaching playlists, AI transcript enrichment (Enterprise Beta), Smart Deal Progression suggestions from call transcripts
- Strengths: Bundled with Sales Hub Pro/Enterprise; native to HubSpot CRM; transcript-to-deal-update flow via Smart Deal Progression
- Gaps: Less mature than Chorus / Gong; capped recording hours; coaching analytics lag standalone CI tools
- Proof quote: "Conversation Intelligence — call stats, insights, transcriptions, tracked terms" — https://www.hubspot.com/pricing/sales
