# Monday CRM — Data Enrichment (Crunchbase + marketplace)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap at the CRM-record-hygiene layer; ZI itself is a marketplace partner)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Auto-enriches accounts/leads with firmographic data from Crunchbase natively, with optional marketplace integrations to ZoomInfo, Clearbit, and Lusha for contact-level enrichment — supporting auto-enrichment from forms, social ads, and a Chrome extension for Gmail.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-side enrichment surfaces. Cite when buyers think about "enrichment built into the CRM" vs. ZoomInfo Operations as a dedicated cross-system data layer; note the dynamic that ZoomInfo itself is a marketplace partner here.

## Cite
- Key features: Crunchbase native firmographic enrichment, Marketplace ZI/Clearbit/Lusha integrations, Auto-enrichment from forms/ads/lead sources, Gmail Chrome extension
- Strengths: Crunchbase firmographics built-in; open marketplace including ZI; Chrome-extension Gmail capture
- Gaps: Firmographic-only natively; no proprietary B2B contact graph; no cross-system enrichment graph
- Proof quote: "ZoomInfo marketplace integration — feed contact info into CRM automatically" — https://monday.com/crm/overview/integrations
