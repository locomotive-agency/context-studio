# Kickbox — Kickbox Email List Verification

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on the verified-email layer of ZoomInfo's data foundation; head-to-head with NeverBounce specifically)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Bulk cleanup of existing email lists — classifies addresses as deliverable, risky, or undeliverable, detects disposable/role-based domains, and refunds credits when a result comes back unknown.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email-verification tools (Kickbox vs NeverBounce vs Bouncer) — especially when the buyer wants the Sendex granularity score and "unknown-result refund" mechanics.

## Cite
- Key features: Deliverable/Risky/Undeliverable classification, disposable + role-based + accept-all detection, typo/syntax detection, ~1hr per 100K, free re-credit on unknown
- Strengths: Sendex score; free re-credit on unknowns; 40B daily signals
- Gaps: Validates only — does not source new contacts; no firmographic context; no GTM signals
- Proof quote: "Fast, Reliable, & Accurate Email Verification — your all-in-one email verification platform" — https://kickbox.com/
