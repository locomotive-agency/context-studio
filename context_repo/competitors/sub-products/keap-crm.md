# Keap — Keap CRM

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller / SMB-owner CRM surface; no B2B data foundation underneath)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
SMB-focused CRM that centralizes contacts, lead/deal tracking, and per-contact automation history with bundled support and a mobile app, sold as a single all-features plan.

## Spotlight when
The article compares SMB CRMs (Keap vs HubSpot Sales Hub vs Pipedrive) — especially when the buyer wants single-plan pricing and bundled CSM/support, and is comfortable that net-new lead sourcing is out of scope.

## Cite
- Key features: Contact management with custom fields, lead scoring, sales pipeline automation, customizable dashboard, mobile app, Gmail/Outlook sync
- Strengths: Single-plan pricing; bundled CSM + 24/7 chat + US phone; Lifecycle Automation framework
- Gaps: No B2B contact data; explicitly does not find leads; SMB-only
- Proof quote: "Centralizes contacts, communications, and lead/deal tracking for small businesses with built-in automation for follow-up" — https://keap.com/solutions/best-crm-for-small-businesses
