# Amplemarket — Amplemarket Multichannel Sequences + Deliverability

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-engagement workflow surface — sequences + dialer + deliverability)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI-built multichannel outreach sequences across email, phone, social DMs, WhatsApp/iMessage, and parallel-dialer calls — with a built-in deliverability suite (Domain Health Center, warmup, spam checker, mailbox recommendation) that monitors and protects sender reputation.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one platforms (data + engagement + deliverability) versus the ZoomInfo + Outreach/Salesloft + warmup-vendor stack, or specifically discusses email deliverability tooling as a wedge.

- Strengths: End-to-end deliverability suite bundled in; native WhatsApp + iMessage channels; multiple mailboxes per user with rotation
