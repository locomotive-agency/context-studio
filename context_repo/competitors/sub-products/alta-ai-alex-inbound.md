# Alta AI — Alex — AI Inbound / Calling Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — voice/multichannel inbound qualification is competitor-only space ZoomInfo's stack does not yet cover at equivalent scope)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Voice-and-multichannel inbound qualification agent that responds to leads 24/7 across voice, chat, email, SMS, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn in 20+ languages — qualifies against custom criteria, books meetings, and updates the CRM in real time.

## Spotlight when
The article discusses inbound qualification automation, AI voice agents, or 24/7 lead response — and the ZoomInfo article angle is GTM Workspace's seller-facing AI agent capabilities.

- Gaps: No proprietary contact data; no Chorus-equivalent coaching surface; gated pricing; no public API or MCP
