# AlphaSense — Tegus Expert Insights

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (no direct overlap — pitched as a flagship qualitative-data layer with no ZI peer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
World's largest library of investor-led expert interview transcripts (250,000+) plus bespoke expert call services and Channel Checks for proprietary qualitative research; flagship differentiator following the Tegus acquisition.

## Spotlight when
Articles about "expert networks" or qualitative research. Tegus is AlphaSense's strongest moat and has no direct ZoomInfo equivalent — call it out as a category-adjacent capability rather than a substitute.

## Cite
- Key features: 250,000+ investor-led expert transcripts, 8K+ new transcripts/month across 29K+ companies, AI summaries/key takeaways/sentiment, Bespoke expert call services, Channel Checks, Autonomous AI Agent Interviewer
- Strengths: Largest investor-led expert transcript library post-Tegus; autonomous AI agent interviewer; transcript compliance framework
- Gaps: Not B2B contact/intent data; no ZI equivalent so spotlight is contrast not substitution; investment-research-workflow oriented
- Proof quote: "the world's largest library of expert insights" — https://www.alpha-sense.com/platform/expert-insights/
