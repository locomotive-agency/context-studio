# Semrush — Semrush Enterprise

**Competes with:** APIs & MCP (adjacent — only on the MCP/API access lane and broad marketing-intelligence framing)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Modular enterprise marketing intelligence platform spanning SEO, AI search optimization, large-scale technical site audits, panel/journey data (Datos), surveys (MFour), and social listening (Insights24).

## Spotlight when
The article covers enterprise marketing-intelligence platforms vs. ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM/intent surface. Important to flag this is a partner / complementary vendor — ZoomInfo is itself a Semrush Enterprise customer.
