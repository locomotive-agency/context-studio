# Salesforce Einstein — Einstein GPT / generative AI for Sales

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on AI-drafted outreach + record summaries)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Generative-AI features grounded in Salesforce CRM data that draft personalized sales emails, summarize records, and produce call summaries — protected by the Trust Layer with BYO LLM support.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses AI-drafted outreach grounded in CRM data; Einstein GPT is the in-CRM competitor to ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI drafting.

## Cite
- Key features: Record summaries, email drafting, Call Summaries, Trust Layer, BYOM
- Strengths: Trust Layer, BYO LLM, native CRM grounding
- Gaps: Metered separately via PDF rate card, Salesforce dependency, no third-party grounding
