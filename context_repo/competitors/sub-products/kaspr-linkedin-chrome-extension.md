# Kaspr — Kaspr LinkedIn Chrome Extension

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the LinkedIn Chrome Extension prospecting surface inside ZoomInfo Sales)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Browser extension that reveals verified B2B phone numbers, emails, and company data directly inside LinkedIn, Sales Navigator, and Recruiter Lite — Kaspr's flagship surface and where its European data coverage shows up.

## Spotlight when
The article compares LinkedIn Chrome extensions for prospecting (Kaspr vs ZoomInfo Sales extension), or when European phone/email coverage with no data-passport fee is the buyer's wedge.

## Cite
- Key features: Reveals on LinkedIn/Groups/Events/Sales Nav/Recruiter Lite, prefix preview before credit, 500M+ records verified against 120 sources, free plan with unlimited B2B emails, GDPR/CCPA aligned
- Strengths: 120M+ European B2B contacts at request time (no passport fee); broadest LinkedIn surface coverage; free-forever tier
- Gaps: Smaller US scale than ZoomInfo; no technographics/intent/Context Graph; LinkedIn-bound
