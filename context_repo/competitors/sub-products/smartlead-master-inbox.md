# Smartlead — Unified Master Inbox

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller front-end inbox / deal-management surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Centralised reply-management workspace with an integrated kanban CRM that consolidates campaign responses, AI reply triage, and deal tracking across campaigns and clients.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo GTM Workspace's seller inbox to alternatives that bundle multi-client reply triage with a lightweight kanban CRM — particularly relevant for agency-led outbound.

## Cite
- Key features: Single shared inbox; auto-categorise replies by intent; AI reply manager prioritises hot leads; kanban CRM synced with campaigns; real-time CRM sync
- Strengths: Bundled reply triage + kanban; multi-client agency inbox; AI prioritisation
- Gaps: Lightweight CRM; no external CI signals; channels limited to email + integrated voice / LinkedIn
- Proof quote: "AI reply manager prioritises hot leads and triggers next steps." — https://www.smartlead.ai/
