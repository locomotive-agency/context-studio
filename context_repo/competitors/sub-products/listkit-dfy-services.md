# ListKit — ListKit DFY Services

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent overlap on the seller-execution surface — managed cold-email/dialer agency model with no direct ZoomInfo counterpart)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Done-for-you cold-email infrastructure (Cold Email Setup) plus a fully managed omnichannel outbound service where ListKit runs both email and SDR cold-call campaigns end-to-end (list build → scripts → dialing → response sorting → meeting booking).

## Spotlight when
The article highlights buyers evaluating in-house SDR vs outsourced outbound, or operators who want managed deliverability infrastructure (private IPs, custom domains, warmup) bundled with their data subscription.

## Cite
- Key features: Cold Email Setup with private "invite-only" sending IP infrastructure (30/60/90 inboxes per tier), Fully Managed Omnichannel Outbound with human SDRs running an AI predictive parallel dialer at 1500-2000 dials/day, AI response sorting (Interested/Meeting Request/Information Request/Not Interested), 24/7 AI deliverability monitoring with auto inbox replacement, AI-personalized 20-30-word cold emails plus AI-written ice-breakers, TCPA-compliant B2B-only dialing with DNC scanning
- Strengths: Same vendor for self-serve data + managed outbound; private sending IPs; quarterly billing
- Gaps: Managed model has no ZoomInfo counterpart; setup fee on Cold Email Setup tier; no CRM data-quality / routing
