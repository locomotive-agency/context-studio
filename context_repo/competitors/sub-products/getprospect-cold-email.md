# GetProspect — GetProspect Cold Email

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent overlap on outbound sequencing — same workflow surface but no intent-data prioritization or full multi-channel coverage)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Built-in cold email sender and sequencer for outreach to enriched contact lists — included on the free plan (one sequence) and all paid tiers, with a built-in email verification step that supports a 97% deliverability claim.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one prospecting bundles or freemium sequencers — Cold Email's strongest spotlight angle is the bundled "find + verify + email" loop on a single freemium price.

## Cite
- Key features: Cold email sequences; 97% deliverability via built-in verification; up to 100 emails/day per domain recommended; available on Free/Starter/Growth; one sequence on free plan
- Strengths: Bundled with email finder + database + enrichment; built-in verification step; single-sequence on free plan
- Gaps: No intent-data prioritization; limited send governance vs. Outreach/Salesloft; no dialer/SMS/LinkedIn channels
