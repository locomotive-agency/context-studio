# Reply — Sales Engagement Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller execution surface — multichannel sequencing + reply handling)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Multichannel sales engagement platform that automates outbound sequences across email, LinkedIn, calls, SMS, and WhatsApp with built-in deliverability tooling.

## Spotlight when
The article compares sales engagement platforms (Outreach / Salesloft / Apollo / Reply / GTM Workspace) — Reply's multichannel breadth (LinkedIn + WhatsApp + SMS) and unlimited mailbox/warm-up framing are the strongest spotlight angles.

- Gaps: No GTM Studio analogue; shallow CI; no MCP / agent-builder ecosystem
