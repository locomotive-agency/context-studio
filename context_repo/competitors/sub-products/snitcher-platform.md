# Snitcher — Snitcher Platform

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap on the website-visitor-identification surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
SaaS app that identifies companies visiting the customer's site via IP intelligence, captures intent signals on pricing / feature / return visits, and pushes alerts and verified contacts into CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho, Dynamics, Attio) and sequencers (Apollo, Lemlist, Smartlead).

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo's WebSights against pure-play visitor-ID alternatives, or argue that SMB / mid-market teams need contract-free visitor identification with multi-CRM sync.

## Cite
- Key features: Company ID via IP; pricing / comparison / return-visit intent; real-time Slack / Teams / email alerts; native sync to 6 CRMs; contact reveal; deal-reactivation alerts
- Strengths: "#1 Zoominfo Alternative" positioning; $49/mo no-contract entry; Dynamics + Attio destinations
- Gaps: Scoped to customer's site only; no CI; no audience building / play orchestration
