# G2 Buyer Intent — G2 Buyer Intent

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on the demand-signal layer inside ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Captures verified, first-party intent signals from 100M+ software buyers researching products, categories, and competitors on G2.com, then routes those signals into the customer's sales / marketing / CS stack with Buying Stage + Activity Level scoring.

## Spotlight when
The article compares intent-data sources or "first-party intent vs. third-party clickstream" — G2 Buyer Intent's strongest spotlight angle is mid/bottom-funnel signals from a review marketplace where buyers are already comparing competitors.

## Cite
- Key features: Nine verified first-party signal types, Buying Stage + Activity Level dual scoring, ICP filters (firmographics, geography, target competitors, target categories, signal types), Detailed company-activity timelines, CSV exports, Add-on to G2 Brand Professional/Enterprise
- Strengths: Verified first-party intent (not inferred); Compare signals show which competitors a buyer benchmarked you against; mid/bottom-funnel intent; 100M+ software buyers/year on G2
- Gaps: Bounded to G2's marketplace; no contact data (relies on ZI/LinkedIn/Bombora); no public pricing
- Proof quote: "Find your best-fit buyers. Fill your pipeline." — https://sell.g2.com/data
