# Highspot — Highspot Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on call recording, summarization, and coaching)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Captures sales calls and meetings, summarizes them, surfaces objections and next steps, scores rep delivery, and feeds insights into Deal Intelligence (CRM + meeting + seller activity).

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation intelligence options. Highspot's CI is most useful as the enablement-anchored CI variant — call out the practice→performance loop with Role Play.

## Cite
- Key features: AI summaries/objections, Coaching feedback (filler/pacing/pitch), Searchable transcripts, Meeting → Deal Intelligence flow, 1:1 coaching feedback
- Strengths: CI ↔ Role Play loop; meeting insights into Deal Intelligence; time-stamped coaching
- Gaps: Enablement-anchored vs. Chorus's market-view; no third-party signals; top-tier gated
