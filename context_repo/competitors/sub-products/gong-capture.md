# Gong — Gong Capture

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on conversation intelligence)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Gong's flagship conversation intelligence — call recording, AI analytics, topic detection, and coaching across video meetings and dialer calls.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation intelligence platforms or the broader "Revenue AI" category. Chorus' role as a context capture engine feeding GTM Context Graph is the structural ZoomInfo counter to highlight.

## Cite
- Key features: Call recording (video + dialer), AI conversation analytics, topic detection + trends, deal-level context, coaching workflows
- Strengths: Most established CI product; analytics depth + accuracy reputation; largest B2B SaaS CI customer base
- Gaps: No B2B data/intent/ABM; standalone vendor; lacks unified reasoning layer (Chorus → GTM Context Graph)
- Proof quote: "The Revenue AI OS that captures every customer conversation and uses it to drive growth." — https://www.gong.io/products/conversation-intelligence/
