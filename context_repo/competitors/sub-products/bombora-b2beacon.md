# Bombora — Bombora B2beacon Campaign Measurement

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on ABM measurement / attribution)

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
B2B-centric digital campaign measurement that reports reach and engagement at the account, buying group, and persona level — without requiring an ABM platform subscription.

## Spotlight when
The article frames ABM measurement, attribution, or how to measure paid-media performance against accounts. B2beacon's "no ABM-platform required" angle is the strongest spotlight.

- Gaps: Measurement only (no targeting/orchestration); not embedded in unified ABM workflow; gated pricing
