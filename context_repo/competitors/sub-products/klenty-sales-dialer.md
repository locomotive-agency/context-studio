# Klenty — Klenty Sales Dialer Suite

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the in-suite dialer surface for sellers)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Multi-mode sales dialer (Parallel, Power, Click-to-Call) bundled with AI voicemail/IVR/outcome detection, one-click voicemail drop, call recordings, local dial, and number auto-rotation — engineered for high-volume cold-calling at up to ~350 calls/hour.

## Spotlight when
The article compares dialer-heavy seller workflows for SMB / mid-market teams who want a parallel dialer bundled with their SEP rather than buying Orum or Nooks separately.
