# Contify — Contify M&CI Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on signal aggregation and context fusion)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-native market and competitive intelligence platform that collects, analyzes, and delivers ready-to-use insights from 1 Mn+ external sources and ingested internal sources (SharePoint, Gong/Fireflies, Slack, MS Teams, email) — customized per team, function, and strategic priority.

## Spotlight when
The article positions ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph against pure-play CI/MI platforms, or argues for analyst-team-shaped intelligence vs. seller-shaped intelligence. Contify's strongest spotlight angle is multilingual / non-English source coverage at scale.

## Cite
- Key features: Athena AI agentic engine, Auto-updating battlecards & 50+ templates, Curated newsfeeds, 117+ language auto-translation, Internal-source ingestion
- Strengths: 1 Mn+ vetted sources incl. 200k+ non-English; bundled analyst-managed services; unlimited-user access
- Gaps: No B2B contact/firmographic dataset; no GTM workflow orchestration; no native conversation intelligence
- Proof quote: "Timely AI-Powered Contextual 360° Intelligence delivered" — https://www.contify.com/platform/
