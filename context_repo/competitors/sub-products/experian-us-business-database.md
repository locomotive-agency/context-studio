# Experian — U.S. Business Database / B2B Marketing Solutions

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on B2B firmographic data + list generation/append for marketing)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
B2B prospecting and marketing data product offering verified U.S. business records used for list generation, append, scoring, prescreen/prequalification, and demand-generation campaigns — backed by Experian's call-center verification and trade-credit attributes.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data sources with credit/trade attributes layered on top of firmographics. Experian's database is the one to spotlight when the angle is "data with credit-bureau-grade verification" rather than seller-workflow-bundled prospecting.

## Cite
- Key features: 16M+ U.S. businesses from 6,000+ sources, 60+ filters, 17.5MM+ verified via Business TargetIQ, Marketing list generation/append, Demand-gen campaigns, Lead enrichment + look-alike + predictive modeling
- Strengths: Daily call-center verification; DEI firmographic filters; trade-credit attributes
- Gaps: 16M U.S. businesses vs ZoomInfo's 100M+ graph; no contact-level parity; no intent/conversation data; no seller front-end
- Proof quote: "Find and connect with the right business prospects with verified U.S. business data" — https://www.experian.com/business-information/business-marketing
