# BuiltWith — BuiltWith Sales Intelligence

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap — tech-stack-movement intent)

**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Surfaces a target account's full website tech profile and competitor / churn movement so sellers can have informed conversations and time outreach to tech-change events.

## Spotlight when
The article frames intent-from-tech-stack-movement vs topic-level intent. Actionable Insights and competitor switcher lists are the spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: One-click Technology Lookup; Competitor Comparison; Actionable Insights (trials / experimentation / rebuilds); LeadsEye; historical usage + registered contacts
- Strengths: Stack-movement intent; competitor churn lists; real-time trial / rebuild signals
- Gaps: Tech-stack-only intent; no topic-level co-op data; narrower signal scope
- Proof quote: "companies showing clear signals they are about to buy, upgrade, or switch technologies" — https://builtwith.com/sales-intelligence
