# ActiveCampaign — Email Marketing

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent — execution channel overlaps but operates only on first-party lists)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Email marketing module with AI-assisted creation, templates, deliverability tooling, and predictive sending — promoted as 8x faster campaign creation.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses ESP-style email execution or deliverability for SMB marketers. Don't lean on this as a core ZI competitor; spotlight only when the comparison frame is "owned-channel execution vs. ZoomInfo's audience-orchestration layer."

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop builder, AI Brand Kit, Predictive Sending, Deliverability + DMARC, Postmark transactional, Split testing, Behavioral triggers
- Strengths: Per-contact predictive send time; Postmark transactional infra; URL-based brand import
- Gaps: First-party-only; no third-party signals; ESP-style scope
