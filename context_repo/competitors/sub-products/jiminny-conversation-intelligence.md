# Jiminny — Jiminny Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on call recording, transcription, deal-risk alerts, coaching workflows)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered conversation intelligence that records, transcribes, and analyzes sales/customer calls (phone + video) and emails, surfacing deal-risk alerts, coaching opportunities, and Ask Jiminny Q&A across the corpus.

## Spotlight when
The article compares Chorus alternatives or coaching-led conversation intelligence for mid-market revenue teams. Lean on the 60+ language coverage and Ask Jiminny natural-language Q&A as Jiminny's strongest CI angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Ask Jiminny instant insights, automatic CRM logging, proactive deal-risk alerts, team performance benchmarking, native Salesforce/HubSpot apps, 60+ language transcription
- Strengths: Multilingual transcription scope; conversational Q&A across calls/deals; transparent free + seat-based pricing
- Gaps: No third-party B2B data foundation; less enterprise analytics depth; mid-market focus
