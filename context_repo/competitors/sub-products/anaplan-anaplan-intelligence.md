# Anaplan — Anaplan Intelligence (Role-Based Agents + CoModeler)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the AI-agent layer over enterprise data)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Predictive, generative, and agentic AI layer with role-specific AI analysts (Finance, Sales, Supply Chain, Workforce) plus CoModeler for natural-language model building, embedded in Anaplan applications.

## Spotlight when
Comparing AI agent strategies. Anaplan Intelligence is the strongest contrast point for ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI-agent layer — Anaplan's agents reason over plans/models, ZoomInfo's agents reason over the GTM Context Graph and execute outbound.

## Cite
- Key features: Sales/Finance/Supply Chain/Workforce Analysts, CoModeler, Conversational NL interface, NL model building
- Strengths: Role-specific AI analysts for four functions; CoModeler builds/extends models from NL; trained on planning corpus
- Gaps: Reasons over Anaplan models not CRM/conversations/intent; no seller execution; limited to Anaplan customers' planning data
- Proof quote: "Trained on a decade of planning expertise from deploying millions of models" — https://www.anaplan.com/platform/intelligent-role-based-agents
