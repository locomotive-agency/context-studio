# ZeroBounce — ZeroBounce Email Validation

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap with the NeverBounce email-validation module of ZoomInfo Operations / RingLead data quality)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Bulk and one-at-a-time email verification platform that cleans email lists at point of capture or in batch — with a 99.6% accuracy guarantee, spam-trap detection, catch-all detection, toxic-domain checks, and Anti-Greylisting Technology to keep bounce rates below 2%.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email-verification alternatives to NeverBounce or pitches "do I need both ZoomInfo and a verification tool?" — ZeroBounce's 99.6% accuracy claim and free-unknowns commercial model are its strongest spotlight angle vs. ZoomInfo's bundled Operations data-quality slice.
