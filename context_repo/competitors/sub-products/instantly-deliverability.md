# Instantly — Deliverability & Inbox Placement

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no overlap — email deliverability infrastructure is outside ZoomInfo's product surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Deliverability infrastructure including unlimited email warmup, SISR (Server & IP Sharding and Rotation), pre-send inbox-placement testing, and Done-For-You / Pre-Warmed domain and inbox marketplace.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to call out a category where Instantly does NOT compete with ZoomInfo — deliverability is unique to cold-email-first vendors and explains why agencies stack Instantly alongside (not instead of) ZoomInfo.

- Strengths: SISR without private servers; DFY domain marketplace; 94-blacklist monitoring
