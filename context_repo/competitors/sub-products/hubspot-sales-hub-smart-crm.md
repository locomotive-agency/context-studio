# HubSpot Sales Hub — HubSpot Smart CRM

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent — Smart CRM is the system of record that ZoomInfo Operations enriches and hygienes, not a substitute for the Operations data-quality layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
The CRM foundation underneath Sales Hub — a free-tier-available system of record that centralizes contact, deal, and activity data and powers HubSpot's Breeze AI agents.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRMs of record (HubSpot vs Salesforce vs Pipedrive) or asks whether HubSpot Smart CRM duplicates ZoomInfo Operations. The spotlight angle is "Smart CRM is the customer's data layer; ZoomInfo Operations is the cleansing/enrichment layer that sits on top."

## Cite
- Key features: Free tier (up to 2 users), Custom objects (Enterprise), 1,500-2,000 App Marketplace integrations, Powers Breeze AI agents, Foundation for Sales/Marketing/Service/Content/Data/Commerce hubs
- Strengths: Free CRM with no credit card; anchors full Customer Platform; large App Marketplace
- Gaps: CRM of record, not a dedup/data-quality engine; no vendor B2B graph; custom objects gated to Enterprise
