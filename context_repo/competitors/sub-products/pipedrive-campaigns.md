# Pipedrive — Campaigns by Pipedrive

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on email/automation surface only)

**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Email-marketing add-on inside the Pipedrive CRM — drag-and-drop builder, CRM-field-aware segmentation, analytics, and automation flows aimed at sales-led teams that don't want a separate marketing platform.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-attached marketing options or frames "marketing without a separate tool" against full ABM platforms. Best as a contrast — Campaigns has no ABM/intent surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop email builder, CRM-field segmentation, campaign analytics, automation flows, $13.33+ add-on
- Strengths: Lives inside the CRM; public add-on pricing; sales-led marketing motion
- Gaps: No ABM/display ads; no intent; no multi-channel orchestration; no FormComplete analog
- Proof quote: "Send sales-led email campaigns from inside the CRM" — https://www.pipedrive.com/en/products/email-marketing-software
