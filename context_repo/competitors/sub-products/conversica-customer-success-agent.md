# Conversica — Conversica Customer Service & Retention Agent

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Chat (direct overlap on the website-chat + conversational-engagement layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI agents focused on the customer-side lifecycle — Account Management & Renewals, Upsell/Cross-Sell, Service/Answers — that hold human-like two-way conversations across email, SMS, and chat to drive renewal coordination, account expansion, and service deflection.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Chat or website-chat workflows, especially when the angle is autonomous customer-side AI agents covering renewals/expansion/service in addition to acquisition.

## Cite
- Key features: Account Management & Renewals, Upsell / Cross-Sell, Service / Answers — resolve common issues in-conversation, Multi-channel inbound across chat/email/SMS, Escalates complex cases without breaking the thread, Conversation analytics with A/B testing
- Strengths: Customer-success-stage agents (renewal/upsell/billing) at uncommon scope; industry-trained brand-controlled agents; account-coordination workflows
- Gaps: Conversational-engagement layer only — no data-powered visitor ID at scale; no proprietary contact data; no deal coaching; pricing gated
