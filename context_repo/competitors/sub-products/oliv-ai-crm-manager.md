# Oliv AI — CRM Manager

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on CRM hygiene — auto-creation, field updates, two-way sync)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Auto-creates and enriches accounts/contacts, updates fields, and creates deals based on qualification criteria — keeping CRM hygiene end-to-end with bidirectional Salesforce and HubSpot sync.

## Spotlight when
The article contrasts conversation-driven CRM hygiene against form-driven enrichment — CRM Manager's "fields update from meeting transcripts" angle is sharpest.

## Cite
- Key features: Auto-creates accounts/contacts from conversations, Field updates from meeting/email signals, Deal creation on qualification criteria, Bidirectional Salesforce + HubSpot sync
- Strengths: Conversation-first hygiene; trigger-based deal creation tied to qualification logic; agentic auto-creation
- Gaps: No third-party enrichment from a B2B graph; narrower CRM coverage vs. ZoomInfo Operations; lighter routing/scoring depth
