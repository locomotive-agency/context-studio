# Cykel AI — Eve — AI SDR / AI Sales Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace — autonomous prospect-to-meeting workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous 24/7 AI sales-development agent that finds prospects from 700M+ contacts, researches accounts, writes personalized email + LinkedIn outreach, follows up, and books meetings — pitched at "$1.63/day" replacement economics vs human SDRs.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights GTM Workspace's AI agent layer for outbound, especially when the angle is replacement-economics framing or native LinkedIn outreach in the same agent as email.
