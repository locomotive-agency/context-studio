# GlobalData — GlobalData Sales Intelligence

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the seller-facing prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted outreach surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered, data-driven, expert-curated sales intelligence solution that supports the entire B2B sales workflow across plan, target, enable, and engage stages, with Sam — an agentic AI Sales Analyst that plans target accounts, monitors expansion signals, drafts personalized outreach, and optimizes campaigns.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enterprise sales-intelligence platforms with industry-vertical research depth — GlobalData is the spotlight when the buyer needs Pharma / Construction / Healthcare / Oil & Gas vertical analyst coverage that ZoomInfo's horizontal data foundation does not offer.
