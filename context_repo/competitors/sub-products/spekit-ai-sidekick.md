# Spekit — AI Sidekick (Agentic Coaching)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — AI-agent layer for sellers, delivered as a Chrome overlay rather than a destination app)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

Note: When this sub-product is referenced as competing with the AI-agent layer, treat the overlap as GTM Workspace's AI-agent surface (not a separate ZoomInfo Copilot product).

## What it does
AI orchestration layer that proactively detects what a rep is doing in real time and delivers coaching, content recommendations, or automated actions (drafts emails, builds Deal Rooms, generates exec summaries) inside the rep's existing tools.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI-agent surface to enablement-led agentic coaching, or position Chrome-overlay execution against destination-app workflows.

## Cite
- Key features: Proactive coaching on deal context; context-aware content recs; auto-drafted follow-ups grounded in Gong + Salesforce; auto-creates Deal Rooms; exec summaries from CI; in-flow enablement inside Salesforce / Gmail / Gong; battlecards / exit criteria / play guidance
- Strengths: Agentic vs chatbot framing; Chrome overlay delivery; grounded in Gong CI + Salesforce
- Gaps: No call recording (depends on Gong / Chorus); narrower context than GTM Context Graph; ZoomInfo is a Spekit customer (category-adjacent)
- Proof quote: "A chatbot answers questions after the fact. Spekit's agentic coaching uses deal stage, buyer behavior, rep activity, and conversation context to recommend what to send, what to say, and what to do next." — https://www.spekit.com/platform/agentic-coaching
