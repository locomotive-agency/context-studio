# Pipedrive — Pipedrive Sales CRM

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — Pipedrive is a CRM of record, ZoomInfo's data feeds it)

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Pipeline-centric sales CRM for SMB sales teams — kanban deal stages, email sync, workflow automations, AI-assisted forecasting, and mobile/meeting tooling in one workspace.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRMs of record for SMBs or frames affordable pipeline-management against enterprise CRM stacks. Pipedrive's $14/seat entry point is the cleanest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Kanban pipeline, two-way email sync (5 accounts), workflow automations, AI Sales Assistant, Insights forecasting, mobile apps
- Strengths: $14/seat public pricing; pipeline-first UX with deal rotting alerts; lower learning curve than Salesforce
- Gaps: Not a data layer; no conversation intelligence; no native ABM/intent
- Proof quote: "The easy and effective CRM for closing deals" — https://www.pipedrive.com/
