# TrustRadius — TrustRadius Intent-Driven Leads

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent — managed lead-gen program vs. seller-led prospecting workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Managed lead-generation program that converts in-market accounts on TrustRadius into ICP-qualified, opted-in leads delivered to the vendor's stack — no bidding, vendor provides ICP criteria.

## Spotlight when
The article frames "intent-driven prospecting" as a buyer's path — TrustRadius is a managed-service alternative to ZoomInfo Sales seller-led prospecting on intent + contact data.

## Cite
- Key features: ICP filtering, Market report / customer story conversion, 75% director+ audience, 56% 3-month buyers, Cisco 4,000 leads, No bidding model
- Strengths: Managed program; high-quality audience; Cisco scale proof
- Gaps: Lead delivery not seller workflow; TR-platform audience only; no contact graph
- Proof quote: "Cisco case study: 4,000 ICP-matched leads delivered in less than two weeks" — https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/intent-driven-leads
