# Fireflies — AI Meeting Assistant (Fred)

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on transcription, summarization, and meeting Q&A)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings across video-conferencing platforms via a notetaker bot, Chrome extension, mobile app, or desktop app — with AskFred natural-language Q&A across past meetings.

## Spotlight when
The article compares Chorus against multilingual or self-serve notetakers. Fireflies' AI Meeting Assistant is the strongest spotlight when the angle is "100+ language transcription with free tier and AskFred Q&A."

## Cite
- Key features: AI Note Taker Bot, 100+ language transcription, 95% transcription accuracy, Speaker tags, AI summaries with action items, AskFred Q&A, Meeting search to sentence + timestamp, Audio/video upload
- Strengths: 100+ languages with auto-detection; AskFred Q&A with citations; free-forever tier with API
- Gaps: No multi-call rollup analytics depth; no B2B data layer; self-authored vs-Chorus claims
