# Bombora — Bombora Premium B2B Digital Audiences

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on ABM audience activation)

**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Pre-built and custom B2B audiences activatable across DSPs, SSPs, and DMPs for ABM and digital ad targeting; layerable with Company Surge Intent for in-market account targeting.

## Spotlight when
The article frames B2B audience activation across paid media or curated ecosystem partner data. The 5x-larger-audience claim and Curated Ecosystem Audiences (G2 / Crunchbase / Definitive Healthcare / HG Insights) are spotlight-worthy.

## Cite
- Key features: 600+ pre-built audiences; 6,350 attributes; DSP/SSP/DMP activation; Curated Ecosystem Audience partners; layerable with Company Surge
- Strengths: 5x audience scale claim; ecosystem partner audiences; direct DSP/SSP/DMP activation
- Gaps: No native ad-buying surface; limited CRM/MAP writeback; gated pricing
