# Marketo Engage — Marketo Engage Sales Insight

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — Sales Insight surfaces engagement signals inside the CRM but is not a full seller front-end with prospecting + data + AI drafting)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Surfaces Marketo buyer engagement data inside the CRM (Salesforce / Dynamics) so sales reps can prioritize leads and have timely conversations — with Smart grid timelines, account watch lists, lead feed alerts, and Sales Insight Actions.

## Spotlight when
The article compares marketing-engagement-to-sales handoff stories or "in-CRM seller views" from a marketing-automation lens. Strongest spotlight: cold-lead indicators that surface deprioritization signals — rare among MAPs.

## Cite
- Key features: Lead scoring with cold-lead indicators, Smart grid timeline, account/opportunity best-bets watch lists, lead feed with instant alerts, in-CRM Insights dashboard, Sales Insight Actions, email alert tokens
- Strengths: Lives natively in SF/Dynamics; bi-directional sync feeds Smart grid in real-time; cold-lead indicators
- Gaps: Engagement view (not full prospecting + data surface); no first-party intent layer; no AI drafting / sequencing
- Proof quote: "Surfaces buyer engagement data inside the CRM so sales reps can prioritize leads and have timely conversations" — https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/sales-intelligence-engagement.html
