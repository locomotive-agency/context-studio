# Lead Onion — Lead Onion Sales Automation

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller cadence + pipeline surface bundled with intent)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Native multi-channel cadence and pipeline layer that auto-enrols accounts into outreach when surge thresholds are hit, with A/B testing, dynamic fields, real-time alerts on buyer-phase change, and a built-in pipeline view from first signal to closed-won. Bundled with Account Intelligence and IQLs at Growth tier and above.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one intent + engagement platforms against ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace + Sales bundle for SMB/mid-market teams that don't want a separate SEP.
