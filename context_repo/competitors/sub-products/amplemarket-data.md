# Amplemarket — Amplemarket Searcher / Data

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the seller-facing prospecting + data UI) and Data (Pillar 1)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Natural-language lead-generation surface backed by Amplemarket's 200M+-contact B2B database — describe a target ICP in plain text and AI returns matching contacts and accounts ranked by buying signals.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Sales' or the Data pillar's data-quality story, especially when claims about refresh cadence or bounce rate are central.

- Key features: Natural-language prompt-to-lead-list, 200M+ verified global contacts with 70M+ weekly refresh, Less than 3% average email bounce rate, 96.5% phone number accuracy, 30+ ICP filters, GDPR/CCPA/SOC 2 certified
- Strengths: Weekly database refresh; sub-3% bounce rate; NL prompt search ranks by buying-signal relevance
- Gaps: 200M+ contacts vs ZoomInfo's 500M; less verification depth than ZoomInfo's research-team-validated layer; narrower technographic/intent coverage
