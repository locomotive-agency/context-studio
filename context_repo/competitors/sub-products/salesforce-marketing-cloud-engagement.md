# Salesforce Marketing Cloud — Marketing Cloud Engagement (legacy MCE)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — B2C-leaning cross-channel)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Scale and automation engine for email, mobile, and web journeys; the legacy ExactTarget-derived B2C side of Marketing Cloud, sold as Pro+ ($2,000/mo) / Corporate+ ($5,500/mo) editions to layer agentic capabilities.

## Spotlight when
The article touches B2C cross-channel campaigns or contrasts ZoomInfo's B2B-only positioning against multi-purpose marketing clouds.

## Cite
- Key features: Bulk email at scale, SMS/push/web messaging, Journey Orchestration, Data Cloud credits, Agentforce on "+" editions
- Strengths: ExactTarget heritage, "+" editions add Agentforce, cross-channel B2C infrastructure
- Gaps: B2C-leaning, first-party-only data, $2,000+/mo entry pricing
- Proof quote: "Scale and automation engine for email, mobile, and web journeys" — https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/engagement/pricing
