# Relevance AI — AI Workforce

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (direct overlap on no-code play orchestration for RevOps / GTM engineers)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Visual builder that turns proven GTM playbooks into coordinated teams of AI agents (Workforces) that run end-to-end autonomously, triggered by events or signals.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses no-code play orchestration / RevOps automation surfaces. Workforce's pre-built SDR / Inbound / Outbound / Onboarding / Expansion library is the sharpest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Visual canvas with triggers/handoffs/routing/escalation, Multi-agent sequential context handoff, Pre-built workforces (SDR/Inbound/Outbound/Onboarding/Expansion), Agent-to-agent feedback, Event/signal triggers
- Strengths: True visual multi-agent design surface; pre-built workforces; continuous play fine-tuning
- Gaps: Builder-targeted — less NL UX than GTM Studio; no first-party data foundation; no standalone CI grounding
- Proof quote: "Turn proven GTM playbooks into coordinated teams of AI agents" — https://relevanceai.com/workforce
