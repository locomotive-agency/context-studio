# IntentData.io — Contact-Level Intent Data

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap — third-party buyer intent signals; both compete in the Forrester Wave intent-provider category)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Contact-level third-party buyer intent signals harvested from publicly available open-web content — named individual + job title attached to each intent action, rather than the account-only model used by typical providers.

## Spotlight when
The article compares intent-data providers (Bombora, ZoomInfo Intent, G2 Buyer Intent, IntentData.io). IntentData.io's contact-level granularity is the strongest differentiation angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Named contact + title per signal, Engaged with Competitor / Industry News / Influencer / Events / Followed & Follower / Key Related Contacts signals, account-level New Office/Product/Funding/Hiring/Position signals, GDPR+ICO open-web sourcing, Streams routing
- Strengths: Contact-level granularity; configurable signals (not black-box); GDPR/ICO open-web sourcing
- Gaps: No public dollar pricing; no proprietary workspace UI; smaller distribution than ZoomInfo Intent
- Proof quote: "Contact the right people at the right time" — https://www.intentdata.io/
