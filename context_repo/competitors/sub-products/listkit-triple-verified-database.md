# ListKit — ListKit Triple-Verified Database

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the verified-contact data foundation underneath the seller surface — credit-based, contract-free pricing as the wedge)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Searchable B2B contact database that lets users build hyper-targeted prospect lists via a plain-English AI prompt; every contact runs through a Triple-Verification engine (validity + domain integrity + recent-activity) before export, with 731M contacts plus daily-refreshed intent leads.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data tools for SMB / agency / founder-led outbound operators on price and simplicity. ListKit is the spotlight when the buyer wants triple-verified contacts with unlimited users at $97/mo entry — explicitly anti-ZoomInfo, anti-annual-contract.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Company Search natural-language prompt interface, 731M Triple-Verified email contacts, Triple-Verification engine (email validity + domain integrity + recent-activity check), Unlimited Intent Data with daily refresh, Triple-Verified phone numbers (mobile + direct dials), Unlimited users on every paid tier
- Strengths: Triple-Verification replaces external verifiers; unlimited users; plain-English AI list-building
