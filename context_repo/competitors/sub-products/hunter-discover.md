# Hunter — Hunter Discover

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent — Discover is a lighter prospecting surface than ZoomInfo Sales's full data + signal suite)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
B2B prospecting surface inside Hunter — searches its database for companies and leads matching an ICP, with an AI Assistant for natural-language queries and lightweight Signals for buying intent.

## Spotlight when
The article compares lightweight prospecting tools or freemium ZoomInfo alternatives. Discover's spotlight angle is "ICP search bundled with email discovery + verification + sequences" for SMB outbound teams.

## Cite
- Key features: B2B database with basic and advanced filters, AI Assistant in Discover (10-300 AI searches/month by tier), Proactive lead suggestions (15-900/month by tier), Saved-leads capacity from 100K (Free) to 30M (Scale), Tiered intent Signals
- Strengths: Bundled with Email Finder + Email Verifier; AI Assistant for natural-language queries; Free tier with basic filters
- Gaps: Smaller database; lacks technographics + deep intent + CI signals; weaker filter set than ZoomInfo Sales
