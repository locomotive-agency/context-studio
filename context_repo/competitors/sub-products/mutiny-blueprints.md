# Mutiny — Mutiny Blueprints

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — templates output ABM-style personalized assets that ZoomInfo Marketing would deliver via display + intent activation)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Pre-built template library for common GTM asset types (1:1 ABM landing pages, executive business cases, meeting recaps, pricing proposals, case study roundups, deal rooms, pitch decks) that Mutiny's agent applies brand and account data to.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights the breadth of asset formats covered by a single AI-agent surface — useful when contrasting Mutiny's template library against single-purpose landing-page or deal-room tools.

## Cite
- Key features: 1:1 ABM Landing Page, Executive Business Case, Meeting Recap, Pricing Proposal, Case Study Roundup, Event/Paid Landing Page, Deal Room, Competitive Comparison, Cold Outreach, Pitch Deck templates
- Strengths: Spans marketing AND sales asset types; standardizes brand fidelity; bridges ABM personalization and deal-room assets
- Gaps: Asset-format only — no campaign orchestration or media buying; no native scoring/routing; relies on parent agent + brand identity
