# Crunchbase — Crunchbase Predictive Intelligence (Pro + Business)

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the predictive private-company intelligence layer used for prospecting + CRM enrichment)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered predictions on private-company milestones (funding, growth, acquisitions, IPOs, closure, layoffs) over 4M+ private companies, with 40+ field auto-enrichment into Salesforce/HubSpot CRM records.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's Data pillar against signal-driven predictive intelligence vendors, especially when the angle is funding-event-driven outbound or private-company prospecting depth.

## Cite
- Key features: Search 4M+ private companies with NL search, AI-powered predictions on growth/funding/acquisitions/IPOs/closure/layoffs, Heat scores and growth scores, CRM integrations with 40+ field auto-enrichment, Auto-refresh Salesforce records, Tracker boards
- Strengths: Predictive private-company milestone signals; ~100K predictions/month from 80M+ users; same dataset across UI/API/Data Licensing
- Gaps: No verified personal contact data; private-company-focused (4M+) not full B2B universe; no conversation intelligence; no public pricing
