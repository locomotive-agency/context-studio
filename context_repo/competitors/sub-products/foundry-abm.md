# Foundry — Foundry ABM (Triblio)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on cross-channel ABM orchestration)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Account-based marketing orchestration platform (powered by Triblio) that runs cross-channel campaigns (display + social ads, website personalization, 1:1 Smart Pages, sales activation) tied to Foundry intent and CRM signals.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM orchestration platforms. Foundry ABM is the strongest spotlight when the angle is "ABM with proprietary intent + owned media inventory underneath" rather than data-database-led ABM.

## Cite
- Key features: Cross-channel orchestration (display/social/website/Smart Pages), AI account scoring on human-verified intent, Sales Acceleration in CRM, Intent-triggered buyer journeys, Salesforce/HubSpot/SugarCRM/Marketo integrations, G2 leader (Account-Based Orchestration)
- Strengths: Closed-loop intent + ABM + owned media; AI scoring on human-verified intent; Sales Acceleration in CRM
- Gaps: No 500M contact DB; no GTM Workspace/AI agent surface; gated pricing
