# GTM Buddy — Ask Buddy AI Co-Pilot

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the in-flow seller AI layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Deal-aware AI copilot embedded in CRM, email, calendar, Slack, and Teams that anticipates the rep's next move and surfaces battlecards, discovery questions, customer references, and objection handling without the rep typing a query.

## Spotlight when
The article compares in-flow seller copilots vs. ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI layer. The "anticipatory, push-mode" framing is the spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Anticipatory deal context, Embedded in CRM/Email/Slack/Teams/Calendar, Persona/deal-stage aware, Knowledge Gap Analysis, Battlecard injection
- Strengths: Push-mode (no query) UX; gap analytics across reps; shelfware alerts
- Gaps: First-party only (no third-party B2B data); no native CI transcripts; no MCP/agent surface
