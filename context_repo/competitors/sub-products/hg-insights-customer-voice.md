# HG Insights — HG Customer Voice (powered by TrustRadius)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on the brand-trust / advocacy proof surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Customer advocacy and review-syndication product that turns verified TrustRadius reviews into web widgets, sales proof, and SEO/SEM content. Reference management routes advocates for calls, quotes, and case studies.

## Spotlight when
The article touches on customer advocacy, TrustRadius proof points, or competitive takeout campaigns. Note the post-acquisition optics: TrustRadius badges ZoomInfo uses for credibility now sit under HG ownership.
