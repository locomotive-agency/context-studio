# Zoom AI Companion — Custom AI Companion

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the AI-agent layer — Custom AI Companion provides agent-building, third-party connectors, and CRM write-back, conceptually parallel to GTM Workspace's AI-agent layer but operating over Zoom's collaboration data, not ZoomInfo's B2B graph)

> **Disambiguation:** Zoom AI Companion is a product of Zoom Communications, Inc. (NASDAQ: ZM) — a separate company from ZoomInfo.

**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Paid add-on that extends AI Companion with company-specific knowledge collections, third-party app connectors (Salesforce, ServiceNow, Slack, Confluence, etc.), low-code agent building via Zoom AI Studio, the Agent2Agent protocol, and deep research mode.

## Spotlight when
The article compares AI-agent layers and CRM write-back capabilities. Custom AI's Salesforce opportunity-update connector and Agent2Agent collaboration with ServiceNow Now Assist are strong points to spotlight as parallels (not substitutes) to GTM Workspace's seller-AI surface.
