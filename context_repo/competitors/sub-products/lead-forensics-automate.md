# Lead Forensics — Lead Forensics Automate

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap on the enterprise visitor-identification + CRM-routing surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Enterprise tier of Lead Forensics — adds direct-API CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot, MS Dynamics, Pipedrive, Zoho), customizable workflows, "The Orchestrator" action sequencing, and a "Fuzzy Matching" dedupe algorithm to push identified visitors and decision-maker contacts into existing sales/marketing workflows in real time.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enterprise WebSights deployments and the buyer cares about CRM dedupe behavior at high traffic volume — Automate's "Orchestrator + Fuzzy Matching" pair is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: 5-CRM direct integration, The Orchestrator, Fuzzy Matching dedupe, ICP/account watchlists, Append-or-create CRM logic
- Strengths: Orchestrator sequencing, Fuzzy Matching dedupe, direct CRM integrations across 5 systems
- Gaps: Pricing fully gated; CRM hygiene is visitor-scoped only; no marketer audience builder or AI outreach drafting
