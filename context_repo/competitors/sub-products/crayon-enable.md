# Crayon — Crayon Enable (Battlecards & Compete Hub)

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap on the in-the-flow seller enablement surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Distributes competitive insights and battlecards into Salesforce, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Highspot, Seismic, Showpad, and SharePoint — with Compete Hub for territory-specific intel, Crayon Answers for in-Slack questions, and Sparks-powered automatic refresh.

## Spotlight when
The article compares battlecard / sales-enablement layers, or covers competitive-objection handling on calls. Enable's spotlight is the "battlecard inside Salesforce/Slack/Teams" pitch.
