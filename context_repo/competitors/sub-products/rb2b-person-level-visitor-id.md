# RB2B — RB2B Person-Level Website Visitor ID

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap on website visitor identification, executed at person level vs. company level)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Identifies the individual US-based people visiting your website (name, job title, LinkedIn URL, business email) in real time via a publisher network and pushes them to Slack, CRM, or other tools.

## Spotlight when
The article is comparing visitor-ID solutions or critiquing ZoomInfo WebSights' company-only resolution. RB2B's person-level US-traffic claim and Free Forever plan are the strongest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Person-level US visitor ID, 5-minute pixel install, Real-time Slack/Teams alerts, Hot Pages and Hot Leads ICP filtering, 70-80% US traffic identification on Pro+
- Strengths: Person-level (vs. company-only) resolution; Free Forever tier; Slack-first workflow
- Gaps: US-only person-level coverage; no outbound TAM database; no CI/intent/technographics
- Proof quote: "RB2B is the only FREE tool that provides person-level visitor information, so you don't have to guess who the decision maker is." — https://www.rb2b.com/learn/zoominfo-review
