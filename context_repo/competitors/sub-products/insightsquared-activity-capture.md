# InsightSquared — Activity Capture

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap — automated activity write-back addressing under-reporting and CRM hygiene gaps)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Automatically captures sales activities (emails, meetings, demos) from every team member touching a deal and writes them back to the CRM — covers the full buying team (AE + SE + overlays + CSMs) with a 15-month look-back window.

## Spotlight when
The article covers CRM data hygiene, automated activity logging, and the difference between activity-only capture (InsightSquared) and enrichment-on-write (ZoomInfo Operations).

## Cite
- Key features: Email/meeting/demo capture across buying group, CRM write-back for lead/opp/account/contact, multi-role capture (SEs/overlays/CSMs), 15-month look-back, single-threaded deal detection
- Strengths: Multi-role buying-team capture; 15-month baselining; single-threaded detection
- Gaps: No B2B data appended on write; no enrichment-on-write; no API/MCP
