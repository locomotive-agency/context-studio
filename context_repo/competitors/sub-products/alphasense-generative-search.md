# AlphaSense — Generative Search

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on the multi-source AI reasoning layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Multi-agent research system that reasons across qualitative insights, structured financial data, and the customer's internal knowledge to deliver cited, auditable answers in seconds against AlphaSense's 500M+ premium document library.

## Spotlight when
The article frames "AI reasoning over enterprise data" — Generative Search is the strongest analyst-side analogue to GTM Context Graph but feeds research deliverables, not GTM execution. Lean on the citation/auditability angle.
