# Fathom — Fathom AI Notetaker

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on transcription, summarization, and meeting intelligence)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI meeting recorder, transcriber, and summarizer for Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams calls — available with or without a meeting bot, and offered with a fully functional free tier.

## Spotlight when
The article compares Chorus against lightweight, self-serve notetaker alternatives. Fathom AI Notetaker is the strongest spotlight when the angle is "free, fast, low-setup conversation capture" rather than enterprise revenue-intelligence depth.

## Cite
- Key features: Unlimited recordings + transcriptions, Bot-free or bot capture, Instant AI summaries, AI action items + follow-up email drafts, Word-perfect transcripts, Custom transcription dictionaries, Clips/playlists/search, Ask Fathom Q&A
- Strengths: Fully functional free tier; bot-free capture; ~5-minute self-serve onboarding
- Gaps: No multi-call analytics depth; no contact data/intent/workflow surface; JS-rendered pricing
