# People.ai — Methodology Enforcement (MEDDPICC)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on seller deal-execution layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Analyzes deal context against the customer's chosen sales methodology (MEDDPICC and others) to surface qualification-stage gaps and next-best actions — without requiring reps to fill out methodology forms.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on AI-driven sales methodology enforcement or deal-coaching layers — the no-rep-data-entry framing distinguishes People.ai from manual scorecard tooling.

## Cite
- Key features: MEDDPICC-and-others scoring, behind-the-scenes (no rep data entry), Salesforce-embedded scorecards, gap surfacing
- Strengths: Methodology without forced form-fills; activity-grounded scoring; new-seller playbook framing
- Gaps: Enterprise-only; no cross-methodology comparison toolkit; less mature than enablement specialists
