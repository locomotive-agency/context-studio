# Spekit — GTM Knowledge Engine

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — content / coaching context that feeds the seller-execution surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Governed, AI-powered content management system that centralizes GTM content (products, messaging, plays, competitive intel) and feeds it into every rep-facing surface (AI Sidekick, Learning Paths, Deal Rooms).

## Spotlight when
Articles position GTM enablement content governance as a missing layer in ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace, or compare AI-content-generation alternatives for revenue enablement.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Content Builder with auto-update; Governance Dashboard; Auto-Tagging; Drive / SharePoint / Confluence connectors; Knowledge Checks; Learning Paths; closed-won analytics
- Strengths: Auto-updating Speks; 90% content-creation-time reduction; closed-won analytics
- Gaps: Adjacent category (no head-to-head ZI product); no data foundation; standalone CMS depth
- Proof quote: "Most enablement platforms still center on portals, file storage, and separate training systems. Spekit is built around execution." — https://www.spekit.com/
