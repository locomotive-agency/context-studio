# Revenue Grid — Inbox Sidebar

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap — Inbox Sidebar is a Salesforce-edit-from-inbox surface that touches data quality and CRM hygiene)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
An Outlook/Gmail sidebar that lets reps view, edit, and manage Salesforce data and activities without leaving the inbox.

## Spotlight when
The article covers Salesforce inbox alternatives (vs. Salesforce Inbox / Cirrus / Match My Email). Inbox Sidebar's edit-from-inbox + Magic Pixel + "Book me" combo is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Email-to-Salesforce save, Salesforce templates in inbox, Edit Salesforce objects from sidebar, Magic Pixel open/click alerts, "Book me" availability share
- Strengths: Edit Salesforce objects from inbox; Magic Pixel real-time alerts; built-in scheduling
- Gaps: Salesforce-only; no analytics or forecasting in sidebar; engagement scoring depth lags Outreach/Salesloft
- Proof quote: "Create and edit Salesforce objects directly in the sidebar" — https://revenuegrid.com/product/email-sidebar
