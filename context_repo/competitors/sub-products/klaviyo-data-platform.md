# Klaviyo — Klaviyo Data Platform (KDP)

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on the unified-profile-with-predictive-AI shape; B2C consumer-level vs ZI's B2B account/contact level)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Built-in CDP that unifies behavioral, transactional, and engagement data into one consumer profile, with identity resolution and predictive AI (churn risk, CLV, next-order date) — activated directly inside Klaviyo Marketing and Service.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CDP architectures (built-in vs bolt-on), or contrasts Klaviyo's consumer-level profile graph with ZoomInfo's B2B account/contact GTM Context Graph.

## Cite
- Key features: Unified profiles, 350+ integrations, identity resolution, predictive AI (churn/CLV/next order), custom objects, Advanced KDP add-on with Snowflake sync
- Strengths: Built-in CDP (no third-party stitch); predictive AI baked in; warehouse syncing on Advanced
- Gaps: B2C consumer level only; no firmographics/technographics/intent; tangential to ZI Context Graph
- Proof quote: "Unifies behavioral, transactional, and engagement data into a single real-time customer profile with identity resolution and predictive AI baked in" — https://www.klaviyo.com/solutions/customer-data-platform
