# Datanyze — Datanyze Chrome Extension

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (no overlap — Datanyze is a ZoomInfo-owned subsidiary; flagged for manual review)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Browser extension that reveals business email, direct dial, and mobile numbers directly on LinkedIn profiles and company websites — sold as a credit-based single-seat plan to individual SDRs and SMB reps.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to acknowledge ZoomInfo's own SMB / Chrome-extension lane (Datanyze is a ZoomInfo-owned subsidiary) when comparing budget LinkedIn-prospecting tools. Lead with the ownership disclosure rather than a head-to-head pitch.

## Cite
- Key features: LinkedIn email/direct-dial/mobile reveal in-browser, Chrome-native contact pull on company websites, Profile export from Chrome, Credit-based reveal model, Data guarantee refunds for inaccurate records
- Strengths: Browser-native single-seat workflow with public credit pricing; free 10-leads-on-us trial; $21–29/mo entry price for individual SMB sellers
- Gaps: No team/enterprise tier; no AI outreach or workspace; no conversation intelligence, GTM Workspace, or APIs/MCP
