# Contify — Contify Account & Sales Intelligence

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on account triggers, battlecards, and competitive alerts)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Sales-team-targeted use of the M&CI platform — auto-updated battlecards, key-account triggers, competitor pricing alerts, and win/loss intelligence — feeding sellers competitive context that wraps the ZoomInfo Sales account view.

## Spotlight when
The article compares trigger/alert layers on top of B2B contact data, or argues for battlecard-shaped intelligence. Contify's spotlight angle is the "auto-updated battlecard from external + internal sources" pitch.

## Cite
- Key features: Auto-updated battlecards, Key-account triggers, Pricing-change alerts, Win/loss tracking, Sales-trigger opportunities
- Strengths: Battlecards refresh from news + Gong/Fireflies; pricing monitoring; bundled with the M&CI surface
- Gaps: No contact data to act on triggers; no prospecting UI; no native engagement layer
- Proof quote: "Track competitor product developments, pricing changes, and customer reviews in real time" — https://www.contify.com/functions/account-intelligence-sales-team/
