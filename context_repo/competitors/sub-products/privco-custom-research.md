# PrivCo — Custom Research / Analyst Reports

**Competes with:** Data as a Service (adjacent overlap — analyst-led custom research, not continuous data delivery)

**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
On-request engagements where PrivCo analysts deliver customized reports on specific companies or market segments — fast turnaround within days, not weeks.

## Spotlight when
The article contrasts analyst-led research against continuous data delivery, or surveys boutique research options for PE / corp-dev teams.

## Cite
- Key features: Custom reports on companies / market segments, direct analyst team engagement, fast turnaround (days)
- Strengths: Hands-on analyst engagement; tailored research; not just self-serve
- Gaps: Service-led — doesn't scale programmatically; no published pricing; not a continuous DaaS substitute
- Proof quote: "Direct work with PrivCo's research analyst team" — https://www.privco.com/
