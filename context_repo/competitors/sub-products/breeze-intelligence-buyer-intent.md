# Breeze Intelligence — Breeze Intelligence — Buyer Intent (Reveal)

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on the website-visitor-to-buying-signal layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Identifies which companies visit a customer's website using IP intelligence and surfaces ICP-matched, in-market accounts plus pricing-page visit signals — feeds the result directly into HubSpot lists, workflows, and ABM activation.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's Intent Data product or website-visitor-to-buying-signal workflows for buyers already on HubSpot.

## Cite
- Key features: IP intelligence to identify companies visiting the website, Visitor dashboard highlighting ICP-matched accounts, Pricing-page visit detection and visit frequency, Filter by channel source, Determine likely product/solution interest based on engagement, Target Markets audience builder for ABM activation
- Strengths: Native to HubSpot Smart CRM; bundled with rest of Breeze Intelligence; Target Markets converts intent into ABM ad audiences
- Gaps: Gated behind paid HubSpot Hub; no third-party intent network at ZoomInfo's depth; no conversation intelligence; no stand-alone licensing
