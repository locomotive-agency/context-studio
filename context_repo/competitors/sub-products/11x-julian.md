# 11x — Julian

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Chat (direct overlap on inbound qualification + meeting booking; adjacent to Chorus on operational transcripts)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI voice agent that answers inbound calls in real time, qualifies prospects on the conversation, and books meetings or transfers to a rep with full context — extending speed-to-lead from minutes to seconds.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses inbound qualification, speed-to-lead, or 24/7 call coverage — Julian's voice-first channel is the differentiator vs. ZoomInfo Chat (text-led) and most CI tools (post-call analytics).

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time inbound call answering, conversational qualification + meeting booking, context-rich rep routing, sub-minute speed-to-lead, call deliverability + number management
- Strengths: Voice as a first-class channel; 24/7 live qualification; CRM-synced transcripts
- Gaps: Transcripts not coaching-grade; no scorecards or forecasting; voice-led not chat-led
- Proof quote: "Julian picks up calls in real time, qualifies prospects, and books meetings or transfers to a rep with full context" — https://www.11x.ai/
