# People.ai — Backstory Revenue Answers Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (direct overlap on first-party activity / signal layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Captures every customer-facing revenue activity automatically across email, meetings, calls, and chat, then matches each interaction to the right Salesforce/Dynamics/Oracle account and opportunity to answer revenue-leader questions about deal risk, pipeline health, and forecast.

## Spotlight when
The article frames first-party activity / signal layers — Backstory's nine-year activity-graph dataset and 49 matching patents are the strongest spotlight angle, especially against pure call-recording or CRM-derived tools.

## Cite
- Key features: Automated activity capture across email/calendar/Zoom/Teams/Slack, AI-powered activity-to-deal matching, engagement signals, multi-CRM support, 2-4 week deploy
- Strengths: Nine-year proprietary activity dataset; multi-CRM simultaneous; "Revenue Answers" UX vs dashboards
- Gaps: No third-party contact data; no outbound surface; no native call recording
- Proof quote: "Other tools record activity. Backstory drives revenue." — https://www.people.ai/
