# Hunter — Hunter Email Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on email-contact discovery)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Returns a verified professional email address given a name + company/domain — Hunter's flagship product, indexed against 100M+ public professional emails with public-source attribution per record.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email-discovery point tools versus ZoomInfo's full B2B data foundation. Hunter's spotlight angle is "transparent public-source attribution + free tier" versus enterprise data depth and signal coverage.

## Cite
- Key features: 100M+ public professional email database, Auto-verification with confidence score, Public-source attribution and discovery dates, Bulk Email Finder for CSV uploads, Browser extensions and Google Sheets add-on, Free 50 searches/month plan
- Strengths: Public-source attribution per email; free 50/month tier; failed lookups free
- Gaps: Email-only; smaller volume than ZoomInfo; web-scraped rather than contributor-network-fed
