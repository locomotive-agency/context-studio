# G2 Buyer Intent — G2 Buyer Intent + ZoomInfo Integration

**Competes with:** Intent Data (adjacent — partnership integration that augments rather than substitutes ZoomInfo's intent layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Bidirectional integration that pipes G2 Buyer Intent signals into ZoomInfo Sales and ZoomInfo Marketing for daily-refresh activation, while surfacing ZoomInfo Suggested Contacts inside my.G2 so sellers can match high-intent G2 accounts to ZoomInfo contact data and ABM/marketing automation triggers.

## Spotlight when
The article frames G2 as a complementary intent partner rather than a competitor — this integration is the strongest evidence that G2 and ZoomInfo are sold side-by-side rather than head-to-head.

## Cite
- Key features: Daily G2 Buyer Intent inside ZI Sales/Marketing, ZI firmographics/technographics layered on G2 intent, ZoomInfo Suggested Contacts in my.G2 (50 free/user/month for 6 months), ABM audience triggers, Requires both licenses
- Strengths: Bidirectional partnership ("two intent ecosystems in a single platform"); G2 intent matched to ZI contacts inline; pre-built ABM activation
- Gaps: Requires both vendors' contracts; no standalone pricing; complementary by design
