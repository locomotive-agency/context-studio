# Melissa — Data Quality Suite / Personator Consumer

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on CRM data hygiene)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
End-to-end data-quality workflow — profile, cleanse, standardize, verify, match/dedupe, and enrich names, addresses, phones, and emails inside Salesforce, Dynamics, or batch lists, with golden-record consolidation via MatchUp.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM data-hygiene tools or data-quality platforms vs. ZoomInfo Operations. Melissa's Data Quality Suite is the strongest direct overlap and the natural head-to-head call-out when a buyer is evaluating CRM cleanse/dedupe/standardize tooling.

## Cite
- Key features: Profiling, ML-based cleansing & standardization, Name Verification, Phone Verification (mobile + landline), Email Verification, MatchUp dedupe, Personator Consumer Cloud API
- Strengths: Postal-authority-certified data; published per-API pricing; golden-record dedupe across all four contact attributes
- Gaps: No B2B contact graph or intent; no seller/CRM-write-back automation breadth; no technographics
- Proof quote: "Profile, cleanse, verify, match/dedupe, and enrich — the full-spectrum DQ workflow" — https://www.melissa.com/data-quality
