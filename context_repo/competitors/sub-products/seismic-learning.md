# Seismic — Seismic Learning

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on the coaching / training surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-powered sales training and coaching software (formerly Lessonly) that onboards, upskills, and improves rep performance with interactive courses, AI role-plays, knowledge checks, and AI-driven assessments.

## Spotlight when
The article compares coaching/training depth between sales-enablement platforms and conversation-intelligence-anchored coaching. Strongest spotlight is the AI role-play + ramp-time reduction story.

## Cite
- Key features: Interactive courses, AI role-plays, personalized coaching plans, AI-driven assessments, video + manager feedback
- Strengths: Lessonly heritage; AI role-play tied to coaching + performance; reported 34-58% ramp-time reductions
- Gaps: Scenario-driven not transcript-grounded; no real-call capture surface; no CRM/conversation/intent fusion
- Proof quote: "AI-powered role-plays simulating real-life scenarios" — https://www.seismic.com/platform/learning/
