# Salesforce Sales Cloud — Sales Cloud Core (CRM)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — Salesforce is CRM-of-record, complementary in most cases; only competitive at the AI/agent layer via Agentforce)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
The dominant CRM platform — opportunity + lead + account management, workflow automation, customizable objects + reports, AppExchange ecosystem.

## Spotlight when
The article references Salesforce as the seller's CRM. Frame ZoomInfo as the data + intelligence layer that powers Salesforce, not as a CRM replacement. Only frame Salesforce as a head-to-head competitor when the article is specifically about Agentforce or AI agents.

## Cite
- Key features: CRM platform of record, opportunity/lead/account management, Flow workflow automation, customizable reports, AppExchange ecosystem
- Strengths: Dominant CRM market share; AppExchange ecosystem; customization flexibility
- Gaps: Salesforce is the CRM, not a data + intelligence platform — articles should frame ZoomInfo as the layer above
- Proof quote: "The world's #1 AI CRM." — https://www.salesforce.com/sales/
