# Foundry ABM — Foundry Sales Acceleration

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller surface — narrower scope than ZI's full prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted-outreach front-end)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Sales-side product layered on the Triblio ABM platform that surfaces best contacts, account research, and next-best actions for sellers already using Foundry Intent — delivered inside their CRM or sales engagement tool.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM-to-sales activation tools or how marketing-sourced intent reaches sellers — Foundry Sales Acceleration's strongest spotlight angle is "intent insights pushed directly into Salesloft / Outreach without a new UI."

## Cite
- Key features: Surfaces top accounts, contacts and next-best-actions to sales reps; delivers Foundry Intent insights directly into the rep's CRM; pushes best contacts into Salesloft or Outreach; automated account research; buying-signal alerts at the territory level
- Strengths: Pushes Foundry Intent signals directly into Salesloft/Outreach; automated account research replaces manual prep; tied to the same first-party publisher intent powering the ABM platform
- Gaps: Narrower than a full GTM Workspace; relies on Foundry Intent license; no conversation intelligence or call coaching layer
