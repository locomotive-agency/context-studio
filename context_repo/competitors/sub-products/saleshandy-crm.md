# Saleshandy — Saleshandy CRM

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on CRM/record management)
**Spotlight rank:** 6

## What it does
Lightweight built-in CRM with Kanban and list views to manage prospects and deals without leaving Saleshandy.

## Spotlight when
The article covers SMB stack consolidation where the buyer wants to skip a Salesforce/HubSpot seat. Less relevant when comparing to ZoomInfo Operations' enterprise CRM data-quality posture.

## Cite
- Key features: Unlimited prospects, Kanban + list views, custom pipelines/stages, free Starter
- Strengths: No-extra-vendor CRM in the outbound stack; free entry
- Gaps: Light vs. Salesforce/HubSpot; no dedup/standardization; no first-party context graph fusion
- Proof quote: "Free for limited time" — https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing
