# Chili Piper — Distro

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on the lead-routing layer of RevOps tooling)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Salesforce-native lead routing engine that distributes any standard or custom Salesforce object using granular AND/OR logic, lead-to-account matching, SLAs, and weighted round-robin. Designed for RevOps and SalesOps teams.

## Spotlight when
The article compares lead-routing engines, ZoomInfo Operations alternatives, or RevOps tooling for Salesforce-first orgs. Distro is the strongest spotlight when "Salesforce-native two-way sync for routing" is the buyer requirement.

## Cite
- Key features: Routes any Salesforce object, lead-to-account matching with exact + fuzzy match, duplicate matching/merging, SLA enforcement, multi-decision workflow builder with preview, automated vacation management
- Strengths: Reads AND writes to Salesforce; granular weighting/capping; calendar-aware vacation auto-removal
- Gaps: Salesforce-only (no HubSpot CRM); no proprietary data layer; routing-engine scope only
- Proof quote: "the only solution that both reads and writes to Salesforce" — https://www.chilipiper.com/products/distro
