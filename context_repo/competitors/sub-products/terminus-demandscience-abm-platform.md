# Terminus (DemandScience) — Terminus ABM Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap — ABM/ABX category)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Account-based marketing platform that helps B2B teams identify, prioritize and engage best-fit accounts across native channels including ads, chat, web personalization, and email signature.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM platforms. Terminus's strongest spotlight angle is the 17-quarter G2 leadership and the TEAM framework as the original ABM playbook.
