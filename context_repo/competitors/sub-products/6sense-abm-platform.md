# 6sense — 6sense ABM Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-driven ABM platform that identifies in-market accounts, predicts buying intent, orchestrates campaigns across channels, and surfaces decision-makers — sold as the marketer's nerve center for ABM motion.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM platforms or discusses orchestrating ABM advertising — 6sense's display/retargeting orchestration is the depth differentiator. Pair the spotlight with ZoomInfo Marketing as the natural ZoomInfo product to lean into.

## Cite
- Key features: In-market account identification, multi-channel orchestration (display + LinkedIn + email), anonymous buyer-journey ID, real-time buying signals, native Salesforce/HubSpot/Marketo integrations
- Strengths: Native ABM advertising channel; AI predictive funnel-stage scoring; Forrester Wave Leader (Q1 2026)
- Gaps: No conversation intelligence; no MCP/agent ecosystem; ABM tier pricing is quote-based
- Proof quote: "Connect, sync, and activate every channel from one canvas." — https://6sense.com/platform/account-based-marketing
