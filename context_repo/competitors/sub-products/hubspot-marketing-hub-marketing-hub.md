# HubSpot Marketing Hub — HubSpot Marketing Hub

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on the marketing automation + ABM surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered marketing automation suite that helps marketers attract, convert, and engage high-intent visitors via forms, email, social, ads, and reporting on top of HubSpot's Smart CRM.

## Spotlight when
The article compares marketing automation platforms or SMB/mid-market all-in-one tools. The Smart CRM unification + Breeze AI layer is the spotlight — note ZoomInfo Marketing's stronger enterprise ABM positioning.
