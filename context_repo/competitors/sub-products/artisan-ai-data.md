# Artisan AI — Artisan B2B Data + Watchtower

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on prospecting data + visitor de-anonymization workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Built-in B2B contact database (250M-300M contacts, 22+ enrichment sources) plus Watchtower — Artisan's website visitor de-anonymization product that resolves anonymous companies and people including email addresses.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Sales / WebSights de-anonymization, especially when contrasted against AI-SDR vendors that bundle data + visitor ID at SMB price points.

## Cite
- Key features: 250M+ verified B2B contacts across 200+ countries, 300M+ contacts including ecommerce/local businesses, Enrichment from 22+ data sources, Real-time intent signals (funding rounds/leadership hires), Watchtower website visitor de-anonymization with email-level resolution, AI-refreshed via job-change and funding signals
- Strengths: Watchtower visitor ID included on every plan; local-business / region-level data; public credit-based pricing with free trial
- Gaps: Smaller absolute scale than ZoomInfo's 500M; less verification depth; weaker technographic intent; no public API or MCP
- Proof quote: "Artisan includes real-time intent signal scraping and web visitor identification in all its plans, while ZoomInfo's intent and visitor-ID products are locked away as add-ons." — https://www.artisan.co/blog/zoominfo-review
