# Unify GTM — Unify GTM Signal Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-execution surface — signal-based warm outbound + AI agents synthesizing context per account)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Signal-based outbound platform aggregating 25+ intent signals (website intent, champion tracking, new hires, social) and waterfall-enriched contacts from 30+ sources, with AI Agents featuring an Observation Model and transparent reasoning traces.

## Spotlight when
The article compares signal-based GTM platforms for warm outbound. Unify is the spotlight when the buyer is a high-growth scale-up (Perplexity / Cursor / Together AI archetype) who wants 25+ aggregated signals plus transparent AI agents — explicitly anti-cold-outbound.

## Cite
- Key features: 25+ intent signals (website intent person + company, AI Infinity Signals, champion tracking, new hires, social), Waterfall enrichment across 30+ verified data sources, Built-in third-party feeds (6sense, Clearbit, G2, Bombora-style email intent), AI Agents with Observation Model that constantly gathers context, Transparent reasoning trace ("follow how Agents gather information"), Unify Browser Extension to source and enrich from web/CRM
- Strengths: 25+ signals with no API-key management; transparent AI agent reasoning; charged only on successful enrichment hits
- Gaps: Data layer is third-party-aggregated (not proprietary); no CI; no native dialer
- Proof quote: "The future of outbound … Intent data and outbound action were meant to be together." — https://www.unifygtm.com/
