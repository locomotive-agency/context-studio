# Salesforce Sales Cloud — Salesforce Data Cloud

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent — first-party-data focused, complementary to ZoomInfo's third-party verified-data layer); secondary overlap with Data (Pillar 1)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Customer Data Platform unifying first-party customer data across Salesforce Clouds with identity resolution and activation.

## Spotlight when
The article is about CDPs, customer data unification, or first-party-data activation. Frame ZoomInfo as the third-party B2B verified-data layer that complements Data Cloud's first-party CDP.

## Cite
- Key features: CDP unifying first-party data, identity resolution + segmentation, activation across Salesforce Clouds, real-time sync
- Strengths: Unified Salesforce-native data; identity resolution at scale
- Gaps: First-party only; no verified B2B contact data; relies on third-party data imports (where ZoomInfo plays)
- Proof quote: "The CDP that unifies all your customer data." — https://www.salesforce.com/data/
