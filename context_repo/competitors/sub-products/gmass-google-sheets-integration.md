# GMass — GMass Google Sheets Integration

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on send-side audience plumbing)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Connects a live Google Sheet to a GMass campaign — each column becomes a merge field and tracking columns (Opened, Clicked, Replied, Bounced) auto-write back into the sheet. Recurring schedules can monitor a sheet for new rows.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses RevOps / spreadsheet-driven outbound workflows that don't sit in a CRM. The two-way Sheets sync is unique vs. ZoomInfo's CRM-anchored Workspace flow.

## Cite
- Key features: Live Sheets read, Spreadsheet filtering, Recurring campaigns on new rows, Auto-write tracking columns, Dynamic lists
- Strengths: Two-way live sheet sync; in-sheet metrics; recurring sends from row triggers
- Gaps: No CRM write-back beyond Sheets; Google Sheets only; no enrichment of sheet rows
