# Momentum — Workflow Automation (Slack Deal Rooms + Notifications)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap — Slack-native seller execution surface vs. ZI's seller workspace)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
No-code workflow builder that turns Slack into the GTM command center — auto-creates Deal Rooms per opportunity, routes Salesforce alerts as Smart Notifications, and lets reps approve / update Salesforce directly from Slack buttons.

## Spotlight when
The article compares Slack-native seller execution surfaces or GTM workflow builders. This sub-product is the strongest seller-execution call-out alongside Deal Execution Agent.
