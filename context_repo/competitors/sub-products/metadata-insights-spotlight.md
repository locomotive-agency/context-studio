# Metadata — Insights / Spotlight

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on attribution / pipeline reporting)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Reporting and attribution layer that ties paid ad spend to CRM pipeline and revenue across campaigns, channels, audiences, and offers — with bi-directional Salesforce / HubSpot sync as the source of truth.

## Spotlight when
The article compares marketing attribution / reporting capabilities. Insights/Spotlight is the natural call-out when buyers want closed-loop pipeline attribution paired with paid-campaign execution.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-dimensional reporting, Bi-directional CRM sync, First-impression-to-closed-won attribution, Spotlight reporting product
- Strengths: CRM as source of truth; closed-loop attribution; multi-dimensional reporting
- Gaps: Reporting confined to Metadata's own campaigns; no cross-system context graph; limited outside platform use
- Proof quote: "CRM as source of truth — attribution from first impression to closed-won deal" — https://metadata.io/
