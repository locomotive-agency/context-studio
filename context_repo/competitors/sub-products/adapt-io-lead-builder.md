# Adapt.io — Adapt Lead Builder (Adapt Data OS)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the seller-facing prospecting UI + list-building workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Lead discovery and list-building over a 250M+ verified B2B contact database, accessed through a web app, Chrome extension, and CRM export with a proprietary 3,000+ tech-sector taxonomy.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo Sales against budget-friendly SMB prospecting tools, or specifically calls out tech-sector targeting precision and self-serve credit-based pricing.

## Cite
- Key features: Adapt Tech Sector taxonomy across 3,000+ tech sub-sectors, Funding Insights for recently funded accounts, Job Change Alerts, Chrome extension for LinkedIn and company websites, Advanced filters across title/department/level/revenue/geography, One-click CRM export
- Strengths: Proprietary 3,000+ tech-sector taxonomy more granular than typical SIC/NAICS filtering; public credit-based pricing with 7-day free trial; SMB-friendly self-serve motion with stated 24-hour implementation
- Gaps: Only 250M+ contacts vs ZoomInfo's larger contact universe; no GTM Context Graph or unified buyer-signal layer; no third-party intent data; daily contact caps until Custom tier
- Proof quote: "B2B Lead Intelligence Platform — Prospecting made easy with our chrome extension. Find verified contact data directly from LinkedIn or the company website. Trusted by 500K+ users globally." — https://www.adapt.io/
