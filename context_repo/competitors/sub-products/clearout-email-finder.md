# Clearout — Email Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — discovery, not a true contact universe)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Discovers verified B2B email addresses from a name + company/domain input, with AI confidence scoring and pre-verification before delivery.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email-finder tools, prospect-discovery workflows, or "find + verify in one step" plays for outbound teams. Strongest spotlight when the buyer wants pre-verified results without double-charging.

## Cite
- Key features: Single + bulk email finding, AI confidence scoring, pre-verification at discovery, Smart Queue + Parallel Finder Engine, Autocomplete API for company-to-domain, role-based discovery
- Strengths: No double-charge for verification; Smart Queue for high-volume API; multi-region/language
- Gaps: Discovery from inputs only (no proprietary universe); no org charts/technographics/intent; smaller scale than ZoomInfo
- Proof quote: "Pre-verification at discovery so emails aren't double-charged for verification" — https://clearout.io/email-finder/
