# Weflow — Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap — sales call recording, transcription, AI summaries, coaching)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls in 96+ languages, then pushes structured insights directly into Salesforce fields (MEDDIC, next steps, close dates, competitor mentions) — flat $39/user/mo with unlimited recordings.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation intelligence platforms or focuses on Salesforce-native CI alternatives to Chorus/Gong. Lean on auto-writes-to-Salesforce-fields and methodology-aware capture.

## Cite
- Key features: Recording + transcription on Zoom/Teams/Meet (96+ languages), AI summaries + coaching insights, Automatic Salesforce field updates (MEDDIC/next steps/close dates), Clips/playlists/topics with retroactive crawl, Coaching scorecards (1–5), Unlimited recordings at flat rate
- Strengths: Auto-writes insights to Salesforce fields; methodology-aware capture; 96+ language at flat rate
