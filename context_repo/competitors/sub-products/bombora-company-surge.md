# Bombora — Bombora Company Surge Intent

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on the intent layer of the GTM Context Graph)

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Flags B2B accounts actively researching topics relevant to a customer's products, derived from Bombora's proprietary Data Co-op of 200+ publishers and 5,500+ B2B media sites.

## Spotlight when
The article compares pure-play intent data sources or argues for / against the "data company, not a platform" pattern. Forrester's "gold standard" framing is Company Surge's strongest spotlight angle.
