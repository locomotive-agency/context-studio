# Data Axle — Data Axle Demand Generation + Audience360

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on the marketer-facing ABM + demand-gen activation surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Managed B2B demand-gen service (Content Syndication, BANT/HQL leads, programmatic display, ABM lists with buying-group insights) plus Audience360 — Data Axle's identity-unification + activation layer that pushes 0–3rd party data into cloud platforms, DMPs/DSPs, MarTech, and AdTech.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Marketing for ABM / demand-gen audiences, especially when the angle is managed services + lead-replacement guarantees or 0–3rd-party identity unification.
