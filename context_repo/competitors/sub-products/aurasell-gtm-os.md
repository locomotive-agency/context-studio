# Aurasell — Aurasell GTM OS (overlay on existing CRM)

**Competes with:** CRM Enrichment (direct overlap on the agentic-intelligence + data-enrichment surface that sits on top of Salesforce/HubSpot)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Augments the customer's existing Salesforce or HubSpot CRM with an AI-powered GTM operating system — same Aurasell agentic intelligence and unified data layer, but applied on top of the existing CRM rather than replacing it.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's CRM Enrichment / Operations layer for buyers who can't migrate off Salesforce or HubSpot, especially when the angle is AI-native overlays.

## Cite
- Key features: Augments Salesforce/HubSpot rather than replacing them, Same agentic intelligence + unified data layer applied on top of existing CRM, Whisper conversational intelligence on CRM activity, Custom AI workflow builder (no-code), AI Smart Columns for enrichment, Monte Carlo forecasting
- Strengths: Sits as overlay on existing CRM — no migration required; "World's First AI-Native OS to Run GTM Workflows on Any CRM"; bundled CPQ + Whisper + forecasting
- Gaps: Less data-quality depth than ZoomInfo Operations (no dedup/match/routing rules engine surfaced); no public API or MCP; gated Mid-market/Enterprise pricing
