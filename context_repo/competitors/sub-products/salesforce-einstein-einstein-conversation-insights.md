# Salesforce Einstein — Einstein Conversation Insights

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on conversation-intelligence transcription + signal extraction)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Captures and transcribes sales calls, emails, and meetings, then flags objections, pricing concerns, competitor mentions, and next steps directly on the Salesforce opportunity record — the Salesforce-native answer to Chorus.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses CRM-native CI vs standalone CI platforms — Einstein Conversation Insights is the canonical Salesforce-bundled alternative to Chorus.

## Cite
- Key features: Automatic transcripts, Call Insights, custom keyword tracking, NL search, coaching analytics, Slack recaps, Call Trend Dashboard
- Strengths: Native CRM updates, Momentum-powered, bundled with Enterprise+
- Gaps: No third-party signals, Salesforce-only, less mature than Gong-class
