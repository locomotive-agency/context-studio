# AdRoll ABM — Cross-Channel Advertising (DSP)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent — programmatic media-buying layer ZoomInfo doesn't own)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-powered cross-channel demand-side platform sold alongside AdRoll ABM — runs full-funnel awareness, retargeting, and conversion campaigns across the open web and social in self-service or managed-service tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses ad-execution infrastructure underneath ABM. Spotlight only when the comparison frame is "owned DSP vs. ad-module on a data platform."

## Cite
- Key features: Single multi-channel campaign + shared bid optimization, AI audience targeting (lookalike/demo/interest/contextual/first-party), brand-safe inventory, ad-format breadth, self-service or managed
- Strengths: Owned DSP infrastructure; self-service or managed; 5X claimed ROAS
- Gaps: Programmatic media-buying — outside ZI's footprint; standalone DSP, not a ZI competitor; no B2B contact graph
- Proof quote: "Average 5X return on ad spend" — https://www.adroll.com/solutions/cross-channel-advertising
