# ZeroBounce — ZeroBounce AI Email Scoring (Activity Data)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on engagement signals — but ZeroBounce reports email activity recency only, not topic-level Intent Data or technographics)

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Appends engagement metadata — last-known activity period, activity type (open/click/send), activity count, and activity location — to verified email addresses so senders can prioritize "Gold Recipients" who recently engaged.

## Spotlight when
The article distinguishes engagement-recency scoring from true topical intent. Activity Data is best spotlighted as a complementary signal that does not replace ZoomInfo's intent platform — strong engagement-recency story, but no topic surge or technographic context.

## Cite
- Key features: Last-known activity period (30/60/90/180/365/365+ days), Activity type (opens, clicks, sends), Number of activities count, Activity location (inbox vs. web), Bulk append or real-time API, Gold Recipients identification
- Strengths: Adds engagement recency to verified emails to prioritize most-active contacts; bulk-append or real-time API; "Gold Recipients" segmentation
- Gaps: Engagement-only (no topic-level intent or technographic signals); no firmographic or persona data; not a substitute for a B2B intent platform
