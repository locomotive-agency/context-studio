# Sprout Social — Sprout Social Listening

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (none — public social listening vs. B2B account intent)

## What it does

## Spotlight when
The article needs to clarify "intent" terminology — Sprout Listening surfaces public-conversation brand signals, not B2B in-market account intent. Useful for distinguishing brand-health from buyer-stage signals.
