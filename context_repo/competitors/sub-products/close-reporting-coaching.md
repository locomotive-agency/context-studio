# Close — Reporting & Coaching

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap — basic CI value via call recording + AI summaries, not full Chorus depth)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Sales dashboards, leaderboards, and call coaching for individual and team performance — including unlimited call recording on the Scale plan.

## Spotlight when
The article compares basic conversation intelligence inside SMB CRMs or Chorus alternatives at low budget. Strongest spotlight when the buyer wants call recording + AI summaries bundled with their CRM rather than as a standalone CI tool.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time dashboards, activity-based reporting, opportunity funnel reports, call recording + coaching (unlimited on Scale), email sequence reporting
- Strengths: Unlimited call recording on Scale; native pipeline reporting; AI summaries via Chloe
- Gaps: Operational reporting only (no "why deals move"); less mature than Chorus/Gong; no predictive forecasting
- Proof quote: "Real-time dashboards and pipeline tracking" — https://close.com/
