# IBM InfoSphere QualityStage — IBM InfoSphere QualityStage

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on dedup / standardization mechanics, but tangential on data assets — IBM cleanses any data, ZoomInfo cleanses + enriches B2B GTM data)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Enterprise data-cleansing module that investigates, cleanses, standardizes, matches, and survives records to support governance, MDM, BI, data warehousing, and application migration.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enterprise data-quality tools the RevOps buyer might consider versus ZoomInfo Operations on dedup mechanics. QualityStage's spotlight angle is "IT-led data quality versus GTM-led data quality" — a useful framing for explaining why ZoomInfo Operations is the GTM-specific choice.

## Cite
- Key features: Probabilistic matching algorithms, Standardization (addresses, phone, email, dates), Survivorship / golden-record selection, Data profiling and investigation, ML-driven auto-tagging of business terms, Address Verification Interface (AVI) with CASS certification
- Strengths: Probabilistic matching at enterprise scale; inline cleansing in DataStage ETL; mainframe / on-prem / Cloud / Hadoop breadth
- Gaps: No B2B dataset; generic enterprise scope; expensive to maintain per Gartner Peer Insights
