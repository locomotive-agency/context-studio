# Voila Norbert — Email Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on a single slice — verified business emails)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Lookup tool that returns a verified business email given a person's name + company domain, with single-search, bulk CSV, and a 100M+ prospect index inside Prospect Finder.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email-finder tools or covers SMB-priced ZoomInfo alternatives for cold-email lead lists. Lean on the Ahrefs #1 ranking and pay-only-on-successful-finds pricing model.

## Cite
- Key features: Single-search by name + domain, Bulk CSV email finder + Google Sheets export, Prospect Finder across 100M+ prospects, Per-email certainty score, Chrome extension for LinkedIn / Sales Navigator, Pay-only-on-successful-finds
- Strengths: Up to 98% success rate (Ahrefs #1); per-email certainty score; no-charge on misses/duplicates
- Gaps: 100M prospects vs ZoomInfo 500M+; email-only; no firmographic / intent depth
