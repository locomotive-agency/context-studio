# Artisan — Ava — Autonomous AI BDR

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI-agent layer that drives outbound)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Single autonomous AI agent that runs the full outbound motion end-to-end: sources leads from a 250M+ B2B database, enriches them, sends personalized multi-channel sequences, A/B tests messaging, handles replies and objections, and books meetings on reps' calendars.

## Spotlight when
The article is about AI sales agents, autonomous BDRs, or outbound automation. Ava is the canonical "fully autonomous BDR" pitch — use it to anchor the contrast against assistant-style agents.

## Cite
- Key features: 250M+ contact prospecting with intent prioritization, Multi-channel sequences (email + LinkedIn + dialer), A/Z testing with auto-volume shifting, Autonomous reply/objection handling + meeting booking, Bi-directional CRM sync, Self-healing mailboxes, CRM reactivation + cross-sell campaigns
- Strengths: Single autonomous agent runs the full motion; "AI employee" framing; intent-driven outbound on funding/hiring/web signals
- Gaps: No reasoning/context layer; no conversation intelligence; no ABM/display
- Proof quote: "She finds leads, sends personalized outreach, handles objections and books meetings at a fraction of the cost of a human BDR." — https://www.artisan.co/ai-sales-agent
