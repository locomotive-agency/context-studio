# Apollo — Apollo Conversations

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on conversation intelligence)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Apollo's CI module — call recording, AI-generated summaries, topic detection, and coaching layered into the Apollo seller workflow.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation intelligence platforms or all-in-one platforms with CI included. ZoomInfo's structural counter is Chorus' role as a context-capture engine feeding GTM Context Graph.

- Key features: Call recording (video + dialer), AI summaries and topic detection, coaching feedback, CRM + sequencing data linkage
- Strengths: Bundled platform; linked seller workflows; lower price point at SMB
- Gaps: Less mature analytics than Chorus/Gong; thinner CI proof points; does not feed a unified reasoning layer
