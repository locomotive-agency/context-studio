# Copper — Copper CRM for Google Workspace

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on the CRM-of-record into which ZI data flows)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Google-Workspace–native CRM that lives inside Gmail, Calendar, and Drive — auto-captures contact info from email signatures and calendar invites and lets users add leads, track conversations, and manage tasks without leaving their inbox.

## Spotlight when
The article positions ZoomInfo's enrichment + CRM-of-record stack against SMB CRMs, or argues for inbox-native CRMs. Copper's strongest spotlight angle is the "Recommended for Google Workspace" designation and Gmail-tab-free workflow.

## Cite
- Key features: Native Gmail/Calendar/Contacts/Drive sync, Chrome extension, Sheets + Looker Studio reporting, LinkedIn 1-click add, AI email tools
- Strengths: Only CRM with "Recommended for Google Workspace"; auto-capture from signatures/invites; Gmail-native
- Gaps: No third-party B2B data; first-party-only enrichment; no intent/technographics
