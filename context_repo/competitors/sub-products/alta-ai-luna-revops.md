# Alta AI — Luna — AI Growth / RevOps Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (direct overlap on the play-orchestration + signal-routing surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Intelligence layer that connects CRM, marketing tools, and 50+ data sources to detect buying signals, build lookalikes, surface campaign-scale-or-pause recommendations, and orchestrate Katie and Alex.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights GTM Studio or revenue-orchestration / signal-routing workflows, especially when the angle is "intelligence layer that sits on top of the CRM."

## Cite
- Key features: Connects 50+ data sources including CRM/intent/job postings/news, Pattern detection across messaging/channels/timing, Lookalike audience building, Surfaces scale-or-pause recommendations to Slack/email/Teams, Native HubSpot and Salesforce integration, Self-optimization via A/B testing across agents
- Strengths: Self-optimizing layer that A/B tests across the entire agent stack; surfaces actions to Slack/Teams (not dashboards); decision layer on top of CRM
- Gaps: No proprietary B2B data depth — relies on ZoomInfo and other vendors; less play-orchestration surface than GTM Studio motion-builder; no multi-source intent + scoops graph
