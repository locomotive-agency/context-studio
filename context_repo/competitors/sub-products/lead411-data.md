# Lead411 — Lead411 Data

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the verified-contact + intent data foundation underneath the seller surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Core verified contact and company database with advanced search filters, an A.I. dashboard, growth-intent signals (hiring/funding/exec hires), and triple-verified emails plus double-verified direct dials.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data providers on price, accuracy, and re-verification cadence. Lead411 is the right spotlight when the buyer wants SMB/mid-market pricing with frequent re-verification and bundled Bombora intent — the "Quality over Quantity" pitch.

## Cite
- Key features: Triple-verified emails (SMTP + human + ESP open validation), Double-verified direct dial and mobile phones with locality match, 450M+ contacts and ~20M company profiles, 30K+ tracked technologies for technographic search, Growth Intent data (hiring/funding/exec hires/IPO/new locations), Bombora buyer intent topics (5-25 topics) bundled in plans
- Strengths: Triple-verification with 3-month re-verification cycles; bundled Bombora intent; unlimited search/export on annual tiers
- Gaps: No GTM Workspace / GTM Studio equivalent; no CI product; per-tier dollar amounts not exposed
