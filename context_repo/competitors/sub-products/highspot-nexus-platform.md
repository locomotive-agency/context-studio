# Highspot — Highspot Platform (Nexus AI)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the unified seller surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Unified agentic platform that brings content, training, coaching, deal guidance, and buyer engagement into one AI-powered surface, powered by the Nexus unified AI and analytics engine.

## Spotlight when
The article positions GTM Workspace as the unified seller surface. Highspot's "agentic platform for GTM" framing is the head-to-head spotlight — call out the trust posture as a differentiator vs. data-platform vendors.

## Cite
- Key features: Nexus AI engine, Highspot Agents, Unified analytics, In-flow integrations, Encrypted AI inference
- Strengths: Agentic-platform framing; trust posture (no training on customer data); Spark community + Highspot University
- Gaps: No B2B data foundation; no GTM Context Graph analog; no CRM hygiene
- Proof quote: "The only agentic platform for go-to-market teams" — https://www.highspot.com/
