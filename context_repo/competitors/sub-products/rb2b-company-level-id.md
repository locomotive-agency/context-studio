# RB2B — RB2B Company-Level ID

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap on company-level visitor identification)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Identifies the companies (not individuals) visiting your website worldwide through a Demandbase Reverse-IP Lookup partnership, available on the Free tier and as the GDPR-compliant fallback for non-US traffic.

## Spotlight when
The article covers global / GDPR-compliant visitor ID, free-tier visitor identification options, or anyone exploring WebSights alternatives below enterprise budget.

## Cite
- Key features: Demandbase Reverse-IP global resolution, 150 free monthly resolutions, Firmographic data, GDPR-compliant, Slack/Teams push on Free
- Strengths: Free tier with 150 monthly resolutions; Demandbase data backbone; Slack/Teams push on Free plan
- Gaps: Company-level only; limited firmographic depth vs. ZoomInfo; no intent/technographic layer
- Proof quote: "Identify the 70-80% of your US website visitors that today go anonymous" — https://www.rb2b.com/
