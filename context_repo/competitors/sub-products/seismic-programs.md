# Seismic — Seismic Programs

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the enablement-orchestration surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Plan, execute, and measure enablement efforts in one workflow with reporting and CRM integrations, used to roll out product launches, sales playbooks, and certifications at scale.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enablement-program orchestration for enterprise launches and playbooks. Useful counterpoint when the buyer expects ZoomInfo to handle messaging rollouts (it doesn't — that's enablement territory).

- Key features: Plan-execute-measure workflow; reporting + CRM integrations; certification tracking; product launches/playbooks

- Gaps: Content-/training-centric orchestration; no third-party intent; opaque pricing
