# Copper — Copper Projects & Service Delivery

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no direct overlap — covers post-sale ground ZoomInfo does not address)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Lets teams duplicate closed deals into projects to handle service delivery, client onboarding, and ongoing client work — extending the CRM into post-sale workflow with project pipelines, task automation, and cross-functional collaboration.

## Spotlight when
The article positions Copper as a service-business CRM rather than a GTM platform competitor, or maps post-sale workflow against ZoomInfo's pre-sale focus. Use to illustrate negative-space rather than head-to-head comparison.

## Cite
- Key features: Project pipelines + templates, Project management views, Centralized tasks / follow-ups, Task/workflow automation, Cross-functional collaboration
- Strengths: Post-sale service-delivery workflow; deal-to-project hand-off; agency/consulting fit
- Gaps: No outreach/dialer/sequences; no CI; ZI doesn't sell into this surface
- Proof quote: "Duplicate closed deals into projects to handle service delivery, client onboarding, and ongoing client work" — https://www.copper.com/
