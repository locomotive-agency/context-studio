# SuperAGI — SuperAGI SuperSales

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the autonomous-seller surface — competes with the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace plus native CI and dialer)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
All-in-one AI Sales Platform combining AI Outbound/Inbound SDRs, sequences, dialer (power + parallel), meetings, conversations, email warmup infrastructure, and CRM data — pitched to "grow your revenue, without the hassle of hiring large sales team."

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one agentic AI sales platforms with native CI, dialer, and CRM. SuperSales is the spotlight when the buyer wants 50+ apps on one platform plus the Humex managed-service option for human SDR augmentation.
