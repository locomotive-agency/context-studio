# RollWorks (AdRoll ABM) — AdRoll (Cross-Channel DSP)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on audience activation)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-powered, full-funnel cross-channel demand-side platform sold alongside AdRoll ABM for retargeting, brand-awareness, and conversion campaigns across the open web and walled-garden social — self-service or managed-service.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses programmatic advertising as part of an ABM stack and how ZoomInfo Marketing routes audiences vs. RollWorks operating its own bidder.

## Cite
- Key features: Cross-channel campaign management, AI audience targeting, retargeting + ad credits, cross-channel attribution, social ads add-on
- Strengths: In-house DSP, walled-garden + open-web orchestration, ad-format breadth
- Gaps: B2C vertical heavy on the umbrella, no native B2B contact data, no CI
- Proof quote: "Most DSPs are unable to target users across the open web to social media sites — but we're not most DSPs." — https://www.adroll.com/solutions/social-web-orchestration
