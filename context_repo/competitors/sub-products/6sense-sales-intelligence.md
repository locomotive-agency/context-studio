# 6sense — Sales Intelligence

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI-powered B2B sales intelligence with real-time buyer data — surfaces in-market accounts and decision-makers for sellers, with a free tier as the lower-friction evaluation entry point.

## Spotlight when
The article compares sales intelligence platforms, prospecting tools, or evaluation-friction (free-tier) sales tools. 6sense Sales Intelligence's free tier is the relevant feature to cite.

## Cite
- Key features: Ready-to-buy account ID, outreach fine-tuning, Sales Copilot AI assistant, Chrome Extension, AI Writer (Beta), Company & People Search, Sales Alerts, List Builder
- Strengths: Free tier with 50 data credits/month; in-market signals tied to advertising
- Gaps: Smaller contact database vs. ZoomInfo's 500M; paid tier pricing quote-based
- Proof quote: "Find accounts that are ready to buy." — https://6sense.com/platform/sales-intelligence
