# Nimbler — Nimbler Data Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the verified-contact + AI-outreach + pipeline workflow surface for SMB/mid-market teams)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
All-in-one B2B contact-data platform with AI-assisted search across 140M global contacts, 100M phone numbers, 40M direct dials, plus a built-in AI Sales Assistant (Engage), pipeline management, and contact-data enrichment.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data platforms with bundled outreach automation for SMB / mid-market sellers — Nimbler is the spotlight when the buyer wants unlimited users plus an all-in-one Prospect/Engage/Close/Enrich workflow.

## Cite
- Key features: AI-assisted search across 140M global B2B contacts, 100M phone numbers and 40M direct dials, Real-time work-email validation (96% validity self-reported), Up to 50 enrichment attributes (mobile phone/personal email/job title/skills), All-in-one Prospect → Engage → Close → Enrich workflow, Unlimited users on every account
- Strengths: Unlimited users on every account; all-in-one workflow on one platform; "10x easier to use than ZoomInfo and Apollo" claim
- Gaps: Inconsistent pricing across third-party catalogs; native Salesforce/HubSpot integrations not confirmed; no CI or third-party intent layer
- Proof quote: "more contacts and higher accuracy and coverage compared to ZoomInfo" — https://datarade.ai/data-providers/nymblr-inc/profile
