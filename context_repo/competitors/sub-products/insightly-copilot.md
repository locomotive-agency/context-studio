# Insightly — Insightly Copilot

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — Copilot is the AI-agent layer; GTM Workspace is ZoomInfo's AI agent layer, but Insightly's runs on first-party CRM data only)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
AI-powered assistant inside Insightly CRM that automates record management, drafts emails, and surfaces context with conversational lookup — available on Professional ($49) and Enterprise ($99) tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article covers the AI-agent / Copilot layer in CRMs and contrasts CRM-only AI (Insightly Copilot, Salesforce Einstein) with ZoomInfo's data-graph-driven AI agents in GTM Workspace.

## Cite
- Key features: Conversational lookup, AI email replies with CRM context, AI summaries from Contacts/Leads/Organizations, chat-based record management, smart drafts/follow-ups
- Strengths: Available at $49/seat Professional tier; conversational record management; CRM-context-aware
- Gaps: No third-party data context graph; no agent-builder/BYO-LLM; adjacent rather than substitute
