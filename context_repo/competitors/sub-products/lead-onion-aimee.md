# Lead Onion — Lead Onion Aimee — AI Buyer Intent Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — both surface AI-driven account/cadence generation, but Aimee is a trial-funnel entry agent)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Free domain-input AI agent that scans signals to identify in-market accounts in under 3 minutes, generates a buyer-intent campaign with targeted contacts and a sales cadence, and serves as the entry path to a 7-day Lead Onion trial bundled with 100 free leads.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI SDR / AI agent entry experiences and the buyer cares about no-friction time-to-value comparisons.

## Cite
- Key features: Domain-input no-CC setup, <3-minute campaign generation, 100 free leads via 7-day trial
- Strengths: No-credit-card entry; <3-minute time-to-first-campaign; 100 free leads
- Gaps: Trial-funnel agent only; not a full GTM Workspace AI stack; no published agent extensibility
