# Fathom — Fathom for Teams

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap — team-wide meeting workspace, not multi-call analytics platform)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Cross-functional workspace that creates a shared meeting source-of-truth across Sales, Marketing, Operations, HR, and Product teams — with account-wide Ask Fathom search and team analytics.

## Spotlight when
The article calls out cross-functional CI use cases (e.g., marketing voice-of-customer + product/UX research). Fathom for Teams is the spotlight when the angle is "shared meeting workspace beyond sales."

## Cite
- Key features: Team recordings/folders/customer/deal views, Account-wide Ask Fathom (beta), Global keyword + attendee search, Comments/mentions/alerts, SSO, Custom retention (Business), Launch Assist (Business)
- Strengths: Cross-functional source-of-truth; account-wide Ask Fathom; custom retention + Launch Assist
- Gaps: Adjacent to Chorus; gated tier features; no underlying B2B data layer
