# Saleshandy — Saleshandy Email Infrastructure

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller email-deliverability surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Add-on that buys domains and mailboxes pre-configured with SPF/DKIM/DMARC, plus warm-up and inbox-placement testing to keep cold sends out of spam — sold inside the Saleshandy Outreach stack.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on cold-send deliverability ops for SMB/agency teams scaling outbound volume. ZoomInfo does not sell a comparable buy-and-configure email-infrastructure add-on, so this is a non-substitute differentiator.

## Cite
- Key features: Domain + mailbox purchase with auto-configured SPF/DKIM/DMARC; Google/Microsoft 365/Azure support; auto warm-up; tiered Inbox Placement Tests
- Strengths: Buy-and-configure infrastructure inside the outbound suite; granular placement-test tiers; native sender rotation in Outreach
- Gaps: Tied to Saleshandy's platform; no SLO-grade deliverability analytics; add-on costs stack on Outreach plan
- Proof quote: "Buy domains + mailboxes with auto-configured SPF/DKIM/DMARC" — https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing
