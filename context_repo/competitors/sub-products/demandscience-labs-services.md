# DemandScience — DemandScience Labs (Managed Demand Generation)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on the marketer-facing demand-gen + ABM execution surface, with complementary services-led framing)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
End-to-end managed services team that runs ABM, content syndication, BANT lead-gen, programmatic ads, managed email, and done-for-you content/events on the customer's behalf — pitched as "execution layer for ZoomInfo intelligence."

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM/demand-gen orchestration, especially when the angle is managed-services execution, guaranteed CPL with lead-replacement guarantees, or done-for-you content production.
