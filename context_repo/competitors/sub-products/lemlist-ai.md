# Lemlist — Lemlist AI

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on AI-drafted outreach / sequence generation; differs by being BYO-LLM rather than running on a proprietary context graph)

**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI layer that segments leads, cleans data, and generates personalized multichannel sequences and copy at scale, sourced via the customer's own OpenAI key.

## Spotlight when
The article compares AI-personalization layers across outbound platforms. Lemlist AI's strongest spotlight: voice cloning + image/landing-page personalization at scale — execution-layer AI that sits ahead of ZoomInfo's seller-side AI on creative output even if it lacks the data graph.

## Cite
- Key features: Campaign Generator (<3 min sequence build), AI Variables, Ask AI assistant, Interest Detector, Voice cloning, Image + landing page personalization
- Strengths: Voice cloning + dynamic visual personalization; BYO LLM (no model lock-in); fast sequence generation
- Gaps: No proprietary context graph; AI quality depends on user-provided context; narrow scope vs. full GTM AI surface
- Proof quote: "Campaign Generator builds multichannel sequences from a brief in under 3 minutes" — https://www.lemlist.com/product/ai
