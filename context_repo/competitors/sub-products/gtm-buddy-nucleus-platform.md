# GTM Buddy — GTM Buddy Revenue Activation Engine

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller in-flow surface)

## What it does
Umbrella platform organized around five "Readiness Levers" — Ramp Acceleration, In-Flow Activation, Content Velocity, Coaching Precision, and Revenue Proof — claiming reps reclaim ~5 hours of selling time per week and 25% more selling capacity.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enablement platforms against ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace as the seller front-end. GTM Buddy's "activation > enablement" framing is the spotlight angle.
