# HubSpot Marketing Hub — HubSpot Forms

**Competes with:** FormComplete (adjacent overlap — HubSpot Forms is form-build + capture; FormComplete enriches at submit)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Drag-and-drop form builder that captures leads and feeds them directly into HubSpot's CRM with intelligent fields that adapt to each visitor (progressive profiling). Triggers automated follow-up workflows on submission.

## Spotlight when
The article compares lead-capture or form-shortening workflows. HubSpot Forms is adjacent to FormComplete — they layer (Forms + Clearbit/Breeze Intelligence) where ZoomInfo bundles enrichment-at-submit natively.

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop builder, CRM auto-sync, Progressive profiling, External embeds, Workflow triggers
- Strengths: Free tier; progressive profiling shortens forms; immediate CRM-sync triggers
- Gaps: Form shortening requires Clearbit add-on; thinner B2B backfill than FormComplete; free-tier branding
- Proof quote: "Capture leads with intelligent forms that adapt to each visitor" — https://www.hubspot.com/products/marketing/forms
