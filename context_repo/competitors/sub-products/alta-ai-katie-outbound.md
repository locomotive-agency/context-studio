# Alta AI — Katie — AI SDR Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace — autonomous outbound prospecting + outreach)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous outbound AI SDR that identifies ICP prospects from the CRM and 50+ data sources, runs multi-channel outreach (email, LinkedIn, SMS), and books meetings 24/7.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo's AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace to autonomous AI SDRs, especially when discussing multi-agent coordination (outbound + inbound + RevOps) or Salesforce-native deployment.
