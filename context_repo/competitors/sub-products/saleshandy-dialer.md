# Saleshandy — Saleshandy Dialer

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller-calling surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Built-in calling for sales teams: buy phone numbers, place calls, and get AI call summaries inside Saleshandy without a separate dialer vendor.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one outbound platforms that bundle dialing alongside sequences. Useful as a counterpoint when an SMB buyer wants in-suite calling without integrating a standalone dialer.

## Cite
- Key features: In-platform dialing, AI call summaries, unlimited recording storage on Starter, no-expiry credits
- Strengths: Unlimited recording storage at entry tier; credits never expire; in-suite calling tied to sequences
- Gaps: No conversation intelligence; no parallel/power dialer variants; limited reporting depth
- Proof quote: "Unlimited recording storage on Starter" — https://www.saleshandy.com/pricing
