# Dun & Bradstreet — D&B Marketing Services

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on marketing audience targeting and execution — but as managed services rather than self-serve software)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Done-for-you marketing services where D&B experts plan and execute campaigns (email, social, display, direct mail, omnichannel) using D&B's first-party data paired with the customer's own data.

## Spotlight when
The article frames the managed-services lane vs self-service software for B2B marketing execution. Use when ZI Marketing's automation/audience-build is contrasted with a "we run your campaigns" outsourced model.

## Cite
- Key features: Email/social/display/direct-mail execution, omnichannel campaign management, website + landing page design, creative + copywriting services
- Strengths: Managed-service / done-for-you marketing offering; combines D&B + customer data for targeting; Rev.Up ABX adjacent ABM platform
- Gaps: Services-led not self-serve software; no conversation intelligence or seller workflow; pricing not published
- Proof quote: "D&B experts plan and execute campaigns using D&B's first-party data paired with the customer's own data" — https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-marketing-services.html
