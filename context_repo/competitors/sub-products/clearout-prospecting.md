# Clearout — Prospecting (incl. LinkedIn Chrome Extension)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent overlap — secondary motion, not a head-to-head universe)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
SQL/MQL list-building with real-time data enrichment, plus a LinkedIn Chrome Extension that captures verified email + phone data from LinkedIn profiles, search pages, Sales Navigator, and connection/invitation pages.

## Spotlight when
The article compares LinkedIn Chrome extensions for prospecting, low-cost ZoomInfo Sales alternatives at SMB scale, or "verify-on-discovery" prospecting workflows. Strongest spotlight when the buyer's universe is LinkedIn-driven and budget-constrained.

## Cite
- Key features: In-app prospect search + bulk enrichment, Chrome Extension on LinkedIn / Sales Nav / connections / invitations, phone enrichment with line type/carrier/timezone, pre-verified results, transparent credit economics
- Strengths: Captures email + phone from Sales Navigator; pre-verified credits; cheaper than enterprise prospecting
- Gaps: Vendor's own positioning says it's secondary; no proprietary universe; no technographics/intent/org charts
