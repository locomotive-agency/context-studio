# Klenty — Klenty Sales Engagement Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller cadence + multi-channel execution surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Multi-channel cadence platform that plans, executes, and tracks outbound and inbound sequences across email, calls, LinkedIn, SMS, and WhatsApp from a single seller surface, with bidirectional CRM sync into Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, and Microsoft Dynamics.

## Spotlight when
The article compares mid-market sales engagement options where the buyer wants public per-seat pricing and native WhatsApp/SMS channels alongside email + dialer — Klenty's SEP is the cleanest expression of that point-solution lane.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-channel sequences (email/dialer/LinkedIn/SMS/WhatsApp), Inbox auto-rotation + email warmup, Triggers & Actions automation, SmartLists + A/B testing, Bidirectional CRM sync
- Strengths: Public $50–$99/user/mo pricing with 14-day no-CC trial; native WhatsApp channel; "active + passive buyer" SEP framing
- Gaps: No B2B contact universe (4,000 bundled credits at Plus only); no marketer/RevOps surface; no GTM Context Graph
- Proof quote: "AI Sales Engagement Platform — Open More Conversations. Build More Pipeline." — https://www.klenty.com/feature/multi-channel-outreach
