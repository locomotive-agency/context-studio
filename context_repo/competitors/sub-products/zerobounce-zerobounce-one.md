# ZeroBounce — ZeroBounce ONE

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap — bundled deliverability suite that competes at the data-hygiene edge of Operations / RingLead, plus tools ZoomInfo doesn't ship at all like inbox-placement and DMARC monitoring)

**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Bundled subscription combining validation credits, the Email Finder, AI Scoring, Inbox Placement Tests, Email Server Tests, Warmup, Blacklist Monitor, and DMARC Monitor under one $79–$99/mo SKU — credits never expire and rollover is supported.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one deliverability suites to ZoomInfo's data-hygiene slice. ZeroBounce ONE's bundle pricing and inclusion of inbox-placement, warmup, blacklist, and DMARC monitoring (none of which ZoomInfo ships) is the best spotlight angle.

- Key features: 10K validation/scoring credits + 10K Email Finder searches per month, 100 Inbox Placement Tests against 20+ providers, 100 Email Server Tests (200+ checks each), 250 Warmup Seeds per month, 10 Blacklist Monitors scanned every 8 hrs, 1 DMARC Monitor domain
- Strengths: Bundles validation + finder + scoring + inbox placement + warmup + blacklist + DMARC under one subscription; credits never expire and rollover supported; $79/mo annual pricing
- Gaps: Deliverability-only (no contact database / sales engagement / conversation intelligence); no prospect intent or technographic signals; limited to inbox-placement and list-hygiene workflows
