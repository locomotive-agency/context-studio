# Insightly — Insightly CRM

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — system of record sitting underneath GTM intelligence rather than substituting for it)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Customizable SMB / mid-market CRM that centralizes lead, contact, and pipeline data with workflow automation and embedded project management — opportunities can be converted into projects with milestones for post-close visibility.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRMs of record (Salesforce / HubSpot / Insightly) vs. the GTM intelligence layer that sits on top. Insightly is the buyer's system of record, not a substitute for ZoomInfo's data foundation.

## Cite
- Key features: Lead/contact management with kanban pipelines, workflow automation, products/price books/quotes, project management post-close, customizable layouts/permissions, dashboards
- Strengths: Project management built into the CRM; embedded quoting; $29/seat SMB entry
- Gaps: No B2B data foundation; no conversation intelligence; no intent or seller AI agents on a context graph
