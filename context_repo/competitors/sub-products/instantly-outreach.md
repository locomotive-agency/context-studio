# Instantly — Instantly Outreach

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the cold-email seller-execution surface — Outreach/Salesloft category)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered automated cold email sequencing with unlimited sending accounts, smart rotation, A/Z testing, and a unified Unibox inbox — multichannel campaigns spanning email, LinkedIn, and webhooks.

## Spotlight when
The article compares cold-email-first sales engagement platforms (Instantly, Smartlead, Lemlist, Reply) and the agency / SMB self-service motion that ZoomInfo does not match. Outreach is Instantly's flagship.

## Cite
- Key features: Unlimited email accounts/warmup, AI sequences/replies/follow-ups, multichannel (email/LinkedIn/webhook), SuperSearch filters, Unibox with AI labels, reputation protection
- Strengths: Unlimited email accounts/warmup; multichannel sequences; bundled deliverability + 450M database
- Gaps: Lags Outreach/Salesloft on enterprise depth; no CI integration; subscription stacking
