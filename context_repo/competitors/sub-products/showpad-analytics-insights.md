# Showpad — Showpad Analytics & Insights

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap on the seller-effectiveness / engagement analytics surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Surfaces patterns across content usage, learning completion, and buyer behavior to attribute revenue impact and forecast more reliably, with a natural-language Question-Based Analytics interface.

## Spotlight when
The article compares engagement-attribution analytics for enablement vs. conversation-intelligence-anchored coaching. Useful when the buyer cares about content-and-buyer engagement signals, not call transcripts.

## Cite
- Key features: Question-Based Analytics, Analytics AI, engagement-to-revenue attribution, Shared Spaces Dashboard, CRM auto-logs
- Strengths: Engagement-to-revenue attribution across content/learning/buyer behavior; natural-language analytics; tied to GenieAI governance
- Gaps: Engagement-derived not transcript-derived; no call scorecards; no third-party B2B layer
- Proof quote: "Analytics AI" — https://www.showpad.com/platform-overview
