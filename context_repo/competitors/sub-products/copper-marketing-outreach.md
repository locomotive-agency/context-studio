# Copper — Copper Marketing & Outreach

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (no direct overlap — Copper deliberately scopes outreach to first-party CRM contacts, not new prospecting)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Lightweight email outreach and re-engagement features layered onto the CRM record — templates, bulk email, sequences (Business), open/click tracking, and "Rekindle" automation for dormant contacts. Deliberately not a full marketing automation platform.

## Spotlight when
The article distinguishes CRM-bundled email from full marketing automation or sales-engagement platforms. Copper's spotlight is the "deliberately limited scope — relationship maintenance, not prospecting" angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Email templates, Bulk email + automation, Open/click tracking, Email series, Rekindle, Contact forms
- Strengths: Embedded in CRM record; Rekindle automation; lightweight form capture
- Gaps: Not a marketing automation platform; no multichannel/dialer; no prospect data
- Proof quote: "CRM is our one and only love" — https://www.copper.com/resources/copper-crm-vs-hubspot
