# Smartlead — SmartProspect

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent — small earned-credit lead feed, not a database)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Bundled prospecting engine that earns the user a verified lead for every three cold emails sent, with each prospect verified across three independent sources before flowing into active campaigns.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo Data to bundled-data plays inside outbound platforms, or position pay-with-sends models as an SMB / agency alternative for low-volume teams.

## Cite
- Key features: 1 verified lead per 3 emails sent; verified across 3 sources; advanced filters + live intent signals; direct feed into running campaigns
- Strengths: Earned-credit lead feed bundled with sender; three-source verification; campaign-feed integration
- Gaps: Tiny scale vs ZoomInfo; no standalone database UI; no technographic / firmographic depth
- Proof quote: "Earn 1 verified lead per 3 emails sent." — https://www.smartlead.ai/
