# Dun & Bradstreet — D&B Finance Analytics Credit Intelligence

**Competes with:** Data as a Service (no overlap — credit/risk decisioning is outside ZoomInfo's GTM product line)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Scalable credit-risk-management platform delivering credit reports, continuous monitoring, and end-to-end credit-to-cash automation backed by D&B's commercial credit data and proprietary scores.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to acknowledge D&B's credit/risk lane as the reason buyers may already have a D&B contract — but cleanly position it as outside ZoomInfo's GTM scope (no crossover).
