# Madison Logic — ABM Social Advertising with LinkedIn Ads

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — Madison Logic activates LinkedIn Ads from the ML Insights intent layer; ZoomInfo Marketing has audience-targeting overlap but not native LinkedIn ad orchestration)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Native LinkedIn Ads activation integrated with ML intent data to target buying groups across Sponsored Content, Message Ads, and Video Ads.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses LinkedIn-driven ABM or buying-group ad targeting. ABM LinkedIn Ads' strongest spotlight: intent-driven custom audience builds wired into LinkedIn Ads — a tighter integration than ZoomInfo Marketing's audience-export model.
