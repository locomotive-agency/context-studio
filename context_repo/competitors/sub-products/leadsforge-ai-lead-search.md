# Leadsforge — Leadsforge AI Lead Search Engine

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the verified B2B contact universe via chat-based search)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Chat-based prospecting tool that translates a plain-language ICP description into a verified contact list across 500M+ profiles using waterfall enrichment for emails, LinkedIn URLs, and mobile phone numbers — with one-click export to Salesforge sequences and native CRM integrations to HubSpot, Salesforce, Folk, Breakcold, and Attio.

## Spotlight when
The article compares SMB-priced ZoomInfo alternatives or chat-style prospecting UIs against ZoomInfo's filter-driven Sales experience — Leadsforge's $49/month credit model and "ChatGPT for leads" framing are the strongest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Chat-based ICP search, Waterfall enrichment (email/LinkedIn/mobile), 500M+ contacts, Lookalike + competitor-follower discovery, Salesforge + 5-CRM integrations
- Strengths: Chat-style ICP entry; $49/mo pricing pitched 25x–65x cheaper than ZoomInfo; 100 free credits rolling over
- Gaps: Pre-launch / early-phase product; data accuracy not independently verified (small review sample); waterfall sources undisclosed
