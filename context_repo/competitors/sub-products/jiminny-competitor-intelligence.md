# Jiminny — Jiminny Competitor Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap on Chorus competitor-mention tracking; not a substitute for dedicated competitive-intel platforms like Klue/Crayon)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Detects, alerts, and reports on competitor mentions inside customer calls — feeding voice-of-customer signals to Product Marketing and win/loss data to revenue leadership.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CI add-ons vs dedicated competitive-intel tools, or shows how Chorus-class platforms surface competitor mentions natively without a separate platform.

## Cite
- Key features: Keyword/theme detection, custom alerts on mentions, voice-of-customer feedback, CRM opportunity write-back, win/loss analysis, leadership dashboards
- Strengths: Productized competitor detection on CI; CRM opportunity write-back; leadership trend dashboards
- Gaps: Only Jiminny-captured calls; no positioning docs/kill cards; mid-market scope
