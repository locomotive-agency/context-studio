# Skrapp — Lead Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the contact/company database surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Searchable B2B lead database surfacing 200M+ business profiles for list-building, with filters across job title, location, company, and industry plus AI search and CSV export.

## Spotlight when
Articles position lower-cost contact databases for SMB list-building or compare ZoomInfo to AI-search-driven prospecting on a self-serve plan.

## Cite
- Key features: 200M+ business profiles refreshed daily; filter by job title / location / company / industry; AI search; CSV export
- Strengths: Natural-language AI search; daily-refresh on the 200M dataset; self-serve CSV workflow
- Gaps: Narrower filter depth vs ZoomInfo; no intent / technographic data; no in-product CRM push
- Proof quote: "200M+ business profiles refreshed daily." — https://skrapp.io/compare/zoominfo-alternative
