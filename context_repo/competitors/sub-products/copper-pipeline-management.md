# Copper — Copper Pipeline Management

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller-pipeline UI surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Drag-and-drop visual sales pipelines with AI flags, automation, and forecasting designed for small sales teams — multiple pipelines per object (prospects, vendors, partners), workflow automations, and forecasting in one view.

## Spotlight when
The article compares pipeline-prioritization surfaces or argues for first-party-driven AI vs. third-party-signal-driven AI. Copper's spotlight is "SMB-shaped pipeline AI without an enterprise data stack."

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop pipelines, AI Pipeline Flags, Pipeline automations, Multiple pipeline templates, Real-time forecasting
- Strengths: SMB-friendly UX; built-in AI flags; automated time-based task assignment
- Gaps: First-party-only AI signals; no third-party intent; no CI-driven insights
- Proof quote: "Spot bottlenecks and surface high-value opportunities with AI Pipeline Flags" — https://www.copper.com/features/pipeline-management
