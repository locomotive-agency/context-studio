# Dreamdata — Dreamdata Audience Hub

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on audience activation and ABM ad-platform sync)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Audience builder and activation layer that creates dynamic B2B audiences from unified GTM data and syncs them daily to LinkedIn, Google, Meta, and Microsoft Ads.

## Spotlight when
The article is about ABM audience activation, LinkedIn CAPI / offline conversion lift, or marketer-led ad-platform sync. Audience Hub's 90%+ LinkedIn match rate is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Unlimited filters across web/ads/pipeline/intent/CRM/firmographic, daily syncs to LinkedIn/Google/Meta/Microsoft Ads, LinkedIn CAPI offline conversion sync, Audience Reach Report, webhooks
- Strengths: 90%+ LinkedIn match rates vs 29-62% industry; 20% CPA reduction / 31% conversion lift via CAPI; audiences built on unified GTM data
- Gaps: BYO third-party contact data; no conversation intelligence; no native 500M-contact list build
- Proof quote: "LinkedIn company match rates above 90% vs industry average 29-62%" — https://dreamdata.io/audience-hub
