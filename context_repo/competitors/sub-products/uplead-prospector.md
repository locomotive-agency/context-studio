# UpLead — UpLead Prospector

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the verified-contact prospecting workflow for SMB/mid-market sellers, with 95% accuracy guarantee + credit refunds as the wedge)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
B2B prospecting tool searching a 180M+ verified contact database with 50+ filters, real-time email verification at moment of download, Chrome extension for LinkedIn / company-site capture, and Bombora-powered Buyer Intent on Plus/Pro tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data tools for SMB/mid-market sellers on price + accuracy. UpLead is the spotlight when the buyer wants $99/mo entry pricing with a 95% accuracy guarantee backed by credit refunds — explicitly anti-ZoomInfo on price transparency.

## Cite
- Key features: 180M+ contacts and 19M+ company profiles in 200+ countries, 50+ search filters (title/industry/location/management level/revenue/technographics), Real-time email verification at moment of download, Bulk download to CSV or push to CRM, Bombora-sourced Buyer Intent Data layered on Prospector filters (Plus/Pro), Chrome extension capturing verified emails/phones from LinkedIn
- Strengths: 95% accuracy guarantee with credit refunds; real-time email verification at download; transparent $99/mo entry pricing
- Gaps: No GTM Workspace/Studio/Chorus/MCP analogs; no GTM Context Graph; no website visitor tracking
- Proof quote: "UpLead is significantly cheaper than ZoomInfo. UpLead's Essentials plan starts at $99 per month with no annual contract required." — https://www.uplead.com/uplead-vs-zoominfo/
