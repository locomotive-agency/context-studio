# Bouncer — Bouncer AutoClean

**Competes with:** CRM Enrichment (adjacent — verification-only CRM hygiene; not net-new enrichment)

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
"Set-and-forget" CRM-integrated automation that continuously verifies new contacts and re-verifies existing lists on a schedule, with rule-based Keep / Suppress / Quarantine actions.

## Spotlight when
The article frames continuous CRM list hygiene, automated verification cadences, or no-ops marketing-ops workflows. The "Bouncer Recommends" CRM field and rules-based actions are the spotlight angles.
