# NeverBounce — Growth AI

**Competes with:** None — NeverBounce is a wholly-owned ZoomInfo product line. Documented for ownership context only.
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
A subscription tier that adds AI lead-scoring and lead suggestions on top of NeverBounce's verification stack — 200 AI-picked leads per month plus AI scoring and "GTM Guard" guardrails.

## Spotlight when
Articles where the AI-layer extension on top of email verification is part of the ZoomInfo + NeverBounce co-sell narrative.

- Key features: 200 AI-picked leads/month, AI scoring, Bad-email flagging pre-CRM, Unlimited parallel list cleaning, GTM Guard

- Gaps: ZoomInfo-owned (not a competitive substitute); AI lead volume capped; tier restricted to under 10K emails/month
