# BuiltWith — BuiltWith Pro (Lead Generation)

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on technographic data slice)

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Builds exportable lead lists of websites filtered by the web technologies they run, with attributes like spend, revenue, location, employee count, and contacts.

## Spotlight when
The article frames technographic-led prospecting, "future customer" stack-similarity targeting, or self-service B2B data lists. BuiltWith Pro's 41-year history + Predictive Leads are the spotlight angles.
