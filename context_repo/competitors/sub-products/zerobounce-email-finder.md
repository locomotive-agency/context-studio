# ZeroBounce — ZeroBounce Email Finder & Domain Search

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent overlap — the email-discovery surface within ZoomInfo Sales / GTM Workspace, but ZeroBounce has no contact database underneath)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Pattern-based email finder that returns a person's likely business email from name + company domain, validated through the same 99.6%-accuracy engine — plus a domain search that surfaces the company's prevailing email pattern.

## Spotlight when
The article frames email-finder tools as a thin alternative to a true contact database — ZeroBounce's pattern + validation approach is fundamentally different from ZoomInfo Sales's contact graph and is best spotlighted to highlight what ZoomInfo provides that finders cannot.

## Cite
- Key features: Name + company-domain lookup, Domain pattern detection, Built-in 99.6% validation of every found address, 20 credits per successful query, 10 free finder lookups on Freemium
- Strengths: Every found email is auto-validated through the 99.6% verification engine; public credit-based pricing; domain pattern detection
- Gaps: No underlying B2B contact database (only pattern + validation); no firmographic, technographic, or intent enrichment; no org chart / hierarchy data
- Proof quote: "Find business emails by entering a person's name and company domain" — https://www.zerobounce.net/email-validation-pricing
