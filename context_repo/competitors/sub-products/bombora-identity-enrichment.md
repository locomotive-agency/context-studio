# Bombora — Bombora Identity & Enrichment

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on visitor-deanonymization / identity resolution)

**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Identifies and enriches anonymous website visitors using a patented composite (deterministic + probabilistic) methodology and pushes results into CDPs, CMSs, chatbots, and other site-personalization tools.

## Spotlight when
The article touches visitor identification, ABM site personalization, or composite identity resolution. Bombora's patented composite + work-from-home model is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Patented composite (deterministic + probabilistic); work-from-home model; real-time delivery into CDPs/chatbots; firmographic + behavioral enrichment; first-party data ownership
- Strengths: Composite identity beyond IP-to-domain; WFH model lift; real-time activation
- Gaps: De-anonymization only (no net-new account enrichment); no CRM hygiene/routing; gated pricing
- Proof quote: "de-anonymizes ~97% of unidentified site traffic" — https://bombora.com/identity-and-enrichment/
