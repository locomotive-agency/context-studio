# Bouncer — Bouncer Email Verification (App)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on email verification — head-to-head with NeverBounce inside ZI Operations)

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
SaaS app that bulk-verifies email lists for deliverability and returns rich per-address output (status, score, toxicity, provider, role, free/disposable, full-mailbox flags).

## Spotlight when
The article frames pure-play email verification, post-export list cleaning, or the "after-market verifier on top of ZoomInfo data" pattern. Bouncer's catch-all verification depth + EU residency are the spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Deep catch-all verification (Google Workspace / M365); rich per-address output; no charge for duplicates/unknown; GDPR-by-design; EU AWS residency
- Strengths: "Verifies the unverifiable"; PAYG credits never expire; EU residency
- Gaps: No contact database; no CRM hygiene/routing; narrow scope vs integrated GTM
- Proof quote: "+4B verified email addresses" — https://www.usebouncer.com/
