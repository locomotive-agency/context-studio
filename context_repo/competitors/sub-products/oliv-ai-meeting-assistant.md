# Oliv AI — Meeting Assistant

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on conversation-intelligence — call recording, scoring, real-time assist, follow-up automation)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI agent that automates meeting prep, live note-taking, action-item assignment, and follow-up emails — with optional Insights pack adding scorecards, topic intelligence, and real-time Answer Assistant.

## Spotlight when
The article frames real-time AI conversation assistance and modular CI pricing as differentiators against Chorus or Gong — Real-time Answer Assistant + free migration from Gong/Avoma/Fireflies/Clari are the standout angles.
