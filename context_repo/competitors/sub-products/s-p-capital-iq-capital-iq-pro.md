# S&P Capital IQ — S&P Capital IQ Pro

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on company firmographic data only)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Web-based financial intelligence platform combining deep company, market, people, and capital-structure data with screening, modeling, charting, and AI-powered research tools — peer to Bloomberg Terminal / LSEG Workspace / FactSet for capital-markets workflows.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses competitor confusion between GTM intelligence and capital-markets intelligence — Capital IQ Pro is the canonical example of a finance-tooled platform that overlaps ZoomInfo's data layer at the firmographic level only.

## Cite
- Key features: 62K public + 4.4M private company profiles, ~99% global market cap financials, 17-vertical SNL modules, 3.8M executive profiles, ChatIQ GenAI, premium broker research
- Strengths: Capital-markets depth, audit-to-source financials, industry templates
- Gaps: No GTM signals, no prospecting workflow, $30K+/yr pricing
- Proof quote: "A powerful array of financial data, analytics and research" — https://www.lib.sfu.ca/system/files/33579/s-p-capital-iq-platform-brochure.pdf
