# Madison Logic — ML Insights

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on ABM intent / account scoring; Madison Logic and ZoomInfo are also strategic partners as of Aug 2025)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered intent data product that captures, scores, and prioritizes engagement signals across accounts and buying-group personas — drawing from a proprietary publisher network of content engagement (500M+ monthly signals, 485M+ professional reach).

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B intent data providers or ABM intent layers. ML Insights' strongest spotlight: 20-year publisher-network signal advantage and buying-group identification baked into the intent feed. Note partner+competitor edge case before drawing sharp head-to-head conclusions.

## Cite
- Key features: 500M+ monthly engagement signals, 485M+ professional network, ICP/technographic/propensity scoring, AI next-best-action, real-time refresh, first+third-party signal aggregation
- Strengths: Proprietary publisher-network signals (vs. web-research-only); 20 years of ABM data + AI; buying-group ID inside intent
- Gaps: No raw contact / direct dial / email data; no CRM hygiene layer; partner+competitor relationship with ZoomInfo
- Proof quote: "500M+ monthly proprietary engagement signals feeding account scoring" — https://www.madisonlogic.com/solutions/ml-insights/
