# Mindtickle — Sales Training

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — seller-facing readiness vs. data-driven prospecting)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Personalized, microlearning-based sales training to reduce rep ramp time and continuously upskill GTM teams — paired with the Readiness Index, which ties enablement programs to skill improvement and revenue outcomes.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to demonstrate Mindtickle's "readiness" framing distinct from GTM Workspace's prospecting workflow. Cite when buyers ask about ramp acceleration or formal enablement curriculum.
