# Showpad — Showpad Sales Readiness

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on the coaching/training surface for field-sales-driven enterprises)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Onboards, trains, and coaches sellers via courses, knowledge checks/certifications, and AI roleplay so they show up prepared for buyer conversations.

## Spotlight when
The article compares coaching depth across enablement and conversation-intelligence categories for regulated/physical-product industries (medical devices, manufacturing, CPG).
