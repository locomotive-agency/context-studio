# Factiva — Factiva (research platform)

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — company/executive profiles for research, not GTM contact reach)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Global news monitoring research platform combining premium licensed news, company profiles, and executive bios across 33,000 sources, 200 countries, and 33 languages — surfaced through GenAI summarization and an AI Assistant grounded in licensed content.

## Spotlight when
The article distinguishes news/research databases from GTM data platforms. Factiva is the strongest example of "premium licensed editorial content as a data layer" — useful for business-development triggers but not for prospecting at scale.

## Cite
- Key features: 33K+ premium sources, 35M company profiles + 70M executive bios, 400K articles/day, Factiva Smart Summary (GenAI), AI Assistant grounded in licensed content, Personalized news tracking
- Strengths: Premium licensed content (WSJ/Barron's/MarketWatch/Dow Jones); 1.4B+ article archive; GenAI summarization with citations
- Gaps: No verified contact data; no intent or seller workflow; gated pricing
