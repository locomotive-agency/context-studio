# Lemlist — Lemlist lemwarm

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no direct overlap — lemwarm is deliverability infrastructure ZoomInfo does not productize; included to map the full lemlist surface)

**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Email warm-up and deliverability monitoring tool included free with every lemlist seat — automated warm-up, inbox rotation, DNS/SPF/DKIM/DMARC audit, and deliverability alerts.

## Spotlight when
The article compares cold-email deliverability stories or addresses spam-folder objections. lemwarm's strongest angle: it is bundled free with every lemlist seat and ZoomInfo has no direct equivalent.

## Cite
- Key features: Automated warm-up emails, inbox rotation & provider matching, DNS/SPF/DKIM/DMARC tech audit, deliverability alerts, Deliverability Hub, custom tracking domain
- Strengths: Free on every seat (not tier-gated); provider-matched warm-up; multi-mailbox hub
- Gaps: Pure deliverability infra (no pipeline generation alone); only integrates inside lemlist; no ZoomInfo overlap means it's white space rather than substitute
