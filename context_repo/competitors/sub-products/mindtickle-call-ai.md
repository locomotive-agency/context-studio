# Mindtickle — Call AI (Conversation Intelligence)

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap — head-to-head conversation intelligence)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Records, transcribes, and analyzes sales calls / emails / meetings; surfaces AI-identified buying signals, themes, and deal risks; integrates with CRM for a 360-degree account view and ties skill assessments to closed business.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation-intelligence platforms vs. Chorus. Call AI is the natural head-to-head call-out, especially when the buyer wants CI bundled with broader enablement.

## Cite
- Key features: Auto capture/analysis/scoring, Buying signals & deal risks, CRM 360 view, Coaching snippets, Voice-of-customer library, Skills-to-closed-business tracking
- Strengths: Bundled with readiness/coaching workflow; skills-to-revenue attribution; voice-of-customer library for marketing
- Gaps: Gated to Transform tier; less mature deal-execution/forecast depth than Chorus; not sold standalone
