# SuperAGI — SuperAGI Super App

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (direct overlap on the marketer/RevOps front-end surface — workflow orchestration plus the MCP Market and Developer Center as the agent-builder ecosystem)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Single platform combining 50+ AI-native apps and Digital Employees across Sales, Sales Data, Marketing (SuperMarketing CDP), Voice, HR, Legal, Ecommerce, and Vibe Coding — installable from a marketplace with an MCP Market and Developer Center for publishing community apps.

## Spotlight when
The article compares marketer/RevOps front-ends or agent ecosystems. The Super App is the spotlight when the buyer wants a single platform for cross-functional agents (sales + marketing + voice + HR) plus an MCP Market for publishing internal apps.

## Cite
- Key features: 50+ AI-native apps and Digital Employees spanning Sales/Sales Data/Automations/BI/Marketing/Support/Meetings/Ecommerce/Vibe Coding/Voice/HR/Legal, Sales Data apps (Prospect, Data Enrichment, Anonymous Website Visitors, Signals), MCP Market at i.web.superagi.com/mcp + Developer Center, Marketing Agents that design social-media visuals and personalize interactions, SuperMarketing CDP across Email/SMS/WhatsApp/Mobile/Facebook/Google channels, Compliance: GDPR / SOC 2 / HIPAA / ISO
- Strengths: MCP Market open-ecosystem play; cross-vertical scope outside ZoomInfo's GTM remit; AI-Native CRM for human + AI agents
- Gaps: No GTM Context Graph fusion of CRM/conversation/intent/behavioral data; external CRM integration list not detailed; tier credit caps
