# GMass — GMass Mail Merge

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on personalized send slice)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Personalized mass-email send with merge fields drawn from Google Sheets, including conditional content blocks and unique personalized attachments per recipient.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses light-touch personalization for SMB or non-CRM workflows. GMass's per-recipient PDF/image attachments are the spotlight — uncommon vs. enterprise sequencers.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time Sheets sync, Conditional content, Personalized attachments, Fallback values, Recurring schedule
- Strengths: Per-recipient attachments; conditional content blocks; recurring sends from row triggers
- Gaps: Limited to supplied fields; no CRM-context personalization; no AI-generated copy at Workspace level
