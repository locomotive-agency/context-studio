# Saleshandy — Saleshandy Lead Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on B2B contact + company database)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
B2B contact and company database advertised at 852M+ verified contacts across 42M+ companies, paired with AI Search and waterfall enrichment across 9 unnamed data providers, with REST endpoints for programmatic enrichment.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B database scale and credit-economics for SMB buyers — Saleshandy's pay-only-for-verified credit model with rollover is the differentiator vs. ZoomInfo's enterprise quote-based contracts.

## Cite
- Key features: 852M+ contacts, 42M+ companies, 75+ filters + AI Search, waterfall across 9 providers, /v1/enrich endpoints, Net New suppression
- Strengths: Pay-only-for-verified credit model with rollover; headline volume claim above ZoomInfo; direct push into in-suite sequences
- Gaps: No first-party data fusion (no GTM Context Graph equivalent); no Forrester-class intent layer; weaker methodology disclosure
