# Clearout — Email Verifier

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the verification slice; closest ZI analogue is NeverBounce subsidiary)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Real-time and bulk email address validation with AI-driven "AI Verdict" deliverability checks claiming 99% accuracy. Flagship product — the wedge that anchors the rest of the platform.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email verification tools, NeverBounce/ZoomInfo data-accuracy alternatives, or list-hygiene workflows for SMB sales teams. Strongest spotlight when the buyer cares about pay-only-for-definitive-results economics.
