# PrivCo — PrivCo Database

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on private-company financial data, narrow ICP)

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered private-company research portal covering 5M+ U.S. private companies with proprietary revenue, EBITDA, and valuation estimates plus verified executive contacts — built for investment-led research workflows, not high-volume prospecting.

## Spotlight when
The article frames bootstrapped private-company coverage or contrasts research-led databases with GTM data foundations. The "80% below the surface" pitch is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: AI chat search, proprietary revenue/EBITDA/valuation models, 5M+ U.S. private companies, advanced filters, AI List Builder, verified executive contacts
- Strengths: Bootstrapped / non-VC-backed coverage; proprietary financial estimates with human QA; mid-market mastery
- Gaps: U.S.-only; no intent/technographics; inconsistent contact counts (1.9M vs 70M); no outbound surface
- Proof quote: "Competitors show you just 20% of the private market — PrivCo reveals the 80% below the surface" — https://www.privco.com/product
