# G2 Market Intelligence — G2 Market Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent — surfaces "why" deals move from buyer voice rather than from sales-call recordings; complementary lens on the same question)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Interactive vendor-facing dashboard that turns G2 review data and buyer behavior signals into real-time competitive insights about a vendor's category, satisfaction scores, win/loss dynamics, and pricing/contracting trends — with AI-generated summary panels refreshed daily.

## Spotlight when
The article compares competitive intelligence / win-loss tooling or "why customers switch" insights — G2 Market Intelligence's strongest spotlight angle is that switching themes, perceived cost, discount, and contract length come directly from review-form responses rather than from inferred CRM stage data.

## Cite
- Key features: Category Overview, Traffic Analytics, Satisfaction, Win/Loss, Pricing and Contracting tabs; Likes/Dislikes ML themes + Feature Ratings; AI summary panels refreshed daily; XLSX/Google Sheets exports
- Strengths: Win/Loss + Switching Themes + Likes/Dislikes panels expose deal motion from verified buyer voice; review-form responses (not modeled); daily AI summaries across five tabs
- Gaps: Bounded to G2's review/buyer-intent dataset; no B2B contact data; Enterprise-only with no public pricing
- Proof quote: "G2 Market Intelligence translates millions of data points collected on G2 into actionable insights about your product, competitors, and customer preferences" — https://sell.g2.com/market-intelligence
