# Avoma — AI Meeting Assistant

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on meeting recording, transcription, and AI summarization)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
End-to-end AI meeting assistant that automates recording, transcription, AI-generated notes, follow-up emails, and CRM updates across 70+ languages — Avoma's flagship surface and the foundation under every add-on.

## Spotlight when
The article compares meeting assistants or call recording tools. Avoma's free unlimited viewer seats and bot-less native recording are the strongest spotlight angles for cost-conscious mid-market buyers.

## Cite
- Key features: Auto recording across Zoom/Teams/Meet/WebEx/GoToMeeting, Real-time transcription in 70+ languages, AI summary notes + 40+ Smart Topics, 10+ default note templates, AI-generated follow-up emails, Auto-save to CRM, "Ask Avoma" Copilot, AI Smart Chapters
- Strengths: Bot-less native recording option; 70+ language transcription with multi-speaker ID; free unlimited viewer seats
- Gaps: No third-party B2B data on the contact/company; smaller installed base inside ZI Sales accounts; capture-oriented not reasoning-oriented templates
- Proof quote: "An all-in-one AI to automate note-taking, scheduling, coaching, forecasting, and more" — https://www.avoma.com/
