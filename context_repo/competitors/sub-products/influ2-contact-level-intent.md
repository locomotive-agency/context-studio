# Influ2 — Influ2 Contact-Level Intent

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on contact-level intent vs ZoomInfo's account-level intent feeds)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Surfaces first-party intent signals tied to named individuals — search, ads, social, third-party content, and the customer's own website — and auto-distributes ads to where each prospect is most active.

## Spotlight when
The article compares intent-data products. Contact-Level Intent's spotlight angle is "person-level intent attribution and zero-click search capture" versus account-level intent feeds.

## Cite
- Key features: Identifies named buyers researching specified topics, Search-intent tracking in zero-click scenarios, Buyer-vs-chatter separation on social posts, Contact-level website visitor identification (Beta), Auto-distributes ads to active channels
- Strengths: Contact-level intent attribution; zero-click search + social-buyer signals; channel auto-routing
- Gaps: Source mix not disclosed; contact-level visitor ID is Beta; bounded to CRM target lists
- Proof quote: "Identifies which named buyers are researching specified topics across the web" — https://www.influ2.com/product/contact-level-intent
