# Melissa — Address Verification

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on the address-hygiene slice of CRM data quality)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Verifies, corrects, standardizes, and autocompletes U.S., Canadian, and international postal addresses at point of entry or in batch, with USPS CASS & DPV and Canada Post SERP certifications and 240+ country coverage.

## Spotlight when
The article centers on form-side address validation, e-commerce checkout hygiene, or postal compliance — areas where Melissa's certifications are load-bearing and ZoomInfo Operations does not compete head-to-head.

## Cite
- Key features: USPS CASS & DPV Certified, Canada Post SERP Certified, Express Entry autocomplete, batch list cleansing, Address Verification APIs, GovCloud (FedRAMP)
- Strengths: 240+ country coverage; postal-authority certifications; FedRAMP-authorized public-sector variant
- Gaps: Postal-only scope; no B2B contact/firmographic/intent data; no GTM workflow
