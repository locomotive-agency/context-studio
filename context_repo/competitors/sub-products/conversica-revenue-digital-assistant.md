# Conversica — Conversica Revenue Digital Assistant (AI Agents — acquisition)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace — autonomous lead qualification + meeting booking)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous AI agents that run two-way conversations across email, SMS, chat, and messaging apps to qualify leads, schedule meetings, follow up on ads/content/events, and trigger CRM workflows mid-conversation.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights GTM Workspace's AI agent layer for inbound lead qualification, especially in vertical contexts (Automotive, Sports & Entertainment) where Conversica has named-CRM depth.

## Cite
- Key features: Autonomous two-way conversations across email/SMS/chat/messaging apps, Multi-language NLU + NLG with deep learning, Autonomous action chains, Industry-trained flows for Auto/Sports/Hospitality/EdTech, Open API for custom integration, Brand-controlled compliance with GDPR/SOC 2 controls
- Strengths: Autonomous two-way conversations with action chains (not script-based); vertical depth in Automotive and Sports/Entertainment; 1.5B conversations powered
- Gaps: No proprietary contact data; no GTM Context Graph; no prospecting/prioritization surface; no public APIs & MCP; no first-party pricing
