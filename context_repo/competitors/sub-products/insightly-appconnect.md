# Insightly — Insightly AppConnect

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap — workflow/integration layer that pulls ZoomInfo data into the CRM rather than competing with the data-ops engine)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Low-code/no-code integration platform that builds drag-and-drop workflows between Insightly and 2,000+ third-party apps — including a native ZoomInfo connector that initiates a search in ZoomInfo and returns companies and contact details to enrich CRM data.

## Spotlight when
The article covers integration / workflow automation buyers, or specifically the ZoomInfo + Insightly enrichment path — AppConnect is the partnership surface that materializes ZoomInfo data inside Insightly.

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop workflow designer, 2,000+ connectors, field mapping/transformation, trigger-based deployment, task-based pricing, native ZoomInfo connector
- Strengths: No-code for non-developers; ZoomInfo enrichment connector; usage-based pricing
- Gaps: $3,000 setup fee; task-based costs at scale; not a standalone CDP/data-ops platform
- Proof quote: "AppConnect will initiate a search in ZoomInfo and will return companies and contact details to enrich your CRM data" — https://www.insightly.com/integrations/zoominfo/
