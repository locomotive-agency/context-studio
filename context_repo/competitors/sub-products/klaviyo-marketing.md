# Klaviyo — Klaviyo Marketing

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (adjacent overlap on the marketer audience-build + multi-channel orchestration surface; B2C-only vs ZI's B2B account/contact targeting)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Multi-channel marketing automation (email, SMS, RCS, WhatsApp, mobile push, reviews, social) for B2C / DTC brands, built natively on the KDP customer profile.

## Spotlight when
The article frames B2C marketing automation parallels to ZoomInfo's GTM Studio — used to differentiate ZI's B2B-account targeting from Klaviyo's consumer-level orchestration.

## Cite
- Key features: Email/SMS/RCS/WhatsApp/push, pre-built flows, segmentation, AI subject lines, reviews + social, Composer prompt-to-campaign
- Strengths: Native multi-channel orchestration; Composer agent; built on KDP without external sync
- Gaps: B2C-only; no intent/firmographic enrichment; tangential to ZI's ICP
