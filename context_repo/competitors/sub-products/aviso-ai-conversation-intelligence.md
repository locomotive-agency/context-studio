# Aviso AI — Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on call recording, scoring, coaching, and theme tracking)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Captures and analyzes 1,000+ signals from sales calls (online and in-person) to produce smart transcripts, sentiment, emotion analysis, and next-best-actions — feature-for-feature overlap with Chorus.

## Spotlight when
Articles about conversation intelligence vendors or call-coaching platforms. Aviso's emotion-with-acoustic-features and 1-click in-person AV recording are useful spotlight angles vs. Chorus.

## Cite
- Key features: Smart Transcripts, Emotion analysis (text + acoustic), Aspect-based sentiment + buyer-intent mining, Real-time in-call coaching, Earnings-call analysis with GenAI summarization, Notetaker for Zoom/Teams/Meet, "Ask MIKI Anything"
- Strengths: 1,000+ signals per call; emotion analysis with acoustic features; 1-click in-person AV recording
- Gaps: Lives inside broader platform, less standalone-CI brand than Gong/Chorus; no standalone CI pricing; no Chorus-style ZI Sales integration
