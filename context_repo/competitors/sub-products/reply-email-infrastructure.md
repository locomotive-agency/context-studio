# Reply — Reply Email Infrastructure

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — Reply occupies email-sending infrastructure space ZoomInfo doesn't directly sell, but inside the seller workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Built-in email-sending infrastructure that lets users buy domains, configure mailboxes, and warm them up without leaving Reply.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses cold-email deliverability stacks or compares "buy + warm + send" infrastructure across Smartlead / Instantly / Lemwarm / Reply. Reply's bundled 14-day-to-first-send positioning is its sharpest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Buy mailboxes inside Reply with auto DNS, SMTP/OAuth connection, Auto peer-to-peer warm-up, Unlimited mailbox rotation, 98% delivery + 14-day setup
- Strengths: Bundled buy + warm-up inside platform; auto DNS config; 14-day setup path
- Gaps: Email-only infrastructure; peer-to-peer warm-up only; no inbox-placement scoring
- Proof quote: "98% email delivery and 14 days from purchase to first send" — https://reply.io/reply-email-infrastructure/
