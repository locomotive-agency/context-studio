# VisitorQueue (Leadinfo) — Autopilot (Outreach Automation)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on follow-up automation triggered off identified visitor signals)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Automates outreach to identified visitors via email and LinkedIn campaigns — connection requests, email sequences, DMU expansion, and conversion reporting layered on top of Leadinfo's visitor ID.

## Spotlight when
The article covers visitor-ID-triggered outreach or compares orchestration tools that act on anonymous-traffic signals. Lean on the bundled-with-visitor-ID angle.
