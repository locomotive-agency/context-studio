# Validity DemandTools — Assess

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on CRM data quality monitoring / scoring)
**Spotlight rank:** 6

## What it does
Profile and score Salesforce data quality across Lead, Contact, Account, and Opportunity objects to guide remediation work — five-tier framework (Unactionable / Insufficient / Limited / Acceptable / Validified) with continuous monitoring.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM data quality scoring or continuous monitoring tools. Lean on the five-tier grading and direct routing into other DemandTools modules for remediation.
