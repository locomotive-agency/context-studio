# Clearout — Form Guard

**Competes with:** FormComplete (adjacent overlap — Form Guard blocks junk; FormComplete enriches/auto-fills)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Real-time email/phone/name validation embedded into web forms, exit pop-ups, chatbots, WooCommerce checkouts, surveys, and newsletters to block fake/spam submissions before they reach the CRM.

## Spotlight when
The article compares form-validation/anti-spam tooling, FormComplete alternatives at the SMB end, or no-code form-protection embeds. Strongest spotlight when buyer's primary problem is junk submissions, not enrichment.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time email/phone/name validation at capture, blocks disposable/invalid/gibberish, no-code dashboard, unlimited forms on paid plans, supports many form surfaces
- Strengths: No-code embed across many surfaces; validates email + phone + name in one widget; freemium entry
- Gaps: Spam-blocking only (no enrichment/auto-fill); no contact lookup; defensive vs offensive
- Proof quote: "Real-time validation of email, phone, and name fields at point of capture" — https://clearout.io/
