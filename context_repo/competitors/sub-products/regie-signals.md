# Regie.ai — Signals & Use-Case Plays

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on signal monitoring and account/buyer-movement plays)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Monitors 100+ signals across web, social, news, technographics, and CRM, packaged into use-case plays such as Named Account Signals, Known Buyer Movement, Behavioral Intent, Marketing Interaction, and Re-Engagement & Expansion.

## Spotlight when
The article compares intent / signal layers or buyer-movement plays. Regie's packaged-play library (M&A, leadership change, tech swap, job change, closed-lost re-entry) is its strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: 100+ signals across web/news/technographics/CRM, Unlimited custom signals + action triggers, Job-change + buyer-movement detection, Account-change plays (M&A / leadership / tech swap), Buying-committee mapping
- Strengths: Pre-packaged play library; Closed-Lost Re-Entry play; unlimited custom signals
- Gaps: No proprietary intent panel (relies on aggregation); no GTM Context Graph reasoning layer; advanced volume gated behind credit packages
- Proof quote: "100+ built-in signal monitoring across web, social, news, technographics and CRM" — https://www.regie.ai/use-cases/named-account-signals
