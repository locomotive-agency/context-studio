# Emailable — Real-Time Email Validation Widget

**Competes with:** FormComplete (adjacent overlap — validation at form capture vs FormComplete's full identity enrichment)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Drop-in HTML form widget that validates email addresses at the point of capture — installable via copy-paste with customizable parameters.

## Spotlight when
The article compares form-capture-time email validation. Use to highlight scope difference: Emailable validates email format/deliverability at submit; FormComplete enriches with full identity data.

## Cite
- Key features: Drop-in HTML widget, copy-paste install, validates at capture on any HTML form, customizable parameters, 1 credit per validation
- Strengths: No-dev copy-paste install; form-agnostic; per-validation credit pricing
- Gaps: Validation only — no firmographic enrichment; no identity resolution; narrower than FormComplete
- Proof quote: "Validates at point of capture on any HTML form" — https://emailable.com/
