# Factiva — Factiva Curated Newsletters

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (no overlap — content licensing / PR distribution, no GTM equivalent)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Editor-curated news briefings and licensed content packages distributed via newsletter, email, intranet, RSS, or mobile — built on top of the Factiva database with human curation by Dow Jones editors.

## Spotlight when
Only spotlight when the article needs to delineate where Factiva diverges from GTM platforms entirely (e.g., "what does ZoomInfo *not* compete with"). Curated Newsletters is fully outside GTM scope.
