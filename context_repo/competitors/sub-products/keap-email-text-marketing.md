# Keap — Keap Email & Text Marketing

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on email send / nurture; smaller SMB scope, no ABM)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
SMB-grade email + SMS + voice marketing — broadcasts, 1:1, triggered nurture, and a dedicated business phone line — all priced inside Keap's single platform plan.

## Spotlight when
The article compares SMB email + SMS suites for service businesses (Keap vs ActiveCampaign vs Mailchimp) — when bundled voice/SMS with email and a dedicated business line are decision criteria.

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop email builder, broadcasts/1:1/newsletters/triggered emails, deliverability dashboard, US SMS + business phone line, public SMS tier pricing
- Strengths: Bundled email + SMS + voice with dedicated business line; public SMS tier pricing; Tier 1 SMS included in base
- Gaps: US-only SMS, US/CA voice; no RCS/WhatsApp/push; no intent/ABM orchestration
- Proof quote: "Sends broadcasts, 1:1 messages, automated nurture campaigns, and SMS/voice broadcasts to segmented audiences" — https://keap.com/marketing/email-marketing
