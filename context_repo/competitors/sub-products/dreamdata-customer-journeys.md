# Dreamdata — Dreamdata Customer Journeys

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on account-based journey unification)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Out-of-the-box interactive customer journey timelines that show every touchpoint (known and anonymous) an account makes through the funnel, with stakeholder, channel, and stage overlays.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to compare account-level journey visualization or multi-stakeholder buying-committee mapping. Use when ZoomInfo Context Graph reasoning is the primary frame.

## Cite
- Key features: Interactive timelines per account, multi-stakeholder view (known + anonymous), stakeholder persona analysis, channel/source/campaign overlay, stage-duration bottlenecks, LinkedIn + G2 intent partner
- Strengths: Out-of-the-box journey timelines; anonymous + known unification; official LinkedIn + G2 intent partner
- Gaps: Visualization layer — not reasoning/agent surface; no Chorus inputs; depends on third-party contact data
