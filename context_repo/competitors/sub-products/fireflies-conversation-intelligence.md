# Fireflies — Fireflies Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on conversation analytics, topic tracking, and coaching)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Analytics suite that extracts speaker, topic, and sentiment insights from recorded meetings to coach teams and surface trends — bundled into the Business+ tier alongside team analytics dashboards.

## Spotlight when
The article frames CI as "self-serve and affordable" vs. enterprise platform pricing. Fireflies CI is the spotlight when the angle is "Chorus-equivalent analytics at $19/seat/mo."

- Key features: Speaker talk-time, Talk-to-listen ratio + monologues + filler counts, Sentiment, Topic trackers, Question + key topic detection, Team analytics for admins
- Strengths: Self-serve $19/seat/mo Business tier; sentiment + topic trackers; admin analytics dashboard
- Gaps: CI gated to Business+; analytics depth lags Chorus; no underlying B2B data
