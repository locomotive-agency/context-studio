# Smartlead — SmartInfra

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-execution / outbound-sender slice)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Dedicated-tenant cold-email sending infrastructure with unlimited mailboxes, automated DNS / warm-up, and IP rotation built to land cold email in the primary inbox.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare GTM Workspace's send capabilities to dedicated-tenant cold-email infra, or argue that agency / Fortune-500 outbound teams need isolated sender reputation that ZoomInfo's bundled seller surface doesn't deliver.

## Cite
- Key features: Unlimited mailboxes; auto-DNS / SPF / DKIM / DMARC; reward-based warm-up; dedicated-tenant IPs; sender rotation across Google + Outlook; real-time reputation tracking
- Strengths: Dedicated-tenant isolation; unlimited mailbox economics; auto-config of authentication
- Gaps: Sender-only; no contact database; no full seller workflow / dialer / CI bundle
- Proof quote: "Unlock the Full Power of AI Outbound." — https://www.smartlead.ai/
