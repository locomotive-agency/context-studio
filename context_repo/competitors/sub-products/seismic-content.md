# Seismic — Seismic Content

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller content / playbook surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Centralized library to build, maintain, and control sales content so reps can find and share personalized assets while preserving the original version, with brand and version control and content-engagement analytics.

## Spotlight when
The article covers enterprise content governance for regulated industries (FS, healthcare, manufacturing) where reps need pre-approved, version-controlled materials. ZoomInfo doesn't sell a comparable content-governance surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Content library + personalization, LiveSend shareable links, brand/version control, content-usage and findability gains, regulated-industry governance
- Strengths: Enterprise content authoring/governance depth; personalization tied to engagement analytics; Oracle 34K+ shares proof
- Gaps: Content is customer-supplied; no CRM enrichment; no conversation-capture surface
- Proof quote: "5-6x increase in content usage" — https://www.seismic.com/solutions/teams/sales-enablement/
