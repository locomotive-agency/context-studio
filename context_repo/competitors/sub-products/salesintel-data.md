# SalesIntel — SalesIntel Data

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the verified-contact data foundation underneath the seller surface, with human-verification depth as the wedge)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-plus-human-verified B2B contact, firmographic, technographic, and intent data layer — 200M+ contacts, 54M+ mobile numbers, 42K+ technologies — verified by 2,000+ human researchers with a 95% accuracy guarantee, plus Research on Demand to fill gaps.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data foundations on data quality + verification rigor. SalesIntel is the spotlight when the buyer wants human-verified mobile coverage at enterprise scale and is willing to "pay one price for one platform" with unlimited credits.

- Key features: 200M+ verified B2B contacts and 54M+ verified mobile numbers, 95% accuracy guarantee with human verification by 2000+ researchers, 42K+ technologies tracked for technographic intelligence, Research on Demand — custom data requests fulfilled "every 45 seconds", RevDriver+ Chrome extension for prospecting on LinkedIn / Sales Navigator / company sites, SOC 2 certified, GDPR and CCPA compliant
