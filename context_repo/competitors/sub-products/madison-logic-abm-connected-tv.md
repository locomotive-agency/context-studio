# Madison Logic — ABM Connected TV

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — CTV is a media channel ZoomInfo does not sell)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Connected-TV advertising channel for ABM, delivering full-screen video ads to decision-makers at target accounts across streaming devices, with funnel-stage personalization.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on enterprise brand-marketing within ABM or buying-group brand exposure. ABM CTV's strongest spotlight: full-screen brand-safe placements driven by ML Insights intent — channel breadth ZoomInfo cannot match.
