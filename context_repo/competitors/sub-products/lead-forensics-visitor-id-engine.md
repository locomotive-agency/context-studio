# Lead Forensics — Lead Forensics Website Visitor Identification engine

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap — both reveal companies visiting a customer's website in real time)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Core technology layer — a lightweight tracking snippet captures sessions and reverse-IP-matches them against Lead Forensics' proprietary IP-to-company database to return company-level records with firmographics, pages visited, session detail, and decision-maker contacts.

## Spotlight when
The article compares website visitor identification platforms head-to-head with ZoomInfo WebSights. The proprietary-IP-database differentiator and "real-time, not end-of-day" pitch are the strongest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Reverse-IP snippet, Proprietary IP-to-company DB, Real-time alerts, ICP/account watchlists, Appended decision-maker contacts
- Strengths: Proprietary database control; real-time vs end-of-day; native decision-maker contacts on each company
- Gaps: Match rate degrades on residential/SMB/remote traffic; contacts are visitor-scoped only; no Context Graph fusion
- Proof quote: "Built and maintained solely for their solution." — https://www.leadforensics.com/website-visitor-identification/
