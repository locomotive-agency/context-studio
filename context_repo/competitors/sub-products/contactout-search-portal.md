# ContactOut — ContactOut Search Portal

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the prospecting database surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Standalone search UI providing access to a database of 350M professionals across 36–40M companies, with 20+ filters for sales and recruiting prospecting, bulk export, and CRM/ATS sync.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B contact databases on coverage, accuracy, or commercial flexibility. ContactOut's consumption-based "$299/mo team" offer and personal-email layer are its strongest counter-pitch points.

## Cite
- Key features: 350M professionals, 20+ filters, Bulk save + CRM/ATS sync, CSV export, Email-campaign push
- Strengths: Personal-email coverage layered on LinkedIn; triple-verified 99% claim; consumption-based pricing
- Gaps: Smaller dataset than ZoomInfo; no intent data; fair-use caps on "unlimited" plans
- Proof quote: "a goldmine for sales & recruitment data... same data Zoominfo offers... lower price" — https://contactout.com/search-portal
