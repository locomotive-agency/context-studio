# Pipedrive — Pipedrive AI

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — AI features that touch the seller's daily surface but no agentic-seller layer)

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Cross-product OpenAI-powered features inside the CRM — email drafting/summarization, suggested replies, natural-language report creation, AI marketplace search, deal-recommendation notifications, and AI import mapping.

## Spotlight when
The article evaluates AI inside affordable CRMs or compares vendors on data-privacy posture. Pipedrive's explicit no-train-on-customer-data clause is unusually clear.

- Key features: AI email writer, AI summarization, AI suggested replies, AI report creation, AI marketplace search, AI notifications, AI import

- Gaps: No agentic SDR layer; no BYO-LLM; no MCP / developer AI surface
