# Oliv AI — Forecaster

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the forecasting/pipeline-rhythm surface — Oliv operates as a forecasting agent rather than full GTM Workspace)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
AI agent that builds an accurate, logic-driven forecast by analyzing each opportunity's activity, free from optimism bias — auto-updates the core four after every meeting and delivers weekly one-pagers.

## Spotlight when
The article positions agentic forecasting as an alternative to Clari/Aviso — Forecaster's "core four" continuous update and Thursday review cadence are the spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Core-four updates after every meeting, Thursday weekly review, Monday weekly one-pager, Roll-up + aggregated forecasting
- Strengths: Activity-based logic-driven forecast; continuous updates; modular pricing
- Gaps: Newer vs. Clari/Aviso/InsightSquared at enterprise; CRM/conversation-only signals; lighter roll-up depth/customization
