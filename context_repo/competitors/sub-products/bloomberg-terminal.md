# Bloomberg — Bloomberg Terminal

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (none — Bloomberg covers market data, not B2B GTM data)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Computer software workstation delivering real-time financial market data, news, analytics, messaging and trade execution to financial professionals across every asset class.

## Spotlight when
The article frames "data platform" as broader than B2B GTM — only spotlight Bloomberg as a tangential / market-data contrast. Do not call it a head-to-head ZoomInfo substitute.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time market data across all asset classes, Bloomberg News, Instant Bloomberg messaging (350K+ decision makers), Bloomberg Launchpad workspace, custom keyboard with B-Unit biometric authentication
- Strengths: 325K+ subscriber closed network; full-asset-class real-time price feeds; two-year lease commitment moat
- Gaps: No B2B contact / company / intent data; no GTM Workspace; no conversation intelligence
- Proof quote: "The financial world in full focus built on next gen technology." — https://www.bloomberg.com/professional/
