# Woodpecker — Woodpecker Cold Email

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller outbound-execution surface — sequences, deliverability, multichannel)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Cold email outreach automation that runs up to 16-step multichannel campaigns combining email, LinkedIn manual tasks, and phone calls — with free warm-up, free catch-all verification, free deliverability monitor, and Adaptive Sending throttling.

## Spotlight when
The article compares cold email / SMB outbound tools against ZoomInfo's seat-based GTM Workspace. Lean on unlimited mailboxes at no cost, free deliverability extras, and per-contacted-prospect pricing.

## Cite
- Key features: Up to 16-step campaigns, Inbox rotation across unlimited mailboxes, Adaptive Sending, Free warm-up + verify + monitor, AI interest-level reply detection, Snippet personalization with empty-field skip
- Strengths: Unlimited mailboxes + team members; free deliverability extras; per-contacted-prospect pricing
- Gaps: No conversation intelligence; no intent / third-party signals; lighter analytics than enterprise sequencing
