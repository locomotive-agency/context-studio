# Regie.ai — Data Enrichment

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the contact data foundation, with smaller scale and waterfall sourcing)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Built-in waterfall enrichment giving access to 220M+ validated contacts and routing requests to the most cost-effective vendor.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses bundled-data AI sales platforms vs. dedicated B2B data foundations — Regie's no-per-contact-fee positioning vs. ZoomInfo's scale advantage is the cleanest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: 220M+ contacts with no per-contact charge, Multi-source waterfall + dedupe + refresh, AI ICP-fit detection from past wins, Signal/intent prioritization, Direct activation into Regie workflows
- Strengths: Bundled contact pool with no per-contact fee; cost-effective vendor routing; direct push to Auto-Pilot agents
- Gaps: Smaller contact universe vs. ZoomInfo; no first-party intent/technographics; resells ZoomInfo Sales as one source
