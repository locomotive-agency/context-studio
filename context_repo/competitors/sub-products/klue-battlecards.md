# Klue — Klue Dynamic Battlecards

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — both put deal-time content in front of sellers, but Klue is a dedicated PMM authoring + distribution surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Authoring + distribution surface for competitor-specific battlecards consumed by sellers in live deals. Battlecards are powered by the Knowledge Hub, embedded in Slack / Salesforce / Teams / Highspot / Seismic / Showpad / SharePoint, and tracked for adoption and revenue-influence via Klue Reports.

## Spotlight when
The article compares sales enablement surfaces or seller-facing battlecard distribution and the buyer wants a PMM-owned authoring tool rather than relying on ZoomInfo's general GTM Workspace surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Dynamic Tier 1 battlecards, Knowledge Hub-driven messaging, Klue Reports adoption tracking, Slack/SF/Teams/Highspot/Seismic/Showpad embeds, Browser/mobile/email distribution
- Strengths: Distribution-first; revenue-influence reporting; high seller adoption (70%+ at HackerOne/Gainsight)
- Gaps: PMM/CI-owned authoring not general workspace; ZoomInfo does not have a direct PMM battlecard product; competitor-only scope
