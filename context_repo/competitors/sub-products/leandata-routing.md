# LeanData — LeanData Routing

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on lead-to-account matching, routing, and Salesforce data hygiene)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Salesforce-native, no-code matching and routing platform that fuzzy-matches inbound leads/contacts to accounts and routes them to the right rep via a drag-and-drop FlowBuilder, with SLA enforcement, audit logs, and multi-object support across Salesforce standard and custom objects.

## Spotlight when
The article compares lead-routing or CRM-data-quality stacks vs. ZoomInfo Operations / RingLead. LeanData's published vs-page is the strongest spotlight angle — it is the rare competitor with a named head-to-head pitch against an actual ZoomInfo product.

## Cite
- Key features: Lead-to-account fuzzy matching at 95% accuracy, drag-and-drop FlowBuilder, multi-object routing, SLA re-routing, Slack notifications with interactive buttons, automated dedupe/merge (Cloudingo node)
- Strengths: Salesforce-native architecture; visual FlowBuilder + detailed audit logs; published vs-ZoomInfo pitch
- Gaps: No B2B contact/company database; no first-party intent or technographics; Salesforce-first (Dynamics supported secondarily)
- Proof quote: "While ZoomInfo Operations/RingLead may work for simple use cases, LeanData offers the most scalable go-to-market tools for organizations focused on growth and precision routing." — https://www.leandata.com/blog/leandata-versus-zoominfo-ringlead-which-is-better/
