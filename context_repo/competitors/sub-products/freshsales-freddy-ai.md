# Freshsales — Freddy AI

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — AI-agent layer scoped to first-party CRM data inside Freshsales rather than ZI's broader GTM context graph)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Generative-AI assistant embedded inside Freshsales that scores leads, drafts and rephrases sales emails, surfaces priority deals, deduplicates records, detects out-of-office replies, and (on Enterprise) provides forecasting insights.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-native AI assistants vs. ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI — Freddy AI's strongest spotlight angle is its inclusion across paid tiers without a separate AI SKU.

## Cite
- Key features: Contact scoring, Sales emails by Freddy AI, Deal insights, Deduplication, Out-of-office detection, Forecasting insights (Enterprise tier)
- Strengths: AI woven through CRM rather than a separate tier; inline email drafting/rephrasing; forecasting insights on Enterprise
- Gaps: Limited to first-party CRM data; no conversation intelligence; metered AI Agent sessions ($49 per 100)
