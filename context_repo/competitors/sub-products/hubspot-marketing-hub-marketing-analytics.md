# HubSpot Marketing Hub — HubSpot Marketing Analytics & Dashboards

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on the attribution / measurement surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Built-in analytics, reports, and dashboards to measure marketing campaigns, multi-touch revenue attribution, and customer journey performance in one place — Pathfinder customer journey analytics covers up to 15 stages and 60 months.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses marketing attribution, dashboards, or campaign measurement. Useful framing: HubSpot's attribution is built-in vs. add-on tools, but ABM rigor at enterprise scale lags ZoomInfo Marketing.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-touch attribution, Website performance metrics, Pre-built dashboards, Custom CRM reports, Customer journey analytics
- Strengths: Built-in attribution; unified tracking code; 15-stage customer journey analytics
- Gaps: Tied to HubSpot-tracked touchpoints; journey analytics Enterprise-gated; limited warehouse blending
- Proof quote: "Track the full customer lifecycle with multi-touch revenue attribution" — https://www.hubspot.com/products/marketing/analytics
