# UpLead — UpLead Data Enrichment

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the CRM-enrichment workflow as part of the broader prospecting suite — 50-attribute appends with real-time verification)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Uploads existing CRM/database files and appends 50+ contact and company data points with real-time verification of both supplied and returned data, with native CRM integrations and bi-directional sync on Pro tier.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights CRM enrichment / data quality workflows for SMB/mid-market teams. UpLead Data Enrichment is the spotlight when the buyer wants real-time-verified CRM enrichment with a 90-day refresh cadence at Plus-tier pricing.

## Cite
- Key features: Uploads CRM/database files and appends 50+ contact and company data points, Real-time verification of supplied and returned data, Bulk processing of uploaded lists, 8-step data collection and verification pipeline; database refreshed every 90 days, Native CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, MS Dynamics 365, Pipedrive, Insightly, Copper, Nimble, Close), Bi-directional CRM sync available on Professional tier
- Strengths: Real-time verification of supplied + returned data; 8-step pipeline + 90-day refresh; accessible at Plus tier without enterprise contract
- Gaps: Bi-directional sync gated to Pro; no dedupe/routing workflows; ~50-attribute enrichment cap
- Proof quote: "UpLead guarantees 95% accuracy with real-time email verification at the moment of download and issues credit refunds for any contacts that bounce." — https://www.uplead.com/uplead-vs-zoominfo/
