# Contify — Contify Business News API

**Competes with:** APIs & MCP (adjacent overlap on programmatic, signal-shaped delivery)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Developer-facing API delivering GenAI-enriched, structured business news data on companies, industries, and topics for embedding into apps, CRMs, BI tools, and LLM pipelines — with entity/event/sentiment tags, AI-generated highlights, and a 99.9% SLA.

## Spotlight when
The article compares developer-accessible signal APIs or argues for non-English news coverage as a differentiator. Strongest spotlight angle is auto-translation across 117+ languages with high throughput.

## Cite
- Key features: 1 Mn+ sources (250k+ non-English), 700k+ companies / 900k+ calls/day, 50+ business activity tags, REST/JSON + webhooks, 99.9% SLA
- Strengths: Non-English coverage at scale; LLM-ready highlights and tags; webhook delivery
- Gaps: News payload only (no contact/firmographic data); REST-only (no MCP); BI-shaped vs seller-shaped
- Proof quote: "GenAI-enriched, structured business news data on companies, industries, and topics for embedding into apps, CRMs, BI tools, and LLM pipelines" — https://www.contify.com/news-api/
