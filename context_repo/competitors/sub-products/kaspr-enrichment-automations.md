# Kaspr — Kaspr Enrichment Automations

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on bulk/CSV enrichment workflows; smaller scale and LinkedIn-source-bound)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Bulk and CSV enrichment workflows that pull contact data from LinkedIn search, Sales Navigator, Groups, Events, or uploaded LinkedIn URLs and push results into CRM or CSV.

## Spotlight when
The article compares lightweight enrichment workflows for SMB RevOps (vs ZoomInfo Operations) — when LinkedIn-sourced bulk enrichment with public pricing is the wedge, but warehouse destinations and governance are not required.

## Cite
- Key features: LinkedIn/Sales Nav/Groups/Events enrichment, CSV upload, 500/1,000/2,500-contact launches by tier, 3/10/Unlimited launches/month, auto-export to CRM/CSV
- Strengths: LinkedIn-source-aware enrichment; tiered + public pricing; self-serve setup
- Gaps: Smaller per-launch caps than ZoomInfo Operations; no warehouse destinations; no governance/lineage
- Proof quote: "Workflows that enrich contacts in bulk from LinkedIn searches, Sales Navigator, LinkedIn Groups, Events or uploaded CSVs" — https://www.kaspr.io/pricing
