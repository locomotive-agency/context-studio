# HubSpot Marketing Hub — HubSpot Clearbit / Breeze Intelligence

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the B2B data + visitor intent surface, bundled into HubSpot)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
B2B data enrichment product (Clearbit acquired by HubSpot 2024, now Breeze Intelligence) that enriches contact and company records with 100+ standardized data points and reveals visitor intent for HubSpot users.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data + intent vendors or addresses the HubSpot ↔ ZoomInfo data overlap. Clearbit-by-HubSpot is the bundled-data story — flag the manual_review:recent_ownership_change context.

## Cite
- Key features: 100+ attribute enrichment, Form shortening, Target market builder (250M+ decision-makers), Reveal Intent (IP-based), Channel-source filtering
- Strengths: Bundled inside Smart CRM; IP visitor reveal + pricing-page activity; form-shortening at submit
- Gaps: Bundled (no longer standalone); shallower than 500M ZoomInfo contacts; IP-reveal vs. topic-volume intent
- Proof quote: "Enrich your records with 100+ B2B data points and reveal anonymous visitor intent" — https://www.hubspot.com/products/clearbit
