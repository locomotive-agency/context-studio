# Showpad — Showpad Content Management

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller content / playbook surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Centralizes, governs, and surfaces marketing-approved sales content (spec sheets, pricing, proof points) across mobile, offline, and browser surfaces with versioning and AI-powered search/FAQs.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses regulated, field-sales industries that need version-controlled, compliance-approved content delivered offline. ZoomInfo doesn't sell this surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Content Library/Pages/Collections; AI content governance; AI Search + Asset FAQs; AI Page Builder; mobile/offline access
- Strengths: Field-first delivery (mobile + offline); compliance/versioning for regulated catalogs; AI Search/FAQs in-context
- Gaps: Customer-supplied content only; no CRM enrichment; no conversation-capture surface
