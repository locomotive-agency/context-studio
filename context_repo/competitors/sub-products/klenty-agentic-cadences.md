# Klenty — Klenty Agentic Cadences

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI-agent layer that researches accounts and drafts outreach)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI agent layer that researches accounts and prospects across 150+ public data sources + LinkedIn, drafts 1:1 emails using sales frameworks, and orchestrates personalized multi-channel cadences automatically — repositioning reps as approvers of AI-executed work rather than executors.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on AI SDR / agentic cadence execution and the buyer is comparing ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI agents against per-prospect 1:1 AI personalization. Klenty's "approver, not executor" framing is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Research Feed (150+ sources), Tailored cadences per prospect, AI-written cold emails + follow-ups, AI Variables for subject/opener/body/CTA, Nurture auto-enrollment
- Strengths: "Approver not executor" positioning; per-prospect 1:1 personalization; insights piped into prospect fields
- Gaps: Narrower AI agent stack than GTM Workspace; no conversation-grounded reasoning loop; no marketer/RevOps agents
- Proof quote: "Move from order takers to approvers of AI-executed work." — https://www.klenty.com/feature/agentic-cadences
