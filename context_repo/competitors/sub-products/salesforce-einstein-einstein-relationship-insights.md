# Salesforce Einstein — Einstein Relationship Insights

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on relationship/company research)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Automated research assistant that searches web, news, and Salesforce Files to map relationships between people and companies — surfaces context inside Opportunities and Cases.

## Spotlight when
The article evaluates account research / relationship intelligence inside CRM; Einstein Relationship Insights is the Salesforce-bundled alternative to dedicated relationship-graph products.

## Cite
- Key features: Relationship graphs, browser extension, news/web/Files research, multiple connection tracking, Opportunity/Case context
- Strengths: Web + news + Files unified, browser extension, two-tier pricing
- Gaps: No verified contacts/dials, web-search reliance, limited intent/technographic reach
