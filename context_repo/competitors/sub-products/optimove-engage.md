# Optimove — Optimove Engage

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — both deliver multichannel campaign execution, but Optimove targets individual consumer profiles vs. ZoomInfo's account-targeted ABM)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-powered customer engagement platform for designing, executing, and optimizing multichannel campaigns (email, SMS, push, web) with embedded CDP functionality.

## Spotlight when
The article positions B2C customer-engagement campaigns vs. B2B ABM as parallel multichannel surfaces — useful for contrast articles, not head-to-head.

## Cite
- Key features: Native email/SMS/push/web/ads/WhatsApp, Generative AI copy + creative, Embedded CDP, Visual journey canvas, 150+ connectors, Incrementality + multitouch attribution + experimentation, AI content governance
- Strengths: Embedded CDP without external dependency; 150+ connectors; built-in incrementality on revenue
- Gaps: B2C consumer profiles only; message-volume pricing unfit for B2B budgets; no first-party intent or firmographic enrichment
