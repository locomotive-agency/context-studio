# HubSpot — Sales Hub

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — CRM-native seller surface; complementary as CRM-of-record but increasingly competitive at AI layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
HubSpot's CRM-native sales platform — sequences, email tracking, dialer, meeting scheduler, with Breeze AI layered in.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-native sales tools or all-in-one SMB/mid-market platforms. Frame ZoomInfo as the data + intelligence layer that powers any CRM, not as a CRM replacement.
