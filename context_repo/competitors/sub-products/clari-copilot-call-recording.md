# Clari Copilot — Sales Call Recording

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on the call-capture layer of conversation intelligence)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
The call-capture layer of Copilot — automatically records, transcribes, and analyzes every sales call (audio and video) and stores them in a searchable cloud library for sharing and coaching.

## Spotlight when
The article compares basic call-recording capabilities, async-coaching workflows, or "searchable call libraries." Strongest spotlight when the buyer is solving for the capture layer specifically (not full CI breadth).

## Cite
- Key features: Auto-record every video/audio call, searchable call logs by category/topic/deal-stage/duration, AI-tagged key moments, shareable snippets, call analytics, 2x playback
- Strengths: Searchable cloud library; AI-tagged key moments; snippet sharing for async coaching
- Gaps: Capture-layer only (coaching/playbooks live in Copilot proper); no firmographic context; not standalone
- Proof quote: "Automatic recording of every video and audio sales call" — https://www.clari.com/call-recording-software/
