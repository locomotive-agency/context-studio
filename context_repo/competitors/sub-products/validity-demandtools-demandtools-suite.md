# Validity DemandTools — DemandTools (suite)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on CRM data quality / hygiene)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Salesforce data quality platform that automates CRM data cleansing — deduplication, standardization, mass updates, imports/exports, and ownership management — to keep Salesforce orgs duplicate-free and report-ready.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM data hygiene tools or Salesforce-native data quality alternatives to ZoomInfo Operations. Lean on the 20-year pioneer status and self-service implementation speed against ZoomInfo Operations' broader (but pricier) RevOps platform footprint.

## Cite
- Key features: 20+ matching algorithms, DupeBlocker native Salesforce app, Mass update / standardization, Bulk import/export with pre-import dedupe, BriteVerify-powered email validation, Scheduled scenarios + restore files
- Strengths: 20-year Salesforce-native pioneer; ~10-minute self-service install; scheduled scenarios with restore files
- Gaps: No third-party data sourcing; no intent or technographic enrichment; Salesforce-only (Dynamics 365 dedupe-only edition)
