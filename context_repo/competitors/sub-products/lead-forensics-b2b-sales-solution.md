# Lead Forensics — Lead Forensics B2B Sales solution

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap on seller-facing visitor-ID alerts and pipeline re-engagement)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Sales-team-oriented packaging of the Lead Forensics platform — real-time alerts when pipeline accounts revisit, ICP segmentation of website traffic, lapsed-customer win-back identification, and decision-maker contact data pushed to reps' channels (email, Slack, CRM) with engagement-criteria-based alert prioritization.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on pipeline re-engagement or lapsed-customer win-back use cases for sales teams using website visitor identification.

## Cite
- Key features: Pipeline-revisit alerts, Lapsed-customer win-back, ICP segmentation, Decision-maker contacts to reps, Engagement-prioritized alerts
- Strengths: Pipeline re-engagement framing, "80% chance" win-back claim, Native decision-maker contact push
- Gaps: Use-case packaging only — same engine as Essential/Automate; no CI; no general B2B contact universe
