# Madison Logic — ABM Display Advertising

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — display-channel ABM activation that ZoomInfo does not sell directly as media inventory)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Targeted display ad activation across video, native, and premium display placements aimed at decision-makers at intent-identified accounts, with account-stage-adaptive messaging.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses ABM media-buying or display-channel activation against named accounts. Display Advertising's strongest spotlight: Madison Logic operates the DSP — ZoomInfo can identify accounts but cannot directly buy the display media against them.

## Cite
- Key features: AI intent data targeting, Video/Native/Premium Display formats, multi-stage targeting, real-time performance + ROI tracking, account-stage-adaptive messaging
- Strengths: Owned DSP for display (ZI does not sell display inventory); account-stage-adaptive creative; premium + video placements
- Gaps: Media spend layered on platform fees; no GTM Workspace overlap; pure media activation (no sales surface)
