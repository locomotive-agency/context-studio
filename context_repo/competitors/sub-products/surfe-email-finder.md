# Surfe — Surfe Email Finder / Waterfall Enrichment

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on verified email / mobile / contact + company records)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Multi-source contact-enrichment engine that combines 15+ underlying data providers and ZeroBounce verification to source verified professional emails and mobile numbers, with credits only deducted on successful matches.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data vendors on accuracy and pricing fairness. Surfe's strongest pitch is the publicly published 89.42% vs. ZoomInfo 52.32% benchmark and the credit-on-match-only model.

## Cite
- Key features: 93% find rate, 15+ provider waterfall, ZeroBounce validation, credits only on success, 500M contacts / 350M companies, multi-channel access
- Strengths: Multi-vendor waterfall in one sub; pay-only-on-match; published find-rate dashboards
- Gaps: Smaller core universe; weaker US enterprise direct dial; no first-party context graph
- Proof quote: "Surfe verified 89.42% of emails versus ZoomInfo's 52.32%" — https://www.surfe.com/blog/surfe-vs-zoominfo-5k-comparison/
