# Braze — Braze Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent — B2C engagement platform vs B2B marketing platform; ICP mismatch)

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Vertically-integrated B2C customer engagement platform that unifies first-party data, orchestrates multi-step journeys, and delivers cross-channel messaging powered by BrazeAI.

## Spotlight when
Generally not spotlighted in ZoomInfo articles — only useful as ICP-mismatched contrast (B2C engagement vs B2B GTM). The platform's scale claims and Gartner MQ Leader status are the strongest spotlight angles when contrast is the goal.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time data unification; Canvas Orchestration; cross-channel delivery; AI reporting; BrazeAI throughout
- Strengths: Vertically integrated; scale (4.5T messages / 17.9B AI decisions); real-time streaming
- Gaps: B2C only; no third-party data layer; no seller workspace
- Proof quote: "Rewrite the rules of customer engagement with AI" — https://www.braze.com/
