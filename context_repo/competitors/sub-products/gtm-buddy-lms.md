# GTM Buddy — GTM Buddy LMS

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no overlap — LMS is a category ZoomInfo does not sell into)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Sales LMS that emphasizes execution capability over completion rates — AI drafts courses from playbooks, reinforces lessons in-flow during real deals, and ties learning to coaching and Ask Buddy in-context reinforcement.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses sales onboarding, ramp acceleration, or training measurement. Useful as evidence that GTM Buddy is broader than its in-flow copilot, but only adjacent to ZoomInfo's footprint.
