# Aviso AI — Revenue Forecasting

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (direct overlap on the multi-signal reasoning layer that produces forecast explanations)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-driven forecasting that auto-rolls up forecasts across reps and teams, ingests CRM and non-CRM data, and supports both ACV and consumption (usage-based) revenue models with 98%+ accuracy claims.

## Spotlight when
Articles comparing forecasting approaches or consumption-revenue platforms. Aviso's consumption forecasting is its strongest differentiator — call it out when the article touches usage-based or hybrid revenue models.

## Cite
- Key features: Auto-roll-up with best-case/most-likely, Winscore Explanations across CRM + engagement + conversations + history, Consumption revenue forecasting, CRM-independent hierarchies, 98%+ accuracy claim, Bidirectional CRM sync + multi-CRM unification
- Strengths: Native consumption forecasting; 98%+ accuracy headline; CRM-independent hierarchies
- Gaps: No B2B data foundation; no marketing/ABM signal mix; relies on customer integrations
- Proof quote: "Get Nearly 100% Forecasting Accuracy" — https://www.aviso.com/product/revenue-forecasting
