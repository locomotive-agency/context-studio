# Snov.io — Sales CRM

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — bundled lightweight CRM alongside the seller front-end)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Free built-in sales CRM for managing leads, deals, tasks, and pipelines alongside Snov.io's outreach tools, with multiple pipelines, up to 100 stages, deal-loss tracking, and Google Calendar sync.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo's CRM-adjacent surfaces (Operations) to bundled sales CRMs, or argue that solopreneurs / SMB want pipeline management without a separate CRM subscription.

## Cite
- Key features: Free with every plan; up to 100 pipeline stages; deal stage + loss-reason tracking; tasks + shared team calendar; Google Calendar sync; unlimited team seats
- Strengths: Free CRM bundled; multi-pipeline up to 100 stages; unlimited seats
- Gaps: Lightweight (not system of record); no CI; no GTM orchestration
