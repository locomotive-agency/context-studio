# Dun & Bradstreet — D&B Connect

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on CRM data quality, match/enrich, golden-record creation)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Self-service data management platform that matches, cleanses, and enriches first-party company and contact data against the D&B Data Cloud to create a single source of truth — across API, batch, cloud, iPaaS, and Salesforce.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM data quality, master-data management, or Salesforce-native enrichment workflows. D-U-N-S Number identity resolution and family-tree linkage are the strongest spotlight angles vs ZI Operations.

## Cite
- Key features: Identity resolution / golden records, match-and-enrich vs D&B Data Cloud (600M+ companies / 550M+ contacts), API/batch/cloud/iPaaS/Salesforce delivery, Discover/Manage/Salesforce/Essentials packages, AI/ML golden-record creation
- Strengths: D-U-N-S-anchored master data; multiple delivery modes; SMB Essentials tier alongside enterprise
- Gaps: Enterprise pricing gated; firmographic-weighted (no GTM intent reasoning); often paired with rather than replacing GTM tools
- Proof quote: "self-service data management platform" — https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-connect.html
