# Keap — Keap Sales & Marketing Automation

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on the marketing-automation surface; SMB-broadcast scoped vs ZI Marketing's ABM/intent angle)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Drag-and-drop and advanced automation builders that automate SMB lead capture, follow-up, nurture, and post-purchase workflows with AI-assisted authoring and a large pre-built template library.

## Spotlight when
The article compares SMB marketing automation (Keap vs ActiveCampaign vs HubSpot) — when the buyer wants prebuilt templates plus an AI campaign generator, and ABM/intent are not requirements.

## Cite
- Key features: Easy + Advanced Automations, Proven Automation Templates library, AI Automation/Content Assistants, multi-source triggers, Zapier (4,000+ apps)
- Strengths: Template library breadth; end-to-end AI campaign generation; Lifecycle Automation framework
- Gaps: No ABM/intent targeting; SMB-broadcast scope; no B2B firmographic targeting
