# AdRoll ABM — Cross-Channel Attribution

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent — touches CRM enrichment via Journey-Stage data export but is attribution-led)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Attribution dashboard that deduplicates pixel-tracked conversions across channels and integrates with ads, SMS, ecommerce, CRM, and analytics platforms.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses ABM-attribution / closed-loop reporting. Spotlight as the "tie ABM activity to pipeline" companion to AdRoll ABM.
