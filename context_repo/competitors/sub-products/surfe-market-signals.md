# Surfe — Surfe Market Signals

**Competes with:** Intent Data (adjacent — community list marketplace vs. first-party intent topics)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Community-driven marketplace of B2B prospecting lists organized as "playbooks" — curated lists of companies tagged with intent and growth trends, free to browse and integrating back into Surfe's prospecting flow.

## Spotlight when
The article compares "lightweight intent" surfaces or community-list features. Use sparingly — Surfe itself concedes ZoomInfo Intent Data is the stronger ABM-grade intent platform.

## Cite
- Key features: Community lists, Most Viewed/Fresh Insights/Tech Installs/Market Expansion, Intent + growth tagging, Free to browse, Integrates with prospecting flow
- Strengths: Community marketplace model; free browsing; tech-install signals
- Gaps: Not first-party intent topics; weaker than ZI for ABM; thin product page
- Proof quote: "ZoomInfo's intent suite is more geared toward operations teams running programmatic ABM" — https://www.surfe.com/blog/surfe-vs-zoominfo/
