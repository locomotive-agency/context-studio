# Mutiny — Mutiny for ABM

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on the 1:1 ABM landing-page personalization job)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Solution positioning of the Mutiny agent for account-based marketing — 1:1 and 1:many personalized campaigns scalable without design, PMM, or web dependencies.

## Spotlight when
The article positions ABM personalization without engineering/design dependencies as the differentiator — Mutiny's "lean team, big output" pitch and the SingleStore "3 months → 1 week" proof point land best here.

## Cite
- Key features: 1:1 and 1:many scalable personalized campaigns, Rep self-serve from on-brand templates, "Lean team big output" framing, SingleStore proof
- Strengths: Replaces design/PMM/web bottleneck; reps generate per-account assets self-serve; 4.5x speed claim
- Gaps: No firmographic/intent data foundation; no media activation; limited orchestration vs. full ABM platform
