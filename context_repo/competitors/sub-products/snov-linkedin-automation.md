# Snov.io — LinkedIn Automation

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — LinkedIn-touch automation alongside the seller-execution surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Automates LinkedIn touches — profile views, likes, connection requests, and follow-ups — inside Snov.io outreach sequences, sold as a $69/mo per-slot add-on alongside the LinkedIn Prospect Finder extension and in-workspace LinkedIn Search.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo GTM Workspace's seller workflow to LinkedIn-native automation, or argue that SMB / agency teams need email + LinkedIn touches in one sequence.

- Strengths: LinkedIn inside multichannel sequence; per-slot parallel senders; in-workspace LinkedIn search
- Gaps: Paid per-slot add-on; limited touch types; LinkedIn TOS risk
