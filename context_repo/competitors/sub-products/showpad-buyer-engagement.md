# Showpad — Showpad Buyer Engagement

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the buying-committee / digital sales rooms surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Provides digital sales rooms (Shared Spaces), Mutual Action Plans, video messages, 3D/AR product demos, and Field Meeting AI so buying committees can collaborate with sellers and stakeholders can see content engagement.

## Spotlight when
The article covers digital sales rooms / mutual action plans for complex buying committees, especially in field-sales / regulated / physical-product categories. ZoomInfo does not sell a comparable DSR surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Shared Spaces, Mutual Action Plans, Buyer Uploads, video messages, 3D/AR asset hosting, Field Meeting AI, Shared Space templates
- Strengths: 3D/AR demos (not in ZoomInfo); Field Meeting AI captures offline interactions; CRM auto-logs of buyer signals
- Gaps: Content-share-driven not call-recording-driven; no third-party intent on buying committees; no WebSights equivalent
- Proof quote: "Automatically log every buyer interaction... directly to the corresponding Contact, Lead, or Opportunity record" — https://www.showpad.com/buyer-engagement
