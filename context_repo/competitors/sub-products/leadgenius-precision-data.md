# LeadGenius — LeadGenius Precision Data

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the underlying B2B contact-data foundation, with managed sourcing as the wedge)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Custom, on-demand B2B contact-data foundation that activates real-time, contextual intelligence at enterprise scale, sourced via AI + Human-in-the-Loop and delivered through dedicated white-glove pods.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enterprise data foundations for global / niche / hard-to-find segments. LeadGenius is the spotlight when the buyer needs custom-sourced data in EMEA/LATAM/APAC or niche industries (hospitality, construction, franchises) that horizontal databases miss.
