# Consensus — Marketing Solution (Consensus for Marketing)

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (adjacent overlap — competes for marketer pipeline-acceleration budget; complementary on play execution)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
AI-powered interactive tours and on-demand video demos embedded in web, outbound, and event motions to convert visitors into Product-Qualified Leads.

## Spotlight when
The article compares PQL-conversion plays, Product-Qualified Lead signal capture, or embedded demos for demand-gen marketers. Strongest spotlight when the buyer's marketer wants to convert anonymous visitors into PQLs via product experience.

- Strengths: PQL claim 6-8x higher than MQL; G2 demo placement; native triggers across major MAPs
