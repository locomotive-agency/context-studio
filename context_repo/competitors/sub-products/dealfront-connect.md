# Dealfront — Dealfront Connect / Buying Intent Signals

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on the trigger-event + first-party-intent layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Trigger-event monitoring layered on first-party website intent — 33 predefined buying signals (hiring, funding, expansion, partnerships, website updates) with real-time alerts and ICP-aware account clustering, augmented by Leadfeeder AI custom-prompt alerts.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's Intent Data product, especially when the angle is combining first-party website intent with third-party trigger events or natural-language signal monitoring.

## Cite
- Key features: 33 predefined Trigger Events including job offers/financials/partnerships/website updates, First-party website intent + third-party trigger events combined, Real-time alerts on target accounts, ICP-aware account clustering, Custom alert prompts via Leadfeeder AI
- Strengths: Combines first-party + third-party signals; custom NL alert prompts; ICP-aware "active focus / future pipeline / ICP cluster" clustering
- Gaps: No proprietary first-party intent at ZoomInfo's depth; limited to 33 predefined trigger types; no conversation intelligence
