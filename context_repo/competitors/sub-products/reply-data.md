# Reply — Reply Data

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the contact data foundation, with larger headline volume but third-party-sourced)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Real-time B2B contact database with 1B+ global contacts, powered by Generect, used to source and enrich prospects directly inside Reply.

## Spotlight when
The article covers B2B data foundations or "contact database scale" claims. Reply Data's 1B+ contact figure is its sharpest spotlight angle — pair with the third-party-sourcing caveat.

## Cite
- Key features: 1B+ global contacts, 16 advanced filters with hiring intent + tech stack, Email + phone validation, Direct push to sequences or CRM, Website visitor reveal
- Strengths: 1B+ contact pool; local-language search across 150+ countries; bundled into seat pricing
- Gaps: Third-party sourced via Generect; limited intent depth; live data credits metered
