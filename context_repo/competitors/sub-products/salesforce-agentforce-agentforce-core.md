# Salesforce Agentforce — Agentforce (core platform)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI-agent layer inside the seller surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Build, deploy, and manage autonomous AI agents at scale on the Salesforce platform; agents work 24/7 across customers, suppliers, and employees by reasoning over CRM data, Data 360, and connected systems via Atlas Reasoning Engine and Agent Script.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses agentic AI vs ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace AI agent layer — Agentforce is the canonical enterprise platform play and Salesforce explicitly positions Agentforce Sales as the #1 ZoomInfo alternative.

## Cite
- Key features: Atlas Reasoning Engine, Agentforce Builder, Agent Script, Voice, Intelligent Context, Trust Layer, AgentExchange
- Strengths: Hybrid deterministic + LLM reasoning, CRM-native context, three buying models with free entry
- Gaps: Salesforce-CRM-tied, no native B2B contact data, vendor relationship with ZoomInfo (Revenue Agent on AppExchange)
- Proof quote: "Agentforce is the only enterprise agentic AI solution..." — https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/
