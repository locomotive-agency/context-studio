# Dreamdata — Dreamdata Signals

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on AI buying-intent surfacing and CRM activation)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI engine that scans all GTM data to identify hidden high-intent signals correlated with pipeline and revenue, then notifies sales via Slack/Teams and creates HubSpot/Salesforce tasks.

## Spotlight when
The article compares anonymous-visitor identification, AI intent scoring, or pipeline-correlated buying signals. Lead with the 80% anonymous-ID rate as the differentiator.

## Cite
- Key features: AI engine (statistical ML + Gemini), Custom Engagement Score, Slack/Teams notifications, CRM syncs with SDR task creation, Chrome Extension on CRM/web/LinkedIn
- Strengths: 80% anonymous-visitor ID via proprietary IP-to-company; scans full GTM dataset; Cognism saw 5x more qualified opportunities
- Gaps: Depends on third-party contact data; no proprietary buyer-intent network; no Chorus-style conversation signals
- Proof quote: "Identifies up to 80% of anonymous website visitors via proprietary IP-to-company resolution" — https://dreamdata.io/signals
