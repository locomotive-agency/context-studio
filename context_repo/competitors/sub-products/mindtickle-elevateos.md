# Mindtickle — ElevateOS

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — agentic seller-facing AI layered on enablement rather than data prospecting)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Mindtickle's agentic operating system that uses AI built on a decade of rep behavior data to coach, guide, and act on every deal across the revenue-enablement stack — pitched as the "first agentic operating system for revenue enablement."

## Spotlight when
The article compares agentic-GTM execution surfaces. ElevateOS is Mindtickle's flagship strategic frame and the call-out for buyers evaluating agent-driven enablement vs. ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace seller agents.
