# Relevance AI — Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)

**Competes with:** APIs & MCP (adjacent overlap on the agent-builder layer — multi-agent orchestration is a step beyond single-agent APIs)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
No-code builder for creating teams of AI agents that work collaboratively to plan and execute tasks toward shared goals.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses multi-agent system orchestration or "team of agents" patterns vs. single-agent designs. Best paired with the AI Workforce sub-product when discussing agent collaboration depth.

## Cite
- Key features: Specialist agents distilled from processes, Escalation paths, Agent-to-agent feedback, "No training on your data", Content/coding/conversational categories
- Strengths: True multi-agent collaboration model; privacy guarantee; built-in quality-control feedback
- Gaps: Builder-flavored vs. packaged; no first-party data foundation; multi-agent privacy posture still evolving
- Proof quote: "No training on your data. Your data is never used to train models." — https://relevanceai.com/multi-agents
