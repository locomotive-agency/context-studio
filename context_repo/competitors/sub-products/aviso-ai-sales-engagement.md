# Aviso AI — Sales Engagement

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the SDR/AE prospecting and sequencing surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Native multi-channel prospecting and outreach module with AI-personalized sequences, AI Co-Pilots, and a Virtual SDR Agent that prospects autonomously across CRM and external sources.

## Spotlight when
Articles comparing autonomous SDR agents or AI personalization layers. Aviso's Agentic Research Workflow pulling from 10-Ks and earnings calls is a useful spotlight for enterprise-research-driven outbound.

## Cite
- Key features: Virtual SDR Agent, AI Personalizer (10-Ks/earnings/news/CRMs), Multi-channel sequences (email/LinkedIn/call/SMS), DISC + LinkedIn profiler, Lead/ICP/Intent Score, Sequence + people analytics
- Strengths: Autonomous prospecting across intent + CRM + external; agentic research from earnings/10-Ks; triple-score (Lead + ICP + Intent) prioritization
- Gaps: No bundled B2B data; sequencing depth lags Outreach/Salesloft at enterprise scale; one of seven Aviso modules
