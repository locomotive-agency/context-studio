# Emailable — Bulk Email List Verification

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap with ZoomInfo's email-verification surface — the former NeverBounce capability acquired 2019)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Validates uploaded email lists in bulk to remove invalid and risky addresses before sending — Emailable's flagship workflow.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email-verification tools head-to-head with ZoomInfo's verification surface. Lead with the credits-never-expire and refund-on-Unknown angles vs the 12-month NeverBounce expiration.

## Cite
- Key features: ~10K emails in 2-3 min (30K+/min claim), customizable exports, refunds on Unknown/Duplicate, 99% deliverability guarantee
- Strengths: Credits never expire; refunds on Unknown/Duplicate; 30K+/min "8x faster" claim
- Gaps: Verification only — no database; no firmographic/intent data; no GTM workflow
- Proof quote: "The most accurate email verification platform" — https://emailable.com/
