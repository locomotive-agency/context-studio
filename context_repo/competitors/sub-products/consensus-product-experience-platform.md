# Consensus — Product Experience Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — buyer-engagement signal layer rather than seller workspace substitute)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Consensus's full demo automation platform combines AI Agents, interactive product tours, simulations, on-demand video demos, and buyer-signal analytics into one buyer-led product experience surface. Flagship platform offering.

## Spotlight when
The article compares demo automation platforms, Product Experience Platforms, or buyer-enablement tooling for B2B software companies. Strongest spotlight when the buyer is solving for "scale demos and surface buying committee" — not a head-to-head with ZoomInfo.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Agents (qualify/educate/demo), Interactive Product Tours with auto-update, Automated Product Simulations, On-Demand Video Demos personalized by buyer/industry/language, Demolytics, Demoboards
- Strengths: Three demo modalities under one roof; Demolytics surfaces buying committee; 15 of top 30 software companies
- Gaps: No B2B contact/firmographic/intent data; Simulations gated to Enterprise; demo-only buyer signals
- Proof quote: "Demos That Sell — Stop educating. Start closing." — https://goconsensus.com/
