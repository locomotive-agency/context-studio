# Regie.ai — Prospecting Agents (Auto-Pilot)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap with the AI-agent layer that autonomously sources/researches/engages prospects)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Autonomous AI agents that source, enrich, research, prioritize, and warm up prospects, then hand off ready-to-talk leads to reps — sold as the AI-SDR / force-multiplier tier above RegieOne.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI-SDR / autonomous prospecting agents — Auto-Pilot's "ready-to-talk lead handoff" framing is the strongest angle and the cleanest counter-pitch to GTM Workspace's AI-agent layer.

## Cite
- Key features: Autonomous prospect discovery, 120k AI + enrichment credits, Research Agents + Reasons to Engage, Parallel dialer up to 9 lines, 10 mailboxes + warming
- Strengths: 4–6x SDR-equivalent output claim; bundled credits + dialer + mailboxes at $499/user; vendor outcomes 69% / 21%
- Gaps: 5-seat minimum gates SMB; weaker intent depth vs. GTM Context Graph; no first-party CI for autonomous calls
- Proof quote: "Pairs autonomous AI agents with reps in a left-brain / right-brain division of labor" — https://www.regie.ai/use-cases/named-account-signals
