# People.ai — AI-Native Forecasting

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller / leader analytics surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Bottoms-up AI forecast that scores engagement health, deal velocity, stakeholder involvement, and historical patterns at the individual deal level — replacing top-down rep-call rollups with deal-grounded predictions.

## Spotlight when
The article covers AI forecasting accuracy or activity-grounded pipeline analytics — the 20-30% accuracy improvement claim and the nine-year dataset moat are the strongest hooks.

## Cite
- Key features: Bottoms-up deal-level forecast, engagement-drop / stakeholder-silence flags, activity-to-close-date pattern detection, grounded in 160M+ contact dataset
- Strengths: 20-30% reported accuracy lift; grounded in actual interactions; trillions in pipeline analyzed historically
- Gaps: Enterprise-only; requires full platform deploy; no standalone forecasting purchase
