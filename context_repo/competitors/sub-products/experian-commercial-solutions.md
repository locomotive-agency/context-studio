# Experian — Experian Commercial Solutions

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — overlaps on company-level firmographic data, but built around credit/risk attributes, not GTM contact reach)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Commercial data + analytics platform for lenders, suppliers, and risk teams across the small-business credit lifecycle — exposed via BusinessIQ 2.0, Ascend Commercial Suite, and Sentinel fraud tooling.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data scale specifically across regulated/commercial datasets. Spotlight when the question is global business-family coverage (190M+ records) not contact-level reach.
