# Klue — Klue Ask Klue

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — both surface AI answers to sellers in deal flow, but Ask Klue is competitor-only)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
In-deal AI assistant that returns instant sourced answers to competitive questions inside Slack, Salesforce, and the Klue app — pulling from the Knowledge Hub and Auto Insights and tied into Deal Tips for proactive in-deal coaching.

## Spotlight when
The article compares in-deal seller AI assistants or Slack/Salesforce-embedded copilots focused on competitive intel.

## Cite
- Key features: Sourced answers to competitive questions, Slack/SF/Klue embeds, Knowledge Hub + Auto Insights powered, Deal Tips integration
- Strengths: Sourced citations reduce hallucination; embedded where sellers work; coupled with Deal Tips
- Gaps: Competitor-only scope; not a general seller AI; no standalone freemium
- Proof quote: "Instant, sourced answers to competitive questions inside Slack, Salesforce, and Klue." — https://klue.com/compete-agent
