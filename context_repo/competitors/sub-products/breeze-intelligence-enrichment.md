# Breeze Intelligence — Breeze Intelligence — Data Enrichment

**Competes with:** CRM Enrichment (direct overlap on the in-CRM enrichment workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Real-time enrichment of contact and company records inside HubSpot's Smart CRM with firmographic, technographic, and contact context — 100+ data points per record, drawn from 100M+ company domains and 380M email addresses indexed.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's CRM Enrichment / Operations layer for buyers running on HubSpot, especially when the angle is native enrichment vs third-party data licensing.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time CRM enrichment within seconds, 100M+ company domains and 380M email addresses indexed, 100+ B2B data points per company/contact, Configurable overwrite/keep/blank field handling, Continuous refresh with dynamic quality scoring and human QA, Global coverage in any language
- Strengths: Native to HubSpot Smart CRM (no separate seat); inherits Clearbit's data foundation; consumption pricing via HubSpot Credits
- Gaps: Gated behind paid HubSpot Hub; no phone numbers at scale; no MCP server; no stand-alone data licensing for non-HubSpot
