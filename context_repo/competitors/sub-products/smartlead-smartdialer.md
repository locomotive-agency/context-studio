# Smartlead — SmartDialer

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent — lightweight conversation-intelligence layer alongside outbound calling)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Parallel-dialing voice / SMS layer alongside cold email, with AI objection tagging, transcripts, summaries, and dial-tone / voicemail skip — all in one dashboard with email outbound.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare Chorus to bundled voice / CI inside outbound platforms, or position email-and-dialer-in-one as an SMB / agency alternative to a separate Chorus subscription.

## Cite
- Key features: Parallel dialing with local presence; AI objection tagging; transcripts + summaries; auto-skip voicemails and dial tones; email + calls in one dashboard
- Strengths: Voice + email together; parallel dialer self-serve; AI objection tagging
- Gaps: No scorecards / deal risk / market intel; no manager coaching surface; no rich CRM-write-back
- Proof quote: "Email + calls in a single dashboard." — https://www.smartlead.ai/
