# Talend Data Quality — Qlik Talend Data Quality and Governance

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent — enterprise governance + AI-readiness vs. GTM data ops)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Unified data quality and governance layer in Qlik Talend Cloud for discovering, remediating, and sharing trusted data, with self-service automation, a metadata-powered catalog, and Trust Score for AI metrics.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI-readiness or data governance — Qlik Talend's Trust Score for AI is the strongest spotlight angle. ZoomInfo Operations is the GTM-native counterpart at the CRM layer.

- Key features: Automated profiling, Qlik Trust Score, Qlik Trust Score for AI, Metadata catalog, Push-down/pull-up execution, Trust Assessor
- Strengths: AI-readiness scoring; metadata catalog; dual execution modes
- Gaps: No B2B persona orchestration; no intent/contact data; IT-buyer focused
