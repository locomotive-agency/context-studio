# Reply — Reply for Agencies

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — agency multi-workspace structure occupies ICP space ZoomInfo doesn't actively serve)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Multi-workspace agency edition with white-label, role-based access, and a consolidated inbox for managing client outbound at scale.

## Spotlight when
The article covers lead-gen agency stacks or white-label / multi-tenant deployment — Reply's agency-purpose-built tier is the sharpest spotlight angle and a clear ZoomInfo "no overlap" zone.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-workspace umbrella, Unlimited clients + users + mailboxes, Consolidated email + LinkedIn inbox, RBAC + agency dashboard, White-label + Agency AI SDR tier
- Strengths: Purpose-built agency structure; white-label; per-client AI SDR
- Gaps: Agency-only ICP; no first-party data foundation; no CI / forecasting in agency tier
