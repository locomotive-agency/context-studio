# Foundry — Foundry Editorial Brand Network

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (no overlap — owned-media layer with no ZoomInfo equivalent; spotlight as differentiator only)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Owned-and-operated tech publishing network (CIO, CSO, Computerworld, InfoWorld, Network World, Macworld, PCWorld, TechHive, Tech Advisor, ChannelWorld + specialty brands) that delivers the audience and content adjacency layer underneath Foundry Intent, Foundry Ads, and lead-gen programs — also runs CIO 100, CSO50, and 800+ industry events annually.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights why Foundry's positioning is structurally different from a data vendor. Editorial Network is the spotlight when the angle is "owned-media moat" — useful as a differentiator but not as a competing GTM product.

## Cite
- Key features: 28M+ monthly visitors, 800+ events/year, CIO reaches 4M+ CIOs across 72 countries, Computerworld reaches 12M enterprise IT managers across 47 countries, CIO 100 + CSO50 award programs, "96% of visitors take action toward purchasing"
- Strengths: 10+ trusted IT-buyer brands; 800+ industry events incl. CIO 100/CSO50; audience moat no data vendor can replicate
- Gaps: Not a GTM product on its own; ZoomInfo has no owned-media equivalent (not head-to-head); content/audience layer, not GTM execution
