# Artisan — Sales Automation Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the bundled outbound execution surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
All-in-one outbound automation platform that bundles data, enrichment, sequencing, deliverability, A/B testing, dialer, and CRM sync into a single autonomous system marketed as a BDR-stack replacement.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one outbound platforms vs. modular GTM stacks. Sales Automation is the strongest "stack consolidator" angle — Artisan explicitly pitches it as a ZoomInfo + Clay + sequencer + signal provider replacement.
