# Reply — Jason AI SDR Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI-agent / autonomous-SDR layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous AI SDR that prospects, writes, sends, and replies across channels on autopilot or in copilot mode using real-time B2B data.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses autonomous AI SDR products or autopilot vs. copilot agent design choices. Jason's multilingual 150+ country reach and Simfoni's 92% in-ICP claim are the strongest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Autopilot or copilot modes, 1B+ contact prospect search with ICP + intent, AI multichannel sequence generation, AI reply + meeting booking, Multilingual 150+ country SDR
- Strengths: True autopilot mode; bundled 1B+ contact pool; multilingual scope
- Gaps: No GTM Context Graph reasoning; no proprietary intent panel; no standalone CI for conversation coaching
