# Jasper — Jasper Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — agent workspace for marketing execution; not a substitute since Jasper has no B2B data layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Agent workspace for marketing teams that orchestrates 100+ specialized AI agents through structured Content Pipelines — Canvas for human + agent collaboration, Jasper IQ for brand context, Studio for custom agent building, Grid for bulk generation.

## Spotlight when
The article covers AI-agent platforms for marketing or contrasts marketing-execution agents (Jasper) vs. GTM intelligence agents (ZoomInfo GTM Workspace). Jasper is the flagship.

## Cite
- Key features: 100+ marketing agents, Content Pipelines, Canvas, Jasper IQ, Studio (Business), Grid, LLM-agnostic routing, AI Image Suite
- Strengths: Purpose-built marketing agents; Jasper IQ brand enforcement; pipelines-as-workflows
- Gaps: No B2B data; no sales workspace; marketing-only ICP
- Proof quote: "The execution platform for intelligent marketing" — https://www.jasper.ai/
