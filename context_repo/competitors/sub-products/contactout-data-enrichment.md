# ContactOut — ContactOut Data Enrichment & ContactSheets

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on CRM/ATS hygiene & enrichment)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Enriches existing CRM/ATS records with ContactOut's verified contact and company data, including bulk LinkedIn-URL enrichment via spreadsheet upload (ContactSheets) — a workflow ContactOut explicitly markets as missing from ZoomInfo.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enrichment / CRM-hygiene workflows. ContactOut's strongest spotlight angle is the LinkedIn-URL spreadsheet enrichment workflow ZoomInfo does not match.

## Cite
- Key features: Bulk LinkedIn-URL enrichment (ContactSheets), CRM/ATS record refresh, Triple-verified emails/phones, Daily refresh
- Strengths: Spreadsheet-first LinkedIn enrichment workflow ZI doesn't offer; ATS connectors (Lever/Greenhouse/Bullhorn); 99% verified
- Gaps: No routing/dedupe like RingLead; no firmographic/technographic depth; no DaaS delivery
- Proof quote: "ContactOut allows you to upload your existing list of LinkedIn URLs to add personal emails, work emails, phone numbers and more — a feature ZoomInfo doesn't have" — https://contactout.com/alternative/zoominfo-vs-contactout
