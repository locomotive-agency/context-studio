# Acxiom — Customer Data Management

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent — same problem space delivered as managed service for consumer databases)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Services-led data foundation work — design, implementation, hygiene, identity matching, and ongoing optimization of customer data platforms and marketing databases.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses CRM/database hygiene as a managed-service vs. self-serve choice. Acxiom's spotlight is the privacy-compliance posture and Snowflake/Adobe/TD partner depth — but flag it's B2C-scoped.
