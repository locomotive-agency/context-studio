# S&P Capital IQ — Capital IQ Excel Plug-in

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (no direct overlap — separate domain workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Microsoft Excel add-in that pulls financial, market, and company data into models with click-through auditability back to source filings — the workhorse productivity tool for IB analysts, valuation teams, and credit analysts.

## Spotlight when
The article contrasts data-into-Excel workflows; Capital IQ's Excel plug-in is the gold standard in finance but irrelevant for GTM workflows ZoomInfo Operations targets.

## Cite
- Key features: 140+ prebuilt templates, Custom Formula Builder, audit-to-source, watch list / screen integration
- Strengths: Audit traceability, finance-tuned templates
- Gaps: Windows-only, tangential to GTM enrichment
