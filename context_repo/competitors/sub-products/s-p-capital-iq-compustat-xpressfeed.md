# S&P Capital IQ — Compustat & Xpressfeed

**Competes with:** Data as a Service (adjacent overlap on bulk data feed model only)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Bulk financial and statistical market data feeds — Compustat (dating to 1962) delivered via Xpressfeed for users to plug into their own quant tools and proprietary models.

## Spotlight when
The article surveys data-feed delivery models for B2B/finance use cases. Compustat is a useful contrast for ZoomInfo's Data as a Service positioning — same delivery model, totally different payload.

- Key features: Compustat fundamentals back to 1962, Xpressfeed delivery, Money Market Directories
- Strengths: Historical depth, quant-system delivery, reference data for funding sources
- Gaps: Tangential payload, no contacts/intent, finance-only ICP
