# Voila Norbert — Verify (Email Verification)

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on email verification — narrower than ZoomInfo's first-party verified emails)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Pay-as-you-go email-list cleaning service that runs uploaded lists through an 8-step verification pipeline (dedup, domain, syntax, catch-all, gibberish, MX, freemail, SMTP) before send.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email verification tools or pay-as-you-go list-hygiene services. Lean on the 8-step pipeline and $0.003/email pricing as the spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Email deduplication, Domain verification, Syntax validation, Catch-all domain check, Gibberish / fake-email check, MX record test, Freemail flagging, SMTP authentication test
- Strengths: 8-step verification pipeline; $0.003/email tier; $2 minimum
- Gaps: Email-only; no CRM-native bulk verify; manual upload required
