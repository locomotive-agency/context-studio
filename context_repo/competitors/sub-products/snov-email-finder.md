# Snov.io — Email Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the verified-email primitive)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Finds and verifies B2B email addresses by name, domain, company name, or LinkedIn profile, with web app, Chrome extension, and bulk search modes against a 500M+ email / 50M+ company database.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo Data scale to lower-cost SMB alternatives, or argue that startups / agencies need a 500M-email database without an enterprise contract.

## Cite
- Key features: 500M+ database with AI search; multi-mode search; Chrome extension at 400K+ users; bulk CSV finder; 98% accuracy claim; 50 free searches/month
- Strengths: 500M-contact scale at SMB pricing; multi-mode search; popular extension
- Gaps: Email-anchored; no technographic / intent / org-chart depth; no GTM Context Graph
- Proof quote: "Database of 500M+ B2B email addresses with smart AI search." — https://snov.io/email-finder
