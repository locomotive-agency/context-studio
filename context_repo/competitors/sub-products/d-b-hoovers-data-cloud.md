# D&B Hoovers — D&B Data Cloud (D-U-N-S Number)

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the underlying commercial-data foundation that powers prospecting + CRM enrichment + ABM)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
The underlying D&B commercial Data Cloud anchored on the D-U-N-S Number — a unique business identifier underpinning corporate hierarchies, CRM data quality, governance, and compliance verification across the entire D&B portfolio (Hoovers, Connect, Rev.Up).

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's Data pillar against incumbent commercial-data foundations, especially when the angle is D-U-N-S-Number-based hierarchies, governance, or government-contracting compliance.

## Cite
- Key features: Underlying commercial Data Cloud powering Hoovers + Connect + Rev.Up, D-U-N-S Number as unique business identifier, 330M+ company records and corporate hierarchies, "90% of the Fortune 1000" as Data Cloud users, D&B Direct API, Government-contracting and compliance verification
- Strengths: D-U-N-S Number as canonical business identifier across the GTM stack; compliance heritage; single underlying graph across Hoovers/Connect/Rev.Up
- Gaps: Gartner reviewers flag data freshness/accuracy issues; no MCP server; pricing not publicly listed; weaker buyer-level intent vs ZoomInfo's Intent Data
