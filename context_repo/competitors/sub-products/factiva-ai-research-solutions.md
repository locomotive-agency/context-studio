# Factiva — Factiva AI Research Solutions

**Competes with:** APIs & MCP (adjacent overlap — content for LLM/RAG, not GTM data feed for agents)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Licensed news content packaged for use in customer GenAI/RAG workflows — improves output accuracy, reduces hallucinations, and provides citable sources by combining proprietary data with rights-cleared editorial.

## Spotlight when
The article covers AI-agent ecosystems and content licensing for LLMs. Factiva AI Research Solutions is the clearest example of "premium content licensed for AI" as an alternative to GTM-data feeds for agent builders.
