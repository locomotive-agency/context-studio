# Instantly — Instantly B2B Lead Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap — 450M+ B2B contact database with waterfall enrichment)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-powered B2B contact database with 13 advanced filters, lookalike search, waterfall work-email enrichment from 5+ providers, and full profile enrichment (description, headcount, job postings, news, technology, funding).

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B contact-data alternatives to ZoomInfo at a lower price point. Instantly publicly positions itself as a cheaper ZoomInfo alternative on the `/blog/zoominfo-alternatives` page.
