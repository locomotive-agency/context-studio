# Marketo Engage — Marketo Measure

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on attribution / pipeline reporting; Marketo Measure is multi-touch-attribution-led, ZoomInfo is intent-data-led)
**Spotlight rank:** 6

## What it does
Multi-touch attribution add-on that measures the impact of campaigns, content, and channels on pipeline and revenue — bundled into Marketo Engage Ultimate or sold as a tiered add-on (Standard / Performance / Performance Plus).

## Spotlight when
The article addresses ABM ROI / pipeline-attribution conversations from a marketing-ops lens. Strongest spotlight: Marketo Measure inside the Ultimate package eliminates the need for a separate Bizible / 6sense attribution stack.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-touch attribution across campaigns/content/channels, channel + pipeline-stage reporting, add-on/Ultimate-included, Standard/Performance/Performance Plus tiers
- Strengths: Native Marketo wiring; Performance Plus 15x throughput; bundled in Ultimate
- Gaps: Bundled / add-on (no standalone visibility); deepest on Marketo channels; different reporting shape than intent-data-led attribution
- Proof quote: "Multi-touch attribution add-on that measures the impact of campaigns, content, and channels on pipeline and revenue" — https://business.adobe.com/products/marketo/marketo-measure.html
