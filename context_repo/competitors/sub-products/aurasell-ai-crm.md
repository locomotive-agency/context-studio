# Aurasell — Aurasell AI-Native GTM Platform (full CRM)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-front-end + AI agent surface, with broader CRM-of-record scope)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Full-stack AI-native CRM for startups and SMB — replaces 12-15+ GTM tools (CRM, sales engagement, conversation intelligence, forecasting, CPQ, data enrichment) with one platform built on a unified data graph plus a built-in 84M+ account / 950M+ contact database.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights GTM Workspace's seller surface, especially when the angle is AI-native architecture or tool-stack consolidation versus best-of-breed sprawl.
