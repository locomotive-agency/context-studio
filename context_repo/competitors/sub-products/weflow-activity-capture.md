# Weflow — Activity & Contact Capture

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on CRM data hygiene — Salesforce-scoped only)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Server-side sync that auto-syncs emails, meetings, and contacts from Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 directly into native Salesforce objects — 24-month historical backfill, $19/user/mo, 20–45 minute setup.

## Spotlight when
The article compares Salesforce activity-capture / data-hygiene tools or covers RevOps-led CRM-cleanup workflows. Lean on Salesforce-native writes (data stays) and the 20–45 minute install.

## Cite
- Key features: Server-side sync to native Salesforce objects, Auto-creation of Contact records and OCR assignment, 24-month historical backfill, Email open tracking, Gmail/Outlook extensions, $19/user/month
- Strengths: Native Salesforce object writes; 24-month backfill; 20–45 minute setup
- Gaps: Salesforce-only; no third-party data sourcing; email/meeting/contact scope only
- Proof quote: "BenchSci 99% activities captured" — https://www.getweflow.com/faq
