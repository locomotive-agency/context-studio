# Fathom — Fathom for Sales

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on sales-call coaching with scorecards + CRM sync)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Sales-focused workflow that captures deal context, updates the CRM, and coaches reps with AI Scorecards graded against the team's chosen methodology — surfaced via Deal View and AI search alerts.

## Spotlight when
The article frames "lightweight CI with sales coaching." Fathom for Sales is the spotlight when the angle is "scorecards + CRM sync without enterprise platform overhead."

## Cite
- Key features: Deal View, Customized call summaries by methodology, Automatic CRM field sync, AI Scorecards graded against methodology, Coaching metrics, AI search alerts via email/Slack, Ask Fathom with citations, Native HubSpot/Salesforce/Slack/Asana/Close syncs
- Strengths: Methodology-graded AI Scorecards; CRM field sync from meetings; Slack/email AI search alerts
- Gaps: Meeting-call-scoped only; coaching gated to Business tier; weaker multi-call analytics vs Chorus
