# Spekit — Unified Deal Context

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent — coaching grounded in conversation intelligence, but Spekit consumes Gong rather than recording itself)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Connects Salesforce opportunity data and Gong call intelligence into a single deal view that follows the rep wherever they work, fueling AI Sidekick's coaching with grounded deal-specific context.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare Chorus's coaching layer to enablement-led deal-context surfaces, or argue that GTM Context Graph reasoning needs more than Salesforce + Gong fusion.

## Cite
- Key features: Salesforce + Gong fused; auto-recognises active opportunity; deal context across CRM / email / call tools; domain-detection in email; fuels Sidekick coaching
- Strengths: Salesforce + Gong fusion; auto-opportunity detection; domain-detection in email
- Gaps: Narrow reasoning (Salesforce + Gong only); no native call recording; not a data provider
