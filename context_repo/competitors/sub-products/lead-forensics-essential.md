# Lead Forensics — Lead Forensics Essential

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap on the SMB visitor identification surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Entry-tier B2B website visitor identification — reveals the businesses visiting your website, surfaces business contact details for key prospects, exposes the keywords driving traffic, and pushes real-time notifications via the Lead Manager portal. CRM integrations limited to Zapier.

## Spotlight when
The article frames "smaller-team WebSights alternatives" or visitor-ID for SMBs that don't want a direct CRM integration tier.
