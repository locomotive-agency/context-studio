# Insightly — Insightly Service

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no overlap — help-desk/ticketing is outside ZoomInfo's product surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Customer service / help desk software for ticket management built on the same platform as Insightly CRM and Marketing — ticket editor, SLA tracking, branded public portals, knowledge articles, and community forums.

## Spotlight when
Only when an article enumerates Insightly's full all-in-one bundle. Service does not compete with ZoomInfo and should be cited as a non-competing module.

## Cite
- Key features: Ticket editor with SLAs, public portals, knowledge articles, community forums, auto-ticket generation from email/forms, configurable macros
- Strengths: Shared data layer with CRM/Marketing; $29/user entry; Customer Insights dashboard
- Gaps: Help-desk only; no CSM/renewal AI signals; no intent data
- Proof quote: "Comprehensive ticket editor with SLA tracking" — https://www.insightly.com/service/
