# Klenty — Klenty AI Call Coaching Suite (Call IQ)

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on conversation-intelligence + coaching scope)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Conversation intelligence layer that ingests sales calls and produces live transcripts, AI Battlecards surfaced in real time, AI Objection Detection, AI Call Scorecards, Coaching Summary, and Talk Track Tables to enforce cold-calling methodology — bundled into the Klenty Plus / Sales Dialer Advanced tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI call coaching against Chorus and the buyer wants a CI layer bundled with their cadence + dialer rather than a standalone CI vendor.

## Cite
- Key features: Live Transcription + AI Topics, AI Battlecards in-call, AI Objection Detection + Scorecard, Coaching Summary + Talk Track Tables, Free 250-minute trial
- Strengths: Bundled with SEP stack; live in-call AI Battlecards; free 250-minute Call IQ trial
- Gaps: Narrower than Chorus on enterprise scorecards/trackers; Transcription gated behind $40/user/mo add-on on lower tiers; no Context Graph fusion
- Proof quote: "Free Call IQ trial — analyze 250 minutes of meetings." — https://www.klenty.com/sales-engagement-platform/pricing/
