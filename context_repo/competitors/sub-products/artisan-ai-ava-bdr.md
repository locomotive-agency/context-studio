# Artisan AI — Ava — the Autonomous AI BDR

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace — autonomous prospect-to-meeting BDR workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous AI sales agent that sources leads from a 250M+ verified contact base, writes hyper-personalized email sequences, runs autonomous A/B testing, handles replies and objections, and books meetings on behalf of the rep — pitched as "1/5th the cost of a human BDR."

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo's AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace against fully-autonomous AI BDR products, especially when discussing self-serve SMB pricing or "AI employee" framing.

## Cite
- Key features: Searches 250M+ verified B2B contacts across 200+ countries, Ghostwriting hyper-personalized email sequences with no templates, Continuous autonomous A/B (A/Z) testing of subjects/CTAs/tone, Handles replies/qualifies leads/books meetings, Watchtower website visitor de-anonymization, Three campaign motions (cold outbound, CRM re-engagement, cross-sell/upsell)
- Strengths: Fully autonomous by default; public 4-tier price ladder ($0/$250/$600/Custom) with Free tier; Watchtower website visitor de-anonymization included
- Gaps: Ava cannot legally make calls; no conversation intelligence; no public API or MCP; no ABM/marketing platform; data depth varies (250M vs 300M)
