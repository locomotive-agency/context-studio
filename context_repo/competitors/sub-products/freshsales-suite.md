# Freshsales — Freshsales Suite

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (adjacent overlap on the marketer/RevOps surface — journeys, segmentation, landing pages, multichannel campaigns)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Combined CRM + marketing automation product that unifies sales and marketing teams on a single 360° customer view, adding journeys, segmentation, landing pages, web forms, CRO tooling, and multichannel campaigns to the core Freshsales CRM.

## Spotlight when
The article compares unified CRM-plus-marketing-automation suites or "GTM Studio alternatives" for SMB/mid-market — Freshsales Suite's strongest spotlight angle is the single-product CRM + MAP bundle priced for SMB.

## Cite
- Key features: CRM + marketing automation, Marketing journey builder, Segments/lists, Email builder, Landing pages and web forms, CRO (heatmaps, session replay, A/B testing), Multichannel engagement (Phone/Email/WhatsApp/Chat/SMS)
- Strengths: CRM + MAP unified on one 360° view; native CRO bundled in; multichannel engagement without bolt-ons
- Gaps: No ABM advertising / programmatic ad orchestration; no first-party intent; no B2B contact data foundation
- Proof quote: "Combined CRM + marketing automation product that unifies sales and marketing teams on a single 360° customer view" — https://www.freshworks.com/crm/suite/
