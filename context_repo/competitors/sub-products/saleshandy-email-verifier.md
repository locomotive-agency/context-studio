# Saleshandy — Saleshandy Email Verifier

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on the data-quality / verification slice)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Free email-list verifier that runs syntax, domain, MX, catch-all, and SMTP checks at an advertised 98%+ accuracy; sold standalone and as a paid add-on inside Outreach plans.

## Spotlight when
The article covers email-list hygiene as a deliverability prerequisite for cold sending, and the buyer needs a free or low-cost verifier rather than the broader ZoomInfo Operations CRM-data-quality suite.

## Cite
- Key features: Syntax/domain/MX/catch-all/SMTP checks; bulk CSV + single-email; Valid/Risky/Invalid labels; 98%+ accuracy; $15 per 5K add-on
- Strengths: Free standalone surface; bundled with Outreach + Lead Finder credit economics
- Gaps: No CRM dedup/standardization workflows; no firmographic enrichment in the verifier; add-on pricing stacks
- Proof quote: "98%+ accuracy" — https://www.saleshandy.com/email-verifier
