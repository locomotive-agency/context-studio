# Fathom — Fathom for Marketing

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap — VoC mining from meetings, not ABM/marketing orchestration)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Marketing-team workflow for capturing campaign decisions, mining customer feedback, and turning calls into marketing assets — extracts quotes, story moments, and shareable snippets for content reuse.

## Spotlight when
The article distinguishes meeting-derived voice-of-customer from ABM/marketing-orchestration platforms. Fathom for Marketing is the spotlight when the angle is "VoC mining" without any campaign-activation pretense.
