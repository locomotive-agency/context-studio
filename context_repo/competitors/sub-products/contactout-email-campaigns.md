# ContactOut — ContactOut Email Campaigns

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent overlap on outbound email sequencing)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Multi-stage outbound email tool to nurture prospects and candidates from inside ContactOut, with AI personalization layered on top of ContactOut's contact data.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on bundled "data + outreach" platforms vs. best-of-breed sequencing. Email Campaigns' value is convenience for SMB sellers, not enterprise-grade sequencing depth.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-stage campaigns, One-click prospect import, AI Email Writer / AI Personalizer, Open/click/reply tracking
- Strengths: Bundled with ContactOut data; AI tied to LinkedIn profiles; lower price point
- Gaps: Email-only (no dialer/LinkedIn in sequences); thinner deliverability infra; no CI
- Proof quote: "Push back the bounce backs" — https://contactout.com/sales-users
