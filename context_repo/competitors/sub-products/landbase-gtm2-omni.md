# Landbase — Landbase GTM-2 Omni

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-facing prioritized-accounts + AI-drafted outreach + execution surface — competes with the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Multi-agent system (Strategy, Research, SDR, RevOps, IT Manager agents) that orchestrates the full GTM workflow autonomously, running on a 300M-contact / 24M-company database with 1,500+ enrichment fields and natural-language Agentic Search.

## Spotlight when
The article compares agentic-AI GTM platforms vs static-database incumbents — Landbase is the right spotlight when the buyer is a RevOps leader looking to consolidate 13+ outbound tools (data + sequencing + CRM + deliverability) into one autonomous system.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-agent architecture (Strategy/Research/SDR/RevOps/IT Manager agents), Trained on 50M+ GTM campaigns and 175M+ sales conversations, Natural-language Agentic Search across 300M+ contacts and 1,500+ fields, Real-time Signals (funding/hiring/tech/news/social), AI Qualification with Tier-1/2/3 scoring, Native HubSpot and Salesforce CRM sync
- Strengths: In-platform email infrastructure (domain buying, DMARC, warmup); multi-agent system trained on 175M+ conversations; 4-7x conversion lift claim
- Gaps: Pricing fully gated; thin integration partner list beyond HubSpot/Salesforce; no CI product
- Proof quote: "Unlike ZoomInfo's static database approach, Landbase creates genuine autonomous systems that continuously learn and optimize... 4-7x higher conversion rates and 80% cost reduction compared to traditional database tools." — https://www.landbase.com/blog/zoominfo-alternatives
