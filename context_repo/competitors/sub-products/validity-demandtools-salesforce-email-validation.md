# Validity DemandTools — Salesforce Email Validation

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on verified business emails — DemandTools validates existing records but does not source new contacts)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Verify Salesforce email addresses in bulk on any object with an email field, powered by sister-product BriteVerify. Adds verification date/status/secondary-status fields to records.

## Spotlight when
The article covers email verification or list hygiene specifically inside Salesforce. Lean on in-CRM verification (no exporting) and BriteVerify real-time verify on Lead/Contact create as the spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Bulk verify on any Salesforce object with email field, Auto-log verification date/status/secondary status, Identify and purge bad emails, Graphical readout of verification statuses, Scheduled recurring verifications, BriteVerify Salesforce App for real-time verify on Lead/Contact create
- Strengths: In-CRM bulk verification with no exporting; real-time BriteVerify on record create/edit; scheduled recurring verifications
- Gaps: Validates existing CRM emails only — no contact sourcing; Salesforce-only; no phone or mobile verification
