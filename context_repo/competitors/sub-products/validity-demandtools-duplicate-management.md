# Validity DemandTools — Duplicate Management

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on dedupe + duplicate prevention inside CRM)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Find, merge, and prevent duplicate records across Leads, Contacts, Accounts, and custom objects in Salesforce — including the DupeBlocker native app for real-time duplicate prevention on record create/update.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses specifically on Salesforce duplicate management or compares CRM dedupe tools head-to-head. The Account+Contact merge claim and Apex-extensible DupeBlocker are the strongest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Account+Contact merge in one process, 20+ exact/fuzzy/custom matching algorithms, DupeBlocker real-time prevention, Apex-customizable DupeBlocker actions, Pre-import dedupe across Leads/Contacts/Accounts, Lead-to-Contact/Account bulk convert
- Strengths: Account+Contact merge in one process; real-time DupeBlocker with Apex extensibility; scheduled self-cleaning dedupe
- Gaps: No cross-system dedupe outside Salesforce; doesn't add new contacts; Dynamics 365 edition lacks DupeBlocker
