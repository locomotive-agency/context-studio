# RocketReach — RocketReach Autopilot

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent overlap on the seller-execution loop — automated list refresh + sequencing as a workflow layer rather than a workspace UI)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Automated workflows that build and refresh prospect lists on a daily/weekly/monthly cadence — 5/10/25 active autopilots per tier — to keep pipeline fresh without manual searches, paired with Messages and Sequences for outreach.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights tools that automate list-build + outreach refresh for individual sellers or lean SDR teams. Autopilot is the spotlight when the buyer wants daily/weekly list refresh bundled with outreach at SMB pricing.
