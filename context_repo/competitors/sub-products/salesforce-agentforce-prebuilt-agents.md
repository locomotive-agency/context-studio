# Salesforce Agentforce — Pre-built / out-of-the-box agents

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on packaged AI seller agents)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Salesforce ships role-specific Agentforce agents — SDR Agent, Sales Coach, Service Agent, Merchandiser, Buyer Agent, Campaign Agent / Journey Agent, Prospecting Agent — as deployable templates across Sales, Service, Marketing, Commerce, and IT.

## Spotlight when
The article evaluates packaged AI agents (Salesforce Prospecting Agent, ZoomInfo's AI SDR) and the head-to-head positioning Salesforce publishes on `/compare/zoominfo-competitors/`.

## Cite
- Key features: SDR Agent, Sales Coach, Service Agent, Merchandiser, Buyer Agent, Campaign/Journey Agent, Prospecting Agent
- Strengths: Role-specific catalog, explicit ZoomInfo positioning, Sales Coach role-play
- Gaps: Reliance on data partners (incl. ZoomInfo), service/commerce overlap is out-of-scope for GTM
- Proof quote: "Prospecting Agent autonomously enriches leads and delivers qualified prospects straight to your team 24/7." — https://www.salesforce.com/agentforce/
