# Keap — Keap Lead Capture & Landing Pages

**Competes with:** FormComplete (adjacent overlap on inbound form capture; no enrichment underneath the form)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Forms, landing pages, and multi-page sales funnels for SMB lead capture — each submission triggers Keap automations and writes back lead source attribution to the contact record.

## Spotlight when
The article compares SMB lead-capture stacks — when the buyer wants forms + landing pages + automation in one platform, and form enrichment / progressive profiling are not requirements.

## Cite
- Key features: Landing page builder, automation-triggering forms, multi-page funnels, internal forms, lead source tracking
- Strengths: Bundled forms + pages + automation; multi-page funnels; native lead source attribution
- Gaps: No real-time enrichment / progressive profiling; no B2B data overlay; SMB scope
