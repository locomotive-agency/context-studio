# HubSpot Sales Hub — HubSpot Sales Hub

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller workflow surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered sales software for building pipeline and closing deals — sequences, forecasting, AI Guided Selling, Smart Deal Progression, and Conversation Intelligence layered on HubSpot Smart CRM. Sold across Free / Starter / Professional / Enterprise tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-anchored, all-in-one sales platforms with ZoomInfo's data + GTM Workspace stack. Sales Hub's strongest spotlight angle is the unified Customer Platform pitch and its free-tier-friendly pricing model, contrasted against vendor-curated B2B data depth.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Guided Selling, Sales Automation, Sequences (5,000/account), Forecasting, Smart Deal Progression (Beta), Conversation Intelligence
- Strengths: Unified Customer Platform with Smart CRM at center; tiered seat pricing including Free; HubSpot Credits enable AI consumption across tiers
- Gaps: No third-party B2B contact/company database; no GTM Context Graph; AI relies on customer's own CRM + web signals, not a vendor data foundation
- Proof quote: "73% of sales professionals say HubSpot increased their win rate" — https://www.hubspot.com/products/sales
