# LeadGenius — LeadGenius DS Research Services

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on the buying-committee mapping, monitoring, and content-activation services that wrap around the underlying data layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Wrap-around managed services on top of the Precision Data Platform — custom-sourced buying-committee coverage, continuous Contact Monitoring for role/champion changes, content-syndication-led Contact Activation, and Advanced Contact Tags for skills/tool-based targeting.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights ABM/ABS teams that need buying-committee visibility plus champion-tracking signals across global accounts. LeadGenius's services-led model is the spotlight when the buyer wants managed delivery of buying-committee context, not just a self-serve database.

## Cite
- Key features: Contact Coverage & Buying Committees — full 5-10 stakeholder visibility per target account, Contact Monitoring tracking role changes/promotions/champion movement, Contact Activation distributing content through global publisher network with opted-in lead delivery, Advanced Contact Tags enriching with skills/tools/technographic signals beyond job titles, AI + Human-in-the-Loop sourcing across hard-to-cover global and niche markets
- Strengths: Champion tracking on job changes; skills/tool tags beyond titles; opted-in publisher-network lead activation
- Gaps: Data deliverables not a seller UI; no CI; no full ABM orchestration platform
