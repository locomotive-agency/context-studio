# Surfe — Surfe Company Search

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on ICP / lead-list building)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
ICP-finder app for building target-company and target-prospect lists with advanced filters and account look-alikes, with one-click export to CRM.

## Spotlight when
The article compares prospect-list builders. Surfe's distinct angles are daily refresh, CRM-dedup-during-build, and Account Lookalikes. ZoomInfo Sales wins on filter depth and Scoops; Surfe wins on freshness + dedup hygiene.

## Cite
- Key features: 350M companies daily-refreshed, broad filters, 10K/day people search, Account Lookalikes, CRM-dedup check, playbook lists
- Strengths: Daily refresh cadence; build-time CRM dedup; pre-built playbooks
- Gaps: Shallower filter depth vs ZI Sales; no native intent; daily caps
- Proof quote: "350M+ company profiles, refreshed daily" — https://www.surfe.com/company-search/
