# Voila Norbert — Email Sequences

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller-outreach surface — sequences and warm-up only)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Cold-email outreach engine layered on top of the email finder — multi-step sequences with warm-up, AI copywriting, A/B testing, and (coming soon) inbox rotation.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one cold-outbound stacks (find → verify → sequence) at sub-$100/user/mo against ZoomInfo's enterprise GTM Workspace. Lean on the $19/user starting price and bundled find/verify/sequence stack.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-step email sequences with scheduled follow-ups, AI email copywriting, A/B testing, Email warm-up, Built-in verification on send, Native CRM sync to Salesforce/HubSpot/Pipedrive
- Strengths: $19/user/mo entry; bundled stack; AI copy on Starter
- Gaps: Inbox rotation "Coming soon"; no GTM Context Graph; lighter analytics than Outreach/Salesloft
