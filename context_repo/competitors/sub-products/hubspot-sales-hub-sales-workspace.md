# HubSpot Sales Hub — Sales Workspace & Lead Management

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller daily action queue)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Purpose-built workspace inside Sales Hub that captures, qualifies, and nurtures prospects with AI-recommended next actions and embeds the Breeze Prospecting Agent for automated research and outreach.

## Spotlight when
The article compares seller workspaces / daily action queues. Sales Workspace's spotlight angle is "lead management + prospecting workspace anchored in CRM" versus ZoomInfo's data-anchored GTM Workspace.

## Cite
- Key features: Daily AI-recommended actions, Leads + target accounts in one view, AI meeting assistant, Automated pipeline progression by engagement, Real-time pipeline insights for managers, Breeze Prospecting Agent embedded
- Strengths: Single workspace anchored in Smart CRM; AI meeting assistant for prep + capture + follow-up; native CRM context
- Gaps: Driven by customer CRM not an external GTM graph; Lead Form Routing gated to Enterprise; Smart workspace requires Pro+
- Proof quote: "78% increase in deal close rate after 6 months" — https://www.hubspot.com/products/sales/sales-leads
