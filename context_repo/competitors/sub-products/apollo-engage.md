# Apollo — Apollo Engage

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-engagement workflow surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Sales engagement layer inside the Apollo platform — multi-step sequences, AI-drafted outreach, dialer, and deliverability tooling.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one platforms for sellers (data + engagement bundled) vs. ZoomInfo's data + GTM Workspace stack. The "all-in-one bundled" pitch is Engage's strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-step sequences (email + dialer + LinkedIn), AI-drafted outreach, Power Dialer with parallel calling, Deliverability monitor, Inbox warm-up
- Strengths: Bundled with Apollo data; public tiered pricing; integrated playbook templates
- Gaps: Lags Outreach/Salesloft on enterprise sequencing depth; credit-overage costs at scale; weaker CRM write-back
- Proof quote: "Connect with prospects faster using AI-powered sequencing" — https://www.apollo.io/product/engage
