# Instantly — Instantly Sales CRM

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — deal-management surface inside the cold-outreach pipeline rather than a head-to-head Salesforce/HubSpot replacement)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Native CRM that combines email, calling, SMS, tasks, and LinkedIn connections in one master inbox to manage deals from cold outreach through close — unlimited seats on a single subscription.

## Spotlight when
The article enumerates Instantly's full bundled stack and the trade-off between Instantly's outreach-attached CRM vs. a system-of-record CRM (Salesforce / HubSpot / Insightly).

## Cite
- Key features: Master inbox (email/calls/SMS/tasks), AI personalisation, automated salesflows, in-browser calling/SMS, native Lead Finder/Outreach connection, unlimited seats, lead-status flows
- Strengths: Unlimited seats; native in-browser calling/SMS; native bundle with Lead Finder + Outreach
- Gaps: Outreach-pipeline surface (not enterprise CRM); no CI; thinner analytics
