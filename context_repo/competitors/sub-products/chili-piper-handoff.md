# Chili Piper — Handoff

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — narrower meeting-booking focus rather than full seller workspace)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Outbound and team-to-team scheduling that lets SDRs book meetings on behalf of AEs in under 30 seconds, with fairness algorithms tracking show-rate and meeting count across reps. Also covers Closed-Won → Implementation handoff to CSM round-robin.

## Spotlight when
The article compares SDR-to-AE meeting handoff workflows or "next-best-action" tooling for outbound teams. Handoff is the angle when the buyer is solving for meeting-booking velocity rather than full pipeline prioritization.

- Strengths: Sub-30-second booking; 45K+ users; auto CRM logging
- Gaps: Narrower than GTM Workspace; no prospecting/sequencer/prioritization layer
