# GetProspect — GetProspect CRM

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (no overlap — GetProspect ships a free CRM to capture scraped leads, while ZoomInfo positions on top of Salesforce/HubSpot/Dynamics rather than as a CRM itself; included for completeness)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Free built-in lightweight CRM for organizing scraped or enriched leads into nested folders, dynamic lists, and prospect cards with custom fields — with multi-workspace team collaboration and direct push from the LinkedIn/web extension.

## Spotlight when
Rarely a primary spotlight — mention only when the article covers GetProspect's "all-in-one bundle" pitch. ZI doesn't compete as a CRM of record.

## Cite
- Key features: Nested folders and dynamic lists; custom lead fields used as filter criteria or email variables; prospect cards; multi-workspace team collaboration; CSV/XLSX import/export; direct push from LinkedIn/web
- Strengths: Free built-in CRM bundled with email finder + database; direct LinkedIn-to-CRM push; custom fields usable as email variables
- Gaps: Lightweight, not a system of record; no deal pipeline/forecasting depth; inverse posture vs. ZoomInfo
