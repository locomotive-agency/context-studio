# Demandbase — Demandbase One

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap as the unified ABX platform)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Unified Account-Based Experience platform — Demandbase's flagship spanning ABM advertising, account intelligence, sales intelligence, and B2B data.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM platforms or all-in-one B2B marketing platforms. Demandbase One is the right surface to spotlight; ZoomInfo Marketing is the natural ZoomInfo product to anchor the comparison.

## Cite
- Key features: Unified ABX (ads + account intel + sales intel + data), account-level orchestration, predictive scoring + intent, native ad targeting + retargeting
- Strengths: Platform consolidation pitch; established tenure; highest-fidelity vs-content
- Gaps: Pricing quote-based; no conversation intelligence; no MCP/agent ecosystem
- Proof quote: "The only platform that brings ABM, sales intelligence, and B2B data together." — https://www.demandbase.com/product/demandbase-one/
