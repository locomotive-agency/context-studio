# Showpad — Showpad GenieAI

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on the AI-coaching + conversation-capture surface, scoped to enablement)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Showpad's AI core/layer built on the customer's proprietary content with a Data + Trust governance layer; powers Genie Assistant chat, Showpad Agents (out-of-the-box and custom), Vision Intelligence, Voice, Roleplay AI, Authoring AI, and Field Meeting AI.

## Spotlight when
The article compares enablement-anchored AI coaching for regulated/physical-product industries against ZoomInfo's external-data-grounded GTM AI. GenieAI's Data + Trust governance is the strongest spotlight differentiator vs. broader copilots.

## Cite
- Key features: Genie Assistant; Showpad Agents (OOTB + custom); Vision Intelligence; Voice; Roleplay AI; Field Meeting AI; Data + Trust Layer
- Strengths: Data + Trust governance for regulated industries; agents tied to approved data; field-first (Field Meeting AI to CRM)
- Gaps: First-party-only grounding (no external B2B data/intent); no MCP/agent-builder ecosystem; no org-wide call search/coaching scorecards
