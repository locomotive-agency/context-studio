# Metadata — Metadata Campaigns (Marketing OS)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on B2B paid-media activation and audience-building)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Cross-channel paid-campaign automation that launches, optimizes, and reports on B2B paid ads — LinkedIn, Meta, Google, Reddit (Bing in development) — from a single tool, with multivariate experimentation, AI-agent bidding/targeting, and CRM-pipeline-driven optimization.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B paid-media platforms or ABM activation tools vs. ZoomInfo Marketing. Campaigns is Metadata's flagship and the natural head-to-head call-out for marketers consolidating LinkedIn/Meta/Google ad operations.

## Cite
- Key features: Cross-channel paid campaigns, Campaign Experimentation, Web Personalization, Lead Enrichment, Revenue Optimization, AI Agents
- Strengths: Single tool for all B2B paid channels; multivariate experimentation at scale; AI-agent-driven execution
- Gaps: No first-party B2B data at ZI scale; no seller/CI surface; no public pricing
- Proof quote: "You run the media strategy. AI runs the ads." — https://metadata.io/platform/
