# Dreamdata — Dreamdata B2B Attribution

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on multi-touch B2B attribution and pipeline measurement)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Multi-touch B2B attribution that connects marketing and sales touchpoints to pipeline and revenue across long, multi-stakeholder buyer journeys with 11 pre-built models.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on B2B attribution depth (vs B2C tools like GA), pipeline-stage credit, or account-based revenue measurement. Time-to-revenue and 11-model breadth are the strongest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: 11 attribution models (incl. Data-Driven), custom models, MQL/SQL/New Business stage attribution, time-to-revenue metric, account-based metrics, offline-touch credit, AI summaries
- Strengths: Built for 272-day / 10-stakeholder B2B journeys; account-based not user-based; AI report summaries on Advanced
- Gaps: Attribution-only layer (Audience Hub sold separately); Free plan limited; Advanced pricing gated
- Proof quote: "#1 B2B Attribution vendor on G2" — https://dreamdata.io/b2b-attribution
