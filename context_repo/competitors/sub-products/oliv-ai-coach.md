# Oliv AI — Coach

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap on coaching surface — Chorus emphasizes conversation-driven coaching, Oliv Coach delivers per-deal and per-rep coaching plans)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Creates personalized coaching plans for each rep — evaluates four skills (conversational, emotional intelligence, meeting management, deal management) — and helps managers set weekly and monthly focus areas.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI-driven per-deal coaching or rep skill development as the differentiator — Coach's per-deal plans plus dual rep/manager one-pager cadence are the spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Four-skill evaluation, Per-deal coaching plans, Weekly rep one-pager, Monthly manager one-pager
- Strengths: Per-deal coaching plans; dual cadence rep/manager artifacts; skill model beyond talk-time
- Gaps: No first-party content library; lighter coaching depth vs. Mindtickle/Highspot; four-skill coverage broader than deep
