# Clari — Clari Capture

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on CRM data quality)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Activity capture from email/calendar/calls into Salesforce — auto-logging that feeds Forecast and Inspect.

## Spotlight when
The article is about RevOps activity capture or CRM hygiene. ZoomInfo Operations' enrichment + GTM Studio's data routing/governance are the structural complement Clari Capture lacks.

## Cite
- Key features: Activity capture (email + calendar + calls), auto-logging, clean data feeding Forecast/Inspect
- Strengths: Frictionless capture; Forecast integration
- Gaps: No data enrichment; no verification rigor; doesn't replace ZoomInfo Operations
- Proof quote: "Get every email, meeting, and call into Salesforce — automatically." — https://www.clari.com/products/capture
