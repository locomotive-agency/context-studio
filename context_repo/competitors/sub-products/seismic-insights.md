# Seismic — Seismic Insights & Analysis

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap on the seller-effectiveness analytics surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
AI-powered sales insights and analytics surface that identifies what is improving sales effectiveness, accelerates deals, and improves win rates by tying content engagement and learning completion to revenue outcomes.

## Spotlight when
The article compares seller-effectiveness analytics across enablement and conversation-intelligence categories. Useful when the buyer cares about content-engagement-to-revenue attribution rather than call coaching.
