# Foundry ABM — Foundry ABM Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on ABM orchestration, intent-driven advertising, web personalization, and visitor identification)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Cross-channel account-based orchestration platform (formerly Triblio) that runs intent-driven campaigns across display/social advertising, website personalization, 1:1 Smart Pages, and CRM activation plays from a single command center.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM platforms or "ZoomInfo Marketing alternatives" — Foundry ABM's strongest spotlight angle is its bundled-orchestration story plus first-party publisher intent fueling the AI scoring layer.

## Cite
- Key features: Account-based display and social advertising, Website personalization, 1:1 Smart Pages, Multi-stage orchestrated buyer journeys triggered by intent, AI scoring on human-verified intent data, CRM-resident sales activation plays
- Strengths: Triblio orchestration heritage with proprietary first-party publisher intent baked in; 1:1 Smart Pages for per-account landing experiences; bundled ABM tactics under one SKU
- Gaps: No public pricing; no B2B contact data foundation; no conversation intelligence; no APIs & MCP / agent-builder lane
- Proof quote: "The account-based orchestration platform — together, sales and marketing can predict and grow pipeline through intent and account-based orchestration campaigns, resulting in more deals won." — https://foundryco.com/our-solutions/software-q2/
