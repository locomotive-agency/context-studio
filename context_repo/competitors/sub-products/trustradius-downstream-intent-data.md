# TrustRadius — TrustRadius Downstream Intent Data

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap — second-party vs. ZoomInfo's third-party intent positioning)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Second-party intent signals from in-market buyers researching the vendor, competitors, and category on TrustRadius — surfaced into vendor CRM, ABM, and paid media stacks.

## Spotlight when
The article frames intent-data freshness or "second-party vs. third-party" framing. TrustRadius's strongest pitch is the explicit contrast with third-party intent ZoomInfo Intent Data is built on.

## Cite
- Key features: Second-party intent, Granular pricing/comparison/demo signals, Salesforce Connector + churn alerts, LinkedIn/Demandbase/6sense activation, Multi-use-case
- Strengths: Second-party signals; behavioral granularity; churn-research alerts
- Gaps: Account-level only (no contact-level); TR-platform audience scope; HG bundling uncertain
- Proof quote: "Marketers make the mistake of interpreting third-party intent data as indicating that a buyer is in-market. However, by activating second-party, downstream intent data you will reach new, in-market accounts" — https://solutions.trustradius.com/products/downstream-intent-data/
