# Dealfront — Dealfront Promote / Campaigns (B2B Display Ads)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on the ABM display + audience-activation surface — Dealfront uniquely runs its own ad platform)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Native B2B display-advertising module that runs IP-targeted programmatic display ads at named accounts and ICP audiences built inside the platform — with LinkedIn audience activation and automatic retargeting of open CRM opportunities.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM activation, especially when the angle is integrated audience identification + ad serving in one platform.

## Cite
- Key features: Native B2B display-advertising module with IP-targeted programmatic ads, LinkedIn audience activation with >90% claimed match, Ad-campaign attribution back to website visits, Automatically retargets open CRM opportunities, Run ads at named accounts and ICP audiences built in-platform
- Strengths: Native programmatic display-ad platform ZoomInfo does not operate; automatic retargeting closes the loop with seller pipeline; >90% LinkedIn audience match
- Gaps: Smaller media reach than dedicated ABM platforms (Demandbase, 6sense); no GTM Studio-level NL audience builder; EUR-anchored billing; weaker first-party intent vs ZoomInfo
