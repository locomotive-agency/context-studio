# Foundry ABM — Foundry Intent

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on multi-source intent capture and ABM activation)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Multi-source intent data product that captures buying signals from the customer's website, public web, Foundry's owned media network, and human-verified research, then activates them inside the ABM platform and partner CRM/MAP/ad ecosystems.

## Spotlight when
The article compares intent-data sources or first-party publisher intent vs. third-party clickstream — Foundry Intent's strongest spotlight angle is the publisher-network heritage and human-verified scoring layer.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-source intent (customer site + public web + Foundry's owned media + human-verified research), 80M+ intent signals captured per week, 150M+ audience members, 7,600+ targeting dimensions, Reverse-IP website visitor identification, Pre-built CRM/MAP/ad activation pipes
- Strengths: First-party intent from owned tech publisher network; human-verified + AI scoring; pre-built activation into Salesforce/HubSpot/Dynamics/SugarCRM/Adobe/Google
- Gaps: Tech-media-biased ICP; no conversation intelligence; pricing gated to sales
