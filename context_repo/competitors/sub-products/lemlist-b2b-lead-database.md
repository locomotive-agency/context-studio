# Lemlist — Lemlist B2B Lead Database

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on B2B contact/company database; differs on sourcing model)

**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
600M+ contact / 65M+ company database with ICP filters, intent signals, and 1-click waterfall enrichment that pushes leads straight into Lemlist multichannel campaigns.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B contact databases or "data + outbound bundled" vendors vs. ZoomInfo's Data (Pillar 1). Lemlist's strongest angle is pay-as-you-go credit pricing wired into a sequence engine — different commercial shape than ZoomInfo's seat model.

## Cite
- Key features: 600M+ contacts and 63M-65M+ companies, ICP filters, 1-click waterfall enrichment, intent signals priced per-credit, direct push to campaigns, CSV/Excel/LinkedIn export
- Strengths: Pay-as-you-go credits with no expiration; database wired into sequences; tighter cost-control commercial model
- Gaps: Third-party-aggregated (not first-party multi-source verified); smaller firmographic depth than ZoomInfo; thinner intent + technographic stack
- Proof quote: "600M+ contact and 63M-65M+ company database" — https://www.lemlist.com/product/b2b-lead-database
