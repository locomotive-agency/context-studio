# Mailshake — Mailshake Data Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — thin contact-lookup add-on, not a comparable B2B data foundation)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Add-on prospect database ($19/mo) that lets users find business contact emails by role, location, or company and push them directly into a Mailshake campaign — with 50–2,500 credits per plan tier.

## Spotlight when
The article compares low-cost ZoomInfo alternatives or cold-email-tool data add-ons. Data Finder's strongest angle: $19/mo bundled-into-campaigns is a credible SMB entry point — but it does not claim ZoomInfo's data scale.

## Cite
- Key features: Filter by role/location/company, 50 free credits on Starter/Email Outreach, 2,500 on Sales Engagement, direct push to campaigns, $19/mo add-on
- Strengths: Wired into campaigns; cheap entry point; bundled credits on Sales Engagement
- Gaps: Thin database vs. 500M-contact ZI scale; low credit ceiling (50-2,500/mo); not claiming equivalent data depth
- Proof quote: "Add-on prospect database that lets users find business contact emails by role, location, or company and add them to Mailshake campaigns" — https://mailshake.com/pricing/
