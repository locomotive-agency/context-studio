# Momentum — Conversation Intelligence Add-On

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on call recording / transcription / summarization)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
$30/user/mo add-on that records, transcribes, and summarizes Zoom / Google Meet / Teams calls; stores them in a centralized video library with SmartClips highlights and Account Briefs with stakeholder mapping.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation-intelligence platforms vs. Chorus. Cite when buyers want CI bundled with execution / CRM-hygiene workflows rather than CI as a standalone product.

## Cite
- Key features: Note-taker bot (Meet), Auto-recording bot (Zoom), Video library, SmartClips, 99+ language summaries, Account Briefs
- Strengths: 99+ language translation; SmartClips highlights; Account Briefs with stakeholder mapping
- Gaps: Add-on (not core); integrates rather than displaces Chorus/Gong/Clari; less mature deal-coaching depth
