# LinkedIn Sales Navigator — Sales Navigator Core

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) — direct overlap on B2B data, but structurally complementary (LinkedIn graph vs. verified contact data)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
LinkedIn's flagship sales-intelligence tool — advanced search across the LinkedIn graph with lead and account recommendations.

## Spotlight when
The article compares LinkedIn-based prospecting workflows or B2B data foundations. Frame the relationship as data-source vs. data-platform — most ZoomInfo customers run Sales Navigator alongside ZoomInfo for verified emails + direct dials.

## Cite
- Key features: Advanced LinkedIn search with 50+ filters, lead/account recommendations, real-time updates + alerts, saved searches + lead lists
- Strengths: LinkedIn graph (largest professional network globally); self-updating member profiles
- Gaps: No verified email/direct-dial delivery; complementary not replacement
- Proof quote: "The world's largest professional network — for sales." — https://business.linkedin.com/sales-solutions/sales-navigator/core
