# ContactOut — ContactOut Chrome Extension

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the LinkedIn-overlay prospecting workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Browser extension that surfaces verified personal/work emails and direct dials for prospects on LinkedIn (Standard, Recruiter Lite, Recruiter Pro, Sales Navigator) and on any company website, with one-click CRM/ATS sync and AI-personalized outreach.

## Spotlight when
The article compares LinkedIn-overlay prospecting tools or argues for personal-email coverage as a differentiator. ContactOut's broader LinkedIn surface support (Recruiter Lite/Pro + Sales Nav + any website) is its strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Personal/work email + direct-dial reveal on LinkedIn, ContactOut Anywhere, AI Personalizer, Bulk list building, One-click CRM/ATS sync
- Strengths: Recruiter Lite/Pro support; ~75% personal-email coverage on LinkedIn; works on any website
- Gaps: No intent signals; no conversation intelligence; weaker prioritization vs GTM Workspace
- Proof quote: "Find anyone's email and phone number 10x faster — the world's most accurate contact data for 350M professionals" — https://contactout.com/chrome-extension
