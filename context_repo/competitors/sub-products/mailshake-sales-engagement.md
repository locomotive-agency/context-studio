# Mailshake — Mailshake Sales Engagement Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-engagement / multichannel-cadence surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Multi-channel outbound sales engagement tool that lets reps build sequences combining cold email, LinkedIn, and phone in a single dashboard, with a unified Lead Catcher inbox and SHAKEspeare AI writer.

## Spotlight when
The article compares low-cost cold-email / sales-engagement platforms vs. ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace. Mailshake's strongest spotlight: $29-$99/user/mo public pricing and same-day onboarding — undercuts ZoomInfo's ~$250/user/mo average cost.

## Cite
- Key features: Automated email sequences w/ mail merge, Lead Catcher unified inbox, LinkedIn Automation, multi-channel email+phone+social, pipeline analytics, SHAKEspeare AI writer
- Strengths: Public per-seat pricing from $29; first-day onboarding; SHAKEspeare AI on every plan
- Gaps: No first-party intent/technographics; no conversation intelligence; thin data layer
- Proof quote: "100,000+ sales teams" — https://mailshake.com/pricing/
