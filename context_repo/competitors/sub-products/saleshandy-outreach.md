# Saleshandy — Saleshandy Outreach

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-engagement workflow surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Multi-step, multichannel cold email outreach engine with AI sequence generation, conditional follow-ups, sender rotation across unlimited email accounts, and a unified inbox with AI reply categorization.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one outbound platforms (data + engagement bundled at SMB/agency price points) against ZoomInfo + a standalone sequencing tool stack. Unlimited mailbox + sender rotation is Outreach's strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: AI Copilot sequence generation, multichannel (email/phone/LinkedIn/WhatsApp), conditional branching, sender rotation, unified inbox, Spintax personalization
- Strengths: Unlimited connected email accounts at every tier; built-in sender rotation; transparent $25/mo entry price
- Gaps: No conversation intelligence depth; no ABM/marketing layer; lightweight CRM vs. enterprise patterns
