# Semrush — Semrush AI Visibility Toolkit

**Competes with:** APIs & MCP (no functional overlap; partner-class capability ZoomInfo doesn't sell)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Tracks how a brand appears in AI-generated answers across ChatGPT, SearchGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, and benchmarks share of voice vs. competitors at the prompt level.

## Spotlight when
The article covers AI-search visibility / generative engine optimization (GEO) as a marketing-leadership concern. ZoomInfo's CRO is a public Semrush customer here — frame as a partner-class capability, not a competitor.

## Cite
- Key features: Prompt-level visibility tracking, AI market share + sentiment, brand visibility across LLMs, persona-based prompt generation, 239M+ prompt corpus
- Strengths: Dedicated AI-visibility / GEO surface; broad model coverage; ZoomInfo CRO public testimonial
- Gaps: Tracks brand visibility not B2B intent; no CRM/conversation context; brand/marketing ICP
- Proof quote: "+373% increase in share of voice" — https://www.semrush.com/
