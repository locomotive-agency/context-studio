# Monday CRM — AI Notetaker

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap — lightweight in-CRM CI vs. dedicated CI surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
AI Notetaker that joins meetings live, transcribes in real time, and produces summaries / action items attached directly to deals and accounts inside monday CRM — paired with mobile in-app calling and call summaries.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-bundled call notes vs. dedicated CI like Chorus. AI Notetaker is the natural call-out when buyers want lightweight CI without a separate platform.

## Cite
- Key features: Live transcription, AI summaries + action items, Notes attached to deals, Mobile in-app calling with summaries
- Strengths: CI baked into CRM workflow; auto-attached deal notes; mobile calling
- Gaps: No Chorus-grade coaching/trackers/forecast modeling; no CI library; bundled-only
