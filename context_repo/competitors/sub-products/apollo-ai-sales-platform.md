# Apollo — Apollo AI Sales Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (specifically the AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace) — direct overlap
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-agent layer of the Apollo platform — autonomous agents for prospecting, outreach drafting, account research, and follow-up.

## Spotlight when
The article compares AI sales agents or AI sales tools. This is the most-cited Apollo spotlight in any AI-agent comparison.

## Cite
- Key features: AI agents for prospecting/outreach, AI-drafted personalized emails, conversation summaries, AI account research, workflow automation across CRM + sequencing
- Strengths: Native ties to Apollo data; public tiered pricing; SMB-friendly time-to-value
- Gaps: No GTM Context Graph equivalent for cross-signal reasoning; thinner enterprise proof points; AI capability claims lack specificity
- Proof quote: "AI agents that prospect, draft, and follow up — built into the platform sellers already use." — https://www.apollo.io/ai
