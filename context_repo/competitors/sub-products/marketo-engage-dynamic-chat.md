# Marketo Engage — Adobe Dynamic Chat

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Chat (direct overlap on conversational marketing / chatbot for B2B web)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Generative AI-powered conversational marketing tool embedded in Marketo Engage with a natural-language interface trained on the customer's brand and products, supporting two-way live chat plus self-qualification.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversational marketing / chatbots vs. ZoomInfo Chat. Dynamic Chat's strongest spotlight: AI trained on customer brand + native Marketo handoff — but it lacks ZI Chat's visitor identification + firmographic enrichment.

## Cite
- Key features: Two-way chat with live sales agents, AI conversation summaries + assisted responses, self-qualification flow, Engaged Conversations 10K/100K per tier, Gen AI trained on customer brand
- Strengths: Embedded in Marketo Engage; Gen AI brand-trained; live agent + self-qualification combined
- Gaps: Capped conversation volume per tier; no visitor identification or firmographic enrichment; not sold standalone
