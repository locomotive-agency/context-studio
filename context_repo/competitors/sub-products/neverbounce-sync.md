# NeverBounce — Sync

**Competes with:** None — NeverBounce is a wholly-owned ZoomInfo product line. Documented for ownership context only.
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Connects to a CRM and continuously verifies contacts in the background with no manual exports, flagging bad emails before they reach workflows.

## Spotlight when
Articles on continuous CRM email hygiene where the ZoomInfo Operations + NeverBounce Sync stack is the recommended path.

## Cite
- Key features: Always-on 24/7 CRM sync, Credits only on new unique emails (Growth), Bad-email flagging pre-workflow, Optional CRM connection
- Strengths: Continuous background hygiene without manual exports; duplicate-free credits; native ZoomInfo Operations fit
- Gaps: ZoomInfo-owned (not a competitive substitute); narrow CRM coverage; verification only — no enrichment
