# ActiveCampaign — Sales CRM

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (adjacent — overlaps on SMB pipelines + AI scoring, but no third-party data feed)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
CRM for nimble SMB sales teams — pipelines, AI lead/deal scoring, sales routing, 1:1 email, and attribution all unified with the marketing automation surface.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses SMB sellers consolidating CRM + marketing in one tool. ActiveCampaign's strongest spotlight angle is "no separate Salesforce instance needed" — but call out that there is no third-party data layer.

- Key features: Customizable pipelines, AI lead scoring + win probability, Sales routing, 1:1 email, Sales reporting, Attribution
