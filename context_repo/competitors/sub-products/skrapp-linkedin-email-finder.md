# Skrapp — LinkedIn Email Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on LinkedIn-sourced verified contact data)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Chrome extension that reveals verified business emails from any LinkedIn profile, Sales Navigator search, or LinkedIn Recruiter result, with bulk extraction up to 1,000 profiles per minute and auto-save back into Sales Navigator lists.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare LinkedIn-native prospecting workflows or argue that sellers want verified emails surfaced in-line on Sales Navigator instead of in a separate database UI.

## Cite
- Key features: Chrome extension on LinkedIn / Sales Navigator / Recruiter, 97% verification accuracy, AI-enriched role/seniority/function, bulk 1,000 profiles per minute, auto-save to Sales Navigator lists, 4.8/5 Chrome with 200K+ users
- Strengths: LinkedIn-native surface; auto-save back to Sales Navigator; high bulk throughput
- Gaps: Scoped to LinkedIn only; no intent or technographic depth; no governance/routing
- Proof quote: "AI-enriched role, seniority, and function data on every lead." — https://skrapp.io/linkedin
