# AlphaSense — Workflow Agents

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on scheduled/agentic research delivery)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Custom and scheduled AI agents that automate repeatable research tasks, turning research from manual pull into automated push for analyst teams.

## Spotlight when
Comparing agent ecosystems. Workflow Agents are AlphaSense's lightweight automation surface — useful as a contrast to GTM Workspace AI agents that drive seller actions.
