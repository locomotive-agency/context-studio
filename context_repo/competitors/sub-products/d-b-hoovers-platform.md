# D&B Hoovers — D&B Hoovers Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the seller-facing prospecting + intelligence UI)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
B2B sales acceleration platform combining D&B's commercial database (330M+ companies, 520M+ contacts) with AI-powered Prospect Scoring, news triggers, intent data, and 175+ company-search filters — with Healthcare and Technology vertical add-ons layered into the same UI.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Sales' classic prospecting UI against incumbent legacy data vendors, especially when the angle is data heritage (D-U-N-S Number, 1841, Fortune 1000 footprint) or vertical-specific add-ons.

## Cite
- Key features: 330M+ companies and 520M+ contacts/70M+ emails/60M+ direct dials, AI-powered Prospect Scoring, 175+ filters, News Triggers and opportunity triggers, Buyer intent data, Healthcare and Technology vertical add-ons
- Strengths: Data heritage going back to 1841 with 90% of Fortune 1000 footprint; vertical add-ons inside the same UI; public SMB self-serve at $49/mo
- Gaps: Gartner reviewers flag data freshness/accuracy issues (~70% accuracy); no conversation intelligence; no GTM Workspace / GTM Studio / APIs & MCP; enterprise pricing gated
- Proof quote: "D&B Hoovers is Your Sales Accelerator — speed up sales prospecting and spend more time closing using rich audience targeting backed by the Dun & Bradstreet Data Cloud." — https://www.dnb.com/en-us/products/dnb-hoovers.html
