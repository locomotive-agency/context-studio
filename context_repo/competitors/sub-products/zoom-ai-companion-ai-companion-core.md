# Zoom AI Companion — AI Companion (core)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — AI Companion is a productivity AI surface bundled into Zoom Workplace; it shares the "AI assistant for sellers" framing but operates over the user's own meeting/email/Drive context, not over third-party B2B prospect data)

> **Disambiguation:** Zoom AI Companion is a product of Zoom Communications, Inc. (NASDAQ: ZM) — a separate company from ZoomInfo.

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Generative AI assistant bundled with paid Zoom Workplace plans that captures meeting context, summarizes conversations, takes notes, drafts content, and surfaces insights across Zoom Meetings, Chat, Mail, and the ai.zoom.us web surface — using a federated mix of Zoom's own LLMs plus OpenAI and Anthropic models.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI assistants for sellers and is careful to distinguish productivity AI (Zoom AI Companion) from B2B sales intelligence AI (ZoomInfo). The "no extra cost / bundled with Zoom seats" angle and the disambiguation point are the strongest spotlight notes.
