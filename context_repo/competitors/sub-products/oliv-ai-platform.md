# Oliv AI — Revenue Intelligence Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller workspace surface — deal tracking, CRM updates, methodology-aligned scoring)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-native revenue intelligence layer that connects to CRM, email, calendar, calls, and meetings to capture deal data and serve as the data layer Oliv's AI agents act on.

## Spotlight when
The article positions agentic seller workspaces or revenue-intelligence platforms as alternatives to a stack of Gong + Clari + CRM. Oliv's Process Graph and 100+ revenue-tuned models are the strongest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: 100+ deal data points (MEDDIC/SPICED/BANT), Deal Scorecard, Buying Committee map, CRM stage automation, Two-way SFDC/HubSpot sync, 100+ revenue ML models
- Strengths: Process Graph for company-specific context; AI-native architecture; modular pack pricing
- Gaps: No B2B database (relies on ZoomInfo/SalesIntel); no marketing/ABM surface; no public API/MCP
- Proof quote: "Runs 100+ models trained specifically on revenue data, so every signal is structured, not just summarized." — https://www.oliv.ai/
