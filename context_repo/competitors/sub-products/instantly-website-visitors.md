# Instantly — Instantly Website Visitors

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap — anonymous-traffic identification with email/LinkedIn resolution and direct campaign import)
**Spotlight rank:** 6

## What it does
Identifies anonymous US-based website visitors and resolves them to business email or LinkedIn for direct import into Instantly outreach campaigns — Hyper ($97/mo) and Lightspeed ($397/mo) tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article compares visitor-ID products (WebSights, Snitcher, RB2B, Lead Forensics) and the activation pattern of routing identified visitors directly into outbound sequences.

## Cite
- Key features: US-based traffic tracking with business emails / job titles / page history, 1 credit = 1 resolved profile, direct import into campaigns, Hyper / Lightspeed tiers
- Strengths: Direct campaign import; per-credit pricing; bundled with Instantly stack
- Gaps: US-only traffic; resolves to person+company only; smaller scope than WebSights
