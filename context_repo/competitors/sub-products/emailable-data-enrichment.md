# Emailable — Emailable Data Enrichment

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent — per-result email-derived enrichment vs ZI Operations' full match/enrich/route workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Returns enrichment attributes alongside each verification result — SMTP provider, MX, name, gender, disposable/role/tag flags, and an Email Quality Score 0-100.

## Spotlight when
The article frames email-derived enrichment vs full firmographic enrichment. Use to highlight that Emailable's enrichment is scoped to email-attribute metadata (not contact/company data).

## Cite
- Key features: SMTP provider detection, typo-correction for misspelled domains, MX record detection, name/gender detection, disposable/accept-all/free/role flags, Email Quality Score 0-100
- Strengths: Per-result attributes including quality score; typo-correction; SMTP provider in every response
- Gaps: Email-derived only — no firmographic data; no full CRM enrichment workflow; not a Match/Enrich/Route substitute
- Proof quote: "SMTP provider detection, MX, name, gender, and Email Quality Score" — https://emailable.com/
