# Zoom AI Companion — AI Companion 3.0

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the agentic / cross-system reasoning surface — AIC 3.0's intelligent retrieval across Zoom + Drive + OneDrive + Gmail + Outlook + Slack is conceptually parallel to GTM Context Graph reasoning across CRM + conversations + intent, but Zoom's data layer is internal collaboration content, not B2B prospect data)

> **Disambiguation:** Zoom AI Companion is a product of Zoom Communications, Inc. (NASDAQ: ZM) — a separate company from ZoomInfo.

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
The Dec 2025 release of AI Companion, positioned as "agentic AI" that goes from "conversation to completion" — supports pre-meeting prep, in-meeting assistance, and post-meeting follow-up via the ai.zoom.us web surface, with new free and $10/mo standalone tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article covers agentic AI launches in 2025 or compares cross-system reasoning surfaces. The standalone $10/mo SKU and the Cross Meeting Analyst feature are the strongest 3.0-specific spotlight angles; the disambiguation note is mandatory for any ZoomInfo-comparison framing.

## Cite
- Key features: AI-first web interface at ai.zoom.us, Daily Reflection Report pulling insights across meetings/docs/notes, Cross Meeting Analyst prompt for recurring topics and unresolved issues, Agentic writing mode with side-by-side AI editing, Intelligent retrieval auto-searches connected work content, AI Companion Basic free tier inside Zoom Workplace Basic, $10/month standalone subscription
- Strengths: $10/mo standalone tier removes need for a paid Zoom Workplace license; Cross Meeting Analyst surfaces insights across multiple meetings; AI-first web surface (ai.zoom.us) is the primary entry point
- Gaps: Cross-meeting reasoning bounded by user's own connected apps (no third-party prospect data); no public MCP server; "conversation to completion" agentic positioning lacks third-party benchmarks vs ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace
