# Regie.ai — RegieOne

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the AI-agent + seller-execution layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Unified AI sales engagement platform that combines enrichment, dialing, email, social sequencing and reporting in one workflow, pairing AI agents with human reps for prospecting.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one AI sales engagement platforms or the AI-agent layer sitting on top of a seller workspace. RegieOne's "world's only AI SEP" claim is its sharpest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-channel sequencing agents, 1st/3rd-party intent prioritization, Power dialer + virtual Sales Floor, AI persona library, Native CRM + SEP integrations
- Strengths: AI SEP positioning; public per-seat pricing; foundational AI-agents-in-GTM patent
- Gaps: No GTM Studio analogue; no APIs/MCP; no standalone CI product
