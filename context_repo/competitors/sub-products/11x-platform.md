# 11x — 11x Platform

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on contact + company + signal layer; adjacent to ZoomInfo Operations on CRM enrichment)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
The underlying platform powering Alice and Julian — data and lead generation, research/messaging, outreach/workflows, inbound handling, analytics/deliverability, and integrations — sold as the data + execution layer rather than a standalone contact database.

## Spotlight when
The article compares data foundations or signal-driven prospecting layers. 11x's strongest spotlight angle is "real-time aggregated data layer feeding an autonomous worker" vs. ZoomInfo's verified-at-rest data foundation.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-Time Company and Lead Database, Visitor Tracking + Signals, Multi-Channel Sequences, Lead Routing + Qualification, Native CRM sync, Deliverability + Number Management
- Strengths: Real-time aggregation; native bi-directional CRM; built-in deliverability eliminates point tools
- Gaps: Far smaller scale than ZoomInfo; no published technographics depth; no MCP / agent-builder marketplace
