# Lead Onion — Lead Onion Intent Qualified Leads (IQLs)

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the verified B2B contact universe)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Converts intent signals into verified, deliverable leads matched to the customer's ICP — each IQL ships with verified email, direct dial, LinkedIn profile, surge-strength, and buyer-phase ranking, and auto-routes to CRM, cadence, or Slack via Leadflows. Backed by Cognism contact data at the platform layer.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B contact databases head-to-head with ZoomInfo Sales — Lead Onion's "intent-ranked contacts at $500/mo" framing is the strongest spotlight angle for SMB / mid-market buyers.

## Cite
- Key features: ICP matching with verified email/dial/LinkedIn, 99% deliverability claim, Surge-strength + buyer-phase ranking, Real-time email verification, Leadflows auto-routing
- Strengths: Contact-level intent (Delivr.ai + PharosIQ); surge + phase ranking on every IQL; 209M contacts at $500/mo entry
- Gaps: 209M vs ZoomInfo's 500M contacts; person-level intent US-only; underlying data is Cognism-powered not first-party
