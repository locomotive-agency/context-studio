# Salesforge — Salesforge Infrastructure

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — Salesforge sells email-infrastructure provisioning + warm-up that ZoomInfo does not, plus Leadsforge data layer adjacent to GTM Studio)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
The Forge Stack — Mailforge (bulk domain + mailbox provisioning), Warmforge (premium warm-up pool), Infraforge (private dedicated infrastructure), Megaforge (Agent Frank-specific multi-ESP), and Leadsforge (chat-based AI lead-search across 500M+ contacts) — all sold as separately billable infrastructure layers under one login.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights agencies / lead-gen ops teams that need email infrastructure (domains, mailboxes, deliverability) alongside their outreach platform. Salesforge Infrastructure is the spotlight when the buyer wants a vertically-integrated stack and ZoomInfo does not sell domain/mailbox/DKIM provisioning.

## Cite
- Key features: Mailforge bulk domain + mailbox provisioning with automated DKIM/DMARC/SPF, Warmforge premium internal warm-up pool (Google Workspace + MS365), Infraforge private dedicated infrastructure with multi-IP/SSL/domain masking, Megaforge premium multi-ESP infrastructure for Agent Frank, Leadsforge chat-based AI lead-search engine across 500M+ contacts, Whitelabel reseller programs (Salesforge Whitelabel, Infraforge Whitelabel)
- Strengths: Vertically integrated outbound stack; Warmforge excludes external SMTPs; Whitelabel reseller programs
- Gaps: Email-infrastructure not a ZoomInfo product; tiers billed separately; Leadsforge pre-launch / early-access
