# Allego — Conversation Intelligence

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on call recording, transcription, topic/competitor tracking, deal-risk signals)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Captures and analyzes sales conversations and ties insights directly to coaching, learning, and rep performance — call recording + transcription + searchable transcripts + topic tracking + deal-risk signals.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation-intelligence platforms head-to-head with Chorus. Allego CI's strongest spotlight is "CI tied into learning + coaching + DSR" — but flag the asymmetric customer relationship (ZoomInfo is an Allego customer).

- Strengths: Tied into learning/coaching/DSRs; multi-language; combined engagement view
