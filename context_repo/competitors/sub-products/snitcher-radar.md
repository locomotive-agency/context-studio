# Snitcher — Radar

**Competes with:** WebSights (adjacent — embedded white-label visitor-ID, a space ZoomInfo does not publicly market)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
White-label visitor identification infrastructure embedded inside another SaaS product or platform — full tracking + enrichment with custom-domain branding and events streamed to the customer's webhook.

## Spotlight when
Articles position Snitcher's edge over ZoomInfo on embedded / OEM use cases, or compare GTM stacks for SaaS product teams that want to surface visitor identity inside their own product.

## Cite
- Key features: Full tracking + enrichment infrastructure; white-label custom domains/brand; events streamed to webhook; SaaS platform / product team focus
- Strengths: White-label infra for SaaS; custom-domain branding; webhook-native events
- Gaps: Embedded infra (not turnkey); no in-app reasoning layer; webhook-only output
- Proof quote: "Complete white-label using customer's domains and brand." — https://snitcher.com/platform/
