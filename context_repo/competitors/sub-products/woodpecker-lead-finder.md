# Woodpecker — Lead Finder

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — 1B record DB without ZoomInfo's verification depth or signal layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Built-in B2B contact-discovery feature that surfaces verified emails from a 1B+ record database for cold outreach — 400 free credits monthly with per-credit billing on additional verified results.

## Spotlight when
The article compares email finders bundled inside outbound platforms or contrasts SMB-friendly contact databases with ZoomInfo's enterprise data. Lean on 400 free monthly credits and no-charge-on-risky-results.

## Cite
- Key features: 1+ billion record B2B database, 400 free credits/month, Additional credits from $28/month per 2,000, No charge on 'Risky' / 'Guess' results
- Strengths: 400 free credits on every paid plan; pay only for verified results; bundled with Cold Email
- Gaps: Verification depth lighter than ZoomInfo; no firmographic/technographic/intent layer; no mobile numbers in dossier
