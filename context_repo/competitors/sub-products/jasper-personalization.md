# Jasper — Personalization (incl. ABM)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap — content-activation lane for ABM; ZoomInfo supplies data, Jasper produces messaging)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Use case bundle that turns CRM data, behavioral signals, and audience segments into on-brand personalized content across ABM, lifecycle, and field-marketing channels — drives account-level messaging from firmographic, role, industry, and intent data sourced from the customer's Salesforce.

## Spotlight when
The article evaluates "where does ABM content actually get written" — Jasper is the strongest ZoomInfo crossover, supplying the messaging layer to ZoomInfo's data + audience graph.

## Cite
- Key features: ABM messaging from firmographic/role/industry/intent data, Personalization Agent for lifecycle emails, Salesforce Beta integration, account-level landing-page generation via Grid, event-marketing personalization
- Strengths: Account-level landing pages via Grid; brand-enforced personalisation; live-CRM event marketing
- Gaps: No first-party data; Salesforce integration Beta & Business-only; no ABM advertising layer
- Proof quote: "deeply personalized, account-level messaging driven by firmographic, role, industry, and intent data" — https://www.jasper.ai/solutions/personalization
