# Mailshake — Mailshake Email Deliverability Suite

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no direct overlap — Mailshake bundles email deliverability tooling that ZoomInfo does not productize; included to map the full Mailshake surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Built-in deliverability tooling included with every plan — email warm-up, validation credits, mailbox rotation, ready-to-use mailboxes, spam-score analyzer, and domain setup assistant — to keep cold outreach landing in the inbox.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses cold-email deliverability or spam-folder objections. Mailshake's strongest spotlight: deliverability is a hero feature on every plan, not an upsell — and ZoomInfo has no native equivalent.

## Cite
- Key features: SMTP email warm-up, email validation credits, mailbox rotation, ready-to-use mailboxes (SPF/MX/DKIM), spam-score + copy analyzer, domain setup assistant
- Strengths: Bundled on every plan; ready-to-use mailbox add-on; in-app spam-score at send time
- Gaps: Pure deliverability infra (no pipeline generation); only inside Mailshake; no ZoomInfo overlap (white space)
