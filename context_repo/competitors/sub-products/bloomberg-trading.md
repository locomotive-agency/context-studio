# Bloomberg — Bloomberg Trading

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (none — pure financial-markets execution; no ZoomInfo overlap)

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Multi-asset electronic trading, order management and execution management products integrated with the Bloomberg Terminal.

## Spotlight when
Generally do not spotlight — included for completeness of Bloomberg's product taxonomy. Useful only when explaining how broad Bloomberg's surface is vs ZoomInfo's narrow GTM focus.
