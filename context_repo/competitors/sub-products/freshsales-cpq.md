# Freshsales — Freshsales CPQ

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no overlap — CPQ is outside ZI's GTM scope; included for completeness because it's a named Freshsales module)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Add-on inside Freshsales that lets sales reps generate branded documents — quotes, invoices, contracts, onboarding documents — directly from CRM deal records, with one license bundled into the Growth plan.

## Spotlight when
Rarely a primary spotlight; mention only when an article frames Freshsales' "all-in-one" deal-execution story or compares CPQ-adjacent offerings. ZI does not compete in CPQ.

## Cite
- Key features: Generate quotes/invoices/contracts/onboarding documents, 1 CPQ license included with Growth plan, Additional licenses at $19/user/month, Linked to deal records inside Freshsales
- Strengths: Quotes/invoices/contracts directly from CRM deal records; bundled license on Growth; branded document generation in core CRM motion
- Gaps: Outside ZoomInfo's GTM scope; limited to Freshsales-originated deals; add-on licensing tax beyond the bundled seat
