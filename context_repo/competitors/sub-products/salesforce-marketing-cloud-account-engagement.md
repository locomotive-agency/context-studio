# Salesforce Marketing Cloud — Marketing Cloud Account Engagement (formerly Pardot)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on B2B marketing automation, lead scoring, ABM dashboards)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
B2B marketing automation platform built on Salesforce CRM that aligns marketing and sales to generate, nurture, score, and convert leads into pipeline — with Account Engagement+ adding Agentforce campaign creation.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B marketing automation platforms (formerly Pardot) against ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM and demand-gen workflows. This is the canonical Salesforce-side B2B MA product.

## Cite
- Key features: Einstein Lead Scoring + Grading, Engagement Programs, email/forms/landing pages, B2B Marketing Analytics, ABM dashboards, native CRM sync
- Strengths: Built on #1 CRM, Gartner MQ Leader, Agentforce campaign creation in "+" tier
- Gaps: No third-party contacts/intent, no CI, $1,250+/mo entry pricing
- Proof quote: "The only marketing automation platform built on the world's #1 CRM" — https://www.salesforce.com/marketing/b2b-automation/
