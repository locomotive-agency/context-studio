# Experian — Experian Aperture Data Studio

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on data hygiene, validation, dedupe, and enrichment for CRM)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Self-service data quality management platform combining profiling, cleansing, validation, transformation, enrichment, matching/dedupe, and monitoring in one web-based application — deployable on-prem, in-cloud, or as a hosted/managed service.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo Operations against pure data-quality vendors. Aperture is the strongest example of a "data engineering" approach to CRM hygiene — drag-and-drop, no SQL, with built-in Experian datasets — vs. ZoomInfo Operations' GTM-native enrichment.

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop profile/cleanse/validate/transform/enrich/dedupe workflows, Address/email/phone verification, Advanced fuzzy matching, Data catalogue with quality monitoring, AI & GenAI automations + SDK, On-prem/cloud/hosted deployment
- Strengths: No-SQL self-service; SOC 2 Type 2 with flexible deployment; Experian datasets bundled in
- Gaps: No GTM seller surface; no conversation intelligence/intent/engagement; gated pricing
