A pre-built, customizable AI BDR/SDR agent that books meetings 24/7, runs inbound and outbound workflows, researches prospects, handles replies, and schedules meetings.

## Spotlight when
The article covers AI SDR / autonomous agent products (vs. ZoomInfo's AI-agent layer inside GTM Workspace). Bosh's "Tier 4–5 accounts" coverage-expansion framing is its sharpest spotlight angle.
