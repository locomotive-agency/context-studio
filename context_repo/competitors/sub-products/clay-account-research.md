# Clay — Account Research

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller research surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Salesforce-native pre-call notes and account research for sellers, with Clay-in-ChatGPT and Claygent powering the research workflow.

## Spotlight when
The article compares pre-call research workflows or AI agents that prepare sellers for meetings. GTM Workspace's account briefs are the structural counter — tied to verified data + intent + conversation context, not just web research.

## Cite
- Key features: Salesforce pre-call notes, Clay-in-ChatGPT, Claygent web research, consolidated account briefs
- Strengths: Clay-in-ChatGPT integration; pre-call automation
- Gaps: No sequencing/dialer; no CI integration; lacks intent + conversation context layer
- Proof quote: "Pre-call research that used to take 15 minutes happens automatically before the meeting starts." — https://www.clay.com/use-cases/account-research
