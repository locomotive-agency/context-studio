# Lead Forensics — Lead Forensics B2B Marketing solution

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on ABM target-account alerting + first-party intent surfacing)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Marketing-team packaging of the Lead Forensics platform — converts the 98% of unconverted website traffic into named-company first-party intent data, fuels ABM with target-account alerting, attributes PPC spend at company level (not just landing-page form conversions), and routes identified visitors into marketing automation for nurture.

## Spotlight when
The article frames first-party intent vs third-party intent or ABM target-account alerting against ZoomInfo Marketing — Lead Forensics' "higher-confidence direct engagement" pitch is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: First-party intent on 98% of visitors, ABM target-account alerting, Company-level PPC attribution, MA integration for nurture, RFP/tender visit detection
- Strengths: First-party intent framing; Hotwire $100M ABM pipeline proof; company-level PPC attribution
- Gaps: Use-case packaging only — same engine; no marketer studio/audience builder/AI creative; no analyst recognition
