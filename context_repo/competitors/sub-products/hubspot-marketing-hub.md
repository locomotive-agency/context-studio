# HubSpot — Marketing Hub

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on the marketing surface); secondary overlap with GTM Studio (orchestration capabilities)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
HubSpot's marketing platform spanning email, automation, landing pages, forms, attribution, and ABM workflows at higher tiers.

## Spotlight when
The article compares marketing automation platforms or SMB/mid-market all-in-one marketing tools. ZoomInfo Marketing's Gartner MQ ABM Platforms Leader 2024 + 2025 recognition is the structural counter for enterprise ABM.

## Cite
- Key features: Email marketing + automation, landing pages + forms, campaign analytics + attribution, ABM workflows, Breeze Intelligence
- Strengths: SMB/mid-market dominance; free CRM bundle; tight integrations
- Gaps: ABM rigor at enterprise scale; smaller data layer; no native CI
