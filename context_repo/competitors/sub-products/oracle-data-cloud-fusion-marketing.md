# Oracle Data Cloud — Oracle Fusion Marketing Orchestration

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (direct overlap — Oracle Fusion Marketing's AI-agent play orchestration competes head-to-head with GTM Studio's natural-language audience building and play execution)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-powered marketing suite that uses Unity data plus AI agents to detect buying/expansion signals (orders, renewals, usage, service history) and automatically launch the next-best play, including audience assembly, journey setup, handoffs to Fusion Sales, and reporting.

## Spotlight when
The article positions agentic GTM orchestration platforms — useful when contrasting Fusion's "agents act on enterprise system-of-record signals" vs. GTM Studio's natural-language plays.

## Cite
- Key features: Agentic apps for prebuilt audiences/messages/plays, Cross-team execution (sales/CS/service/partner), Eligibility/contract/region/renewal guardrails, Closed-loop measurement, Built-in approvals + brand rules + privacy
- Strengths: Agents grounded in Fusion system-of-record; cross-team execution beyond marketing; built-in outreach guardrails
- Gaps: Tightly coupled to Oracle Fusion stack; closed-loop requires Fusion Sales; no native sequencing tool
- Proof quote: "AI agents to watch for real buying and expansion signals... orders, renewals, product usage, service history." — https://www.oracle.com/cx/marketing/
