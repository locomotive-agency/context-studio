# VisitorQueue (Leadinfo) — Contacts (Decision-Maker Data)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on B2B contact data)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Provides decision-maker contact details — LinkedIn profiles, verified emails, business phones, and (Pro tier) personal mobile numbers — at companies identified by Leadinfo's visitor ID.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B contact data inside visitor-ID platforms or covers GDPR-compliant decision-maker outreach. Lean on the bundled visitor-ID + contacts pairing as the spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: 300M+ decision-maker profiles, Verified emails + business phones, Mobile phone numbers (Pro tier), Filter by seniority/role/location, Persona filtering by job title or department
- Strengths: Bundled with visitor ID; GDPR/ISO European data; persona filtering on visiting accounts
- Gaps: Mobile gated to Pro; smaller scale than ZoomInfo; no technographic / org-chart layer
