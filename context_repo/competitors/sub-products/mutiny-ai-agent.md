# Mutiny — Mutiny AI Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (adjacent overlap on the marketer/AE asset-creation surface — Mutiny generates the customer-facing artifacts that GTM Studio's plays would orchestrate distribution of)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI agent that creates personalized, on-brand customer-facing assets (landing pages, business cases, deal rooms, meeting recaps) by ingesting a brand identity, CRM data, and call transcripts.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM-personalization or AI-agent-for-GTM tooling, especially when the angle is "asset generation" or "1:1 landing pages at scale" — Mutiny's brand-fidelity engine and credit-based agent model are its sharpest spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Brand auto-extraction (colors/fonts/logos from URL), CRM + call-transcript ingestion, Visitor analytics on assets, Account Studio list builder, Credit-based usage, Custom subdomain + password protection
- Strengths: Polished on-brand 1:1 customer-facing assets from a URL; account-level asset visibility; agent-first rather than retrofit personalization
- Gaps: No B2B contact database; no conversation intelligence; narrow surface needing a data + signals layer underneath
