# Dealfront — Dealfront Target (Prospecting Database)

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on the firmographic + contact data foundation)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Prospecting database for building company and contact lists outside of website-visitor traffic — 60M companies and 400M verified contacts with 100+ firmographic, technographic, and behavioral filters, plus email/phone/LinkedIn reveal.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's Data pillar, especially when the angle is EU-anchored coverage versus US-skewed databases.

## Cite
- Key features: Global database of 60M companies and 400M verified contacts, 100+ firmographic/technographic/behavioral filters, Decision-maker search by job role/seniority/location, Email/phone/LinkedIn profile reveal and export, EU-anchored coverage
- Strengths: Comprehensive European B2B data depth; bundles with Web Visitors + Promote; GDPR-first sourcing posture
- Gaps: Smaller absolute scale than ZoomInfo; less verification depth claim; weaker technographic intent; no conversation intelligence
