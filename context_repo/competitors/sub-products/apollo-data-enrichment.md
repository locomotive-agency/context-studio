# Apollo — Apollo Data Enrichment

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on enrichment); CRM Enrichment (direct overlap on the CRM-data-quality use case)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
CRM and form enrichment using Apollo's contact + company data. Sold as part of the all-in-one Apollo subscription.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM data quality + enrichment vendors. Apollo's bundled pricing is the cost story; ZoomInfo Operations + GTM Studio's data governance and routing are the structural counter.

## Cite
- Key features: Bulk CSV enrichment, Salesforce + HubSpot CRM enrichment, real-time form enrichment, credit-based consumption
- Strengths: Public pricing transparency; bundled with Engage and AI agents
- Gaps: Less rigorous data quality SLA; no routing/workflow orchestration vs. GTM Studio; limited enterprise governance
- Proof quote: "Enrich your CRM in seconds with Apollo's verified data." — https://www.apollo.io/solutions/b2b-data-enrichment
