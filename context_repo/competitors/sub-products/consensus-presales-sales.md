# Consensus — Presales / Sales Solution

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — demo-intent capture rather than seller-workspace substitute)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Demo automation purpose-built for presales (solutions consulting / sales engineering) and sales reps to scale demos, eliminate repetitive calls, and surface stakeholder intent.

## Spotlight when
The article compares presales / sales-engineering tooling, demo-scaling workflows, or stakeholder-multi-threading plays. Strongest spotlight when the buyer's persona is presales/SC leader and the goal is reducing live-demo load.

## Cite
- Key features: Scale presales capacity 30%+, eliminate 90% unqualified demos, stakeholder discovery + automated multi-threading, heatmaps and analytics, leaderboard + personalized links, CRM sync
- Strengths: Purpose-built for presales/SC/SE; surfaces full buying committee; hidden-influencer detection
- Gaps: Demo-intent only (no firmographic/intent/call signals); sales analytics gated to Pro; no proprietary contact data
- Proof quote: "Scale presales capacity by 30% or more without adding headcount" — https://goconsensus.com/solutions/presales-sales
