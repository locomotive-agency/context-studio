# GMass — GMass Deliverability Optimization Suite

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on email-hygiene slice only)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Bundle of free and built-in tools to test, diagnose, and improve inbox placement — Spam Solver, Inbox/Spam/Promotions tester (17 reference accounts), Email Analyzer (SPF/DKIM/blacklist), built-in verification, custom tracking domains.

## Spotlight when
The article touches on email deliverability or send-side hygiene. GMass's free tools are usable even outside its sender — useful framing as a low-stakes complement, not a CRM hygiene replacement.

## Cite
- Key features: Spam Solver, Inbox/Spam/Promotions tester, Email Analyzer (SPF/DKIM/blacklist), Email verification, Custom tracking domains
- Strengths: Free tools usable on any sender; deliverability wizard with aggregate factors; sender-reputation isolation
- Gaps: Outbound-list scope only — not CRM-wide enrichment; no technographics/firmographics; no multi-source-verified data
- Proof quote: "99% of GMass users report the best deliverability they've ever had" — https://www.gmass.co/features/deliverability-optimization
