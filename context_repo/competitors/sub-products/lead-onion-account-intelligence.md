# Lead Onion — Lead Onion Account Intelligence

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on multi-source intent surfacing for sellers and marketers)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Predict, prioritize, and engage the ~10% of accounts that are actively in-market right now using a 'research surge' analysis powered by 24 unified intent data sources, with a proprietary Research Quadrant that classifies each account into Initial / Interested / Active / In-Depth phases and routes them automatically into cadences.

## Spotlight when
The article compares multi-source intent platforms or buyer-stage classification approaches. Lead Onion's "24 unified sources + Research Quadrant" framing is the strongest spotlight angle against ZoomInfo's Bombora-powered intent.

## Cite
- Key features: Research Quadrant 4-phase classifier, Watchlist, 24 intent sources unified, 30+ firmographic attributes, Account-level signals (topics/competitors/events/tech/hiring/funding/leadership)
- Strengths: 24 unified intent sources; Research Quadrant routes accounts into cadences; churn-prevention Watchlist use case
- Gaps: No Forrester Wave/MQ recognition; Topic Intent capped at 3 across all tiers; no Context Graph fusion
- Proof quote: "The Premium Intent Platform — 24 intent data sources unified into a single platform." — https://leadonion.ai/our-data/
