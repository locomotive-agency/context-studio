# Data Axle — Data Axle Salesgenie + Business Data

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the seller-facing prospecting workflow + underlying Business Data foundation)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
SMB-tier prospecting platform (Salesgenie, from $99/mo) backed by Data Axle's Business Data foundation (90M+ business profiles, 19.5M+ premium-verified, 183M+ multichannel contacts) — with built-in outreach tools, Salesforce sync, and B2B + B2C blended personas via ProfileFuse.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Sales for SMB / cost-sensitive buyers, especially when the angle is transparent SMB pricing or Forrester Wave Q1 2026 leader status for the underlying data.
