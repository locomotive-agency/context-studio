# Contify — Contify Athena AI

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the AI-agent / research-assistant layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Proprietary agentic AI insights engine that transforms unstructured market updates into structured, verified insights and answers ad-hoc business questions grounded in Contify's curated intelligence datasets — powering Business Facts, Automated Insights, Ask Athena, and embedded dashboards.

## Spotlight when
The article compares AI agents / "research copilots" for revenue teams, or argues for grounded-in-curated-data AI vs. open-web LLM hallucination. Athena's strongest spotlight angle is the "no hallucinations grounded in curated datasets" pitch.

## Cite
- Key features: Business Facts (30+ auto-extracted), Automated Insights, Ask Athena, Real-time embeddable Dashboards
- Strengths: Scheduled agentic workflows; embeddable in CRM/KMS/ERP; MI/CI-tuned research assistant
- Gaps: No execution surface (vs GTM Workspace); no contact data underneath; no MCP server
- Proof quote: "50% improvement in competitive awareness, 30% reduction in research time, 10x faster insight generation" — https://www.contify.com/athena-ai/
