# Zoom AI Companion — My Notes (AI note-taker)

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on AI-generated meeting summaries, action items, and post-call notes — but lacks Chorus's deal-coaching, scorecards, and CRM-write-back depth)

> **Disambiguation:** Zoom AI Companion is a product of Zoom Communications, Inc. (NASDAQ: ZM) — a separate company from ZoomInfo.

**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI note-taking feature inside AI Companion that automatically captures, summarizes, and extracts action items from any virtual or in-person meeting; works across Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, and other third-party platforms with 9 languages for notes and 30+ languages for summaries.

## Spotlight when
The article compares AI note-takers / conversation intelligence tools. My Notes's cross-platform reach (works on competing meeting platforms) is its strongest spotlight angle, while the absence of coaching/scorecards is the load-bearing gap vs. Chorus.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time transcription with visible meeting indicator, AI-generated summary/key takeaways/action items, Cross-platform Zoom/Teams/Google Meet/Webex/in-person, 9 languages for My Notes (30+ for meeting summaries), Send summaries to Slack automatically, Notes stored in Zoom Hub and convert to collaborative Zoom Docs
- Strengths: Cross-platform note-taking on competing meeting platforms; in-person meeting transcription via mobile; notes convert to collaborative Zoom Docs
- Gaps: No deal-coaching scorecards or rep-performance benchmarking; no CRM auto-write-back of call insights without Custom AI Companion; no conversation-intelligence library or call review surface for managers
- Proof quote: "Cross-platform AI note-taking — works on Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, and in-person meetings" — https://www.zoom.com/en/products/ai-assistant/features/ai-note-taking/
