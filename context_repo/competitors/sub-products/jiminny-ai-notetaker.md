# Jiminny — Jiminny AI Notetaker

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on the meeting-recording / summary / action-item surface inside Chorus)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, summarizes, and pulls action items from sales calls, customer calls, huddles, 1:1s, and coaching sessions — including in-person meetings via mobile.

## Spotlight when
The article compares AI notetakers / meeting assistants vs Chorus, especially when the buyer needs in-person mobile capture, EU/US data residency, or 60+ language coverage in a notetaker-scoped surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Instant summaries + action items, speaker-distinguished transcription, Ask Jiminny across meetings, mobile in-person recording, Salesforce/HubSpot in-CRM access, 60+ languages
- Strengths: In-person mobile recording; EU/US data residency; multilingual auto-detect
- Gaps: Slimmer than full Chorus deal intelligence; no non-revenue meeting analytics; thin non-CRM integration list
