# Hunter — Hunter Sequences

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — Sequences is a narrower email-only cadence layer than GTM Workspace's full seller surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Multi-step cold email outreach platform inside Hunter — sends from the user's own Gmail / Outlook / SMTP mailboxes with personalization, A/B testing, AI Writing Assistant, and Inbox Protection.

## Spotlight when
The article compares cold-email cadence tools versus GTM Workspace's broader seller experience. Sequences' spotlight angle is "find-verify-send in one tool, sending from your own mailbox."

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-step cold email sequences from user mailboxes, Personalization and A/B testing, Inbox Protection and email account rotation, 1-20 connected mailboxes by tier, AI Writing Assistant, Open and link tracking
- Strengths: Sends from user's own mailboxes; Inbox Protection + rotation; bundled with Email Finder/Verifier
- Gaps: Recipients-per-sequence cap; no dialer; weaker CRM write-back vs enterprise cadence tools
