# Terminus (DemandScience) — Terminus Email Signature Marketing

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (none — email signature marketing has no ZoomInfo analog)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Turns every employee 1:1 email into a targeted marketing channel via dynamic, recipient-based banner ads in the email signature, synced to ABM target lists.

## Spotlight when
The article maps Terminus's full multi-channel execution and needs to call out signature marketing as an ABM channel ZoomInfo does not offer.

- Key features: Dynamic banners synced to target lists, Recipient-based banners, Shuffle creative rotation, GetApp top-rated, Sigstr lineage
- Strengths: No ZoomInfo analog; per-recipient personalization; mature Sigstr tooling
- Gaps: Narrow channel scope; no CRM intent linkage; depends on email volume
