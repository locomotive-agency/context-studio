# BookYourData — BookYourData Pay-As-You-Go Database (Prospector + Verification + Enrichment)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the verified-list-building prospecting workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Pay-as-you-go B2B contact database with self-serve list builder, 8-step real-time email verification, 60K+ technographics, and CRM auto-enrichment — pitched as the SMB/agency alternative to ZoomInfo's subscription model.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo Sales' prospecting workflow and the angle is cost-flexibility, never-expiring credits, or accuracy-guaranteed pay-per-record models for SMBs.
