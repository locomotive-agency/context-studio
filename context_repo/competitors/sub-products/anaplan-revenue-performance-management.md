# Anaplan — Revenue Performance Management

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on territory/quota/segmentation workflows over CRM data)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Solution suite for designing, measuring, and optimizing a data-driven GTM and sales strategy — GTM planning, capacity planning, territory and quota management, incentives, and sales forecasting.

## Spotlight when
Articles comparing RevOps/territory/quota planning approaches. Use RPM as the planning-side complement to ZoomInfo Operations: RPM models the plan; ZoomInfo Operations supplies the data + executes hygiene/routing on top of that plan.

## Cite
- Key features: GTM Planning, GTM Capacity Planning, Territory Planning & Management, Quota Planning & Management, Sales Incentives, Sales Forecasting, Segmentation & Scoring
- Strengths: Unifies planning + capacity + territory + quota + incentives; AI-driven segmentation and scoring; Marketing Finance/Performance modules
- Gaps: Sits on top of CRM data — no contact/intent/firmographic supply; no prospecting/outreach; ICM is a distinct lane
- Proof quote: "The only revenue performance management solution with AI-driven territory management and account segmentation and scoring" — https://www.anaplan.com/solutions/sales-revenue-performance-management/
