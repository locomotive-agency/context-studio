# SalesIntel — SalesIntel Signal360

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on the buying-intent / signals layer — fuses predictive + demand-capture signals across 30+ categories)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Tracks 30+ buying-signal categories across predictive (future intent) and demand-capture (active evaluation) signals across the customer's TAM, with 60K+ intent topics powered by Bombora and SalesIntel PredictiveIntel, PredictiveIntent AI suppression, and signal-triggered sequences in Outreach/Salesloft.

## Spotlight when
The article compares intent-signal platforms — Signal360 is the spotlight when the buyer wants both predictive and demand-capture signals fused in one tool, with PredictiveIntent AI suppression to remove false-positive matches.
