# Melissa — Data Enhancement & Appends

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — append/hygiene rather than B2B prospecting)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Enriches consumer and business records with demographic, firmographic, geographic, property, and hazard attributes; completes contact records with missing name / phone / email / postal address from Melissa's reference datasets.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to acknowledge Melissa's data-append catalog as a "data foundation" alternative for hygiene-first buyers, while contrasting with ZoomInfo's broader B2B prospecting/intent breadth. Strongest call-out is when comparing append/hygiene scopes.

## Cite
- Key features: BusinessCoder firmographics, Consumer Demographic Data, Property & Mortgage Data, Property Hazard Risk, Geocoding & IP Location, Contact Record Completion
- Strengths: Property/mortgage/hazard datasets unique vs. ZI; consumer/B2C demographics outside ZI's B2B scope; built-in geocoding
- Gaps: ~25M U.S. businesses vs. ZI's 100M global; no intent or technographic; no prospecting graph
