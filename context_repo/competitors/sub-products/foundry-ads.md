# Foundry — Foundry Ads

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — intent-targeted ad activation, not ad-tech parity)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Display, native, video, social, and OTT ad programs run against Foundry's owned editorial sites and vetted external inventory — targeted using Foundry intent and 7,600+ targeting dimensions, priced on CPC or vCPM.

## Spotlight when
The article distinguishes ABM/audience activation that runs on owned publisher inventory from networks/aggregators. Foundry Ads is the spotlight when the angle is "intent-driven advertising on owned IT-buyer publisher inventory."

## Cite
- Key features: 150M+ audience members + 300M+ records, Placements adjacent to CIO/CSO/Computerworld/InfoWorld/Network World, CPC or vCPM, Display/native/search/video/social/OTT formats, Awareness → engagement → full-funnel outcomes, Frequency capping
- Strengths: Direct ad placements on owned editorial inventory; CPC + vCPM pricing transparency; default frequency capping
- Gaps: Not ad-tech parity with ZoomInfo Marketing — adjacent; no CI/seller-workflow surface; specific rates gated
