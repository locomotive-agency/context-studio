# Acxiom — Consumer Marketing Data

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent — conceptually parallel data foundation but a wholly different consumer audience)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Third-party consumer data, segments, and audiences brands use to enrich profiles, target, and personalize across channels — InfoBase as the consumer-insights database, Personicx as the segmentation overlay.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2C vs. B2B data foundations or addresses brands evaluating consumer-data vendors alongside B2B intelligence. Spotlight as the B2C-equivalent scale point.

## Cite
- Key features: 260M US / 2.6B global consumers, 10K+ attributes, InfoBase, Personicx, audience packages, 35+ markets
- Strengths: Six-decade B2C heritage; breadth of attribute categories; seasonal audience packages
- Gaps: No B2B firmographics / contacts / dials; no technographics; no B2B intent
- Proof quote: "InfoBase is the gold standard" — https://www.acxiom.com/customer-data/
