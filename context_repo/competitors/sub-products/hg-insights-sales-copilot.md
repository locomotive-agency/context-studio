# HG Insights — HG Sales Copilot

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the seller signal-to-outreach workflow)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Seller-facing module that turns buyer signals into prioritized accounts, account research, and outreach playbooks. Activates contact reveals and pushes outreach into Salesforce, Gong, Outreach, Salesloft, and HubSpot.

## Spotlight when
The article compares signal-based seller workflows. Sales Copilot is HG's clearest direct shot at ZoomInfo Sales — the third-party "account strategy vs rep activation" framing remains the cleanest decision lens.

## Cite
- Key features: Daily prioritization, AI account briefs, Outreach/Salesloft/Gong activation, Custom playbooks
- Strengths: Bundled Contact Intelligence; technographic+IT-spend+intent prioritization; multi-cadence push
- Gaps: Contact data is enrichment, not primary; no native CI; third-party "not a replacement on contacts" framing
