# Klenty — Klenty CRM Acceleration

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on CRM sync + activity-driven automation, not full data-quality scope)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Native bidirectional CRM integration that lets reps run cadences inside Salesforce (Lightning + Classic), auto-imports lists/reports/new contacts, BCC-syncs email activity, and triggers Sales Stage Automation that starts/stops cadences as deals move opportunity stages — across Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, and Microsoft Dynamics.

## Spotlight when
The article touches CRM-resident cadence execution or Sales Stage Automation and the buyer is comparing ZoomInfo Operations' broader data-quality + routing scope against Klenty's narrower cadence-aware sync.

## Cite
- Key features: Salesforce Lightning + Classic plugin, Auto-import lists/reports, BCC sync + activity logs, Sales Stage Automation, 5-CRM bidirectional sync
- Strengths: Cadence-from-CRM Salesforce plugin; Sales Stage Automation; covers MS Dynamics + Zoho
- Gaps: Not a replacement for Operations' routing/dedupe; no marketer/RevOps audience builder; cadence-scoped only
