# GetProspect — GetProspect B2B Contact Database

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (direct overlap on volumetric verified emails, mobile phone numbers, and technographics)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Searchable B2B database of 200M contacts and 26M companies, with 230M corporate emails, 130M mobile phone numbers, 3,000 technologies, and eight ICP filters — used to build prospect lists and export up to 50K records to CSV.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data providers' contact scale and pricing — Database's strongest spotlight angle is the freemium price point combined with mobile-phone and technographic coverage that mirrors ZI's framing at SMB-friendly costs.

## Cite
- Key features: 200M B2B contacts, 26M companies, 230M corporate emails, 130M mobile phone numbers, 8 advanced filters, 3,000 technologies tracked, bulk lists/folders, 50K CSV export free, 95%+ accuracy guarantee
- Strengths: Volumetric scale at fraction of ZI's price; generous 50K CSV export free; EU-hosted, GDPR-compliant
- Gaps: ~40% of ZI's contact scale; no intent-data layer; only 8 filters; undisclosed funding/headcount
