# Mailshake — Mailshake Phone Dialer

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — adds dialer functionality to the seller cadence; ZoomInfo's seller surface includes calling but with deeper conversation-intelligence)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Built-in browser-based VOIP dialer that lets reps insert cold calls into the same multi-channel cadence as email and social tasks, with Power Dialer for auto-advancing call queues.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one outbound platforms with built-in dialing vs. multi-vendor stacks. Mailshake's dialer is a competent built-in option for SMB outbound — though it lacks the conversation intelligence, parallel dialing, or coaching of dedicated dialer platforms.

## Cite
- Key features: One-click click-to-call, Power Dialer, in-call notes + post-call tasks, mixed cadence with email/social, team-level dashboard, one phone number per user
- Strengths: Built-in VOIP (no 3rd-party dialer); Power Dialer on Sales Engagement plan; calls in same cadence as email
- Gaps: No parallel/AI-power dialing (Orum/Nooks); no call recording or transcription; no conversation intelligence
