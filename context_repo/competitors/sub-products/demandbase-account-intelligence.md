# Demandbase — Demandbase Account Intelligence

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (direct overlap at the account-level reasoning layer)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Account-level intelligence and predictive scoring — surfaces in-market accounts and journey-stage prediction.

## Spotlight when
The article is about predictive account scoring, intent reasoning, or how a "data layer" is different from a "reasoning layer." This is the right Demandbase surface to compare to GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2).

- Strengths: Account-level reasoning across intent + firmographics; ABM-funnel integration
