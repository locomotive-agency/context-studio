# Salesforce Agentforce — Agentforce Sales (vs ZoomInfo positioning)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap — explicitly positioned as the #1 ZoomInfo alternative)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Salesforce's sales-intelligence-and-prospecting variant of Agentforce, combining Sales Cloud with Data 360 and the Prospecting Agent, marketed in `/compare/zoominfo-competitors/` as a head-to-head alternative to ZoomInfo's GTM platform.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses Salesforce's published vs-ZoomInfo positioning. This is the only Agentforce variant where Salesforce explicitly takes a swing at ZoomInfo.

## Cite
- Key features: Data 360 sourcing, predictive lead scoring, Prospecting Agent, pipeline dashboards, real-time deal management
- Strengths: Explicit ZoomInfo positioning, predictive + agent + coach bundle, CRM-native context
- Gaps: Partner-data dependency (incl. ZoomInfo), simultaneous partnership/competition, Salesforce-CRM lock-in
- Proof quote: "Sales teams often choose Agentforce Sales for its AI-powered capabilities, powerful Prospecting Agent, and predictive insights." — https://www.salesforce.com/compare/zoominfo-competitors/
