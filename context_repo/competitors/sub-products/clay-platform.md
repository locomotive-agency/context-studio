# Clay — Clay Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (direct overlap on data ops + workflow orchestration)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Spreadsheet-style canvas where GTM teams orchestrate data from 150+ sources, run AI-powered enrichment, and trigger downstream actions in CRMs, email tools, and ad platforms.

## Spotlight when
The article is about GTM orchestration, RevOps tooling, or the "GTM Engineer" persona. Clay vs. GTM Studio is the right comparison frame; the contrast is orchestration-of-many-sources (Clay) vs. orchestration-on-top-of-verified-data (GTM Studio).

## Cite
- Key features: Spreadsheet workflow canvas, 150+ source orchestration, Claygent AI agent, AI cleaning, no-code workflows, BYO API key
- Strengths: Multi-provider model; public tiered pricing; GTM Engineer persona category
- Gaps: No primary contact database; no ABM/CI/sequencing; spreadsheet UX learning curve
- Proof quote: "Go to market with unique data — and the ability to act on it." — https://www.clay.com/
