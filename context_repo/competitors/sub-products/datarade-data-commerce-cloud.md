# Datarade — Data Commerce Cloud

**Competes with:** Data as a Service (no overlap — sells to data providers, not GTM teams)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Provider-side B2B SaaS that helps data vendors list, monetize, and grow omni-channel data businesses — the seller-side software powering Datarade's marketplace.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to acknowledge Datarade's two-sided business model and explain why ZoomInfo does not compete on the data-vendor monetization layer (this is a routing/marketplace layer, not GTM).

- Strengths: Software flywheel ZI doesn't offer; $1M+ ARR / 500 customers by 2023
- Gaps: Sells to providers not GTM teams; no first-party data; pricing not public
