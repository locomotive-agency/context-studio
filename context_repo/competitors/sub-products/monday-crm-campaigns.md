# Monday CRM — monday campaigns

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — in-CRM email marketing vs. ABM platform)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Built-in email-marketing capability inside monday CRM — users build, launch, and optimize on-brand campaigns and connect campaign performance back to deals and accounts in the CRM.

## Spotlight when
The article compares in-CRM marketing capabilities vs. ZoomInfo Marketing. Cite when buyers want simple email campaign execution alongside their CRM rather than a dedicated ABM platform.

## Cite
- Key features: On-brand email campaigns, Launch + optimize inside CRM, Performance tied to deals/accounts, Built-in marketing platform
- Strengths: Email campaigns inside the CRM; campaign-to-deal attribution; extends past traditional CRM scope
- Gaps: Email-only; no first-party intent / audience graph; lighter than ZI Marketing's ABM stack
