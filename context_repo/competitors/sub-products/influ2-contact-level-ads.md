# Influ2 — Influ2 Contact-Level Ads

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on ABM advertising orchestration, with Influ2 explicitly contrasting "person not account")
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Targets named individuals (synced from CRM) with cross-channel display and social ads, sends real-time alerts to sales when targeted contacts engage, and attributes ad engagement back to specific people and pipeline without form fills.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ABM advertising platforms, especially when contrasting account-level vs contact-level approaches. Contact-Level Ads' spotlight angle is "person-level targeting and form-less attribution" — the strongest argument against account-level ABM platforms.

## Cite
- Key features: Sync target contacts directly from CRM (Salesforce / HubSpot), Cross-channel ad distribution (LinkedIn / Facebook / Instagram / Google / Bing / Amazon), Real-time sales alerts on contact ad engagement, Per-contact impression / click / visit tracking, Form-less revenue attribution
- Strengths: Person-level (not account) targeting; form-less attribution; six-channel cross-network routing
- Gaps: No B2B database — relies on customer CRM; no phone/email/technographics; gated pricing
- Proof quote: "ABM that starts with people. Because people make decisions—not accounts." — https://www.influ2.com/
