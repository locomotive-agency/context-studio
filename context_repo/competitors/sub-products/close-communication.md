# Close — Communication (Power Dialer, Email, SMS)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — Close's seller front-end, but as part of a CRM-of-record not a data-rich workspace)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Native voice, email, and SMS layered into the CRM — calls, sequences, and texts run inside Close without third-party dialers or email plugins.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one sales-execution surfaces, native dialer + CRM combos, or "no integrations needed" inside-sales stacks. Strongest spotlight when the buyer wants to consolidate calling/email/SMS away from separate vendors.

## Cite
- Key features: Built-in VoIP in 200+ countries, One-Click Calling logged to lead, Power Dialer, Predictive Dialer (Scale), two-way email + AI Email Assistant, two-way SMS in Workflows, WhatsApp + unlimited recording (Scale)
- Strengths: Native VoIP/email/SMS in CRM; Predictive multi-line dialer; 200+ country coverage
- Gaps: Telephony minutes priced separately; not Outreach/Salesloft enterprise depth; unlimited recording is Scale-tier only
- Proof quote: "This CRM calls your leads for you" — https://close.com/
