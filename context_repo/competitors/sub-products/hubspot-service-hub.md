# HubSpot — Service Hub

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — CS workflow surface; complementary in most cases)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
HubSpot's customer service platform — ticketing, knowledge base, feedback surveys, SLAs.

## Spotlight when
The article is about CS workflows or expansion/retention motions. GTM Workspace + GTM Context Graph signals (intent-based expansion, churn risk detection) are the structural counter Service Hub does not offer.

## Cite
- Key features: Ticketing, knowledge base, feedback surveys, SLAs + reporting
- Strengths: Sales Hub integration; SMB/mid-market CS patterns
- Gaps: CS-focused, lacks expansion + churn-signal intelligence
