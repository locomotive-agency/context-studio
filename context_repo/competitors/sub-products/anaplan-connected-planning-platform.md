# Anaplan — Connected Planning Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (adjacent overlap on cross-functional planning + segmentation workflows)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Cloud planning platform that unites financial, strategic, and operational plans across departments with real-time scenario modeling — Anaplan's flagship surface and the foundation under every other module.

## Spotlight when
Comparing enterprise-wide planning platforms vs. ZoomInfo's GTM-execution-focused platform. Use Connected Planning to anchor the "plan vs. execute" framing — Anaplan plans on top of customer CRM data, ZoomInfo brings the data and execution.

## Cite
- Key features: AI-driven scenario planning, Cross-functional modeling, Data Management & Integrations, Planning Experience, Security & Administration, Out-of-the-box Apps
- Strengths: Connected Planning across finance/sales/supply chain/HR; enterprise-grade scale; modular OOTB apps
- Gaps: Plans on top of customer-owned CRM data — no B2B graph underneath; no intent/contact data; no GTM execution
- Proof quote: "The only AI-driven scenario planning and analysis platform designed to navigate uncertainty and decide with confidence" — https://www.anaplan.com/connected-planning/
