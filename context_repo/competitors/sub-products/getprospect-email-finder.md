# GetProspect — GetProspect Email Finder

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the classic prospecting workflow — find a verified business email from a name + company)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
LinkedIn-led B2B email finder that surfaces verified business emails by name + company domain — via a Chrome extension on LinkedIn / Sales Navigator and via the GetProspect web app — with a 95% accuracy guarantee and credit refund if lower.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo alternatives or freemium prospecting tools — Email Finder's strongest spotlight angle is GetProspect's own 84-vs-60-emails head-to-head claim and its "pay only for valid emails" model.

## Cite
- Key features: LinkedIn Chrome extension; lookup by first/last name + company domain (17 filters); bulk via domain filter or CSV (50K domains); reverse email lookup; catch-all verification; 95% accuracy guarantee; 50 free emails/month
- Strengths: Charges only for valid emails; LinkedIn Chrome extension purpose-built for SDR/recruiter; transparent free + freemium pricing vs. ZoomInfo's gated pricing
- Gaps: Bounded to LinkedIn-led email-find; no intent layer; no mobile dialer/sequencing depth of enterprise tools; no conversation intelligence
- Proof quote: "GetProspect is the best ZoomInfo alternative because it gives much greater results during the prospect search, and the info is fresher." — https://getprospect.com/zoominfo-alternative
