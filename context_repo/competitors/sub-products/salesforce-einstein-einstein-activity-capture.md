# Salesforce Einstein — Einstein Activity Capture

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on CRM activity hygiene)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Syncs Microsoft or Google email and calendar with Salesforce, auto-logging email and calendar activity to Contact, Lead, and Opportunity records — included across every Sales Cloud edition.

## Spotlight when
The article touches CRM activity hygiene or first-party signal capture; Einstein Activity Capture is the Salesforce-bundled alternative to standalone activity-capture vendors.

## Cite
- Key features: Email/event/contact auto-sync, activity-to-record linking, Starter through Agentforce 1 Sales availability
- Strengths: All-tier inclusion, native MS365 + Google Workspace sync
- Gaps: First-party-only, no enrichment/dedupe/routing, signal-only
- Proof quote: "Auto-syncs emails, events, and contacts" — https://www.salesforce.com/sales/pricing
