# Freshsales — Freshsales Sales CRM

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on seller surface; CRM-of-record posture rather than head-to-head data + workspace)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered sales CRM that helps teams capture, qualify, route, and track leads, manage pipeline, and close deals using a 360° customer view, native multichannel engagement (email, phone, chat, WhatsApp, SMS), and Freddy AI.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-of-record alternatives or "ZoomInfo + CRM" stacks. Freshsales' strongest spotlight angle is the bundled native telephony + chat + sequences inside the CRM at SMB/mid-market price points.

## Cite
- Key features: Kanban view for Contacts/Accounts/Deals, Built-in Chat/Email/Phone (cloud telephony), Contact lifecycle stages and auto profile enrichment, Sales sequences (multi-step email/call/SMS), Multiple pipelines and deal management, Workflows + auto-assignment + territory management
- Strengths: System-of-record CRM with built-in cloud telephony/chat/WhatsApp/SMS; transparent low-cost pricing; Freddy AI included
- Gaps: No B2B contact data foundation (relies on integrations like ZoomInfo Sales); no intent/technographics; no conversation intelligence
- Proof quote: "AI-powered sales CRM that helps teams organize better, sell smarter, and close deals faster" — https://www.freshworks.com/crm/sales/
