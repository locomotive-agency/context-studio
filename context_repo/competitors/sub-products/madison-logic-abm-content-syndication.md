# Madison Logic — ABM Content Syndication

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — Madison Logic distributes gated content to generate verified target-account leads via CPL; ZoomInfo does not sell content syndication)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Distributes gated content (whitepapers, reports, webinars) across Madison Logic's publisher network to generate verified, intent-qualified leads from target accounts — with 3-step proprietary lead verification and 45-minute delivery SLA.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses ABM lead-gen budgets or the build-vs-buy question on demand-gen services. ABM Content Syndication's strongest spotlight: ZoomInfo cannot match the CPL/publisher-network motion natively, so this is a "complementary stack" angle rather than a head-to-head.

## Cite
- Key features: Global publisher network, Single/Double Touch + BANT qualification, 3-step lead verification (client + 6-point + manual review), 45-min GDPR opt-in delivery, AI-validated QA, buying-group-targeted content
- Strengths: CPL pricing shape ($60–$150/lead); 3-step verification with manual review; buying-group-aligned content
- Gaps: Pure service motion (no SaaS workflow); reach gated by publisher network; ZoomInfo doesn't sell this so it's white space
