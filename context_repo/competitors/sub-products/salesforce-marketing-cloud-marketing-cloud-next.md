# Salesforce Marketing Cloud — Marketing Cloud Next (Growth & Advanced)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on multi-channel journey orchestration)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Agentic, cross-channel marketing automation platform built natively on the Salesforce platform, designed for both B2B and B2C journeys with autonomous AI agents — Growth ($1,500/mo) and Advanced ($3,250/mo) editions.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses agentic marketing automation and autonomous campaign creation. Marketing Cloud Next is Salesforce's flagship next-gen activation layer.

## Cite
- Key features: Agentforce Campaign Creation, multi-channel journeys, forms/landing pages, Path Experimentation, Unified Conversations, Distributed Marketing
- Strengths: Native Salesforce metadata, agentic NL campaign creation, two-way conversational
- Gaps: No third-party contact data, no intent feed, Salesforce dependency
