# HG Insights — HG RGI Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the unified seller + RevOps + marketer surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
SaaS GTM platform that unifies HG's market, account, technographic, IT spend, and buyer intent data with first-party data, then activates it through three AI copilots (Market Analyzer, Data Studio, Sales Copilot) and an MCP server connected to Claude, ChatGPT, and Cursor.

## Spotlight when
The article positions GTM Workspace as the unified surface for sellers, marketers, and RevOps. RGI Platform is HG's most direct head-to-head — call out the technographics-anchored framing.

## Cite
- Key features: Three AI copilots, Contact Intelligence waterfall, MCP server, Salesforce/HubSpot/Marketo/Eloqua, TrustRadius 2nd-party intent
- Strengths: Agentic infrastructure with MCP + Agent Builder; three unified copilots; native contact enrichment
- Gaps: Contact data is enrichment, not primary database; no own CI; pricing gated
