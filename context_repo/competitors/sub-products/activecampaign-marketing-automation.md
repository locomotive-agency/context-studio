# ActiveCampaign — Marketing Automation

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on goal-driven, AI-orchestrated cross-channel campaigns)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Autonomous marketing automation where users describe goals in everyday language and AI agents build, optimize, and launch cross-channel campaigns across email, SMS, WhatsApp, social, and web.

## Spotlight when
The article frames AI agents replacing manual workflow building. ActiveCampaign's "build what you need" autonomous framing is the strongest spotlight angle — particularly when contrasting goal-driven prompts (AC) vs. NL audience builders (GTM Studio).

## Cite
- Key features: AI Campaign Builder, Predictive Sending, Cross-channel orchestration, Site & event tracking, AI-Suggested Segments, AI Actions Library, Business Goals tracking
- Strengths: Autonomous goal-driven framing; AI Brand Kit; 1,000+ integrations
- Gaps: No third-party B2B intent / contact data; no ABM advertising; SMB-scoped
- Proof quote: "Cut 13 hours of marketing busywork each week with autonomous marketing" — https://www.activecampaign.com/
