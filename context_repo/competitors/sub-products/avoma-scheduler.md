# Avoma — Scheduler & Lead Router

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (adjacent overlap on lead routing and SDR-to-AE handoff)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Sales call scheduling software with instant booking links, no-show reminders, calendar sync, CRM activity logging, and lead-routing rules — bundled to replace ChiliPiper-class point solutions.

## Spotlight when
Comparing scheduler / lead-routing approaches. Avoma's bundling of scheduler + meeting + CI in one platform is its strongest angle vs. point-solution routers.
