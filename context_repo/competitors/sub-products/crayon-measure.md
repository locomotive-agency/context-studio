# Crayon — Crayon Measure

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (no direct overlap — measures CI program ROI, not seller execution)

## What it does
Reports on the ROI of the competitive-intelligence program by tying battlecard usage to pipeline and revenue outcomes — win/loss trends, Competitive Pipeline view, Seller Engagement metric, influenced-revenue reporting, and a rep-level engagement leaderboard.
