# Aviso AI — Customer Success Intelligence

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap via agent-driven post-sale workflows)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Applies the same agentic pattern to renewals/retention with a virtual CSM avatar and AI health scoring — Aviso's post-sale module.

## Spotlight when
Articles framing the CRO's full motion (acquire → expand → retain). CSI is the post-sale anchor; useful as a contrast to ZI's primarily acquisition-focused surface.
