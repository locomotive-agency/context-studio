# PitchBook — PitchBook Desktop

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap — different ICP, different data depth)

**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Flagship workflow hub for the full investment lifecycle — search/filter companies, deals, funds, and LPs with rich profiles, IRR/TVPI/DPI benchmarking, analyst research, Market Maps, and Analyst Workspaces.

## Spotlight when
The article frames private-company financial coverage or PE/VC deal sourcing — Desktop's 11.9M-company / 3M-deal scale and Morningstar parentage are the spotlight angle. Use as a contrast to ZoomInfo's GTM contact depth.

## Cite
- Key features: Search/filter across companies/deals/funds/LPs, rich profiles, capital research, IRR/TVPI/DPI benchmarking, Market Maps + Analyst Workspaces
- Strengths: 11.9M companies / 3M deals / 621k investors / 161k funds; Morningstar research authority; fund-returns benchmarking
- Gaps: Investor-first ICP; no verified contact scale; no intent/technographics/CI
