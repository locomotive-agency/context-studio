# Datarade — Datarade Marketplace

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) (adjacent overlap on B2B contact/firmographic discovery)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
B2B data marketplace where buyers discover, compare, and procure data products from 2,600+ third-party providers across 560+ categories — free for buyers, provider-funded.

## Spotlight when
The article frames the buyer-research layer for B2B contact data, alternative-vendor shopping, or third-party data procurement (where ZoomInfo competes against multi-vendor marketplaces rather than head-to-head).

## Cite
- Key features: 560+ data categories, free sample previews and pricing benchmarks, free-for-buyers / provider-paid, sourcing-advisor concierge, 240+ use-case search hubs, post-a-data-request workflow
- Strengths: Multi-vendor marketplace ZI doesn't run; buyer-free model; cross-vendor benchmarks
- Gaps: Aggregator only — no first-party data; no unified intent/reasoning; no GTM workflow
- Proof quote: "The #1 Global Data Marketplace — find the right data products, effortlessly" — https://datarade.ai/
