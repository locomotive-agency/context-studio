# Terminus (DemandScience) — Terminus Chat Experiences

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Chat (direct overlap — website-visitor chatbot tied to account graph)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Native chatbot and live chat product for converting website visitors, with ABM-aware routing that delivers different experiences to target vs. non-target accounts.

## Spotlight when
The article compares website-visitor chat tied to account-graph identification. Terminus Chat's ABM-aware routing is the strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Native chatbot + live chat, ABM-aware routing, Web visitor conversion, Intent tethering
- Strengths: Target-vs-non-target routing; intent prioritization; native to ABM platform
- Gaps: No CI/call recording; account-list graph not contact-level; no outbound seller workflows
- Proof quote: "Chatbot and ABM display ad platform that works best when tethered to intent data" — https://terminus.com/demandscience/
