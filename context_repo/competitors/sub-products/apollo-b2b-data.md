# Apollo — Apollo B2B Data

**Competes with:** Data (Pillar 1) — direct overlap; secondary overlap with ZoomInfo Sales
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Apollo's contact + company database — the data foundation under Engage and the AI Sales Platform.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B contact databases or evaluates data accuracy/coverage tradeoffs. Apollo's free tier and public pricing are the differentiator to acknowledge; ZoomInfo's verification rigor (300+ human researchers, 95%+ verified) is the structural counter-argument.

## Cite
- Key features: 275M+ contacts, verified email + mobile data, technographics + intent signals, Chrome Extension, CRM integrations
- Strengths: Free tier; public credit-based pricing; SMB market penetration
- Gaps: Verification depth vs. ZoomInfo 95%+; mobile data accuracy weaker; less rigorous EU/GDPR than Cognism Diamond
- Proof quote: "275 million B2B contacts — verified emails and direct dials, all in one platform." — https://www.apollo.io/b2b-data
