# Sprout Social — Sprout Social Engagement

**Competes with:** Chorus (none — public social conversations vs. sales-call conversation intelligence)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Unified social inbox that consolidates messages, comments, and reviews across networks with AI-driven response assistance, case management, and a Salesforce Service Cloud integration.

## Spotlight when
The article needs to delineate Chorus (sales-call CI) from social-care inboxes — Sprout Engagement is the canonical example of the latter. Useful to clarify "conversation" boundaries.
