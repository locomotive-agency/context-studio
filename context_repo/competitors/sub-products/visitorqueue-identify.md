# VisitorQueue (Leadinfo) — Identify (Website Visitor Identification)

**Competes with:** WebSights (direct overlap on IP-based visitor identification)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Reveals the company behind anonymous website visits using IP-based identification, returning company name, viewed pages, visit details, and ML-driven ICP scoring in real time.

## Spotlight when
The article focuses on website visitor identification, anonymous-traffic conversion, or European GDPR-compliant visitor-ID alternatives. Lean on Audience Match (ML against actually-visited companies) and the GDPR/ISO positioning as the spotlight angles.

## Cite
- Key features: Real-time IP-based visitor identification, 220M+ companies / 45 datapoints across 195 countries, Bot detection + AI traffic filtering, Audience Match ML ICP recognition, Automated lead scoring on session/page behavior, Custom segments for pricing/quote-page hits
- Strengths: European-first GDPR/ISO 27001 positioning; Audience Match ML; "Fair Pricing" deduping repeat/ISP traffic
- Gaps: No third-party intent network; no intent topic library; smaller addressable scale than ZoomInfo
