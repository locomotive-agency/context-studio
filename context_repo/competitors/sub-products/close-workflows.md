# Close — Sales Automation / Workflows

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — sequencing surface inside the seller workspace)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Multi-channel automated sequences and triggered workflows for follow-up, qualification, task creation, and lead routing.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRM-native sequencing, SMB outbound automation, or "Outreach alternative bundled with CRM." Strongest spotlight when buyer wants email + SMS + tasks in one templated sequence inside the CRM.

## Cite
- Key features: Multi-channel Workflows (email + SMS + tasks) with templates and triggers, automated follow-ups, task creation, lead routing, AI Email Assistant on Growth/Scale
- Strengths: Multi-channel sequences in the CRM; AI Email Assistant on Growth+; task + routing automation
- Gaps: Not on Solo plan; lags Outreach/Salesloft at enterprise scale; no advanced branching/experimentation
