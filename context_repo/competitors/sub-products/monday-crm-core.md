# Monday CRM — monday CRM (core platform)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — system of record + seller workflow rather than data-driven prospecting)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-first, no-code CRM on the monday.com Work OS that lets revenue teams manage the full sales cycle (leads, contacts, accounts, deals) and post-sale workflows on a customizable visual platform — with two-way email sync, sequences, quotes & invoices, and 500+ integrations.

## Spotlight when
The article compares CRMs of record — especially flexible, no-code, SMB/mid-market alternatives to Salesforce/HubSpot. monday CRM is the flagship spotlight when buyers want a CRM that adapts to their workflow.

## Cite
- Key features: Visual pipeline, Two-way email + AI composer, Sequences, Sales analytics, Code-free automations, Sales forecasting, Mobile CRM, 500+ integrations
- Strengths: No-code workflow flexibility; pre-sale + post-sale on one platform; Work OS extension
- Gaps: Reliant on third-party data vendors; no Context Graph; lighter forecasting depth
- Proof quote: "monday CRM adapts to your workflow, not the other way around" — https://monday.com/crm
