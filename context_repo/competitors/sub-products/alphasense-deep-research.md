# AlphaSense — Deep Research

**Competes with:** GTM Context Graph (adjacent overlap on autonomous AI research orchestration)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Autonomous AI agent that functions like a team of analysts to generate investment-grade briefings, running 50–100 searches per task and synthesizing cited insights across hundreds of sources in 5–30 minutes.

## Spotlight when
Comparing autonomous AI agent depth on the research/reasoning side vs. ZoomInfo's GTM-execution-oriented agents. Use Deep Research to anchor the analyst-deliverable end of the spectrum.

## Cite
- Key features: Autonomous multi-step research runs (50–100 searches per run), Investment-grade briefings, 5–30 min reports, Comprehensive cited reports
- Strengths: Autonomous analyst-grade briefings; multi-step research orchestration; citation-backed long-form output
- Gaps: Targets investment/strategy decisions not GTM; no CRM/seller workflow connection; sources are documents not contact/intent data
