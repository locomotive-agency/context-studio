# Contify — Contify Marketing Intelligence

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on competitive signal layer for marketing teams)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Marketing-team-targeted use of the M&CI platform — monitoring competitor positioning, marketing collateral, event participation, executive thought-leadership, customer reviews, and awards across 1 Mn+ public-web sources.

## Spotlight when
The article positions ZoomInfo Marketing's intent layer against public-web competitive monitoring tools, or argues for global / multilingual brand-tracking. Contify's spotlight angle is event/conference monitoring marketing tools usually miss.

## Cite
- Key features: Marketing collateral tracking, Positioning monitoring, Event/conference tracking, Thought-leadership tracking, Reviews/awards monitoring
- Strengths: 1 Mn+ public-web sources; multilingual coverage; event/conference tracking
- Gaps: No intent or first-party visitor signals; no campaign orchestration / ABM ads; no outbound activation layer
