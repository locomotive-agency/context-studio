# Metadata — Marketing Automation

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on paid-media campaign automation)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Auto-launches and adjusts paid campaigns across LinkedIn, Meta, Reddit, Google, and Bing using real performance signals — pairing bidirectional CRM-synced audiences/goals with automatic budget reallocation and underperformer pausing.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B marketing automation / paid-channel orchestration. This use case is the closest analog to ZoomInfo Marketing's automation surface for paid execution.

- Key features: Build once deploy everywhere, Group campaigns by objective, Bidirectional CRM sync, Auto-pause underperformers
- Strengths: One-click deploy across all paid channels; performance-signal optimizer; CRM-synced audiences
- Gaps: Paid-media scope only; not a general MAP; no nurture/email automation
