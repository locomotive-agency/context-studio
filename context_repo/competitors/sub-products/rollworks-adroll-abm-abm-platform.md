# RollWorks (AdRoll ABM) — AdRoll ABM (formerly RollWorks)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on ABM workflows + audience activation)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Account-based marketing and advertising platform that fuses an 18M-company B2B database, ICP/Journey-Stage modeling, and Bombora/G2 intent with an in-house cross-channel DSP for display, native, video, mobile, social, and connected-TV ads against target accounts.

## Spotlight when
The article compares ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM workflows against advertising-first ABM platforms. RollWorks' "ABM intelligence + native DSP in one app" angle is the strongest differentiator vs ZoomInfo Marketing's audience-only stance.

## Cite
- Key features: 18M-company B2B database, 2.6B digital identities, 92M contacts, Command Center, Journey Stages, InIQ/BidIQ AI engines, cross-channel display/native/video/social/CTV
- Strengths: In-house DSP, walled-garden + open-web orchestration, predictive intent targeting via Bombora + G2
- Gaps: Smaller contact graph than ZoomInfo, no CI / no seller surface, gated pricing, rebrand confusion
- Proof quote: "Advertising is your largest budget item, and we know ads better than anyone." — https://www.adroll.com/account-based-marketing-platform
