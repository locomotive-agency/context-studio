# Clari Copilot — Clari Copilot (Conversation Intelligence & Coaching)

**Competes with:** Chorus (direct overlap on the conversation intelligence + coaching surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI-powered conversation intelligence platform that records, transcribes, and analyzes every sales call, then feeds buyer signals into Clari's revenue process for forecasting, coaching, and CRM auto-capture. Layers live battlecards on top of standard CI mechanics.

## Spotlight when
The article compares conversation intelligence platforms, Chorus alternatives, or affordable CI for SMB/mid-market sales teams. Strongest spotlight when the buyer cares about real-time in-call coaching or affordable CI bundled into a forecasting platform.
