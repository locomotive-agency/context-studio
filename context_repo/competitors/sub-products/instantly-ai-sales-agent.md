# Instantly — Instantly AI Sales Agent

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap — autonomous AI agent that prospects, writes outreach, follows up, and books meetings)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Autonomous AI agent that finds ICP leads, writes personalised messages, follows up, and books meetings after a 30-second URL-based setup — reads the customer's website to derive product, ICP, and differentiators.

## Spotlight when
The article covers AI sales agents and the autonomous-prospecting category (11x, Artisan, Regie, Relevance AI). Instantly's URL-based setup and per-prospect personalisation are the strongest differentiators.

## Cite
- Key features: 30-second URL setup, website-reading ICP derivation, autonomous lead finding, lead enrichment with buying signals, per-prospect personalisation, automated follow-up, calendar booking
- Strengths: 30-second URL setup; self-configuring; per-prospect (not template) personalisation
- Gaps: Cold-email-only scope; no MCP/agent ecosystem; limited data sources
