# NeverBounce — Email List Cleaner

**Competes with:** None — NeverBounce is a wholly-owned ZoomInfo product line (acquired 2018). This sub-product is documented for ownership context only.
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Bulk-cleans uploaded CSV email lists, returning each address tagged as valid, invalid, risky, or duplicate; removes hard bounces, spam traps, and malformed addresses with up to 99.9% accuracy.

## Spotlight when
Articles about email verification or list hygiene where NeverBounce's ownership inside ZoomInfo is the angle — useful for "ZoomInfo Operations + NeverBounce stack" framing rather than as a vs-target.

## Cite
- Key features: Bulk verification up to 10K emails in 3-10 minutes, Hard-bounce/spam-trap/malformed removal, 99.9% accuracy claim, PAYG credits, SMTP + MX test, Typo auto-fix
- Strengths: ZoomInfo-owned with 20% cross-sell promo; GTM Guard AI-agent framing; bulk throughput at scale
- Gaps: ZoomInfo-owned (not a competitive substitute); single-purpose verification; credit-expiration inconsistency
- Proof quote: "Improve your email deliverability and protect your sender reputation by removing invalid emails, hard bounces, and spam traps before you hit send." — https://www.neverbounce.com/
