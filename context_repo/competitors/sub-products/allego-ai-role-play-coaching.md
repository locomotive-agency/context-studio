# Allego — AI Role Play & Coaching

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent — overlaps coaching surfaces but practice/role-play is materially deeper than Chorus ships)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Sales coaching software for unscripted practice simulations and AI-driven scorecards — covers messaging rollouts, objections, interviews, and hot-seat drills in 32 languages with scoring in 59.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses AI role-play / pre-call practice, especially when comparing to Chorus's post-call coaching analytics. Strongest spotlight is multilingual unscripted role-play depth.
