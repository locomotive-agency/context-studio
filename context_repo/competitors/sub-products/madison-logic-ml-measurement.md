# Madison Logic — ML Measurement

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap on attribution / pipeline reporting; Madison Logic's measurement is media-attribution-led while ZoomInfo's is GTM-intelligence-led)
**Spotlight rank:** 6

## What it does
Analytics and attribution layer that ties ABM campaign activity (display, CTV, audio, LinkedIn, syndication) to pipeline influence and revenue impact, with CRM-integrated reporting in Salesforce and HubSpot.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses ABM ROI or proving marketing pipeline contribution. ML Measurement's strongest spotlight: closed-loop attribution across the same channels Madison Logic activates — fewer integration gaps than stitching Marketo Measure to outside DSPs.

## Cite
- Key features: Cross-channel pipeline attribution, revenue/pipeline-influence reporting, CRM-integrated (Salesforce/HubSpot), real-time campaign visibility
- Strengths: Built for ABM-channel attribution; closed-loop with ML Activation; bundled into enterprise contracts
- Gaps: Madison-Logic-channel-centric (limited non-ML channel view); often bundled (low pricing visibility); overlaps Marketo Measure / Bizible / 6sense
