# Anaplan — Territory Planning & Management

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (adjacent overlap on territory design over CRM data)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Sales-ops solution for designing, deploying, managing, and adapting territories aligned to GTM strategy and market opportunity, with AI + geospatial optimization.

## Spotlight when
Comparing how vendors handle territory carving, account balancing, and quota assignment. Anaplan's geospatial + scenario capability is its strongest territory-planning angle.
