# Braze — Braze Cross-Channel Messaging

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent — multi-channel reach; ICP mismatch B2C vs B2B)

**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Native cross-channel orchestration across email, mobile push, in-app, web, SMS/RCS, WhatsApp, LINE, KakaoTalk, and audience sync to ad platforms.

## Spotlight when
The article frames multi-channel reach. Braze's regional-consumer-messenger coverage (LINE / KakaoTalk / WhatsApp) is the contrast point against B2B GTM channels.
