# GTM Buddy — GTM Buddy CMS

**Competes with:** GTM Studio (adjacent overlap on content/play orchestration touchpoints)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
AI-powered sales content management that auto-tags assets and delivers them contextually inside CRM/email/calendar instead of forcing reps to search a portal. Governance Copilot detects duplicates, policy violations, and version drift.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses sales-content governance or measurement of content ROI. The influenced-revenue framing is unique vs. download-count CMSes.
