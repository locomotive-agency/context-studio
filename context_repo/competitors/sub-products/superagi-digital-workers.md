# SuperAGI — SuperAGI Digital Workers

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on AI-agent-driven outbound + a managed Humanoid SDR layer that ZoomInfo does not offer)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Humanoid SDR (Humex) — managed Sales Development service that pairs trained human SDRs with SuperAGI's AI-native GTM stack — pitched at "up to 4x higher productivity" and "10x more cost-effective" vs. traditional in-house SDR teams.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights buyers evaluating in-house SDR vs outsourced AI-augmented SDR services. SuperAGI Digital Workers is the spotlight when the buyer wants the Humex managed-service tier on top of SuperSales — a wedge ZoomInfo does not address with a managed-service motion.
