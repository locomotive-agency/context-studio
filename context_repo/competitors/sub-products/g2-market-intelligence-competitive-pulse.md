# G2 Market Intelligence — G2 Competitive Pulse

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on intent/ABM workflows that combine CRM data with buyer intent to flag at-risk and in-market accounts)

## What it does
Unified view announced March 2026 that combines a customer's CRM opportunity data, G2 Buyer Intent signals, and competitive research behavior to flag at-risk deals and surface Churn Threats when existing customers engage with competitors on G2.
