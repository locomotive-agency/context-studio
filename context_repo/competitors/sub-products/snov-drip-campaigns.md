# Snov.io — Drip Campaigns

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller-execution / multichannel-sequencing surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Automated multi-step email and LinkedIn sequences with personalization, A/B testing, AI persona-driven email building, mailbox rotation, Calendly booking detection, and a shared Unibox for replies.

## Spotlight when
Articles compare ZoomInfo's GTM Workspace seller surface to all-in-one outreach platforms or position email + LinkedIn multichannel as the SMB / agency alternative to enterprise GTM stacks.

## Cite
- Key features: Email + LinkedIn multichannel; unlimited follow-ups / senders / campaigns; AI Studio Persona-driven email; A/B testing + spintax; Unibox + reply sentiment + mailbox rotation; Calendly detection
- Strengths: Multichannel email + LinkedIn in one sequence; persona-driven AI email; unlimited follow-ups
- Gaps: No dialer / voice; no GTM Context Graph reasoning; LinkedIn automation as paid add-on
