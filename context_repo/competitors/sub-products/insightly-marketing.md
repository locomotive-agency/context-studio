# Insightly — Insightly Marketing

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (adjacent overlap — marketing automation layer enriched by ZoomInfo data, not an ABM/intent substitute)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Marketing automation for email, journeys, landing pages, and prospect scoring built on the same platform as the CRM — drag-and-drop templates, dynamic segmentation, A/B testing, and multi-touch attribution.

## Spotlight when
The article covers marketing automation for SMB / mid-market buyers and the relationship between ZoomInfo's data + intent foundation and the marketing-execution tools downstream of it.

## Cite
- Key features: Email automation, landing pages, journey builder, dynamic segmentation, lead scoring, A/B testing, attribution dashboards
- Strengths: Native CRM sync; account-based pricing; bundled all-in-one suite
- Gaps: No third-party intent or audience graph; no ABM advertising; no conversation intelligence
