# LeanData — LeanData BookIt

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller-execution / meeting-booking surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Salesforce-native intelligent scheduling suite that connects routing logic directly to live calendars so prospects book the right rep instantly from forms, handoffs, or shared links — with abandoned-calendar recovery and APIs for AI SDR agents.

## Spotlight when
The article compares speed-to-lead, AI SDR / Agentforce orchestration, or scheduling-as-part-of-GTM-orchestration stories. The BookIt API → Agentforce / 1mind / Qualified angle is the strongest spotlight — it positions BookIt as the deterministic meeting layer for agentic workflows.

## Cite
- Key features: BookIt for Forms, BookIt Handoff (Meet Now from Salesforce), BookIt Links, 95%+ matching accuracy, live SF-data lookups at booking, abandoned-calendar recovery, BookIt APIs for AI SDR agents
- Strengths: Scheduling tied to FlowBuilder routing logic; AI-agent APIs; live SF lookups not stale snapshots
- Gaps: No outreach drafting / sequencing; no conversation intelligence; paid add-on on top of routing license
- Proof quote: "Lattice — 50% faster speed-to-lead after switching from Chili Piper to BookIt" — https://www.leandata.com/platform/scheduling/
