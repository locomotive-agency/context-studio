# Persana AI — Persana AI Platform

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller workflow surface — composable workflows + native sequencer + signal-triggered plays)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
All-in-one AI sales prospecting workspace that unifies 100+ data sources, AI-powered enrichment, 75+ buyer signals, and CRM automation in a single subscription, with a native AI Sequencer for multi-channel outreach.

## Spotlight when
The article compares all-in-one AI prospecting platforms with bundled data + signals + sequencing. Persana's platform is the spotlight when the buyer wants a single workspace that aggregates data providers and runs signal-triggered plays end-to-end (with the platform-sunset caveat noted).
