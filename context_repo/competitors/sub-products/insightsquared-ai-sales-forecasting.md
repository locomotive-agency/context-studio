# InsightSquared — AI Sales Forecasting

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — ML-validated forecasting alongside ZoomInfo's GTM Context Graph + CRM forecasting surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Automates and validates forecast submissions with machine learning to predict close confidence and ideal-customer-profile fit on every deal — replaces Excel rollups with Confidence to Close and ICP scores.

## Spotlight when
The article covers AI-powered sales forecasting and the difference between forecasting from customer first-party CRM data only (InsightSquared) vs. forecasting that incorporates a third-party context graph (ZoomInfo).

## Cite
- Key features: Automated forecast submission, Confidence to Close ML score, ICP model on every deal, multi-dimension slicing, Forecast Summary, override tracking
- Strengths: ML models on every deal; multi-dimension slicing; override history
- Gaps: First-party CRM data only; no API/MCP; cold-start lag
- Proof quote: "Confidence to Close machine learning score on every deal" — https://www.insightsquared.com/revenue-intelligence-platform/sales-forecasting/
