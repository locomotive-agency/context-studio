# Valley AI — Valley AI LinkedIn Outbound

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller-execution surface — LinkedIn-only channel-narrow alternative to GTM Workspace's multi-channel scope)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
AI LinkedIn outbound engine — captures warm signals (profile views, post engagers, company-page visitors, competitor followers), enriches and scores leads against ICP, deep-researches across 60+ data points, drafts messages in the user's voice, and sends connection requests / InMails inside LinkedIn safety limits.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights LinkedIn-only AI SDR / warm-outbound platforms. Valley is the spotlight when the buyer is a founder / lean sales team / GTM agency that wants signal-based LinkedIn outreach with account-safety guarantees.
