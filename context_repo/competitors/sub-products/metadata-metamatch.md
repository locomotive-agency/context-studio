# Metadata — Metadata Audiences (MetaMatch)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on audience-building and intent-driven activation across paid channels)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Patented personal-to-corporate identity graph that converts personal emails into B2B-targetable audiences across Facebook, Google, Instagram, and LinkedIn — built from first-party CRM data, intent (6sense, G2, Bombora, Qualified), and demographic / firmographic / technographic signals.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B audience-targeting / identity-graph capabilities — MetaMatch is the unique angle Metadata leads with vs. ZoomInfo Marketing's audience-building.

## Cite
- Key features: Patented personal-to-corporate identity graph, 1.5B email match, audience activation on FB/Google/IG/LI, multi-source audience building, audience preview
- Strengths: Patented identity graph — only platform bringing LinkedIn-grade B2B targeting to Meta and Google; free trial with 3 custom audiences; preview before launch
- Gaps: Ad-channel scope, not sales prospecting; no CRM enrichment/routing; no seller surface
- Proof quote: "MetaMatch matches 1.5B emails to ~800M B2B profiles to bring LinkedIn-grade B2B targeting to non-LinkedIn channels" — https://metadata.io/platform/audience-targeting
