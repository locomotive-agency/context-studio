# Surfe — Surfe Chrome Extension

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the seller's day-to-day prospecting surface)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Chrome extension that brings the user's CRM into LinkedIn (and other business socials) so reps can add contacts, enrich data, log activity, and trigger sequences without leaving the social timeline.

## Spotlight when
The article frames seller workflow inside LinkedIn — Surfe's strongest spotlight angle is the LinkedIn-native CRM sync where it claims ZoomInfo lacks direct LinkedIn-to-CRM syncing.

## Cite
- Key features: 1-click LinkedIn-to-CRM, Auto Waterfall Enrichment, Bulk Sales Nav export, Job change alerts, In-extension cadences/dialer, 30-second setup
- Strengths: LinkedIn-native CRM sync; Job Change Alerts; cadence enrollment without leaving LinkedIn
- Gaps: No AI Copilot context graph; less mature dialer; no CI layer
- Proof quote: "ZoomInfo does not currently support direct LinkedIn-to-CRM syncing" — https://www.surfe.com/blog/surfe-vs-zoominfo/
