# DemandScience — DemandScience IONIC Identity Graph + Intent Data

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on the buyer-intent + identity-graph layer, with complementary "Better Together" partnership framing rather than displacement)

247M-contact IONIC Identity Graph + multi-source buyer intent data + HG-Insights-powered Winnable Account System that scores accounts for "switchability" (not just topic intent) and feeds DemandScience-managed activation programs.

## Spotlight when
The article spotlights ZoomInfo's Intent Data product, especially when the angle is intent-data complementarity, "switchability" scoring beyond topic intent, or the patented Content-IQ AI visibility wedge.
