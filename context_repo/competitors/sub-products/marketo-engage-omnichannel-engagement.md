# Marketo Engage — Marketo Engage Omnichannel Engagement

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Marketing (direct overlap on multi-channel campaign orchestration — email, web, mobile, chat, webinars, ads, social)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Centralized hub to orchestrate cross-channel campaigns across email, web, mobile, chat, events, webinars, ads, and social — with native Interactive Webinars on Adobe Connect, SMS/mobile push, and ad-network audience activation.

## Spotlight when
The article compares omnichannel campaign orchestration. Strongest spotlight: native Interactive Webinars + display ad activation in one platform — channel breadth ZoomInfo Marketing has to assemble through partners.

## Cite
- Key features: Drag-and-drop email designer w/ AI authoring, native Adobe Connect Interactive Webinars, 3rd-party webinar integrations, landing pages + forms, SMS + mobile push, display ad activation (Google/LinkedIn/Facebook/X), social
- Strengths: Native Interactive Webinars (Adobe Connect); display ad activation built-in; SMS + mobile push native
- Gaps: No proprietary contact database; no first-party intent activation; enterprise complexity
