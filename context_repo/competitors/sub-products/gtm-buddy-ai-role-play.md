# GTM Buddy — GTM Buddy AI Role Play

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap on the coaching/simulation slice)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
AI sales simulator that pressure-tests rep judgment across 200+ scenarios spanning sales stage, stakeholder, scenario type, channel, and emotional state. Live call scoring identifies the one slip that risked the deal.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses rep coaching or training. GTM Buddy's "one slip" call-scoring + simulator is the spotlight angle vs. Chorus's transcript-led coaching.

## Cite
- Key features: 200+ scenarios, Stakeholder simulations, Multi-channel coverage, Live-call scoring, Targeted simulation assignment
- Strengths: "One slip" coaching narrative; pre-built scenario library; closed-loop simulation/coaching architecture
- Gaps: Simulation only — no own call recording; thinner real-call library than Chorus; no buyer engagement layer
- Proof quote: "Live call scoring surfaces the one moment that moved the deal" — https://gtmbuddy.ai/product/ai-role-plays
