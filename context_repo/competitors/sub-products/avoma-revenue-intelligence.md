# Avoma — Revenue Intelligence

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (direct overlap on the deal/forecast/risk surface for sellers and managers)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
AI-driven pipeline review, deal risk alerts, sales methodology tracking, forecasting, and win-loss analysis — Avoma's seller-deal-side add-on layered on top of the meeting platform.

## Spotlight when
Comparing forecast/deal-risk tools or methodology adherence trackers. Avoma's MEDDIC/SPICED/BANT/Sandler/custom tracker is its strongest spotlight angle.

## Cite
- Key features: Dealboard with 2-way CRM updates, AI Deal/Churn Risk Alerts, AI Sales Methodology Tracker, Roll-up forecasting + deal health, AI Win-Loss Analysis, Pipeline/forecasting reports, Revenue "Ask Avoma" Copilot
- Strengths: Built-in methodology adherence scoring; conversation-sentiment-driven risk; public $29/seat add-on pricing
- Gaps: Forecast lives in Avoma not CRM-of-record; first-party signals only (no third-party intent); requires Meeting Assistant
