# Jiminny — Jiminny Revenue Intelligence

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on the seller / manager pipeline-execution surface; first-party-data scoped vs ZoomInfo's B2B-graph-backed view)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Pipeline visibility, forecasting, and deal-risk software that fuses conversation evidence with CRM activity for reps, managers, and execs.

## Spotlight when
The article compares pipeline-management and forecasting tooling for mid-market revenue orgs that already own a CI tool, or where conversation-grounded forecasts are the differentiator vs CRM-only forecasting in GTM Workspace.

## Cite
- Key features: Deal intelligence with stage progression, Ask Jiminny pipeline Q&A, native Salesforce/HubSpot sync, at-risk deal alerts, conversation-driven forecasting
- Strengths: Forecasts grounded in actual call evidence; natural-language pipeline Q&A; runs on first-party data
- Gaps: No B2B graph underneath; mid-market focus; thin sales-engagement integration list
