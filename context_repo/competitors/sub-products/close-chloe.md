# Close — Chloe (AI sales agent)

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap — AI agent layer on the seller front-end for SMB inside-sales)
**Spotlight rank:** 3

## What it does
Embedded AI sales agent inside Close that calls inbound leads in seconds, runs structured discovery, books meetings, and logs the entire interaction back into the CRM.

## Spotlight when
The article compares AI sales agents inside CRMs, inbound auto-call qualification, or AI-driven cold-lead re-engagement. Strongest spotlight when the buyer wants the AI agent embedded in the CRM (not bolted on).

## Cite
- Key features: Auto-calls inbound leads in seconds, books meetings or routes with context, transcript-driven follow-ups, AI summaries, cold-lead re-engagement, contact enrichment from LinkedIn/web
- Strengths: Embedded in CRM (no sync); free during Beta on Growth/Scale (telephony only); auto cold-lead re-engagement
- Gaps: Beta product; per-lead enrichment only (no data asset); inbound + cold re-engagement scope
- Proof quote: "This CRM calls your leads for you" — https://close.com/chloe
