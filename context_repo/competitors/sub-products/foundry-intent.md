# Foundry — Foundry Intent

**Competes with:** Intent Data (direct overlap on multi-source B2B intent for activation)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Multi-source B2B intent data product capturing buying signals from a customer's website, the public web, Foundry's owned editorial network, and content syndication — then matching signals to buyers and buying committees for personalized activation.

## Spotlight when
The article compares intent-data providers feeding ABM/marketing platforms. Foundry Intent is the spotlight when the angle is "intent fed by an owned editorial publisher network" — distinct from co-op/network-only providers.

## Cite
- Key features: Intent across customer website + public web + editorial + syndication, 80M+ signals/week, 150M+ audience members, 7,600+ targeting dimensions, Buying-committee matching, Salesforce/HubSpot/Dynamics/SugarCRM/Google/Adobe integrations
- Strengths: Multi-source fusion across first-party + public + editorial + syndication; owned editorial audience feeding signals; G2 leader for Buyer Intent
- Gaps: No GTM Context Graph reasoning; no standalone APIs/MCP; gated pricing
