# Fireflies — Fireflies Chrome Extension

**Competes with:** Chorus (adjacent overlap — bot-free capture lane, complementary to platform CI)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
Browser extension that records, transcribes, and summarizes Google Meet calls without a bot joining the meeting — also captures and summarizes Loom and Soapbox async videos.

## Spotlight when
The article highlights bot-free capture or async video transcription. Chrome Extension is the spotlight when the angle is "no visible bot in the meeting + Loom/Soapbox coverage."

## Cite
- Key features: Botless Google Meet capture, Live transcription, Auto-speaker ID, Auto-language detection, AskFred for Web, Loom + Soapbox video capture/summarization
- Strengths: Botless capture; async Loom/Soapbox summarization; AskFred for Web
- Gaps: Google Meet-only for live botless capture; no team analytics surface; no CRM integration in the extension itself
- Proof quote: "Botless capture of Google Meet sessions" — https://fireflies.ai/product/chrome-extension
