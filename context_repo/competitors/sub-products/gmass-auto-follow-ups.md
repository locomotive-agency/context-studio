# GMass — GMass Auto Follow-ups & Sequences

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent overlap on cadence/sequencing slice)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Sends automated follow-up emails to non-replies, threaded under the original send, until a configurable stop criterion (reply, click, etc.) is met. Domain-level stops end the sequence for everyone at a company once one person replies.

## Spotlight when
The article addresses lightweight Gmail-based cadence vs. enterprise sales-engagement (Outreach / Salesloft / GTM Workspace). GMass is the cheap-and-cheerful Gmail option — call out the threading and domain-stop UX.

## Cite
- Key features: Unlimited follow-up stages, Threaded follow-ups, Test sends, Engagement-driven follow-ups, Domain-level reply stops
- Strengths: Native Gmail threading; domain-level stops; calendar view
- Gaps: Lags Outreach/Salesloft on enterprise scale; no multi-channel cadence; no CRM step-state write-back
