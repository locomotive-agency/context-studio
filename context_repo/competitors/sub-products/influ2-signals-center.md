# Influ2 — Influ2 Signals Center

**Competes with:** GTM Workspace (adjacent — Signals Center is a narrow seller surface scoped to Influ2 ad + intent signals, not a full GTM Workspace substitute)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Sales-facing live feed that consolidates Influ2's contact-level signals, CRM context, and recommended next steps so AEs and SDRs can craft outreach without tool-hopping.

## Spotlight when
The article compares seller signal feeds / "smart inbox" surfaces. Signals Center's spotlight angle is "ad + intent signals delivered into the seller's existing tools," contrasted with GTM Workspace's broader unified surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Live feed of recent buyer signals across the buying group, In-app context and "story" behind each signal, CRM company/account background pulled into one view, Automatic notifications delivered where the seller already works
- Strengths: Seller-facing surface for ad + intent signals; "story" framing per signal; notification routing
- Gaps: Influ2-pipeline only — not unified across data + conversations + CRM; thin page; no CI / dialer / data layer
- Proof quote: "Live feed of recent buyer signals across the buying group" — https://www.influ2.com/product/signals-center
