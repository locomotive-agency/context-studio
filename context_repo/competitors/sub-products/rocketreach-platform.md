# RocketReach — RocketReach Platform

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Sales (direct overlap on the verified-contact data foundation + bundled outreach loop, with public self-serve pricing as the wedge)
**Spotlight rank:** 1

## What it does
Self-serve "lead intelligence" platform — verified B2B emails, mobile/direct phones, and firmographics across 700M+ profiles — bundled with native outreach (Messages, Sequences, AI Email Writer), Autopilot list refresh, Intent Data, and Chrome extension.

## Spotlight when
The article compares B2B data tools with bundled outreach for SMB / mid-market sellers on transparent pricing. RocketReach is the spotlight when the buyer wants public $25-$128/seat pricing plus 700M+ contacts and native sequencing.

- Key features: 700M+ verified professional profiles plus 60M companies and 989M emails, Real-time email prediction + verification on every lookup, Native Messages + Sequences + AI Email Writer for in-platform outreach, B2B Intent Data via Intentsify (35K+ topics weekly refresh) bundled at $175/mo, Chrome extension with 300K+ Store users for inline LinkedIn/company-site lookup, Salesforce/HubSpot/Outreach/Salesloft/Bullhorn integrations
- Strengths: Public self-serve pricing from $25/mo; bundled outreach at standard pricing; niche depth in healthcare/legal/founders + Bullhorn ATS
- Gaps: No GTM Context Graph reasoning; no Chorus-equivalent CI; no GTM Studio orchestration
