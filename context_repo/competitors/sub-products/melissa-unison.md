# Melissa — Unison (Customer Data Validation Platform)

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on continuous CRM/customer-data validation)
**Spotlight rank:** 5

## What it does
No-code customer data validation platform that continuously cleanses, validates, and maintains customer records across systems via reusable DQ workflows.

## Spotlight when
The article needs a "modern continuous DQ" angle alongside the older Data Quality Suite — Unison is the natural call-out for buyers wanting an always-on, no-code DQ surface vs. ZoomInfo Operations' enrichment/routing/governance combo.

## Cite
- Key features: No-code DQ workflows, continuous customer-data validation, Customer Data Management, cross-system maintenance
- Strengths: No-programming workflow builder; continuous-DQ posture; single platform for customer data validation
- Gaps: Newer/lighter product depth; no B2B contact graph; no seller execution layer
