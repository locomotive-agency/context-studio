# Similarweb — Similarweb Web Intelligence

**Competes with:** Intent Data (adjacent — web-traffic signals are intent-adjacent but not B2B-account intent)
**Spotlight rank:** 4

## What it does
Market research, competitive analysis, and SEO/PPC tools for marketers, researchers, and SEO/PPC teams across 100M+ websites and 10 years of historical data.

## Spotlight when
The article covers brand/category-level web-traffic intent vs. B2B-account intent. Useful when the buyer needs SEO/PPC competitive intelligence alongside sales-data needs.

- Key features: 100M+ website competitive analysis, keyword research, PPC, ad intelligence, affiliate insights, market sizing
- Strengths: 100M+ website coverage with 10-year history; ad-network intelligence (not in ZI); bridges marketing + sales via Sales Signals
- Gaps: Brand/category-level intent not B2B-account intent; not B2B-sales-specific ICP; no conversation/CRM context
