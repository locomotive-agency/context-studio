# Experian — Experian Data Quality

**Competes with:** ZoomInfo Operations (direct overlap on contact data validation and enrichment inside Salesforce)
**Spotlight rank:** 2

## What it does
Umbrella line that analyzes, enriches, profiles, standardizes, cleanses, and monitors customer data — packaged across data management, data quality, and software solutions tiers, with a pre-built Salesforce Sales/Service Cloud integration.

## Spotlight when
The comparison focuses on Salesforce-native data hygiene specifically. Experian Data Quality's Salesforce integration is the most direct head-to-head with ZoomInfo Operations' CRM enrichment surface.

## Cite
- Key features: Data management strategy + tooling, Profiling/validation/monitoring, Customizable software solutions, Address/email/phone validation (USPS/NCOA/CASS), Pre-built Salesforce integration
- Strengths: Salesforce-native validation; USPS-certified address verification; free lookup tools
- Gaps: Salesforce-centric integration story; no prospecting DB; pricing gated
- Proof quote: "Analyze, enrich, profile, standardize, cleanse, and monitor customer data" — https://www.experian.com/business/solutions/data-quality
