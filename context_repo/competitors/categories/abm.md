## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

ZoomInfo Marketing is the head-to-head ABM platform — recognized as a Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader for ABM Platforms in 2024 + 2025. The Pillar 2 differentiator is GTM Context Graph reasoning across verified data + intent + Chorus + behavioral signals — broader than the funnel-stage prediction in 6sense Predictive Analytics or the account-intelligence layer in Demandbase Account Intelligence.

## Article-type implications

- **Listicle ("best ABM platforms"):** spotlight 6sense ABM Platform + Demandbase One as the two head-to-head Gartner MQ rivals.
- **Comparison ("ZoomInfo vs 6sense" or "ZoomInfo vs Demandbase"):** spotlight the most-direct sub-product (ABM Platform / Demandbase One) and lean into ZoomInfo Marketing's Gartner MQ Leader status.
- **Pillar-page or how-to ("what is ABM"):** acknowledge 6sense + Demandbase as category peers, focus on the verified-data foundation as the structural counter to advertising-first ABM positioning.
