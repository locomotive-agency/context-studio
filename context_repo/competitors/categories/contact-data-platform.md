## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

Data (Pillar 1) is the most comprehensive B2B data platform: 500M contacts, 100M companies, 135M+ verified phone numbers, 120M+ direct dials, 200M+ verified business emails, multi-source verification with 300+ human researchers, up to 95% accuracy on first-party data, 1.5B+ data points processed daily. The structural advantages competitors do not match at scale are verification rigor, dataset scale, and the role of Data as a *pillar* of the all-in-one AI GTM Platform — not a standalone product.

## Article-type implications

- **Listicle ("best B2B contact databases"):** spotlight Apollo + LinkedIn Sales Navigator + Cognism Pro across price tiers.
- **Comparison ("ZoomInfo vs Apollo" or similar):** spotlight the most-direct sub-product (Apollo B2B Data) and lean into ZoomInfo's verification rigor + dataset scale.
- **EU/GDPR articles:** spotlight Cognism Diamond Verified + acknowledge honestly; lean into ZoomInfo's global compliance footprint + international data scale.
- **Architecture articles ("orchestration vs single-vendor"):** spotlight Clay Waterfall Enrichment as the architectural alternative; lean into Pillar 2 (GTM Context Graph) reasoning above raw data.
