## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

ZoomInfo Marketing is the head-to-head ABM platform — recognized as a Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader for ABM Platforms in 2024 + 2025. The Pillar 2 differentiator is GTM Context Graph reasoning across verified data + intent + Chorus + behavioral signals — broader than the funnel-stage prediction in 6sense Predictive Analytics or the account-intelligence layer in Demandbase Account Intelligence.
