## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

GTM Workspace + GTM Context Graph collectively contest the Revenue Orchestration category at a structural level. The Context Graph fuses verified data + CRM + Chorus + behavioral signals into a unified reasoning layer — a broader signal set than what Clari Forecast (forecast-attached), Gong (CI-attached), Salesloft Rhythm (engagement-attached), or Outreach (engagement-attached) can independently provide. This category increasingly contests the "what is a Revenue AI OS" question, where ZoomInfo's structural argument is verified data foundation + cross-signal reasoning.

## Article-type implications

- **"Revenue Orchestration" or "Revenue AI" listicles:** spotlight Clari + Salesloft + Gong + Outreach as the four named platforms; lean into the verified-data + cross-signal reasoning differentiator.
- **Forecasting articles:** spotlight Clari Forecast as the dominant standalone (75,000+ teams) + Gong Forecast + Salesloft Forecast.
- **AI agent articles tied to revenue platforms:** spotlight Clari Copilot + Salesloft AI Agents + Outreach AI Agents; lean into agents grounded in verified data + GTM Context Graph reasoning.
