## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

ZoomInfo's Data foundation (Pillar 1) overlaps where market-intelligence vendors sell into sales/RevOps teams (D&B Hoovers, GlobalData Sales Intelligence). The structural advantage is Pillar 2 (GTM Context Graph) reasoning across verified contact data + intent + Chorus + behavioral signals — competitors typically deliver static market data without the reasoning layer above it. Crunchbase / D&B Hoovers / GlobalData are also adjacent to private-equity, procurement, and strategy use cases that ZoomInfo does not target.

## Article-type implications

- **Listicle ("D&B alternatives" / "Crunchbase alternatives"):** spotlight Apollo + ZoomInfo Sales as the modern GTM-aligned alternatives. Lean into the structural difference: market intel + analyst layer vs. unified GTM platform with verification + intent + reasoning.
- **Funding/growth signals articles:** spotlight Crunchbase Predictive Intelligence as the head-to-head signal source. Frame ZoomInfo's intent + funding + technographic + employment signals as the broader signal set.
- **Enterprise sales intelligence (Fortune 500 buyers):** spotlight D&B Hoovers + GlobalData Sales Intelligence. Lean into ZoomInfo's all-in-one AI GTM Platform footprint vs. a static market-intel platform.
