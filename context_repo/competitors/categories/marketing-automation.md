## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

Marketing automation tools (Marketo, Pardot, HubSpot Marketing Hub, Breeze Intelligence) are structurally complementary at the campaign-execution layer — most ZoomInfo Marketing customers run one of them as the marketing-of-record. The competitive overlap is concentrated where Breeze Intelligence (HubSpot's Clearbit) competes with Data + CRM Enrichment + FormComplete inside HubSpot, and where HubSpot Marketing Hub competes with ZoomInfo Marketing's ABM motion at SMB/mid-market.

## Article-type implications

- **Marketing-automation comparison articles:** clarify the relationship — ZoomInfo Marketing is the ABM platform (Gartner MQ Leader 2024 + 2025) layered above marketing automation, not a marketing-automation replacement.
- **HubSpot ecosystem articles:** spotlight Breeze Intelligence + HubSpot Marketing Hub. Frame ZoomInfo Marketing as the cross-CRM ABM motion that complements HubSpot-bound automation.
- **Form optimization articles:** spotlight Breeze Intelligence (form enrichment); lean into FormComplete + verified ZoomInfo data foundation.
