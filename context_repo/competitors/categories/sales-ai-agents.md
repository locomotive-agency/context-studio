## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

The AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace is the head-to-head competitor to every entry above. The structural differentiator is GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2) — agents grounded in verified data + intent + Chorus + behavioral signals fused into a unified reasoning layer. Most competitor AI agents work without verified-data + cross-signal grounding, which is the "AI that knows your customers" vs. "AI that guesses" framing.

- **Listicle ("best AI sales tools"):** spotlight Apollo AI Sales Platform + Outreach AI Agents + Salesloft AI Agents as the three most-direct rivals.
- **Comparison ("Agentforce vs ZoomInfo"):** spotlight Agentforce; lean into the Salesforce-bound vs. cross-platform reasoning contrast.
- **Email-specific articles:** spotlight Lavender as a narrow point-tool; frame GTM Workspace as the full-workflow AI agent context.
- **CRM-native AI articles:** spotlight HubSpot Breeze + Salesforce Agentforce + Salesforce Einstein; lean into GTM Context Graph reasoning across multiple data sources vs. CRM-bound reasoning.
