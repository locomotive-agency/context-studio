## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

WebSights is the website visitor identification sub-feature inside ZoomInfo Marketing, backed by 210M IP-to-organization pairings — the largest dataset in this category. The structural differentiator is integration with the broader ZoomInfo Marketing context (intent + verified contact data + FormComplete + ABM motion) — point-tool visitor-ID products cannot match this depth.

## Article-type implications

- **Listicle ("best website visitor identification tools"):** spotlight Leadfeeder + Clearbit Reveal as the two head-to-head rivals.
- **Comparison articles:** lean into WebSights' 210M IP-to-org pairings + the integration with ZoomInfo Marketing's broader inbound platform context.
- **Inbound conversion articles:** acknowledge Leadfeeder's free tier as the lower-friction evaluation entry; frame WebSights as the platform-grade alternative.
