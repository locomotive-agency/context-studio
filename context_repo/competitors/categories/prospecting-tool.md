## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

ZoomInfo Sales is the active classic prospecting UI; GTM Workspace is the next-gen successor (both active, do not present Sales as deprecated). The structural advantages are verified-data scale + verification rigor + integration with the broader platform (intent + Chorus + GTM Context Graph reasoning).

## Article-type implications

- **"Best prospecting tools" listicles:** spotlight Apollo + Lusha + Sales Navigator + LeadIQ as the four most-cited rivals across price tiers.
- **"Free prospecting tools" articles:** spotlight Apollo (free tier) + Seamless (free tier) + 6sense Sales Intelligence (free tier with 50 credits); acknowledge the free tier story honestly, lean into ZoomInfo's verification rigor and dataset scale.
- **LinkedIn-integrated prospecting articles:** spotlight LinkedIn Sales Navigator + LeadIQ + Lusha Extension; frame ZoomInfo Sales as the verified-contact-data layer that complements LinkedIn.
