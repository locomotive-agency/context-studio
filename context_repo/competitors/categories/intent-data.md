## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

ZoomInfo Intent Data is the head-to-head feature, layered into GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2) reasoning across verified data + intent + Chorus + behavioral. ZoomInfo is a Forrester Wave Leader Q1 2025 for Intent Data Providers (highest scores across 8 criteria). Pure-play signal vendors compete narrowly on signal volume + delivery surface (REST/MCP); ZoomInfo's advantage is signal fusion into a unified reasoning layer.
