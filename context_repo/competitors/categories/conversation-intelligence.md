## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

Chorus is ZoomInfo's standalone CI product AND the context capture engine feeding GTM Context Graph (Pillar 2) — the dual role is the structural differentiator vs. standalone CI vendors. TrustRadius Buyer's Choice 2025 recognition. Articles about CI specifically should spotlight Gong + Clari Conversation Intelligence; articles about all-in-one platforms with CI included should spotlight Apollo Conversations + Outreach Meet + Salesloft Conversations.

## Article-type implications

- **Listicle ("best conversation intelligence tools"):** spotlight Gong + Clari as the two head-to-head standalone CI rivals.
- **Comparison ("Gong vs Chorus" or "Gong vs ZoomInfo"):** lean into Chorus' role as context capture engine feeding GTM Context Graph — the unified reasoning layer Gong lacks.
- **Bundled-platform articles:** spotlight Apollo Conversations + Outreach Meet + Salesloft Conversations; lean into Chorus' analytics maturity + analyst recognition.
