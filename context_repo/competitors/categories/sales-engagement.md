## How ZoomInfo plays in this category

GTM Workspace (next-gen, launched October 2025) is the seller front-end that absorbs all competitive overlap with sales engagement vendors. The structural advantage is bundled verified data + intent + Chorus context inside the workspace — vendors like Outreach, Salesloft, and Gong run alongside data vendors but do not bundle them at parity with ZoomInfo. The AI agent layer inside GTM Workspace competes with each vendor's agentic offering.

## Article-type implications

- **Listicle ("best sales engagement tools"):** spotlight Outreach + Salesloft as the two enterprise leaders + Apollo Engage + Gong Engage as the all-in-one + revenue-AI alternatives.
- **Comparison ("Outreach vs Salesloft vs ZoomInfo"):** lean into GTM Workspace's bundled data + intent + Chorus context as the structural differentiator.
- **AI agents articles:** spotlight Outreach AI Agents + Salesloft AI Agents + Apollo AI Sales Platform; lean into GTM Context Graph as the verified-data + cross-signal grounding layer.
