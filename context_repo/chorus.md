# ZoomInfo Chorus AI: Conversation Intelligence for Sales

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/products/chorus](https://www.zoominfo.com/products/chorus)

## Summary

Conversation intelligence platform backed by 14 proprietary ML patents. Captures and analyzes customer calls, meetings, and emails to reveal why deals are won or lost, improve forecast accuracy, and coach reps based on real conversation data.


## Key Capabilities

- Automatic transcription and analysis of calls, meetings, and emails
- Deal intelligence — identify win/loss patterns and forecast risks
- Rep coaching based on conversation behaviors and outcomes
- CRM integration for conversation-to-deal attribution
