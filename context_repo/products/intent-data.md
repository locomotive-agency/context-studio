# ZoomInfo Intent Data

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/features/intent-data](https://www.zoominfo.com/features/intent-data)

## Summary

Real-time B2B buying signal platform tracking 4,500+ intent topics across a network of 210M+ IP-to-organization pairings and 6T+ data signals. Identifies accounts actively researching solutions before they contact competitors.


## Key Capabilities

- 4,500+ intent topics across technology, security, finance, HR, and more
- Account-level and contact-level intent signal resolution
- Integration with Sales, Marketing, and Operations workflows
- Competitive displacement signals and topic surge detection


## Connected Products

- [[gtm-context-graph|GTM Context Graph]] — intent topics surface as signals inside the graph, where they are reasoned over alongside CRM data and conversation intelligence
