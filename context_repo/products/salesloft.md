# ZoomInfo + Salesloft

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/solutions/salesloft](https://www.zoominfo.com/solutions/salesloft)

## Summary

Integrated sales engagement platform combining ZoomInfo's GTM data with SalesLoft's multi-touch execution tools. Gives sales teams verified data, better workflows, and engagement capabilities to turn insights into action without busywork.


## Key Capabilities

- ZoomInfo data enrichment embedded in SalesLoft workflows
- 360-degree buyer view combining real-time signals and CRM data
- Multi-touch engagement across email, phone, and social
- Seamless execution without toggling between platforms
