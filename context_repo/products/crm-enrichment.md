# CRM Enrichment with ZoomInfo

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/solutions/crm-enrichment](https://www.zoominfo.com/solutions/crm-enrichment)

## Summary

Automated CRM enrichment that keeps contact and account records accurate and current. Enriches new and existing records with verified data, then maintains them through scheduled or event-triggered updates — eliminating manual CRM cleanup.


## Key Capabilities

- Automatic enrichment of new and existing CRM records
- Scheduled and event-triggered data refresh cycles
- Contact and company data append with verification
- Powers downstream routing, scoring, segmentation, and outreach
