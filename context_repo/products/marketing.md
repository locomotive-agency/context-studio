# ZoomInfo Marketing - Contact-Level ABM Platform

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/products/marketing](https://www.zoominfo.com/products/marketing)

## Summary

ABM platform that stacks real-time buyer signals to trigger automated, multi-channel execution. Enables marketers to build targeted audiences, launch campaigns, and capture high-intent demand with contact-level precision.


## Key Capabilities

- Contact-level ABM targeting (not just account-level)
- Real-time buyer signal triggers for automated campaign execution
- Multi-channel orchestration across display, email, and social
- Audience syndication to DSPs and ad networks
- Form optimization to increase conversion rates
