# ZoomInfo Labs

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/labs](https://www.zoominfo.com/labs)

## Summary

ZoomInfo's GTM advisory and innovation arm. Collaborates with customers to build AI and data-driven playbooks — transforming first-party and third-party data into company-specific insights that power personalized outreach at scale.


## Key Capabilities

- GTM advisor collaboration for playbook development
- Data-driven strategy consulting (1st and 3rd party data)
- Automated workflow design for relevance at scale
- Custom AI-powered personalization playbooks
