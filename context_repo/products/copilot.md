# ZoomInfo Copilot: Your AI Sales Agent

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/copilot](https://www.zoominfo.com/copilot)

## Summary

AI sales agent that surfaces verified decision-makers, prioritizes accounts showing buying signals, and provides real-time account intelligence — giving sellers everything they need to prospect, prep, and engage in one place.


## Key Capabilities

- AI-powered account prioritization based on buying signals and fit scoring
- Automated prospect research and account briefings
- Verified contact surfacing with direct dials and emails
- Unified workspace consolidating prospecting, research, and engagement


## Connected Products

- [[gtm-context-graph|GTM Context Graph]] — intelligence layer powering Copilot's account prioritization, signal scoring, and AI-generated briefings (Copilot's AI engine is now the agent layer inside [[gtm-workspace|GTM Workspace]])
