# Go-to-Market Software & Solutions

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/platform](https://www.zoominfo.com/platform)

## Summary

ZoomInfo's all-in-one GTM platform — ranked #1 in 30+ G2 categories with 7,000+ reviews. Combines intelligence, engagement, and orchestration products powered by real-time B2B data to identify who to reach, how to reach them, and when.


## Key Capabilities

- Intelligence: Sales prospecting, buyer intent, contact & company search
- Engagement: Chorus conversation intelligence, Chat, SalesLoft integration
- Orchestration: Workflow automation, CRM enrichment, ICP modeling
- Unified data foundation powering all products
