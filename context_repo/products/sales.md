# ZoomInfo Sales - B2B Sales Prospecting Software

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/products/sales](https://www.zoominfo.com/products/sales)

## Summary

B2B sales prospecting platform with access to 420M+ global contacts, 120M+ direct dials, and 145M+ companies. Combines verified contact data, buyer intent signals, and conversation intelligence to help sales teams build pipeline and close deals faster.


## Key Capabilities

- Verified B2B database with 300+ search attributes (tech stack, funding, firmographics)
- Buyer intent signals to prioritize accounts actively researching solutions
- Conversation intelligence via Chorus integration
- CRM integration with automatic data updates and enrichment
- Chrome extension for prospecting from LinkedIn and the web
