# Conversational Sales & Marketing Platform

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/products/chat](https://www.zoominfo.com/products/chat)

## Summary

Data-powered conversational sales and marketing platform that turns website traffic into pipeline. Routes qualified visitors to reps in real time, triggers tailored chat experiences based on visitor identity, and converts conversations into booked meetings.


## Key Capabilities

- Real-time alerts when high-value visitors or existing customers are on-site
- Intelligent routing to the right rep based on visitor data
- One-click meeting booking from chat conversations
- Tailored chat experiences triggered by visitor firmographics and behavior
