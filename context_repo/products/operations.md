# ZoomInfo Operations

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/products/operations](https://www.zoominfo.com/products/operations)

## Summary

Revenue operations platform for maintaining clean CRM data, automating lead routing, and triggering workflows based on buying signals. Handles deduplication, enrichment, and continuous verification to keep GTM records accurate.


## Key Capabilities

- Automated deduplication, enrichment, and record verification
- Lead routing and scoring based on real-time signals
- Workflow triggers on buying signals, job changes, and firmographic updates
- Territory management and TAM analysis tools
