# GTM Workspace - AI that guides your reps to the next best action

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/gtm-workspace](https://www.zoominfo.com/gtm-workspace)

## Summary

AI-powered selling workspace where reps research accounts, prep for meetings, spot risks early, and prioritize deals — all with complete GTM context. Unifies CRM data with ZoomInfo intelligence for instant account briefs and real-time signals.


## Key Capabilities

- AI-generated account briefs and meeting prep
- Real-time buying signals and account health monitoring
- Smart Views for deal prioritization and risk detection
- Unified CRM + ZoomInfo intelligence in one interface
