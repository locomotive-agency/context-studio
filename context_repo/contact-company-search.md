# Find Verified Company Contacts

**Source:** [https://www.zoominfo.com/features/contact-company-search](https://www.zoominfo.com/features/contact-company-search)

## Summary

Core B2B database search tool used by 35,000+ businesses. Provides access to verified contact data — direct dials, emails, LinkedIn profiles — with firmographic filters to identify and connect with decision-makers across the buying committee.


## Key Capabilities

- 95% accurate verified emails and direct dials
- Advanced search filters by title, department, company size, technology stack
- Buying committee mapping across target accounts
- CRM integration for direct list export and enrichment
